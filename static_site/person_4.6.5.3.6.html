JONAS HAIT

ID: 4.6.5.3.6 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.3.6) JONAS HAIT (#3392) born December 2, 1778 Pound Ridge and died there June 12, 1828. He was a farmer in Pound Ridge, though he seems to have moved to Bedford NY about 1815, but apparently returned to Pound Ridge where he died.
