WILLIAM HAIT

ID: 2.5.2.7.1.3 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.7.1.3) WILLIAM HAIT (#3034) born April 25, 1743 and died probably in Stamford, where he lived, November 15, 1771 of small pox; married December 4, 1766 Mary Weed, who after William’s death married, in November, 1784, Samuel Hait (#2973).
