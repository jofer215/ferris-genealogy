THERON N HOUGHTON

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.5.2.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.5.2.3) THERON N HOUGHTON born September 29, 1906 Elmwood Township, Leelanau Co MI and died January 15, 1938 Traverse City MI; buried there Oakwood Cemetery; USNR Veteran; Zapf Fruit Packing Co, Grand Traverse Auto Co, Salesman for City Beverage Co; married April 21, 1936 in Elk Rapids MI, Margaret Hickey [dau of James]

No children recorded
