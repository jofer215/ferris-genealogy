ANN ELIZA FERRIS

ID: 1.4.1.5.1 | Source: John
(1.4.1.5.1) ANN ELIZA FERRIS (Eliza Ann) born June 10, 1791 White Plains NY and died April 18, 1877 NYC; married March 14/23, 1816 Alexander James Cranston Hamilton [son of Alexander James & Mary (Deane) Hamilton] born May 16, 1786 NYC and died August 2, 1875 Westchester Co NY. Born to them were:

Children (6)

  • SARAH ADELINE HAMILTON 1.4.1.5.1.1
  • HELEN HAMILTON; unmarried 1.4.1.5.1.2
  • ALEXANDER JAMES F. 1.4.1.5.1.3
  • CATHARINE MARTHA HAMILTON 1.4.1.5.1.4
  • MARGARET LORING HAMILTON; unmarried 1.4.1.5.1.5
  • HENRY 1.4.1.5.1.6
