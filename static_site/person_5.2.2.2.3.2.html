ISAAC BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.2.3.2 | Source: James
(5.2.2.2.3.2) ISAAC BUXTON born August 16, 1788 Stamford CT and died January 7, 1867 Bedford, Westchester, NY; buried Buxton Cemetery, Bedford; a farmer; married 1st February 12, 1818 in Westchester County NY, Jane Gage born c1790 and died April 15, 1834 Westchester Co NY; married 2d October 13, 1835 in Manhattan NY, Rebecca M. Tucker born c1790 and died July 1866. Born to Isaac and Jane were:
