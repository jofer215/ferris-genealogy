FRED TERRILL

ID: 1.2.6.2.1.1.7.7.1 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.1.1.7.7.1) FRED TERRILL born November 8, 1884 Colchester IL and died April 23, 1969 Covallis, Benton, OR; married March 18, 1909 in Seattle WA, Jennie May Robinson [dau of James & Margaret (Schnatterly) Robinson] born October 6, 1886 McDonough Co IL and died July 25, 1957 Eugene OR; both buried Rest Haven Memorial Park, Eugene; and they had 4 daughters
