KEZIAH FERRIS

ID: 3.1.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6) KEZIAH FERRIS (8RLL-52) born c1706 probably Greenwich and died c1754 Fairfield; married {as his second wife?} November 14/19, 1729 in the Greenwich Second Congregational Church, Timothy Canfield born December 28, 1701 (or September 16, 1700) Ridgefield/Norwalk CT and died 1754 South Salem, Westchester, NY [son of Ebenezer and Mary (Hendrick/Hennery/Henry) Canfield]; resided Ridgefield CT; and born to them were:
