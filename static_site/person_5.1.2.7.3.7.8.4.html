ALONZO GAYLORD HALE

ID: 5.1.2.7.3.7.8.4 | Source: James
(5.1.2.7.3.7.8.4) ALONZO GAYLORD HALE born September 9, 1886 Belle Plaine IA and died October 12, 1955 Seattle WA; married August 28, 1912 Moose Jaw, Canada Gertrude Grace Humbert [dau of Prosper & Mary Rozina (VanValin) Humbert] born April 15, 1882 Caldwell WI and died November 21, 1951 Seattle; and born to them were: (5.1.2.7.3.7.8.4.1) MARIE CLARABELL HALE born June 12, 1913 Seattle WA and died April 26, 2000 Vancouver WA; married November 10, 1938 in Seattle, Robert Allen Hannan [son of George Gilbert & Mary Florence (Griffith) Hannan] born March 26, 1912 Winatchee WA and died October 30, 1999 Vancouver WA; and they had 2 kids

No children recorded
