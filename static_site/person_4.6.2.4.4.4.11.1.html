SARAH ANN DANN

ID: 4.6.2.4.4.4.11.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.2.4.4.4.11.1) SARAH ANN DANN born April 5, 1853 Stamford and died there January 10, 1934; buried there Long Ridge Cemetery; married c1884 as his 2d wife, John William Holly born May 16, 1850 Stamford and died January 7, 1924 Stamford; buried with Long Ridge Cemetery; John’s 1st marriage was to Josephine Ferris (5.1.3.3.1.7.1.2) – apparently no offspring; and born to Sarah and John were:

Children (2)

  • HOWARD E. HOLLY 4.6.2.4.4.4.11.1.1
  • WALTER L. HOLLY 4.6.2.4.4.4.11.1.2
