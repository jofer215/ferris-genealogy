RINALDO ALPHONSO BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.6.1.2.2 | Source: James
(5.2.2.6.1.2.2) RINALDO ALPHONSO BUXTON born May 18, 1830 Lowville, Lewis, NY and died March 12, 1902 Stockbridge, Calument, WI. It was said that Rinaldo had titian red hair. He married November 27, 1857 in Dotyville, Fond du Lac, WI, Lucy Jane Scofield [dau of Selah & Harriet (Barber) Scofield] born March 27, 1840 Essex, Chittenden, VT and died June 2, 1916 North Stockridge WI.
