WAUNETA PEARL MAYNARD

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.6.6.3.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.6.6.3.1) WAUNETA PEARL MAYNARD born September 12, 1892 Ruggles Township, Ashland Co OH and died November 11, 1987 Shelby, Richland, OH; married September 4, 1912 in Boughtonville OH, Ray Cullis Boardman [son of Benjamin &Edith (McBride) Boardman] born October 11, 1890 New Haven Township, Huron Co OH and died November 6, 1976 North Fairfield, Huron, OH. Wauneta attended high school in North Fairfield OH. After their wedding they lived in New Haven Township, Huron County OH - Ray was a farmer. Circa 1926 they moved to North Fairfield OH to a farm at the city limits. They were active in the Methodist Church, the Grange, and a Farm Women's Club. Ray was also a Scout Master.
