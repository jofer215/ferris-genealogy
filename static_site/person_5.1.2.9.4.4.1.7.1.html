GEORGE E. HORTON

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.1.7.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.1.7.1) GEORGE E. HORTON born October 3, 1897 Jefferson Valley NY and died June 3, 1974; married Susan Mackey. Obituary, Star, Peekskill, NY, June 4, 1974: “George (Doc) Horton of 105 Kings Ferry Road, Montrose, died at the Community Hospital, yesterday at the age of 76. Mr. Horton was employed as a machinist for Standard Coated Products prior to his retirement. Born in Jefferson Valley on Oct. 3, 1897, he was the son of the late Wilbur Horton, Sr., and Emma Christian. He was a veteran of World War I and a member of the American Legion Peekskill Post 274. He was also a member of the Trinity Boscobel Methodist Church. Mr. Horton is survived by his wife the former Susan Mackey, of the home address.” Buried Hillside Cemetery. Susan apparently brought a son with her to the marriage. No offspring from this marriage.

No children recorded
