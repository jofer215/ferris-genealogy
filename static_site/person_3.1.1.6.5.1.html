MARMADUKE EARL FERRIS, M.D.,

ID: 3.1.1.6.5.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.5.1) MARMADUKE EARL FERRIS, M.D., born December 26, 1805 and died September 1, 1850 Illinois; married 1st September 18, 1828 in Dearborn Co IN, Sarah Jane Hunter; married 2d July 6, 1834 in Perry MO as her 2d husband, Adeline W (Buckner) Shade [dau of Nicholas & Eleanor Magdalena (Sullivan) Buckner] (Adaline W Shade); and born to him and Adeline was: (3.1.1.6.5.1.1) W. C. FERRIS

No children recorded
