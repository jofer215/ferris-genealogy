REBECCA WATERBURY

ID: 5.2.4.4.5.2 | Source: James
(5.2.4.4.5.2) REBECCA WATERBURY born January 18, 1792 CT and died November 13, 1869 Stamford; married May 12, 1816 in Stamford, Davis Lockwood [son of Ezra & Anna (Davis) Lockwood – is this (4.6.3.5?)] born March 11, 1795 Watertown CT and died April 7, 1844 Stamford; both buried there Woodland Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • HENRY DAVIS LOCKWOOD 5.2.4.4.5.2.1
  • SAMUEL LOCKWOOD, JR., 5.2.4.4.5.2.2
  • WILLIAM WATERBURY LOCKWOOD 5.2.4.4.5.2.3
  • JAMES LEWIS LOCKWOOD 5.2.4.4.5.2.4
  • THEODORE LOCKWOOD 5.2.4.4.5.2.6
