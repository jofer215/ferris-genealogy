HATTIE L. HOYT

ID: 4.6.5.11.2.9.3 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.11.2.9.3) HATTIE L. HOYT born November 2, 1859 and died September 24, 1865; buried private cemetery near corner at Sarles Corner, Pound Ridge NY - cemetery records have her father’s name as Asabel - apparent discrepancy in copying info from the tombstone
