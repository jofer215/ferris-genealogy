PHILENA MEKEEL

ID: 5.2.10.11.2.4 | Source: James
(5.2.10.11.2.4) PHILENA MEKEEL born September 12, 1833 (obviously an incorrect year - probably a typo.) at Philipstown and died at her home in the North Highlands, June 28, 1875; married, as his second wife, William Knapp born Cold Springs, Dutchess, NY, August-October 30, 1825 and died at his home in the North Highlands, October 25, 1904; both were buried in the North Highlands Cemetery; Philen's tombstone spells her maiden name as McKeel. Mr. Knapp's whole active life was devoted to agricultural pursuits and was known as one of the prosperous farmers of the North Highlands. Born to them were:
