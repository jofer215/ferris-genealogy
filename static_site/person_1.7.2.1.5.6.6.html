EDWARD GIRARD SCHIEFFELIN

ID: 1.7.2.1.5.6.6 | Source: John
(1.7.2.1.5.6.6) EDWARD GIRARD SCHIEFFELIN born March 25, 1836 Charleston PA and died February 16, 1922 Wellsboro, Tioga, PA; he served with the Pennsylvania Volunteers during the Civil War and settled in Wellsboro, Tioga Co; married 1st April 8, 1878 Barbara Duttenaffer (-1884); married 2d July 15, 1882 Elizabeth Schmitt; and married 3d October 17, 1894 in Wellsboro PA, Mary Monroe Summerville; and born to Edward and Elizabeth was:

Children (4)
