JOSEPH ELLISON LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.1.4.1.3.4.6 | Source: James
(5.1.4.1.3.4.6) JOSEPH ELLISON LOCKWOOD born May 18, 1833 Stamford CT and died there 1917; buried there Woodland Cemetery; shoemaker 1850; butcher 1859-63; and market man 1872; married December 22, 1857 in Stamford, Mary Jane Talmadge [dau of John L & Clarinda] born May 1838 New Canaan CT and died November 4, 1883 Stamford; buried with Joe; and born to them were:
