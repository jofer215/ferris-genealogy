REBECCA UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.3.9 | Source: John
(1.2.5.3.9) REBECCA UNDERHILL (1st born) born June 23, 1757 York, Westchester, NY and died May 15, 1835 New Castle, Westchester, NY; Quaker; married September 4, 1780 in Amawalk NY, Moses Sutton [son of Richardson & Elizabeth (Quimby/Quinby ) Sutton] born March 15, 1756 Cortland Manor NY (Somers, Westchester, NY) and died December 24, 1844 Croton Valley, Westchester, NY; buried York NY; Quaker; and born to them were: (1.2.5.3.9.1) SARAH SUTTON born 1783 Somers, Westchester, NY and died Scipio NY; married May 11, 1827 in New Castle, Westchester, NY Nehemiah Merritt
