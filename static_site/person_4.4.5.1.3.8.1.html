HENRY CLAY LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.5.1.3.8.1 | Source: Mary
(4.4.5.1.3.8.1) HENRY CLAY LOCKWOOD (#2491) born June 21, 1839 Ossining NY; served in the Civil War, March 31, 1862 to January 4, 1866; lawyer; brevetted Major for gallant services at the storming of Fort Fisher; author of a political work entitled, Abolition of the Presidency, which attracted much attention in both this country and Europe

No children recorded
