DEBRA SUE PAGEL

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.2.3.2.7.4.2.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.2.3.2.7.4.2.2) DEBRA SUE PAGEL born October 24, 1968 Milbank SD; graduated from South Dakota State University with a Bachelor's degree in Early Childhood Development 1996. Debra previously attended the Anoka MN Technical College and worked four years in day care centers in MT and SD. 1998-Deb moved to Hayti SD, where she started a new job as an early childhood advocate for the Head Start Program.
