MARY SCOFIELD

ID: 5.2.3.2.5.3 | Source: James
(5.2.3.2.5.3) MARY SCOFIELD born September 17, 1807 Stamford and died there November 1, 1848; married February 11, 1838 in Stamford, as his 1st of 3 wives, George Scofield (2.1.5.6.1.4.4); both buried there North Street Cemetery – apparently no offspring from this union – see him

No children recorded
