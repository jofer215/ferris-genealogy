JOHN PATCH

ID: 4.6.9.8.3 | Source: Mary
(4.6.9.8.3) JOHN PATCH (PARTCH) born September 29, 1780 Danbury CT and died July 13, 1873 Hinesburg VT; buried there; married April 13, 1814 Ruby P Lawrence born April 19, 1794 and died July 24, 1855; John’s brother married Ruby’s sister, Lephia Ann; the two couples built a log cabin in a clearing in the woods and when the families grew they built an addition, one family living in one part and the other family in the wing; in early life John was a carpenter but later became a farmer; he entered the War of 1812 and was stationed for a time at Burlington VT, serving as a musician; it is said that John was unpretending, intelligent, and much respected; and born to them were: (4.6.9.8.3.1) CASSANDRA MELVINA PARTCH (Cascenda) born May 1, 1815 and died January 30, 1896; married April 1, 1845 in Shelburne VT, William R Lawrence
