THOMAS BRIAN WADE

ID: 3.1.1.6.1.6.5.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.1.6.5.2) THOMAS BRIAN WADE born August 15, 1875 Sadorus IL; attended Champaign High School and U of Illinois; received a DDS degree from the U of Pennsylvania; was on their faculty for 4-5 years after which he specialized in x-ray work; opened an office in the Medical Arts Building on the corner of Sixteenth and Chestnut Strees, Philadelphia; married March 30, 1899 Jennie Hunter Moore and they had 4 kids, 3 of which were:
