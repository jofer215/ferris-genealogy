ELAINE DAVIS

ID: 3.1.1.5.2.3.12.5.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.5.2.3.12.5.2) ELAINE DAVIS born June 5, 1914 and died March 2, 1989 Smethport, McKean, PA; buried there Rose Hill Cemetery. She was born Violet Elaine Davis and changed her name on marriage; married June 15, 1933 Robert King Palmer [son of Willett Sheridan & Orra Hamlin (King) Palmer] born November 4, 1907 and died September 9, 1965 Shinglehouse PA; and they had 1 child

No children recorded
