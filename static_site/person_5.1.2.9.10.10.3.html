EMERETTA E. HORTON

ID: 5.1.2.9.10.10.3 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.10.10.3) EMERETTA E. HORTON born August 1865 Putnam Valley NY and died there March 28, 1945; married Edward Augustus Tompkins (5.1.2.9.2.5.1.5); both buried Hillside Cemetery, Peekskill NY. Obituary, Star, Peekskill NY March 29, 1945: Mrs. Emeretta Horton Tompkins, widow of the late E. Augustus Tompkins of Oregon, died yesterday at her home. She was in her 80th year. Mrs. Tompkins was born at Lake Oscawana, daughter of James and Anne Travis Horton. She married Mr. Tompkins on Nov. 9, 1887, in Peekskill. She and Mr. Tompkins lived all their life Putnam Valley. She was a charter member of Putnam Valley Grange No. 841, and one of the oldest members of the Oregon Methodist Church. Surviving are two daughters, Mrs. John B. Reinhardt and Mrs. Harry Moshier, of Oregon, one granddaughter, Mrs. William S. Anderson and one great-grandson. Interment will be made in Hillside Cemetery. See him for offspring.

No children recorded
