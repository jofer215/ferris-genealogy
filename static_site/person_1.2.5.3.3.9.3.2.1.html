DAVID SHEARMAN TABER

ID: 1.2.5.3.3.9.3.2.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.3.3.9.3.2.1) DAVID SHEARMAN TABER III born September 29, 1917 NYC and died November 4, 1994 Waterville OH; buried Woodlawn Cemetery, Bronx NY; married Ruth VanDerveer Hoyt [dau of William & Adelaide (Hickcox) Hoyt] born July 25, 1922 and died June 3, 2001 Maumee, Lucas, OH; buried Woodlawn Cemetery, Bronx NY. Ruth graduated 1965 State U. at Geneseo NY; Masters of Education. They had 5 kids (1.2.5.3.3.9.3.3) WILLIAM WOOD TABER born August 19, 1878 NYC and died January 22, 1879

No children recorded
