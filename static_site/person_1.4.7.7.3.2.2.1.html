RICHARD BOLLES PADDOCK, Colonel

ID: 1.4.7.7.3.2.2.1 | Source: John
(1.4.7.7.3.2.2.1) RICHARD BOLLES PADDOCK, Colonel (Photo) born April 16, 1891 Ft McKinney WY; graduated West Point June 1914; C.A.C. on Mexican border with Signal Corps; went to France with First Expeditionary Forces May 1917, served on several fronts, gassed once and wounded once; given French War Cross; returned to USA October 1918; a Major Richard B. Paddock attended the Army General Staff College, Fort Leavenworth KS, 1925-26; student at U. of Nebraska; and apparently married 3 times: 1st June 12, 1914 Anne Cunningham; 2d September 20, 1920 Doris Van D Johnson; and 3d December 31, 1931 Alice Warwick.
