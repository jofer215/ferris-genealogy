MARY ANN UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.1.6.2.9 | Source: John
(1.2.3.1.6.2.9) MARY ANN UNDERHILL born May 19, 1821 NYC and died 1846, if the dates are correct she must have died shortly after getting married; married September 3, 1846 Lyman Fisk, M.D. [son of Samuel & Maria (Williams) Fisk] born 1822 NYC and died August 1, 1859; buried Brooklyn NY; after Mary’s death he married and apparently had 2 kids, one a namesake.

No children recorded
