JAMES BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.6 | Source: James
(5.2.2.6) JAMES BUXTON born 1744 Stamford CT and died there May 19, 1812; Rev War Vet served between 1776-77 in Jonathan Whiting’s Company; a farmer; married November 8, 1767 in Greenwich, Rachel Newman [dau of John & Mary (Hoyt) Newman] born April 15, 1745 Stamford and died there May 16, 1812; both buried Turn of River Cemetery, Stamford.
