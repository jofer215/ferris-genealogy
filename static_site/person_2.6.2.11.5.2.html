HANNAH SCOFIELD

ID: 2.6.2.11.5.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.11.5.2) HANNAH SCOFIELD born February 23, 1798 (?) and died April 5, 1842; buried Fishkill DRC churchyard cemetery; married 1833 in Presbyterian Church, Rombout, Dutchess, NY, as his 2d wife, her brother-in-law, Abraham Voorhis.

No children recorded
