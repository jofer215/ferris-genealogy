SAMUEL PEARL LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.5.8.4.3.8.4 | Source: Mary
(4.5.8.4.3.8.4) SAMUEL PEARL LOCKWOOD (#2270) born March 12, 1871 Chicago IL (Glencoe IL); 1921 resided in Portland OR; and died there January 15, 1937; buried Lockwood Plot, River View Cemetery, Portland; married 1st April 28, 1893 in Portland, Katherine Lincoln Marquam; married 2d November 27, 1901 in Portland, Harriet Millard Morse [dau of Henry Bromwell & Harriet Newell (Millard) Morse] born August 29, 1878 Portland and died April 26, 1967 Milwaukie OR; buried Lockwood Plot, River View Cemetery; and born to Samuel and Katherine were:
