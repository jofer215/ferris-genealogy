MARY MAUD SLAWSON

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.2.2.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.2.2.2) MARY MAUD SLAWSON born September 17, 1870 Prairieville Township, Barry Co MI and died December 9, 1918 Urbandale, Battle Creek, Calhoun, MI; nursed son at Fort Custer during WWI and died from flu contacted then; married September 17, 1890 in Banfield, Barry, MI, Jason Edson Tobias [son of Ezra & Lura (Edson) Tobias] born February 11, 1861 Baltimore Township, Barry Co MI and died November 25, 1939 Urbandale, Battle Creek MI; both buried Bedford, Barry Co MI; he wrote family history, real estate salesman in Battle Creek and founded Urbandale subdivision of Battle Creek; after Mary’s death, Jason married, 1920, Anna Maud Bouck; and born to Mary and Jason were:

Children (2)

  • MYRTLE ADDA TOBIAS 3.1.2.1.4.1.2.2.2.1
  • CARL ARTHUR TOBIAS 3.1.2.1.4.1.2.2.2.2
