HARRIET SCOFIELD

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.7.4.5.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.7.4.5.2) HARRIET SCOFIELD born December 3, 1892 and died June 21, 1970; buried Moss Ridge Cemetery (Photo), Carthage IL; unmarried; for many years associated with the Western Reserve Historical Society in Cleveland OH. She was a primary researcher on the Ferris Family and her work was a major contribution to Chaplain Ferris’ work, and thus, this compilation. Believe Harriet was also a high math teacher at Glenville High School, Cleveland OH. We all are in debt to Harriet for her efforts.

No children recorded
