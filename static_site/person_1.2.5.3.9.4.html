ISAAC SUTTON

ID: 1.2.5.3.9.4 | Source: John
(1.2.5.3.9.4) ISAAC SUTTON born March 8, 1789 Croton Valley, Westchester, NY and died 1861 Battle Creek, Calhoun, MI; buried Turner’s Burying Ground; Quaker 1828, disowned, joined Hicksites; 1838 moved to Michigan; 1840-60 Bedford, Calhoun, MI; married October 17, 1810 Sarah Underhill (1.2.5.4.1.6); and born to them were: (1.2.5.3.9.4.1) TOWNSEND SUTTON born February 12, 1812, Chappaqua (Shappaqua), Westchester, NY; 1840 resided Bedford MI
