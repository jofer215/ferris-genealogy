PETER KNAPP BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.2.3.5 | Source: James
(5.2.2.2.3.5) PETER KNAPP BUXTON born October 11, 1794 Stamford CT and died February 2, 1878 Bedford, Westchester, NY; buried Buxton Cemetery, Bedford. He was a farmer and town supervisor. He married first March 3, 1819 in Bedford, Catharine Robertson born October 18, 1796; married second c1825 in Westchester County, Phebe Smith [dau of Isaac & Elizabeth] born March 19, 1804 and died April 7, 1858 Bedford; buried Buxton Cemetery, Bedford; married third after 1858 in Bedford, Sophia Raymond [dau of David & Millicent (Pennoyer) Raymond] born July 18, 1791 Stamford and died there January 18, 1879.
