BETSIE REGINA KABISCH

ID: 4.6.5.9.5.1.1.1.1.3 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.9.5.1.1.1.1.3) BETSIE REGINA KABISCH (Betsey) born December 3, 1921 Jackson OH; married July 21, 1940 at Ashland KY James Francis Essman [son of Leonard Henry & Anna (Hogan) Essman] born August 8, 1919 Wellston OH and died September 9, 1983 Mansfield OH; buried Mt. Calvary, Wellston OH; Betsy was the first female foreman for Hartman Electric and resides in Fayette County OH. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • JAMES KEITH ESSMAN 4.6.5.9.5.1.1.1.1.3.1
  • THOMAS WILLIAM ESSMAN 4.6.5.9.5.1.1.1.1.3.2
