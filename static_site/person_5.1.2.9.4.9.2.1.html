NILES T. CHRISTIAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.9.2.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.9.2.1) NILES T. CHRISTIAN born October 26, 1883 Putnam Valley and died April 6, 1972 Putnam Community Hospital, Carmel NY; buried there Raymond Hill Cemetery; educated in the Putnam Valley Schools; married 1st April 10, 1904 Caroline M. Travis who died May 22, 1913; married 2d Dolly Marie Dickinson born April 13, 1878 and died May 13, 1943; buried Raymond Hill Cemetery, Carmel NY. Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, Thursday, May 20, 1943: Dolly Marie Christian, of Lake Oscawana, died a Dutchess County hospital on Thursday, May 13, 1943. She was 65 years of age. She born in Essex County, N.Y., April 13, 1878 and was the daughter of Emma Jean Hathaway and David Dickinson. She was a retired schoolteacher. Surviving are her husband, Niles Christian, and children. Burial was made in the family plot in Raymond Hill Cemetery. They resided at Lake Oscawana Road Putnam Valley. He was a self-employed mason contractor. Born to them were:
