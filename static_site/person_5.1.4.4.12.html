SARAH JESSUP

ID: 5.1.4.4.12 | Source: James
(5.1.4.4.12) SARAH JESSUP (Sally) born October 14, 1779 Greenwich; baptized January 16, 1781 and died September 8, 1829 Greenwich; buried Westover Cemetery (Photo), Stamford; married May 9, 1802 in Stamford, Daniel Lockwood III [son of Daniel & Mary (Bellamy) Lockwood] born January 21, 1769 Stamford and died there October 8, 1857; buried Westover Cemetery. After Sarah’s death, Daniel married 1830 Sally Palmer and 1839 Lydia Provost. Born to them were:

Children (7)

  • RUFUS ALLEN LOCKWOOD 5.1.4.4.12.2
