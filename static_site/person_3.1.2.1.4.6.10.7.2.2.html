JAMES HENRY STAHLMAN

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.6.10.7.2.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.6.10.7.2.2) JAMES HENRY STAHLMAN born December 16, 1931 Perry Township, Jefferson Co PA and died January 21, 1972 Punxsutawney, Jefferfson, PA; married November 24, 1953 in Punxsutawney, Margaret Bernadette Lenoe [dau of Andrew & Anna Margaret (Stanko) Lenoe] born November 7, 1933 Brooklyn NY and died December 8, 1982 Pittsburgh PA; both buried Calvary Cemetery, Punxsutawney; and they had 4 kids – James’ son has a wonderful website with great information and wonderful photos – check it out http://freepages.family.rootsweb.com/~stahlman/

No children recorded
