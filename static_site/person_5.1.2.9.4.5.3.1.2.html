WILLIAM J. BAXTER

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.5.3.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.5.3.1.2) WILLIAM J. BAXTER born 1898 Putnam Valley NY and died March 29, 1975; married Grace Graig; resided Cleveland OH 1956; Obituary, Star, Peekskill, NY, April 2, 1975: “William J. Baxter of Seahorse Road, South Harwich, Mass., died Saturday at Cape Cod Hospital in Hyannis. He was 76. Mr. Baxter lived in the greater Peekskill area for many years working locally as a builder together with his late father George Baxter. His mother was the late Louise Smith Baxter. Mr. Baxter was a first cousin to the late Chester Smith, a prominent Peekskill resident. Mr. Baxter born Putnam Valley, and lived on Ringgold Street, Peekskill for many years. He was educated in Peekskill schools and graduated from the University of Pennsylvania with the Class of 1920 with a degree in electrical engineering. He worked for General Electric in Schenectady, Newark, NJ and retired from G.E. in Cleveland, Ohio. Mr. Baxter was a summer resident of Cape Cod since 1935. He retired to Cape Cod seven years ago. He was treasurer of the Harwich Historical Society and a member of the Orleans Retired Men’s Club and the Elfin Society of New York. He attended the Evangelical Baptist Church in South Yarmouth. Interment was at Island Pond Cemetery in Harwich.” Born to them were:
