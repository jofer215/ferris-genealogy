FLORENCE MAUDE CLARK

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.3) FLORENCE MAUDE CLARK born August 3, 1881 Bingham Township, Leelanau, MI and died October 8, 1906; married August 3, 1898 in Fouch MI, William Henry Bowen (his 2d marriage) born 1873 Ontario, Canada and died August 31, 1905 Ann Arbor MI; farmer, laborer; and born to them were: (3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.3.1) AGNES MARION BOWEN born December 10, 1900 Petoskey, Emmet, MI and died September 1986; married Frederick J Stinson born August 10, 1890 Petersbourgh, Canada and died November 10, 1953 Pontiac MI; and born to them were:
