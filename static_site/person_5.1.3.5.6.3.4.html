JEROME SNEDEKER WHELPLY

ID: 5.1.3.5.6.3.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.5.6.3.4) JEROME SNEDEKER WHELPLY born January 1859 Queens NY and died November 20, 1934 White Plains, Westchester, NY; buried Valhalla (Kensico Cemetery), Westchester NY; was well known horse trainer and a familiar figure at many horse shows, races and trotting tracks; married 1st name unk; married 2d c1892 Clara Smith born November 1867 NY and died March 16, 1947 White Plains NY; buried with Jerome; and born to him and his first wife were:
