LUCIEN B. TOMPKINS

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.3.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.3.1) LUCIEN B. TOMPKINS born c1856 Putnam Valley NY and died there December 28, 1884; married Mary Louise Tompkins [dau of Cornelius & Abbie Jane (Silleck)] born July 27, 1852 Putnam Valley NY and died there November 3, 1938; both buried Hillside Cemetery, Peekskill NY. Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, Thursday, Nov. 10, 1938: Mrs. Mary Louise Tompkins, widow of the late George Tompkins, died at her home on the Peekskill Hollow road, near Tompkins Corners, on Saturday, Nov. 3, 1938, at the age of 86 years. Mrs. Tompkins, a highly respected citizen, born Putnam Valley July 27, 1852, daughter of Cornelius Tompkins and Abbie Jane Silleck. Surviving are two sons, Walter Tompkins, who lived with his mother; Zaccheus Tompkins, of Peekskill; and one sister, Lillian Tompkins, of Mill Street. Interment was in Hillside cemetery.
