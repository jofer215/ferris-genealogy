ELIZABETH KNAPP

ID: 2.6.1.9 | Source: Peter
(2.6.1.9) ELIZABETH KNAPP born December 20 (30?), 1733 Stamford and died January 8, 1787 Greenwich; buried Old Sound Beach Cemetery, Greenwich; married 1st April 4, 1751 at Stamford, Nathaniel Palmer; married 2d between March 20, 1753 - January 23, 1763 Enos Lockwood (4.2.7.1) at Greenwich. For Elizabeth’s and Enos’s offspring, see Enos.

No children recorded
