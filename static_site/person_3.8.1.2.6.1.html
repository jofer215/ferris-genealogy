AMANDA FERRIS

ID: 3.8.1.2.6.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.8.1.2.6.1) AMANDA FERRIS born July 10, 1797 Ferrisburg VT and died February 11, 1854 age 56y 7m 1d (from her tombstone); married Joseph Jones. [one source has Amanda marrying after the 1820 census, probably in Hectory, Tompkins County NY, as his second wife, Obadiah Brown, Jr. born November 7, 1777 and died February 19, 1854 at the age of 76 years 3 months 12 days (from tombstone); buried East Troy Cemetery, Bradford County PA.] [we might surmise that Joseph Jones died between 1816-1820 and this was Amanda’s second marriage]; both Amanda and Obadiah buried East Troy Cemetery, East Troy, Bradford, PA (Photos).
