JAMES WASHINGTON JEVENS

ID: 1.1.4.4.4.4.11 | Source: John
(1.1.4.4.4.4.11) JAMES WASHINGTON JEVENS born August 7/8, 1871 Cornwall, Orange, NY and died May 3, 1936 Highland Falls, Orange, NY; married Sarah Armstrong born December 6, 1877 Enniskillen, North Ireland and died April 30, 1936 Highland Falls NY; both were buried May 5, 1936 Union Cemetery, Highland Falls.
