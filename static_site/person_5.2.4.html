JAMES BELL

ID: 5.2.4 | Source: James
(5.2.4) JAMES BELL (Sergeant) born March 17, 1709/10 Stamford CT and died there 1775; married 1st October 11, 1733 in Stamford, Sarah Weed born January 21, 1711 Stamford; married 2d after 1755 Joanna Holly born September 5, 1715 Stamford. James’ will was dated April 18, 1776 and probated June 6th, 1776. Born to him and Sarah were:
