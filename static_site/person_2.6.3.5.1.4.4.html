MARY CLARISSA QUINTARD

ID: 2.6.3.5.1.4.4 | Source: Peter
(2.6.3.5.1.4.4) MARY CLARISSA QUINTARD born March 16, 1828 Stamford and died there March 30, 1912; married July 17, 1851 in NYC, Rufus Hoyt [son of William & Harriet (Hubbell) Hoyt] born August 28, 1820 Greenfield CT and died August 27, 1874 Stamford; both buried Woodland Cemetery; he was a merchant 1860 in NY; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • MINNIE MATILDA HOYT 2.6.3.5.1.4.4.2
  • HARRIET HOOKER HOYT 2.6.3.5.1.4.4.3
