CAROLINE ELIZABETH HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.4.5.9.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.5.9.2) CAROLINE ELIZABETH HOYT (#4556) born June 11, 1824 Morristown, Middlesex, NJ and died February 9, 1866 Sycamore, DeKalb, IL; married as his 1st wife, September 20, 1841 at Sycamore IL (at Newburg, Winnebago, IL) John Calvin Waterman [son of John Dean & Mary Graves (Waldo) Waterman] born September 9, 1814 Salisbury, Herkimer, NY and died October 19, 1883 Sycamore IL; merchant; and born to them were:
