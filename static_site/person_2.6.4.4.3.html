ELIZABETH ARNOLD

ID: 2.6.4.4.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.4.4.3) ELIZABETH ARNOLD born c1752 and died before 1788; married c1770 Joseph DeMille [son of Peter & Abigail (Banks) DeMille] born February 27, 1740/41 and died November 21, 1800 Stamford; after Elizabeth’s death, Joseph married Sarah Holly; and born to Elizabeth and Joseph was: (2.6.4.4.3.1) PETER DEMILLE married September 8, 1774 Stamford and died there July 23, 1852; married November 15, 1795 Sophia Mead (2.6.5.2.1.1) – see her for offspring since it was entered there first Born to Peter and his second wife, Mary Sloan (Mary Slason), were:

No children recorded
