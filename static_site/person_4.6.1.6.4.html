MOLLY WOOD

ID: 4.6.1.6.4 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.6.4) MOLLY WOOD (4.6.1.6.5) EBENEZER WOOD, Jr., (#697) born November 21, 1772/73 Salem, Westchester, NY and died January 7, 1862 South Salem NY; married 1st July 6, 1798 in South Salem, Hannah Keeler [dau of Jeremiah & Elizabeth (Weed) Keeler] born 1776 New Canaan CT and died March 30, 1799, South Salem; married 2d Margaret Hazard Strang [dau of Henry & Margaret (Hazard) Strang] born c1777 and died June 22, 1822 South Salem NY

No children recorded
