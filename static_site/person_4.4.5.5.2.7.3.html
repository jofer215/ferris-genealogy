LUKE VINCENT LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.5.5.2.7.3 | Source: Mary
(4.4.5.5.2.7.3) LUKE VINCENT LOCKWOOD (#2477) born February 1, 1872 Brooklyn; married November 16, 1897 Alice Gardner Burnell. Luke attended Trinity College, A.B., 1893; A.M., 1895; NY Law School, LLB, 1895; President, Greenwich Trust Co.; Greenwich News Graphic; Trustee, Brooklyn Institute Arts and Sciences; President, Greenwich Hospital; Director, Norfolk and Southern Railroad; member Phi Beta Kappa Connecticut Historical Society; New York Yacht Club, etc.; author of various books and articles on Colonial and other old furniture of which he collected; business was on Broadway in NYC - resided in Riverside CT.
