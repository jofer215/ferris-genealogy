ETHAN FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.3) ETHAN FERRIS born May 10, 1766 Greenwich and died there January 3, 1833; buried Old Sound Beach Cemetery, Greenwich CT (tombstone photo); married March 1799 Charlotte Peck (3.10.5.11.5).
