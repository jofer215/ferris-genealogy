MARGARET FERRIS

ID: 1.4.1.1 | Source: John
(1.4.1.1) MARGARET FERRIS (Peggy) born August 14, 1754; married October 14, 1778 Colonel Jotham Loring [called notorious Col. Loring] of Massachuetts; commander of a Massachusetts Regiment during the Revolution; if this is the same Jotham Loring (Lieutenant Colonel service January 1777 – August 1779) that married 1785 Luna {maiden name unk}, then Margaret apparently died prior to 1785

No children recorded
