THANKFUL LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.11.5 | Source: Mary
(4.6.11.5) THANKFUL LOCKWOOD (#295) born 1747 and died January 26, 1826. {Conflict here from the Churchyard/Orr Family Museum Homepage on the Web - it is stated that this Thankful should not be confused with the same name who was the daughter of Ephriam & Thankful (Grummon) Lockwood - she is almost certainly the person who married Samuel St. John, in 1781.}{I will list both below, maintaining the same numbering sequence, for now.} She married May 25, 1763 James Benedict [son of Caleb & Mehitable (Hoyt)] born December 25, 1743 and died June 17, 1838. Both joined the Congregational Church, December 27, 1772. Born to them were:
