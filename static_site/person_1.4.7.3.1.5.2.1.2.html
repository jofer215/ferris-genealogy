HOWLAND GALLATIN PELL

ID: 1.4.7.3.1.5.2.1.2 | Source: John
(1.4.7.3.1.5.2.1.2) HOWLAND GALLATIN PELL born August 17, 1889 East Hampton NY and died 1937; graduated Harvard 1911. After the declaration of war against Germany in 1917, he was presented by his father with a patrol boat for the U.S. Naval Reserves, of which he became a member, with headquarters at Newport

No children recorded
