DELLE LEROY FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2.1.2) DELLE LEROY FERRIS born July 23, 1931 Chamberlain SD; married February 9, 1952 at Wilcox AZ, Sarah Blanche Wootan born April 27, 1935 Willcox. Delle received a B.S. degree in Business Administration from Arizona State U., 1954, and a M.A., in Business Administration from George Washington U., Washington, D.C. He served in the USAF for 21 years retiring as a Lieutenant Colonel, 1975. Delle is currently (1999) a Vice President with Merrill Lynch and he and Blanche reside in Paradise Valley AZ.
