NELLIE MYERHOLTS

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.10.5.5.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.10.5.5.1) NELLIE MYERHOLTS born April 24, 1894 Ottawa Co OH and died June 17, 1947 Toledo OH; buried in the Morenci Cemetery, Morenci MI; married 1915 Louis John Martin [son of Ludvig John and Catharine (Mentall) Marten] who died 1950 Toledo OH. Nellie was active in the Red Cross in WWI, working in canteens and preparing medical supplies. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • LOUIS GEORGE MARTIN, M.D., 5.1.3.3.1.10.5.5.1.1
  • LOIS LORRAINE MARTIN 5.1.3.3.1.10.5.5.1.2
