PHOEBE FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.7.7 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.7.7) PHOEBE FERRIS born August 16, 1826 and died December 19, 1896; never married; survived all her brothers and sisters; bequeathed the old family homestead in Linwood to the village for a Library with a fund for its support as a free library; was a much-traveled woman; buried Ferris Farm Cemetery

No children recorded
