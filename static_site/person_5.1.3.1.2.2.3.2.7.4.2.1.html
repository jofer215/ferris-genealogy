REBECCA JO PAGEL

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.2.3.2.7.4.2.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.2.3.2.7.4.2.1) REBECCA JO PAGEL born September 5, 1963 Bismarck ND; received her Art Degree from the College of St. Benedict, St. Joseph MN 1985; an artist and an excellent pianist; worked for publishing companies in the past, but since the birth of Emma, she works from the home. 1998 - Becky is working a telecommuting job with MotorBooks. She married October 3, 1987 at Minneapolis MN, Jeffrey Michael Allen born March 3, 1960, MN – divorced 2001. Jeff is a RN and worked in the psychiatric ward of a hospital in Minneapolis, but the hospital closed, so he now works for an insurance company. Reside at Stillwater MN. Born to them was:
