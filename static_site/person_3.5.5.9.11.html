MOSES LYON

ID: 3.5.5.9.11 | Source: Joseph
(3.5.5.9.11) MOSES LYON (twin) born April 2/3, 1790 Greenwich CT and died February 27, 1874; buried King Ferry Cemetery (West Genoa Cemetery), Genoa NY. He came with his parents to Genoa Township, Cayuga County in 1799 and made his permanent home there, becoming a prosperous farmer. Moses, his brother Caleb and their father, Caleb, were all decons in Congregational or Presbyterian Churches. He married 1st Hester Peet born January 1793 and died April 8, 1824; 2d October 3, 1826 at Groton NY, Laura Briggs (Riggs) born Ballston Spa NY [dau of Miles & Suse] who died November 6, 1844; buried King Ferry Cemetery, Genoa; and 3d February 28, 1846 Clarissa (Bradley) Eaton [dau of Major Bradley] – third marriage for both of them; buried with Moses.
