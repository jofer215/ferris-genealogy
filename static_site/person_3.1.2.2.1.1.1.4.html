NORTON C. FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.2.1.1.1.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.1.1.1.4) NORTON C. FERRIS (Norton H. Ferris) born 1839/40 Dry Prairie, Calhoun, MI and died January 6, 1903 Kankakee IL; buried Sherwood Cemetery, Athens MI; moved to Kankakee 1871; married October 8, 1865 in MI, Frances E.
