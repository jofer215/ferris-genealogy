CHARLES A LANE

ID: 4.2.5.5.7.1.3.8 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.5.7.1.3.8) CHARLES A LANE (Photo) born March 11, 1843 Niles Township, Berrian Co MI and died December 11, 1878; Civil War Vet – Private with Company B, 64th Illinois Infantry Regiment – resided in Princeton IL, mustered in November 1, 1861 and mustered out October 31, 1864; another active Illinois Regiment – see ‘Net for info; married Susannah E {maiden name unk)

No children recorded
