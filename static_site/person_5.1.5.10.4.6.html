CHARLES F HENDRIE

ID: 5.1.5.10.4.6 | Source: James
(5.1.5.10.4.6) CHARLES F HENDRIE born May 8, 1828 Stamford and died June 14, 1904; married November 4, 1850 Caroline Elizabeth Ferris (5.1.3.8.6.2). He was Trustee of The First Congregational Church, Old Greenwich, member of Building Committee for the stone church, and donor; and born to them were:
