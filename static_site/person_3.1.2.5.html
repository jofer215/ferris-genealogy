DANIEL FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.5) DANIEL FERRIS born August 27, 1729 Greenwich CT {probably the Daniel, son of Peter, who was baptized at the Second Congregational Church in Greenwich, December 24, 1732}; was first taxed in Charlotte Precinct from February 1763 through 1775 and was then taxed in Beekman from 1773 to 1775; a path master in Beekman 1775 and from 1779 through 1784; granted Land Bounty Rights for service in the 5th Beekman Regiment of Militia; and born to him and his unk wife were {the following placement is in question}:

Children (3)

  • PETER FERRIS 3.1.2.5.1
  • JOHN FERRIS 3.1.2.5.2
  • DANIEL FERRIS, Jr. was probably the man in Charlotte who in 1774 was responsible for 3.1.2.5.3
