WHITMAN PECK, A.M.,

ID: 3.10.7.6.2.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.7.6.2.4) WHITMAN PECK, A.M., (Rev) born May 16, 1815 Greenwich CT; married November 6, 1844 Ruth Maria Keeler born December 30, 1816 Greenwich; he directed academic department Delaware College, 1858-60; 1872 authored Manual of the Christian Faith, designed to facilitate the instruction of the young in families, Sabbath Schools, etc.

No children recorded
