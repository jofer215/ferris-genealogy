WILLIAM HAIGHT LEGGETT “Willie”

ID: 1.4.7.7.10.3 | Source: John
(1.4.7.7.10.3) WILLIAM HAIGHT LEGGETT “Willie” (Photo) born November 7, 1840 Peekskill, Dutchess, NY and died February 1921 Birmingham, Oakland, MI; buried Sashabaw Cemetery in Sashabaw Plains, Independence Township, Oakland Co MI; married November 7, 1860 in Sashabaw Plains, Anna Beardslee (Beardsley) [dau of John Wheeler & Elizabeth Ann (Carpenter)] born May 20, 1840 in Sashabaw Plains and died March 25, 1910 Wayne Co MI; buried Sashabaw Cemetery.
