BENJAMIN HAIT

ID: 2.5.2.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.2) BENJAMIN HAIT (Hoyt) (#2752) (NZJV-WK) born April 24, 1700 Stamford and died young (2.5.2.3) DAVID HAIT (Hoyt) (#2753) (NZLJ-G5) born June 23, 1702 Stamford and died there 176162; landowner in Stamford; married 1st June 22, 1727 Hannah Hait (#2727) born June 30, 1705; and 2d 1743-49 the widow, Abigail Andros (Blachley). David and Hannah bought, April 29, 1735, the rights of all the other children to land at Hecquit’s in Stamford that was Joseph Hait’s (#2641). Born to David and Hannah were:
