CECILIA DEMILLE

ID: 2.6.5.2.1.1.2.1.2.1.1 | Source: Peter
(2.6.5.2.1.1.2.1.2.1.1) CECILIA DEMILLE (1908-1984) (Photo) was an excellent equestrian and in later years was also a horse breeder. (2.6.5.2.1.1.2.1.2.2) WILLIAM CHURCHILL DEMILLE (Photos) born July 25, 1878 Washington, D.C. and died March 8, 1955 Playa del Ray CA; attended Columbia U.; director, writer, producer, etc., see Internet; married 1st 1903, Anna Angela George (1877-1947)– divorced 1927 [dau of economist Henry George, author of Progress and Poverty; married 2d 1928 Clara Beranger (1886-1956); fathered Richard de Mille out of wedlock with actress Lorna Moon – Richard adopted by Cecil B.
