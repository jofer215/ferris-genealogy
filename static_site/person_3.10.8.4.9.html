CHLOE PECK

ID: 3.10.8.4.9 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.8.4.9) CHLOE PECK born June 11, 1799 Pompey, Onondaga, NY and died 1882 Van Buren, Onondaga, NY; buried there; married June 30, 1816 in Van Buren, Eleazer Waterman [son of Calvin & Nancy (Remington) Waterman] born 1792 Pittsfield, Berkshire, MA and died October 7, 1856 Delta, Eaton, MI; buried there; served in NY Militia 1812-14; served as a Fifer in Colonel Woods’ Regiment 1814; he received 160 acres of land for his military service during the War of 1812; and born to them were:

Children (6)

  • ALMOND PECK WATERMAN 3.10.8.4.9.1
  • CAROLINE BUTLER WATERMAN 3.10.8.4.9.2
  • HORACE PEABODY WATERMAN 3.10.8.4.9.3
  • JAMES D WATERMAN 3.10.8.4.9.4
  • ASA DUDLEY WATERMAN 3.10.8.4.9.5
  • MARY JANE WATERMAN 3.10.8.4.9.6
