ELIZA FERRIS COCK

ID: 1.4.7.8.1 | Source: John
(1.4.7.8.1) ELIZA FERRIS COCK born February 2, 1811, NYC and died July 25, 1902 Denver CO; married June 8, 1831 James Bowne Parsons, of Flushing NY [son of Samuel & Mary (Bowne) Parsons] born May 17, 1809 and died September 2, 1894 Litchfield CT. They were Friends and he a wholesale dry goods merchant in NYC. Born to them were:

Children (8)

  • JOHN BOWNE PARSONS 1.4.7.8.1.2
