JACOB MILLER BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.6.1.10 | Source: James
(5.2.2.6.1.10) JACOB MILLER BUXTON born April 11, 1820 Fonda NY (or Casterland NY or Deerfield NY) and died May 12, 1890 Casterland NY; married 1st 1844 Caroline L. Cheney [dau of David & Hulda (Blake) Cheney] born August 19, 1824 and died May 29, 1848 Denmark, Lewis, NY; buried Hillside Cemetery; married 2d January 25, 1854 in Denmark NY, Caroline H. Barrett [dau James & Eddice (Bassett) Barrett] born June 21, 1837 Evans Mills, Jefferson, NY and died April 15, 1910 Casterland NY. Born to Jacon and the first Caroline was:
