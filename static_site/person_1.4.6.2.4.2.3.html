MARY ALICE FERRIS

ID: 1.4.6.2.4.2.3 | Source: John
(1.4.6.2.4.2.3) MARY ALICE FERRIS (May) born c1868; married William J. Doherty. The Doherty family were tradesman of carriages, establishing a factory 1853 on the corner of Zulette Avenue and Pelham Road, now Ericson Place.
