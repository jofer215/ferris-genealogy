HANNAH LENT

ID: 1.2.2.2.3.11.5 | Source: John
(1.2.2.2.3.11.5) HANNAH LENT (Hannah M Lent) (c1828-) married March 27, 1847 in Orange Co NY, George Perry Goodrich of Highland Mills NY, died September 24, 1864 Berryville VA; 1862 George enlisted as a Private in Company K, 162d Regiment (17th Cav) at age 37y, residence was Susquehanna Co PA, shoemaker; and born to them were:
