EDGAR FERRIS

ID: 1.4.1.3.7.1 | Source: John
(1.4.1.3.7.1) EDGAR FERRIS [placement in question] born c1822 CT/NY and died May 24, 1890 Greenwich CT; buried in Stamford CT; married c1844 at North Castle, Elizabeth Tucker [dau of William & Mary/Maria (Conklin)] born September 1824 Armonk NY and died November 27, 1896 Stamford CT. In the 1850 census of North Castle, they were living next to Elizabeth’s parents. Edgar was a shoemaker. In the 1860 census, they lived four houses from Edgar’s parents. They are found next in the 1870 census in Stamford CT and Edgar is listed as a farm laborer. Edgar is found living with his daughter Eliza J., in the 1880 census of Stamford, prior to the death of his wife. Born to them were:

Children (7)

  • WILLIAM L. FERRIS listed as 5 years of age in the 1850 census; killed 1.4.1.3.7.1.1
  • EDGAR RAYMOND FERRIS 1.4.1.3.7.1.2

  • ALSMINIA FERRIS 1.4.1.3.7.1.4
  • MARY E. FERRIS listed as 5y of age in the 1860 census 1.4.1.3.7.1.5
  • ELIZA JANE FERRIS 1.4.1.3.7.1.6
