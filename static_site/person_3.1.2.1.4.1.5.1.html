MARY JANE TURNER

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.5.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.5.1) MARY JANE TURNER born July 26, 1822 Westchester or Orange Co NY and died August 7, 1901; remembered as an old lady c1900, who carried a small clay pipe which she smoked after meals; married 1st April 30, 1840 in Springbook PA, Reese Scull born September 24, 1814 Welsh Woods, Luzerne Co PA and died May 23, 1865 Spring Brook, Lackawanna, PA by falling off a bridge; married 2d a Mr. Murphy – divorced; and born to Mary and Reese were:
