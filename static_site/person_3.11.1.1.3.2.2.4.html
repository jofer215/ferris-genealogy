WILLIAM NEWMAN

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.4) WILLIAM NEWMAN born September 3, 1852 St. Lawrence, Jefferson, NY (St. Lawrence Co NY) and died October 30, 1931 Maple City MI; buried there East Kasson Cemetery; farmer, mercantile business; married 1st April 13, 1873 in Elk Rapids MI, (Hattie) Harriet M Bull/Buhl [dau of Isaac & Sarah (Burrows) Bull] born February 1851 Merton, Waukesha, WI and died August 1, 1903 Cedar Run MI; buried with William; married 2d July 4, 1905 in Traverse City, Sarah J (Cogshell) Miller; married 3d December 19, 1912 in Grand Traverse Co MI, Ella Totten; and 4th Annette Cooper Leoofser; and born to William and Hattie were:

Children (5)

  • FRANK IRA NEWMAN 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.4.1
  • CHARLES MYSON NEWMAN orn May 24, 1878 Kasson, Leelanau, MI and died 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.4.2
  • LODUSKY NEWMAN 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.4.3
  • GRACE NEWMAN 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.4.4
  • ARCHIE W NEWMAN 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.4.5
