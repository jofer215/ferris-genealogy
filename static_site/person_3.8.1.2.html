PETER FERRIS

ID: 3.8.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.8.1.2) PETER FERRIS born c1742 Greenwich and died 1795 Salem, Westchester, NY; married 1st October 1, 1761 at the 1st Congregational Church of Stamford, Martha Weed [dau of Benjamin and Mary (Smith) Weed] born June 15, 1751 (is this correct – she would have been 10y when married?) and died c1768; and 2d May 11, 1769 at the Church of Christ, Salem NY, Hannah Benedict [dau of Peter and Agnes (Tyler)(Heaton) Benedict] born February 20, 1748. Peter was Deacon, Church of Christ, Salem NY; and born to him and Martha were:
