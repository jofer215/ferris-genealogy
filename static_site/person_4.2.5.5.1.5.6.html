LAVINA A SWARTOUT

ID: 4.2.5.5.1.5.6 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.5.1.5.6) LAVINA A SWARTOUT (Lovina) born September 18, 1849/50 Woodstock, Lenawee, MI and died April 1, 1902; 1900 resided Hillsdale Co MI; married February 5, 1868 in Branch Co MI, Alonzo Lewis [son of Thomas Jefferson & Dolly (Derby) Lewis] born April 10, 1844 Napoleon, Jackson, MI and died September 9, 1926 Michigan City, LaPorte, IN; farmer 1900; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • EMMIT LEWIS 4.2.5.5.1.5.6.1
  • DOLLIE LEWIS resided c1951 Ann Arbor MI; married 1st John Vorhees; 2d E. T. 4.2.5.5.1.5.6.2
  • HAZEL BELL LEWIS 4.2.5.5.1.5.6.3
  • CLARENCE LEWIS 4.2.5.5.1.5.6.4
