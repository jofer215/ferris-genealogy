JOSIAH KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.1.4 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.1.4) JOSIAH KNAPP born January 4, 1756 Stamford and died May 8, 1838 South Westerlo, Albany Co NY. [Fred Knapp has doubts that Josiah was a son of John and Hannah (Blackman) Knapp, but we will retain him here for now.]; married 1 st c1777 in Stamford, Miss Simpkins and 2d, at Westerlo after 1796, the widow Anna (Leonard) Gifford died between 1836-38. Born to Josiah and his Miss Simpkins were:

Children (7)

  • JOSEPH KNAPP 2.6.2.1.4.2
