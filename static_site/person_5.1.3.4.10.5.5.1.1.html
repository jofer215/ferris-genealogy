GERALD E FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.5.5.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.5.5.1.1) GERALD E FERRIS born January 21, 1904 Upland, Dickinson, KS and died August 1969 Moline, Rock Island, IL; married 1929 in Topeka KS, Ruth M Gugler; in 1926-27, a Gerald E Ferris was involved in the State of Kansas Cooperation Extension and then later the Ohio Cooperative Extension Service – probably same guy

No children recorded
