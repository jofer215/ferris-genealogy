MORDECAI KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.1.4.6.1.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.1.4.6.1.3) MORDECAI KNAPP born 1841 probably Maryland, Otsego, NY and died there May 28, 1932; a Civil War Veteran serving with the New York Cavalry - he was kicked in the head by a stallion and had to have silver plates in his head in May 1863; married August 10, 1868 in Maryland NY, Rosamond Griswold born 1842 NY and died February 5, 1912 Maryland NY; buried there Schenevius Cemetery; and born to them were:
