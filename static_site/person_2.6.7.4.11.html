TAMMA KNAPP

ID: 2.6.7.4.11 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.4.11) TAMMA KNAPP born September 15, 1783 Lake Champlain VT and died 1860 Browning Township, Schuyler, IL; married c1803 in St. Albans, Franklin VT, Charles McEvers [son of James & Maria Louisa (Howard) McEvers] born September 15, 1783 Hillsdale, Columbia, NY and died 1836 Montezuma, Pike, IL.
