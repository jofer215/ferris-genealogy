ALMIRA J DUNNING

ID: 4.2.5.4.5.2.3 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.4.5.2.3) ALMIRA J DUNNING born c1817 New Milfod CT and moved with her family from Bridgeport CT to Rootstown OH 1820; married c1830 in OH (pretty young), Elbridge G Moulton [son of Harrison G] born c1811 MA, moving with his family to Portage Co OH 1817; farmer; and born to them were:

Children (2)

  • ALMIRA JEANETTE MOULTON 4.2.5.4.5.2.3.1
  • JOHN QUINCY MOULTON 4.2.5.4.5.2.3.3
