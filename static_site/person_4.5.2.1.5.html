NATHANIEL REYNOLDS

ID: 4.5.2.1.5 | Source: Mary
(4.5.2.1.5) NATHANIEL REYNOLDS III born September 7, 1745 Greenwich and died June 6, 1822 [from the Reynolds Family Records] (will dated June 12, 1820 and probated July 6, 1822); married 1st January 23, 1772 Rebecca Mead born February 18, 1743/44/45 and died June 5, 1822 (if this date is correct, Nathaniel must have divorced her since he remarried); and 2d April 2, 1778 Deborah Husted [dau of Benjamin & Sarah (Newman)] born August 6, 1745 and died April 18, 1839 Greenwich; Rev. War Veteran between 1776-777 as a Sergeant and Lieutenant; and born to them were (not in birth sequence):
