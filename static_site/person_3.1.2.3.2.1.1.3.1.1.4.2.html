CLARENCE WILLIAM COMER

ID: 3.1.2.3.2.1.1.3.1.1.4.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.3.2.1.1.3.1.1.4.2) CLARENCE WILLIAM COMER born November 28, 1924 Fort Bragg, Mendocino, CA; married February 7, 1947 in Reno NV, Evalee Rombie Seagraves [dau of Floyd & Arizona (Presley) Seagraves]; Clarence was a WWII Veteran enlisting July 22, 1943 at Sacramento CA - served in the European African Middle Eastern Theater August 24, 1944 through September 1, 1944; Asiatic Pacific Theatre July 19, 1945 through September 6, 1945; discharged February 19, 1946 Camp Beale CA - attained the rank of Staff Sergeant; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • GARY ALAN COMER 3.1.2.3.2.1.1.3.1.1.4.2.1
  • STEVEN LARRY COMER 3.1.2.3.2.1.1.3.1.1.4.2.2
  • BRADRICK DARRYL COMER 3.1.2.3.2.1.1.3.1.1.4.2.3
