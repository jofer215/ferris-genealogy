GERALDINE RUTH DAVENPORT

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.4.4.6.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.4.4.6.2) GERALDINE RUTH DAVENPORT born November 24, 1915 Riverdale and died October 8, 1998 Gratiot Community Hospital; buried Wright Cemetery in Alma MI. She lived in Alama all her life and married Wayne G. Burt August 22, 1937. He preceded her in death August 5, 1992. She worked for many years with Montgomery Ward in Alma as a head cashier and was also responsible for sales and bookkeeping. She worked for a short time as head of Secretary of State office in Alma. She also did bookkeeping for her stepbrother, Ora Burlingame, at the Ford Lincoln-Mercury dealership and for her father John at the Hudson Terra Plan dealership. She was a member of the St. Louis Church of the Nazarene. Born to them were:
