CYRUS YALE FERRIS, JR.,

ID: 1.4.7.5.8.1.2.4 | Source: John
(1.4.7.5.8.1.2.4) CYRUS YALE FERRIS, JR., born June 10, 1921 Newton MA and died December 14, 1996; S.B., M.I.T. 1944; President, N.E. Label Co., Montpelier VT; Rock of Ages, Consulting Engineer; married 1st Barbara Agapeyeff born November 8, 1926 New York City - they divorced 1972, and married 2d August 8, 1974 Marguerite (Hayes) Ward born July 31, 1927 Monpelier VT; B.A. from Goddard College, 1978.
