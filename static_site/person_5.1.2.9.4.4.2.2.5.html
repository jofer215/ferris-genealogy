SARAH EDITH TOMPKINS

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.2.2.5 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.2.2.5) SARAH EDITH TOMPKINS (1881-) married Thaddus G. Tompkins. Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, March 3, 1955: Thaddeus G. Tompkins in his 82nd year, passed away last Sunday morning, Feb. 27, 1955, after a short illness. Mr. Tompkins was a farmer and retired about thirty years ago from the dairy business. Born Putnam Valley on Aug. 28, 1872, he was the son of John and Anna M. Barger Tompkins. Over twenty members of the Putnam Valley Subordinats Grange attended special services in the funeral home on Monday evening, he having been a long-standing member of the grange. Mr. Tompkins lived at the entrance to Abele Park. He leaves his wife, Edith, whose maiden name was also Tompkins; and several nieces. Interment was at Hillside Cemetery.

No children recorded
