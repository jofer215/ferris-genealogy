BENJAMIN HOYT, JR.

ID: 2.5.2.5.5.5 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.5.5.5) BENJAMIN HOYT, JR. (#3552) born October 27, 1779 and died February 1844 South Salem NY where he lived; was Benjamin, Jr., until 1840; married April 5, 1802 Betsey Northrup (Northrop) (1782-1855); buried Presbyterian Church, South Salem; and born to them were: (2.5.2.5.5.5.1) HENRY HOYT (#4598) born January 17, 1806; married November 13, 1839 Polly Platt; Henry was a farmer and was living in South Salem NY 1871. Born to them were:
