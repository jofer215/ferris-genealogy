SAMUEL PECK

ID: 3.10.1.3.2.1.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.3.2.1.6) SAMUEL PECK born December 20, 1806 Greenwich CT and was living there 1861; married January 20, 1833 widow Mary Peck (Ferris) Jackson (5.1.3.3.1.1.1). (3.10.1.3.2.1.7) EMELINE PECK born November 16, 1808 Greenwich CT and died there February 13, 1831 age 22y 3m; buried there at Old Sound Beach Cemetery (Tomac Ave)
