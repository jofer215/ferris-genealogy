MARY QUINTARD

ID: 2.6.3.6 | Source: Peter
(2.6.3.6) MARY QUINTARD born (October 21, 1722) May 21, 1722 Stamford and died there after 1772; married May 18, 1742 Nathaniel Hubbard (1712-) (1702-1772); 4th Doctor recorded in Stamford; a physician who originally came from Braentree MA, but practiced medicine in Stamford; a man of unusual skill and energy; and born to them were:

Children (7)

  • NATHANIEL HUBBARD 2.6.3.6.2

  • WILLIAM HUBBARD 2.6.3.6.4
  • MARY HUBBARD 2.6.3.6.5
  • MARGARET HUBBARD 2.6.3.6.6
