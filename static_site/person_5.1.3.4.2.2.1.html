WILLIAM EDGAR FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.2.2.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.2.2.1) WILLIAM EDGAR FERRIS (Photograph) born November 22, 1836 Hilltop Farm, Greenwich CT and died December 31, 1894 Greenwich; a farmer and attended Christ Church (Episcopal), Greenwich; married probably January 5, 1869, Mercy ‘Jane’ Hunter born February 22, 1846 Chappaqua NY and died November 10, 1915 Greenwich. According to family lore, Jane was apprenticed to a dressmaker in New York City and left her family a number of quilts in as many patterns. She was raised in a Quaker family. Both are buried in the Ferris Plot, at the cemetery at First Congregational Church, Old Greenwich. Born to them were:
