JUDE ANN HILL

ID: 3.6.3.2.2.5.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.2.5.2) JUDE ANN HILL born March 28, 1825 and died August 11, 1915; married 1st October 3, 1844 Elias Trauger born February 2, 1823 and died September 16, 1862 Old Flatbrookville NJ when his team accidently ran him down; interred in Old Walpack Cemetery, Walpack, Sussex Co NJ. Elias was a farmer and a Constable in Walpack Township. Jude married 2d March 16, 1885 Ingraham Deats. Born to Jude and Elias were:

Children (6)

  • MANUEL EDMUND TRAUGER 3.6.3.2.2.5.2.1
  • ANN CELIA TRAUGER 3.6.3.2.2.5.2.2
  • FRANCES A. TRAUGER 3.6.3.2.2.5.2.3
  • MARY E. TRAUGER 3.6.3.2.2.5.2.4
  • JOHN W. TRAUGER 3.6.3.2.2.5.2.5
  • HIRAM LINDEN TRAUGER 3.6.3.2.2.5.2.7
