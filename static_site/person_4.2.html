ROBERT LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.2 | Source: Mary
(4.2) ROBERT LOCKWOOD (#12) (K7FP-TL) born c1665 Greenwich CT and died between May 7, 1731, the date of his will and January 23, 1732, the date the will was probated; married Mary {maiden name unk}. He called himself husbandman of Greenwich August 13, 1694, when he was appointed to take the town list in Greenwich; 1694-95, he was taxed on 61 pounds. On March 1, 1698, the town sold him Hosick meadows. Born to Robert and his wife were: (4.2.1) PHEBE LOCKWOOD (#45) born March 13, 1693; married a Mr. Austin
