GEORGE BANONY GILBERT

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.2.3.2.7.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.2.3.2.7.4) GEORGE BANONY GILBERT born December 7, 1901 Lisbon ND and died there January 7, 1972; buried Oakwood Cemetery; married July 1, 1929 at Moorhead MN, Pauline Wunder born March 30, 1907 Russia. George owned an International Harvester store, which he sold in 1958; Pauline was his bookkeeper. George later sold Oldsmobiles and when TV finally came to ND c1952, he sold the first TV set in Lisbon. They owned quite a lot of farmland around Lisbon, which they had farmed for them. Pauline still owns the land and has it farmed for her. In his retirement years, George went to auctions, tried coin and stamp collecting. George was active in Civic and Commerce and served on the park board; a member of the Trinity Lutheran Church. He bought into the Johnson Seed Company in Lisbon and worked there, almost on a full-time basis, until his sudden heart attack and death. Pauline celebrated her 91st birthday -1998 - she lives in her home and takes care of her yard and flowers and loves to play bingo and cards and go to the casinos in the area. Born to them were:
