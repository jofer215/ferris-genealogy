CLARISSA BURCH

ID: 3.10.8.3.6.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.8.3.6.3) CLARISSA BURCH born October 23, 1814 Fort Ann, Washington, NY and died (September 28, 1896 Fairchild, Eau Claire, WI) March 23, 1878 Springdale, Dane, WI; buried there; married January 12, 1837 at Erie PA (Greenfield, Erie, PA) Thomas Brunker Miles [son of Samuel & Anna (Townley) Miles] born September 11, 1805 Greenfield PA and died March 23, 1878 Springdale WI; buried there.
