LEROY W. CHRISTIAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.9.8.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.9.8.1) LEROY W. CHRISTIAN (Judge) born July 22, 1888 Newburgh NY and died January 16, 1942 in Butterfield Hospital; buried Cold Spring Cemetery; married 1st Jennie Myers; and 2d 1913 Florence Buchanan of Stamford CT. Although stern in the administration of his public office Judge Christian's pleasing personality and willingness to aid his community made him well liked and popular throughout the town. While Judge Christian devoted much time to his home and office he found time to take an interest in hunting and bowling. He was well known as a huntsman and for many years was regarded as one of the best bowlers in this section. Republican himself, he worked diligently for his party at every election. He was a member of the Methodist church, Philipstown Lodge, F. and A.M. and a member of the town board of Philipstown. He was a toolmaker by trade and employed at the Cold Spring Manufacturing Co. until failing health made it necessary for him to retire.
