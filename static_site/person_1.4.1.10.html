DAVID FERRIS

ID: 1.4.1.10 | Source: John
(1.4.1.10) DAVID FERRIS born August 8, (or December 16?) 1771 Throg’s Neck, Westchester, NY and died 1842; purported to have married three times; one of his wives was Ann Ferris (1.4.6.4). Born to them were:

Children (6)

  • CORNELL FERRIS 1.4.1.10.1
  • EDWARD 1.4.1.10.2
  • GEORGE FERRIS 1.4.1.10.3
  • ANN FERRIS 1.4.1.10.4
  • JOHN THOMAS FERRIS 1.4.1.10.6
  • CLINTON FERRIS died 1881 1.4.1.10.7
