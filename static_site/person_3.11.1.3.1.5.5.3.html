JOHN DICKINSON MILBURN

ID: 3.11.1.3.1.5.5.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.3.1.5.5.3) JOHN DICKINSON MILBURN born May 15, 1842 Easton’s Corners, Ontario and died August 7, 1915 Niles, Berrien, MI; buried there; 1867-70 co-owner of Milburn & Dodge Hardware, Niles MI; 1870 purchased stock in Milburn Wagon Co, went to Memphis; established Milburn, Walker & Co; before 1883 established Carver Gin & Machinery Co in Memphis, later changed name to Milburn Gin & Machinery Co making cotton gins and related ginning machinery; was President until c1893 when the company was liquidated; married May 25, 1869 in Niles MI, Katie Mae Jones [dau of John & Elizabeth (Pugh) Jones] born April 2, 1847 Waterville, Oneida, NY and died October 10, 1937 Altadena, Los Angeles, CA; buried Niles MI; and born to them were: (3.11.1.3.1.5.5.3.1) MARTHA MAUD LOUISE MILBURN born February 29, 1872 Memphis TN and died January 13, 1922 Niles, Berrien, MI; buried there; Nurse
