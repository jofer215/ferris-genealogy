RUTH FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.10.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.10.2) RUTH FERRIS born 1825 and died June 9, 1900; married Charles E. Curtice; and born to them was: (5.1.3.1.2.10.2.1) LILLIAN ELIZABETH CURTICE married, December 16, 1875 in Whitewater WI, Frederick Henry Blood [son of Henry Laveque & Theresa Catherine (Southmayd) Blood] born August 6, 1848, Green Bay WI and died February 11, 1905, Appleton WI; buried there in Riverside Cemetery. Lillian buried Rosedale Cemetery, Calif. Born to them were:

No children recorded
