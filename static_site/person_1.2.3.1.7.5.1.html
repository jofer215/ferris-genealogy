JOHN TORBOSS UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.1.7.5.1 | Source: John
(1.2.3.1.7.5.1) JOHN TORBOSS UNDERHILL (1842-1924) (President of Underhill Society 1905-24) May – September 1862 served as 2d Lieutenant in Company D, 37th Regiment NY National Guard; November 1862 appointed Captain, 37th Regiment, 3d Brigade NY National Guard; served June – July 1863 as Captain in Company I, 37th Regiment NY National Guard; Lieutnant Colonel, Civil War Vet; married 1st 1866 (Anna Maria Thorne) (1843-1876) Euphemia M. Thorne; married 2d 1880 Lydia Emma Thompson (1853-1920); gave many family documents/Bible to the Underhill Society; and born to him and Anna were:

Children (4)

  • GRACE THORNE UNDERHILL 1.2.3.1.7.5.1.1
  • EFFIE TORBOSS UNDERHILL 1.2.3.1.7.5.1.2
  • JAMES THORNE UNDERHILL 1.2.3.1.7.5.1.3
  • FLORENCE THOMPSON UNDERHILL 1.2.3.1.7.5.1.4
