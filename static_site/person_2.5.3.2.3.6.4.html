CHARLES FOX

ID: 2.5.3.2.3.6.4 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.2.3.6.4) CHARLES FOX born 1817 Stamford and died September 15, 1879 Merrick, Nassau, NY; married Mary Jane Hoyt [dau of Samuel & Sally (Husted) Hoyt] born August 9, 1819 Stamford and died there December 28, 1851; buried there Woodland Cemetery;

No children recorded
