FRANKLIN R. CHADEAYNE

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.4.3.3 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.4.3.3) FRANKLIN R. CHADEAYNE (Photos) born November 7, 1867 Inningwood Road, Ossining NY and died there March 11, 1947; buried Fair View Cemetery, Chappaqua NY; married October 18, 1899 at Ossining, Manuella (Ella) Rebecca Henning [dau of Joseph Luther & Rebecca (Rudman) Henning] born April 21, 1872 and died March 9, 1967; buried at Fair View Cemetery. As a young man, Frank attended a one-room school near the Maplehurst farm and upon graduation attended Mt. Pleasant Military Academy in Ossining, NY. Upon graduation he took a clerical position with Van Tassell Lumber Co. in New York City but shortly returned home to help work on the farm with his brother, Wilbur. A farmers life is a busy one, rising at 4AM to milk the cows, feed the livestock, and the off with a team to deliver milk to a long list of customers in Ossining, NY. There was hay to be raised for the horses and cows, corn for the chickens and a large garden had to be maintained to provide food for the table together with apple, pear and peach orchards to care for.
