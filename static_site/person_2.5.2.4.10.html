SAMUEL BATES HAIT

ID: 2.5.2.4.10 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.10) SAMUEL BATES HAIT (Hoyt) (#3023) was baptized June 14, 1752; died young. (2.5.2.4.11) BATES HAIT (Hoyt) (#3023) born July 7, 1754 and died September 4, 1776; it is said he went to New York, August 14, took the small pox, came home, September 1 and died on the 4th. He was in the Revolutionary Army in New York City with his brothers, in Captain Webb’s Company.

No children recorded
