DAVID BROWN HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.3.2.9.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.3.2.9.2) DAVID BROWN HOYT (#4503) born August 16, 1807 Stamford; resided New Canaan and in 1871 in Laclede, Linn, MO; married 1st November 18, 1832 in New Canaan, Hannah Jennings [dau of Hezekiah & Hannah (Hoyt) Jennings] born c1811 New Canaan CT and died there before 1839; 2d Maria Foot. Born to David and Hannah was:

Children (3)

  • EMILY HOYT 2.5.2.3.2.9.2.1
  • FRANCIS HOYT died in camp hospital at Springfield IL during Civil War age 21y 2.5.2.3.2.9.2.2
  • CHARLES HOYT was living in Laclede MO 1871 2.5.2.3.2.9.2.3
