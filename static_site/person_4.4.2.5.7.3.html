ELON DUNBAR LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.2.5.7.3 | Source: Mary
(4.4.2.5.7.3) ELON DUNBAR LOCKWOOD (#1494) born November 26, 1838 Ashtabula OH. In 1864, he was an active member of the Committee on Labor, Income and Revenue of the Great Central Fair, for the benefit of the Sanitary Commission during the Rebellion (Civil War). [Jim - I note he did not enlist to fight in the War.] He was one of the Committee of Fifty appointed at a mass meeting held at Independence Hall, Philadelphia March 16, 1874 to raise money to erect the Centennial Buildings; plus numerous other civic involvements. (4.4.2.5.8) OLIVER FORD LOCKWOOD (#767) born April 26, 1807 Greenwich and died July 6, 1879; buried Brunswick, Medina County OH; married September 17, 1829 Hannah Maria Finney [dau of Solomon & Mary (Betts) Finney] born January 12, 1808.
