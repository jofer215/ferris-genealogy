AUSTIN WILLIAM FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.2.4.1.2.1.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.4.1.2.1.1) AUSTIN WILLIAM FERRIS born September 2, 1874 Johnstown Township, Barry Co MI and died March 11, 1952 Pennock Hospital, Hastings MI after a lingering illness of a blood clot in the heart; a stonemason in the early part of his adult life, learning the trade from his father and his grandfather Aaron; built many barn and house walls and bridges in the vicinity. Before he married, he bought the William P. Bristol farm of 160 acres, known as the Stagecoach Inn or Bristol Tavern near Bristol Corners, in Johnstown Township; and then devoted most of his life to farming. He married May 12, 1909 at the Presbyterian Church, Hastings MI Mary Amelia Warner [dau of Daniel Christian and Angeline Maria (Morgan) Warner] born November 25, 1887 Baltimore Township, Barry Co MI and died November 24, 1959 Leila Hospital, Calhoun Co MI; both buried in the family plot, Banfield Cemetery, Johnstown Township, Barry Co MI; resided at Route 1, Dowling MI; and born to them were:
