CHARLES A. BRUCE

ID: 4.6.2.4.4.6.1.4 | Source: Mary
(4.6.2.4.4.6.1.4) CHARLES A. BRUCE born February 18, 1846 Stamford and died there February 13, 1929; buried there High Ridge Cemetery; married 1st March 18, 1875 in Stamford, Hannah S. Lockwood [dau of Nathaniel & Hannah (Everett) Lockwood] born July 14, 1843 Stamford and died there March 30, 1890; buried High Ridge Cemetery; married 2d November 27, 1890 in Stamford, as her 2d husband, Lydia Ann (Weed) Buxton [dau of William Lewis & Cynthia Eliza (Boughton) Weed] born June 24, 1850 Stamford and died there December 27, 1897; buried High Ridge Cemetery

No children recorded
