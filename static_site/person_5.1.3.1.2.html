ISRAEL FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2) ISRAEL FERRIS born October (17) 25, 1751 Horseneck, Greenwich CT; {‘on the Connecticut shore of Long Island Sound, near the New York State line’} and died January 2, 1844 Whitewater WI, at the home of his son, Thompson; buried Second Milo Baptist Church Cemetery, Milo, Yates, NY [Jim – this Israel has floated between the (5) James and (3) Joseph line – he has been returned here based on the research of Shelia Endres – thanks Shelia!]. (some say his middle initial was ‘H.’); married 1st c1774/5 in Dutchess Co NY, Ruth Meade (Mead) [dau of Lieutenant Jonathan Mead (1736-1800) and Sarah (Thompson) (1736-1802)] born March 28, 1757 Northeast, Dutchess, NY and died October 27, 1827 Milo NY; married 2d widow Mary Sanders. Moved to Dutchess Co NY before 1774; thence to Hillsdale, Columbia, NY between 1784-88; thence to North Norwich, Chenango, NY c1796; and before 1815 to Milo, Yates, NY (Deacon at Milon 1827) where his 1st wife died and after 1840 moved to Whitewater WI; served as a Private in Captain Abraham Meade’s Company, 9th Regiment Connecticut Militia under Colonel John Meade from November 18, 1776 to February 11, 1777; supposedly served in the Dutchess Co Militia also.
