PHOEBE FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.2.1.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.1.5) PHOEBE FERRIS born August 12, 1777 and died February 14, 1858 Ballston Spa NY; married January 10, 1799 in Dutchess Co NY, Robert Benedict [son of Robin & Sarah (Ketchum) Benedict] born July 14, 1770 Danbury, Fairfield, CT and died March 9, 1862 Ballston Spa, Saratoga, NY; both buried there Ballston Spa Village Cemetery (Photo); resided at Charlton and Ballston Spa NY [Jim - most records indicate the spelling of ‘Fari’ for Phoebe and apparently her marriage license says she was a daughter of James Faris of Dutchess County NY - so, this assignment may be in error, but for now, she will stay here pending further research - Thanks to Fred Knapp for this info.] Born to Phoebe and Robert were:
