EBENEZER S CRABBE

ID: 4.6.3.4.4.6.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.3.4.4.6.1) EBENEZER S CRABBE born February 4, 1825 Stamford CT and died there November 11, 1878; buried there Scofieldtown-Lockwood #4 Cemetery; Civil War Vet with Co B, 17th Connecticut Vol; 1860-1870 cooper; married 1st c1847 Eliza J {mnu}; 2d 1858 Sarah F Monroe

No children recorded
