AUGUSTUS WRIGHT IVES, M.D.,

ID: 1.4.7.7.10.4.1 | Source: John
(1.4.7.7.10.4.1) AUGUSTUS WRIGHT IVES, M.D., born January 21, 1861 Detroit MI and died c1954 Alexandria VA; attended College de France and U. of Heidelberg, 187881; Detroit College of Medicine, 1891, M.D.; Alienist; professor and head of department of neurology and psychiatry, Detroit College of Medicine and Surgery; Superintendent State Public Schools (See Who’s Who in America); married 1st 1893 Eugenia Schnoor (1870-1897) [dau of Maxmillian & Linda]; married 2d July 31,1901 in MA, Julia Claire Chandler [dau of Henry] born August 25, 1873 Richville NY and died AlexandriaVA, date unk.
