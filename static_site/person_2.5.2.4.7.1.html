FREDERICK HAIT

ID: 2.5.2.4.7.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.7.1) FREDERICK HAIT (Hoyt) born January 24, 1767 Stamford and died February 23, 1859 Darien CT; married 1st, May 10, 1788 in Stamford Abigail Lockwood [dau of Isaac & Rebecca (Seely) Lockwood][may be the (4) Mary Ferris line?] born May 20, 1768 Stamford and died May 22, 1838; married 2d, Mary Patterson (1788-).
