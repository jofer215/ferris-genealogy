WILLIAM PECK HABBERTON

ID: 3.10.5.10.1.7.2.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.5.10.1.7.2.2) WILLIAM PECK HABBERTON born March 1847 NYC and died January 18, 1926 Mt Carmel, Wabash, IL; buried there Rosehill Cemetery; Civil War Vet; Private with Company H, 136th Indiana Infantry Regiment – served 100 days; in 1900 he and his family moved to Mt Carmel where he was a coal merchant; one of the city’s ‘most substantial citizens’; president of Board of Education and member of same for several terms; elected to city council; owner of water boats that plied the Wabash and White Rivers hauling/towing timber, lumber, grain, etc.; sold between 4000-5000 tons of coal; married July 15, 1883 in Wabash Co, Helen M Redman born February 1863 IL and died February 13, 1941 Mt Carmel; and born to them were:
