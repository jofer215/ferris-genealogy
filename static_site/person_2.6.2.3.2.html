NATHANIEL KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.3.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.2) NATHANIEL KNAPP (III) born July 6, 1753 at Stamford and died February 12 (22?), 1812 at Warwick, Orange Co NY.[there remains doubt to his death date - some believe he died earlier than shown here, i.e., 1787.] Nathaniel was a Revolutionary War Veteran, serving as a Private in the Goshen Prescient, Orange County Militia. He married, probably at Goshen NY, c1768, Sarah Sutton born February 21, 1748 and died April 22, 1825; both buried in Baptist Church Burial Grounds (Old School), Warwick NY. Born to them were:
