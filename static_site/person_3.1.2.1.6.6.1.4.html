FRANK ORSEMUS FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.1.6.6.1.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.6.6.1.4) FRANK ORSEMUS FERRIS (Frank Osmun) (Captain) born February 26, 1857 Wilson, Niagra, NY and died April 25, 1938 Berkeley CA; held several commissions, fought in the Indian Wars on the plains; was stationed for a time at Fort Leavenworth KS; retired before WWI, but was called back; married November 13, 1897 at San Francisco CA, Anna Goodwin Hobbs [dau of Hiram Hayes & Frances Ann (Pray) Hobbs] born February 22, 1870 CA and died May 27, 1960 Hayward, Alameda, CA; both Frank and Anna are buried at the San Francisco National Cemetery, San Francisco CA (Photos).
