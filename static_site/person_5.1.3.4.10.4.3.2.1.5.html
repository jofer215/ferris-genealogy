MERRY KAYE MANNING

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.3.2.1.5 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.3.2.1.5) MERRY KAYE MANNING born September 5, 1947 Richfield UT; graduated from Purdue U., 1970, BS, 1971, MS in Nutrition Research and Dietetics; married June 1969 Dennis Senchak (Photo), who graduated from Purdue 1971, B.S., Electrical Engineering and was the Assistant Treasurer of Northern Indiana Public Service Company (1998); resided in Munster IN; Vice President, Investor Relations, Assistant Treasurer and Assistant Secretary of NiSource, Inc, Merrillville IN (2004).
