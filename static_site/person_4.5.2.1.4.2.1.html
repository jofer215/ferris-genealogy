WILLIAM T. REYNOLDS

ID: 4.5.2.1.4.2.1 | Source: Mary
(4.5.2.1.4.2.1) WILLIAM T. REYNOLDS (Photo) born July 18, 1814 Greenwich CT and died 1881; he remained at home until he was married, receiving such advantages for an education as the district schools afforded. He married February 23, 1836 in Greenwich, Mary Ann Halsey [dau of Stephen & Rachel (Brown)] born May 29, 1818 in NYC. In the Spring of 1836, they settled on the place where he died, which was formerly owned by Mrs. Josiah Brown, the grandmother of Mary. It was a fine farm of 125 acres. William was a strong believer in the principles of the Republican Party. He and Mary were members of the Congregational Church of Stanwich. He never sought the political honors preferring the quiet of home. Born to them were (not all these kids are listed in all sources):
