FLORA ANN LINKLETTER

ID: 3.10.2.9.2.2.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.2.9.2.2.1) FLORA ANN LINKLETTER born June 26, 1856 Stanhope, PEI and died June 1936 Colorado Springs CO; married at PEI, Isaac Murray Auld [son of Edward & Elizabeth (MacCallum) Auld] born June 6, 1855 Stanhope and died April 1934 Colorado Springs CO; hearing news of the gold rush, they migrated to Central City CO 1879; he was a carpenter; moved the family to Colorado Springs 1890 and went into the building business with a Mr. Honeyman – Honeyman & Auld, contractors built many of the finest buildings in Colorado Springs; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • MAE AULD 3.10.2.9.2.2.1.1
  • LOUISE AULD 3.10.2.9.2.2.1.2
  • ELIZABETH AULD 3.10.2.9.2.2.1.3
