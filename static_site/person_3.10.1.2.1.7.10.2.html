CHESTER FAYETTE DAVIS

ID: 3.10.1.2.1.7.10.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.1.7.10.2) CHESTER FAYETTE DAVIS born 1857 Solon Township, Cortland Co NY and died December 18, 1923 State Hospital, Binghamton, Broome, NY. An orphan at age 12y, brought up in the family of his maternal uncle Lyman Peck in Cortland, where he attended the State Normal School for about 3y; finishing his preparatory schooling at Cazenovia NY Seminary; later, entering Princeton U, he became violently insane, a condition from he never recovered

No children recorded
