FRANCES LISPENARD WHEELER

ID: 1.2.3.5.8.5 | Source: John
(1.2.3.5.8.5) FRANCES LISPENARD WHEELER born June 13, 1823 Johnstown, Fulton, NY and died December 22, 1854; buried Rest Vale Cemetery, Seneca Falls NY; married February 15, 1843 in Waterloo NY, as his 1st wife, John Samuel Clark (John Spangler Clark) (Photo) [son of Jehiel & Nancy (Casey) Clark] born November 19, 1821 NY and died August 31, 1895 Chicago IL; buried Graceland Cemetery. After Frances death, John married Fannie Campbell and they had offspring; practiced in Chicago – family records were burnt in the Chicago fire 1871 – this updated info is provided by a great-granddaughter of John and Fannie, thanks Jan! Born to them were: (1.2.3.5.8.5.1) CLARINA UNDERHILL CLARK born March 3, 1844 and died March 12, 1934 at Whitelocks, Kingston near Canterbury, Kent, England; ashes buried at Camphill Cemtery with her husband; married September 15, 1864 in Chicago, William Bruce Slayter, M.D., born November 12, 1841 and died August 5, 1898; buried Camphill Cemetery, Halifax. William studied medicine in New York 1857, London 1858, and Dublin 1859; M.R.C.S London 1860; House Surgeon by competition of Westminister Hospital London 1860-61; traveling physician with General Hitchins and his son Captain Hitchins, 1862-63 with whom he visited some of the battlefields of the American Civil War. He was present and helped with wounded at the battle of Antietam. He settled to practice in Chicago in 1864. On the death of his brother, Dr. John Slayter at Halifax in 1866 from chlorea, he and family removed from Chicago to Halifax the same year. He became the leading physician and obstetrical surgeon of Halifax, Professor of Obstetrical Surgery to the Halifax Medical College and the consulting surgeon to Halifax Hospitals.
