LEWIS ELBERT CHRISTIAN, D.D.

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.9.1.5 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.9.1.5) LEWIS ELBERT CHRISTIAN, D.D. (Rev.) born February 16, 1887 Fishkill NY and died October 12, 1949; buried New Hackensack Cemetery; a Dutchess County native and was pastor of the Yonkers Methodist Church 1922 and the minister of the Washington Square Methodist Church, New York City at his death. Dr. Christian was ordained an elder in the Methodist church in April 1914, and was admitted to the New York Conference of the Methodist church, the same year. He was a past president of the Dutchess County society. He also was a member of Phi Delta Kappa, Kappa Delta Pi and the international society of Theta Phi. He was a member of the Alumni council of Drew university; the historical society of the Methodist church and of Poughkeepsie lodge, Free and Accepted Masons. He married June 19, 1916 in Staten Island, Mollie Archer Merritt - apparently no offspring

No children recorded
