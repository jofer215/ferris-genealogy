JOHN ROSE

ID: 3.1.6.8.12.1.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.12.1.1) JOHN ROSE (See Photo) born September 27, 1841 Spencerville, Grenville, Ontario and died July 17, 1933 Haley Station, Renfrew Co; buried there; a farmer; married 1st 1871 in Renfrew Co, Jane (Jennie) McMullen [dau of William and Jane (Shields) McMullen] born September 5, 1853 Renfrew Co and died January 5, 1889 Haley Station, Renfrew Co; married 2d October 15, 1890 in Renfrew Co, Annie Louise McQuitty [dau of William and Mary Ann (Edwards) McQuitty] born February 21, 1869 Horton Township, Renfrew Co and died there September 9, 1945; and born to John and Jane were:
