HARRIET ELIZABETH HOYT

ID: 2.5.3.4.1.1.5.4 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.4.1.1.5.4) HARRIET ELIZABETH HOYT (#5991) born October 19, 1844 Stamford and was living there 1871, and died there December 19, 1906; married February 16, 1866 George E. Reynolds [son of Hanford & Nancy] born April 13, 1840 Stamford and died there April 8, 1878; both buried Roxbury Cemetery, Stamford; 1878 he was a carpenter; and born to them were:

Children (2)
