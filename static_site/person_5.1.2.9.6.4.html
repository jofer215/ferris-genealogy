HENRY L. CHAPMAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.6.4 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.6.4) HENRY L. CHAPMAN born December 9, 1815 Putnam Valley and died January 10, 1889; married December 31, 1845 Catherine Croft [dau of John & Mary (Meeks)] born March 29, 1828 at what became to known as Croft’s Mines in Putnam County and died February 28, 1921 at the home of her daughter at Spring Valley. (they have to be related). About 24 years before her death, Catherine moved to Brooklyn to reside with her daughter, Mary. Both buried at Hillside Cemetery. Henry conducted a meat market in Peekskill for years. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • CURTIS CHAPMAN 5.1.2.9.6.4.1
  • MARY CHAPMAN 5.1.2.9.6.4.2
  • EMMA CHAPMAN 5.1.2.9.6.4.3
