KATHY CLYDEAN MITCHELL

ID: 1.2.5.1.1.1.1.7.1.2.3 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.1.1.1.7.1.2.3) KATHY CLYDEAN MITCHELL born July 9, 1949 Elk City OK; married 1st January 14, 1967 at Kansas City KS, Timothy Andrew Gower born November 22, 1949 Kansas City [son of Ival Andrew and Ethel Irene (Pollock) Gower] they divorced 1975; married 2d October 26, 1975 at Bethany MO, James Stephen Erickson born June 15, 1950 [son of James Edgar Herbert and Cleva Pearl (Nuckolls) Erickson]. Kathy is a Nurse Practicianer and enjoys reading. Timothy was in construction and an electrician. James is a pharmacist and enjoys reading, farming and golf. They reside in Cameron MO.
