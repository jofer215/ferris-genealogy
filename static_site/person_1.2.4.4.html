DAUGHTER UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.4.4 | Source: John
(1.2.4.4) DAUGHTER UNDERHILL married James Sniffin (1.2.5) ABRAHAM UNDERHILL born 1697 New Castle NY and died October 1750 Westchester City NY; Quakers; married c1715 (June 23, 1720 Westchester) Hannah Cromwell [dau of James, sister of Thomas], born pre-1708 (1701 NY); resided in White Plains NY; Abraham made his will August 18, 1750 which was proven in Westchester County NY before Samuel Purdy, Esq, October 2, 1750 and is recorded in New York published wills, Vol 4, p 302. In it he mentions his wife Hannah, sons Abraham, Jacob, and Isaac; daughters Hannah and Martha, and makes his brother, Nathaniel, and caleb Horton his executors; and born to them were:

No children recorded
