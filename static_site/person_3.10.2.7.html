ANNA PECK

ID: 3.10.2.7 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.2.7) ANNA PECK born c1738 Greenwich and died after 1810 (Stamford) Long Ridge, Greenwich CT; married March 5, 1777 in Stamford, as his 2d wife, David Hoyt [son of James & Mary (Waterbury) Hoyt] born October 3, 1728 Stamford and died there June 23, 1810; Rev War Vet served between October 25, 1776 and February 27, 1779 Private in the 9th Militia Regiment; and born to them was:
