PHEBE HAIGHT

ID: 1.1.6.8.2 | Source: John

Pleasant, Westchester, NY and died July 20, 1859 New Castle, Westchester, NY; buried Quaker Burying Ground, Chappaqua, Westchester, NY; married January 21, 1796 in New Castle, Samuel Haight [son of James & Bathsheba (Palmer) Haight] born March 15, 1774 Chappaqua NY and died there March 30, 1860; buried with Phebe; and born to them were:
