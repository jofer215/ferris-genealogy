URIAH HILL

ID: 3.6.3.2.2.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.2.5) URIAH HILL born December 7, 1794 Sussex Co NJ and died March 6, 1863; married Sarah Blackford; both buried Millbrook Village by the school. They have headstones. They were productive in starting Millbrook Methodist Church; Millbrook is a National Preserved Village near Blairstown and the Delaware River. Born to them were:

Children (2)
