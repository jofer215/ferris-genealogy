ELIZABETH JARVIS FERRIS

ID: 3.6.1.2.2.2.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.1.2.2.2.5) ELIZABETH JARVIS FERRIS born 1834 Stamford and died there May 10, 1874; died 6 days after giving birth to her 8th child in 18 years; married October 29, 1855 in Stamford, as his 1st of 2 wives, William Robbins Fosdick born 1817 Long Island NY and died December 11, 1891 New Brighton, Richmond, NY; banker 1874-79; both buried Woodland Cemetery, Stamford; and born to them were:

Children (7)

  • SAMUEL PETERS FOSDICK 3.6.1.2.2.2.5.1
  • WILLIAM FOSDICK 3.6.1.2.2.2.5.2
  • ROBERT ALDRICH FOSDICK 3.6.1.2.2.2.5.3
  • HARRIETTE J FOSDICK 3.6.1.2.2.2.5.4
  • ELIZABETH F FOSDICK 3.6.1.2.2.2.5.5
  • JOSHUA B FOSDICK 3.6.1.2.2.2.5.6
