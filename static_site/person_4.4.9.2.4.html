CHARITY MEAD

ID: 4.4.9.2.4 | Source: Mary
(4.4.9.2.4) CHARITY MEAD born July 14, 1762 Greenwich CT and died there November 16, 1847; engaged to Obadiah Mead [son of Benjamin] but he was killed during the Rev War by cowboys; later married Joshua Knapp (3.10.7.4.4) – see him for offspring

No children recorded
