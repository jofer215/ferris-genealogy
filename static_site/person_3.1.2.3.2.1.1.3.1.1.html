MARY JANE FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.3.2.1.1.3.1.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.3.2.1.1.3.1.1) MARY JANE FERRIS (Photograph) born August 11, 1880 Osh Kosh, Winnebago, WI and died September 4, 1950 Tuolumne, Tuolumne, CA; buried there in Carters Cemetery, with her husband; married October 27, 1898 in Bemidji, Beltrami, MN, August Daniel Anderson [son of Carl & Augusta] born November 20, 1876 Sweden and died March 28, 1966 Tuolumne; came to USA 1882 from Sweden on the Liner Crown Swedish arriving at Crostal Gardens NY then went to homestead in MN; moved from MN to Eureka CA 1904 then to Merced Falls from 1913-16 where he worked as a pond foreman; 1916 they moved to Santa Rosa CA, then to Tuolumne 1922; and born to them were:
