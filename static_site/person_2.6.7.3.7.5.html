CAROLINE KNAPP

ID: 2.6.7.3.7.5 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.3.7.5) CAROLINE KNAPP (2.6.7.3.8) SEYMOUR KNAPP born July 17, 1781 Bethlehem CT and died date unk but after 1832 at Onondaga Hill NY; married 1st name unk; married 2d Betsey Luddington (c1776-); married 3d Margaret Kasson born September 3, 1741 Woodbury CT (married third Betsey’s sister, Polly Luddington, who after Seymour’s death married a Mr. Pomeroy ??); Seymour was a Veteran of the War of 1812 [There is confusion here as to which Luddington is Luddington and which is mother to the children.] Born to Seymour and Betsey were:

No children recorded
