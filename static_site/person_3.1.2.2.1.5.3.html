CZAR HAMILTON BENEDICT

ID: 3.1.2.2.1.5.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.1.5.3) CZAR HAMILTON BENEDICT born December 1, 1802; married 1st September 3, 1826 Mary Ann Forester born October 19, 1809 and died February 3, 1836; buried Ballston Spa Village Cemetery; married 2d April 29, 1843 widow Martha (Denton) Sweet born March 6, 1811; last know residence was at Ballston Spa NY, and died October 2, 1890; also buried Ballston Spa Village Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • ROBERT FORESTER BENEDICT 3.1.2.2.1.5.3.1
  • CHARLES EDWIN BENEDICT 3.1.2.2.1.5.3.2
  • MARY ANN BENEDICT 3.1.2.2.1.5.3.3
  • SARAH ANN BENEDICT 3.1.2.2.1.5.3.4
