NATHANIEL KIMBERLY FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.6.4.5.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.6.4.5.2) NATHANIEL KIMBERLY FERRIS (tombstone photos) born March 31, 1851 (February 7, 1853) and died June 4, 1913; married October 1, 1873 (Hannah E) Hannah Hopkins Watson (Watkins?) August 30, 1851 and died December 28, 1888. They had a daughter who died infancy. Born to Stephen and his second wife, Adeline, were: (5.1.3.6.4.5.3) HERBERT LODER FERRIS born September 27, 1858 Sound Beach and died 1930 an invalid; unmarried
