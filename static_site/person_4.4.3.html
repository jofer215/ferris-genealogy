ROBERT LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.3 | Source: Mary
(4.4.3) ROBERT LOCKWOOD (#75) (K7FP-GP) born c1714 Greenwich and died before 1783; married in June 1739, Rachel Stevens [dau of Thomas & Sarah (Hubbell) Stevens] born September 1, 1718, Stamford. He was a singer of the articles of association for Newburgh NY. Born to them were:

Children (5)

  • LYDIA LOCKWOOD 4.4.3.2

  • DAVID LOCKWOOD 4.4.3.4
  • JESSE LOCKWOOD 4.4.3.5
