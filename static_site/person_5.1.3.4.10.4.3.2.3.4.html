FRANCES KAREN THORNBURG

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.3.2.3.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.3.2.3.4) FRANCES KAREN THORNBURG born April 16, 1933 Kansas City MO and died January 9, 1978 San Diego CA; married 1st September 23, 1953, Santa Monica CA, R. L. Whisenant born July 23, 1930 and died 1986 - divorced March 1, 1956; married 2d October 4, 1962 Santa Barbara CA, John William Foxworthy [son of John W. & Vernice (Deacon) Foxworthy] born May 4, 1926 Craig County OK and died February 11, 1986 Encino CA - divorced; married 3d November 10, 1963 at Reno NV, Ronald Charles Horn, Sr. [son of Elmer J. & Tillie (Breit) Horn] born March 29, 1931 Fulda MN.
