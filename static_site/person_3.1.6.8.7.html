SARAH ROSE

ID: 3.1.6.8.7 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.7) SARAH ROSE born July 5, 1777 Manchester, Bennington County VT; baptized May 28, 1793 in Williamstown, Glengarry County, Ontario, Canada and died, in childbirth, February 27, 1821 in Edwardsburg Township, Grenville County, Ontario; buried N. Chan Cemetery, Ontario; married November 25, 1793 in Edwardsburg Township, Ontario, Ezra Adams (1772-1852) [son of Dr. Samuel & Martha (Curtis) Adams]. One source says they had 14 children and another has 9 kids; born to them were:
