JESSE HAIT

ID: 4.6.5.8 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.8) JESSE HAIT (#2904) (Hoyt) was baptized/born April 24, 1743 New Canaan CT and died May 5, 1831 Pound Ridge, Westchester, NY; a soldier in the French War, actively engaged along the northern frontier; he was taken prisoner by the British troops in their raid upon Pound Ridge in 1779, but escaped. Jesse married c1765 Lydia FERRIS (*). Born to them were [the name may have been either Hait or Hoyt; will stay with Hait for now] [it is said that all of these offspring lived to or past the age of 78 years]:
