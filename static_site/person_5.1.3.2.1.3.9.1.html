ELEANOR AUGUSTA FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.2.1.3.9.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.1.3.9.1) ELEANOR AUGUSTA FERRIS born October 14, 1872; edited The Villager, begun 1929 and published by the Bronxville Women’s Club; resided in Bronxville, Westchester Co NY. Her own short stories also appeared in The Villager. The Dwight and Ferris Family papers are at Yale University, Beinecke Rare Book and Manuscript Library.

No children recorded
