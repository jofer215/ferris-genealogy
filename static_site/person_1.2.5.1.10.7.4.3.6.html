RICHARD VERN SUTTON

ID: 1.2.5.1.10.7.4.3.6 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.10.7.4.3.6) RICHARD VERN SUTTON born August 14, 1918 Battle Creek MI and died there May 1, 1997; buried there Fort Custer National Cemetery; Kendall School of Design; Ringling Brothers Commercial Art School; US Army Air Corps, Private, WWII; married 1st Miss Iams and they had 4 kids; 2d February 27, 1971 in Battle Creek, Barbara Hoffman born December 26, 1926
