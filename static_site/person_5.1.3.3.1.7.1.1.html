GEORGE ALFRED FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.7.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.7.1.1) GEORGE ALFRED FERRIS born October 16, 1851 Stamford and died there August 18, 1926; grocer 1880-87; married 1st 1880, Ada Sutherland Lockwood born May 15, 1861 Greenwich and died January 30, 1881 Stamford; and 2d October 11, 1882 in Stamford, Annie Stevens Curtis [dau of Hiram & Mary Frances (Jones) Curtis] born November 1, 1861 Stamford and died there April 19, 1942; all buried Long Ridge Cemetery, Stamford CT (See Photo); and born to him and Ada was:
