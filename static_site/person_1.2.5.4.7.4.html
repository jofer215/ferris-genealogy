JOHN GRIFFIN

ID: 1.2.5.4.7.4 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.7.4) JOHN GRIFFIN born 1795 New Castle, Westchester, NY and died 1819 Yorktown NY; married 1st 1816 as his 1st husband, Hannah U Underhill (1.2.5.1.9.6); 2d name unk; and born to him and Hannah were:

Children (4)

  • AARON GRIFFIN 1.2.5.4.7.4.1
  • MOSES GRIFFIN 1.2.5.4.7.4.2
  • JOHN L GRIFFIN 1.2.5.4.7.4.3
  • ZOPHER GRIFFIN died July 30, 1822 Yorktown, Westchester, NY; 1.2.5.4.7.4.4
