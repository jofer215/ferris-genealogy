HELEN AMELIA FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.5.1 | Source: John
(1.4.7.5.1) HELEN AMELIA FERRIS born May 4, 1804 Swanton VT and died there, September (21?) 27, 1887; married March 23, 1831 Horatio Hickok Brayton, M.D. (1800-1850). Horatio was a physician in Swanton, but removed to New York where he sold drugs and medicine. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • JOHN KICKIO BRAYTON 1.4.7.5.1.1
  • MARY ELIZABETH BRAYTON 1.4.7.5.1.2
  • AMELIA HELEN BRAYTON 1.4.7.5.1.3
