OLIVER SCOFIELD

ID: 2.1.5.6.1.4.6 | Source: Peter
(2.1.5.6.1.4.6) OLIVER SCOFIELD born June 13, 1809 Stamford and died there June 26, 1870; 1850 merchant; 1860 coal dealer; 1870 merchant; married 1st February 5, 1837 in Stamford, Mary E Selleck (2.6.5.3.4.2.1); married 2d March 24, 1862 in NYC, Ann Maria Lockwood [dau of Lewis & Priscilla (Mead) Lockwood] born April 8, 1820 Stamford and died there April 21, 1882; all buried there North Street Cemetery; and born to him and Mary were:

Children (3)

  • EMILY SCOFIELD 2.1.5.6.1.4.6.1
  • HENRY SCOFIELD 2.1.5.6.1.4.6.2
  • ROBERT BRUCE SCOFIELD 2.1.5.6.1.4.6.3
