JULIA SCOFIELD

ID: 2.5.2.4.7.2.4 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.7.2.4) JULIA SCOFIELD born September 9, 1802 CT and died November 15, 1866 Darien; buried Spring Grove Cemetery; married May 12, 1829 in Stamford, as his 2d wife, her brother-in-law, Abram Clock (5.2.3.5.2.2) – see him for offspring

No children recorded
