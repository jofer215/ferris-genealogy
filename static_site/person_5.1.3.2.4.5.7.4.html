DELLA ANN SNELL

ID: 5.1.3.2.4.5.7.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.4.5.7.4) DELLA ANN SNELL born January 14, 1889 KY and died February 28, 1935 Adams IN; married September 18, 1907 John T Porter [son of John E & Margaret Ann (West) Porter] born December 24, 1877 Harris City, Decatur, IN and died December 9, 1930 Greensburg, Decatur, IN; buried there South Park Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (2)

  • MARGARET FRANCES PORTER 5.1.3.2.4.5.7.4.1
  • JOHN HENRY PORTER 5.1.3.2.4.5.7.4.2
