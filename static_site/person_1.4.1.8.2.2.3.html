CHARLES EDWARD CROWELL

ID: 1.4.1.8.2.2.3 | Source: John
(1.4.1.8.2.2.3) CHARLES EDWARD CROWELL born July 24, 1847 and died July 10, 1925; LL.B., Columbia; compiler of much of this family’s information; married 1st August 4, 1878 Harriet Spencer Popham [dau of William Hill & Sarah Hulda (Spencer) Popham] born July 20, 1847 Scarsdale, Westchester, NY and died February 16, 1893, and 2d April 29, 1895, married Mary Smith Clark.
