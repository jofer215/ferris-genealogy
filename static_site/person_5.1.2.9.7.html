JOHN CHAPMAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.7 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.7) JOHN CHAPMAN born July 5, 1787 Putnam Valley NY, which doesn’t track with the 1850 census which lists him as 60y; a carpenter; married Letita Owen [dau of Samuel] (listed as 47y in the 1850 census) and they removed to Iowa. [With the differences in the age of the kids, I wonder if Letita might have been John’s second wife.?] Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • SUSAN ANN CHAPAM listed as 26y in the 1850 census 5.1.2.9.7.1
  • GEORGE CHAPMAN listed as 12y in the 1850 census 5.1.2.9.7.3
  • MARY E. CHAPMAN listed as 10 in the 1850 census 5.1.2.9.7.4
  • BENJAMIN F. CHAPMAN listed as 8y in the 1850 census 5.1.2.9.7.5
