POLLY FERRIS “Mary” born June 17, 1787 and died March 30, 1852 Middlebury,

ID: 3.1.2.1.6.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.6.3) POLLY FERRIS “Mary” born June 17, 1787 and died March 30, 1852 Middlebury, Elkhart, IN; buried there Forest Grove Cemetery; married George Weyburn (Claybourne in one source) [son of Samuel & Jane (Bratton) Weyburn] born September 23, 1781 Derry Township PA and died October 25, 1864; buried in the family plot on the Alexander Bower farm, not far from Trumansburg NY. Polly and George lived for several years on a farm he owned in Chemung Co NY at or near Tioga Point, which was apparently adjacent to the farm owned by Polly's father, Samuel. They had gone to live with their daughters who lived in Indiana where Polly died, thus she was buried there. After Polly's death, George lived with his eldest daughter and family and when he died, was buried on their farm. Born to them were:
