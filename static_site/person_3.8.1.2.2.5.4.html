BENJAMIN SHERMAN FERRIS

ID: 3.8.1.2.2.5.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.8.1.2.2.5.4) BENJAMIN SHERMAN FERRIS born October 1, 1830/31 Ferrisburg VT and died Denver CO; moved to Princeton IL where he became a banker with his brother Henry; Benjamin was President of the First National Bank of Princeton until 1873, when he moved to Denver CO because of health reasons. He married, lady’s name unknown and born to them were (possibly):

Children (2)

  • PAUL W. FERRIS was a dentist in Princeton Ill; Paul and his brother Frank, both 3.8.1.2.2.5.4.1
  • FRANK B. FERRIS was a dentist in Princeton IL; organized the first company from 3.8.1.2.2.5.4.2
