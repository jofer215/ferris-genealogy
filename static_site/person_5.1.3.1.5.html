TIMOTHY FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.5 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.5) TIMOTHY FERRIS (1756-1829) [there is confusion as to the placement of this line – either here or in the (3) Joseph line – information is duplicated.] married Mary, maiden name unk [not listed in Chaplain Ferris’ book]; they settled at Sherbourne NY before 1810. The 1820 Chenango County NY census shows Timothy as a resident of Columbus. Born to them were: (5.1.3.1.5.1) JESSE FERRIS born c1789 and died May 5, 1864 Smyrna NY; buried Smyrna East Cemetery, aka, Baptist Cemetery, Smyrna; married Zeruah Decker born 1798 Columbus NY and died January 10, 1883 Smyrna NY; buried with Jesse; and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • WILLIAM FERRIS died Ohio - killed by Indians; married Rebecca 5.1.3.1.5.2
  • ABRAM HOWE FERRIS 5.1.3.1.5.3
  • MARY FERRIS 5.1.3.1.5.4
  • CATHARINE FERRIS 5.1.3.1.5.5
  • SALLY FERRIS 5.1.3.1.5.6
