JOHN DICKINSON MILBURN

ID: 3.1.6.8.5.5.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.5.5.3) JOHN DICKINSON MILBURN born May 15, 1842 Easton’s Corners, Grenville, Ontario and died August 7, 1915 Niles, Berrien, MI; buried there; 1867-70 co-owner of Milburn & Dodge Hardware, Niles MI; 1870 purchased stock in Milburn Wagon Co and moved to Memphis; before 1883 established Carver Gin & Machine Co, later changing name to Milburn Gin & Machinery Co, Memphis, President until 1892/93 when company was liquidated; married May 25, 1869 in Niles, Katie Mae Jones [dau of John & Elizabeth (Pugh) Jones] born April 2, 1847 Waterville, Oneida, NY and died October 10, 1937 Altadena, Los Angeles, CA; buried Niles; and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • MARTHA MAUD LOUISE MILBURN 3.1.6.8.5.5.3.1
  • JOHN DICKINSON MILBURN, M.D., 3.1.6.8.5.5.3.2
  • THOMAS LEROY MILBURN 3.1.6.8.5.5.3.3
  • CATHERINE ROSE MILBURN 3.1.6.8.5.5.3.4
  • EDITH VICTORIA MILBURN 3.1.6.8.5.5.3.5
