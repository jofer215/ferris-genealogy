OLIVE SOPHIA UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.6.2.1.1.2.11 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.1.1.2.11) OLIVE SOPHIA UNDERHILL (Photo) born July 10, 1862 Rushville IL and died August 20, 1952 Pasedena CA; married September 8, 1881 Oliver Perry Fry born August 30, 1858 Belleville, Richland, OH and died January 6, 1951 Ceres CA; they lived many years at the home of their daughter, Estella in Ceres; Olive went to visit her son, William in Pasadena and died there; and born to them were:
