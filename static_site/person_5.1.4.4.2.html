JONATHAN JESSUP, JR.,

ID: 5.1.4.4.2 | Source: James
(5.1.4.4.2) JONATHAN JESSUP, JR., born January 7, 1761 Greenwich; baptized June 28, 1761 and died there September 28, 1826; married 1st December 25, 1797 in Stamford, Rheva (Ruah) Lockwood born April 2, 1773 Stamford and died November 17, 1799; 2d July 3, 1808 Keziah Green born June 25, 1787 Stamford and died 1854 Clinton Co OH. After Jonathan’s death Keziah married David Jenks and they had a dau. [Apparently, there were children born to both of Jonathan's marriages. His sons of the first marriage made their choice of Keziah to be their guardian, who was also appointed guardian of John Anson Jessup, Julius Augustus Jessup, Cornelius James Jessup, Charles Edward Jessup, Sarah Jeannette Jessup and Ann Eliza Jessup, apparently children of hers of Jonathan's -- this from Stamford Probate Records, 1824.] [Might be a conflict with dates since all birth dates shown are after his second marriage.] Born to him and Keziah were:
