REUBEN LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.3.3 | Source: Mary
(4.6.3.3) REUBEN LOCKWOOD (#253) (Captain) born July 3, 1743 Stamford and died May 30, 1831 Greenwich CT; enlisted July 6, 1775 in the Army; Private in Company 4, 7th Connecticut Regiment July 12 – December 24, 1776; highway surveyor and fence viewer; married 1st in Stamford, Lydia {maiden name unk} who died giving birth; married 2d November 5, 1774 in Stamford, Mary Mead.
