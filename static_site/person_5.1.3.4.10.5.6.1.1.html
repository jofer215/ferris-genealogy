GLENN WOOD DAVIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.5.6.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.5.6.1.1) GLENN WOOD DAVIS born October 2, 1920 (5.1.3.4.10.5.7) ASA W. FERRIS (Photographs) born August 19, 1860 and died 1944; buried North East Cemetery, North East PA; married January 1, 1884 in Clay Center KS, Clara Riblet [dau of Thomas & Caroline]. Clara’s family moved to Kansas when she was 14 years old; homesteaded a claim in Trego County KS. Clara taught school there and family tradition has it, that Asa saw her riding horseback to or from school, and said that that was the girl he was going to marry, and did; lived in Kansas, then returned to PA c1887; resided Harbor Creek Township, Erie Co PA 55 years; and born to them were:

No children recorded
