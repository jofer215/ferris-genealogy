WILLIAM JOSEPH CHURCH

ID: 3.1.6.8.3.2.3.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.3.2.3.2) WILLIAM JOSEPH CHURCH born October 9, 1847 Frankville, Grenville, Ontario and died June 12, 1914 Radville, Sask., Canada; buried there; married December 25, 1878 in Dwight MI, Mary Angelina Helferich; buried May 20, 1948 Radville Community Cemetery [now known as Laurier Cemetery], Radville, Sask. [Mary was the mother of 13 kids, born in a span of 27 years incredible!] Born to them were:
