MINNIE ARVILLA CANFIELD

ID: 3.1.6.6.4.2.6.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.6.4.2.6.1) MINNIE ARVILLA CANFIELD born May 15, 1871 Hazel Dell IL and died June 15, 1930 Wichita KS; married December 3, 1890 at Charleston IL, Aaron John Melick born June 6, 1870 Liscomb, Marshall, IA and died July 26, 1944 Halstead, Harvey, KS; both buried at the Old Mission Cemetery, Wichita KS; Aaron married Mary Lody after Minnie’s death and they had one child.
