FREDERICK ALONZO FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.6.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.1) FREDERICK ALONZO FERRIS born February 2, 1826 Greenwich and died January 7, 1903 DeWitt NY; buried there; lived in Dewitt, near Syracuse, Onondaga Co where he owned and operated a farm of about one hundred acres that was given to him by his father, adjacent to his brother Gideon's farm. [A F.A. Ferris owned 102 2 acres, Lot 61 in the Town of Dewitt, Onondaga County NY, 1874.] In 1846, it was a mile east of Syracuse on E. Genesee Street; in 1926 it was included in a section that was annexed to Syracuse and called Syracuse Manor when it was developed into an attractive residential section. Frederick married 1st December 6, 1847 Mary Jane Lockwood (4.4.5.5.11.3) born March 18, 1827 and died May 3, 1858; married 2d December 25, 1858 by Rev. Lewis Hubbard Reid, at the Fayetteville Presbyterian Church, his sister-in-law, Laura Wooster Lockwood (4.4.5.5.11.6) born August 25, 1834 and died September 6, 1918 Syracuse NY.
