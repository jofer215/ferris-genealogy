ARNOLD EARL CLARK

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.8 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.8) ARNOLD EARL CLARK (Earl) born August 26, 1891 Leelanau Co MI and died May 26, 1974 Traverse City MI; buried there Oakwood Cemetery; attendant at the Traverse City State Hospital for 37y; married April 4, 1914 in Bingham, Eveline Marie Flohe [dau of Edmund & Catherine (Lingner) Flohe] born April 14, 1894 Bingham, Leelanau, MI; school teacher; Earl married 2d June 8, 1928 in Traverse City, Gladys LaBelle Budd [dau of Frank & Ruth Ann (Cunningham) Budd] born November 17, 1903 Delphos, Allen, OH and died May 3, 1994 Traverse City MI; buried Oakwood Cemetery; telephone operator for Michigan Bell; and born to Earl and Gladys was:
