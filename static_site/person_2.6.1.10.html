NATHAN KNAPP

ID: 2.6.1.10 | Source: Peter
(2.6.1.10) NATHAN KNAPP born June 12, 1735 Stamford and died there January 27, 1797; married 1st September 23, 1761 in Stamford, Rebecca Wardwell who died February 17, 1767 (in childbirth); married 2d January 22, 1770 in Stamford, Mary Lockwood (4.6.11.1). Nathan and both wives are buried in the West Stamford Cemetery, Stamford CT. Born to Nathan and Rebecca were:
