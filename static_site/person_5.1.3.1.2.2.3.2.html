EMMA BORDEN

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.2.3.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.2.3.2) EMMA BORDEN born March 6, 1828 Yates NY and died January 11, 1916 Lisbon ND; married February 25, 1845 Hiram Darius Gilbert born November 17, 1818 Bath NY and died of pneumonia, October 26, 1907 Lisbon ND. The Gilberts were some of the original settlers of Lisbon ND. Hiram and 4 sons arrived in the Lisbon area in the spring of 1880; they lived in a dugout and later prepared a crude shack, awaiting Emma's arrival in the fall of that year. A family story has it that an Indian came up to them and told them to camp there because there was always plenty of water. Hiram was issued 160 acres near Lisbon ND, December 31, 1890, Homestead Certificate No. 5666. Born to them were:
