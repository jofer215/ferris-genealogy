RICHARD BURCHAN FERRIS

ID: 1.1.1.7.3.3.3.3 | Source: John
(1.1.1.7.3.3.3.3) RICHARD BURCHAN FERRIS (Photograph) born February 27, 1891 Kansas City MO and died April 25, 1973; graduated from U. of Pennsylvania, E.E., 1917; served with the 316th Infantry, as a First Lieutenant, during WWI, AEF, France.
