ALFRED MEAD

ID: 4.4.9.2.3.3.1 | Source: Mary
(4.4.9.2.3.3.1) ALFRED MEAD born 1827 and died 1893 MI; married Charlotte (maiden name unk) and they had 4 kids, all of whom died young (4.4.9.2.3.3.2) AUGUSTUS W. MEAD born September 8, 1829; a Civil War Vet – 1st Lieutenant with the 97th New York Volunteers; married 1st September 7, 1854 Mary Richards who died April 9, 1895; married 2d October 28, 1899 Mrs. Louisa Bentley Lounsbury; and born to him and Mary were 3 kids who died young and:

No children recorded
