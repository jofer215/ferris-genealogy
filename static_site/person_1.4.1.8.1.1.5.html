GLOVER CLAPHAM JR

ID: 1.4.1.8.1.1.5 | Source: John
(1.4.1.8.1.1.5) GLOVER CLAPHAM JR born April 23, 1844 Norwalk CT; received his education at St Matthews Hall, the Farmers’ Hall, Bradley School, and also the Well’s Academy and the Lester MA Military Academy. He led a life of leisure, having inherited an ample fortune which included 4-5 large farms; married June 27, 1868 in Orange Co NY Emma Oakley [dau of Wakeman B & Emily (Savage) Oakley] born December 20, 1850; and born to them was (and apparently more) (2006 no living descendants):
