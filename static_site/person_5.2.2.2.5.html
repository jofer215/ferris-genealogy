SAMUEL BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.2.5 | Source: James
(5.2.2.2.5) SAMUEL BUXTON born January 17, 1763 Stamford CT and died there 1812; buried North Street Cemetery; married 1st April 1, 1793 in Stamford, Abigail Davenport [dau of Hezekiah & Ruth (Ketchum) Davenport] born 1768 Stamford and died there November 9, 1807; buried Scofieldtown Cemetery, Stamford; married 2d March 20, 1808 Abigail Weed (2.1.1.1.2.5) ; and born to him and Abigail were:

Children (5)

  • HARVEY BUXTON 5.2.2.2.5.1
  • BENJAMIN KNAPP BUXTON 5.2.2.2.5.2
  • WILLIAM BUXTON 5.2.2.2.5.3
  • HARRIET BUXTON 5.2.2.2.5.4
  • SAMUEL JOSEPH BUXTON 5.2.2.2.5.5
