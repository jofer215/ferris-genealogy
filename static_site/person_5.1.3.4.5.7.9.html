LAVINA DAY BRAMBLE

ID: 5.1.3.4.5.7.9 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.5.7.9) LAVINA DAY BRAMBLE born January 8, 1845 Madisonville, Hamilton, OH and died January 29, 1893, Washington C. H., Fayette, OH; married 1872 in Madisonville, George Fitzwater Robinson [son of William Newell & Nancy (Fitzwater) Robinson] born November 28, 1845 Miamiville, Clermont, OH and died October 24, 1914 Washington C. H., Fayette, OH; and after Lavina’s death, he remarried; Civil War Vet Private with Company M, 2d Regiment Ohio Cav 1862-65; Miami U 1868; lumber dealer; Mayor Washington Court House OH 1906-10.
