GEORGE SHIRLEY TUBBS

ID: 1.2.6.2.1.3.2.3 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.1.3.2.3) GEORGE SHIRLEY TUBBS born January 9, 1874 Kirkwood, Warren, IL and died January 23, 1907 Bombay, India; buried Kirkwood Cemetery, Kirkwood IL; married September 6, 1899 in Carthage IL, Nora May Shaffer [dau of Gustavus Cresap & Agnes Dean (Henderson) Shaffer] born December 17, 1876 Williamstown WV and died September 8, 1970 Monmouth, Warren, IL

No children recorded
