HANNAH HAIT

ID: 2.5.2.11.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.11.2) HANNAH HAIT (Hoyt) (#3051) born April 15, 1753 Stamford and died there June 25, 1825; buried North Stamford Cemetery; married April 21, 1780 in Stamford, Asahel Weed [son of Amos & Mary (Clason) Weed] born January 1, 1755/56 Stamford and died there December 3, 1825; buried with Hannah; Private in Army between 1776-77; and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • ELIZABETH WEED 2.5.2.11.2.1
  • MARY WEED 2.5.2.11.2.2
  • JOHN WEED, JR., 2.5.2.11.2.3
  • ANNA WEED 2.5.2.11.2.4
  • ASAHEL WEED, JR. 2.5.2.11.2.5
