ANNIE LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.5.2.3.1.6 | Source: Mary
(4.5.2.3.1.6) ANNIE LOCKWOOD (#1296) Anna born December 24, 1792 Manhattan, NYC and died September 22, 1866 in Warwick, Orange, NY; married January 1, 1815 in Mt. Vernon, Westchester, NY, as his 2d wife, Sylvanus Loud [son of Sylvanus & Lydia (Lovell) Lord] born February 27, 1791 Weymouth, Norfolk, MA and died June 18, 1858 Mt. Vernon, Westchester, NY.
