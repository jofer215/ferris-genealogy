HARRY BURR FERRIS, M. D.,

ID: 5.1.3.6.4.9.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.6.4.9.2) HARRY BURR FERRIS, M. D., born May 21, 1865 Greenwich CT and died October 12, 1940 New Haven CT; graduated Yale, B.A., 1887; Graduated Yale Medical School, M. D., 1891; practiced medicine for a while but soon gave it up for teaching; Professor of Anatomy at Yale, 1895-1933; early student of cancer. The FERRIS ROOM, a library and seminar room in the School of Medicine was presented after his retirement in 1933. Author of "The Indians of Cuczo and the Apurimac", 1916, "The Quichua and Machiganga Indians", 1921; contributed to many works on medical subjects; resided at New Haven CT; married June 23, 1892 Helen Whiting Ferris (5.1.3.2.1.3.1.2).
