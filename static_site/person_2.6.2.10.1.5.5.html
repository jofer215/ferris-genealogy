JOSEPH H. KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.10.1.5.5 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.10.1.5.5) JOSEPH H. KNAPP born June 9, 1850 Stamford and died there December 29, 1928; 1885 mason; councilman from the Third Ward in the first Common Council of Stamford, 1894; Police Commissioner 1894; served as a member of the Board of Relief; as a member of the Republican Town committee and as assistant postmaster; retired from the building contractor business which he conducted for many years; married November 1, 1883 in Stamford, Emma Jessup (dau of Paris Robins & Hannah O. Jessup] born 1853 Mianus CT; and born to them was:
