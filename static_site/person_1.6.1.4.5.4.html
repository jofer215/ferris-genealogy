ELIZABETH FERRIS

ID: 1.6.1.4.5.4 | Source: John
(1.6.1.4.5.4) ELIZABETH FERRIS born June 17, 1827 (1820) Bedford, Westchester, NY and died June 30, 1899 Mt. Kisco NY; buried with George; married November 6, 1844 in Bedford NY, George Brown Davis [son of James & Nancy Ann (Corking) Brown] born December 18, 1822 New York City and died April 23, 1892 Mt. Kisco NY; buried Bedford Union Cemetery. Born to them were:
