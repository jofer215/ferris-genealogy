SAMUEL FERRIS, Jr.,

ID: 1.3.4.1 | Source: John
(1.3.4.1) SAMUEL FERRIS, Jr., born May 10, 1740 Newtown, Fairlfield, CT and died June (January?) 12, 1796; married April 9, 1763 in Newtown, Phebe Beers born August 14, 1741 Newtown. There is a Samuel Ferris listed in the 1790 Wayne County NY census listed as living in Kortright – not sure this is the same guy; listed as having 4 females in the household with him. Born to them were:

Children (6)

  • LUCY FERRIS 1.3.4.1.1
  • BETTY FERRIS 1.3.4.1.2
  • DIANTHY FERRIS 1.3.4.1.3
  • RUTH ANN FERRIS 1.3.4.1.4
  • PHEBE FERRIS 1.3.4.1.5
  • CLEMIND FERRIS 1.3.4.1.6
