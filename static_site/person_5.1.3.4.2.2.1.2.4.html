MILDRED FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.2.2.1.2.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.2.2.1.2.4) MILDRED FERRIS (twin) (Photograph) born December 11, 1913 Greenwich CT [Note - Mildred and Edgar are twins, but do not share a birthday; Edgar was born before midnight on the 10th and Mildred born after midnight, on the 11th!] Mildred played the piano, the classics and also piano rags and had a gift for sight-reading and still plays the piano. She became a comptometer (accounting machine) operator and worked in New York City until after her marriage. Later, she worked as a nursery school teacher and also went back to the comptometer. Mildred married, December 4, 1937 at Greenwich, Whitman Arthur Wescott, Jr. born February 20, 1912 at Greenwich and died there, April 1, 1988. Born to them were:
