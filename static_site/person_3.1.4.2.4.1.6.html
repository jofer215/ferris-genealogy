AARON HOYT HUSTED

ID: 3.1.4.2.4.1.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.4.2.4.1.6) AARON HOYT HUSTED born 1813 and died July 31, 1866; buried Methodist CGM Clarksfield, Huron, OH; 1817 moved from Danbury to Clarksfield Township OH and built first house there; 1818 brought family to their log home; 1818 began having services on the Sabbath; 1818 established a grist mill, the first manufacturing industry in Clarksfield; Civil War Vet; married March 22, 1837 in Clarksfield, Sarah Gray [dau of Abram & Anna (Starr) Gray] born March 22, 1821 (1824) and died March 9, 1858; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • HESTER PAUL HUSTED 3.1.4.2.4.1.6.1
  • HENRY HUSTED 3.1.4.2.4.1.6.2
  • EVELINE E. HUSTED 3.1.4.2.4.1.6.3
