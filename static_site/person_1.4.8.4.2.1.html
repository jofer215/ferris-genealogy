LOUISA HANNAH DRAKE

ID: 1.4.8.4.2.1 | Source: John
(1.4.8.4.2.1) LOUISA HANNAH DRAKE born c1790 NY and died August 29, 1852 Woodlawn Plantation, Napoleonville, Assumption Parish, LA; married June 21, 1814 Thomas Clark Nicholls [son of Edward Church & Williamina (Hamilton) Nicholls] born in MD and died June 12, 1847; he was a veteran of the War of 1812; member of the Louisiana legislature and was a Judge of the Louisiana State Court of Appeals; practiced law in Donaldsonville LA; later became a district judge and the first president of the Temperance Society; they settled in Donaldsonville shortly after their marriage; and born to them were:

Children (8)
