PETER FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.5.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.5.1) PETER FERRIS born c1754; enlisted at age 21, August 1, 1776 in the Dutchess Co Minute Company under Captain Cornelius Van Wyck of Beekman; a farmer and was 5'7" with a fresh complexion, brown eyes and brown hair; sent to Jail in February 1781 and was considered dangerous; June 4, 1781 he was still a prisoner in the jail and was ordered not to depart more than 200 yards from the Court House in Poughkeepsie; later that year he was released and ordered to the house of John Ferris in Peekskill

No children recorded
