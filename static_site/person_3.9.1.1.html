ISAAC AUSTIN

ID: 3.9.1.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.9.1.1) ISAAC AUSTIN born 1746 and died October 14, 1813 Red Mills NY (now Mahopac Falls); buried Mahopac Falls NY Presbyterian Church Yard; married Ann Curry born October 20, 1745 and died December 30, 1811; buried Mahopac Falls. In 1788 purchased of the Commissioners of forfeiture, 92 acres in Lot No. 5, Phillips Patent. He was a Private under Captain Haight, Colonel Drake, Westchester Co Militia during the Rev. War.
