JOHN ALEXANDER FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.5.2 | Source: John
(1.4.7.5.2) JOHN ALEXANDER FERRIS born February 17, 1806 at Burlington VT and died September 20, 1878 at San Francisco CA; unmarried. He had a M.A. from the U. of Vermont, 1824. Translated Florian’s NUMA POPPILUS and gave copy to Vermont U. He had extensive real estate holdings in California. Had a large wart on the side of his nose.

No children recorded
