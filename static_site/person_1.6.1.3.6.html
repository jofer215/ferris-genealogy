WILLIAM HENRY FERRIS

ID: 1.6.1.3.6 | Source: John
(1.6.1.3.6) WILLIAM HENRY FERRIS born North Castle February 18/22, 1815 and died Mattawan (Beacon) (North Castle) NY February 9, 1887; married 1st 1836 Eliza Green born North Castle March 7, 1813 and died there July 16, 1840 (Plot 13); and 2d Henrietta Holmes born 1820 and died August 22, 1887 (The marriage sequence is sometimes reversed so not sure whom he married first or mother(s) of the kids.); William was admitted to the New York annual conference of the Methodist church in 1843 and became a renowned and important pastor. He spent forty-five years in the ministry serving many large and important churches (Jim- are there unimportant churches?) in New York City, Poughkeepsie and Westchester. He died suddenly while pastor at First Church, Beacon NY. Once director of New York Mission Society, he also served as a trustee of Syracuse University. Union College conferred on him an honorary D.D. His burial place is Ossining, NY. Born to him and Eliza was:
