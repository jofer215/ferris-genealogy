DEBORAH BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.1 | Source: James
(5.2.2.1) DEBORAH BUXTON born May 30, 1729 Stamford CT and died there March 29, 1804; married January 14, 1747/8 Gideon Lounsberry (Lounsbury) [son of Henry & Mary (Scofield) Lounsbury] born c1720 Stamford and died there June 1, 1792. In 1746, he was listed as an Episcopal member of the 1st Congregational Church at Stamford. In 1784, he was listed as a member in good standing of St. John’s Episcopal Church at Stamford.
