HAROLD FERRIS

ID: 1.6.1.1.3.1.1.2.5.2.2 | Source: John
(1.6.1.1.3.1.1.2.5.2.2) HAROLD FERRIS born April 13, 1911 and died Homossassa Springs FL; married 1st Irene {maiden name unk} who died in FL; married 2d name unk, sister of Irene; married 3d July 12, 1930 in Mt. Kisco NY Datie Johnson born September 1909 Glasgau, Scotland and died December 28, 1981 Long Island NY. Harold and Irene were cremated. I think the marriage order is reversed.
