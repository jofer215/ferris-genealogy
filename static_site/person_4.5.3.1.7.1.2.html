CHARLES BROWN LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.5.3.1.7.1.2 | Source: Mary
(4.5.3.1.7.1.2) CHARLES BROWN LOCKWOOD (#2241) born May 1, 1829 Mexico, Oswego, NY. At the age of 3 years, he went with his parents to Solon OH. He was the senior partner of the wholesale hardware firm of Lockwood, Taylor, & Co., at 110 & 112 Water Streets, Cleveland OH; married 1st March 4, 1856 Julia Hathaway [dau of Zephaniah & Fannie (Briggs) Hathaway] who died January 26, 1863; married 2d September 1864 Azora Varney [dau of William & Harty (Hathaway) Varney] who died August 24, 1884; married 3d February 5, 1885 Lucelia Varney, sister of Azora.
