HARRIET EMILY FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.7.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.7.4) HARRIET EMILY FERRIS born August 24, 1826 Stamford and died there May 20, 1896; married Allen Brown [son of David] born March 30, 1818 North Castle NY and died February 13, 1895 Stamford; he was a shoemaker 1863-95; both buried there Long Ridge Union Cemetery; and born to them were: (5.1.3.3.1.7.4.1) ANGELINE BROWN born October 27, 1850 Stanwich CT and died November 6, 1902 Stamford CT; married 1897 Timothy S Reynolds [son of Samuel Hoyt Reynolds and his 1st wife] born October 8, 1836 Stamford and died there August 11, 1914; both there buried Long Ridge Union Cemetery; no offspring
