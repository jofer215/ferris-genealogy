HIRAM F WETMORE

ID: 1.1.3.14.3 | Source: John
(1.1.3.14.3) HIRAM F WETMORE born January 13, 1808 Delaware Co NY and died November 13, 1872 Cornwallville, Greene, NY, 64y 10m; married Betsey Caroline Watrous [dau of Christopher & Lucy (Sears) Watrous] born August 9, 1814 Greene Co NY and died December 21, 1897 Cornwallville NY; both buried there Cornwallville Cemetery (Photo); in 1832-33, he was Collector for the Durham School District #4; 1843, 1853, 1858, 1866 on the Board of Trustees for District #4; and born to them were:
