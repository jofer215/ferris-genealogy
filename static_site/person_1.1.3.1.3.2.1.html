MARY

ID: 1.1.3.1.3.2.1 | Source: John
(1.1.3.1.3.2.1) MARY (LYDIA) FEARING (1.1.3.1.3.2.2) LILLIAN BLANCHE FEARING (Lillien) (Photo) born November 27, 1863 Davenport IA and died 1901; educated Iowa College, Vinton IA graduating 1884; 1888 moved to Chicago and entered the Union College of Law graduating spring 1890 the only woman in her class; practiced law in Chicago; author of 2 volumes of verse entitled The Sleeping World, and other Poems, 1887 and In the City by the Lake, 1892; stricken blind at age 8; age 12, she recovered her eyesight, but 4 years later a serious illness blinded her permanently; with her Mother's aid, she became an author and prominent lawyer in Chicago

No children recorded
