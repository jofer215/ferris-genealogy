HERMAN CHARLES WHITNEY

ID: 1.2.2.8.4.1.1.1 | Source: John
(1.2.2.8.4.1.1.1) HERMAN CHARLES WHITNEY born January 1, 1867 Summit Co OH and died February 6, 1920 St Louis MO; married Laura J Evance and born to them was: (1.2.2.8.4.1.1.1.1) KENNETH JOHN WHITNEY born February 6, 1904 Buchanan Co MO and died March 9, 1969 Poplar Bluff Co MO; married March 19, 1927 in St Louis, Ruby Kathleen Benfield [dau of Dennis & Cora Elminia (Chronister) Benfield] born August 21, 1911 Stoddard Co MO; and they had 3 kids – this was the 1st of 3 marriages for Ruby

No children recorded
