THEODORE W. UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.5.1.2.11 | Source: John
(1.2.3.5.1.2.11) THEODORE W. UNDERHILL (Theodore Reginald Underhill) born September 1857 NY (April 20, 1857 Jerusalem, Yates, NY) and died March 20, 1950 LeRoy, Genesee, NY; farmer at Bethany NY; married March 20, 1886 at Jerusalem, Narcissa Augusta Rogers [dau of Matthew & Elizabeth Buckingham (Simmons) Rogers] born June 3, 1851 Pavilion, Genesee, NY and died there April 27, 1926; and born to them were:
