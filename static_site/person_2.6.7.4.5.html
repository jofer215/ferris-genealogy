SAMUEL KNAPP

ID: 2.6.7.4.5 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.4.5) SAMUEL KNAPP born March 5, 1782 Litchfield CT and died December 18, 1862 Brownsville, Jefferson, NY; buried with his wife at the Brownsville Cemetery; married April 28, 1808 in Lewis Co NY Abiah Thompson born 1790 and died September 23, 1862 Brownsville.
