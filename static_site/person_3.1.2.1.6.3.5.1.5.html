ALFRED HOWARD STATES

ID: 3.1.2.1.6.3.5.1.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.6.3.5.1.5) ALFRED HOWARD STATES born May 7, 1875 Norwalk OH and died there January 26, 1945; buried Woodlawn Cemetery; a carpenter, fireman (drove hook and ladder when horses pulled it), had a moving business, bus and taxi business and dealt in horses; married April 17, 1901 at Toledo OH Rachel Josephine 'Susa' Taylor [dau of John Bernadot & Sarah Jane (Stanberry) (Veader) Taylor] born July 3, 1872 near Sandusky OH and died November 13, 1928 Norwalk; buried Woodlawn Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (2)

  • JUANITA AILEEN STATES 3.1.2.1.6.3.5.1.5.2
