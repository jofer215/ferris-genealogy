CHARLES WARREN UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.5.1.2.10.5 | Source: John
(1.2.3.5.1.2.10.5) CHARLES WARREN UNDERHILL born October 4, 1890 Boston MA and died May 31, 1976 Winooski VT; Yale Sheffield Scientific School, civil engineering Columbia, MA and High School Principal’s certficate; Master Mason High12 Club; Mathematics Professor; WWI; Chile Exploration Co; married June 26, 1926 in Boston, Miriam I. Ross [dau of J. Arthur & Amelia J. (Dove) Ross] born February 11, 1893 Boston. She was an art teacher (MA Normal Art School) and work at the MA Public Welfare Department.
