HARRY A. DITTO

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.4.2.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.4.2.1.1) HARRY A. DITTO born August 3, 1886 Petosky, Emmet, MI and died March 14, 1980 Shady Acres Nursing Home, Unionville, Lake, OH; buried Chester Township Cemetery, Chesterland, Geauga, OH; married May 13, 1916 in Geauga Co, Mary M. Wicks [dau of Harry and Nellie] born December 2, 1893 Chesterland and died October 27, 1988 Madison Health Care, Unionville OH; buried with Harry. He lived in Chesterland the last 51 years of his life; charter member of the Chesterland Baptist Church, former member of the Chesterland and Russell School Board nd member of the volunteer fire department. He was also active in the Chesterland Golden Agers, past grand marshall of the Fourth of July Parade and former Novelty postmaster. Along with his wife, he owned Harry’s and Mary’s Handy Store in Chesterland at the site of the Geauga Market from 1928 to 1947. Mary was a member of the Chesteland Baptist Church, where she was a former pianist and Sunday school teacher; also active in the Ladies Aid of the church; and charter member of the Blue Star Mothers of Chester and Chester Study Club. Her hobbies included sewing, baking and flower gardening. Born to them were:
