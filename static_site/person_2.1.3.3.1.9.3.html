JOHN HENRY FERRIS

ID: 2.1.3.3.1.9.3 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.1.9.3) JOHN HENRY FERRIS born March 5, 1836 Stamford CT and died there December 31, 1899; married Phebe Waters (Phoebe Hester Waters) born February 26, 1838 CT and died January 14, 1912. In 1860, John was a cooper. Both buried at the Long Ridge Cemetery, Stamford CT. Born to John and Phebe were:
