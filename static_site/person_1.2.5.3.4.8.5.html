LUCRETIA VANDERVOORT UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.3.4.8.5 | Source: John
(1.2.5.3.4.8.5) LUCRETIA VANDERVOORT UNDERHILL (Kitty) born June 21, 1848 Whitestone NY and died February 6, 1933 Rhinebeck NY; resided Rosemond (Rosemount?) IL 1868; Little Rest, Dutchess, NY 1871; Clinton, Dutchess, NY 1880; Rhinebeck, Dutchess, NY 1890; Quaker; Friends Academy; married June 10, 1868 in Chestnut Ridge NY, Stephen Jackson Thorn [son of Herrick & Sarah Ann (Hoag) Thorn] born June 8, 1845 Milan NY and died July 18, 1930 Rhinebeck; Mason, Society of Friends, Claverack Academy, medicine; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • SARAH ELIZABETH THORN 1.2.5.3.4.8.5.1
  • IDA UNDERHILL THORN 1.2.5.3.4.8.5.2
  • HERRICK THORN 1.2.5.3.4.8.5.3
