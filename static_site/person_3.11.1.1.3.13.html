WILLIAM W. NEWMAN

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.13 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.13) WILLIAM W. NEWMAN born December 7, 1812 Butternuts Township, Ostego Co NY and died March 25, 1899 Aurora IL; buried there West Aurora Cemetery; miller; operated a 222-acre farm in Sugar Grove IL; sold it and moved into Aurora and became a successful building contractor; married January 17, 1839 in Lewis Co NY, Laura L McCoy [dau of William] born c1820 and died February 23, 1903 Aurora; buried with William; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • JAMES D. NEWMAN 3.11.1.1.3.13.1
  • MALDEN C NEWMAN 3.11.1.1.3.13.2
  • LESTER A NEWMAN 3.11.1.1.3.13.3
  • JOHN B NEWMAN 3.11.1.1.3.13.4
