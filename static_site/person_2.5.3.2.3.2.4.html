HANFORD JUNE

ID: 2.5.3.2.3.2.4 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.2.3.2.4) HANFORD JUNE born 1807 Stamford and died there July 8, 1888; buried there Woodland Cemetery; carpenter 1850-60; married 1st 1828 Harriette Provost born October 28, 1809 Stamford and died there December 20, 1858; buried there North Street Cemetery; married 2d June 19, 1862 Stamford (October 2, 1860); married 3d February 7, 1884 in Stamford, Lydia Ann Lockwood born 1818 Stamford; and born to him and Harriette were:
