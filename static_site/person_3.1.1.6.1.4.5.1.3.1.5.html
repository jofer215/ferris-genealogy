BETTY JO STEVENS

ID: 3.1.1.6.1.4.5.1.3.1.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.1.4.5.1.3.1.5) BETTY JO STEVENS (Photo) born December 9, 1949 Kansas City MO; married July 15, 1972 Merl Dale Coleman [son of Merl Olando & Bessie Fern (Lowery) Coleman] born June 17, 1944 Lebanon MO - they divorced and then remarried March 19, 1981; Betty Jo attended the Kansas City public grade and high school, spending her last year at the Missouri School for the Deaf; began her insurance career with Gilbert-Magill Insurance Agency, and is now employed by the AFCO Credit Corporation; Dale attended the Missouri School for the Deaf in Fulton MO where he learned to be a pressor; mechanically minded and currently works for the Jones Store in downtown Kansas City

No children recorded
