HENRY HANFORD MILBURN

ID: 3.1.6.8.5.5.2.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.5.5.2.1) HENRY HANFORD MILBURN born July 15, 1869 Bristol, Elkhart, IN and died 1943; clerk/cashier with Milburn Gas & Machinery Co, Toledo OH; 1890-91 resided Memphis TN and evidently moved to Seattle prior to 1905; married 1st June 11, 1902 Anna Thomsen; married 2d before 1920 Laura Ryder; and born to him and Anna were:
