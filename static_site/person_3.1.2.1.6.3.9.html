JOSHUA WEYBURN

ID: 3.1.2.1.6.3.9 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.6.3.9) JOSHUA WEYBURN born November 22, 1821 Ulysses NY and died January 9, 1906/07; removed to Middlebury IN, later South Bend IN (1892); married November 18, 1847 at Fairfield, Huron, OH Angeline A. States who died March 11, 1903; buried North Fairfield Cemetery

No children recorded
