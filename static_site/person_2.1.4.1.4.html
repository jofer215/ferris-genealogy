JAMES FERRIS was first settler on the east side of Lackawanna

ID: 2.1.4.1.4 | Source: Peter
(2.1.4.1.4) JAMES FERRIS was first settler on the east side of Lackawanna (what is now Olyphant PA) and built a cabin in 1798; married lady’s name unk, but born to them were:

Children (4)

  • SARAH FERRIS 2.1.4.1.4.2
  • JOHN FERRIS 2.1.4.1.4.3
