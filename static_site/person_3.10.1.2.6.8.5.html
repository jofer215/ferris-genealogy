MARIA PECK

ID: 3.10.1.2.6.8.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.6.8.5) MARIA PECK born April 22, 1839 Clifton Park NY; was living there 1870; and died December 26, 1914 Saratoga NY; buried Baptist Church Cemetery, Clifton Park; married November 1858 in Clifton Park, John Morehouse Clark [son of Cyrus & Nancy (Morehouse) Clark] born July 21, 1830 Saratoga NY and died there May 15, 1907; farmer; and born to them were:
