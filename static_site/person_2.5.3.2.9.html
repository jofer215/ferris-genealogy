THANKFUL SCOFIELD

ID: 2.5.3.2.9 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.2.9) THANKFUL SCOFIELD born March 18, 1750 Stamford and died December 27, 1825; married Enos Miller [son of Jonathan & Jersuah (Roberts) Miller III] born 1750 Bedford NY and died there c1803, his will is dated November 13, 1803; and born to them were: (2.5.3.2.9.1) JOSEPH MILLER
