GEORGIANNA MILLINGTON BISHOP

ID: 5.1.3.4.3.1.1.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.3.1.1.1.2) GEORGIANNA MILLINGTON BISHOP (Photograph) was the 1904 US Women's Amateur Golfing Champion {maybe the first?}; a member of the Brooklawn Club, Bridgeport CT; made the first informal visit to the British Isles to play team matches and to compete in the British Championship - never married; buried Mountain Grove Cemetery, Bridgeport CT

No children recorded
