HANNAH KNAPP

ID: 2.6.5.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.5.3) HANNAH KNAPP born March 29, 1736 Stamford and died there July 7, 1810; married 1st December 24, 1755 at Stamford, John Klock Jr [son of John & Debro (Scofield) Klock] born August 8, 1731 Stamford and died August 4, 1776 (or August 19, 1791?); married 2d at Stamford, Enos Lockwood (4.2.7.1).
