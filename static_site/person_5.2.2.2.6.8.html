JAMES NEWMAN BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.2.6.8 | Source: James
(5.2.2.2.6.8) JAMES NEWMAN BUXTON born August 15, 1813 Stamford CT and died there April 11, 1891; buried Roxbury Rural Cemetery, Stamford; a farmer; married 1st April 11, 1844 in Stamford, Anna Smith born March 15, 1811 Stamford and died there September 15, 1866; buried Roxbury Rural Cemetery; 2d November 28, 1867 in Stamford, (Sarah A. June) Sarah H. June [dau of Ezra & Sarah (Hait) June] born March 12, 1819 Stamford and died there October 13, 1892; buried Roxbury Rural Cemetery. Born to him and Anna were:

Children (3)

  • MARY CLARA BUXTON 5.2.2.2.6.8.1
  • JAMES LEWIS BUXTON 5.2.2.2.6.8.2
  • GEORGE P. BUXTON died September 3, 1890 5.2.2.2.6.8.3
