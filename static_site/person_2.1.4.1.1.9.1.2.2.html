ALBERT EUGENE FERRIS

ID: 2.1.4.1.1.9.1.2.2 | Source: Peter
(2.1.4.1.1.9.1.2.2) ALBERT EUGENE FERRIS born May 7, 1885 Madison WI and died January 26, 1967 Verona WI; married February 2, 1906 in Chicago IL, Mary Isabel Butler born January 20, 1888 Waukesha WI and died June 6, 1966 Verona WI. He was a resident of Madison & Mazomanie WI; died in a nursing home in Verona; buried Forest Hill Cemetery - employed by the Milwaukee Railroad and member of the Brotherhood of Railway Trainmen.
