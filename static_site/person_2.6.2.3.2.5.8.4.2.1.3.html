ALBERTA ELIZABETH TRAVIS

ID: 2.6.2.3.2.5.8.4.2.1.3 | Source: Peter

Alberta married 2d December 19, 1953 at Mineral Wells TX Wendell Lamar Lacy born January 18, 1933 Lanet AL. Born to them were:
