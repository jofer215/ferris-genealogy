SILVANUS SCOFIELD

ID: 2.5.3.2.11.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.2.11.2) SILVANUS SCOFIELD born September 25, 1785 Stamford and died October 16, 1870 Darien CT; buried Newfield Cemetery, Stamford; married 1st June 15, 1807 Stamford, Sally Dibble born June 12, 1781 Danbury CT and died June 14, 1816 Stamford; married 2d November 19, 1816 Patty Lyon born July 19, 1790 Bedford NY and died June 21, 1862 Darien CT; buried Newfield Cemetery, Stamford; married 3d February 4, 1863 Anna Elizabeth Easton (1810-1867); and married 4th January 14, 1868 Julia Graham (1808-); and born to him and Sally were:
