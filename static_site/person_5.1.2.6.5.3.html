PHEBE FERRIS

ID: 5.1.2.6.5.3 | Source: James
(5.1.2.6.5.3) PHEBE FERRIS born June 7, 1807 Penn Yan, Yates, NY; married 1st c1827 in Steuben Co NY, David Thomas; 2d c1836 in Steuben Co, Nehemiah Solomon (Salmon). Phebe and Henry Ferris bought 2 farms on Lent Hill from the Land Office in Bath. Lent Hill is 3 miles from Cohocton NY. Born to Phebe and Nehemiah was:
