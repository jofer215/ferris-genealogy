KEITH WILLIAM FERRIS

ID: 2.1.4.1.3.3.4.2.9 | Source: Peter
(2.1.4.1.3.3.4.2.9) KEITH WILLIAM FERRIS born January 25, 1923 and died April 2, 1992; American Legion marker, WWII; buried Osceola Grove Cemetery, Stark Co IL; married March 25, 1950 at Lacon IL, Emily June Whitehead born March 19, 1915 and died January 14, 1997; buried with Keith. She was also married to Jame Enos – don’t know which marriage came first.

No children recorded
