GERSHOM LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.5 | Source: Mary
(4.5) GERSHOM LOCKWOOD (#13) (K7FP-WX) (3d born) [sometimes referred to as Gersham] born c1676 [LDS says 1667] Greenwich and died May 1752/57(before March 22, 1762); admitted a freeman February 7, 1697; May 1726 he was Deputy for Greenwich to the Colonial Assembly of Connecticut. He was Justice of the Peace for Fairfield, 1726-1728; Deputy for Greenwich 1749-50; married c1701 Hannah (Mary) Weed born April 21, 1684(?); 1717 he was of Cos Cob, Greenwich. Born to them were:
