SAMUEL PEARL LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.5.8.4.3.8.4.3 | Source: Mary
(4.5.8.4.3.8.4.3) SAMUEL PEARL LOCKWOOD Jr born January 28, 1906 Portland OR and died there October 8/19, 1972; buried there Sunset Memorial Gardens; married 1st August 15, 1934 in Portland OR, Margaret Spencer (Peggy) born August 17, 1906 Portland OR – divorced c1943 – and died June 20, 1960 Portland; buried Spencer Plot, Riverview Cemetery; Portland; married 2d December 20, 1957 in Multnomah Co OR, Anita Cluff [dau of Claude A. & Florence (Barth) Cluff] born July 22, 1921 and died April 8, 1960 Portland; buried Sunset Memorial Gardens; married 3d Miss Killinger; 1 child born to 1st marriage; 5 kids born to 2d marriage; and 1 child born to 3d marriage

No children recorded
