ANNA FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.5 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.5) ANNA FERRIS born November 26, 1771 and her death was reported in a Warren Co OH newspaper notice on July 6, 1811; married 1793/4 Benjamin Stites III [son of Benjamin & Rachel (Waldon) Stites] born February 14/19, 1772 near Redstone PA (Berkley Co VA) and died February 18, 1846 Warren Co OH. Benjamin married, second, Mary Drake, and third, Mary (?or Hannah?) Ferris (see 4.3.8.7.2). Born to them were:
