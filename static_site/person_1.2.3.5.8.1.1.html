EDWARD DEZENG WHEELER

ID: 1.2.3.5.8.1.1 | Source: John
(1.2.3.5.8.1.1) EDWARD DEZENG WHEELER born 1843 Brooklyn NY and died October 27, 1904 Jamestown NY; married March 21, 1869 in Brooklyn, Charlotte Eugenia Whiting born August 5, 1849 and died July 25, 1912 Byron NY. Born to them were: (1.2.3.5.8.1.1.1) HOBART WHITING WHEELER born January 20, 1870 Buffalo NY and died there March 24, 1939; married October 16, 1901 in Buffalo, Minnie Adele Belger born December 25, 1876 Akron OH and died November 9, 1970 Williamsville NY.
