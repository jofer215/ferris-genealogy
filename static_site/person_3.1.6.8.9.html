RACHEL ROSE

ID: 3.1.6.8.9 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.9) RACHEL ROSE born January 4, 1783 Williamstown, Glengarry, Ontario; baptized there, February 28, 1793, and died September 23, 1859 in Westmeath Township, Renfrew County, Ontario. She married Samuel Adams (1782-1860). Samuel was in the lumber business in Wesmeath Township. No offspring; adopted a girl who lived with them.

No children recorded
