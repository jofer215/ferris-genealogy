ANN PELL

ID: 1.4.8.4 | Source: John

Children (7)

  • HANNAH LAWRENCE 1.4.8.4.2

  • BALTUS VAN KLEECK, JR 1.4.8.4.4
  • JACOBA VAN KLEECK ? 1.4.8.4.5
  • ELIZABETH VAN KLEECK ? 1.4.8.4.6
  • LAWRENCE VAN KLEECK ? married name unk but born to them were: 1.4.8.4.7
