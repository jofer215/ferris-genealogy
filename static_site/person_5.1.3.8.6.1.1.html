EMMA JANE FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.6.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.1.1) EMMA JANE FERRIS born February 25, 1848 (or October 15, 1849) DeWitt NY and died May 6, 1926 Syracuse NY; married November 11, 1875 Charles Lester Meade born 1848 and died December 24, 1917 Syracuse [son of George Meade of Fayetteville]. Born to them were: (5.1.3.8.6.1.1.1) FREDERICK FERRIS MEADE born October 30, 1877 Syracuse NY and died there May 16, 1942; in the automobile business; married Florence May Corwin and born to them was:

Children (2)

  • EVA CORNELIA MEADE 5.1.3.8.6.1.1.3
