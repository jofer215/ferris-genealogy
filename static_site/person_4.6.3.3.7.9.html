LYDIA AMELIA LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.3.3.7.9 | Source: Mary
(4.6.3.3.7.9) LYDIA AMELIA LOCKWOOD born July 29, 1837 Stamford and died there June 3, 1885; buried there Long Ridge Cemetery; married 1st c1857 a Mr. Finch; 2d c1863 Alonzo Dixon born February 16, 1836 NY; and born to her and Alonzo were:

Children (6)

  • CHARLES DIXON 4.6.3.3.7.9.1
  • LORENZO W. DIXON 4.6.3.3.7.9.2
  • ARTHUR L. DIXON 4.6.3.3.7.9.3
  • EVA F. DIXON 4.6.3.3.7.9.4
  • LYDIA DIXON 4.6.3.3.7.9.5
  • ELLA DIXON died June 29, 1865 age 3y, 4m, 4d; buried Long Ridge Cemetery, 4.6.3.3.7.9.6
