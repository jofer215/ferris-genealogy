GERSHOM BATES

ID: 2.3.3.1.2 | Source: Peter
(2.3.3.1.2) GERSHOM BATES born November 26, 1772 Stamford and died there 1822; married April 17, 1803 at Charleston MA Hannah Buckman [dau of Nathan & Elizabeth (Stowers) Bucknam] born August 19/28, 1785 Malden, Middlesex, MA and died September 10, 1858 Stamford; buried there Long Ridge Cemetery; she remarried after Gershom’s death; and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • LEWIS HARVEY BATES 2.3.3.1.2.1
  • SAMUEL GERSHOM BATES 2.3.3.1.2.2
  • MARTHA SMITH BATES 2.3.3.1.2.4
  • WILLIAM HENRY BATES 2.3.3.1.2.5
  • HANNAH ELIZABETH BATES 2.3.3.1.2.6
