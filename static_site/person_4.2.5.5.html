GILBERT LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.2.5.5 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.5) GILBERT LOCKWOOD (#183a) (Captain) born (1742) 1747 Greenwich and died August 9, 1836 (Olive, Ulster, NY); married 1768 Phebe Hall born 1752 and died November 8, 1833. He was commissioned a Captain, September 20, 1775, of the 3d Regiment of the North Battalion, Westchester County NY. Born to them were:
