DAVID HARRIS GODFREY

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.3.3) DAVID HARRIS GODFREY born October 29, 1813 Bloomingburg, Sullivan, NY and died January 7, 1895 Calhoun Co MI; buried there Marshall; farmer 1850, retired farmer 1880, Justice of the Peace in Marshall; married November 24, 1836 Isabel McCrory (Isabella) born c1816 Ireland and died January 20, 1892 Calhoun Co MI; buried with David; and born to them were:
