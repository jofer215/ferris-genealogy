JOHN BARNES UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.4.2.4 | Source: John
(1.2.3.4.2.4) JOHN BARNES UNDERHILL (Joshua Barnes Underhill) (1793-) resided 1850 Fishkill, Dutchess, NY; married Elizabeth Hannah Secor; and born to them were: (1.2.3.4.2.4.1) HENRY MORRIS UNDERHILL (1816-1892) in 1850 was a tinsmith, Liberty NY; at one point resided Lakewood NJ; married 1843 Sarah Bradbury (c1825-); and born to them were:
