CLARA ESTHER KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.3.2.5.8.4.2.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.2.5.8.4.2.3) CLARA ESTHER KNAPP born January 26, 1910 Barrington NY and died December 1999; married 1st August 12, 1930 at Dundee NY, Charles Adelbert McCarrick born August 27, 1906 Branchport NY and died April 4, 1970 Dundee; married 2d November 26, 1975 at Pearl River NY, Leighton Lamphier Shaw born July 3, 1906 Boonton NJ and died September 26, 1987 Dundee. Clara taught school at the little schoolhouse mentioned above. Both husbands were buried at Hillside Cemetery, Dundee. No offspring.

No children recorded
