WILLIAM LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.1.2.4.6 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.4.6) WILLIAM LOCKWOOD (#1385) born September 14, 1800 Pound Ridge NY and died November 15, 1880, Bedford NY, where he resided; married 1st December 6, 1826 Maria J. Crocker [dau of Rev. Daniel & Anna (Austin) Crocker] born at New Haven CT and died December 14, 1843; buried Pound Ridge Cemetery, Pound Ridge NY; they were married by the Rev. Crocker. William married 2d April 29, 1847 Jane Raymond [dau of Joshua & Reuhuma], of Bedford, who died January 25, 1858; married by the Right Rev. William Heathcote De Lancy, Bishop of the Protestant Episcopal Diocese of Western New York; William married 3d January 2, 1860, Rebecca Raymond [dau of Uriah & Sarah] who died September 9, 1875.
