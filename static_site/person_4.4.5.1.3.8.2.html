INGERSOLL LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.5.1.3.8.2 | Source: Mary
(4.4.5.1.3.8.2) INGERSOLL LOCKWOOD (#2492) born August 2, 1841 Ossining NY and in 1888, resided at 499 Fifth Avenue, NYC; business address was 128 Duane Street, NYC; married November 14, 1884 in NYC, Winifred Wallace Tinker [dau of Henry Franklin & Elizabeth M. (Boss) Tinker] born July 8, 1862 Erie PA; lawyer; for four years, U.S. Counsel for the Kingdom of Hanover; a well-known lecturer

No children recorded
