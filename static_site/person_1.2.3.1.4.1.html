BRITISH NATHANIEL UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.1.4.1 | Source: John
(1.2.3.1.4.1) BRITISH NATHANIEL UNDERHILL Jr born June 22, 1773 and died October 9, 1850 Blackville, Northumberland, New Brunswich; buried there Anglican Cemetery; married September 25, 1794 Maugerville Anglican Church, as her 2d husband, Abigail (Balch) Christy [dau of Freeborn & Mary (Perkins) Balch] born August 19, 1768 Beverley MA and died August 7, 1842 Underhill, Northumberland; buried Anglican Cemetery, Blackville; she brought 2 sons from her first marriage; and she and B.Nat had 8 kids.

No children recorded
