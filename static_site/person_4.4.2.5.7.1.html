WILLIAM EDWARD LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.2.5.7.1 | Source: Mary
(4.4.2.5.7.1) WILLIAM EDWARD LOCKWOOD (#1492) born October 7, 1832 Ashtabula OH; 1889 resided at Loch-Erie, Glen-Loch, Chester Co PA; married 1st June 5, 1860 at Wilmington DE, Louisa Virginia Bush [dau of George & Anna (Steele) Bush] born August 21, 1840 and died May 31, 1868. George Bush was President of the Bank of Wilmington and Brandywine for many years. William married 2d February 7, 1874 at Philadelphia, Ella Eby [dau of Ephraim C. & Sarah E. (Faunce) Eby.
