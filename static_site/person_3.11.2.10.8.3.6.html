ASA SMITH HAWLEY

ID: 3.11.2.10.8.3.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.2.10.8.3.6) ASA SMITH HAWLEY born June 30, 1835 Yarmouth, London, Upper Canada and died January 24, 1917 Inverury (Central), Sevier, UT; buried there; married 1st November 15, 1854 in Pleasant Grove UT, Mary Beers [dau of William & Susannah (Gazey) Beers] born June 27, 1834 Banbury, Oxford, England and died August 2, 1909 Inverury UT; (Asa either divorced, separated, or had 2 or 3 wives – 1880 census has wife Mary as 45y which fits Mary (Beers) dob; 1900 census lists Mary as having had 13 kids with 8 still alive (this has to be 1st wife Mary (Beers)); 1910 census lists Asa with son, Earl and wife Jennie (c1884-) and their children, Vivian 6y, Dwain 4y, Lester 2y, and Keith 1m – thanks, John); married 2d January 30, 1871 in Salt Lake City, Mary Priscilla Stewart (maybe they didn’t marry); and born to Asa and Mary Beers were:
