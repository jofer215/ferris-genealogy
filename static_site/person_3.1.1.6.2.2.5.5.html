GEORGE MONROE HAMMEL

ID: 3.1.1.6.2.2.5.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.2.2.5.5) GEORGE MONROE HAMMEL born March 6, 1855 Jefferson Co IN and died April 15, 1919; buried Springdale Cemetery, Madison, Jefferson, IN; married April 9, 1883 in Madison, Ida Shockley [dau of William & Eliza Shockley] born December 24, 1870 Madison and died February 14, 1931 Chicago IL, where she was buried (interesting she is not buried in the same cemetery as her husband and numerous children); and born to them were:
