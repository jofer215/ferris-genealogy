HERMAN WALDRON

ID: 5.1.2.6.10.4.3.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.6.10.4.3.1) HERMAN WALDRON (might have been Herman Leroy Waldron) born July 8, 1875 Surprise NY and died November 18, 1958 Purling NY; married February 13, 1897 in Greennville NY, Jennie May Barlow born August 25, 1855 and died September 1, 1914 Surprise; after the death of his young wife, Herman placed all of the sons with other families and built a small cabin on the ResultSurprise road across from the Waldron family cemetery and lived alone there until c1952; and born to them were:

Children (4)
