THOMAS JEFFERSON LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.1.5.5.6.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.5.5.6.1) THOMAS JEFFERSON LOCKWOOD (#2298) born April 16, 1827 Olean NY; removing in 1851 to Buda IL (farmer), where he resided; presumably was deceased by 1889. Thomas enlisted in Company C, 93d Illinois Infantry Regiment; a Second Lieutenant and had both eyes shot out at the battle of Champion Hills MS, May 16, 1863; married January 3, 1874 Ann Baxter [dau of Eber & Mary (Roberts) Baxter] born February 5, 1837 Wheatland NY. No offspring.

No children recorded
