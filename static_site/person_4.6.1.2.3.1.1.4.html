CHARLES PORTER COFFIN

ID: 4.6.1.2.3.1.1.4 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.3.1.1.4) CHARLES PORTER COFFIN (#3335) born April 23, 1858 Batavia IL; married January 1886 at Escanaba MI, Adaline Selden. Charles graduated from Yale 1880 and was in the railroad business in Chicago.

No children recorded
