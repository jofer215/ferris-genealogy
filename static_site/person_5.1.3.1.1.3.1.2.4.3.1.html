BRYON HARTLEY SPENCER

ID: 5.1.3.1.1.3.1.2.4.3.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.1.3.1.2.4.3.1) BRYON HARTLEY SPENCER (Bud) born September 27, 1913 Gray’s Ridge, Stoddard, MO and died December 8, 1976 Poplar Bluff, Butler, MO; between 1934-54 barrel stave manufacturer, Poplar Bluff Stave Co, Poplar Bluff MO and B.E. Spencer Cooperage Co, Pocahontas AR; married April 17, 1937 in Poplar Bluff, Katherine May Reed [dau of Lee Alpheus & Susie (Turner) Reed] born March 29, 1914 Garden City, Cass, MO and died October 4, 2001 Poplar Bluff; both buried City Cemetery, Poplar Bluff (5.1.3.1.1.3.1.2.4.3.2) RAYMOND REES SPENCER born March 9, 1917 Gray’s Ridge MO and died July 5, 1974 Memphis TN; married in Pocahontas AR, Thelma Lucille Wiley born May 4, 1922 Pocahontas
