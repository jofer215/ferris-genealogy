JOHN JESSUP BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.2.9.5.3 | Source: James
(5.2.2.2.9.5.3) JOHN JESSUP BUXTON born June 4, 1834 Stamford C T and died there January 11, 1909; buried Turn of River Cemetery, Stamford; a farmer and shoemaker; married December 7, 1865 at Stamford, Mary Ann Weed (Mary Augusta) [dau of Benjamin & Deborah A. (Jones) Weed] born November 27, (1845) 1847 Stamford (New Canaan) and died there January 7, 1894 (1893); buried Turn of River Cemetery. Born to them were:
