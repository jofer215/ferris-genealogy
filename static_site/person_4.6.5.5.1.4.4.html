DE WITT C.

ID: 4.6.5.5.1.4.4 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.5.1.4.4) DE WITT C. (CALEB?) HOYT (#5857) born August 25, 1824 Greenfield NY and was living there 1871; De Witt was supervisor of the town of Greenfield several times; President of the Saratoga County Agricultural Society, and a member of the New York Legislature in 1869. He married, April 4, 1844, Cynthia A. Smith and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • ALICE HOYT 4.6.5.5.1.4.4.1
  • ISAAC W. HOYT 4.6.5.5.1.4.4.2
  • IDA M. HOYT 4.6.5.5.1.4.4.3
