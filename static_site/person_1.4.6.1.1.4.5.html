CAROLINE M. COLEMAN

ID: 1.4.6.1.1.4.5 | Source: John
(1.4.6.1.1.4.5) CAROLINE M. COLEMAN born October 19, 1872 Sheldrake, Seneca, NY and died May 12, 1906 Ithaca, Tompkins, NY; buried Interlaken; married September 14, 1893, as his first wife, Wakeman Ryno Auble, Sr. [son of Thomas W. & Phoebe C. (Vining) Auble] born December 12, 1876 Townsendville, Schuyler, NY and died August 7, 1957 Rochester, Monroe, NY; buried Trumansburg Grove Cemetery, Ulysses NY. After Caroline’s death, Wakeman remarried to Susan Pettit and they had 2 kids. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • HOWARD THOMAS AUBLE 1.4.6.1.1.4.5.1
  • RAYMOND COLEMAN AUBLE 1.4.6.1.1.4.5.2
