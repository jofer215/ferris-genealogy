PETER SHERMAN was baptized November 28, 1762 at Christ Church, Salem NY and died

ID: 3.1.6.2.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.2.5) PETER SHERMAN was baptized November 28, 1762 at Christ Church, Salem NY and died April 1848 Seneca Co NY; buried Sheldrake Cemetery; a Rev. War Vet; “Uncle Peter” started the Methodist Episcopal Church, Sheldake NY; married Martha Hait “Aunt Patty”; thanks to Karl and Sue Kramer who have done research and provided information on this “twig” – Thanks Folks!

No children recorded
