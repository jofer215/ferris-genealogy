ALEXANDER SMITH

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.2.5 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.2.5) ALEXANDER SMITH born c1864 Putnam Valley NY and died October 29, 1927; married Annie Augusta Rhoades [dau of Samuel & Antha Jane (Cargain)]. Obituary, Danbury Times, Danbury, Conn., Monday, Oct. 31, 1927: “Alexander Smith, of 20 Summit street, died Saturday afternoon at his home, following an illness which had made him practically an invalid for the past three years. He born in Peekskill Hollow, NY, sixty-three years ago, and was a painter by trade. He is survived by three sons, Louis Smith, of Lake Mahopac, NY; Samuel Smith, of Tarrytown, NY; and James R. Smith, of Danbury; also by four daughters, Mrs. William Baldwin, Mrs. Jesse Adams and Mrs. Edward Muehlfeld, of Danbury; and Mrs. Louis Ferguson, of Irvington-on-the-Hudson, NY. The funeral will be held at the Baptist church at Carmel, NY, tomorrow afternoon.” Born to them were:
