JOTHAM HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.11.4.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.11.4.2) JOTHAM HOYT (#3570) born March 30, 1793 Stamford and died September 20, 1857 NYC; buried North Stamford Cemetery; lived in North Stamford until 1843 when he moved to New York. He was a leading man in Stamford; was selectman for 6 years and represented that town in the General Assembly. Jotham married January 4, 1817 in Stamford, Julia Ann Davenport [dau of John & Prudence (Bell) Daven port] born November 30, 1794 Stamford and died there July 2, 1868; buried North Stamford Cemetery; and born to them were:
