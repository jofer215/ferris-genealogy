ISAAC FERRIS HART

ID: 2.1.3.3.1.4.7.2 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.1.4.7.2) ISAAC FERRIS HART born April 22, 1836 Sandusky OH and died November 28, 1913 State of Washington; married May 20, 1866 in Mantorville, Dodge, MN Jane Miller [dau of Abraham & Miami (Price) Miller] born 1847 Bradford PA. They lived in Ellendale ND and Jane was an inmate in the North Dakota Asylum for the insane. Born to them were:
