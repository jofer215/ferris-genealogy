STEPHEN LOUNSBURY

ID: 4.7.8.5 | Source: Mary
(4.7.8.5) STEPHEN LOUNSBURY (Phineas Stephen) (Lounsberry) born February 3, 1758 Bedford, Westchester, NY and died there December 3, 1839; buried there Lounsbury Family Cemetery (on a farm situated on the road from Bedford to Mt Kisco NY); Rev War Vet, Private, served December 1776 for 3 months under Captain Marcus Moseman, Colonel Thomas; September 1777 for one month under the same; and three months under Captain Olmsted, Colonel Thomas; February 1779 for two months under Captain John Thomas; in 1779 for eighteen months under Captain Moseman, Colonel Thomas; and in 1782 for six months under Captain Thomas Hunt, Colonel Weisenfeldt; married November 1783 at Bedford, Sally/Sarah Raymond born February 28, 1765 and died February 27, 1852 Bedford; buried with Stephen; and born to them were (different surname spelling from different sources – see note at top of chapter!):
