JEREMIAH LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.5.3.1 | Source: Mary
(4.5.3.1) JEREMIAH LOCKWOOD (#209) born c1733 Greenwich and died November 1786; enlisted September 15, 1779 as a Private in Captain Thorpe’s Company of Colonel Duckees’ 4th Connecticut Regiment; member of Congregationalist Church, Stamford; married January 8, 1758 Abigail Smith [dau of Gabriel & Mary (Bell) Smith][Mary Bell is a half-sister of (5.2)] born March 1, 1738/39. Born to them were:

Children (7)

  • HENRY LOCKWOOD 4.5.3.1.2
  • JEREMIAH LOCKWOOD, 4.5.3.1.3
  • EBENEZER LOCKWOOD 4.5.3.1.4
  • ABRAHAM LOCKWOOD 4.5.3.1.5
  • ABIGAIL LOCKWOOD 4.5.3.1.6
  • JAMES LOCKWOOD 4.5.3.1.7
