ABIGAIL WATERBURY

ID: 3.6.5.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.5.1) ABIGAIL WATERBURY born (July 29, 1737) December 8, 1737/38 Stamford CT and died April 2, 1777 at Noroton CT. She married, January 25, 1766 at Stamford CT, Daniel Gorham [son of George & Hannah (Banks)] born May 17, 1737 Rye NY and died January 25, 1820 at Noroton CT. After Abigail’s death, Daniel married Jane Bates and they had 9 kids. Born to Abigail and Daniel were:

Children (4)

  • JOHN GORHAM 3.6.5.1.2
  • DANIEL GORHAM 3.6.5.1.3
  • HANNAH GORHAM 3.6.5.1.4
