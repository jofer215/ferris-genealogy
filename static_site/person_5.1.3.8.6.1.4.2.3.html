ROBERT SHERMAN MERRILL, JR.,

ID: 5.1.3.8.6.1.4.2.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.1.4.2.3) ROBERT SHERMAN MERRILL, JR., born October 26, 1916; entered Army 1940; Chief Warrant Officer - radar instructor at Fort Bliss TX 1956; married Mary Kimman (5.1.3.8.6.1.4.3) ALBERT BACON MERRILL born December 10, 1888 Fabius NY; married September 16, 1913 Rena Stiles Wicks [dau of Dr. Parke W.]; Albert was president of First Trust and Deposit Bank, Syracuse; member of Board of Trustees, Syracuse University; honorary LLD, Syracuse University, 1955; active in banking interests, Chamber of Commerce, Community Chest and many other civic affairs. An Albert B Merrill, Republican, was a Presidental Elector for NY 1952; there is an Albert B Merrill Memorial Fund at the Maxwell School of Public Administration, Syracuse U. Born to them were:

No children recorded
