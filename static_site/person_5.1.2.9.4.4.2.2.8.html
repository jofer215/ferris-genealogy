FRANK TOMPKINS

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.2.2.8 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.2.2.8) FRANK TOMPKINS (1888-) married Alice Tompkins [dau of Ransom & Hannah (Sutton)]. (Alice had a brother, Clarence that died 1987; he had an Estate Probated in Putnam Co., NY, in 1988. It had the following information: Had only one sister, Alice Tompkins who died at the age of 40 leaving the following children: 1. Edna Tompkins Maurice, now deceased; 2. Edith Anderson, now deceased; 3. Marion McGill, the petitioner; 4. Howard Tompkins, now deceased; 5. Margarite Jones of South Caroling; 6. Phyllis Tompkins, now deceased; 7. Virginia Costuk, now deceased; 8. Eleanor Benjamin, now deceased; 9. Earl Tompkins of Mahopac, NY; 10. Walter Tompkins of Mahopac Falls, NY; 11. Joan D'Angelo of Beacon, NY; 12. Barbara Schroll of Wappinger Falls, NY) Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, Dec. 1937: Mrs. Alice Tompkins; wife of Frank Tompkins, and sister-in-law of Mrs. Allen G. N. Gilbert, died last Friday morning, Dec. 3, 1937, in the Peekskill hospital following a short illness of pneumonia. She was 43 years old. Mrs. Tompkins born in Jefferson Valley and had spent her entire life in that section and in recent years had lived on Wood Street. Surviving besides her husband are twelve children; Edna, Edith, Marion, Howard, Marguerite, Phyllis, Virginia, Eleanor, Earl, Walter, Joan and Barbara. She is also survived by her parents, Ransom and Hannah Tompkins, and one brother, Clarence. Interment was made in the Ballard-Barrett Cemetery. Born to them were:
