SARAH ANN KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.3.7.6 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.7.6) SARAH ANN KNAPP (Sally) born August 27, 1798 at Canterbury NY and died March 23, 1825 (1823?); married, October 7, 1820, probably at New Windsor, Joseph Lawrence and born to them, as their only child, was: (2.6.2.3.7.6.1) JAMES HARVEY LAWRENCE born April 12, 1820 Canterbury NY and died August 29, 1913 Walden, Marion, NY; married May 15, 1842 at Crawford NY, Cornelia Ann Milles (or Wilkes?) who died May 29, 1901 Bullville NY.
