EDITH MELLICENT HARTWELL

ID: 2.1.3.3.2.4.1.2.2 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.2.4.1.2.2) EDITH MELLICENT HARTWELL born May 25, 1874 Honolulu HI and died 1964; married October 12, 1895 in Honolulu, Alfred Wellington Carter (A.W.) [son of Samuel & Harriet (Layman) Carter] born April 22, 1867 and died 1949; need to read Loyal to the Land, The Legendary Parker Ranch 750-1950 by Dr. Billy Bergin to learn about this interesting man. Born to them were 4 kids, 3 of which were:

Children (3)

  • ALFRED HARTWELL CARTER 2.1.3.3.2.4.1.2.2.1
  • EDITH MELLICENT CARTER “Edie” born February 17, 1898; married H Ernest 2.1.3.3.2.4.1.2.2.2
  • DOROTHY LAYMAN CARTER 2.1.3.3.2.4.1.2.2.3
