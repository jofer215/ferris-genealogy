BENJAMIN BLAKENEY

ID: 1.1.3.1.4.6.3 | Source: John
(1.1.3.1.4.6.3) BENJAMIN BLAKENEY (Benji) (Photos) born February 21, 1872 San Francisco CA and died April 28, 1928 Fresno CA; laborer, single (1.1.3.1.4.7) LYDIA AMEILA BLAKENEY born August 6, 1835 Peekskill; married 1855 Alexander Henry Coulter, of NYC, where they lived a good portion of their married life, and then removed to Brooklyn; Alexander born 1828 NYC and died June 19, 1902 Brooklyn; both buried Greenwood. Alexander was a devotee to Free Masonry, have been elected 12 consecutive times as Worshipful Master of the George Washington Lodge No. 1, of NYC. Born to them were:

No children recorded
