ELIZABETH FERRIS

ID: 2.1.5 | Source: Peter
(2.1.5) ELIZABETH FERRIS born March 19 (29?), 1698/9 Stamford CT and died December 11, 1766 Salem, Westchester, NY; married December 25, 1723 in Stamford, Joseph Purdy (Pardee) Jr born (August) October 9, 1693 New Haven CT and died 1762 Stamford. One source has Joseph Purdy born 1704, son of Francis and Sarah (Brown) Purdy. Born to them were:
