JONATHAN HAIT

ID: 4.6.5.5.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.5.1) JONATHAN HAIT (#3394) (Deacon) born 1761 Pound Ridge, Westchester, NY and died June 20, 1826 Saratoga Co NY; married 1st Chloe Miller born 1758 and died September 27, 1815 Saratoga Co age 57y; 2d Hannah Taylor born c1768 and died August 31, 1834 age 66y; resided Greenfield NY. Born to Jonathan and Chloe were (note the name change): (4.6.5.5.1.1) ELIZABETH HOYT (#4359) born c1785 and died October 4, 1828 Greenfield, Saratoga, NY where she had lived with her husband Timothy Calkins
