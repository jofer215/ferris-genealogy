GEORGE JOSEPH STAUFFER

ID: 4.2.5.4.5.1.2.7.2 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.4.5.1.2.7.2) GEORGE JOSEPH STAUFFER born June 26, 1869 Jersey City NJ and died April 5, 1938 Kent OH; buried there Standing Rock Cemetery; George’s family moved to Kent when he was 2 years old; he attended Kent schools and graduated from old Central High School 1887; he was associated with his father in the grocery business and later resided in Cleveland where he was employed by a wholesale grocery company; he also owned and operated a retail grocery store in Cleveland; he returned to Kent in 1903 and in August of that year accepted a position in the Kent National Bank; appointed assistant cashier in 1909 and in 1922 was made cashier, which position he held until he retired in 1934 because of ill health; married 1st before 1894 Bertha Anna Jones [dau of Edward Maurice & Ellen Virginia (David) Jones] born March 20, 1873 Kent OH and died there January 17, 1913; buried there Standing Rock Cemetery; married 2d c1913-21, Margueritte Ellen Jones [sister to Bertha] born March 15, 1885 Kent OH and died there July 14, 1961; and born to him and Bertha were: (4.2.5.4.5.1.2.7.2.1) CALDWELL EDWARD STAUFFER born December 6, 1894 Kent OH and died August 23, 1964; married December 5, 1917 Rose Orscheidt born October 6, 1895 and died April 1985 Lakewood, Cuyahoga, OH; and they had 4 kids
