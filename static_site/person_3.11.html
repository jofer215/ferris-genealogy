LYDIA FERRIS

ID: 3.11 | Source: Joseph
(3.11) LYDIA FERRIS (8RLK-QT) born c1673 (1675?) Stamford (Greenwich) CT and died by or before 1732 (1746/66?) Greenwich; married c1695 at Greenwich, John Reynolds (c1670-1712/32); a cooper; son of John and Judith (Palmer) Reynolds; Judith was the daughter of Lieutenant William and Judith (Feake) Palmer, who was Jeffrey’s third wife. They resided in Greenwich and were Congregationalists. Born to Lydia and John were:

Children (6)

  • PETER REYNOLDS 3.11.1
  • JUDITH REYNOLDS 3.11.2
  • DAVID REYNOLDS 3.11.3
  • LYDIA REYNOLDS 3.11.4
  • RUTH REYNOLDS 3.11.5
  • REUBEN REYNOLDS – not mentioned in his father’s will – may have died early – 3.11.6
