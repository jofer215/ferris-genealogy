EMMA FRANCES LEEDS

ID: 5.2.3.2.5.4.2 | Source: James
(5.2.3.2.5.4.2) EMMA FRANCES LEEDS born July 4, 1846 Stamford and died there February 29, 1912; married January 3, 1878 in Stamford, Mortimer Mosher [son of Isaac & Sally (Mead) Mosher] born September 18, 1841 NYC and died June 23, 1899 Stamford; both buried there St. John’s & St. Andrew’s Cemetery

No children recorded
