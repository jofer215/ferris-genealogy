DOROTHY ELLEN LAWSON

ID: 3.1.5.1.3.1.2.2.6.3.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.5.1.3.1.2.2.6.3.3) DOROTHY ELLEN LAWSON born February 28, 1922/23 (probably Lawrence or Butte Co SD) (Belle Fourche, Butte, SD) and died November 26, 1999 Belle Fourche SD; buried there Pine Slope Cemetery; married 1st November 18, 1939 George Norton Jr born 1912 and died May 4, 1950 Homestake Mine, Lawrence Co SD; buried Pine Slope Cemetery; and they had 2 kids; 2d April 18, 1953 Edward Cecil Henwood [son of William & Emma (Bingham) Henwood] born September 13, 1926 Lawrence Co SD and died June 22, 2001 Butte Co SD; and they had a child

No children recorded
