FRANK LEWIS FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.6.6.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.6.6.1) FRANK LEWIS FERRIS born December 29, 1856 Chesterville, Morrow, OH and died March 28, 1927 Sioux City IA; married 1st February 17, 1883 in New London OH, Louise J. Smith who died September 20, 1884 Redfield SD; married 2d December 15, 1887 in Hudson MI, Sarah Sargent born May 21, 1859 Fayette OH and died March 21, 1915 Sioux City OH. Frank moved to Ruggles Township c1866; he taught school several years before going into law; lived for many years in Rapid City SD. Born to them was:
