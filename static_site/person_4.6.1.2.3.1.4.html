REBECCA ANN LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.1.2.3.1.4 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.3.1.4) REBECCA ANN LOCKWOOD (#2274) born June 7, 1838 Jacksonville IL; married July 7, 1857 at Batavia IL, Rev. William Edward Merriman [son of Addison & Prudence (Adams) Merriman] born October 20, 1824 Hinsdale MA and died August 1, 1892 Boston MA; William was a graduate of Williams College; a Congregational minister; moved to Batavia IL; became President of Ripon College WI and in 1888, resided in Boston MA. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • MARY LOCKWOOD MERRIMAN 4.6.1.2.3.1.4.1
  • WILLIAM ADAMS MERRIMAN 4.6.1.2.3.1.4.2
  • EDWARD BUTLER MERRIMAN 4.6.1.2.3.1.4.3
  • FRANK WHITE MERRIMAN 4.6.1.2.3.1.4.4
