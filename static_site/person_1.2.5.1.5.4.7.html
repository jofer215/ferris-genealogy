ESTHER WHITSON

ID: 1.2.5.1.5.4.7 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.5.4.7) ESTHER WHITSON born March 8, 1808 and died October 13, 1857; married January 13, 1831 Edward Seaman Willets [son of Richard & Mary (Seaman) Willets] born September 22, 1803 Bethpage, L.I., NY and died December 11, 1869; he may have married is sister-in-law, Amy, after Esther’s death; and born to them were:

Children (7)

  • WILLIAM WILLETS 1.2.5.1.5.4.7.2
  • ABRAHAM WILLETS 1.2.5.1.5.4.7.3
  • RICHARD WILLETS 1.2.5.1.5.4.7.4
  • RACHEL H WILLETS 1.2.5.1.5.4.7.5
  • RACHEL HICKS WILLETS 1.2.5.1.5.4.7.6
