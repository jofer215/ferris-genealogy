JESSE HAIT

ID: 3.10.1.4.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.4.6) JESSE HAIT (#2939) lived in Stamford and died c1804, probably without issue as his estate was divided among his brothers and sisters. Jesse was a Lieutenant of a company for the defence of Stamford, to serve for the year, 1782. He also served 176 days in 1776.

No children recorded
