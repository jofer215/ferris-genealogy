SAMUEL LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.2.5.1.6.1.1 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.1.6.1.1) SAMUEL LOCKWOOD (#2151) born August 19, 1824 Stamford and died there September 6, 1889; carpenter 1860-89; married 1st December 17, 1849 in Stamford, Phebe Seely (2.6.7.1.9.3.3); and 2d January 29, 1868 Emily Frances Barlow (4.6.3.1.2.5.1); both buried Roxbury Cemetery; no offspring
