BENJAMIN FRANKLIN YELTON

ID: 4.5.3.1.2.5.7 | Source: Mary
(4.5.3.1.2.5.7) BENJAMIN FRANKLIN YELTON born 1842 Putnam IN and died January 31, 1913; buried Hot Springs National Cemetery, Hot Springs SD; enlisted in Company C, 6th Kansas Cavalry November 2, 1861; this regiment participated in a number of engagements in Kansas, Missouri, and the Indian Territory; also veteran of Spanish-American War; 1890 resided Nevada City MO; married Martha Louisa {maiden name unk} born March 1854 and buried May 29, 1941 Double Butte Cemetery, Tempe AZ; and born to them were:

Children (4)
