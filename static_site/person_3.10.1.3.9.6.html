ENOS K. PECK

ID: 3.10.1.3.9.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.3.9.6) ENOS K. PECK born April 15, 1805 Greenwich CT and died September 18, 1837 Madison GA; married Mary Waterbury. After Enos’ death Mary wed his brother, David, and after David’s death, she married Lester Markham. Born to Enos and Mary were:

Children (3)

  • CHARLES E. PECK 3.10.1.3.9.6.1
  • FRANCIS ANN PECK 3.10.1.3.9.6.2
  • DAVID W. PECK 3.10.1.3.9.6.3
