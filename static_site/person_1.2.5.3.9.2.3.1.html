ABRAM SUTTON UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.3.9.2.3.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.3.9.2.3.1) ABRAM SUTTON UNDERHILL (Abraham) born August 28, 1852 Chappaqua NY and died April 11, 1942 Ossining NY; Wesleyan U, AB, AM; 1881 Columbia U Law School, LLB; real estate attorney Ossining; Ossining National Bank & Trust VP; Corporation of Haverford College member; New York Society of Friends Treasurer; delegate 1920 Friends World Conference in London; married 1877 Anna Taber Murray (1855-1919) [dau of Robert Lindley & Ruth S (Taber) Murray]; DAR #25852; and born to them were;

Children (3)

  • HELEN MURRAY UNDERHILL 1.2.5.3.9.2.3.1.1
  • RUTH MURRAY UNDERHILL 1.2.5.3.9.2.3.1.2
  • ROBERT LINDLEY MURRAY UNDERHILL 1.2.5.3.9.2.3.1.3
