AMELIA RYERSE

ID: 1.2.3.3.4.3 | Source: John
(1.2.3.3.4.3) AMELIA RYERSE born February 1798 Port Ryerse, Norfolk, Ontario (London, Ontario) and died March 24, 1882 Eldon House, London, Ontario; she was a diarist and author; married c1815 John Harris (Captain British Naval Service) born 1782 County Devon, England (Eldon House, Lond, Ontario) and died 1850 [son of Rev John & Catharine (Bulteel) Harris]; and born to them were:
