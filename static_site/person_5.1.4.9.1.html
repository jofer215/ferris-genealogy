JACOB LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.1.4.9.1 | Source: James
(5.1.4.9.1) JACOB LOCKWOOD born Greenwich CT and died Stamford CT; a carpenter; married July 25, 1765 in Stamford, Catherine Knapp [dau of Timothy Knapp] born c1737 probably Rye NY and died Stamford CT. After Jacob's death, Catherine married a Waterbury. Born to them were:
