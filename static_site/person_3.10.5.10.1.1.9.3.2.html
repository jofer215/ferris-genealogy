MARJORIE DITMARS

ID: 3.10.5.10.1.1.9.3.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.5.10.1.1.9.3.2) MARJORIE DITMARS born December 15, 1889 Brooklyn and died after 1951; married 1st April 22, 1913 DuBois Beale [son of Charles Frederick Tiffany & Margaret (DuBois) Beale?] born January 29, 1895 and died February 1, 1930; NYC lawyer; married 2d March 16, 1932 Pompeo Margherita Maresi born August 20, 1888 Brooklyn and died August 10, 1940; NYC lawyer – his godmother was Queen Marherita of Italy; married 3d October 24, 1944 at Chicago, as his 3d wife, Charles Yates Grayson [son of William Edwin & Sarah Elizabeth (Roads) Grayson] born February 29, 1884 Luray VA – Charles went to work when he was 12y; 1914 joined the Remington Typewriter Co retiring from there 1946 – retiring to Delray FL

No children recorded
