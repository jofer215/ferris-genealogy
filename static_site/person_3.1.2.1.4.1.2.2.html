IRA MILES SLAWSON

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.2.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.2.2) IRA MILES SLAWSON (Photos) born March 30, 1833 Yates Co NY and died October 17, 1885 Hastings, Barry, MI; buried Cressey Cemetery, Barry Co MI; Civil War Vet served with the 23d New York Infantry – Bull Run, Antietam (injuried there) and Fredericksburg; 1864-65 with 120th New York Infantry; received a field commission to 2d Lieutenant on March 30, 1865 with the 39th New York Infantry; married October 17, 1866 in Barrington NY, Nomie Meeks [dau of Elias & Mary O (Bassett) Meeks] born December 1, 1845 NY and died February 6, 1923 Urbandale, Calhoun, MI; after Ira’s death, Nomie married John Smith; and born to Ira and Nomie were:

Children (4)

  • CLAUDE LEE SLAWSON 3.1.2.1.4.1.2.2.1
  • MARY MAUD SLAWSON 3.1.2.1.4.1.2.2.2
  • MATTIE MAY SLAWSON 3.1.2.1.4.1.2.2.3
  • MYRTLE ADDA SLAWSON 3.1.2.1.4.1.2.2.4
