DEODATE HAIT

ID: 2.5.2.7.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.7.1) DEODATE HAIT (#3032) born September 13, 1738 and died March 1, 1796 probably at Stamford where he lived; married November 20, 1784 Mary Weed who died before June 1817. Born to them was:

Children (7)
