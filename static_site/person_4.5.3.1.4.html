EBENEZER LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.5.3.1.4 | Source: Mary
(4.5.3.1.4) EBENEZER LOCKWOOD (#653) born November 12, 1765/66 Stamford and died January 3, 1839 Florida, Schenectady, NY; enlisted 1780 in Captain Scoville’s Company of Colonel Campbell’s Regiment in the Connecticut Line and served 15 and a half months at Stanwich, Bedford, North Street, Horseneck and other places; pensioned; married Ann {maiden name unk} who was 70y of age September 6, 1855

No children recorded
