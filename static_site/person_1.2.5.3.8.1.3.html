GEORGE HAVILAND

ID: 1.2.5.3.8.1.3 | Source: John
(1.2.5.3.8.1.3) GEORGE HAVILAND born November 11, 1833 and died September 3, 1874 Brooklyn; buried Friend’s Cemetery, Prospect Park, Brooklyn; married June 9, 1859 Sarah Louise Child [dau of William & Ann (Thomas) Child] born July 25, 1842. She later married Theodore Sheffield. Born to George and Sarah were:
