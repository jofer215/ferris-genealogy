WALTER V DENNIS

ID: 1.2.3.3.5.1.5.3.3.1 | Source: John
(1.2.3.3.5.1.5.3.3.1) WALTER V DENNIS born June 17, 1904 Albany and died 2005 (of Eugene OR) 101y old; served as superintendent of schools in Rogue River, Roseburg, and Clatskanie before moving to Eugene in 1944; before his retirement in 1976, he was president of Best Western for 3y; received his masters of business admin from U of Oregon; during WWII he and his family spent 2 summers tending lookouts near Reedsport and Haceta Head; won awards for his lapidary displays; married October 27, 1929 in Blachly, Nellie M Rust born April 5, 1906 and died August 3, 1996; and born to them was:

Children (1)
