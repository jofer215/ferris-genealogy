ETHEL ARMENIA REYNOLDS - twin -

ID: 3.1.1.3.1.4.10.13 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.3.1.4.10.13) ETHEL ARMENIA REYNOLDS - twin - born January 16, 1882 Cross River and was living 1924; married April 29, 1899 Peter Van Colt Baker, of Brooklyn NY, who died 1944. Peter was the nephew of Charles E. Baker, Ethel's sister Delilah's husband. Born to Ethel and Peter were:
