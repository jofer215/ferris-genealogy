SAMUEL BAILEY COLE

ID: 1.2.3.7.2.6.3.8 | Source: John
(1.2.3.7.2.6.3.8) SAMUEL BAILEY COLE born January 24, 1847 Catlin NY; married July 27, 1869 at Townsend NY, Ursula Dean born April 10, 1849 Six Nations, Schuyler, NY [dau of Jervis and Mary (Miller) Dean]. Samuel was a dealer in general merchandise. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • DEAN JERVIS COLE 1.2.3.7.2.6.3.8.1
  • DON SAMUEL COLE 1.2.3.7.2.6.3.8.2
  • LLOYD ALBERT COLE 1.2.3.7.2.6.3.8.3
  • WILLIAM JAY COLE 1.2.3.7.2.6.3.8.4
