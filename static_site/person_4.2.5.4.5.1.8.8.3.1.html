SUSAN ELIZABETH HOLBROOK

ID: 4.2.5.4.5.1.8.8.3.1 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.4.5.1.8.8.3.1) SUSAN ELIZABETH HOLBROOK born December 27, 1937 Glendale CA and died November 28, 2001 Los Angeles CA; buried Fairfax VA; B.A. Education from George Mason U, Fairfax VA 1976; Masters of Education, James Mason U, Harrisonburg VA; taught in the Fairfax Co School system for 19y as a School Librarian/Media Specialist retiring in 1997; married a Mr Ferguson and they had 3 kids

No children recorded
