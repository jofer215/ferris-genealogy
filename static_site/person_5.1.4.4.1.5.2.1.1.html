KATHERINE E RANDALL

ID: 5.1.4.4.1.5.2.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.4.4.1.5.2.1.1) KATHERINE E RANDALL (DAR # 52459) born 1870 CT; married Ernest Kilborn Loveland, M.D. [son of Clark Stone & Mary Catherine (Kilbourn) Loveland] born January 27, 1871 Morris, Litchfield, CT; and they had 2 kids (5.1.4.4.1.5.2A) RACHEL SAMANTHA RANDALL born 1819 Bridgewater CT and died there June 13, 1827; buried there Old Ground – one source has this Rachel listed, but she is not listed in others – sounds feasible looking at the dates, so she is listed for info

No children recorded
