RACHEL ELIZABETH BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.6.5.4 | Source: James
(5.2.2.6.5.4) RACHEL ELIZABETH BUXTON born April 30, 1812 Stamford and died there August 6, 1883. She married February 6, 1832 in Stamford, Noah Webb [son of William & Susannah (Weed) Webb] born July 20, 1803 Stamford and died there May 15, 1850; buried St. John’s Episcopal Cemetery. Born to them were:

Children (6)

  • MARY ELIZABETH WEBB 5.2.2.6.5.4.3
  • LATHAM ALLEN WEBB 5.2.2.6.5.4.4
