JOSEPH FERRIS JR

ID: 3.6.1.1.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.1.1.1) JOSEPH FERRIS JR (use of Jr here is unusual – named for his g.grandfather?) born c1766 apparently Greenwich (date of birth is questioned as it c3y before his parents marriage); baptized August 27, 1794 at St. Johns Episcopal Church, Stamford by his mother; married Sybil Wood Palmer (2.1.1.3.6.3); and born to them were:

Children (7)

  • JOSEPH FERRIS 3.6.1.1.1.4
