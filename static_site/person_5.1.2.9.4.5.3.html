SARAH E. WILSON

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.5.3 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.5.3) SARAH E. WILSON born May 23, 1849 and died June 26, 1907 Putnam Valley NY; buried Hillside Cemetery, Peekskill NY; married 1st Gilbert Lewis Baxter [son of Gideon & Eliza Ann (Denney)] born October 23, 1847 Putnam Valley NY and died Missouri; married 2d December 25, 1886 in Philipstown NY, as his first wife, James Griffin [son of Jacob & Sarah (Odell)]; he married second (5.1.2.9.2.5.1.7). Born to Sarah and Gilbert were:

Children (2)

  • WILLIAM C. BAXTER 5.1.2.9.4.5.3.2
