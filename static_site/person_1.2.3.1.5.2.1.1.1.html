NORMAN WALTER WHITE

ID: 1.2.3.1.5.2.1.1.1 | Source: John
(1.2.3.1.5.2.1.1.1) NORMAN WALTER WHITE born May 10, 1888 Sidney, Australia and died there August 6, 1958; married 1st February 1, 1915 in Sydney, Elvira Lucy Herdson; married 2d Miss Tomlison; and born to him and Elvira were 2 kids; and to him and his 2d wife was a child

No children recorded
