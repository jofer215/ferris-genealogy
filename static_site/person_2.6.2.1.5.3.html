POLLY ESTHER HOYT

ID: 2.6.2.1.5.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.1.5.3) POLLY ESTHER HOYT (#3470) born December 1786 Stamford; baptized December 21, 1788 at Stamford CT and died 1856 at Brooklyn NY; buried at Greenwich Cemetery, Washington Co NY; married September 8, 1805 in Stamford, Ezra Knapp (2.6.4.7.5). They belonged to the 1st Congregational Church of Stamford and moved to Brooklyn after 1827. See him for offspring.

No children recorded
