JOHN FERRIS JEWETT

ID: 5.1.3.4.7.2.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.7.2.2) JOHN FERRIS JEWETT (5.1.3.4.7.3) ANDREW JACKSON FERRIS born March 23, 1818 and died May 2, 1890; buried Ferris Farm Cemetery; never married [Speculation - Andrew J. Ferris’ will was filed May 24, 1890, Hamilton County OH - executors and beneficiaries of the will were Phoebe Ferris and Joseph Ferris his brother and sister]

No children recorded
