MINNIE K. VALLY

ID: 1.1.4.4.1.3.1.5 | Source: John
(1.1.4.4.1.3.1.5) MINNIE K. VALLY born 1874 WI; married 1895 Frederick R. Miller born April 1870 Switzerland; immigrated to the US 1884 with his parents; 1900 U. S. Census, Fredman is listed as a farmer and owned the farm free and clear. Born to them were:
