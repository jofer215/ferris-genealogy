EZRA TINKER

ID: 1.2.3.4.6.1.1.14 | Source: John
(1.2.3.4.6.1.1.14) EZRA TINKER (Rev) born May 18, 1842 North Norwich CT (Plymouth NY) and died April 20, 1918 Marine City MI; Madison U, M.A.; Drew Theological Seminary, B.D., Yale M.A.; married 1st August 2, 1865 in Jerusalem NY, Sarah Janet Wright [dau of Dr Samuel Hart & Joanna (McLean) Wright] born October 21, 1847 Starks NY and died 1908 Lyons NY; married 2d 1910 Cora Damon; and born to him and Sarah were:

Children (5)
