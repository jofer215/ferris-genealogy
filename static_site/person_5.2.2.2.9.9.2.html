THEODORE T. BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.2.9.9.2 | Source: James
(5.2.2.2.9.9.2) THEODORE T. BUXTON born March 12, 1847 Stamford CT and died there 1909; buried Long Ridge Cemetery; a shoemaker at Turn of River CT; married November 23, 1868 at Stamford, Harriet Ann Scofield [dau of Legrand & Augusta (Peppard) Scofield] born November 1848 Stamford and died there 1916; buried Long Ridge Cemetery. Born to them were: (5.2.2.2.9.9.2.1) FITCH ELBERT BUXTON born April 26, 1870 (May 21, 1870) Stamford CT and died there 1947; buried Long Ridge Cemetery; a plumber and metalworker; married April 26, 1891 in Stamford, Nelly Louise Holly born 1874 Stamford and died there 1940; buried Long Ridge Cemetery. Born to them were:

Children (1)
