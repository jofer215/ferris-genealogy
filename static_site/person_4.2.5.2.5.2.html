THEODORE WATERBURY

ID: 4.2.5.2.5.2 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.2.5.2) THEODORE WATERBURY born July 19, 1821 Stamford CT and died there September 6, 1898; buried there North Street Cemetery; 1850 coaster; 1860 seaman; married 1st c1848 Hannah M. Webb born December 16, 1827 NY and died July 13, 1874 Stamford; buried there with Theodore; married 2d Margaret A {maiden name unk}. Born to him and Hannah were:
