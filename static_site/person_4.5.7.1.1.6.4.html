FRANCES ISABELLE LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.5.7.1.1.6.4 | Source: Mary
(4.5.7.1.1.6.4) FRANCES ISABELLE LOCKWOOD (#2260) (Photo) born June 28, 1854 Newark NJ and died March 28, 1937; a prolific artist and illustrated books, prints, trade cards, greeting cards, calendars and more; it is said her first sale was a professional artist was to Louisa May Alcott for illustrating one of her poems; perhaps due to her grief and loss of her only child, she became one of the most prolific artists of children in her time; resided in Brooklyn; married July 6, 1886 William Tyson Brundage (-1923) also an artist; and born to them was:
