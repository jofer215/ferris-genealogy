ISAAC H. GILMAN

ID: 3.1.2.3.1.3.2.7 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.3.1.3.2.7) ISAAC H. GILMAN born 1840 IN; a farmer near Mishawaka, St. Joseph, IN; married 1866 Lizzie A. Bartlett [dau of Josiah and Hannah (Clark) Bartlett] born 1844 NH; Lizzie's great grandfather was one of the signers of the Declaration of Independence. Isaac and Lizzie were members of the Methodist Episcopal Church and he was a Republican. They owned 180 acres of good land. In 1861, Isaac enlisted in the Army, at Goshen, under Colonel Eddy and he was in the service for three years and eight months; was in the battles at Iuka, Florence, and the Siege of Vicksburg.
