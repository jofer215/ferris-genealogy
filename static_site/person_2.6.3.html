HANNAH KNAPP

ID: 2.6.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.3) HANNAH KNAPP (8M0K-5R) born at Stamford CT, March 10, 1697/98/99 and died there in 1790 (May 1801); married 1st, June 16, 1716 in Stamford, Isaac Quintard (G2KW-9M) [son of Isaac & Jeanne (Fume) Quintard] born Bristol, England December 12, 1696 and died February ye last day 1738, aged 42y Stamford; married 2d c1740 a Mr. Jessup. Born to Hannah and Isaac were (not in sequence): (2.6.3.1 & 2) UNNAMED TWINS QUINTARD born April 3, 1721 and died at birth
