MURIEL IRENE ROSS

ID: 3.1.6.8.1.3.5.5.9 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.1.3.5.5.9) MURIEL IRENE ROSS born November 13, 1921 Lamont, Alberta; secretarial/clerical; married July 11, 1944 in Lamont, Alberta, John Brander Parsons born April 15, 1918 Edmonton – separated; he enlisted as a pilot with the Air Observer School and RCAF program to train navigators from the Commonwealth countries for the war. They had 3 kids

No children recorded
