CHARLES AUGUSTUS FERRIS

ID: 1.4.1.3.7.1.2.1 | Source: John
(1.4.1.3.7.1.2.1) CHARLES AUGUSTUS FERRIS born January 4, 1875 Rye NY and died January 26, 1954 Port Chester NY; married August 17, 1895 in NYC Johanna Christine Schmid [dau of Christen Jacob & Christine] born January 18, 1876 Altensteig, Germany and died July 11, 1954 Rye NY; both buried South Salem NY. Born to them were: (1.4.1.3.7.1.2.1.1) GEORGE A. (AUGUSTUS?) FERRIS born December 1896 Stamford CT and died 1987 Falmouth MA; married Alice Handy who died 1992; no offspring
