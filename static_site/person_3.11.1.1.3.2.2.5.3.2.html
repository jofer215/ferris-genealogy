KENNETH LAWRENCE NEWMAN

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.5.3.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.5.3.2) KENNETH LAWRENCE NEWMAN born February 25, 1920 Traverse City MI and died there December 24, 2002; buried there Memorial Gardens; tire repair Ford garage, Parsons Aircraft Corp, United Tech; Army WWII Veteran; married September 8, 1945 in Leelanau Co, Georgia Temple [dau of Grady & Bessie (Pitts) Temple] born c1926 MS and died 2001 Traverse City; and they had 4 kids (3.11.1.1.3.2.2.5.3.3) WILLIAM JEROME NEWMAN (Bill) born October 9, 1929 Traverse City MI and died September 7, 1997 Grand Traverse Co MI; construction; Korea War Veteran; married 1st September 5, 1953 at Traverse City, Loretta Elaine Worm [dau of Raymond T & Violet F (Hoch) Worm]; married 2d October 11, 1958 in Grand Traverse Co, Myrtle L Braun; and they (Bill & Myrtle) had 7 kids

No children recorded
