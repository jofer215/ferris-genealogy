FLORENCE CORNELIA PELL

ID: 1.4.7.3.1.5.1.2 | Source: John
(1.4.7.3.1.5.1.2) FLORENCE CORNELIA PELL born January 17, 1864 Flushing, L.I., NY; married 1st June 25, 1887 Nathan Clifford Brown born October 13, 1856, of Portland ME, educated Bowdoin College, AB 1876; author and ornithologist, Fellow of the American Ornithological Union; and 2d Pierre C. Waring, a hat manufacturer of Yonkers NY. In her later years, Florence resided at ‘Pine Crest’, Hastings-on-Hudson. Born to Florence and Nathan was:
