DRAKE LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.2.5.1.2 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.1.2) DRAKE LOCKWOOD (#597) born August 10, 1763 Greenwich and died March 7, 1839; buried in the Old Greenwich Cemetery; a soldier in Town or Coast Guard, in the Company commanded by Captain Timothy Lockwood at Greenwich from April 1, 1780 for nine months; re-enlisted for two years; married Mary Ferris Peck (3.10.5.11.4); and born to Drake and Mary were:
