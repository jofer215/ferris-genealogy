HARRIET LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.3.1.2.5 | Source: Mary
(4.6.3.1.2.5) HARRIET LOCKWOOD (#1431) born October 11, 1818 Turn of the River, Stamford and died April 16, 1890 Stamford; married December 27, 1840 in Stamford, (Daniel Barton) Daniel R. Barlow [son of Robert R. & Huldah (Holly) Barlow] born September 17, 1816 Stamford and died there August 6, 1885; both buried Roxbury Cemetery; and born to them were: (4.6.3.1.2.5.1) EMILY FRANCES BARLOW born October 1841 Stamford and died there November 23, 1888; buried Roxbury Cemetery; married January 29, 1868 in Stamford as his 2d wife, Samuel Lockwood (4.2.5.1.6.1.1); no offspring
