FLOYD DAVID MCQUEER

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.1.3.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.1.3.4) FLOYD DAVID MCQUEER born July 14, 1912 Cedar MI and died May 14, 1975 Traverse City MI; buried Kasson Township Cemetery, Leeelanau Co MI; carpenter, farmer; married June 4, 1932 in Traverse City, Florence I Goddard [dau of Philo Lee & Flora Bell (White) Goddard] born July 25, 1913 Traverse City MI and died there May 12, 1989; buried there Oakwood Cemetery; and they had 8 kids (3.11.1.1.3.2.2.1.4) ELLA I. NEWMAN born November 1880/81 Dubuque IA and died August 27, 1961 Traverse City MI; married July 1, 1903 in Traverse City, Earl Eugene Irish [son of Albert & Melissa (Jackson) Irish] born May 24, 1878 Ingham Co MI and died March 3, 1959; plumber, woodsman; divorced 1917; and born to them were:

No children recorded
