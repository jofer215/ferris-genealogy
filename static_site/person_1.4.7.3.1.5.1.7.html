MARY HOWLAND PELL

ID: 1.4.7.3.1.5.1.7 | Source: John
(1.4.7.3.1.5.1.7) MARY HOWLAND PELL born December 12, 1876 Yonkers NY; married August 21, 1897 Samuel Cornell Hopkins [son of Henry & Mary Elizabeth (Cornell) Hopkins] born February 19, 1858 NY. Samuel graduated from Yale 1882, and was a director of the Catskill National Bank. They resided in Catskill. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • SAMUEL CORNELL HOPKINS 1.4.7.3.1.5.1.7.1
  • HOWLAND PELL HOPKINS 1.4.7.3.1.5.1.7.2
