CATHARINE LEEDS

ID: 2.1.7.1.3.5 | Source: Peter
(2.1.7.1.3.5) CATHARINE LEEDS born in July 1804 CT and died December 7, 1873 Stamford; buried Leeds Cemetery, Darien; married June 4, 1826 in Stamford, (as his 2d wife) Thomas Purdy Dixon born in May 1800 Greenwich and died May 9, 1859 Stamford after falling off a roof; buried Leeds Cemetery; carpenter; (his first wife was Elizabeth Hoyt (2.5.2.8.1.3.2); and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • JAMES HENRY DIXON 2.1.7.1.3.5.1
  • MARY ELIZABETH DIXON 2.1.7.1.3.5.2
  • JOHN LEONARD DIXON 2.1.7.1.3.5.4
  • CHARLES THOMAS DIXON 2.1.7.1.3.5.5
  • SARAH E. DIXON 2.1.7.1.3.5.6
