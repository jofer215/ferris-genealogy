BYRON ALLAN GILBERT

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.2.3.2.7 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.2.3.2.7) BYRON ALLAN GILBERT born January 13, 1867 Bath NY and died November 2, 1961 Webster SD; buried Lisbon ND; married November 2, 1895 Marie Fredrikke Rhoede (Rhode) born November 2, 1874 Oslo, Norway and died January 14, 1958 Lisbon ND. Maria came to America when she was 5 years old and settled near LaMoure ND. They moved to ID for a year and then back to Lisbon where they remained. Byron lost an eye while chopping wood; a wood chip flew into his eye. Born to them were:
