EMELINE CHRISTIAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.3 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.3) EMELINE CHRISTIAN born December 19, 1831 Putnam Valley NY and died there April 16, 1905; married Sands H. Tompkins [son of Nathaniel & Mary (Cole)] born c1832 and died August 25, 1909 Putnam Valley NY; both buried Hillside Cemetery, Peekskill NY. Obituary, Highland Democrat, Peekskill, NY, Aug. 28, 1909: “Sands H. Tompkins died at his home at Oscawana Lake early last Wednesday morning, aged 77 years and 7 months. He had been ill some time. Mr. Tompkins born Putnam Valley, the son of Nathaniel and Mary Cole Tompkins. He had lived in that section all his life. He was well known by the people of Putnam County as well as by thousands of out of town people who at one time or another had visited or spent some time at Oscawana Lake. He kept a hotel and shebang at the lake for many years. His place and his face were familiar to Peekskillers. He leaves two brothers and a sister and a daughter. His wife, a Miss Christian died April 17, 1903. The interment was in Hillside Cemetery.” Born to them were:
