SARAH LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.1.4.5 | Source: James
(5.1.4.5) SARAH LOCKWOOD (#505) born c1736 Greenwich CT and died 1774 Spencer Town NY, near the Hudson River, very near to Rhinebeck where General Richard Montgomery had his estate. Richard and Alexander were cousins; married c1752 Alexander Montgomery, Sr. He was a Loyalist and likely even a spy for the British and eventually they moved to Gagetown, New Brunswick 1783

No children recorded
