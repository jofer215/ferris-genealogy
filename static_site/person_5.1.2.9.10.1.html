ELIZA TRAVIS

ID: 5.1.2.9.10.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.10.1) ELIZA TRAVIS born January 4, 1814 Putnam Valley NY and died April 11, 1893; buried Shrub Oak Methodist Cemetery, Shrub Oak NY. Obituary, Highland Democrat, Peekskill, NY, Saturday, April 15, 1893: Under Adams Corners & Vicinity: Miss Eliza Travis, who had been ill for many months, passed away on Tuesday morning at the home of her sister, Mrs. Sarah Gilbert, with whom she had lived for many years. She was the daughter of William and Ann Travis, and was the eldest of a family of twelve children, eight of whom survive her. When a young woman she embraced religion and united with the Shrub Oak M.E. church, and at the good ripe age of 79 years she died still in the true Christian faith. Her whole life had been spent in doing and caring for those she loved; never thinking of herself, so eager was she to make others happy. Her work is done and we trust that her soul rests in peace. Interment was in the churchyard, opposite the Shrub Oak M. E. Church. She has a Estate Probated in Putnam County, NY, #3371, it names her heirs as: Sarah Gilbert, sister of Putnam Valley, NY; Henry Travis, brother of Putnam Valley, NY; Ebenezer Travis, brother of Putnam Valley, NY; Ann Horton, sister of Putnam Valley, NY; Lorania F. Christian, sister of Peekskill, NY; John W. Travis, brother of Peekskill, NY; Mary J. Denike, sister of New York City; William Travis, brother of Napa County, Calif.; Cornelia Pierce, niece of Putnam Valley, NY; Mattie Travis, niece of Putnam Valley, NY; Ida Dalton, niece of Yorktown, NY; Albert Travis, nephew of Yorktown, NY; William Travis, nephew of Providence, R.I.; David E. Travis, nephew of New York City; Frederick Travis, nephew of New York City; and Louisa Waldron, niece of New York City.
