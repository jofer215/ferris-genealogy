CAROLINE H. KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.3.2.1.3.10 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.2.1.3.10) CAROLINE H. KNAPP born December 8, 1840 Sugar Loaf NY and died January 15, 1924 probably Westtown NY; married March 2, 1870 in Orange Co William Wallace Elmer born July 15, 1842 Unionville NY and died November 15, 1887 Westtown.
