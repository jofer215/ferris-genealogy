FREDERICK WILLIAM UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.6.2.4.2.4.4 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.4.2.4.4) FREDERICK WILLIAM UNDERHILL born December 5, 1893 Wayne Township, Lafayette Co WI and died February 2, 1984 Beloit WI; married August 22, 1915 in Maxwell IA, Mary Jesse Bowen [dau of Frederick U & Arnetta (Earp) Bowen] born July 22, 1898 Eagle Grove IA and died March 11, 1997 Madison WI; both buried East Lawn Cemetery, Beloit; and born to them were: (1.2.6.2.4.2.4.4.1) GERTRUDE LOUISE UNDERHILL born September 17, 1916 Maxwell IA and died March 15, 1998 Madison WI; buried East Lawn Cemetery, Beloit; married March 20, 1943 in Beloit WI, Howard William Sowl [son of DeLoss & Mary Louise (Berg) Sowl] born July 19, 1916 Beloit and died there June 1, 1946; and they had a child
