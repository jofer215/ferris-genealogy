JESSE CARMAN UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.4.5.4.4.2 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.5.4.4.2) JESSE CARMAN UNDERHILL born September 1835 Livingston NY; Civil War Vet (85th NY Infantry Regiment), Private captured April 20, 1864 Plymouth NC – prisoner at Andersonville; mustered out July 21, 1865 Elmira NY; 1867-79 resided MI; 1880 Chippeawa Co MN; 1885 MI; 1900 Cold Water MI; married Ursula Stone [dau of Joseph G & Susan A (Pierce) Stone] born May 1847 PA and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • JOSEPH UNDERHILL 1.2.5.4.5.4.4.2.1
  • GRACE UNDERHILL 1.2.5.4.5.4.4.2.2
  • EDWARD Z UNDERHILL 1.2.5.4.5.4.4.2.4
  • FRED C UNDERHILL 1.2.5.4.5.4.4.2.5
