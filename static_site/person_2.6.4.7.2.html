SARAH ANN KNAPP

ID: 2.6.4.7.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.4.7.2) SARAH ANN KNAPP born April 16, 1773 Stamford and died there March 17, 1839; buried North Street Cemetery; married July 23, 1803 (July 13, 1802?) at Stamford, Abraham Hoyt (Hait) (2.5.2.4.7.3); see Abraham for offspring.

No children recorded
