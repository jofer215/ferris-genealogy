EZRA ST. JOHN

ID: 2.5.2.4.1.6 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.1.6) EZRA ST. JOHN born November 2, 1765 Stamford and died there 1825; married March 4, 1810 in Stamford, Rhua Scofield [dau of Epenetus & Susanna (Bates) Scofield] born August 29, 1768 Stamford and died January 13, 1864; buried Simsbury Cemetery – formerly Scofield 418-31, Stamford; cemetery records have her Rheuama and dying at age 85y which doesn’t track with these days; and born to them were:

Children (2)

  • WILLAM HENRY ST JOHN 2.5.2.4.1.6.2
