GEORGE WASHINGTON FERRIS

ID: 1.6.1.4.5.7 | Source: John
(1.6.1.4.5.7) GEORGE WASHINGTON FERRIS born Bedford 1833 and died there 1902; buried Ferris Cemetery, but later exhumed and interred in the Bedford Cemetery, Bedford NY [Jim - the Ferris Cemetery is now on the Westmoreland Sanctuary and is under the care of the sanctuary - thanks to Mike Ferris.] [We have a conflict here with this birth date 2 years after the death date of the father.]; married 1st 1848 [at 15 years of age?] Jeanette Brundage born near Armonk NY, town of North Castle 1832 and died Bedford 1852 {after childbirth?}; married 2d 1853 Phoebe Brundage probably born Bedford 1841 and died there 1859; and 3d 1861 Anne Buttemer born Five Mile River, Cork, Ireland 1831 and died Bedford December 20, 1895. He was a farmer and spent his entire life on a farm adjoining the other Ferris farm. Born to George W. and Jeanette were:
