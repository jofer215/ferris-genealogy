LUCY FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.5 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.5) LUCY FERRIS born July 15, 1861 near Metamora, Franklin Co IN and died May 5, 1898 Tampico, Whiteside, IL from typhoid fever; married December 24, 1879 in Tampico, Ezekial B. Murray [son of George & Elizabeth (Richardson) Murray] born October 23, 1855 Laura, Franklin, IN and died January 31, 1896 Tampico of typhoid fever. In 1883, they resided and were engaged in farming 100 acres in Hahnaman Township, Whiteside Co near Rock Falls IL. She was a member of the Methodist Episcopal Church and he was a Democrat. Both buried near her parents at the Tampico Cemetery, Tampico IL. Born to them were (the kids orphaned at early age, were raised by their Aunt and Uncle Mary and Ralph Glassburn):

Children (3)

  • MARTHA ELIZABETH MURRAY 5.1.3.4.10.4.5.1
  • ROLLA J. MURRAY 5.1.3.4.10.4.5.2
  • JOHN LUTHER MURRAY 5.1.3.4.10.4.5.3
