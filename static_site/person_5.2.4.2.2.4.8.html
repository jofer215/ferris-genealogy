CHARLES EDWARD ROGERS WINTHROP

ID: 5.2.4.2.2.4.8 | Source: James
(5.2.4.2.2.4.8) CHARLES EDWARD ROGERS WINTHROP born October 8, 1816 NYC and died June 27, 1888 Tamaroa, Perry, IL; settled in Perry Co IL 1839; farmer and stock raiser, attorney, county judge (1869); school commissioner (1861) ; married February 24, 1842 Delilah Lipe [dau of Jonas & Esther] born September 16, 1822 Jackson Co IL and died January 18, 1900 Tamaroa IL; both buried there; and born to them were:
