POLLY LEEDS

ID: 2.1.7.1.3.2 | Source: Peter
(2.1.7.1.3.2) POLLY LEEDS born March 18, 1795 Stamford CT and died there August 13, 1875; married August 25, 1814 Peter Brown [son of Peter & Martha (Chichester) Brown] born July 9, 1795 Stamford and died January 14, 1826 Stamford; both buried there (Northfields) North Street Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • MARY ELIZABETH BROWN 2.1.7.1.3.2.2
  • JULIA ANN BROWN 2.1.7.1.3.2.3
  • ELIZA CATHERINE BROWN 2.1.7.1.3.2.4
