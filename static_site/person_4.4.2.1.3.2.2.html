HANNAH E BATES

ID: 4.4.2.1.3.2.2 | Source: Mary
(4.4.2.1.3.2.2) HANNAH E BATES born May 21, 1836 Darien CT and died January 21, 1871 New Canaan CT; married 1st unk (but must have been a Bates since the kids are named Bates); married 2d, as his 2d wife, c1865 William Henry Everett (son of Joseph & Abigail (Seely) Everett] born January 26, 1817 New Canaan CT and died there May 12, 1878; shoemaker 1850-70; and born to her and her 1st husband were:

Children (3)

  • EMMA E BATES 4.4.2.1.3.2.2.1
  • JAMES H BATES 4.4.2.1.3.2.2.2
  • BERTHA EVERETT 4.4.2.1.3.2.2.4
