JAMES LEWIS LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.2.4.4.5.2.4 | Source: James
(5.2.4.4.5.2.4) JAMES LEWIS LOCKWOOD born July 8, 1824 Stamford and died November 1, 1895 Chicago IL; tinsmith 1860-63; married 1st April 18, 1854 in Stamford, Hannah Maria Thompson [dau of Sylvanus Brown & Maria (Webb) Thompson] born December 21, 1828 Stamford and died there June 6, 1867; both buried Woodland Cemetery, Stamford; married 2d April 1, 1870 in Stamford, Mary Anne Philips born January 24, 1841 NYC and died October 23, 1921 Stamford; buried Woodland Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • ARTHUR DAVIS THOMPSON 5.2.4.4.5.2.4.2
  • GRACE LOCKWOOD 5.2.4.4.5.2.4.3
