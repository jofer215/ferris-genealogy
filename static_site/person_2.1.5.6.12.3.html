ANDREW NICHOLS

ID: 2.1.5.6.12.3 | Source: Peter
(2.1.5.6.12.3) ANDREW NICHOLS born April 2, 1806 Oneida, Madison, NY and died May 12/13, 1891 Kennedy, Chautauqua, NY; relocated with his parents to Jefferson Co NY and then in 1870 to Kennedy; farmer and lumbergin; member of local school board; married 1831 Cordelia Holcomb [dau of Benjamin Sullivan & Abigail Johnston (Lee) Holcomb] born March 23, 1811 Essex Co NY (March 1, 1811 Cape Vincent, Jefferson, NY) and died October 21, 1900; and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • BENJAMIN NICHOLS 2.1.5.6.12.3.1
  • SETH LEE NICHOLS 2.1.5.6.12.3.2
  • IRA C NICHOLS 2.1.5.6.12.3.3
  • ANDREW NICHOLS 2.1.5.6.12.3.4
  • ISAAC C NICHOLS owns and operates iron mines in Wisconsin 2.1.5.6.12.3.5
