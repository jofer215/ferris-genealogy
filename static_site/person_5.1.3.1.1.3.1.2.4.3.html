BYRON EDWARD SPENCER

ID: 5.1.3.1.1.3.1.2.4.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.1.3.1.2.4.3) BYRON EDWARD SPENCER (B.E.) born April 20, 1892 Mount Vernon, Posey, IN and died September 1, 1944 Pocahontas, Randolph, AR; 1913 farmer Stoddard Co MO; between 1933-44 owner of stave mills at Poplar Bluff MO and Pocahontas AR (B.E. Spencer Cooperage Co); married December 18, 1912 in Gray’s Ridge MO, Maria Artemesia Hartley [dau of Thomas & Sylvia Jane (Wiseman) Hartley] born September 9, 1891 near Petersburg, Menard, IL and died November 8, 1989 Bloomfield, Stoddard, MO; both buried Dexter City Cemetery, Dexter MO; and born to them were:

Children (2)

  • BRYON HARTLEY SPENCER 5.1.3.1.1.3.1.2.4.3.1
  • JANY MARIE SPENCER 5.1.3.1.1.3.1.2.4.3.3
