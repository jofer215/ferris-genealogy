WESLEY FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.1.6.10.10 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.6.10.10) WESLEY FERRIS born November 1, 1843 Eaton Co MI and died August 9, 1913 Eaton Rapids MI; a Civil War Veteran; a wrestler; a farmer; and a singing teacher; married June 22, 1877 in Eaton Co MI, Marian (May) Ervay born March 17, 1854 Rome, Oneida, NY and died May 20, 1923 Eaton Co MI; both buried Rose Hill Cemetery, Eaton Rapids, Eaton, MI; May was a schoolteacher; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • ERVA FERRIS 3.1.2.1.6.10.10.1
  • NORMAN LYLE FERRIS 3.1.2.1.6.10.10.2
  • EDITH ALICE FERRIS 3.1.2.1.6.10.10.3
