SAMUEL FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.2.3.5 | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.3.5) SAMUEL FERRIS (Sam Darney) born March 7, 1816 and died March 31, 1901; died of senile decay; lived at the landing; was worth $7.00 when he married and $40,000 when he died; married Frances Louisa Lockwood (4.4.5.5.2.3); and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • WILLIAM HENRY FERRIS 5.1.3.2.3.5.1
  • STEPHEN WARING FERRIS 5.1.3.2.3.5.2
  • MARY ELIZABETH FERRIS had not spoken to her father for 40 years; reason not 5.1.3.2.3.5.3
  • HATTIE FERRIS kept house for her father 5.1.3.2.3.5.4
