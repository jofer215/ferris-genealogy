EDMUND BURKE UNDERHILL, JR

ID: 1.4.7.6.6.3.4 | Source: John
(1.4.7.6.6.3.4) EDMUND BURKE UNDERHILL, JR born March 1, 1877 Poughkeepsie NY and died there January 8, 1976; Strawberry Hill Farm & Mayfield Farm – grew apples and strawberries; his strawberries were served to King George VI & Queen Elizabeth with the Roosevelts in Hyde Park 1939; married 1st September 25, 1901 in Poughkeepsie, Ethel White [dau of Henry Harrison & Adelaide (Lee) White] born October 16, 1879 Freedom Plains NY and died December 10, 1906 Poughkeepsie; married 2d February 28, 1926 in Poughkeepsie, Esther Mae Yager [dau of Ethan A & Cora (Kerr) Yager] born March 16, 1894 West Saugerties NY and they had 2 kids; and born to him and Ethel were:

Children (2)

  • EDMUND KENNETH UNDERHILL 1.4.7.6.6.3.4.1
  • HELEN ADALAIDE UNDERHILL 1.4.7.6.6.3.4.2
