GEORGE WASHINGTON FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.5.1.6 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.5.1.6) GEORGE WASHINGTON FERRIS born September 3, 1827 DeRuyter, Madison, NY (or Columbus, Chenango, NY) and died October 5, 1864 shortly after he was discharged from the Army as a result of being wounded/disability. In the 1850 census, he was listed as a carpenter. He married September 3, 1849 at Smyrna, (Lepha) Sophia Ann Tuthill [dau of Ziba] who was 19 years of age. They were both of Smyrna when married.
