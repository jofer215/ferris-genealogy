MARIA LOUISE MOTT

ID: 1.4.7.7.3.3.1 | Source: John
(1.4.7.7.3.3.1) MARIA LOUISE MOTT born February 11, 1854; married 1st November 26, 1872, the Honorable William C. Olliffe of NYC who died March 9,1885; married 2d April 2, 1887 the Honorable Charles Frasier MacLean, Justice of the NYC Supreme Court.

No children recorded
