WILLARD EUGENE KETTLES

ID: 1.2.3.4.2.7.3.6.1 | Source: John
(1.2.3.4.2.7.3.6.1) WILLARD EUGENE KETTLES born March 7, 1868 Fremont Center NY and died June 7, 1956 Seattle WA; buried there; married July 2, 1895 in Des Moines IA, Effie Leanora Flower [John Hugh & Clara Jane (Hathaway) Flower] born February 2, 1878 Ruby Valley MT and died August 3, 1962 Bellingham WA; buried Washelli Mausele, Seattle. Born to them was: (1.2.3.4.2.7.3.6.1.1) VERA MILDRED KETTLES (KETTELLS) born August 17, 1903 Des Moines IA and died February 27, 1987 Canyon Co CA; buried Washelli Mausele, Seattle; married January 1, 1922 in Seattle, Lester Brown Sthay [son of Joseph Alfred & Mary Laura (Brown) Sthay] born January 1, 1897 Taylor, King, WA and died December 25, 1982 Seattle. Born to them was:

No children recorded
