POLLY AMBLER

ID: 2.5.2.5.5.1.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.5.5.1.1) POLLY AMBLER born March 3, 1798 and died December 20, 1855 Somers, Westchester NY; married October 1826 Soloman Bailey [son of Gilbert & Sarah (Field) Bailey] born September 14, 1788 North Salem, Westchester, NY and died Somers NY; both buried there Ivandale Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • HARRIET ANN BAILEY 2.5.2.5.5.1.1.1
  • MARY CAROLINE BAILEY 2.5.2.5.5.1.1.2
  • GILBERT FIELD BAILEY 2.5.2.5.5.1.1.3
