HENRY HOBART HOLLY

ID: 2.3.3.2.4.1.4 | Source: Peter
(2.3.3.2.4.1.4) HENRY HOBART HOLLY born August 20, 1840 Stamford and died there December 17, 1901; buried St. John’s & St. Andrew’s Cemetery; married c1863 Margaret Ann Carnley [dau of Robert & Fanny] born December 20, 1839 and died January 2, 1902 Stamford; buried with Henry; and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • HARRIET W. HOLLY 2.3.3.2.4.1.4.1
  • SARAH LEVINA HOLLY 2.3.3.2.4.1.4.2
  • FANNY CARNLEY HOLLY 2.3.3.2.4.1.4.3
  • HENRY HOBART HOLLY, JR., 2.3.3.2.4.1.4.4
  • MARGARET CARNLEY HOLLY 2.3.3.2.4.1.4.5
