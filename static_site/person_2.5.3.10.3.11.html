JOSEPH SMITH WHITING

ID: 2.5.3.10.3.11 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.10.3.11) JOSEPH SMITH WHITING born December 1, 1788 Stamford Born to Sarah and her 2d husband, Benjamin, were: (2.5.3.10.4) THADDEUS WEED born October 27, 1748 Stamford and died February 12, 1804 Greenfield, Saratoga, NY; buried North Street Cemetery, Stamford; Rev War Vet serving between 1777-83 as a Lieutenant and Captain; married 1st Prudence Hoyt (2.5.3.6.2); 2d Anne {maiden name unk} (1752-)

No children recorded
