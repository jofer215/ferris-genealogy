RHEUA ANN LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.1.4.4.12.1 | Source: James
(5.1.4.4.12.1) RHEUA ANN LOCKWOOD (Rhona Ann) born April 23, 1803 Stamford and died there February 23, 1857, age 53y 10m; buried there Westover Cemetery; married September 12, 1824 in Stamford, Josiah Converse born c1799 Troy NY

No children recorded
