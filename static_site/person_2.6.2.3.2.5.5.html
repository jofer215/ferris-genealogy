WILLIAM KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.3.2.5.5 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.2.5.5) WILLIAM KNAPP born August 13, 1809 (September 20, 1809) Barrington NY and died date unk West Cameron, Steuben, NY (or June 20, 1872 Schuyler Co NY); married 1st July 6, 1828 at West Cameron, Eliza Jane Osborne (Osborn) born September 24, 1808 Barrington and died June 20, 1872 South Hill, Yates, NY; married 2d at West Cameron, widow Elizabeth (Whitman) Boyd.
