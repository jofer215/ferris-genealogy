MARY M SMITH

ID: 3.1.5.1.6.7.1.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.5.1.6.7.1.1) MARY M SMITH born 1843 Hayfield, Crawford, PA and died May 13, 1866 Pithole City, Venango, PA; married March 31, 1863, as his 1st wife, James Nelson Bolard [son of David & Elizabeth (Nelson) Bolard] born February 12, 1839 McGuffintown, Crawford, PA and died July 7, 1919 Denver CO

No children recorded
