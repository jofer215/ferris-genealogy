CLAYTON CHRISTIAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.9.11.5 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.9.11.5) CLAYTON CHRISTIAN born November 2, 1917 Peekskill NY and died December 13, 1983 Lake Mohegan NY at his residence at the Mohegan Country Club; buried Old VanCourtlandtville Cemetery; a welder for General Casting Company and had worked for them for 26 years. He lived most of his life in Peekskill and several years before his death at Mohegan Lake. His longtime area residency was interrupted during World War II, when he served with the U.S. Army apparently no offspring

No children recorded
