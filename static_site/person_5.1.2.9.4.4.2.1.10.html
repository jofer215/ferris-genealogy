ORLANDO SMITH JR.

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.2.1.10 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.2.1.10) ORLANDO SMITH JR. born September 8, 1901 Kent NY and died March 28, 1954 Mahopac NY; buried Raymond Hill Cemetery, Carmel NY. Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, Thursday, April 1, 1954: Orlando Smith, Jr. of Mahopac died Sunday, March 28, 1954, in the Mahopac hospital where he had been a patient for two days. His age was 52. The deceased was a son of Mrs. Sarah Bennett Smith of Mahopac Falls, and the late Orlando Smith, and he born in Kent, Sept. 8, 1901, but had spent most of his life in Mahopac. He was a farmer by occupation and worked for many years on the Ira Crane farm, on Crane road near Secor Corners in the town of Carmel. He was a bachelor. Surviving are his mother, five brothers, Chauncey and Alex, both of Danbury, George of Brewster, David and Charles of Mahopac, and three sisters, Mrs. Jay Nelson and Mrs. Sherman Roscoe, both of Carmel, and Mrs. William Hawkins of Mahopac. Interment was made in the family plot in Raymond Hill Cemetery.

No children recorded
