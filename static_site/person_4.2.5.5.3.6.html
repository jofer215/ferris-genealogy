CATHARINE LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.2.5.5.3.6 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.5.3.6) CATHARINE LOCKWOOD (Catherine H. Lockwood) born January 26, 1820 (January 20, 1810) Woodstock NY and died March 18, 1885; buried Woodstock; married September 1, 1831 Andrew Bostwick [son of Nehemiah & Sarah (Van Bunschoten) Bostwick] born August 3, 1810 Woodstock and died July 12, 1884; buried there; farmer, livery business, street commissioner; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • SARAH M. BOSTWICK 4.2.5.5.3.6.1
  • ANGELINE BOSTWICK 4.2.5.5.3.6.2
  • JAMES L. BOSTWICK 4.2.5.5.3.6.3
