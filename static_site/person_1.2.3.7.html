SARAH UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.7 | Source: John
(1.2.3.7) SARAH UNDERHILL born March 9, 1715 [second born] and died November 2, 1805 White Plains NY; buried Netherwood Baptist Churchyard, Salt Point NY or in the Presbyterian Cemetery on North Broadway, North Plains; married 1st Elisha Hyatt born April 24, 1714 Westchester Co NY and died between April 12, 1759 - April 20, 1761 [son of Caleb Hyatt] at White Plains NY; [there was an Elisha Hyatt as clerk of White Plains, 1742; an overseer, 1751; and supervisor, 1754 - assume this is the same one.]; and 2d presumably after 1760, Samuel Embree. Elisha’s will mentioned Negro wenches, a Negro boy given to Elisah and Negro girls given each daughter. Born to Sarah and Elisha were (not in birth sequence):

Children (6)

  • ELIZABETH HYATT 1.2.3.7.1
  • SARAH HYATT 1.2.3.7.2
  • MARY HYATT 1.2.3.7.3
  • ELISHA HYATT 1.2.3.7.4
  • SARAH HYATT apparently 1.2.3.7.6
  • SYLVANUS HYATT is not listed by Hoyt, but mentioned as a brother of 1.2.3.7.7
