JOHN B UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.6.2.1.1.2.3 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.1.1.2.3) JOHN B UNDERHILL (Photo) born March 26, 1845 OH and died September 7, 1939; married October 17, 1866 Rebecca Stutsman [dau of Alexander & Rhoda (Seybold) Stutsman] born February 7, 1845 IL and died January 5, 1925; she grew to womanhood on the homestead farm in Woodstick Township in Schuyler Co IL; he was a Civil War Vet who served as a Private in Company F, 148th Illinois Volunteer Infantry Regiment from February 11, 1861 to September 5, 1865 when he was honorably discharged at Nashville TN; the appeared to have moved from IL to IA between 1875-76; and born to them were (15 kids!): (1.2.6.2.1.1.2.3.1) JULIA JOSEPHENE UNDERHILL born June 18, 1867 Rushville, Schuyler, IL and died January 31, 1955; buried Greenwood Cemetery, Canton IL; remained single and stayed at home with he parents
