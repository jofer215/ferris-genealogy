CHARLENE IONA FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.5.5.4.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.5.5.4.1) CHARLENE IONA FERRIS born September 14, 1928 Chicago IL; had a Certificate from the Art Institute as a drawer and painter; married September 14, 1948 in the Chapel on the campus of Northwestern U, Evanston IL, Ralph David Palm [son of David Walter & Bernice (Glave) Palm] born December 2, 1924 Chicago IL and died August 11, 1999 Greeneville, Greene, TN; buried Fairmont/Willow Springs Ceemtery, Willow Springs IL; he was a mechanical engineer in the paper coating industry; a sculptor and studied at the School of Art Institute in Chicago which is where he met Charlene; WWII Vet wounded in the Battle of Luzon, May 5, 1945; offspring

No children recorded
