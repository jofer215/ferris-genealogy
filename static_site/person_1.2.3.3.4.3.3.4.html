ARTHUR EDWARD DALZELL

ID: 1.2.3.3.4.3.3.4 | Source: John
(1.2.3.3.4.3.3.4) ARTHUR EDWARD DALZELL born December 25, 1851 London, England and died March 9, 1941; fought in the Burma War between 1891-92; Colonel of the 52d Light Infantry between 1892-96; fought in the Boer War; invested as a Companion, Order of the Bath (C.B.) in 1900; decorated with the Commander, Order of St. Nento Avis of Portugal; fought in WWI, raising the 12th Service Battalion, Cheshire Regiment; with the 207th Brigade 1915-16; gain the rank of Honorary Brigadier General 1917; he succeeded to the title of the 13th Earl of Carnwath July 11, 1931; held the office of Representative Peer (Scotland) 1935-41; married December 4, 1902 Muriel Wyndham Knatchbull [dau of Coloen Norton & Elizabeth Florence Paxton (Wavell) Knatchbull]; born before 1887 and died July 5, 1958; and born to them was (no surviving male issue, so earldom became dormant):

Children (2)

  • ARTHUR ROBERT DALZELL 1.2.3.3.4.3.3.4.2
