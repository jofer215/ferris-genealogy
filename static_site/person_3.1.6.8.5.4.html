JOHN ROSE

ID: 3.1.6.8.5.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.5.4) JOHN ROSE born November 26, 1806 Roseville, Montague Township, Lanark, Ontario and died July 7, 1886 Easton’s Corners, Wolford Township, Grenville, Ontario; buried there; married 1st July 1, 1835 in Wolford Township, Harriet Coolidge [dau of John & Keturah (Reeve) Coolidge] born January 6, 1816 Nelson, Madison, NY and died October 21, 1861 Easton’s Corners; married 2d July 6, 1864 in Kitley, Leeds, Ontario, Margaret Wood (c1839-1877); and married 3d September 9, 1879 in Bastard Township, Leeds, Mary Jane Farnham; and born to him and Harriet were:
