ADA ELNORA PHILLIPS

ID: 2.5.2.5.1.3.9.6.5 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.5.1.3.9.6.5) ADA ELNORA PHILLIPS (1857-1920) married December 25, 1873 in Tecumseh NE, Robert Montgomery Erwin [son of Peter & Mary Israel (Goodlander) Erwin] born November 21, 1847 Ashland, Northumberland, PA and died September 1, 1936; buried Oaklawn Cemetery, Tulsa OK; he was a teamster on the Oregon Trail; farmed in Johnson Co NE and moved to OK 1903; and born to them were:
