MARTHA JULIA CHRISTIAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.9.10 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.9.10) MARTHA JULIA CHRISTIAN born c1872 Putnam Valley NY and died there December 13, 1936 at her home in Adams Corners; buried Adams Corner Cemetery; married 1st February 4, 1888 Putnam Valley NY, Dr. James Hadden [son of Samuel & Nancy (Satterly)] born September 19, 1827 Putnam Valley NY and died there January 25, 1892; buried Travis Cemetery, Putnam Valley NY; married 2d April 25, 1894 at Putnam Valley NY Charles Andrew Morley [son of William & Jane (Evans)] born January 1865 Putnam Valley NY and died there January 24, 1942; buried there Adams Corners Cemetery; Mrs. Morley was a member of the Putnam Valley Grange No. 841 for the past 30 years. She was survived by 16 grandkids. Born to Martha and James Hadden was (apparently more): (5.1.2.9.4.9.10.1) RUTH INES HADDEN born September 3, 1892 Putnam Valley NY; married Louis Bushee; resided Oregon 1936 Born to Martha and her second husband, Charles Morley were:
