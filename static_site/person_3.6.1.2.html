JOSHUA FERRIS

ID: 3.6.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.1.2) JOSHUA FERRIS (c1742-1776) married Mary Johnson (4.4.11.1). After Joshua’s death, apparently at Greenwich, Mary married Thomas Peck (3.10.2.13); see him for their offspring; and after Thomas’s death she married Nathaniel Ferris (5.3.8). Born to Joshua and Mary were:
