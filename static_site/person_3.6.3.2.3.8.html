WILLIAM H. KNAPP

ID: 3.6.3.2.3.8 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.3.8) WILLIAM H. KNAPP born July 1, 1830 CT and died February 21, 1883; married Mary Jane Denny (Mary Denna) who died February 21, 1883; interred Hortontown Cemetery. Note that they died the same day. After William’s death, Storm Emmons was made guardian of the children. Born to them were:

Children (5)

  • RICHARD KNAPP 3.6.3.2.3.8.1
  • RUSSELL E. KNAPP of Poughkeepsie NY 3.6.3.2.3.8.2
  • ENOS KNAPP 3.6.3.2.3.8.3
  • WILLIAM H. KNAPP, JR., 3.6.3.2.3.8.4
  • CATHERINE KNAPP 3.6.3.2.3.8.5
