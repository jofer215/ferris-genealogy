ID: 2.6.7.3.5.8.1.1.1 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.3.5.8.1.1.1) ROY WESLEY SMITH born November 18, 1893 Pullman, Cherry, NE and died May 9, 1975 Ogden, Weber, UT; buried Memorial Gardens of the Wasatch, Wasatch UT. He married January 3, 1922 in SLC UT, Arvilla May Eddins (Dot) [dau of William Kemp W. & Lelia May (Perry) Eddins] born November 26, 1894 Provo UT and died March 9, 1979 Roy UT; buried with Roy.
