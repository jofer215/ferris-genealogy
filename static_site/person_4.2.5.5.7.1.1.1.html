ELIZABETH ANN LANE

ID: 4.2.5.5.7.1.1.1 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.5.7.1.1.1) ELIZABETH ANN LANE born c1841/42 MI and died March 6, 1877 Tampico IL; buried there; married 1857 in MI, William Oscar Hixson (Photo) [son of George Washington & Priscella (Gregg) Hixon Sr] born 1836 Warren Co OH and died November 16, 1917 Hillsdale IA; buried Blanchard IA; farmer; Civil War Vet – Private with Company B, 64th Illinois Infantry Regiment – on ILGen website, listed as Oscar Hixon, Recruit from Yorktown IL enlisted at age 25y, December 14, 1861; 5’10” with black hair, dark eyes and complexions, discharged October 21, 1862 because of a disability; he survived several battles/skirmishes only to injure a hip while unloading wheat from a steamer at Cornish MS, the injury ultimately became subject to discharge; after Elizabeth’s death, William married Mary Agnes (Hixson), widow of William’s brother, Charles.
