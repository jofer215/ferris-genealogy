MARY ANN

ID: 1.4.7.3 | Source: John
(1.4.7.3) MARY ANN (ANNA) FERRIS born 1757 (c1761) Grove Farm, Throgs Neck, Westchester, NY and died before April 25, 1795 age 33; married November 25, 1778 in Westchester Co, as his first wife, Benjamin Pell [son of Joshua & Phoebe (Palmer)] born 1740/50 Pelham Manor, Westchester Co and died December 2 (March 4), 1828 age 87. Benjamin was a Quaker. He served with the Loyalist during the Rev. War and was forced to flee to Canada. He probably went to Sheleburne, Nova Scotia and returned to New York City after the war. He married secondly a Ms. Titus and had offspring. Born to Mary and Benj were:

Children (5)

  • ALFRED SANDS PELL 1.4.7.3.2
