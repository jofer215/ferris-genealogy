JAMES BERNARD ZANIBONI

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.1.5.2.10.7 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.1.5.2.10.7) JAMES BERNARD ZANIBONI born August 14, 1967 White Plains NY and died August 6, 1998 Lee MA; lived with Tracy L. Dushane; lived with Mary J. Brown; married May 7, 1994 Kelly A. Beeler. Obituary: Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, Aug. 13, 1998: James Bernard Zaniboni, a former Pawling resident, died unexpectedly at his home in Lee, Mass. He was 30. He born Aug. 14, 1967, to Emile Robert and Dorothy Tompkins Zaniboni in White Plains. Mr. Zaniboni, who attended Wahconah High School in Dalton, Mass., was employed by the Meade Paper Corporation in Lee as a paper maker. In addition to his mother of Peru, Mass.; survivors include two daughters, Stephanie M. of Pittsfield, Mass. and Ashley J. of Lenox, Mass.; one brother, Robert of Peru, Mass.; five sisters, Mary Keenan of Vestal, NY; Malda Miller and Debra Brill, both of Pawling, Donna Baker of Pittsfield, Mass.; Linda Baczek of Lenox, Mass., and many nieces and nephews.
