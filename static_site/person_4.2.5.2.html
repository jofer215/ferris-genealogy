PHILLIP LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.2.5.2 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.2) PHILLIP LOCKWOOD (#181) (Philip) born February 28, 1750 Greenwich and died October 29, 1831; served as a Private in George Peck’s Company, 1776-77; married 1st in 1773 Hannah Clason born July 15, 1750 Stamford CT and died December 15, 1814; 2d c1823/25 Hannah Wickes born March 19, 1761 and died August 18, 1836; they were buried at the Tomac Avenue Cemetery, Old Greenwich. Born to Phillip and the 1st Hannah were:
