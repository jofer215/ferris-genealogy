(5.1.2.9.4.4.2.1.6) DAVID S. SMITH Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, Thursday, Jan. 26, 1956: David Smith, a resident of Mahopac Falls for many years and foreman for the town of Carmel highway department, died Monday, Jan. 23, 1956 in the Peekskill hospital where he had been a patient for two days. His age was 65. He was born in the town of Kent, April 18, 1890, a son of Sarah Bennett Smith and the late Orlando Smith. Surviving are his mother of Mahopac Falls, four brothers, Chauncey and Alex of Danbury, George of Brewster and Charles of Mahopac and three sisters, Mrs. Sherman Roscoe, Mrs. Wm. Harkins and Mrs. Jay Nelson, all of Carmel. He was a member of the Mt. Carmel Baptist church and the Mahopac Fire department. The body was placed in the vault in Raymond Hill Cemetery and interment will be made in the family plot there in the spring.

No children recorded
