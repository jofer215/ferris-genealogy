SARAH UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.4.4 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.4) SARAH UNDERHILL born July 29, 1751 Philipsburg, Westchester, NY and died January 1, 1828 Chappaqa, Westchester, NY; married December 11, 1778 at Chappaqua, as his 2d wife, Daniel Haviland [son of Benjamin & Charlotte (Parke) Haviland III] born October 20, 1746 Rye, Westchester, NY and died September 19, 1828 Chappaqua; and born to them were: (1.2.5.4.4.1) JACOB HAVILAND (1779-1831) married 1801 Roby Howland (17751865)
