SUSAN ELIZABETH SNELL

ID: 5.1.3.2.4.5.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.4.5.1) SUSAN ELIZABETH SNELL born November 27, 1831 Rushville, Franklin, IN and died June 4, 1911 Urbana IA at the home of her daughter Anna; buried Bear Creek Cemetery, Vinton IA; married November 12, 1854 in Greensburg IN, Joel Jackson Long [son of John & Nancy (White) Long] born November 10/12, 1828 near Greensburg IN and died August 14/19, 1902 Greensburg IN where he and Susan had gone on a visit; buried Harrison Township, Vinton IA; they moved to Harrison township, Vinton Co IA in 1856; and born to them were:
