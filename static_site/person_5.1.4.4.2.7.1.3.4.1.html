ROGER E PIEHL

ID: 5.1.4.4.2.7.1.3.4.1 | Source: James
(5.1.4.4.2.7.1.3.4.1) ROGER E PIEHL born June 12, 1921 Marion ND and died February 15, 2003 Brainerd ND; buried there Evergreen Cemetery; graduated from Maroin High School, attended U of ND-Grand Forks, until he joined the Army; while stationd at Fort Sill OK, he was in charge of organizing and managing an Army orchestra; after his discharge, he continued this education at Valley City State College graduating with an instrumental music degree; had several teaching assignments in ND for 4y, then superintendent of schools in Winbledon ND for 10y; in 1963 in took a job at Bemidji State U supervising student teachers; 1966 he college stationed his supervisory activities in the Brainerd area and he continued with that job until he retired in 1983; married Harriet {mnu}; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • JOHN PIEHL of Sauk Rapids 2003 5.1.4.4.2.7.1.3.4.1.1
  • JANE PIEHL 5.1.4.4.2.7.1.3.4.1.2
