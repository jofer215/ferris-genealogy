WILLIAM L. LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.11.2.1.2.2.6 | Source: Mary
(4.6.11.2.1.2.2.6) WILLIAM L. LOCKWOOD (#3407) born June 25, 1858 Oswego IL; married November 11, 1879 in Kendall Co IL, Etta Ellsworth [dau of William Josiah & Sarah Jane (Winans) Ellsworth] born c1864 IL; 1880 census finds William in Plano, Kendall, IL; a harness maker
