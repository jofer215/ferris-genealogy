JOSEPH HAIT

ID: 2.5.2.11.6 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.11.6) JOSEPH HAIT (Hoyt) (#3055) born May 20, 1760 Stamford and died there August 5, 1796 probably at North Stamford where he lived; buried North Stamford Cemetery; married May 13, 1790 in Stamford, Hannah Smith [dau of Charles & Hannah (Weed) Smith] who died October 22, 1847 age 85 y; buried North Stamford Cemetery. Hannah remarried, after Joseph’s death, to Amos Weed Jr. Born to Joseph and Hannah were (Hait or Hoyt - will go with Hoyt):

Children (3)

  • BENJAMIN HOYT 2.5.2.11.6.1
  • HARVEY HOYT 2.5.2.11.6.2
  • JOSEPH SMITH HOYT 2.5.2.11.6.3
