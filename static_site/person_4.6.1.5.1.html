BETSEY LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.1.5.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.5.1) BETSEY LOCKWOOD (#688) born April 4, 1762 Pound Ridge NY and died February 14, 1848; buried Pound Ridge Cemetery; married February 16, 1777 Alsop Hunt. Alsop was captured by Colonel Tarleton of the Royal Dragoons; he was carried to New York and lodged in the Provost prison, July 2, 1779. Alsop died June 16, 1816.

No children recorded
