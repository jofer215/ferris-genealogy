THOMAS PURDY BRUNDAGE

ID: 3.10.7.10.4.2.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.7.10.4.2.5) THOMAS PURDY BRUNDAGE born January 9, 1849 North Castle NY and died September 12, 1917 Armonk, Westchester, NY; dairy farmer; married 1st September 18, 1873 Mary Emily Banks [dau of John & Emily (Miller) Banks] born September 16, 1848 and died September 30, 1892; married 2d November 3, 1897 Anna D Alley [dau of Harvey] born October 3, 1867 and died January 16, 1937; all buried Middle Patent Cemetery; and born to him and Mary were:

Children (2)

  • MARY AUGUSTA BRUNDAGE 3.10.7.10.4.2.5.2
