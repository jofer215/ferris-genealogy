MARION LOUISE MARTIN

ID: 3.10.8.3.6.1.1.1.3.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.8.3.6.1.1.1.3.3) MARION LOUISE MARTIN born November 30, 1921 Cleveland OH; married May 15, 1942 at Clearwater FL, Robert Clifton Foskett [son of Roy Clifton & Juanita Esther (Shepard) born December 30, 1913 Worcester MA. Robert is a WWII USAAF Veteran; a mechanic. Marion, a school teacher, is the provider of this twig - thanks Marion!. They reside in St. Petersburg FL. Born to them were:
