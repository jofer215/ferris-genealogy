CHARLES MATTERSON LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.1.4.1.4.2 | Source: James
(5.1.4.1.4.2) CHARLES MATTERSON LOCKWOOD born 1810 VT and died May 20, 1887 Stamford; married 1st September 24, 1834 in Stamford, Phebe Webb Todd (3.11.4.2.3.1.9); both buried there Long Ridge Cemetery; married 2d Betsey A Scofield born 1835 CT; and born to Charles and Phebe were:

Children (4)

  • CHARLES ALVAH LOCKWOOD 5.1.4.1.4.2.2
  • EDWARD ALONZO LOCKWOOD 5.1.4.1.4.2.3
  • ANN ELIZABETH LOCKWOOD 5.1.4.1.4.2.4
