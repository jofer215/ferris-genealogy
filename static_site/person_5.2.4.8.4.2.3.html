CHARLES AMBROSE BATES

ID: 5.2.4.8.4.2.3 | Source: James
(5.2.4.8.4.2.3) CHARLES AMBROSE BATES born April 27, 1837 Darien CT and died there May 30, 1915; buried Spring Grove Cemetery, Darien. He married October 13, 1859 Darien, Ann Eliza Slason [dau of George Nelson & Eliza (Turney) Slason] born January 7, 1832 Darien and died there June 11, 1906; buried Spring Grove Cemetery.
