JACOB SCHIEFFELIN

ID: 1.7.2.1.5.6 | Source: John
(1.7.2.1.5.6) JACOB SCHIEFFELIN IV born April 20, 1793 Montreal, Canada (or NYC) and died December 27, 1880 Tioga, Tioga, PA; studied medicine in NYC 1813-14 and afterwards joined the NY State infantry where he rose to the rank of Colonel when he left in 1825, to rejoin his brothers in H. H. Schiefflin & Co; he was stationed in Mexico for most of his service with the company; in 1827 he returned home, left the company and turned his attention to his real estate holdings in Tioga and Lycoming Counties PA; moved to Willardsburg, later Tioga PA and acted as an agent for himself, his family, and others; in January 1849, he traveled to California with his sons Alfred and Edward on the ship Morrison; they arrived in September but book passage home via Panama in November; married Elizabeth (Berard) Chapman [dau of James & Jane (Black) Berard] born April 22, 1797 NYC and died January 27, 1881 Tioga PA [Chapman was name of her stepfather]; and born to them were:
