WILL THOMAS STATES

ID: 3.1.2.1.6.3.5.1.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.6.3.5.1.3) WILL THOMAS STATES born October 6, 1864 near Norwalk OH and died March 18, 1947 in his daughter's home near Berea OH; buried Woodlawn Cemetery, Norwalk; married 1st December 31, 1887 at Los Angeles CA, Katherine Wheeler and born to them was a daughter; married 2d April 8, 1891 at Sundusky, Erie, OH, Flora Morris and they had 7 kids

No children recorded
