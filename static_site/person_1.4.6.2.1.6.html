WILLIAM FERRIS BOWNE

ID: 1.4.6.2.1.6 | Source: John
(1.4.6.2.1.6) WILLIAM FERRIS BOWNE born October 19, 1819 Westchester NY and died September 1873 San Francisco CA; married June 27, 1844 in San Francisco, Emma Louise Stebbins [dau of William & Bridget Eliza (Jordan) Stebbins] born February 5, 1814 St. Croix, West Indies and died in San Francisco. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • LOUISE BOWNE 1.4.6.2.1.6.1
  • WILLIAM STEBBINS BOWNE 1.4.6.2.1.6.2
  • JAMES STEBBINS BOWNE 1.4.6.2.1.6.3
  • KATE STEBBINS BOWNE 1.4.6.2.1.6.4
