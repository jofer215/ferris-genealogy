EDWARD BENNETT CLOSE

ID: 3.10.7.2.7.3.1.5.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.7.2.7.3.1.5.1) EDWARD BENNETT CLOSE born c1860 (1882) NYC and died 1955 Greenwich CT ; stockbroker, investment banker; managing governor of the American Hospital in Paris; married 1st , 1905, as her 1st of 4 husbands, Marjorie Merriweather Post (Photo) [dau of Charles William & Ella Letitia (Merriweather) Post] born March 15, 1887 Springfield IL and died Septemb 12, 1973, heiress to Postum Cereal Co and then founder of General Foods Corporation – divorced 1919 – her 2d marriage was to Edward Francis Hutton, thus the name change for the daughters, and they had a child, Nedenia Hutton (Dina Merrill), the actress; 2d Elizabeth Taliaferro [dau of Edwin Munford & Catherine (Mitchell) Taliaferro] born 1897 Galveston TX and died 1988; plenty of information on the “net on Ms Post; and born to them were:
