DONALD LEROY FERRIS

ID: 1.4.1.3.7.1.2.1.3.1 | Source: John
(1.4.1.3.7.1.2.1.3.1) DONALD LEROY FERRIS born March 28, 1926 Port Chester, Rye NY; graduated from Rye High School 1944 and immediately upon graduation was drafted into the Army; served from June 1944 until March 1946; graduated from the New York State Institute of Applied Arts and Sciences in White Plains NY with a degree in mechanical engineering; married June 8, 1952 at Mamaroneck NY, (Lottie) Loretta C. Brewio who died March 31, 1997. Donald work for Sikorsky Aircraft in Bridgeport CT from 1955 to 1987, when he retired. During this timeframe, he was granted 41 US patents in his name and assigned to United Technologies of which Sikorsky Aircraft was a division. He was also assigned many foreign patents pertaining to rotor heads and their components. In 1991, a special lifetime designer’s achievement award was presented to him and in his name for future recipients.
