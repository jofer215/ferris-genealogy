ANNA SEAMAN LEGGETT

ID: 1.4.7.7.10.6 | Source: John
(1.4.7.7.10.6) ANNA SEAMAN LEGGETT (Photo) born April 19, 1848 Roslyn, Queens, L. I., NY and died December 5, 1934 Chicago IL; buried with her parents at Oak Hill Cemetery, Pontiac MI; married 1st June 6, 1867 in Detroit, William Henry Christian born December 2, 1845 Detroit and died May 11, 1911; married 2d May 7, 1874 in Detroit, Corrydon Chandler Randall born June 14, 1846 Hamilton, Ontario, Canada and died in August 1907 in Drayton Plains, Oakland, MI.
