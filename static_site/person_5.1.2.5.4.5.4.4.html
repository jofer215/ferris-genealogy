NATHANIEL LINCOLN BALES

ID: 5.1.2.5.4.5.4.4 | Source: James
(5.1.2.5.4.5.4.4) NATHANIEL LINCOLN BALES born November 1, 1860 Conkeytown IL and died March 5, 1941 Vermilion Co IL; they lived in Ogden until they purchased their farm near Fithian 1910; married June 30, 1884 in Vermilion Co, Rachel Margaret Henderson [dau of Milton & Mary (Chenoweth) Henderson] born November 21, 1863 Newton, Jasper, IA and died May 7, 1942 Vermilion Co IL; both buried Stearns Cemetery, Muncie IL; and born to them were: (5.1.2.5.4.5.4.4.1) JOHN MILTON BALES born April 28, 1885 IA (Vermilion Co IL) and died November 8, 1945; married Mary Louise Tudor born September 14, 1891 and died April 1981; and born to them were 6 kids, 4 of which were:
