FLORENCE REBECCA

ID: 1.1.4.4.1.3.4.6 | Source: John
(1.1.4.4.1.3.4.6) FLORENCE REBECCA (ALICE) MOFFAT born July 9, 1906 Menomonie, Dunn, WI and died March 1984 Houston TX; 1930 resided Seattle WA; married three times with her 1st marriage being to a Mr. Archambeau - apparently no offspring; married 2d Archie M. Halsall and they lived in New Orleans for a time prior to their divorce; married 3d a Mr. Lloyd. Born to Alice and Archie were:

Children (3)

  • PATRICIA HALSALL 1.1.4.4.1.3.4.6.1
  • VIRGINIA HALSALL 1.1.4.4.1.3.4.6.2
  • DARROCH LLOYD who was described as a dwarf 1.1.4.4.1.3.4.6.3
