HENRY HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.5.5.3 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.5.5.3) HENRY HOYT (#3550) born August 2, 1775 and died North Salem NY; married 1st December 27, 1796 Joanna Halstead who died December 6, 1816 at age 41y; and 2d Mina Smith. He lived in South Salem and was town-clerk there in 1817. Born to them were:

Children (2)
