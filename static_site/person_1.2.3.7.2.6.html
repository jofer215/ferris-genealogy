PHOEBE FROST

ID: 1.2.3.7.2.6 | Source: John
(1.2.3.7.2.6) PHOEBE FROST (Phebe) born January 28, 1776 Carmel NY and died May 26, 1862 Beaver Dams, Schuyler, NY; buried Beaver Dams Cemetery; married February 2, 1797 at Carmel, Joseph Cole born October 29, 1775 Carmel NY and died March 9, 1855 Beaver Dams NY [son of Joseph and Rebecca (Berry) Cole]. Joseph was a veteran of the War of 1812 and was granted a warrant for 40 acres of government land for his services, but he never took up this land and probably sold his right to it. They settled in Beaver Dams in 1833; Beaver Dams so named because of fact that there were two dams, one at and one nearby. It was formerly called West Catlin and first called Beaver Dams in 1843.
