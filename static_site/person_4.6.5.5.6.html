ZILPAH HAIT

ID: 4.6.5.5.6 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.5.6) ZILPAH HAIT (#3399) born June 16, 1772 Pound Ridge NY and died August 8, 1852 Westminster, Canada; married June 10, 1794 in Saratoga Co, Amos Clark born May 14, 1768 Bedford NY and died May 2, 1840; both buried in Westminster, near London, Can. W.

No children recorded
