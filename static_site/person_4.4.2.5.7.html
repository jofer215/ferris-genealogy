EPENETUS WEBB LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.2.5.7 | Source: Mary
(4.4.2.5.7) EPENETUS WEBB LOCKWOOD (#766) born January 12, 1805 Greenwich and died September 30, 1843 Ashtabula OH of dysentery and bilious affection; married January 4, 1832 at Ashtabula, Elizabeth Smith [dau of Marshall & Rheuhama (Dunbolton) Smith] born May 19, 1810 Buffalo NY and died September 27, 1884 Philadelphia PA. After Epenetus’s death, she married, her brother-in-law, August 30, 1845, Elon Dunbar, as his second wife, one of the most prominent merchants in Philadelphia. Born to Epenetus and Elizabeth were:

Children (3)

  • WILLIAM EDWARD LOCKWOOD 4.4.2.5.7.1
  • CHARLES LORENZO LOCKWOOD 4.4.2.5.7.2
  • ELON DUNBAR LOCKWOOD 4.4.2.5.7.3
