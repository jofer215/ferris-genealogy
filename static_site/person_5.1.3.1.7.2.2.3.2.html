OLIN F. FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.2.3.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.2.3.2) OLIN F. FERRIS (Olen F) born January (June) 2, 1861 Newbury, Geauga, NY and died there November 9, 1895; age 34y 9m 3d; married January 17, 1883 Abbie Downing born May 9, 1865 Roughemont, Canada (sister to above) and buried July 11, 1940 – services held at the Newberry Center Church.

No children recorded
