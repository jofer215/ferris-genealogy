WRIGHT KNAPP

ID: 2.6.7.4.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.4.2) WRIGHT KNAPP born December 29, 1773 Stamford CT and died August 23, 1853 Pembroke, Genesee, NY; buried with his wives at Pembroke Cemetery, Corfu NY; married 1st Sarah Wey born 1772 and died November 3, 1840; married 2d Olive Tubbs born 1780 and died March 24, 1856 Pembroke NY; and born to Wright and Sarah were:

Children (5)

  • GERMAN KNAPP 2.6.7.4.2.1
  • MELINDA KNAPP died age 18y 2.6.7.4.2.2
  • LEONARD D. KNAPP 2.6.7.4.2.3
  • MASON KNAPP 2.6.7.4.2.4
  • MARIA KNAPP 2.6.7.4.2.5
