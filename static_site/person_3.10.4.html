DAVID PECK

ID: 3.10.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.4) DAVID PECK (GJ59-7J) born December 15, 1694 Greenwich and died 1756 North Castle, Westchester, NY; married Elizabeth {maiden name unk} who survived David; a farmer and resided in Greenwich until about 1743, when they moved to the adjoining town of North Castle; and born to them were: (3.10.4.1) DAVID PECK was living in North Castle NY 1772

Children (1)

  • JONAS PECK was living in North Castle NY 1756. 3.10.4.2
