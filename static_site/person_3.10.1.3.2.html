MOSES PECK

ID: 3.10.1.3.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.3.2) MOSES PECK born c1750 Greenwich and died there March 11, 1828 age 77y; buried in the Tomac Avenue Cemetery, (Old Sound Beach) Old Greenwich; served as a Private in Captain George Peck’s Company of Colonel John Mead’s Regiment in the Revolutionary War at New York, from August 13 to September 5, 1776; reenlisted and served in the same Company from November 1, 1776 to January 23, 1777. Moses married Hannah Reynolds born 1751 and died October 11, 1828 age 77y (buried with her husband) and born to them were:

Children (2)

  • SAMUEL PECK 3.10.1.3.2.1
  • SYLVANUS PECK 3.10.1.3.2.2
