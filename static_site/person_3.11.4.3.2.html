PETER KNAPP

ID: 3.11.4.3.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.4.3.2) PETER KNAPP born February 25, 1755 Greenwich CT and died April 13, 1839 Spafford, Delaware, NY; buried Spaffod Cemetery. Rev War Vet April 1775 Minute Man serving under Captain Abraham Mead, Westchester County Militia. He married May 18, 1775 in Rye NY, Dinah Guion [dau of John & Annah (Hart) Guion] born May 7, 1757.
