JOHN HOMER MAGEE

ID: 4.6.1.2.7.1.5.3.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.7.1.5.3.1) JOHN HOMER MAGEE born June 1, 1904 Sioux City, Woodbury, IA; educated at the U. of Washington and Boston U School of Theology; served in Massachusetts before going to Montana; in 1970 associate secretary of Commission on World Service and Finance of the Methodist Church since 1953; ordained minister, a pastor in Washington State and Montana and a district superintendent for several years in Montana; 1948-53 district superintendent of Glacier Park District; married Bernice {maiden name unk}

No children recorded
