MINNIE MILDRED BOWEN

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.3.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.3.4) MINNIE MILDRED BOWEN born September 4, 1905 Traverse City MI and died November 1, 1994; married August 6, 1924 in Traverse City, Jesse Dale Hines [son of Daniel & Grace I (Gilbert) Hines] born March 10, 1898 Traverse City and died December 16, 1989 DeLande FL; buried Oakwood Cemetery, Traverse City MI; machinist retired from Ford MoCo after 38y; WWI Army Veteran; and they had 2 kids
