JAMES H. FERRIS,

ID: 3.3.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.3.2) JAMES H. FERRIS,(Jr.?), born March 22, 1720 Greenwich and died c1777 Poundridge, Westchester, NY; March 6, 1739, James choose Captain John Mead as his guardian; married Mary {maiden name unk} who died October 13, 1803 age 77. They joined the New Canaan CT Congregational Church, 1747, and later moved to Poundridge. Born to them were:

Children (7)

  • SARAH FERRIS was baptized September 20, 1747 New Canaan CT; married June 18, 1766 3.3.2.1
  • RACHEL FERRIS 3.3.2.3
  • JAMES FERRIS was baptized May 20, 1753 New Canaan CT; married February 24, 1774 3.3.2.4
  • ELIZABETH FERRIS was baptized July 13, 1755 Church of Christ, Salem, Westchester, 3.3.2.5
  • GOULD FERRIS 3.3.2.6
  • HANNAH FERRIS 3.3.2.7
  • ANNA FERRIS was baptized August 12, 1764 Salem NY; married Daniel Nichols of 3.3.2.8
