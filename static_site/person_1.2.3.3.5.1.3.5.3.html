ANNA M UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.3.5.1.3.5.3 | Source: John
(1.2.3.3.5.1.3.5.3) ANNA M UNDERHILL born October 1, 1892 MI and died August 28, 1968; married October 1, 1912 Arthur E Day [dau of Nelson Elkaney & Frances Marie (Pinch) Day] born December 23, 1890 MI and died May 31, 1980 Charlotte MI; both buried Maple Hill Cemetery, Charlotte, Eaton, MI; attended Olivet College; maple sugar bush farmer; and they had 4 kids

No children recorded
