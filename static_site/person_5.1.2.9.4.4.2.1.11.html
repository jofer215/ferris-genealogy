(5.1.2.9.4.4.2.1.11) CHARLES W SMITH Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, Jan. 16, 1964: Charles W. Smith, 60, of Mahopac Falls, died Sunday, Jan. 12, 1964 in the Mahopac Hospital where he had been a patient eight days. The deceased born March 28, 1903 in Towners, a son of the late Orlando and Sarah Bennett Smith. When he was very young the family moved to Mahopac Falls where he had since lived. He was employed by the Town of Carmel highway department. Surviving are his wife, the former Marion P. Grady, a son, Richard J., of Carmel and two daughters, Mrs. Dominick Farina of Mahopac and Miss Nancy Smith of Mahopac Falls. He also leaves one brother, George Smith of Brewster and two sisters, Mrs. William Harkins of Mahopac and Mrs. Jay Nelson of Carmel, and three grandchildren. Interment will be made in Raymond Hill Cemetery, Carmel. Born to them were:
