CHAUNCEY H AUSTIN

ID: 5.1.3.1.5.4.6.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.5.4.6.4) CHAUNCEY H AUSTIN born September 21, 1832 Erie Co PA and died December 18, 1908 San Diego CA; buried there Greenwood Cemetery; Civil War Vet, Private with Co F, 156th Illinois Infantry; shoe manufacturer; married 1st June 26, 1853 Teresa Pearson; 2d March 10, 1878 in Mankaota, Blue Earth, MN Albertine Crocker [dau of Lovel Ranson Jackson & Hannah (Small) Crocker] born October 12, 1843 Springfield ME and died February 2, 1929 San Diego; buried with Chauncey; and born to him and Teresa were:
