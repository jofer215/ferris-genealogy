ASAHEL S HOYT

ID: 4.6.5.11.2.9 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.11.2.9) ASAHEL S HOYT (#4403) born May 13, 1820 Pound Ridge NY; a farmer and resided in Pound Ridge; married 1st February 14, 1844 Jennett Miller who died June 18, 1849 age 31y 3m; buried private cemetery near corner at Sarles Corner, Pound Ridge; and 2d October 13, 1852 Emma L. Greenly; cemetery records have him listed as Ashabel S. Hoyt. Born to Asahel and Jennett were:

Children (3)

  • GEORGE E. HOYT 4.6.5.11.2.9.1
  • MARY M. HOYT 4.6.5.11.2.9.2
  • HATTIE L. HOYT 4.6.5.11.2.9.3
