KATHERINE CLAPHAM

ID: 1.4.1.8.1.1.2 | Source: John
(1.4.1.8.1.1.2) KATHERINE CLAPHAM born September 21, 1847 NYC and was a widow in June 1880, and died July 23, 1880 Middletown NY; married July 20, 1869 Dr. Edward Clapham (photo), her cousin, of Leedes, England, born October 25, 1836 and died October 11, 1879 Astoria, Long Island NY; surgeon with 7th Michigan Infantry Regiment, Civil War; a song writer. From the Brooklyn-Union Argus, January 19, 1878: Physicians Settle Their Quarrel – The quarrel between Dr. Otto Rotton and his young partner, Dr. Edward Clapham an action in the City Court to dissolve a partnership has been settled by a mutual agreement. Dr. Rotton alleged that Dr. Clapham was immoral and intemperate, and their practice sufferance because of it. The parties have agreed to release each other from all claims, Dr. Rotton assuming the debts of the firm. Same Guy? And born to them was (and probably more);
