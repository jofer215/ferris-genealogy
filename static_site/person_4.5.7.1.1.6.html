REMBRANDT LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.5.7.1.1.6 | Source: Mary
(4.5.7.1.1.6) REMBRANDT LOCKWOOD (#1364) (1815-1889) (Photo) was an architect and artist. He painted The Last Judgment, a work of nine years, which was commenced in Germany, where he resided for four years, and completed in Newark NJ; married Sarah Ursule Despeaux [dau of Joseph] (1820-); abandoned his family when his daughter Francis was 17y; and born to them were:
