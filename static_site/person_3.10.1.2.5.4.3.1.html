WILLIAM SHAVER

ID: 3.10.1.2.5.4.3.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.5.4.3.1) WILLIAM SHAVER born and died 1847 Steuben Co NY and died 1920-30 CA; married 1861 in St Louis MI, Ozema Allen born 1853 NY and died in CA; and born to them were: (3.10.1.2.5.4.3.1.1) LILLIE MAY SHAVER born June 10, 1872 Gratiot Co MI and died 1935 MI; married 1st January 14, 1888 in Mecosta Co MI, Clarence Alfonso Sanderson [son of John & Clarissa (Covey) Sanderson] born 1862 Isabella Co MI – divorced c1903 – and died 1933 Crow WV; buried there Scott Cemetery; 2d c1907 in Gratiot Co MI, Samuel Sipe born 1876 MI; and born to her and Clarence were:

Children (2)

  • DAISY JANE SHAVER 3.10.1.2.5.4.3.1.2
