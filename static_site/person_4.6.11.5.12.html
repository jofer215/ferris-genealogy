RUTH BENEDICT

ID: 4.6.11.5.12 | Source: Mary
(4.6.11.5.12) RUTH BENEDICT (#744) born May 17, 1785; married Jacob Reed ****************************************************************************** From the Churchyard/Orr Family Museum Homepage: Thankful Lockwood [dau of James & Mary (Norton) Lockwood] baptized in May, 1762 at St. John’s Episcopal Church, Stamford and died October 3 (30), 1843, Eden, Erie, NY; married 1st c1781, Samuel St. John born in or before 1756 and died 1819 in the Village of Hamburg, Erie, NY; served in the Rev. War; Thankful married 2d September 7, 1828 Simeon Clark born November 17, 1754 Wrentham MA and died April 25, 1841 Eden NY. Simeon settled in the hollow of Eighteen Mile Creek about 1820; he built the grist mill that was still in operation in 1898; he also built a saw mill and a shop for making spinning wheels. Thankful St. John was admitted to the Bedford Presbyterian Church in Bedford, Westchester County NY on November 4, 1787. The St. John family lived in Pound Ridge, Westchester County NY in 1790; shortly thereafter moving to Greenfield, Saratoga County NY and stayed there until about the time of the War of 1812, when they moved to Hamburg or Eden, Erie County NY.
