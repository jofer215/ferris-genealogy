WAYNE ARTHUR FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2.5 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2.5) WAYNE ARTHUR FERRIS born March 2, 1919 Draper SD; graduated from Nettleton College, Sioux Falls SD; employed by North American Aviation and in the shipyards until 1944, when he went to Saudi Arabia returning 1946; graduated from the Kansas City Arts Institute 1951, and worked for Burns & McDonnell the same year, retiring 1981; never married; enjoyed performing volunteer work for different organizations; resided in Overland Park KS

No children recorded
