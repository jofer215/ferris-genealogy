FRANCES MARTHA HOEHLE

ID: 3.8.1.2.2.4.4.3.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.8.1.2.2.4.4.3.1) FRANCES MARTHA HOEHLE born March 22, 1909 Waukesha WI and died September 12, 1987 Delray Beach FL; married 1st October 3, 1931 in Waukesha, Thomas Joseph McGurn [son of Thomas Joseph, Sr. & Mary Florence (Graham) McGurn] born June 1, 1903 Philadelphia PA and died November 13, 1941 Waukesha WI; and 2d Ernst Otto Lehnbeuter.
