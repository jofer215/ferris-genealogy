ALEXANDER FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.6.3.1.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.6.3.1.3) ALEXANDER FERRIS born December 1842 Greenwich and died May 16, 1864 at the Battle of Drury Bluff VA. Alexander enlisted, as a Private, October 2, 1861 at Hartford CT in Company P., 10th Regiment of Connecticut Volunteers to serve 3 years. He was a Corporal and died of wounds received in action at Proctor's Creek VA May 16, 1864; received wounds on the 16th and died on the 18th. Surgeon General Report shows him dying May 19th at Hammonds Hospital, Point Lookout MD of gunshot wounds. He married, March 19, 1864, Hannah Gibson; this was documented by a statement by Samuel Close, the town clerk of Greenwich. Hannah received a pension #58409. There were no offspring - from National Archives, Pension Records

No children recorded
