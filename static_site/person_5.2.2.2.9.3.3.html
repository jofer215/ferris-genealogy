JULIA ANN BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.2.9.3.3 | Source: James
(5.2.2.2.9.3.3) JULIA ANN BUXTON born May 12, 1837 Stamford CT and died Winfield, Cowley, KS; married October 3/5, 1855 in Stamford, Charles Keeler [son of Thomas Smith & Mary Emily (Gilbert) Keller] born July 17, 1825 Ridgefield CT and died Winfield KS. They resided in Kane County IL (1873) before moving on to Kansas. Born to them were:

Children (5)

  • GEORGE SMITH KEELER 5.2.2.2.9.3.3.1
  • WILLIAM FRANKIN KEELER 5.2.2.2.9.3.3.2
  • MILON HERBERT KEELER 5.2.2.2.9.3.3.3
  • LOUISE LENORA KEELER 5.2.2.2.9.3.3.4
  • EMMA Y. KEELER 5.2.2.2.9.3.3.5
