EDGAR RICHARD KNAPP, M.D.,

ID: 2.6.2.3.9.2.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.9.2.3) EDGAR RICHARD KNAPP, M.D., born February 7, 1834 Danby NY; a hemeopathic physician and served in the Civil War with Company E, 6th U.S. Cavalry; discharged September 10, 1864; married 1st November 14, 1865 at Flint MI, Helen Clark (-1868); married 2d April 9, 1871 at Saginaw MI, Mary Emma Fisher who was a teacher.
