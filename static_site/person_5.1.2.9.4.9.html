WASHINGTON LAFAYETTE CHRISTIAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.9 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.9) WASHINGTON LAFAYETTE CHRISTIAN born December 2, 1826 Putnam Valley (Phillipstown) NY and died March 7, 1921 (age 94) Peekskill NY at the home of his niece, Mrs. Arthur Odell. He married Emily Barrett [dau of Joshua & Rachel] born c1832 and died Feb. 18, 1890 Putnam Valley, NY. Both are buried at Adams Corners Cemetery, Putnam Valley, NY. Born to them were: (5.1.2 .9.4.9.1) THEODORE WASHINGTON CHRISTIAN born February 21, 1849 Putnam Valley NY and died January 26, 1922 Wappingers Falls NY at the home of his son; buried New Hackensack Cemetery (Photo); married Martha Julia Travis [dau of Tompkins & Vashti (Adams)]. He was one of Dutchess County's most respected and beloved citizens. Born to them were:
