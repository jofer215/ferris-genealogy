GEORGE ALLEN HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.4.7.6.3 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.7.6.3) GEORGE ALLEN HOYT born August 16, 1811 Stamford and died there December 3, 1887; buried there Woodland Cemetery; 1887 merchant; married 1st August 16, 1838 Sarah Elizabeth Hawley [dau of Elisha & Electa (Smith) Hawley] born April 11, 1815 Stamford and died there March 14, 1853; buried Woodland Cemetery; married 2d November 27, 1855 in Stamford, as her 1st husband, Jeannie Penniman Smith [dau of Truman & Maria (Cook) Smith] born 1834 Litchfield CT and died August 17, 1927 Washington, Litchfield, CT; born to him and Sarah were:
