SUSAN FERRIS

ID: 3.6.1.2.2.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.1.2.2.4) SUSAN FERRIS (Susan Letitia Ferris) born May 3, 1799 (probably first born) and died October 2, 1866; married December 23, 1822 Seth Palmer Quintard (2.1.1.3.1.1.3); he is buried Woodland Cemetery, Stamford – don’t know about Susan; see him for offspring (3.6.1.2.2.5) MARY KIMBERLY FERRIS born 1809 and died December 9, 1836; unmarried
