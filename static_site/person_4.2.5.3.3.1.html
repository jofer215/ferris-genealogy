HENRY LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.2.5.3.3.1 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.3.3.1) HENRY LOCKWOOD born August 3, 1807 Greenwich and died November 21, 1884; a missionary on the Island of Java; married 1st February 17, 1836 on the Island of Java, Sarah Sophia Midhurst (c1807-c1839); married 2d August 11, 1840 in Geneva NY, Cathalina Lansing Dox (c1807-); and born to him and Cathalina were:
