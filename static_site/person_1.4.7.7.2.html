WILLIAM FERRIS SEAMAN, M.D.,

ID: 1.4.7.7.2 | Source: John
(1.4.7.7.2) WILLIAM FERRIS SEAMAN, M.D., born February 13, 1797 New York City and died January 12, 1827, the first white child vaccinated in New York; married October 30, 1822 at Westbury, Hempstead Township, Queens Co, L.I. NY, Elizabeth Hicks [dau of Isaac & Sarah (Doughty) Hicks] born June 10, 1805 Westbury, Queens, NY and died there January 2, 1865. After his death, she married William Townsend Cock. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • WILLIAM H. SEAMAN 1.4.7.7.2.1
  • SARAH H. SEAMAN 1.4.7.7.2.2
