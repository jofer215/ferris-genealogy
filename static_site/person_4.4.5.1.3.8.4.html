HOWARD LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.5.1.3.8.4 | Source: Mary
(4.4.5.1.3.8.4) HOWARD LOCKWOOD (#2494) born March 9, 1846 White Plains; was the publisher of the Paper Trade Journal; American Stationer; American Mail and Export Journal; The Wine and Fruit Reporter; The Miller’s Journal; and The Jeweler, Silversmith and Watchmaker, NYC, the Howard Lockwood & Co., Lockwood Press; married Carrie Baker [dau of John & Abigail (Colgate) Baker] born December 1, 1851 NY. No offspring.

No children recorded
