DORA M. FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.4.5.2.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.4.5.2.4) DORA M. FERRIS (Photo) born November 23, 1863 LaPorte Co IN and died 1947; buried Mt. Hope Cemetery, city unk; married 1st April 9, 1885 in LaPorte Co IN, Horace J. Robinson [son of Elisha J. & Priscilla (Doughty) Robinson] born January 29, 1862 Jennings Co IN – they divorced; married 2d May 30, 1906 in Fulton Co IN, Amos Fisher – divorced before 1920. In the 1930 census, Dora and her ex-husband Horace are living in their son Aubrey’s home in Miami Co IN. Born to Dora and Horace were:
