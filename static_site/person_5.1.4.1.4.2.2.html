CHARLES ALVAH LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.1.4.1.4.2.2 | Source: James
(5.1.4.1.4.2.2) CHARLES ALVAH LOCKWOOD born September 3, 1837 Stamford and died there February 28, 1900; buried there Long Ridge Cemetery; married Mary Elizabeth Potts born January 13, 1834 CT; and born to them were: (5.1.4.1.4.2.2.1) ANNA SAMUELS LOCKWOOD born June 23, 1860 Stamford CT and died there April 2, 1936; buried there Woodland Cemetery; married January 11, 1882 in Stamford, Charles F Waterbury (4.2.5.2.5.1.5); see him for offspring
