JOHN FERRIS

ID: 3.4.5.1.4.3 | Source: Joseph

Children (3)

  • JANE FERRIS died May 12, 1833 age 8m 5d 3.4.5.1.4.3.2
  • RANSOM FERRIS died May 26, 1842 age 5y 6m 3.4.5.1.4.3.3
