ROBERT AUGUSTUS FERRIS, JR.,

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.10.5.6.3.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.10.5.6.3.1) ROBERT AUGUSTUS FERRIS, JR., born September 13, 1942 Tulsa OK; a National Merit Scholar in high school; attended the U. of Chicago and graduated from Oklahoma State U. with a degree in civil engineering; Shell Oil Co 1966-69 in Birmingham AL; moved with Shell to Tampa and left Shell in 1971 to get into the construction business and stayed in Tampa until 1990 when he moved to Colorado; now a llama and horse breeder at his Tumble Creek Ranch, Buena Vista CO; married 1st Virginia Swanson at Stillwater OK - divorced February 1998; and 2d December 12, 1998 in CO, as her 2d marriage, Katie Bell. http://www.foxtrotter.com/TumbleCreek/index.htm Born to Rob and Virginia were:
