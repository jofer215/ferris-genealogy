JAMES HENRY PLAKA PIMLEY

ID: 3.10.1.2.1.10.2.4.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.1.10.2.4.1) JAMES HENRY PLAKA PIMLEY (1861-1925) married Lorena Eade. He lived in Park Rapids MN for several years prior to his death. Phoebe married 2d July 18, 1870 the Rev. Julius Buss [son of Jesse & March (Crittenden) Buss] born February 23, 1834 East Sussex, England and died August 31, 1922, Park Rapids, Hubbard County MN. Rev. Buss came to the U. S with his parents and some of his siblings in 1850. They lived in Guthrie County IA, 1873-1877, when they moved back to Stephenson County IL; in 1880 they lived in Ogle County IL. At the age of 60, Phoebe and Julius, who was a circuit preacher [participated in revivals in Iowa, Illinois, Wisconsin and Minnesota] but also listed as a farmer, moved to Osage, Hubbard County MN, in 1902.
