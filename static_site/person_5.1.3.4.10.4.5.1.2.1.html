KENNETH MURRAY PEARSON, JR,

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.5.1.2.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.5.1.2.1) KENNETH MURRAY PEARSON, JR, born February 21, 1931 Sterling, Whiteside, IL; married June 17, 1956 in Rock Falls, Marjorie Ann Cantlin born November 1, 1928 Sterling IL. “Marge” is a graduate of the MacMurray College in Jacksonville IL and taught the Sciences in High School until the children came along. Later she enjoyed a career in retailing. She has had MS for 20 years and requires special care. Born to them were:

Children (2)
