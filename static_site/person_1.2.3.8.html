MARY UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.8 | Source: John
(1.2.3.8) MARY UNDERHILL born January 2, 1720 Westchester NY (New York Colony); married 1st c1741 Obadiah Purdy [son of Joseph & Mary (Parke) Purdy] born 1712 White Plains NY; 2d Dr. Nicholas Bayley (c1715-1788) [son of Nathaniel & Leah (DeVeaux) Bayley]. According to one source Obadiah married first Phebe Underhill and 4 kids, but the dates don’t match up. Born to Mary and Obadiah were:

Children (8)

  • OBEDIAH PURDY 1.2.3.8.1
  • JOSEPH PURDY 1.2.3.8.2
  • AMELIA PURDY 1.2.3.8.3
  • ELIZABETH BAYLEY 1.2.3.8.4
  • NATHANIEL BAYLEY 1.2.3.8.5
  • JOHN BAYLEY 1.2.3.8.6
  • FREDERICK BAYLEY 1.2.3.8.7
  • OLIVER BAYLEY 1.2.3.8.8
