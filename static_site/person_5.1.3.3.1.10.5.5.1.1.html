LOUIS GEORGE MARTIN, M.D.,

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.10.5.5.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.10.5.5.1.1) LOUIS GEORGE MARTIN, M.D., born September 28, 1922 Toledo OH and died there 1999; married c1951 in Toledo, Virginia Withrow. Louis graduated from Case Western Reserve Medical School; served as a Lieutenant in the US Army 101st Airborn Division as a front line doctor (in front of the MASH units) in Korea, receiving multiple Purple Hearts and other distinguished service medals. He was a physician and board member of the Toledo Hospital. Born to them were:
