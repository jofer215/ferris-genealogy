WILLIAM CULLEN HOYT

ID: 4.6.5.5.1.3.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.5.1.3.1) WILLIAM CULLEN HOYT (#5849) born September 3, 1815 Tribes Hill, Montgomery, NY. William moved with his father to Auora, Erie, NY in 1817 and was educated at the common schools and the academy at Middlebury, Wyoming, NY; studied law in Buffalo and was admitted to the bar of the Supreme Court in New York City, 1838; practice his profession for a short time in Buffalo; removed to Oakland County MI, 1842, where he practice law and was at one time one of the judges of the county court. He removed to Detroit in 1852 and was living there in 1871. William was the commissioner of the Hoyt Family Union for Michigan. The above quotes about his father are his. He married, September 4, 1839, Harriet M. Green and born to them was: (4.6.5.5.1.3.1.1) JOSEPHINE LOUISA HOYT born November 16, 1840 and died December 17, 1840

No children recorded
