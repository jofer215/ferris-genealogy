SUSANNAH MILLS

ID: 3.11.4.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.4.3) SUSANNAH MILLS born c1726 Greenwich and died there July 27, 1815; married 1st c1751 (1749) at Greenwich, Jonathan Knapp [son of Jonathan & Mary (Husted) Knapp] born c1727 Greenwich and died there June 8, 1766; married second, as his third wife, Benjamin Close (1712-1782) who was a Loyalist sent within British lines in 1778 and died there on Long Island. Born to Susanah and Jonathan were:

Children (6)

  • PETER KNAPP 3.11.4.3.2
  • SILAS KNAPP 3.11.4.3.3
  • RACHEL KNAPP 3.11.4.3.4
  • HANNAH KNAPP 3.11.4.3.5
  • ABIGAIL KNAPP 3.11.4.3.7
