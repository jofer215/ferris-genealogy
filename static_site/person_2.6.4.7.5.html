EZRA KNAPP

ID: 2.6.4.7.5 | Source: Peter
(2.6.4.7.5) EZRA KNAPP born August 4, 1781 Stamford CT and died there April 8, 1848 Brooklyn NY; buried Greenwich Cemetery, Brooklyn, Queens County NY. He resided at Stamford and Brooklyn; belonged to the 1st Congregational Church, Stamford. He married September 8, 1805 at Stamford, Polly Esther Hoyt (2.6.2.1.5.3) and born to them were:
