HORACE SCOFIELD

ID: 2.5.3.6.4.1.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.6.4.1.1) HORACE SCOFIELD born November 29, 1804 Stamford CT and died after 1850; a schoolteacher; married 1st November 22, 1827 in Stamford, Martha Smith [dau of Eber & Betsey (June) Smith] born September 2, 1802 Stamford and there June 17, 1846; buried Roxbury Cemetery; married 2d 1847 in Stamford Emma Purdy born 1812 NJ. Born to him and Martha were:

Children (5)

  • BETSEY ANN SCOFIELD 2.5.3.6.4.1.1.1
  • ADELINE S. SCOFIELD 2.5.3.6.4.1.1.2
  • MARTHA EMILY SCOFIELD 2.5.3.6.4.1.1.3
  • HENRY M. SCOFIELD 2.5.3.6.4.1.1.4
  • EMMA PURDY SCOFIELD 2.5.3.6.4.1.1.5
