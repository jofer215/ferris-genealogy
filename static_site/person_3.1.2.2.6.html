LUCY FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.2.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.6) LUCY FERRIS born December 26, 1760 - not listed by Chaplain Ferris, but listed by Schofield and Mead; married c1780/81 near Hillsdale NY, Christian Loop [son of Captain Johann Peter & Cousya (Springer) Loop, Sr.] born April 16, 1759 Hillsdale, Columbia, NY and died 1799 Chemung NY; served in the Revolution; Christian and his brother, Peter, Jr., moved to Elmira then called Newtown; the 1860 Gazetteer of New York State says that he and Colonel Hendy made the first settlement there in 1788; the Hillsdale Dutch Reformed Church, Hillsdale has records showing Christian Loop and 'Luica Farres'; and born to them were 2 daughters and 7 sons, one of which was:
