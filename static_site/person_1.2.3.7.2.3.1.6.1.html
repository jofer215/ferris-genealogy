ROSELLA AMANDA FROST

ID: 1.2.3.7.2.3.1.6.1 | Source: John
(1.2.3.7.2.3.1.6.1) ROSELLA AMANDA FROST born June 14, 1862 Bentonville IN and died 1948 Lebanon OH; buried Washington PA. She married October 17, 1888 in Harrisburg IN, Ellsworth Alvin Cole born August 5, 1861 Bentonville IN and died April 11, 1945; buried with Rosella. Ellsworth was a minister in Christian Church; held pastoratres in Richmond VA, Angola, Tipton, Martinsville IN, and Pittsburgh PA. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • PAUL FROST COLE 1.2.3.7.2.3.1.6.1.2
  • VIRGINIA GARRISON COLE 1.2.3.7.2.3.1.6.1.3
  • ESTHER FRANCES COLE 1.2.3.7.2.3.1.6.1.4
