ALBERT GEORGE COLE

ID: 1.2.3.7.2.6.3.3.4 | Source: John
(1.2.3.7.2.6.3.3.4) ALBERT GEORGE COLE born March 28, 1857 Beaver Dams NY; married September 25, 1901 at Zanesfield OH, Leota Peters born there July 9, 1881 [dau of George and Elizabeth (Shoots) Peters]; Albert was a railroad conductor and they resided in Springfield OH. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • LUCILLE RUTH COLE 1.2.3.7.2.6.3.3.4.2
