ANGELINE PIPHER

ID: 3.6.3.2.2.2.2.1.1.8 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.2.2.2.1.1.8) ANGELINE PIPHER born June 13, 1888 probably in Warren Co NJ and died May 7, 1965; married 1st George E. Conklin who died in 1917 NJ; married 2d June 4, 1919 probably in Warren Co NJ, as his 2d wife, Charles Thomas Lee [son of Charles & Jessie] probably born in England.
