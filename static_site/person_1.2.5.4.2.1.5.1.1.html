CORA MARSLAND

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.5.1.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.5.1.1) CORA MARSLAND (Photo) born December 2, 1858 and died October 4, 1944; buried Dale Cemetery, Ossining NY; unmarried; educated at New York State Normal College (1882); special student – oratory and literature Wellesley College (1884); Emerson College of Oratory (1888); Master of Oratory (1889). She lived in New Castle Township NY; Emporia KS; Orangeburg NY (1919); Ossining NY (1925/1938). She was Head, Department of Oratory, Iowa State Agricultural College, Ames IA (1889 appointed librarian and professor of elocution – resigned August 1890); Head Departments of Oratory and English Literature, Olivet College MI; 3 years Professor of English Literature, Dean of Women, Head – Department of Oratory, Kansas State Normal School, Emporia KS (189-94; 1898-1911). She was author of Interpretive Reading (1902) and The Angel of the Gila (1911) (1.2.5.4.2.1.6) SUSAN CHADEAYNE born June 19, 1803 and died December 20, 1869 Ossining NY; buried there Dale Cemetery; married William Ricketts Van Cortland [son of Philip & Mary (Banker) Van Cortland] (1801-); he was a farmer residing in Yorktown NY 1850.
