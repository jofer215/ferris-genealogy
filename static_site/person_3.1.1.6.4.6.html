JOHN FERRIS

ID: 3.1.1.6.4.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.4.6) JOHN FERRIS (JR?) born June 21, 1815 Columbia and died 1888 near Cincinnati; inherited 24 acres of his father’s land 1840, which he sold 1849 for $5,500. The original home of John, Jr., was added onto and became known as ‘The Hermitage’, one of Hyde Park’s two great estates. In 1966, the Hermitage was sold to become the site of the Regency high-rise apartments. He married 1st May 6, 1838 Harriet S. Forman born December 31, 1816 and died September 5, 1849; and 2d Mary Minshall

No children recorded
