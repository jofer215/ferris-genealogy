ELOISE EVELYN SANDA

ID: 1.2.6.2.1.2.2.4.6.1.1 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.1.2.2.4.6.1.1) ELOISE EVELYN SANDA married Robert Roy Kincaid [son of Frank & Anna (Graap) Kincaid] born October 26, 1908 Sweet Briar, Morton, ND and died November 4, 1992 Anchorage AK; cremated and ashes spread in Seldovia AK; he attended Teachers College ND and spent many years as a refrigeration engineer; and born to them were:
