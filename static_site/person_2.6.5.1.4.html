BETSY KNAPP

ID: 2.6.5.1.4 | Source: Peter
(2.6.5.1.4) BETSY KNAPP (II) born May 5, 1766 Stamford and died there November 27, 1811; married 1st c1786 in Stamford, John Bedient; and 2d, April 4, 1793 at Stamford Thomas Lounsberry (Lounsbury) born c1769 Stamford and died there March 11, 1812; both Betsey and John buried West Stamford Cemetery

No children recorded
