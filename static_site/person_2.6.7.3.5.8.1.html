ALVIRA BEATTIE KNAPP

ID: 2.6.7.3.5.8.1 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.3.5.8.1) ALVIRA BEATTIE KNAPP born June 8, 1846 Erie Co NY and died July 17, 1891 Mullen, Hooker, NE; buried Bullen; married December 21, 1864 in Tama Co IA Charles Wesley Smith [son of Charles Gaylord & Harriet (Bennett) Smith] born October 12, 1844 Centerville, Delaware, OH and died April 20, 1923 Stratton, Kit Carson, CO; buried Forest Lawn, Omaha NE. Born to them were:

Children (5)

  • CLARENCE KNAPP SMITH 2.6.7.3.5.8.1.1
  • BENNETT WILLIAM SMITH 2.6.7.3.5.8.1.2
  • LEE WESLEY SMITH 2.6.7.3.5.8.1.3
  • ELIAS ROWLAND SMITH 2.6.7.3.5.8.1.4
  • HARRIET ALVIRA SMITH 2.6.7.3.5.8.1.5
