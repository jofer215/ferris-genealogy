GEORGE A. FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.6.3.8 | Source: James
(5.1.3.6.3.8) GEORGE A. FERRIS born March 30, 1824 and died February 20, 1907; married Angeline Hutchings (5.1.3.6.4) SAMUEL FERRIS born January 25/26, 1787 Greenwich CT and died there June 1, 1842; will dated May 14, 1842, probated June 13, 1842 mentioned his wife Esther (Esther Ferris) and children, Caroline, Susan, Henrietta, Ann, Sarah, Mary, Hannah, Stephen R, Nathaniel H, and Samuel H; Esther appointed guardian of Nathaniel H, Samuel H, Mary, and Hannah H; Captain in the War of 1812; married January 7, 1811 Esther FERRIS (5.1.3.8.5); Samuel died in the prime of life leaving his widow with 10 kids to raise and a 300-acre farm (in 1967 the Innis Arden Golf Club) to tend; Esther Ferris was “Aunt Hetty” to most of the villagers who said, “Her teeth were always comfortable, she wore them in her pocket.”; and born to them were:

No children recorded
