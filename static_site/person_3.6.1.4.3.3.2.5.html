WATSON LEWIS SAVAGE

ID: 3.6.1.4.3.3.2.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.1.4.3.3.2.5) WATSON LEWIS SAVAGE Jr (1898-1939) – Rye Neck High School, Mamaronick 1916; Cornell 1920; after leaving Cornell he was with the New York branch of the Oakland Motor Car Co; then was salesman with Ward Baking Co, Bronx; secretary of Dr. Savage Physical Development Institute; physical director and secretary of Dr. Savage Exercise Institute; and secretary of The Savage School for Physical Education, 454 West 155th Street, NYC. During the War he served in the Navy; was commissioned Ensign in the USN Reserve Force; and was in the Naval Communications Service in Washington, D.C.; married Juliet Douglas Clark

No children recorded
