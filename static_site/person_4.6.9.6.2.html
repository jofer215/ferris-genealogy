SAMUEL LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.9.6.2 | Source: Mary
(4.6.9.6.2) SAMUEL LOCKWOOD (#718) died young. (4.6.9.6.3) NATHAN LOCKWOOD (#719) born c1795 Pound Ridge NY and was residing at Irvington NY 1881; and died October 7, 1884 age 89y 9m; married at Bedford NY Mary Miller [dau of Hachaliah & Margaret (Runshaw) Miller] died February 26, 1876 age 79y 4m; both buried Quaker Cemetery, Greenville NY; and born to them were [4 were born at Pound Ridge; 2 were born at Hastings; and 2 were born at Dobb’s Ferry – don’t know which]:

No children recorded
