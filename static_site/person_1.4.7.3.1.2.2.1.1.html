JOHN PELL ARCHER SHEE

ID: 1.4.7.3.1.2.2.1.1 | Source: John
(1.4.7.3.1.2.2.1.1) JOHN PELL ARCHER SHEE (Jack) born November 3, 1906 and died 1980; Colonel John Pell Archer-Shee, MC, DL (1971), CC (1961) Hants, joined 10th Royal Hussars 1926, served in WWII (despatches) in France, North Africa and Burma, commanded 10th Royal Hussars and 149th RAC (Koyli), Military Advisor to British High Command in Pakistan 1953-56, retired 1957; educated at Eton and RMC Sandhurst; married 1st Nancy Edith Magor; 2d 1940 Mary Henrietta Stapleton Bretherton and they had a child; 3d 1953 Diana Nancy Boyd-Rochfort

No children recorded
