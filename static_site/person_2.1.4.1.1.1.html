FREDERICK C. FERRIS

ID: 2.1.4.1.1.1 | Source: Peter
(2.1.4.1.1.1) FREDERICK C. FERRIS born August 5, 1784 Providence NY and died November 7, 1856 Royalton, Niagara or Orleans Co NY; married October 1, 1809 Hannah Haviland born August 5, 1788. They apparently moved to Hillsdale MI and then returned to New York. Born to them were:

Children (6)
