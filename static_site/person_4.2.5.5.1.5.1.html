DANIEL E SWARTOUT

ID: 4.2.5.5.1.5.1 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.5.1.5.1) DANIEL E SWARTOUT born 1836/37 Woodstock, Lenawee, MI; 1870 resided Lenawee Co MI – carpenter and joiner; 1880 resided Mission Creek, Pawnee, NE – merchant; 1900 resided Marshall Co KS – hardware clerk; married September 4, 1867 in Columbia Township, Jackson Co MI, Mary Mulnix born August 1837 NY; 1910 she resided in El Paso Co TX and 1920 Ysleta, El Paso, TX; and born to them were:
