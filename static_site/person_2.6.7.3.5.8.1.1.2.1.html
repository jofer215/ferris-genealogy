NELLIE GERTRUDE BLISS

ID: 2.6.7.3.5.8.1.1.2.1 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.3.5.8.1.1.2.1) NELLIE GERTRUDE BLISS born July 19, 1914 Grand Junction, Mesa, CO and died September 28, 2000 Beckley WV. She married May 3, 1952 in Grand Junction CO Dewitt Clinton Barton (Bart) [son of Lafayette & Mary Ann (Parisho) Barton] born December 24, 1901 Pawhuska, Osage, OK and died May 2, 1980 Boulder CO; buried Mountain View Memorial Park Cemetery, Boulder. This must have been Dewitt’s 2d marriage as he also married Beulah Charity Mayfield and they had 3 kids.

No children recorded
