PHINEAS SCOFIELD

ID: 4.6.3.4.3 | Source: Mary
(4.6.3.4.3) PHINEAS SCOFIELD born 1772 Stamford and died there March 10, 1820; married 1st January 12, 1797 in Stamford, Mercy Finch [dau of Nathaniel & Mehitable (Hoyt) Finch] born 1773 Stamford and died there February 24, 1809; married 2d after 1809 Mercy Seely [dau of Abijah & Lydia (Hoyt) Seely] born September 7, 1774 Stamford and died there December 8, 1832; all buried there Scofieldtown-Lockwood #4 Cemetery; marriage sequence has been reversed; and born to him and Finch were: (4.6.3.4.3.1) HIRAM SCOFIELD born October 3, 1797 Stamford; married December 19, 1820 in Stamford, Sally Lockwood; and born to them was:
