DAVID HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.4.5.8 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.5.8) DAVID HOYT (#3536) born December 13, 1784 Stamford and died there July 5/6, 1838; buried North Street Cemetery; married February 2, 1812 in Stamford, Abigail Knapp (2.6.4.7.7). David was a farmer and merchant and lived in Stamford. Born to them were:

Children (6)

  • GEORGE CLINTON HOYT 2.5.2.4.5.8.1
  • MARY HOYT 2.5.2.4.5.8.2
  • WILLIAM WALLACE HOYT 2.5.2.4.5.8.3
  • MARY ELMORE HOYT 2.5.2.4.5.8.4
  • HARRIET ATWOOD HOYT 2.5.2.4.5.8.5
  • HENRY MARTYN HOYT 2.5.2.4.5.8.6
