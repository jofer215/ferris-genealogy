HARRIET KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.3.7.10 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.7.10) HARRIET KNAPP (twin) born January 23, 1807 New Windsor NY and died January 11, 1885 Coldenham NY; married October 24, 1827 probably at New Windsor, James Knapp [cousin of some degree] born July 10/11, 1802 and died June 19, 1875. He was a farmer and they followed the Presbyterian faith. They resided 2 miles from Montgomery NY. Born to them were:

Children (8)
