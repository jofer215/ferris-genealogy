ALONZO GODFREY

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2) ALONZO GODFREY born November 29, 1811 near Bloomingburg, Sullivan, NY and died September 30, 1855 Jackson Co MI; buried there Campbell Cemetery, a few miles north of Parma; migrated to MI c1844 where he taught school in Campbell and Quakertown; married 1st March 8, 1838 in Stafford, Genesee, NY Harriet Newell Waterman [dau of James & Polly (Payson) Waterman] born April 23, 1816 Windsor, Birkshire, MA and died July 3, 1842 Stafford NY; married 2d 1843 in Stafford, Betsey Douglas [dau of John & Achsah]; married 3d 1852 in Jackson Co MI, Susan Joslin; and born to him and Harriet were:

Children (3)

  • GEORGE H GODFREY 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.1
  • HARRIET NEWELL GODFREY 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.2
  • JAMES DOUGLAS GODFREY 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.3
