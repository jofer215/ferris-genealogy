JOSEPH JAMES MAYER

ID: 1.1.4.4.4.4.11.4.1 | Source: John
(1.1.4.4.4.4.11.4.1) JOSEPH JAMES MAYER born May 6, 1921; Retired US Navy, having served in the US Submarine Service during WWII; married 1st ,1943 Rose Alice Denis and 2d Joyce Dorwity. Born to Joseph were possibly three children, names unknown.

No children recorded
