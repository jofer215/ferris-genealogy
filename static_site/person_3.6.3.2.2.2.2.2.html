ANDREW R. HILL

ID: 3.6.3.2.2.2.2.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.2.2.2.2) ANDREW R. HILL born September 24, 1839 (?) Warren Co NJ and died March 28, 1924 at 84y; interred Cedar Ridge Cemetery, Blairstown, Warren, NJ; married 1st June 23, 1864 Catherine Chrissman born June 26, 1844 and died April 26, 1900; buried with Andrew; married 2d as her 2d husband, Flora Belle Hayward born c1856 Sherburne VT
