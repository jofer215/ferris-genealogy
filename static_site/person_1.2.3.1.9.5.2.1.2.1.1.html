BERTHA MAY CHRISTIAN

ID: 1.2.3.1.9.5.2.1.2.1.1 | Source: John
(1.2.3.1.9.5.2.1.2.1.1) BERTHA MAY CHRISTIAN born June 10, 1898 Mahopac, Putnam, NY and died September 7, 1988 Keene NH; married June 27, 1917 in Jewett City CT, Clyde Wallace Kenyon born October 4, 1886 Voluntown CT and died August 31, 1971 Keene NH; both buried South Village Cemetery, Westmoreland NH; and they had 6 kids, including the following: (1.2.3.1.9.5.2.1.2.1.1.1) LLOYD ELLERY KENYON born February 14, 1918 Canterbury, Windham, CT and died 1996 Keene NH; railroad conductor; married December 9, 1939 in Brattleboro VT, Edna Anna Tucker born July 20, 1917 Brattleboro and died July 23, 1989 Keene NH; both buried South Village Cemetery, Westmoreland NH; offspring
