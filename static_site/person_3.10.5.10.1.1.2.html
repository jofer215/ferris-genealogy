ANN ELIZA PECK

ID: 3.10.5.10.1.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.5.10.1.1.2) ANN ELIZA PECK born June 9, 1811 Flushing NY and was living there 1860; apparently died pre-1868; married November 10, 1831 in Flushing, William Hallett Fairweather [son of Samuel & Lydia (Hallett) Fairweather] born c1808 Sussex, Vale, N. B., Canada and died 1883 Flushing NY; he was a dry goods merchant, Fairweather & Peck; after Ann’s death, William married in 1868, Catherine Lawrence Luyster. Born to Ann and William was:

Children (1)
