SARAH A UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.3.5.1.3.1 | Source: John
(1.2.3.3.5.1.3.1) SARAH A UNDERHILL born August 1836 Ontario and died December 20, 1912 MI; buried Lake View Cemetery, Antrim Co MI; married 1st Theodore Newell Chapin born c1829 and died June 1/8, 1863 Nashville TN; buried there National Cemetery; he was mustered into the US Army September 4, 1862 from Grattan, Kent, MI and died as a Private (website has his name spelled Chapiin – apparent error); married 2d March 14, 1869 in Lowell MI, Charles L Cleveland born December 1839/42 Denmark NY and died November 29, 1929 Bellaire MI; buried Lake View Cemetery; and born to her and Theodore were:

Children (2)

  • FRANK F CHAPIN 1.2.3.3.5.1.3.1.1
  • LLEWELLYN FRANK CLEVELAND 1.2.3.3.5.1.3.1.3
