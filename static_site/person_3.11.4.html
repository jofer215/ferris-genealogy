LYDIA REYNOLDS

ID: 3.11.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.4) LYDIA REYNOLDS born (c1698) 1700 Greenwich CT and died there age 33y; married c1720 in Greenwich, Samuel Mills IV [son of Samuel III & Sarah (Denton) Mills] and born to them were:

Children (6)

  • SARAH MILLS 3.11.4.1
  • SAMUEL MILLS V 3.11.4.2
  • SUSANNAH MILLS 3.11.4.3
  • DENTON MILLS 3.11.4.4
  • LYDIA MILLS died before 1786; married December 20, 1750 at Norwalk, Ebenezer Fitch 3.11.4.5
  • CLEMENTS 3.11.4.6
