ANDREW VANTUYL KIP

ID: 1.2.3.4.2.4.4.3 | Source: John
(1.2.3.4.2.4.4.3) ANDREW VANTUYL KIP born March 3, 1846 Sullivan NY and died c1890 Brooklyn; Civil War Vet enlisted February 2, 1865 and mustered out August 30, 1865; served with Company A, 47th New York Infantry Regiment, Private, wounded; regimental history on ‘Net; married 1867 Theresa Van Schaick [dau of Jacob Van & Mary Jane (Berry) Schaick] born 1850 Fulton NY and died October 25, 1916; buried Cypress Hills Cemetery, Brooklyn; and born to them were:

Children (1)
