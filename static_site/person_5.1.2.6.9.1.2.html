JOHN SOLOMON

ID: 5.1.2.6.9.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.2.6.9.1.2) JOHN SOLOMON (SYLVANUS) FERRIS born July 26, 1846 and died April 1886 Syracuse NY; married c1866 Dora Brown (1845-1907) - Dora was the first woman to own and run a construction company in Syracuse after John died [John and Dora are both buried in the New Cemetery, Bridgeport, Onondaga, NY; located nearly one-half mile from the village on the BridgeportCollamer Road. According to the tombstone, John born July 9, 1847 and died April 28, 1886; Dora born September 26, 1845 and died May 19, 1907]; and born to them were:
