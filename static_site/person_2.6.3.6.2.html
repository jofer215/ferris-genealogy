NATHANIEL HUBBARD

ID: 2.6.3.6.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.3.6.2) NATHANIEL HUBBARD born April 29, 1745 and died March 25, 1818 Burton Parish, New Brunswick; United Empire Loyalist went to St. John, New Brunswick 1783 and settled in Burton Parish, where he was a magistrate; his Connecticut estate was confiscated by the Patriots in 1779

No children recorded
