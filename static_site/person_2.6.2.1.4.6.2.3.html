CHARLES W. KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.1.4.6.2.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.1.4.6.2.3) CHARLES W. KNAPP born 1857 Westerlo NY and died January 8, 1926 Little Falls, Herkimer, NY; married 1877 in Cazenovia NY, Amelia Schmeltz who died January 23, 1927 Little Falls NY.

No children recorded
