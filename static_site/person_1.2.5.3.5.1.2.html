ANNA VAN EPPS

ID: 1.2.5.3.5.1.2 | Source: John
(1.2.5.3.5.1.2) ANNA VAN EPPS (Anna Maria Van Eps) born May 14, 1831; married 1st October 4, 1849 in Utica, Oneida, NY Gustavus Adolphus Kellogg [son of Spencer & Margaret Stuart (Palmer) Kellogg] born April 1, 1824 Plainfield Township, Otsego Co NY and died 1850; 2d Charles Cushman Kellogg (brothers) born September 2, 1828 Plainfield Township; educated at Hamilton College; became a partner with his brother, Palmer V., in the mercantile business; subsequently engaged in the lumber business, founding Charles C Kellogg & Sons Company; prominent businessman in Utica NY; and born to her and Gustavus was:
