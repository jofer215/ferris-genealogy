WILLIAM BAILEY WEEKS

ID: 1.2.2.2.2.2.2.3.2.1 | Source: John
(1.2.2.2.2.2.2.3.2.1) WILLIAM BAILEY WEEKS (1924-) (1.2.2.2.2.2.2.3.3) MARION LOUISE WEEKS born April 22, 1901 Brooklyn NY and died March 8, 1984, Delray Beach FL; married, first, June 6, 1922 in New Rochelle NY, Donald Randolph Waugh [son of Archie Elwin & Martha Adeline (Sides) Waugh] born July 18, 1900, Philadelphia PA and died March 13, 1974, Waterbury CT. Donald was a political editor, business executive; attended MIT and Columbia. They divorced in 1935. Marion married, second, Harold Beers Naramore {her brother-in-law} and third, George Rand.
