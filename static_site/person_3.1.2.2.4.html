JESSE FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.2.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.4) JESSE FERRIS born (1753?) Greenwich CT and died c1795 {found in the 1790 census in Ballston, Saratoga, NY but gone in 1800; listed with wife and 4 males under 16 years}; married in Dutchess Co NY Elizabeth Slate/Sleight. [Tradition has it that Elizabeth married Leonard Tuthill after Jesse’s death and apparently one of their sons was baptized as children of Leonard Tuthill.][After the death of Leonard Tuthill, the widow moved with her sons to Chenango County where they lived near Benjamin and Timothy Ferris]; and born to them were:

Children (6)

  • STEPHEN FERRIS 3.1.2.2.4.2
  • JOHN FERRIS 3.1.2.2.4.3
