NEWMAN BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.6.3 | Source: James
(5.2.2.6.3) NEWMAN BUXTON born October 24, 1773 Stamford CT and died August 20, 1831 Johnstown, Fulton, NY; buried Pine Grove Cemetery, Tribes Hill, Montgomery County NY; a farmer. Newman and his brother James left CT early in their lives for the Mohawk Valley in NY where they married and raised their families.
