WILLIAM WEED

ID: 4.6.5.9.5 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.9.5) WILLIAM WEED (Deacon) born January 14, 1774 Pound Ridge NY (Stamford CT) and died May 16, 1861 Springfield Township, Galia Co OH; married 1st March 1, 1797 at Scipio, Cayuga Co NY, Olive Branch [dau of Asa & Elizabeth (Tracey) Branch] born January 1, 1772 Preston CT and died April 30, 1827 Gallipolis OH; married 2d May 12, 1835 Margaret Taylor (c1780-); William served in the War of 1812 in the Quartermaster Department; a farmer; and born to him and Olive were:

Children (4)

  • GILBERT WEED 4.6.5.9.5.1
  • OLIVE WEED 4.6.5.9.5.3
  • WILLIAM BRANCH WEED 4.6.5.9.5.4
  • TRACY HOYT WEED 4.6.5.9.5.5
