JEREMIAH PECK

ID: 3.10.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.2) JEREMIAH PECK (GJ59-56) born December 29, 1690 Greenwich and died there 1765; married 1725 in Greenwich, Mary Johnson [dau of Samuel and M. (Sage) Johnson]. He was a farmer in Greenwich and died there, survived by his wife. Born to them were:
