SARAH BATES

ID: 2.3.3.2 | Source: Peter
(2.3.3.2) SARAH BATES born July 5, 1747 Stamford and died November 5, 1794 Noroton CT; buried Noroton River Cemetery, Darien; married May 20, 1770 in Stamford, Jacob Howe, Jr., [son of David & Rebecca (Whiting) Howe] born October 16, 1746 Stamford and died March 20, 1804 Noroton; buried How Cemetery, Darien; after Sarah’s death, Jacob married Elizabeth Slauson; and born to Sarah and Jacob were:

Children (10)
