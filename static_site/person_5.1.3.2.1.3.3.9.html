ELLEN MARIA FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.2.1.3.3.9 | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.1.3.3.9) ELLEN MARIA FERRIS born January 2, 1855 and died August 11, 1879 [Chaplain Ferris lists Ellen as a possibility] Volume IV of the Municipalities of Essex County NJ, (1924) says that Edwin is the son of Samuel & Isabella (Whiting) Ferris (5.1.3.2.1.3) - he was listed as a brother to Samuel in earlier versions of this “twig” and not a son. Looking at the dates, it appears that Edwin was a son of Samuel and not his brother.

No children recorded
