ALSON MOLONSO NEWMAN

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.12.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.12.2) ALSON MOLONSO NEWMAN born 1837 Jefferson Co NY and died March 9, 1903 Soldier’s Home, Minneapolis MN; Civil War Vet; enlisted June 1861 at Chicago into Company I, 7th Missouri Infantry; numerous campaigns – see ‘Net; discharged at Petersburg VA June 23, 1865; married August 2, 1866 Jefferson Co NY, Mary Holdenhouse [dau of Henry & Mary (Smith) Holdenhouse] born 1845 Theresa, Jefferson, NY – divorced December 9, 1876 Geneva, Kane, IL; married April 17, 1877 in Aurora IL, Emma Brown (1851-1925); and born to him and Mary were:

Children (4)

  • CORA HELENA NEWMAN 3.11.1.1.3.12.2.1
  • MARY NEWMAN 3.11.1.1.3.12.2.2
  • CHARLES NEWMAN 3.11.1.1.3.12.2.3
  • NINA NEWMAN 3.11.1.1.3.12.2.4
