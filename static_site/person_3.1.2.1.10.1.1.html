JOSHUA LORENZO FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.1.10.1.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.10.1.1) JOSHUA LORENZO FERRIS born March 7, 1815 Landoff, Grafton, NH and died September 11, 1870 Goshen, Utah, UT; buried Payson City Cemetery, Payson, Utah, UT (Photos); married 1st Elizabeth Odell and tradition has it she died in her first child birth; married 2d in Porter Co IN Elizabeth Marry Nelson who died 10 days after child birth; married 3d at Keokuk IA June 26, 1849 Elizabeth Gardner born 1825 Ohio and died “on the plains” or shortly after they arrived in the Salt Lake Valley 1852; married 4th in May or June 1854 Emma Beckstead; married 5th, May 3, 1864 at Antelope Island, Davis, UT, as her third husband, Dorothy Sarah Jennison – Lichfield – Baptist Hatfield born January 4, 1827 Nottingham, England and died August 27, 1903 Goshen UT.
