MONTGOMERY CLARK

ID: 3.10.1.2.6.8.5.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.6.8.5.1) MONTGOMERY CLARK born December 15, 1859 Saratoga NY and died May 27, 1915 Schenectady NY; married c1880 in Saratoga, Margaret E Brooks [dau of Israel & Harriet (Evans) Brooks] born February 9, 1841 Saratoga and died there February 11, 1910; both buried Clifton Park Baptist Church Cemetery, Clifton Park NY – she was a school teacher 1860
