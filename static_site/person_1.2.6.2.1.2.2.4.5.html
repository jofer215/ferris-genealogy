MATILDA JANE UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.6.2.1.2.2.4.5 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.1.2.2.4.5) MATILDA JANE UNDERHILL (Tillie) born January 28, 1879 Holdredge, Franklin, NE and died November 2, 1939 Salmon ID; married December 25, 1898 near Holdredge NE William James Conklin (Chunky) [brother of Franklin above] born March 3, 1862 near Iron Hills, Jackson, IA; both buried Salmon Cemetery, Salmon ID; Chunky and Tillie farmed near Stockville until 1907 when they moved to a farm near Scenic SD; after 5y there, they moved to Garrison ND 1912; however, they returned to Scenic 1915; 1923 they left there for Salt Creek WY; they then moved to Yakima WA, Hardin MT and then to Salmon ID 1935 where is where they spent their remaining years; and born to them were:

Children (6)

  • SANFORD CONKLIN 1.2.6.2.1.2.2.4.5.1
  • LOYAL CONKLIN 1.2.6.2.1.2.2.4.5.2
  • GLADYS CONKLIN 1.2.6.2.1.2.2.4.5.3
  • ADRIAN CONKLIN 1.2.6.2.1.2.2.4.5.5
  • EDORTHY CONKLIN 1.2.6.2.1.2.2.4.5.6
  • LESLIE CONKLIN 1.2.6.2.1.2.2.4.5.7
