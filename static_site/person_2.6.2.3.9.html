WALTER KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.3.9 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.9) WALTER KNAPP born May 16, 1776 Bethel, Sullivan, NY, although another source says Stamford, and died July 4, 1844 Black Lake, Sullivan, NY; a sawyer - saw mill owner; married probably in Cornwall NY, Sarah Chapman who died July 5, 1857; both buried Glen Cove Cemetery, Sullivan Co, NY.
