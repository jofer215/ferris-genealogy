MELANCTON B. FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.3.7.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.3.7.2) MELANCTON B. FERRIS born July 23, 1795 Panton, Addison, VT and died there July 9, 1871; buried Kent Cemetery, Panton VT; a cooper; married 1st Ann Elizabeth Shepard (Betsey) [dau of William and Margaret (Judd) Shepard] born June 6, 1800 and died February 25, 1843; and 2d Ruth Emma (Emma Ruth) Place [dau of John and Elsie Place] born March 1803/7 Westport NY and died September 6, 1871; and born to them were:
