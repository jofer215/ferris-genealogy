EBENEZER HAIT, JR.,

ID: 2.5.3.6 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.6) EBENEZER HAIT, JR., (#2767) (Hoyt) born c1712-20 (February 16, 1718/19 Stamford) and died after 1783 probably at Stamford where he lived; multiple land owner; married 1 st January 24, 1738/39/40 in Stamford, Hannah Clason [dau of Johnathan & Elizabeth (James) Clawson (Clason)] born May 28, 1722 and died November 21, 1746 Stamford; married 2d November 1, 1749 in Stamford, Mary Green.
