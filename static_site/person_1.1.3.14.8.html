ANGELINE ANN WETMORE

ID: 1.1.3.14.8 | Source: John
(1.1.3.14.8) ANGELINE ANN WETMORE born 1830 NY and died November 17, 1898 Hamilton, Ont; married February 2, 1864 in Grimsby, Ont, James DeWitt Bates [son of James & Susannah (Smith) Bates] born 1824 Glanford Township, Wentworth Co, Ont and died April 13, 1894 Hamilton, Ont; both buried North Glanford Cemetery; and born to them were: (1.1.3.14.8.1) SUSAN E BATES (1862-)

Children (4)

  • JAMES HENRY BATES 1.1.3.14.8.2
  • JOSEPH WETMORE BATES 1.1.3.14.8.3
  • SARAH VICTORIA BATES 1.1.3.14.8.4
  • CORA MARIETTE BATES 1.1.3.14.8.5
