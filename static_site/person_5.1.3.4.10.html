JOHN FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10) JOHN FERRIS born March 6, 1784 Greenwich CT and died January 11, 1871 Metamora IN; first came to Ohio from Connecticut May 1813. In his party were Frederick Ferris and family; Captain Robert Barnard and family; David Hobby and family and two other families. Each family, with a wagon and two horses, crossed overland to Pittsburgh, where they bought flat-boats in which they descended the Ohio River to Columbia OH, a few miles above Cincinnati. He later moved to Laural, Franklin County IN where he owned a 240-acre farm near Brookville IN. He married, March 27, 1814, Mary Waring (5.1.3.10.10). They both died Franklin County, Indiana.
