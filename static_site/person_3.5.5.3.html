MARTHA MEAD

ID: 3.5.5.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.5.5.3) MARTHA MEAD (8RK2-0V) born July 18, 1732 Greenwich and died January 1809 South Salem, Westchester, NY; married c1762-63 Hon. Nathan Rockwell (8RKG-P5) born c1730 and born to them were: (3.5.5.3.1) WILLIAM ROCKWELL baptized October 11, 1767 South Salem NY and died February 20, 1820; married December 7, 1786 Huldah Boughton/Bouton [dau of Gould & Elizabeth (Gilbert) Bouton] born May 3, 1767 South Salem NY and died 1827; and born to them was:

Children (4)

  • STEPHEN ROCKWELL 3.5.5.3.2
  • BENJAMIN ROCKWELL 3.5.5.3.3
  • THOMAS ROCKWELL baptized February 16, 1777 South Salem NY and died August 20, 3.5.5.3.4
  • MARGARET ROCKWELL died August 1, 1812; married c1796 Dr. Malachi Foot born 3.5.5.3.5
