JOSEPH FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.2.4.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.4.4) JOSEPH FERRIS born October 27, 1789 and died June 11, 1864; buried in Ferris Cemetery, Smyrna, Chenango, NY [there is a conflict here - this Joseph is listed also in the (5) James line.] [cemetery records say he died at age 72y 7m 15d which makes this birth date wrong] Joseph purchased a farm from Robert Ferris on Smyrna Hill, where he and his son, Ziba, lived and died; married Sally Wood [possibly dau of Thankful Wood, second wife of Israel B. Ferris (3.1.2.2.1.3)?] born June 13, 1793 and died October 18, 1868 [cemetery records say she died age 75y 4m 14d which makes this birth date wrong]. There is a Joseph Ferris who died June 11, 1861 at 72y 7m 15d and his wife, Sally who died October 18, 1868 at 75y 4m 14d buried at the Ferris Cemetery, believed to be these folks. Born to them were:

Children (5)

  • RUTH FERRIS 3.1.2.2.4.4.1
  • JOSEPH FERRIS “Ziba” born 1824 Smyrna NY and died there April 14, 1865 of 3.1.2.2.4.4.2
  • THANKFUL FERRIS 3.1.2.2.4.4.3
  • JULIA FERRIS – adopted; married Charles O. Tuttle 3.1.2.2.4.4.4
  • ELIZABETH FERRIS - listed as a possible - 3.1.2.2.4.4.5
