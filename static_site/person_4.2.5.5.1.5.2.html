ALBERT SWARTOUT

ID: 4.2.5.5.1.5.2 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.5.1.5.2) ALBERT SWARTOUT born January 20, 1838/39 Woodstock Township, Lenawee, MI and died March 12, 1903 Summerfield, Marshall, KS; buried there Summerfield Cemetery; resided in Lenawee Co through 1880s where he was a farmer; moved to Summerfield 1893 – 1900-03 was a broom maker; married October 22, 1881 in Jackson Co MI, Hannah Swartout [dau of Cornelius & Maria (Ellsworth) Swartout] born 1842 NY and died c1887; buried Cement City Cemetery; Jackson Co MI; and born to them was:
