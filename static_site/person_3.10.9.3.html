EBENEZER PECK

ID: 3.10.9.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.9.3) EBENEZER PECK born March 12, 1743/44 Greenwich CT and died there September 8, 1833; buried Palmer Hill Cemetery, Stamford; married c1771 Hannah Lockwood [dau of Edmund & Hannah (Scofield) Lockwood] born November 29, 1750 Stamford and died April 19, 1830 Greenwich; buried with Ebenezer; and born to them were: (3.10.9.3.1) EBENEZER PECK born March 22, 1771 Greenwich CT and died November 9, 1837 Stamford CT; married November 8, 1799 Elizabeth Raymond [dau of Paul & Elizabeth (Reed) Raymond] born April 18, 1777 Norwalk CT and died March 16, 1848 Stamford; buried Palmer Hill Cemetery; and born to them were:
