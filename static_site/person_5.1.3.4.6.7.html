PRISCILLA FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.6.7 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.6.7) PRISCILLA FERRIS born c1810/11 and died September 6, 1847 age 36y; buried Ferris Farm cemetery; never married. From her original stone marker "Thou are gone to the grave but twer wrong to deplore then, when God was thy ransom, thy guardian and guide. He gave thee and took thee, and will restore thee, where death hath no sting since the Savior hath died".

No children recorded
