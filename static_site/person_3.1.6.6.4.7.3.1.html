ROSINA LAURA ANDERSON

ID: 3.1.6.6.4.7.3.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.6.4.7.3.1) ROSINA LAURA ANDERSON born April 11, 1860 on the family farm outside of Golconda IL and died June 20, 1949 Pope Co IL; married January 20, 1881, as his 2d wife, Columbus Wilson born August 31, 1845 Staffordsburg, Kenton, KY and died January 24, 1919 Eddyville IL; Columbus brought with him from his first marriage, 3 kids: William b. 1856; James b. 1858; and Lora M., who married Albert Harris.
