MARION RUSSELL FERRIS

ID: 2.1.4.1.3.9.2.1.6 | Source: Peter
(2.1.4.1.3.9.2.1.6) MARION RUSSELL FERRIS (Sam) born April 9, 1921 Scottville, Mason, MI and died there August 18, 1964; buried there Brookside Cemetery (Photo); Marion was a Captain in the U.S. Army during WWII and was wounded at the Remagen Bridge; married January 1, 1942 Eldonna Bosworth born March 5, 1922.
