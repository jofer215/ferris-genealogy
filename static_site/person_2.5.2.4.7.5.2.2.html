MARIA LOUISE HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.4.7.5.2.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.7.5.2.2) MARIA LOUISE HOYT (Louisa Maria) born April 19, 1844 Stamford and died there March 20, 1930; married May 19, 1880 in Stamford, Francis Edward Skelding (2.5.2.5.3.5.1.5); both buried Woodland Cemetery; – see him for offspring.

No children recorded
