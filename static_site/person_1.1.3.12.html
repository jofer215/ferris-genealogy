ELIZABETH FERRIS

ID: 1.1.3.12 | Source: John
(1.1.3.12) ELIZABETH FERRIS (Eliza) born June 2, 1783 Peekskill (12th child, 1st born to Elizabeth) and died April 27, 1866. Elizabeth and John gave quitclaim deed to Elizabeth Ferris, widow of Jonathan Ferris, for land owned jointly by John Jacobs and Peter Ferris, formerly occupied by Caleb D. Ferris. They purchased a farm from the Ferris tract in Kortwright (near Hobart), Delaware Co NY called 'Ferris Hill'. Elizabeth married February 4, 1801 John Jacobs [son of Samuel & Rebecca (Seaman) Jacobs] born February 4, 1776 Peekskill and died May 18, 1854 Masonville; and born to them was:
