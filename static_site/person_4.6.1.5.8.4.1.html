JOSEPH ALSOP LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.1.5.8.4.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.5.8.4.1) JOSEPH ALSOP LOCKWOOD (#2317) born November 5, 1847 Pound Ridge and in 1888 resided at Yonkers NY; graduate of Union College, 1867; Assistant Engineer Yonkers Water Works; married February 18, 1880 at Yonkers, Emma Leah Clark [dau of Joseph & Ann Maria (Smith) Clark] born in NYC. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • FLORENCE CLARK LOCKWOOD 4.6.1.5.8.4.1.1
  • HOWARD ALSOP LOCKWOOD 4.6.1.5.8.4.1.2
