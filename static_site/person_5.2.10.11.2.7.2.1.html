(5.2.10.11.2.7.2.1) SHERMAN BROWN (5.2.10.11.2.7.3) SHERMAN B. MEKEEL born July 11, 1867 Philipstown and died Nelsonville, July 16, 1908; married at Farmer's Mills, Town of Kent, Putnam Co NY January 1, 1890 Lucy Smalley; Sherman conducted a grocery store in the village of Nelsonville and continued at it up to the time of his death.
