MARY LYDIA FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.1.11.8.2.2.4 | Source: Joseph

Swan (Dr.); Director of Shanghai YMCA; founder of hospital and health spa in Palms CA; and born to them were: (3.1.2.1.11.8.2.2.4.1) FREDERICK SWAN
