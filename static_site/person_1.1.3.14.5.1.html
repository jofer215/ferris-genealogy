MARY ADELAIDE MERRITT

ID: 1.1.3.14.5.1 | Source: John
(1.1.3.14.5.1) MARY ADELAIDE MERRITT born May 9, 1845 Caistor Township, Lincoln County, Ontario and died August 20, 1895; married, as his 1st wife, Israel Dody Marshall (Israel Dowdy Marshall) [son of Charles & Lydia C. (Sweazey) Marshall] born September 6, 1842 Saltfleet Township, Wentworth Co, Ontario, Canada and died there at Stoney Creek, May 31, 1929; buried Ker United Cemetery, Caistor Township, Lincoln Co, Ontario. After Mary’s death, Israel remarried; one source has 2 children born to this 2d marriage, but their birth dates are before Mary’s death so here they are listed as Mary’s. Born to Mary and Israel were:

Children (8)
