ID: 5.1.3.8.6.4.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.4.1) LOUIS FRANKLIN FERRIS born July 20, 1865 and died June 23, 1952 New York City; a banker in New York with the Broadway National Bank, New York City 1885-1908; then the Broadway Saving Bank, Secretary, Treasurer and Trustee until his death; unmarried
