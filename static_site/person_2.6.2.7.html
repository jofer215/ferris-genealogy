ISRAEL KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.7 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.7) ISRAEL KNAPP (Dr.) born March 28, 1738 Stamford and died 1787 Crompond-Downsville, Westchester, NY; served in the Revolutionary War from Westchester Co NY; married c1761/62 at Stamford, Prudence Scofield [dau of Nathaniel & Susannah (Waterbury) Scofield] born June 11, 1740 Stamford and died October 5, 1835 Downsville, Delaware, NY.
