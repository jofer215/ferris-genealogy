MUNSON BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.6.10 | Source: James
(5.2.2.6.10) MUNSON BUXTON born June 26, 1787 Stamford CT and died there August 24, 1866; a farmer; married 1st c1807 in Stamford, Sally Gray [dau of Joseph & Hannah (Leeds) Gray] born August 26, 1789 Stamford and buried Turn of River Cemetery, Stamford; married 2d November 5, 1808 in Stamford, Elizabeth Gray [sister of Sally] born July 21, 1787 Stamford and died there February 1, 1877; buried Turn of River Cemetery. Born to him and his wives were 4 sons born between c1805-20

No children recorded
