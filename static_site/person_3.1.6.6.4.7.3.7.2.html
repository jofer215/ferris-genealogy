OPAL LOIS ANDERSON

ID: 3.1.6.6.4.7.3.7.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.6.4.7.3.7.2) OPAL LOIS ANDERSON born October 11, 1906 Golconda IL; living 1981 Brooklyn NY; married June 29, 1926 Lloyd Wentzell born March 7, 1900 and died June 24, 1978 Brooklyn NY; they had one son who was a hemophiliac who lived to be almost 40 years of age before he died. He married and had two children in Brooklyn.

No children recorded
