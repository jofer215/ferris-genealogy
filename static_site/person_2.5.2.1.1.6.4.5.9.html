RICHARD ALVA BUXTON

ID: 2.5.2.1.1.6.4.5.9 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.1.1.6.4.5.9) RICHARD ALVA BUXTON born August 29, 1879 Stockbridge WI and died November 11, 1945 Appleton, Outagamie, WI; married 1st June 10, 1903 in Wittenberg WI, Myrtle Helms [dau of Frank & Rose] born June 24, 1884 Wittenberg and died October 22, 1918 Appleton; 2d May 4, 1921 in Appleton, Irene Louise Hinchcliff born July 3, 1889 Appleton and died December 16, 1954 Appleton; and born to him and Myrtle were:

Children (5)

  • MELL HELMS BUXTON 2.5.2.1.1.6.4.5.9.1
  • FRANCIS SCOFIELD BUXTON 2.5.2.1.1.6.4.5.9.2
  • RICHARD ALVA BUXTON 2.5.2.1.1.6.4.5.9.3
  • WARREN ORVILLE BUXTON 2.5.2.1.1.6.4.5.9.4
