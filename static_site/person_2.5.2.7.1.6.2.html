WILLIAM HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.7.1.6.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.7.1.6.2) WILLIAM HOYT (#3559) born April 29, 1783 and died November 7, 1841 probably at North Stamford where he lived; a farmer; William III when he married and William Jr. in 1811; married September 9, 1806 Mrs. Mary Augur of Northford CT, who died February 3, 1865 age 79y 6m; and born to them were:
