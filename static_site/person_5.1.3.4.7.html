JOSEPH FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.7 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.7) JOSEPH FERRIS born September 20, 1776 Greenwich CT and died May 17, 1831 Madisonville OH; moved Ohio 1799 and settled on 700 acres of land in Madisonville, Columbia Township, Hamilton Co OH; followed farming, milling and distilling; took his produce to New Orleans on a flatboat, and return by way of New York; c1820 built a frame house (Photo) for the purpose of educating his children and any others who wished to attend; many Indian relics yielded by aboriginal graves on the farm; 1934 house was occupied by W. F. Jewett, a descendant. He hired the teachers and paid them himself. Joseph was a Whig and a member of the Masonic Lodge of Cincinnati; married 1818 Priscilla Knapp (5.1.3.3.4.3); both buried Ferris Cemetery (on the farm), now Mariemont Cemetery, Cincinnati OH (Ferris Family Cemetery).
