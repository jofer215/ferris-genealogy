ODLE CLOSE KNAPP

ID: 3.10.7.4.4.1.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.7.4.4.1.1) ODLE CLOSE KNAPP (Photo) born May 26, 1815 Round Hill, Greenwich CT; received a common-school education and was reared as a merchant in his father’s store and then continued at the store until at least 1880; postmaster most of his life; director in the Stamford National Bank and for many years, the 1st Vice President of the Greenwich Savings Bank and a trustee since its organization; married 1st January 19, 1841 Caroline B Hobby [dau of Guy B] died February 27, 1848; married 2d March 8, 1849 Eunice A Brown (3.10.7.4.4.5.1); married 3d August 2, 1883 in Stamford, Mary A Thompson born 1847 Danby NY; and born to him and Caroline were:
