MARY ESTHER KNAPP

ID: 2.6.4.7.5.6 | Source: Peter
(2.6.4.7.5.6) MARY ESTHER KNAPP (Polly) born February 16, 1819 Stamford and died there, March 4, 1860; married at Stamford c1838 John T. Moore. After her death, he married his sister-in-law, Jane Caroline (Hudson) Knapp.

No children recorded
