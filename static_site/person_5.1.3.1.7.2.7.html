HANNAH FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.7 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.7) HANNAH FERRIS born January 10, 1819 Eaton, Madison, NY and died November 10, 1848 Fullerton OH; married March 1, 1843 her brother-in-law, Dr. John Milton Burlingame who died April 17, 1860. Hannah lived with her sister Mary and after Mary died 1842, Hannah and John wed. After Hannah’s death, John married Elizabeth Campbell and they had a son. Born to Hannah and John were:

Children (2)

  • LYDIA M. BURLINGAME 5.1.3.1.7.2.7.1
  • WILLARD D. BURLINGAME 5.1.3.1.7.2.7.2
