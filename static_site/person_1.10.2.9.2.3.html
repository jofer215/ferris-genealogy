VALENTINE MOTT, M.D.,

ID: 1.10.2.9.2.3 | Source: John
(1.10.2.9.2.3) VALENTINE MOTT, M.D., born July 22, 1822 and died September 20, 1854 New Orleans LA; married Isabella Fitzpatrick, of Edinburg; his will of April 26, 1852, probated October 11, 1854, disinherited his wife. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • ARTHUR MOTT 1.10.2.9.2.3.1
  • FRANCES S.L. MOTT died April 25, 1912 S. Orange NJ; married 1.10.2.9.2.3.2
