ESTHER FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.2.2.1.2.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.2.2.1.2.2) ESTHER FERRIS (Photograph) born November 26, 1909 Greenwich CT and died December 18, 1968 Fillmore CA; played the piano and played straight classical repertoire. She inherited her Grandfather Munson's artistic talent. During WWII, when her husband was away, she worked in the art department of Conde Nast in Old Greenwich, drawing. She married, May 5, 1934 at Greenwich, Edward Upson Beach and born to them was: (5.1.3.4.2.2.1.2.2.1) ELEANOR FERRIS BEACH (Photograph) born July 6, 1946 Pelham NY; graduated Fillmore High School 1964; married 1st William E. Johnston, Jr., and 2d August 10, 1996, Robert Haak - no offspring. Eleanor is the scholar of the family, having degrees from a couple of colleges in California, including a PhD in religion. She participates in digs in Israel and does other archeological research. She teaches at Augustana College, Rock Island IL., lectures, publishes papers, etc.; both Eleanor and Robert are Associate Professors of Religion at Augustana

No children recorded
