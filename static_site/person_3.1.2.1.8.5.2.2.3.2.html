ID: 3.1.2.1.8.5.2.2.3.2 | Source: Joseph

Clemens, John Tabb Snodgrass [son of Elisha Kelly & Matilda Lucille (Marsh) Snodgrass] born December 23, 1908 Paterson NJ and died June 7, 1966 Annapolis, Ann Arundal, MD; buried Arlington VA, Section 1, Site 1874 – they divorced 1931 (Major General, US Army); BG Snodgrass head a taskforce (Operation Snodgrass) which involved full-scale firing of the Hercules missile system 1958 (he remarried – Ruth Wheelwright – they divorced) married 2d February 21, 1834 in NJ, Harold Francis Traua born May 7, 1906 in Utica NY and died February 13, 1974 at Fort Ord CA; buried Mt.
