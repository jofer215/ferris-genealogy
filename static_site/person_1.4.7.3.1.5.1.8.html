THEODORE ROOSEVELT PELL

ID: 1.4.7.3.1.5.1.8 | Source: John
(1.4.7.3.1.5.1.8) THEODORE ROOSEVELT PELL born May 12, 1878 Yonkers NY (NYC) and died August 18, 1967; married Florence Cramp [dau of Edwin S. Cramp, of Philadelphia]. He was a member of the firm of Pell & Tibbits, real estate. He was known to lawn tennis enthusiasts as one of the country’s ranking players both in singles and doubles, having taken part in many tournments. In 1907, 1909, and 1911 he won the national indoor championship in singles and the national indoors doubles four times between 1905-1912; ranked in the US Top Ten 5 times between 1910-1918; member of International Tennis Hall of Fame

No children recorded
