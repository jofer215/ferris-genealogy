EMILY SOPHIA ROGERS

ID: 5.2.4.2.2.7 | Source: James
(5.2.4.2.2.7) EMILY SOPHIA ROGERS born July 10, 1794 Stamford and died there 1832; buried there St. John’s & St. Andrew’s Cemetery; married December 25, 1812 in Stamford, Henry Fitch Rogers [son of Henry & Catherine (Van Raust) Rogers]

No children recorded
