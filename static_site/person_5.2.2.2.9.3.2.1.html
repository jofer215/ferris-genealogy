CLARA LOUISE BALDWIN

ID: 5.2.2.2.9.3.2.1 | Source: James
(5.2.2.2.9.3.2.1) CLARA LOUISE BALDWIN born September 7, 1876 Stamford CT and died there October 8, 1972; buried Roxbury Rural Cemetery; educated in the local schools and a newspaper composer at the The Stamford Advocate; married 1st October 2, 1900 in Stamford, Richard Milton Clark [son of Charles Allen & Irene Amy (Dewey) Clark] born May 2, 1871 Higganum, Middlesex, CT and died July 16, 1932 Stamford; buried Roxbury Cemetery; married 2d 1932 in Stamford Frank E. Downes born September 1, 1867 Greenwich CT. Born to her and Richard were: (5.2.2.2.9.3.2.1.1) DOROTHY FRANCES CLARK born October 3, 1900 Stamford and died January 8, 1984 Bethel CT; buried Roxbury Rural Cemetery, Stamford; a bookkeeper, stenographer and housewife; married January 1, 1922 in Stamford, William Ernest Spiers [son of Charles Edward & Harriet Louisa (Biggs) Spiers] born May 4, 1892 Stamford CT and died May 20, 1988 Rogers AR; buried Roxbury Rural Cemetery. William was a WWI Vet, served in France. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • ELIZABETH LUCILE CLARK 5.2.2.2.9.3.2.1.3
