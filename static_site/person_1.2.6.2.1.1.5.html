SARAH JANE UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.6.2.1.1.5 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.1.1.5) SARAH JANE UNDERHILL born June 1825 Zanesville OH and died there March 28, 1904; married June 23, 1842 in West Alexander PA, John J Arter [son of William & Elizabeth (Proven) Arter] born September 10, 1823 Irville, Muskigum, OH and died January 30, 1883 Zanesville OH; he was elected City Marshall and served 2 terms; then repeatedly elected as Constable; both buried Greenwood Cemetery, Zanesville; and born to them were:
