CHARLES FERRIS BASSETT

ID: 5.1.3.8.2.2.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.2.2.3) CHARLES FERRIS BASSETT born 1844 Linwood, OH (Civil War Vet.); married Bertha "Bertie” Stewart born Newport KY and died age 42y. The family was living in the 1st Ward of Cincinnati in the 1880 census. Charles was a lumber merchant and a veteran of the Civil War, having served three years in the armies of Burnside. He was connected with the National Flag Company in Cincinnati. Born to them were:

Children (4)
