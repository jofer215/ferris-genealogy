JOHN HOLMES

ID: 5.2.3.2 | Source: James
(5.2.3.2) JOHN HOLMES Jr born July 24, 1732 Stamford and died March 6, 1818 Darien CT; served as a Private in 1st Regiment between July 1 and December 9, 1780; married January 30, 1769 in Stamford Sarah Hait (Hoyt) (2.5.2.4.6); both buried Noroton River Cemetery, Darien CT; and born to them were:

Children (6)

  • BETSEY HOLMES 5.2.3.2.1
  • SARAH HOLMES 5.2.3.2.3
  • JOHN HOLMES 5.2.3.2.4
  • REBECCA HOLMES 5.2.3.2.5
  • MARY HOLMES 5.2.3.2.6
  • ABBA HOLMES 5.2.3.2.7
