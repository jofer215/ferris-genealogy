PERCY W WEYER

ID: 4.2.5.5.1.5.1.1.2 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.5.1.5.1.1.2) PERCY W WEYER born November 4, 1893 TX and died March 22, 1963 Contra Costa Co CA; 1920 resided Pershing Co NV – telegrapher for railroad; 1930 resided Stanislaus Co CA - was telegrapher for steam railroad depot; married c1925 Georgia Loretta {maiden name unk} born April 3, 1908 WA and died July 20, 1996 Sacramento CA; 1930 she was a telephone operator

No children recorded
