BENJAMIN FERRIS went West

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.11 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.11) BENJAMIN FERRIS went West (meaning west from Connecticut?); married Elizabeth/ Eliza Briggs. (?) Maybe the twig of Edgar Ferris (1.4.1.3.7.1) belongs here. (?) The Briggs’s were among the very early settlers of eastern Dutchess County NY. Warren H. Wilson says the first settlers, Benj. Ferris and Nathan Birdsall came to Quaker Hill in 1728, and that they were soon followed by others bearing the names, Irish, Wing, Briggs, Toffey, Akin, Taber, Russell, Osborn, Merritt, Dakin and Hoag, and that in ten years the tide of settlement was full flowing.

No children recorded
