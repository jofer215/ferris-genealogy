HEZEKIAH GRAHAM CANFIELD

ID: 3.1.6.6.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.6.4) HEZEKIAH GRAHAM CANFIELD born September 20, 1781 Manchester VT and died May 13, 1863 Versailles, Ripley, IN; buried Franklin Church Cemetery, Ripley Co IN; married August 26, 1802 (1805) at Luzerne Co PA, Mary ‘Polly’ Rowley born October 6, 1788 PA and died Ripley Co IN February 17, 1868; buried with her husband; according to family tradition, Hezekiah went to Steuben Co NY from which he served in the War of 1812; and born to them were:
