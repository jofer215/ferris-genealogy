IDA MAY COLE

ID: 1.2.3.7.2.6.3.3.5 | Source: John
(1.2.3.7.2.6.3.3.5) IDA MAY COLE born May 1, 1860 Beaver Dams NY and died June 7, 1916 Geneva, Ontario, NY; buried Whitney Cemetery, Seneca Castle NY; married 1st August 19, 1877 at Corning NY, John Wilson Bryan born December 23, 1855 Geneva NY and died there August 22, 1898 [son of William & Sarah Jane (Wilson) Bryan]; buried Glenwood Cemetery, Geneva NY; married 2d Jay Prosser Jones born January 16, 1856 Seneca Castle and died May 8, 1906 Geneva NY [son of Prosser and Catherine (Fitch) Jones].
