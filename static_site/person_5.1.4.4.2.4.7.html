JULIUS AUGUSTUS JESSUP

ID: 5.1.4.4.2.4.7 | Source: James
(5.1.4.4.2.4.7) JULIUS AUGUSTUS JESSUP Jr born February 28, 1849 Burlington, Lawrence, OH and died February 3, 1908 Saline Co MO; married M Belle {mnu} born June 18, 1849 and died November 11, 1909; both buried Miami Cemetery, Saline Co MO; and they had an infant buried with them.

No children recorded
