HUGH ROBERT ROSS

ID: 3.1.6.8.1.3.5.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.1.3.5.3) HUGH ROBERT ROSS born February 1, 1871 Teeswater, Ontario and died 1937; established a business in Rossburn and became a prominent Tory bagman; and biographer of Sir Rodmond P. Roblin, the Premier of Manitoba; married December 22, 1897 at Renfrew, Ontario, Rebecca Rankin; Hugh was a pioneer school teacher in Manitoba and a local preacher in the Methodist United Church, and a general merchant in Rossburn for a time; moved to Winnipeg in 1921; strong Conservative and served the party for a number of years

No children recorded
