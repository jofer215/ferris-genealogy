GLORIA JUNE GILBERT

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.2.3.2.7.4.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.2.3.2.7.4.2) GLORIA JUNE GILBERT born June 7, 1940 Fargo ND; married September 2, 1960 at Lisbon ND, Thomas Waldo Pagel born March 21, 1939 Rugby ND; resided in Milbank SD; both Gloria and Tom graduated from high school at Lisbon ND and North Dakota State University, Fargo ND. They married while in college and after graduation, moved to Bismarck ND 1962. Tom’s Bachelor degree is in Business Economics and Gloria’s is History and English; Gloria has never taught school. Tom went to work first in the Economic Development Commission of ND and ended up as the Secretary of the Governor’s Commission on Aging in North Dakota. They moved to Milbank SD in December 1966 and went into the jewelry business with Jesse and Bob Riddle; selling the Riddles their interest in 1984. Tom then did some consulting work for Dakota Granite Company in Milbank. In 1987, they bought a family shoe store and sold it in 1992. Tom was appointed Grant County Treasurer in 1994 to fill the term out after the treasurer retired and was reelected in the 1996 election - 1998 still serving as Treasurer; retired April 2003. Gloria has a home sewing and alternation business and does transcription for an insurance company, plus part-time bookkeeping. They were involved in a Lube Shop and cattle leasing. Gloria has always been an 'artist', from an early age. Since her Dad sold TV sets, their home was one of the first houses in Lisbon to have one. She was also able to drive new Olds demonstrators, as a teenager.
