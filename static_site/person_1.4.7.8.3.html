EMILY LOUISA COCK

ID: 1.4.7.8.3 | Source: John
(1.4.7.8.3) EMILY LOUISA COCK born December 18, 1815(1814) NYC and died 1888 Rahway NJ; married, May 10, 1837 Richard Hartshorne Bowne [son of Robert H. & Sarah (Hartshorne) Bowne] born October 22, 1810 and died May 1, 1881. They were Friends and he was a laywer. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • ROBERT S. BOWNE 1.4.7.8.3.1
  • SARAH E. BOWNE 1.4.7.8.3.2
  • ELIZA PARSONS BOWNE 1.4.7.8.3.3
  • ANN AUGUSTA BOWNE 1.4.7.8.3.4
