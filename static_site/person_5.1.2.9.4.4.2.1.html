ORLANDO SMITH

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.2.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.2.1) ORLANDO SMITH born September 1849 and died May 3, 1910 Towners NY; married Sarah Elizabeth Bennett [dau of David & Amanda (Tompkins)] born December 14, 1859 Kent NY and died February 7, 1959 Mahopac Falls NY; both buried Raymond Hill Cemetery, Carmel NY. Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, Friday, May 6, 1910: Orlando Smith died at his home in Towners, last Tuesday, May 3, 1910. His death was due to valvular heart disease and he died very suddenly while feeding his horses. His father was Alexander Smith and his mother, Mary Jane Christian Smith. The deceased was 67 years, 7 months, and 21 days old. The funeral was held at his late residence, this morning at 11 o'clock. Interment in Raymond Hill Cemetery. Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, Thursday, Feb. 12, 1959: Mrs. Sarah Elizabeth Smith, widow of Orlando Smith, died at her home on Myrtle avenue, Mahopac Falls, on Saturday, Feb. 7, 1959. Her age was 99. Mrs. Smith had been ill for two weeks but until her recent illness was able to be about her home most of the time. The deceased was a daughter of David Bennett and Amanda Tompkins Bennett and born in the town of Kent, Dec. 14, 1859. When a young woman she was united in marriage with Orlando Smith and they operated farms in the town of Kent and later in the Haviland Hollow section of the town of Patterson. Mr. Smith died over 40 years ago and for the past 35 years, Mrs. Smith, had resided in Mahopac Falls. Surviving are seven children, Alex Smith and Chauncey Smith, both of Danbury, Conn., George Smith of Brewster, Charles Smith of Mahopac, Mrs. Ida Roscoe, Mrs. Johanna Harkins and Mrs. Mary Nelson, all of Carmel. She also leaves 33 grandchildren, 56 great grandchildren and 22 great-great grandchildren. She was a member of the Richardsville Episcopal church at Kent Cliffs. The body was placed in the vault at Raymond Hill Cemetery and interment will be made in the family plot there at the convenience of the family. Born to them were:
