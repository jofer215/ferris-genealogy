ELIZA W. CHADEAYNE

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.4.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.4.1) ELIZA W. CHADEAYNE born September 20, 1824 at the farm in Tarrytown NY and died October 31, 1904 Bedford Station NY; buried Buxton Cemetery, Bedford Hills NY; married November 5, 1851 at Ossining NY Stephen Conklin Sutton [son of Willet & Amy (Conklin) Sutton] born October 12, 1818 Bedford NY and died there November 5, 1894. He was a cattle dealer. Born to them were:

Children (5)
