FLORENCE LOUISE MILBURN

ID: 3.11.1.3.1.5.5.2.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.3.1.5.5.2.6) FLORENCE LOUISE MILBURN born March 29, 1881 Bristol IN and died May 18, 1973 Elkhart, Elkhart, IN; married April 17, 1906 in Elkart, Charles Lytton Haas born September 3, 1871 St George, Ontario and died December 21, 1952; graduated from Veterinary School with DVM Degree; joined Ringling Brothers Circus for a year as the circus Vet and baritone horn player before 1906; practice vet medicine in Bristol IN and worked in the Buescher Band instrument company in Elkhart; and they had 7 kids, 4 of which were:

Children (4)

  • CHARLES MILBURN HAAS 3.11.1.3.1.5.5.2.6.2
