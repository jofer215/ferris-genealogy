ALONZO CARPENTER

ID: 1.2.5.1.3.2.2.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.3.2.2.1) ALONZO CARPENTER born August 30, 1830 Ossining NY and died 1885 Crawford Co IA; buried Oakland Cemetery, Denison, Crawford, IA; married 1st May 2, 1852 in NYC, Phoebe Jane South born November 26, 1833 NY and died February 21, 1865 WI; buried Walnut Hill Cemetery, Baraboo, Sauk, WI; 2d January 28, 1878 in Richland Co WI, Elizabeth Waterman [dau of Rufus & Hannah (Webber) Waterman] born October 27, 1843 NY and died March 3, 1913 Denison, Crawford, IA; buried with Alonzo; and born to him and Phoebe were:
