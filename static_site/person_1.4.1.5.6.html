CHARITY FERRIS

ID: 1.4.1.5.6 | Source: John
(1.4.1.5.6) CHARITY FERRIS born January 10, 1802 and died December 1873 (or November 17, 1875?); married Captain Philip H. Woodhouse and born to them were:

Children (6)

  • ANNA B. WOODHOUSE; unmarried 1.4.1.5.6.1
  • JAMES WOODHOUSE; bachelor 1.4.1.5.6.2
  • PHILLIP H. WOODHOUSE, JR. 1.4.1.5.6.3
  • SARAH WOODHOUSE 1.4.1.5.6.4
  • CLAIBORNE WOODHOUSE 1.4.1.5.6.5
  • ELIZABETH WOODHOUSE; unmarried 1.4.1.5.6.6
