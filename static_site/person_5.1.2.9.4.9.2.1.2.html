RALPH S. CHRISTIAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.9.2.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.9.2.1.2) RALPH S. CHRISTIAN born December 9, 1907 Peekskill NY and died November 3, 1980 FDR Memorial VA Hospital, Montrose NY; married Dorothy {maiden name unk}; resided Putnam Valley 1972. He was a decorated WWII Veteran having been awarded two bronze stars, three oak leaf clusters, the New York State Silver Star, the Purple Heart as well as Good Conduct and Sharpshooters medals. He was a member of the 36th Infantry Division, 141st Infantry Regiment, Able Company. The 141st’s homepage is interesting reading: “Five Years, Five Countries, Five Campaigns”.

No children recorded
