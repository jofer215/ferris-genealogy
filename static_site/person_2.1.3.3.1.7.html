RANSFORD AVERY FERRIS JR

ID: 2.1.3.3.1.7 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.1.7) RANSFORD AVERY FERRIS JR born September 15, 1786 Stamford; baptised February 11, 1787; and died July 4, 1853; married c1811 Lydia Lockwood (4.5.2.3.9.1). They were both buried in Woodland Cemetery, Stamford. Ransford was a farmer. He and his wife joined the Congregational (Presbyterian) Church in Goshen NY November 5, 1816 and were dismissed (?) April 28, 1817. Born to them were [note the difference in age between the two daughters]:
