WILLIAM WARREN BAKER

ID: 5.1.3.1.5.6.2.4.3.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.5.6.2.4.3.1.1) WILLIAM WARREN BAKER born October 8, 1926 Warren Co PA and died May 7, 2001 in the VA Hospital, Tuscon AZ; joined the Army 1941; served in WWII and Korea; Provost Marshal’s office in Japan; instructor at West Point NY and Fort Dix NJ; discharged 1953 and told he might not live more than 3 years from wounds of service; worked for boilermakers building steel structures such as water tanks and city skyscrapers; retired to Tuscon 1981 after living with a friend on the Salamanca Indian Reservation for a number of years; married 3 times; 2d marriage was in 1951 in Camp Kilmer, New Brunswick NJ to Gian Zimmerman [dau of Charles Louis & Mary Louise (Inconstanti) Zimmerman] born August 1927 Orange NJ – divorced c1968 NJ; they had 8 kids, 1 of which was:
