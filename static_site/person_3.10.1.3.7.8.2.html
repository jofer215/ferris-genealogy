REBECCA VAN LOAN PECK

ID: 3.10.1.3.7.8.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.3.7.8.2) REBECCA VAN LOAN PECK born August 14, 1835 Catskill NY; was living in Roxbury NY 1870, and died March 13, 1885 NYC; married August 18, 1857 Orrin Rice Bouton [son of David Sanford & Angelina (Hill) Bouton] born February 25, 1829 Roxbury, Delaware, NY and died August 1893 Flushing NY; buried Poughkeepsie, Dutchess, NY; he was appointed president of Chapel Hill College 1858; served as such until 1860, when he was called to the presidency of Macon College MO, and remained there until c1863, and then appointed principal of the academy at Roxbury NY; he filled that position until 1870, retiring there to enter the ministry of the Methodist Episcopal church, and serving as such at Bovina NY 1871-72, at Windham NY 1873-75, Stamford and Frnaklin until 1882, thence to Coeymans Hollow, Albany Co, until 1883, in 1884, was assigned the superintendency of the FIVE POINTS MISSION, 63 Park St, NYC, and was still engaged there in the spring of 1888; mission dedicated 1853; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • FRANK RUSS BOUTON 3.10.1.3.7.8.2.3
  • SARAH JANETTE BOUTON 3.10.1.3.7.8.2.4
