HARRY A CARPENTER

ID: 1.2.5.1.3.2.2.1.6 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.3.2.2.1.6) HARRY A CARPENTER born October 1879 Denison, Crawford, IA and died December 14, 1951 Hotel Clinton, Marquette MI; 1925 from Denison to Sault Ste Marie MI; 1926 Marquette MI; 1930 brush company district manager, Marquette; carpenter; 1951 Clinton Hotel night clerk; married before 1913 in Denison IA, Myrtle Barber [dau of Warren & Frances (Tatkins) Barber] born December 1879 Estherville, Emmet, IA and died March 11, 1933 Ann Arbor MI; both buried Park Cemetery, Marquette MI; she graduated from U of Iowa with degree in education; and they had 3 kids, 1 of which was:
