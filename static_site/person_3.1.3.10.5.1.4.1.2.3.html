CHARLES RANDALL PALMER

ID: 3.1.3.10.5.1.4.1.2.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.3.10.5.1.4.1.2.3) CHARLES RANDALL PALMER born October 10, 1951 San Francisco CA; served in the U.S. Army, 1971-73 being in the last draft of the Viet Nam era; served as an administrative Sergeant with the 1st Infantry Division at Fort Riley KS; 4 years college degree; Masters in Accounting; and Register Nursing Degree; now a R.N., in a Massachusetts State Hospital; married 1st January 6, 1975 Mary Preneaveau - divorced 1987; married 2d January 27, 1989 at Methuen MA, widow Mary Elizabeth (Manzelli) Rose born January 27, 1950 Boston MA; Charles and Mary reside Methuen MA; and born to Charles and his first wife, Mary, were:
