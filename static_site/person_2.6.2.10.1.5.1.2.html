HANNAH E. BROADWAY

ID: 2.6.2.10.1.5.1.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.10.1.5.1.2) HANNAH E. BROADWAY born January 9, 1855 Stamford and died there December 15, 1955; married 1st September 10, 1871 in Bedford NY, Thomas Grant Ackley [son of Thomas & Mary (Taylor) Ackley] born November 5, 1848 North Castle, Westchester, NY and died March 10, 1892 Stamford; in 1880 he was a wheelwright and in 1892 a driver; 2d 1898 George Francis Weymer born November 22, 1870 NY and died March 31, 1951 Stamford; in 1900 he was a photographer; all buried there Long Ridge Cemetery; and born to her and Thomas was:
