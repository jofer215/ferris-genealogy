ELIZA MARIA SCOFIELD

ID: 2.1.3.3.2.2.1 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.2.2.1) ELIZA MARIA SCOFIELD born September 1815 (October 29, 1810) StanwichStamford CT and died March 18, 1868 Stamford; both she and her husband were buried in the Holly Family Plot, Sarles Farm, Hunting Ridge- Stamford (Woodland Cemetery); married November 9, 1832 at Stanwich, William Walmsley Youngs [son of William & Elizabeth Elwood (Walmsley) Youngs] born March 3, 1809 Stamford and died there May 31, 1874; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • MARY E YOUNGS 2.1.3.3.2.2.1.1
  • SARAH J YOUNGS 2.1.3.3.2.2.1.2
  • ANN E YOUNGS 2.1.3.3.2.2.1.3
  • CHARLES EDWIN YOUNGS 2.1.3.3.2.2.1.4
