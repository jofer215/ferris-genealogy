NANCY FERRIS

ID: 3.1.9.1.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.9.1.1.2) NANCY FERRIS born January (28) 29, 1780 Filkintown NY; married as his 2d wife c1799 Robert Andrus Odell born January 11, 1777 [there is confusion concerning Mr. Odell – same guy married Nancy Ferris (3.1.2.3.2.4)]; and born to them was: (3.1.9.1.1.2.1) ROBERT FERRIS ODELL born 1805 (Essex Co NY); married 1stHarriet Lobdell; married 2d 1843 Roxy Ann Hanchett [dau of Ebenezer & Mary (Collins) Hanchett] born January 17, 1826 and died 1906; they are listed in the 1850 census at Westport NY; he was a carpenter; and in 1869, they resided in Cedar Falls IA. Born to him and his 1st wife were:

No children recorded
