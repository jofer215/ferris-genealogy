LOUISA FERRIS

ID: 1.1.4.1.1.9 | Source: John
(1.1.4.1.1.9) LOUISA FERRIS born May 21, 1826/1828 Savannah Township, Wayne, NY and died August 4, 1894, Cheshire, Allegen, MI; buried there in Pearson Cemetery; married October 30, 1849 in Savannah NY, Elijah Hawes born June 1, 1828 Conquest, Cayuga, NY and died February 21, 1917, at his daughter’s home in Kalamazoo MI; buried there in Riverside Cemetery. Elijah was a Civil War Veteran serving as a Private in Company A of the 3d Michigan Volunteery Calvary. He suffered progressive loss of vision due to his military service and was receiving a Civil War Invalid Pension at the time of his death. From Louisa’s obit: ‘She was an estimable woman and enjoyed the friendship of many’. Born to them were:
