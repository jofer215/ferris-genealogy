MOSES KNAPP, JR.,

ID: 2.6.7.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.3) MOSES KNAPP, JR., (3V8G-1S) born June 5, 1736 Stamford and died October 11 (15), 1821 probably at Bethlehem, Litchfield, CT; served with the Connecticut forces during the Revolutionary War; a Congregationalist and Presbyterian; married September 18, 1760 at Bethlehem, Margaret Kasson born August 30, 1815 Woodbury CT and died at Bethlehem.
