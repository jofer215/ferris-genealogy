JOHN BEDIENT KNAPP

ID: 2.6.5.1.5.9 | Source: Peter
(2.6.5.1.5.9) JOHN BEDIENT KNAPP born August 7, 1814 Stamford CT and died April 13, 1890 New Rochelle, Albany, NY; farmer 1850; married, February 2, 1841 at Stamford, Elizabeth Knapp (2.6.5.9.3.10); both buried Woodland Cemetery, Stamford; and born to them were: (2.6.5.1.5.9.1) JAMES HOWE KNAPP born May 26, 1847 Stamford and died after 1905; ice dealer 1877-81; married c1869/74 at New Haven CT, Anne Elizabeth Rice born c1853/49 New Haven CT; and born to them were:
