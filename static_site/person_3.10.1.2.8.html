ELIZABETH PECK

ID: 3.10.1.2.8 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.8) ELIZABETH PECK born July 19, 1763 Greenwich, Fairfield, CT and died November 16, 1820 Otsego, Otsego, NY; buried there Otsdawa Cemetery; married as his 1st wife, June 16, 1783 in Stamford CT, Joseph Youngs [son of Clements & Lydia (Nash) Youngs] born June 9, 1760 Stamford CT and died December 28, 1842 Otsego NY at age 82 years 6 months 19 days - buried Otsdawa Cemetery. After Elizabeth’s death, Joseph remarried to Mary (maiden name unk) who died October 4, 1837 at age 76 years 11 months 14 days; buried at Otsdawa Cemetery. She had 7 sons and 9 daughters, all of whom married and settled in Broome, Chenango and Otesego Counties, NY. Resided in Stamford until November 1784, then removed to Ballston NY, and then in 1789 to Amsterdam NY. In June 1807, she removed to what is now the town of Otego NY where she died.
