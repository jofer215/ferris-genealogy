PETER FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2) PETER FERRIS (8RLL-1C) born c1697/1700 probably Greenwich; married August 6, 1719 at Greenwich, Mary A. Tucker; he was a member of Second Congregational Church, Greenwich, March 10, 1729; received land from his father 1729; went to Crum Elbow, Nine Partners Patent, Dutchess Co NY very early and was influential in negotiating the settlement between the Nine Partners and the Beekman interests over the Patent boundaries in April 1748; taxed in Crum Elbow from February 1755 through June 1763; and born to them were:

Children (6)

  • SAMUEL FERRIS JR., 3.1.2.1
  • TIMOTHY FERRIS 3.1.2.2
  • PETER FERRIS 3.1.2.3
  • MERCY 3.1.2.4
  • DANIEL FERRIS 3.1.2.5
  • CLAUDIA FERRIS 3.1.2.6
