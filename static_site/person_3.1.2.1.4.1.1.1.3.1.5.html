BESSIE B WHITTALL

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.1.1.3.1.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.1.1.3.1.5) BESSIE B WHITTALL born June 19, 1884 Algoma Township, Kent Co MI and died November 27, 1973 Rockford, Kent, MI; 1930 worked at Wolverine shoe factory; Algoma Township Treasurer; married 1905 Floyd Ray Seeley [son of Samuel Brown & Martha (Depew) Seeley] born February 5, 1881 Whitewater Township, Grand Traverse Co MI and died November 21, 1921 Algoma Township, Kent Co MI; both buried Algoma Cemetery, Rockford, Kent, MI; both were involved in The Grange; and born to them were:
