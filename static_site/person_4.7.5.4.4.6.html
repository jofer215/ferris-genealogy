RUFUS RODMAN LOUNSBERRY

ID: 4.7.5.4.4.6 | Source: Mary
(4.7.5.4.4.6) RUFUS RODMAN LOUNSBERRY born March 10, 1811 Conklin NY and died December 20, 1849 Mongo (Union Mills), LaGrange, NY; married 1st October 25, 1832 in NY, Sarah Weeks born August 28, 1809 and died June 5, 1848; married 2d October 1848 in NY, Eliza Maxwell; and born to him and Sarah were; (4.7.5.4.4.6.1) SARAH A LOUNSBERRY (twin) born June 19, 1835 and died January 28, 1848
