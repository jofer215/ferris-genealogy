ISAAC BELL

ID: 5.2.4.2 | Source: James
(5.2.4.2) ISAAC BELL born September 20, 1736 Stamford CT and died there January 5, 1809; a Tory – joined the enemy April 1, 1779; married 1st 1755 in Stamford, Jemima Holly [dau of John & Hannah (Slauson) Holly] born May 1, 1738 Stamford and died there February 23, 1760; married 2d September 14, 1761 in Stamford, Susannah Smith [dau of Ephraim & Esabel (Bartlett) Smith] born 1740 New Canaan and died October 23, 1820, Stamford; all buried St. John’s & St. Andrew’s Cemetery, Stamford.
