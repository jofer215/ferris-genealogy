JAMES REYNOLDS

ID: 4.5.2.1.4 | Source: Mary
(4.5.2.1.4) JAMES REYNOLDS born May 8, 1759 Greenwich CT and died March 3, 1833; married October 18, 1786 Abigail Knapp (3.11.4.3.7) born October 4, 1755 Greenwich. He was a farmer and reared his large family to industry and economy and they became some of Fairfield County'’ best citizens.
