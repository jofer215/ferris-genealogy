WILLIAM IRVING FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.6.4.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.4.2) WILLIAM IRVING FERRIS born December 31, 1866 Greenwich and died July 4, 1929; was lost overboard from his brother's yacht while sailing in Long Island Sound; was Vice President and Treasurer of the L. E. Waterman Pen Company in New York. He had taken out many patents and originated machinery for the production of the pen. William married, March 9, 1903, Charlotte Pauline Meister. Born to them were: (5.1.3.8.6.4.2.1) IRVING MEISTER FERRIS born January 18, 1904 Brooklyn NY and died March 1935; Alumnus of Lehigh University; with Waterman Pen Company; married May 25, 1929 (or February 4, 1931) Gertrude Joy born August 16, 1904 Plainfield NJ; no offspring
