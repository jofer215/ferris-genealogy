BETSEY SKELDING

ID: 2.5.2.5.3.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.5.3.1) BETSEY SKELDING born October 14, 1764 Stamford and died there June 20, 1849; buried there St. John’s & St. Andrew’s Cemetery; married December 23, 1780 in Stamford, Jared Lockwood [son of Joseph & Charity (Knapp) Lockwood] born July 7, 1758 Greenwich. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • ORICE LOCKWOOD 2.5.2.5.3.1.1
  • NANCY LOCKWOOD 2.5.2.5.3.1.2
  • SAMUEL MILLS LOCKWOOD 2.5.2.5.3.1.3
