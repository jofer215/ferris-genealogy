DOROTHY EDITH GROSS

ID: 1.2.5.1.10.6.1.10.1.3 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.10.6.1.10.1.3) DOROTHY EDITH GROSS born November 30, 1921 Westchester Co NY and died January 27, 1998 Montgomery Co VA; resided CT, SC, AL, CO, NM, FL, MD, NY and VA; married March 29, 1943 in Yonkers, William Henry Parmer [son of Arthur Odell & Hattie Lulu (Laymon) Parmer] born October 25, 1919 VanHornesville NY and died October 6, 1999 King George VA; both buried Shawsville VA; a newspaper printer; and they had 9 kids
