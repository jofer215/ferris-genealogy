LOIS LORRAINE MARTIN

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.10.5.5.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.10.5.5.1.2) LOIS LORRAINE MARTIN born January 26, 1925 Toledo OH; married July 31, 1948 John Suprock [son of Mike and Elizabeth] born 1923, Baden PA. Lois attended the U. of Toledo and later became an associate instructor there, actually teaching her husband, prior to their marriage. She went on to study at Harvard U., and later became a high school teacher. After her children were grown, she returned to college and learned programming. She became a programmer with the Northrop Aircraft Company, later working with United Grocers. She was then recruited to work for Coopers and Lybrand Consulting Group in San Francisco CA. John served as a pilot in the China Theatre in WWII. He is the recipient of the Distinguished Flying Cross (with clusters), Purple Heart, and Air Medal (with clusters). He attended the U. of Toledo and graduated with a degree in Engineering. He worked in the trucking industry, as the Vice President of Real Estate and Construction for Consolidated Freightways and later PIE. They reside (2002) in Sonoma CA. Born to them were:

Children (2)
