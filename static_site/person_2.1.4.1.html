SAMUEL FERRIS

ID: 2.1.4.1 | Source: Peter
(2.1.4.1) SAMUEL FERRIS (KM52-97) born October 17, 1730 Stamford CT and died c1810; a Minuteman at Stamford in the American Revolution; had two sons who marched on one of the Canadian Expeditions, from which no tidings ever came back. With the remaining children, he moved to Blakely, Lackawanna County, PA, after 1794. The name of his wife is unk but born to them were (not in birth sequence) [LDS has Samuel marrying twice: Mrs. Ruth Weed born c1657 and Mary FERRIS born c1668]:
