SAMUEL SHUNK SMITH

ID: 3.1.1.5.5.6.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.5.5.6.2) SAMUEL SHUNK SMITH born July 2, 1838 Chandlerville, Cass, IL; served with the 51st Illinois Infantry, Civil War, and was wounded in a knee by mini-ball; married 1 st Susan Stucky born September 19, 1843 OH - they separated 1880 and divorced June 9, 1886; married 2d Amanda Sumpter; [Jim - interesting - Samuel had a grandchild before his last child born!] and born to Samuel and Susan were:
