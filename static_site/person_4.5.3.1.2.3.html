JOHN LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.5.3.1.2.3 | Source: Mary
(4.5.3.1.2.3) JOHN LOCKWOOD (#1329) (placement in question) born Darien CT and died age 74y March 30, 1843; buried at the Old St. Peters Cemetery, VanCortlandtville NY; moved to NY; married Bathsheba Maibee (photo) who died at age 88. It is purported that Bathsheba’s father was born in France, fought in the American Revolution, married at Yorktown and died leaving landed estates. Born to them were:
