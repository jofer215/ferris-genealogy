ALMIRA ELIZABETH INGERSOLL

ID: 2.5.1.1.5.1.3 | Source: Peter
(2.5.1.1.5.1.3) ALMIRA ELIZABETH INGERSOLL born c1814 Stamford and died there April 23, 1890; buried Scofield Cemetery #3, Stamford; married May 8, 1853 at Stamford, as his 2d wife, Samuel Augustus Holly (2.1.3.3.2.1.1) – see him for offspring

No children recorded
