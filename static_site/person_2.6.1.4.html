DAVID KNAPP

ID: 2.6.1.4 | Source: Peter
(2.6.1.4) DAVID KNAPP born November 20, 1725 Stamford; married c1747 in Stamford, Rebecca {maiden name unk}; David served in the French & Indian Wars as a Sergeant in Company 4, 3d Regiment, Stamford Militia.

No children recorded
