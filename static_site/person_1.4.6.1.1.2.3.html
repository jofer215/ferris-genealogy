EDWIN HORACE WATERBURY

ID: 1.4.6.1.1.2.3 | Source: John
(1.4.6.1.1.2.3) EDWIN HORACE WATERBURY born August 12, 1858 CT and died 1923 Stamford CT; buried there at Woodland Cemetery; 1883 carpenter; married c1882 Nettie M. Scofield [dau of George C. & Julia F. (Searles) Scofield] born 1861 Norwalk CT and died 1925 Stamford CT; buried with Edwin; and born to them were:
