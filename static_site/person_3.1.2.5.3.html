ID: 3.1.2.5.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.5.3) DANIEL FERRIS, Jr. was probably the man in Charlotte who in 1774 was responsible for the road from Jonah Talmags south to John Reynolds and from Samuel Badgleys west to Comfort Rounds; 1779 elected to the post of Constable in Charlotte Precinct; was in Washington Town, Dutchess Co NY 1790 and was Stanford Town, Dutchess Co 1800

No children recorded
