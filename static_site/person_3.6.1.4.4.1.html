HUGH JOSEPH WHITING

ID: 3.6.1.4.4.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.1.4.4.1) HUGH JOSEPH WHITING born March 18, 1820 New York City and died July 27, 1899 Fairfield NJ; buried Bound Brook Cemetery; married December 4, 1845 Josephine Shannon [dau of George W.] born July 1, 1825 Bloomfield NJ and died March 1, 1884 Brooklyn NY. Born to them were: (3.6.1.4.4.1.1) WILLIAM HENRY WHITING born September 3, 1846 Brooklyn and died August 28, 1934 Harwichport MA; married October 13, 1868 Esther Ann Slaght born May 6, 1846 Brooklyn and died June 16, 1921.
