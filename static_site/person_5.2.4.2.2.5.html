FITCH ROGERS

ID: 5.2.4.2.2.5 | Source: James
(5.2.4.2.2.5) FITCH ROGERS born 1790 (January 1802) Stamford and died July 6, 1843 Stamford; buried there St. John’s & St. Andrew’s Cemetery; married December 10, 1828 in Stamford, Mary Elizabeth Smith [dau of Daniel & Catherine (Webb) Smith] born October 28, 1804 Stamford and died there November 8, 1870; buried there North Street Cemetery

No children recorded
