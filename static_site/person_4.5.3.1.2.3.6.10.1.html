WILLIAM PECK BANNING

ID: 4.5.3.1.2.3.6.10.1 | Source: Mary
(4.5.3.1.2.3.6.10.1) WILLIAM PECK BANNING born May 25, 1880 (in New York City) Mt Vernon NY and died June 18, 1962 River Edge NJ; married 1916 Helen Cameron Vroom born March 12, 1890 and died April 12, 1982. He received an AB degree from New York U., in 1902; went to China in 1911 as one of the founders of the newspaper, China Press. He joined AT&T in 1920; assistant VP of Public Relations in 1944.
