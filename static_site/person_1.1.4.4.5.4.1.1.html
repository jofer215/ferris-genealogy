STEPHEN E. MEKEEL

ID: 1.1.4.4.5.4.1.1 | Source: John
(1.1.4.4.5.4.1.1) STEPHEN E. MEKEEL born December 15, 1883 Nelsonville and died September 8, 1974 Syracuse NY. He owned the Ovid NY Gazette and Independent and was editor for many years; married October 1, 1913 at Ovid NY, Sarah Frances Kinne

No children recorded
