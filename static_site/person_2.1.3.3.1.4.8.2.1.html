HORACE STILLMAN CLASON

ID: 2.1.3.3.1.4.8.2.1 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.1.4.8.2.1) HORACE STILLMAN CLASON born September 28, 1870 and died August 3, 1951 Napa Co CA; buried Uriak CA; never married – supposedly Horace and his brother Samuel, were the founders of the Clason Map Company of Denver

No children recorded
