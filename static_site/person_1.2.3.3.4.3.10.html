HELEN VIDAL HARRIS

ID: 1.2.3.3.4.3.10 | Source: John
(1.2.3.3.4.3.10) HELEN VIDAL HARRIS (Photo) born 1834 London, Ontario (July 25, 1834 Port Ryerse, Norfolk, Ontario) and died March 30 1860 of childbirth complications; married June 4, 1856 Maurice Berkeley Portman born 1833 Blandford, Dorset, England and died January 12, 1888 North Petherton, Somerset, England; Hon, Gentleman, Justice of Peace 1881 North Petherton; he owned a cotton plantation in the American south and later elected to parliament; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • MAURICE WILLIAM PORTMAN 1.2.3.3.4.3.10.2
  • MAURICE VIDAL PORTMAN 1.2.3.3.4.3.10.3
