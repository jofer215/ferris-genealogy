DAISY MAY ARTER

ID: 1.2.6.2.1.1.5.2.8 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.1.1.5.2.8) DAISY MAY ARTER (Mary Jane) born August 27, 1881 Zanesville OH and died August 12, 1961 Ashland OR; buried Greenwood Cemetery, Zanesville; married November 14, 1899 in Muskigum Co, Alvin Wesley Keith born c1863 Lowell OH and died February 20, 1938 Zanesville – divorced 1917 after he ran off to live with Daisy’s older sister Lunetta – this caused a riff in the family that lasted until Lunetta’s death

No children recorded
