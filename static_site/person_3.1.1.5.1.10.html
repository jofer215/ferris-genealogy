SAMUEL KNIFFEN FERRIS

ID: 3.1.1.5.1.10 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.5.1.10) SAMUEL KNIFFEN FERRIS born October 17, 1813 Carmel NY and died there July 5, 1890; married 1st November 4, 1841 Mary Esther St. John [dau of Darius and Harriet (Comstock) St. John] born April 16, 1820 and died December 11, 1858; and 2d D. J. Knapp. Note: The St. John Genealogy has Mary Esther as the dau of William & Sally (Saunders) St. John. According to the history of Putnam Co NY, a Samuel Ferris, grandson of Reuben lived on the farm and homestead of Reuben Ferris on the north side of the road which runs to Drewville from the highway leading from Carmel to Croton Falls; this tract is in the town of Carmel, and is bounded by the original line between Lots 6 and 9. Interesting to note that it is said that Reuben sold the 140-acres to Solomon Fowler – is this in error or did Samuel buy the land? Born to Samuel and Mary was:
