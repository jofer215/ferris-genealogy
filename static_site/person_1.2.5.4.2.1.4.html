JACOB CHADEAYNE

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.4 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.4) JACOB CHADEAYNE born January 3, 1799 at “Just a Mere” farm, Yorktown NY and died September 28, 1839 New Castle NY; buried Friends Cemetery, Chappaqua NY; a farmer; married November 5, 1823 Anna Conklin (Photo) [dau of Stephen & Mehitable (Knapp) Conklin] born April 3, 1803 and died May 12, 1858; buried with Jacob.
