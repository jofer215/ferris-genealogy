THEODOSIA FERRIS

ID: 3.1.9.1.9.8 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.9.1.9.8) THEODOSIA FERRIS born February 8, 1800 and died April 24, 1854 Balliston Spa NY; married April 24, 1829 Elisha Carpenter [a carpenter] born 1805 and died April 2, 1854 Ballston Spa NY. Born to them were:

Children (5)

  • MARY ANN CARPENTER 3.1.9.1.9.8.1
  • AMERIAH CARPENTER 3.1.9.1.9.8.2
  • GEORGE WASHINGTON CARPENTER 3.1.9.1.9.8.3
  • AMANDA ADALINE CARPENTER 3.1.9.1.9.8.4
  • A SON CARPENTER died young 3.1.9.1.9.8.5
