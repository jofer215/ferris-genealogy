GEORGE LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.4.2 | Source: Mary
(4.4.4.2) GEORGE LOCKWOOD (#315) born Greenwich; 1838 he lived in New York City, at the age of 86 years, appeared in the Marine Court of the City of New York claiming he enlisted in 1775 in an artillery company. He remembered the following persons in the same company: Millington Lockwood, Heth Peck, Timothy Knapp, and Elnathan Lockwood. He remembers that Millington Lockwood belonged to the same company with him in the early part of the war, but that he afterwards deserted and went to the enemy. He married Sarah {maiden name unk} and born to them were:
