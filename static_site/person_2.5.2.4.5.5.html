SILAS HOYT,

ID: 2.5.2.4.5.5 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.5.5) SILAS HOYT, Jr. (#3533) born November 8, 1775 Stamford CT and died there March 15, 1852; buried North Street Cemetery, Stamford; married February 22, 1807 in Stamford, Charlotte Smith (2.5.2.4.3.1.1). Silas was a farmer and lived in Stamford. Born to them were:

Children (5)

  • ALFRED HOYT 2.5.2.4.5.5.3
  • HARRIET S. HOYT 2.5.2.4.5.5.4
  • SARAH ANN HOYT 2.5.2.4.5.5.5
