PALMER HALL CUSHMAM

ID: 1.1.4.1.1.1.1.7.2 | Source: John
(1.1.4.1.1.1.1.7.2) PALMER HALL CUSHMAM born April 28, 1891 Savannah, Wayne, NY; Bethany College, Lindsbory KS, 1908; assistant managing director, International Harvester Co, South Africa; served in WWI 1918-19, Sergeant, Co. E, 9th Motor Supply Train; resided Salt Lake City UT; charter member of Utah Mayflower Society; married May 16, 1915 Ruth Virginia Allen born December 5, 1888 Neodesha KS; in 1960 she was the State Regent, National Society Daughters of the American Revolution

No children recorded
