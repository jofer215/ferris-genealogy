HANNAH KNAPP

ID: 3.11.4.3.2.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.4.3.2.5) HANNAH KNAPP born March 9, 1789 Spafford, Delaware, NY (Stanford, Delaware, NY) and died December 14, 1868 Lodi NY; married August 20, 1807 in Spafford, John Ingersoll [son of Samuel & Phebe (Wilson) Ingersoll] born May 6, 1782 Stanford NY and died November 11, 1863; served as 2d Lieutenant in Seneca NY receiving appointment 1812.
