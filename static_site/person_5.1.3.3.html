JOSEPH FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3) JOSEPH FERRIS born March 29, 1735 Greenwich and died there May 27, 1810; served as a Private in the 5th Company, 4th Regiment under the command of Captain David Waterbury, in the expedition against Canada in the French-Indian Wars 1756; he also served in the Rev. War in Captain George Peck's Company; married 1st Ruth Peck (3.10.2.6); and 2d Mary (Ferris) Austin (5.1.2.2). Born to Joseph and Ruth were:

Children (4)

  • SOLOMON FERRIS 5.1.3.3.1
  • ANDREW FERRIS 5.1.3.3.2
  • CLARA FERRIS was baptized April 11, 1764 St. Johns Episcopal Church, Stamford; 5.1.3.3.3
  • RUTH FERRIS 5.1.3.3.4
