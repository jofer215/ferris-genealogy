ROBERT MORRIS WHITING

ID: 3.6.1.4.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.1.4.4) ROBERT MORRIS WHITING born April 15, 1783 and died March 2, 1874; married January 26, 1820 Margaret Murray (1800-1888); Margaret was a sister of Helen Murray. Both buried First Congregational Church Cemetery, Old Greenwich CT – Plot 17, graves 11 and 12; the Church records say that Robert was born 1794 and have Margaret interned 1889; and bborn to them were:
