EDWARD WILLIAM HARRIS

ID: 1.2.3.3.4.3.9 | Source: John
(1.2.3.3.4.3.9) EDWARD WILLIAM HARRIS (Photo) born 1832 London, Ontario (March 16, 1832 Port Ryerse, Norfolk, Ontario) and died October 1, 1925 London, Ontario; also a lawyer and in 1857 became partners with his elder brother; he also took a great interest in Port Ryerse and owned a mill and a brick yard there; he also suffered from deafness and went to England for medical attention at least once; 1881 was a tinsmith in Toronto; married July 7, 1860 in Port Ryerse, Sophia Howard Ryerson [dau of Adolphus Egerton & Mary (Armstrong) Ryerson] born December 29, 1836 Kingston, Frontenanc, Ontario and died March 20, 1898 Toronto; 1881 resided Sunningham, England

No children recorded
