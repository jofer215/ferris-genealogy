JOHN WASHINGTON FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.2.4.7 | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.4.7) JOHN WASHINGTON FERRIS born May 11, 1810 CT and died June 2, 1867 Fairfield, Franklin, IN; married 1st October 2, 1835 Elizabeth Brookbank (Broadbank?) who apparently died before 1843; and 2d in Franklin Co IN, Mrs. Susannah T. (Sarah) (Adiar) Barrickman born August 3, 1816 and died December 25, 1852; and 3d June 8, 1866 Ellanor (Eleanor) E. Pumphrey born March 19, 1819; and born to John and Elizabeth was:

Children (6)

  • THOMAS D. FERRIS 5.1.3.2.4.7.1
  • SUSANNAH FERRIS 5.1.3.2.4.7.3
  • JOHN WASHINGTON FERRIS, JR., 5.1.3.2.4.7.4
  • HANNAH FERRIS 5.1.3.2.4.7.5
  • SARAH FERRIS 5.1.3.2.4.7.6
  • ELEANOR E. FERRIS died infancy; buried Sims Cemetery, Fairfield CT 5.1.3.2.4.7.7
