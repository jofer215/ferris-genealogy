JOHN FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.4.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.4.1) JOHN FERRIS (Captain) (tombstone photo) born January 15, 1819 Greenwich and died there April 26, 1904; married December 5, 1838 Mary E. Husted (2.4.4.1.3.1.1); John was Captain of sailing vessels Pierson and Caroline Peck that ran between Connecticut and Long Island shore points and New York City.
