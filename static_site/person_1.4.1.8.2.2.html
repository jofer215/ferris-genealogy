ELIZABETH FRANCES RICHARDSON

ID: 1.4.1.8.2.2 | Source: John
(1.4.1.8.2.2) ELIZABETH FRANCES RICHARDSON born 1818 and died November 3, 1883; married March 1, 1842 Dr. Charles Eugene Crowell (See Appleton's Encyclopedia for 1895); he died October 29, 1895, age 77 years. Born them were:

Children (9)

  • EUGENE WHARTON CROWELL; died as an infant. 1.4.1.8.2.2.1
  • JOHN FRANCIS CROWELL; died as an infant. 1.4.1.8.2.2.2
  • CHARLES EDWARD CROWELL 1.4.1.8.2.2.3
  • CAROLINE FRANCES CROWELL; died as an infant. 1.4.1.8.2.2.4
  • FRANK CROWELL; B.S., NYU; LL.B. Columbia; married Caroline 1.4.1.8.2.2.5
  • HARRY CROWELL; died as an infant. 1.4.1.8.2.2.6
  • EMMA THERESA CROWELL 1.4.1.8.2.2.7
  • CLARENCE CROWELL died as an infant 1.4.1.8.2.2.8
  • EDGAR FERRIS CROWELL died as an infant 1.4.1.8.2.2.9
