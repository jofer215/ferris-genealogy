CATHERINE FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.1.1.3 | Source: John
(1.4.7.1.1.3) CATHERINE FERRIS (Photo) born November 18, 1818 North Castle NY and died January 1, 1916 Stamford CT; buried there Roxbury Cemetery; married c1838 Charles Caldwell [son of David & Rachel] born October 11, 1816 Bedford NY and died February 18, 1897 Stamford CT; buried there Roxbury Cemetery. Born to them were:

Children (8)

  • RACHEL CALDWELL died before 1897; married a Mr. Merritt and born 1.4.7.1.1.3.1
  • CHARLES W. CALDWELL 1.4.7.1.1.3.2
  • JOHN W. CALDWELL 1.4.7.1.1.3.3
  • SARAH ELLEN CALDWELL 1.4.7.1.1.3.4
  • ABNER GEORGE CALDWELL 1.4.7.1.1.3.5
  • MARY I. CALDWELL 1.4.7.1.1.3.6
