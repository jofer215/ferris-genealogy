CHARLES EDWIN ARTER

ID: 1.2.6.2.1.1.5.2.9 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.1.1.5.2.9) CHARLES EDWIN ARTER born April 23, 1884 Zanesville OH and died May 2, 1972 Ashland OH; he was a coal miner by profession; member of Board of Education at Adamsville OH; served as Tax Assessor for Salem Township; married 1st December 3, 1903 in Los Angeles CA, his 1st cousin, Margaret Ellen Nosker [dau of Edwin L & Caroline Matilda (Ragor) Nosker] born March 6, 1880 Zanesville and died there April 10, 1910; buried Greenwood Cemetery, Zanesville; 2d April 25, 1914 in Zanesville, Fanny Mabel Janney [dau of Robert T & Mary Elizabeth (Francis) Janney] born October 16, 1887 Muskigum Co OH and died January 21, 1947 Zanesville; buried New Hope, Adamsville OH; and born to him and Fanny were:

Children (4)

  • LILLIAN RUTH ARTER 1.2.6.2.1.1.5.2.9.1
  • MARY GRACE ARTER 1.2.6.2.1.1.5.2.9.2
  • TREASURE LUCILLE ARTER 1.2.6.2.1.1.5.2.9.3
  • VIRGINIA ANN ARTER 1.2.6.2.1.1.5.2.9.4
