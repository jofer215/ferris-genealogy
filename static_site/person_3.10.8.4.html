ENOS PECK

ID: 3.10.8.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.8.4) ENOS PECK (P12X-DW) born 1752 Greenwich CT and died May 23, 1835 Camillus, Onondaga, NY; buried there; served as a Private with the Vermont forces during the Rev War; married 1st c1774 in New Haven VT, Anna (Annis) Marsh (P12V-G0) (c1755-); 2d Lavina Wilcox; moved from New Milford CT to New Haven CT and to Onondaga Co NY.
