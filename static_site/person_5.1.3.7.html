MARY FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.7 | Source: James
(5.1.3.7) MARY FERRIS born February 28, 1742/3 Greenwich CT and died there c1820; married c1764 Captain George Peck (3.10.5.11) {Commander of his Company, in the 9th Connecticut Regiment; Revolutionary War Veteran.}. See George for offspring.

No children recorded
