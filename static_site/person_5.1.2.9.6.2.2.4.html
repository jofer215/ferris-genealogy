LOUIS ARMSTRONG BARMORE

ID: 5.1.2.9.6.2.2.4 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.6.2.2.4) LOUIS ARMSTRONG BARMORE born c1878 and died August 1, 1918; lived in Peekskill nearly all of his life – resided at Walnut Street with his wife, Henrietta (Lane); employed by Henry Armstrong, coal dealer. He and five others lost their lives fighting a warehouse fire at the Fleischmann Plant. An article on the ‘Net states that 7 firemen lost their lives fighting a disastrous fire at the Fleischmann Plant at Charles Point; and a Firemen Memorial Wing was built 1919, added to the Peekskill Hospital, now the Hudson Valley Hospital Center; and born to them was:
