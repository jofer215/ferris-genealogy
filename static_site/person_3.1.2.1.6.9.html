JOSHUA FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.1.6.9 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.6.9) JOSHUA FERRIS (twin) born May 8, 1798 Harpersfield NY and died August 23, 1880 Delaware Co OH; married 1st Elizabeth Bean born March 1, 1795 VA and died December 24, 1833; and 2d Anna L. Hoger or Hazen born March 9, 1795 Nova Scotia and died December 11, 1878; all buried Millcreek Cemetery, Ostrander, Delaware, OH (Photos); Joshua went to Ohio with his brother Joseph 1818; the personal property tax list of 1826 shows he bought land in Scioto Township; an application was signed by John Fields, M.D., H.B. Felkner and Henry C. Ferris, February 28, 1879 stating that Joshua had been insane for more than two years; and born to Joshua and Elizabeth were:
