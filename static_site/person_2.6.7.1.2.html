HANNAH WATERBURY

ID: 2.6.7.1.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.1.2) HANNAH WATERBURY born December 29, 1756 Stamford and died there May 5, 1819; married May 17, 1772 in Stamford, Joseph Wilmot [son of Francis & Charity (Ketchum) Wilmot] born December 4, 1745 Greenwich and died December 6, 1827; both buried Noroton River Cemetery, Darien CT; Rev.
