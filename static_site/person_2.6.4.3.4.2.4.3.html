EMILY LOUISE BOSTWICK

ID: 2.6.4.3.4.2.4.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.4.3.4.2.4.3) EMILY LOUISE BOSTWICK born September 17, 1850 Stamford and died there May 19, 1913; buried Simsbury Cemetery (tombstone photo); known for her work in church, patriotic, and social services societies, and counted among her acquaintenances Presidents Taft, McKinley and Theodore Roosevelt; married April 4, 1874 in Stamford, Edwin Ruthven Fay born 1826 Rutland VT

No children recorded
