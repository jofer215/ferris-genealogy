SUSAN JANE COLE

ID: 5.1.2.9.9.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.9.1.1) SUSAN JANE COLE born April 9, 1838 and died February 5, 1911; married April 23, 1861, Rev. Thomas Stockton Lent [son of Benson Oakley & Julia Ann (Gilbert) Lent] born November 23, 1839 and died January 25, 1913; both buried Hillside Cemetery, Peekskill NY. He was ordained a minister in the Methodist Episcopal Church in 1861, but withdrew in 1893 and was confirmed in the Protestant Episcopal denomination at the “Field Home” in Yorktown NY, July 7, 1893 by Bishop Seymour of Illinois.
