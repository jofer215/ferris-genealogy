FREDERICK AUGUSTUS UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.6.2.4.2 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.4.2) FREDERICK AUGUSTUS UNDERHILL born December 23, 1817 and died 1897; adopted by William & Abigail (Bigelow) King; married July 1, 1846 in Freeport IL, Elizabeth Schofield [dau of George Wheeler & Miria (Demot) Schofield] born February 17, 1830 Chatauqua Co NY and died December 2, 1908 Wayne WI; buried there Hoffman Cemetery; and born to them were: (1.2.6.2.4.2.1) HORATIO KING UNDERHILL born April 15, 1846/47 and died September 3, 1912; buried Hoffman Cemetery, South Wayne WI

Children (4)

  • HORACE YOUNG UNDERHILL 1.2.6.2.4.2.2
  • WILLIAM WALLACE UNDERHILL 1.2.6.2.4.2.3
  • FREDERICK HOMER UNDERHILL 1.2.6.2.4.2.4
  • ABBY MARIA UNDERHILL 1.2.6.2.4.2.5
