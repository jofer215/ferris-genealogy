MARY A. AUSTIN

ID: 5.1.2.9.6.2.2.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.6.2.2.1.1) MARY A. AUSTIN born April 26, 1899 Putnam Valley and died September 12, 1986 at Chandler Hall, a nursing home in Newtown PA; married Jessie W. Barger (5.1.2.9.2.4.1.3); both buried at Hillside Cemetery. Mary spent her childhood Putnam Valley before moving to Shrub Oak where she was a long time resident. She was a graduate of Drum Hill High School in Peekskill and the Eastman Business School of Poughkeepsie. She was a member of the Shrub Oak Methodist Church and was a former member of the Shrub Oak Garden Club. She moved to New Hope PA in 1983 to live with her niece, Lois (Mrs. Robert) Wright - apparently no offspring

No children recorded
