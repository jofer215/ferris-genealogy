SAMUEL FERRIS SCOFIELD

ID: 5.1.3.6.4.2.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.6.4.2.2) SAMUEL FERRIS SCOFIELD born November 11, 1839 Stamford and died there October 20, 1913; farmer 1872-74; married September 28, 1871 Frances Elizabeth Hoyt [daughter of Ira Ford & Mary Pell (Wixon) Hoyt] born August 7, 1846 South Norwalk CT and died February 1, 1931 Stamford; both buried Woodland Cemetery, Stamford; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • MARY WIXON SCOFIELD 5.1.3.6.4.2.2.1
  • SUSAN EMILY SCOFIELD 5.1.3.6.4.2.2.2
  • ADA IRENE SCOFIELD 5.1.3.6.4.2.2.3
  • FRANCES H SCOFIELD 5.1.3.6.4.2.2.4
