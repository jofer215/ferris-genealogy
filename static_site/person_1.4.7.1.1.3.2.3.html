INA MAE CALDWELL

ID: 1.4.7.1.1.3.2.3 | Source: John
(1.4.7.1.1.3.2.3) INA MAE CALDWELL (Photos) born September 20, 1865 Stamford CT and died there January 5, 1916; buried there Long Ridge Union; married July 14 at Poundridge NY, Frank Haddon Haviland [brother of Henry A. below] [son of William B] born May 10, 1861 Mamaroneck NY and died January 2, 1919 Stamford; buried there Long Ridge Union.
