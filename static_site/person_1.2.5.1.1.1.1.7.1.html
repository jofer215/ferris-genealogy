JUANITA JANE HOVEY

ID: 1.2.5.1.1.1.1.7.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.1.1.1.7.1) JUANITA JANE HOVEY born December 1, 1904 White Church KS and died July 12, 1987 Lawrence KS; married January 3, 1923 at Kansas City KS, Frederick William Chubb born September 28, 1895 at The Lodge, Eggbuckland, Devon, England and died March 17, 1934 Kansas City KS. Fred came to the U. S. with his family from South Hampton on the boat St. Paul; they arrived in New York, July 30, 1906. He was naturalized, June 7, 1918 and served in the U. S. Army in WWI. After Fred’s death, Juanita married, second, George Leimkuhler who died, 1957, Albuquerque NM; and third, April 7, 1968 at the 1st Southern Baptist Church, Salina KS, Millard E. Sullivan born October 29, 1891 and died February 28, 1972, Lincoln KS. Born to Juanita and Fred were:
