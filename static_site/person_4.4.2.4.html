SOLOMON LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.2.4 | Source: Mary
(4.4.2.4) SOLOMON LOCKWOOD (#302) born c1757 Greenwich and died between 1803-12. He went West, as it was called then, and settled on the Kayaderosseras Creek, near the village of Ballston Spa, Saratoga County NY, where he died; married 1st a Quaker lady, surname Hull, in New York City, who had brothers named John and Oliver, wealthy druggists; married 2d widow Sally Lockwood, whose first husband was Captain Oliver Lockwood, a successful West India sea captain (is this (4.2.5.1.1))?; and born to Solomon and presumably his first wife, were:
