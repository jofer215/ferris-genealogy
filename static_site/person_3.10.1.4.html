RUTH PECK

ID: 3.10.1.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.4) RUTH PECK (GJ59-K8) born in January 1724, Greenwich and died there September 3/5, 1807; married November 25, 1742 Nehemiah Haight (#2724) [son of Samuel and Mary Hait][Chaplain Ferris lists the name as Haight, but the authority on this surname spells it Hait, which is what I will do.] born April 13, 1718 and died 1785. Nehemiah sold land in Stamford in 1741, 1742, 1743, and 1744. They resided in Stanwich Society in the town of Greenwich. Born to them were:
