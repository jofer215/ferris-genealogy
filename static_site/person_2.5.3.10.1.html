HANNAH SMITH

ID: 2.5.3.10.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.10.1) HANNAH SMITH (Hannah Hoyt Smith) born February 8, 1738/39 Stamford and died July 16, 1823 Greenfield, Saratoga, NY; married March 8, 1756 in Stamford, (Isaac Pennoyer Weed) Isaac Weed [son of Benjamin & Mary (Pennoyer) Weed] born September 10, 1732 Stamford and died October 29, 1803 Greenfield; both buried Cornell Cemetery, North Milton NY (Isaac was the son of Hannah’s step-parents, so Isaac was her step-brother, right?); and born to them were:
