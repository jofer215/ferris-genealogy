MARY ELIZABETH SCOFIELD

ID: 2.1.5.6.1.2.1 | Source: Peter
(2.1.5.6.1.2.1) MARY ELIZABETH SCOFIELD born August 30, 1810 Stamford and died there September 28, 1890; buried (Northfield) North Street Cemetery; unmarried; in 1860 and 1880 living with her brother John and his family (2.1.5.6.1.2.2) JOHN RALSEY SCOFIELD born May 21, 1812 Stamford and died there May 30, 1888; farmer 1850; married September 13, 1854 in Stamford, Sarah Ann Hoyt (2.5.2.4.5.5.5); both buried (Northfield) North Street Cemetery, Stamford; and born to them were:

No children recorded
