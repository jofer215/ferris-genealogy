ANNA ALLISON HAVILAND

ID: 1.2.5.4.3.6.5.1.2 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.3.6.5.1.2) ANNA ALLISON HAVILAND (1880-1971) married 1903 Samuel Northrup Castle – presume this is the Samuel Northrup Castle born February 6, 1880 Honolulu HI and died 1959; his grandfather, of the same, wsa the founder of Castle & Cooke (see ‘Net); during his heyday in the mid-1900s, Samuel was considered one of Hawaii’s top engineers and industrialist; received an engineering degree from Harvard 1901 and did graduate work at Cornell for 2y; once out of school, he worked for some major U.S. companies, including Westinghouse and GE; he was director of the Honolulu Advertiser, Halemano Co, and Kohala Sugar Co; if this is the correct guy, and I think it is, born to them were:
