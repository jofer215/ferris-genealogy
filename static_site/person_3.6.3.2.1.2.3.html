SARAH AMANDA KNAPP

ID: 3.6.3.2.1.2.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.1.2.3) SARAH AMANDA KNAPP born July 19, 1822 and died January 11, 1903, at 80 years of age; interred Bethel Baptish Church Cemetery, East Fishkill NY; married 1854 James I. (Zenas) Knapp [son of William X. & Elizabeth (Organ) Knapp] born August 12/18/19, 1828 Putnam Co NY and died January 18, 1892 at 63 years of age; interred Bethel Baptist Church Cemetery, East Fishkill.
