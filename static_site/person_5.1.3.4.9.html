MARY FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.9 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.9) MARY FERRIS born February 6, 1782 Greenwich CT and died 1845; married 1st December 29, 1799 Solomon Peck born March 15, 1768 and died June 2, 1816 Greenwich [son of Joseph and Elizabeth (Peck) Peck]; married 2d Timothy Wilcox (1787-1862); and born to Mary and Solomon were: (5.1.3.4.9.1) SOLOMON PECK born December 31, 1802 Greenwich and died there May 1861; married July 15, 1829 Caroline Selleck (3.3.1.5.9); and born to them were:
