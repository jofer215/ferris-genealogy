BENJAMIN CLASON

ID: 2.1.3.3.1.4.3 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.1.4.3) BENJAMIN CLASON born January 5, 1806 Stamford CT (Orange Co NY) and died October 30, 1874 Clason Prairie (Beaver Dam) WI; buried East Beaver Dam Cemetery, Clason Prairie WI; married c1826 (January 1, 1829) in Steuben Co NY, Cornelia Maria Acker born c1806 (December 4, 1811 Scotuck NY) Rensselaer Co NY and died February 3, 1887 Clason Prairie (Waukeska Co) WI; she is buried Beaver Dam City Cemetery; and born to them were: (2.1.3.3.1.4.3.1) ABRAHAM CLASON born September 16, 1829 Steuben Co NY; married 1st November 2, 1850 Ann Eliza Bass (-1864); 2d November 17, 1864 Olive Brigham; they resided Rochester MN; and born to him and Ann were:
