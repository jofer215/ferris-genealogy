JOSHUA SUTTON

ID: 1.2.5.1.10.2 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.10.2) JOSHUA SUTTON born November 21, 1793 New Castle NY and died January 17, 1880; Quaker; resided York NY 1810; moved from Chappaqua NY to NYC 1816; moved from Purchase NY to NYC 1828; from NYC to Philadelphia PA 1832; from NYC to Purchase NY 1835; to NYC 1855; married October 17, 1827 in Harrison, Westchester, NY Phebe Burling (1.7.7.12.2); and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • JOSHUA BURLING SUTTON 1.2.5.1.10.2.1
  • SAMUEL S. SUTTON 1.2.5.1.10.2.2
  • ALBERT SUTTON 1.2.5.1.10.2.3
