RICHARD BOLLES PADDOCK, Captain,

ID: 1.4.7.7.3.2.2 | Source: John
(1.4.7.7.3.2.2) RICHARD BOLLES PADDOCK, Captain, born December 2, 1859 Princeton IL and died March 9, 1901 Peking, China, probably as a member of the U.S. China Relief Expedition involving the Boxer Rebellion; married June 5, 1890 in Chicago, Grace Pershing (sister of General “Black Jack”) born March 29, 1869 and died April 25, 1904; and born to them were:
