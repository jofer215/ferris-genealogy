DAVID HOLLY BISHOP

ID: 2.3.3.3.2 | Source: Peter
(2.3.3.3.2) DAVID HOLLY BISHOP born June 22, 1771 Stamford and died there September 25, 1801; married January 21, 1799 in Stamford, Hannah Bishop [dau of Silas & Sarah (Holmes) Bishop] born October 19, 1772 Stamford and died there February 10, 1845; buried North Street Cemetery; after David’s death, Hannah married Joseph P. Cook; and born to them were:
