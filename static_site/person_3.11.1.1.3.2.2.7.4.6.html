PERRY FLOYD NINK

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.4.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.4.6) PERRY FLOYD NINK born November 18, 1919 Leelanau Co MI and died October 9, 1987 Traverse City MI; worked at Traverse City Regional Psychiatric Hospital as an attendant for 19y; USAAF WWII Veteran; married 1st July 18, 1942 in Saginaw, Betty Jayne Yancer [dau of Jesse V & Mary E (Doyle) Yancer] born May 16, 1923 Saginaw MI and died August 18, 1968 Traverse City MI; both buried there Oakwood Cemetery; married 2d in October 3, 1970 in Traverse City, (Louise) Elizabeth (Strauss) Couturier; and born to Perry and Betty were 5 kids

No children recorded
