KENNETH LEE GELLHAUS

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.10.6.1.1.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.10.6.1.1.1) KENNETH LEE GELLHAUS born October 14, 1961 Philadelphia PA; a 1983 graduate of the School of Management at the State U., of New York at Binghamton and received his J.D. degree cum laude from Albany Law School of Union U., 1986. He was executive editor of the Albany Law Review, 1985-86; admitted to the New York Bar, 1987. He has worked for the law firm of McNamee, Lochner, Titus, and Williams in Albany since 1988 and was named a partner in 1995; resides Waterford NY; married 1998 Michelle Fobare born November 7, 1960 Troy NY

No children recorded
