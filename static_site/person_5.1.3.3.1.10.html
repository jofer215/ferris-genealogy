ANDREW FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.10 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.10) ANDREW FERRIS born Connecticut (probably second born) and died July 25, 1862 age 82y 1m 2d according to his tombstone; buried Harrington (old) Cemetery, Elmore, Ottawa Co OH; married in Hamilton Co OH, Desire Larned [dau of Ezekial & Desire (Hall) Larned] and they moved to Clark Co OH sometime prior to 1829; Desire died July 23, 1832 and was buried in Clark Co OH. Andrew moved from Connecticut to Hamilton Co OH when a young man and there he purchased land, Section 14-Township 3, Range 1; then moved to Greene Co OH, where he bought land in 1814, NW/4 Section 17, Township 6, Range 8; this land was that part of Greene County that was later made part of Clark County. He moved from Clark County with eight of his children to Ottawa County in about 1836. Born to Andrew and Desire were:
