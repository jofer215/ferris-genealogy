MARY ELIZABETH FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.4.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.4.4) MARY ELIZABETH FERRIS born July 27, 1823 Greenwich; married William Henry Peck (3.10.2.13.2.3); Mary acquired her father's home, which was built on the original Jeffrey Ferris plot in Old Greenwich and which was known as "Keofferam Lodge", built by Nathaniel Ferris; see him for offspring

No children recorded
