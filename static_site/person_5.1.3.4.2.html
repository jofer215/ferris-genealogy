JEDUTHAN FERRIS JR.,

ID: 5.1.3.4.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.2) JEDUTHAN FERRIS JR., born May 3, 1764 Greenwich CT and died there April 9, 1831; buried Old Sound Beach Cemetery (tombstone photo); resided at Hilltop Farm, Greenwich; will dated March 25, 1831, probated April 25, 1831, mentioned his wife Mary Lockwood and children Isaac, Joseph, and Mary; married Mary Lockwood Ferris (5.1.3.6.2); and born to them were: (5.1.3.4.2.1) ISAAC FERRIS (tombstone photo) born January 28, 1803 and died September 28, 1881; married Mary {maiden name unk} born April 6, 1801 and died April 8, 1860; both buried Ferris Plot, at the cemetery at First Congregational Church, Old Greenwich
