ABIGAIL BELL

ID: 5.2.4.7 | Source: James
(5.2.4.7) ABIGAIL BELL born November 2, 1748 Stamford and died there August 31, 1782; married June 17, 1773 in Stamford, Samuel Scofield [son of Samuel & Elizabeth (Ambler) Scofield] born December 26, 1744 Stamford and died there December 31, 1831; buried there North Stamford Cemetery; Samuel remarried shortly after Abigail’s death. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • ELIJAH SCOFIELD 5.2.4.7.1
  • CATHERINE SCOFIELD 5.2.4.7.2
  • POLLY SCOFIELD 5.2.4.7.3
