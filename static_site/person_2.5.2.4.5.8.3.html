WILLIAM WALLACE HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.4.5.8.3 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.5.8.3) WILLIAM WALLACE HOYT (#4551) born June 19, 1818 Stamford and died there April 27, 1890; buried North Street Cemetery; 1850 coaster; Sea Captain 1860-90; married March 31, 1844 in Stamford, Sarah Frances Scofield (5.1.1.3.5.3.1) and in 1871, they were living in Stamford, near the depot. Born to them were:

Children (6)

  • FRANCES ELMORE HOYT 2.5.2.4.5.8.3.1
  • WILLIAM FILMORE HOYT 2.5.2.4.5.8.3.2
  • CHARLES EDWARD HOYT 2.5.2.4.5.8.3.3
  • GEORGE HENRY HOYT 2.5.2.4.5.8.3.4
  • GEORGE WALLACE HOYT 2.5.2.4.5.8.3.5
  • JENNIE ATWOOD HOYT 2.5.2.4.5.8.3.6
