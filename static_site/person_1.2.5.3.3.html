JOSHUA UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.3.3 | Source: John
(1.2.5.3.3) JOSHUA UNDERHILL born July 9, 1765 Westchester Co NY and died February 14, 1839 NYC; buried Woodlawn Cemetry, Brooklyn. He was a Quaker and a flour merchant. He married, by Friends Ceremony, November 19, 1789 Mary Sutton [dau of Richardson & Elizabeth (Quimby) Sutton] born March 7, 1767, Cortland Manor (Somers) NY and died July 12, 1820.
