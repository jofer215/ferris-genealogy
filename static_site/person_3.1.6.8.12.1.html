WILLIAM ROSE

ID: 3.1.6.8.12.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.12.1) WILLIAM ROSE born c1818 (Spencervile) Grenville Co, Ontario and died after 1891; a farmer; married 1st October 22, 1839 in Spencerville, Grenville Co, Ann Bennett [dau of William and Margaret (?) Bennett] born c1814 Cork Co, Ireland and died 1871; buried Haley Station, Renfrew Co, Ontario; married 2d 1875 in Ross Township, Renfrew Co, Priscilla Barber born September 13, 1831 Wolford Township, Grenville Co and died August 31, 1912 Haley Station, Renfrew Co; and born to William and Ann were:
