NOAH WILCOX HOYT

ID: 2.5.3.4.1.13.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.4.1.13.2) NOAH WILCOX HOYT (#4665) born January 3, 1817 Stamford and was living in New Canaan CT 1871 where he was Postmaster; a tailor and formerly resided in New York City; married July 4, 1842 Cornelia Bennet [dau of Robert & Mary] born 1819 Middletown, Monmouth, NJ; and born to them were:
