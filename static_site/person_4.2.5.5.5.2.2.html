JAMES W LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.2.5.5.5.2.2 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.5.5.2.2) JAMES W LOCKWOOD born c1841 Shandaken, Ulster, NY and died June 1, 1863 Brook Station VA – Civil War (?) – could be the James W in Company I of 80th New York Infantry Regiment – the 80th was in Virginia in June 1863 until ordered to Gettysburg.

No children recorded
