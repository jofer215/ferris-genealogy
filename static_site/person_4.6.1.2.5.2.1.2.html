HIRAM SLAUSON BROWN

ID: 4.6.1.2.5.2.1.2 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.5.2.1.2) HIRAM SLAUSON BROWN born March 13, 1834 Pound Ridge, Westchester, NY and died January 11, 1904 Norwalk CT; buried there Riverside Cemetery; a farmer and Justice of Peace; married 1st March 29, 1854 Sally Maria Scofield born November 26, 1833 and died February 22, 1856 Pound Ridge; buried there; married 2d June 16, 1858 in Pound Ridge, Sarah Jane Fancher [dau of Aaron Beers & Harriet (Scofield) Fancher] born February 26, 1840 and died 1921. Born to him and Sarah were:

Children (2)

  • HERBERT STANLEY BROWN 4.6.1.2.5.2.1.2.2
