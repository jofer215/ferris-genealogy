AUGUSTUS L. PECK

ID: 3.10.1.3.7.2.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.3.7.2.1) AUGUSTUS L. PECK born September 18, 1816 Sharon CT; was living there 1877; married January 6, 1841 Mary Parsons (Mary Louisa Pierson born December 11, 1812 and died September 6, 1888; buried Ellsworth CT) [dau of Freeman Washington & Mary (Skiff) Pierson]
