LODUSKY NEWMAN

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.8 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.8) LODUSKY NEWMAN (Dussie) (Photo) born October 19, 1861 NY and died September 6, 1935 Elmwood Township, Leelanau Co MI; married Joseph W (or P) Clark [son of Hezekiah & Mary (Courtney) Clark] born about October 19, 1845 Highland Township, Defiance Co OH and died January 11, 1923 Elmwood Township, Leelanau Co MI; both buried Oakwood Cemetery, Traverse City; millhand, farmer; brickmaker; they had some foster children

No children recorded
