DARRELL EDWARD EMERY

ID: 3.6.3.2.2.2.2.6.1.10.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.2.2.2.6.1.10.1) DARRELL EDWARD EMERY born April 19, 1940 Binghamton, Broome, NY and died May 7, 2006; married 1st November 26, 1960 in Binghamton, Janice Marie Hektor [dau of Karl William & Mary (Kubik) Hektor] born April 20, 1942 Johnson City, Broome, NY – moved to Elmhurst IL and he worked at Argonne - they divorced 1981 KY – where Janice resides, an artist; married 2d June 16, 1996 at the Broadmoore Hotel, Colorado Springs CO, Karen Aho born February 18, 1953 Berea OH.
