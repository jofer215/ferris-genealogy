VALENTINE SEAMAN FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.4.1.5 | Source: John
(1.4.7.4.1.5) VALENTINE SEAMAN FERRIS born March 4, 1841 Flushing, Queens, NY and died 1861; partially educated at Flushing and afterward in Brooklyn; early life he was a clerk, then engaged in the lumber business on his father’s land in Hamilton County for 12 years, afterward in Herkimer County for 11 years; married December 28, 1868 Amela (or Alma J.) Miller [dau of Nicholas & Angeline (Barker) Miller] of Herkimer Co who died in 1846; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • JESSE S FERRIS 1.4.7.4.1.5.1
  • ANN AUGUSTUS FERRIS 1.4.7.4.1.5.2
  • EUGENE B. FERRIS; a farmer 1.4.7.4.1.5.3
