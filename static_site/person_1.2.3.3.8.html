ISRAEL UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.3.8 | Source: John
(1.2.3.3.8) ISRAEL UNDERHILL born before 1775 Westchester Co NY and died 1811/15; Ensign 3d Regiment NY Milita 1778; 1790 resided Westchester Co NY; 1800 resided Hurley, Ulster, NY; 1810 resided Newburg, Orange, NY; married Elizabeth Garrison and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • ELIZABETH UNDERHILL 1.2.3.3.8.1
  • GARRISON UNDERHILL 1.2.3.3.8.2
  • ISRAEL UNDERHILL 1.2.3.3.8.3
  • BARTOW UNDERHILL 1.2.3.3.8.4
  • ROBERT UNDERHILL 1.2.3.3.8.5
