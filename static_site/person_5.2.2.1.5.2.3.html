JULIA A. BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.1.5.2.3 | Source: James
(5.2.2.1.5.2.3) JULIA A. BUXTON born December 18, 1818 New Canaan CT and died there August 13, 1858; buried Lakeview Cemetery, New Canaan; married February 10, 1839 New Canaan, Charles Jones [son of Thomas & Deborah Ann] born July 20, 1815 Dantown, Stamford CT and died April 24, 1900 New Canaan; buried Lakeview Cemetery. Charles remarried after Julia’s death and had another kid. Born to Julia and Charles were:

Children (2)

  • MARY JONES 5.2.2.1.5.2.3.2
