HARRIS SCOFIELD

ID: 2.5.3.2.11.5 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.2.11.5) HARRIS SCOFIELD born March 17, 1794 Stamford and died there January 30, 1870; buried there Newfield Cemetery; married 1st March 2, 1819 in Stamford, Lydia Ambler Weed [dau of Jonathan & Lydia (Ambler) Weed] born June 3, 1785 Stamford and died there February 9, 1840; buried there with Harris; married 2d March 1843 in Stamford, Eliza Husted born April 6, 1807 Stamford and died there December 16, 1889; buried Newfield Cemtery; and born to him and Lydia were:

Children (5)

  • ELIZABETH AMBLER SCOFIELD 2.5.3.2.11.5.1
  • JULIA WEED SCOFIELD 2.5.3.2.11.5.3
  • CAROLINE MARIA SCOFIELD 2.5.3.2.11.5.4
  • CHARLES HARRIS SCOFIELD 2.5.3.2.11.5.5
  • SARAH E SCOFIELD 2.5.3.2.11.5.6
