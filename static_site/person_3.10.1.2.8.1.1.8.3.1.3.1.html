ROBERT ANDERSON GRANSBURY

ID: 3.10.1.2.8.1.1.8.3.1.3.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.8.1.1.8.3.1.3.1) ROBERT ANDERSON GRANSBURY (Robert Dean Gransbury) born April 5, 1954 Burley, Cassia, ID and died February 15, 2005 WA; “Bob Dean ‘Cowboy’ Gransbury attended schools in Twin Falls ID; started his sales career at Kenny Shoes; promoted to manager; as a manager transferred to California; moved back to Twin Falls 1980 where he began his career in car sales at Theison Motors; 1987 he relocated to Olympia WA continuing as a sales manager in the car business; resided in Lacey WA – he may have been adopted, but will continue; and born to him and his unnamed wife were:

Children (3)
