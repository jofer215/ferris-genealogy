SARAH M WEED

ID: 2.5.3.10.1.4.6.3 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.10.1.4.6.3) SARAH M WEED born June 1828 Greenfield, Saratoga, NY and died 1914 Jackson Co IA; buried Mount Hope Cemetery, Maquoketa; married December 13, 1848 Daniel S Haight [son of Stephen] born June 22, 1821 Plattekill, Ulster, NY; two terms as Mayor of Maquoketa; Township Clerk for many years; City Assessor; and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • GEORGE S HAIGHT 2.5.3.10.1.4.6.3.1
  • MARY E HAIGHT 2.5.3.10.1.4.6.3.2
  • EDWIN W HAIGHT 2.5.3.10.1.4.6.3.3
  • SARAH A HAIGHT 2.5.3.10.1.4.6.3.4
  • CHARLES H HAIGHT 2.5.3.10.1.4.6.3.5
