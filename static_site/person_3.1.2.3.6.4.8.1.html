LUCILLE SABRA FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.3.6.4.8.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.3.6.4.8.1) LUCILLE SABRA FERRIS (listed as Lucile Sabra Ferris in CA death records) born July 4, 1868 Kingsland MI and died February 11, 1944 Inglewood CA; took nurse training at Battle Creek Sanitarium and Hospital at the same as her future husband; married August 27, 1893 at Ionia MI, (Milton Millard Brayshaw) Millard M. Brayshaw (1863-1959) [son of Carlile & Mildred (Campbell) Brayshaw] and born to them were: (3.1.2.3.6.4.8.1.1) MAUDE LOIS BRAYSHAW born December 10, 1894 DuQuoin, Perry, IL and died December 4, 1988 San Bernardino Co CA; married Dr. H. Dale Wheeler and born to them were:
