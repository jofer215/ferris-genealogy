ELIZABETH LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.5 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5) ELIZABETH LOCKWOOD (#66) (NM7W-LJ) born May 15, 1708 Stamford CT; married c1728 Job Hoyt (Hait #2707) (NM7W-B5) (son of Joshua and Mary (Pickett) Hait) born January 22, 1703/04 Stamford and died October 13, 1754 North Castle. Job received land from his father near the head of Noroton River in Stamford and he bought and sold other land in the same locality. He was taxed in the New Canaan Parish, 1732; the church was formed in this Parish, 1733 and Elizabeth was admitted to the church, July 21, 1734 and Job, September 7, 1735. Their children were baptized there from 1736-45.
