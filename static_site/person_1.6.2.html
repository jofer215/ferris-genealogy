JOSIAH FERRIS

ID: 1.6.2 | Source: John
(1.6.2) JOSIAH FERRIS (1730-1795) born after his father’s death; died in New York City. Josiah’s father names this unborn child in his will. Josiah married, wife’s name is unknown [there was a Josiah Ferris married Letitia Van Alst, June 16, 1763 – don’t know if this is the same fella] and born to them were:
