PHEBE PECK

ID: 3.10.1.2.1.2.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.1.2.1) PHEBE PECK born August 17, 1796, Norwich NY and was living in Canton PA 1861; married c1821 as his 1st wife Abijah Dann [son of Seth & Jane (Munger) Dann] born November 25, 1796 probably North Norwich, Chenango, NY and died November 16, 1880 probably Abington Township, Lackawanna Co PA

No children recorded
