LEONA EMMALYN WILSON

ID: 1.2.6.2.1.1.2.11.7.2 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.1.1.2.11.7.2) LEONA EMMALYN WILSON born May 13, 1919 Fort Collins CO and died November 15, 1993; married 1st Walter Knapp who died March 1961 in an auto accident; 2d Guy Brown who died December 26, 1976 Denver CO and they had a child (1.2.6.2.1.1.2.11.7.3) ERNEST ALLEN WILSON (Photo) born April 16, 1921 Vernon, Yuma, CO and died November 7, 1986 Denver CO; founded Mobile Concrete Co in the early 1950s and then sold it and became a self-employed contractor until his retirement in 1982; married 1st July 4, 1941 in Littleton CO, Olive Louise Funk [dau of Elbert Peter & Olive Louise (Corlette) Funk] born February 8, 1925 Bloomington IN and died October 18, 1995 Midwest City OK – divorced 1946, she remarried; 2d 1948 in Gresham OH, Marjorie Louise Seamon born October 26, 1925 Casper, Natrona, WY and died May 11, 1986 Denver CO; and born to him and Olive were:

No children recorded
