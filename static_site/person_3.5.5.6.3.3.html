SYLVANUS MEAD

ID: 3.5.5.6.3.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.5.5.6.3.3) SYLVANUS MEAD born July 5, 1802 and died August 19, 1882; married October 31, 1839 Lucretia C. Barton born May 6, 1817 and died September 4, 1875. Born to them were: (3.5.5.6.3.3.1) PHILO S. MEAD born March 2, 1843; married May 24, 1876 Alverdo R. Minor, of Manhattan KS, born September 15, 1848
