HARRIET N. FERRIS died before 1848; married, December 31, 1844 in St. Joseph

ID: 3.1.2.3.1.3.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.3.1.3.3) HARRIET N. FERRIS died before 1848; married, December 31, 1844 in St. Joseph County IN, John Holston (John Holstein, Jr.) [son of John & Nancy (Baily) Holstein] born 1820 – after Harriet’s death, John married Rosanna Ketcham and had they had 1 child. Born to Harriet and John were:

Children (2)
