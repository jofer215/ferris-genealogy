ALBERT B. CHAPMAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.6.3.10 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.6.3.10) ALBERT B. CHAPMAN born March 29, 1862 at the old Chapman homestead, Putnam Valley NY and died September 1 (7), 1942 Peekskill; buried Hillside Cemetery; married April 13, 1893 Lillian Cromwell [dau of Alfred & Adaline (Gardner)] born c1852 (1859) NYC and died October 10, 1911; buried Oakwood Cemetery, Troy NY. As a young man he and his brother, Edmund worked on the Putnam Valley farm. The brothers operated the Chapman Lumber Company in Peekskill but sold that concern after several years. After the turn of the century, Albert engaged in various business activities. He bought a schooner and for a time, carried merchandise up and down the river. Later, he was employed for many years as a bookkeeper for the Wightman Roofing Company on Central Ave. Still later, he was bookkeeper for the Peekskill Daily Union. At the time of his death, he lived at Valentine boarding house on Main St. Apparently no offspring.

No children recorded
