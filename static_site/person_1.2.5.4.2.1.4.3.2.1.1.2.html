THEODORE LEANDER CHADEAYNE

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.4.3.2.1.1.2 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.4.3.2.1.1.2) THEODORE LEANDER CHADEAYNE (Photo) born November 18, 1968 Columbus OH; married October 30, 1999 at Baltimore MD, Karen Legler [dau of Glenn & Elizabeth]. Ted holds a bachelor’s degree in geology and a master’s degree in environmental engineering and is a geologist with the Maryland Department of the Environment. Karen holds a bachelor’s degree in England from the U. of Maryland, College Park, and a doctoral degree from the U. of Maryland, Baltimore; pursuing a nursing degree and hopes to work on issues of health and related law.

No children recorded
