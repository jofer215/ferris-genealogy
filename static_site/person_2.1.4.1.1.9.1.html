PHILO DORSEY FERRIS

ID: 2.1.4.1.1.9.1 | Source: Peter
(2.1.4.1.1.9.1) PHILO DORSEY FERRIS born November 20, 1825 Providence NY and died July 17, 1885 Hudson, Black Hawk, IA; married 1st October 12, 1847 Thankful (Hattie) M. Stevens (18081887) divorced and married 2 more times, names unk. In May 1850, Philo, Thankful and son, Jay, moved with his in-laws from Orleans County NY to Dane County WI, where his brother-in-law, Jesse Stevens had previously settled. By May 1876, he was separated from his wife and family and had moved to Cedar Falls IA, where he brother Orville resided. While there he remarried, twice. On one occasion he took his pistol, saying “will go out to the grove and shoot quail or prairie chicken” and later he was found shot dead, no one ever knowing of how it happened.
