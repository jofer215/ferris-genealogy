GIDEON CLINTON FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.6.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.3) GIDEON CLINTON FERRIS born September 9, 1828/29 Greenwich CT and died October 1, 1898 DeWitt NY; moved from Stamford to Dewitt NY 1855, where he owned and operated a farm of about one hundred acres [114 acres], given to him by his father, adjoining the farm of his brother Frederick; married April 17/19, 1852 Phebe Clock Selleck (2.6.5.3.4.5.2); Gideon filled the office of assessor, supervisor, inspector of the Onondaga County Penitentiary, president of the Farmer's Club, and was either trustee or clerk of his district for over thirty years; was also the president or secretary of the Dewitt Cemetery Association for many years.
