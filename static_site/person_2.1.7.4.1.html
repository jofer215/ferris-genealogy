JOHN THORP

ID: 2.1.7.4.1 | Source: Peter
(2.1.7.4.1) JOHN THORP born in 1774 at Bridgeport CT and died October 22, 1845 at Batavia NY; married September 26, 1804 in Gilbertsville NY, Polly Ann Skidmore born March 22, 1784 and died November 7, 1882.

No children recorded
