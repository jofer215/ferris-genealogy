MAURINE PEARLE PARTCH

ID: 4.6.9.8.11.3.3.2.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.9.8.11.3.3.2.1) MAURINE PEARLE PARTCH born May 26, 1906 Oberlin OH; 1923 graduate of Columbus H. S.; U of Wisconsin, B.A., 1927; Simmons College, Boston, M.S., Social Science 1928; licensed medical technologist in CA; married July 19, 1936 Allen Lewis Ryan born April 25, 1906 Cheyenne WY; WWII Vet; Stanford U 1928, B.A.

No children recorded
