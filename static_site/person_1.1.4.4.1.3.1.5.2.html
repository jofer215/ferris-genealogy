MURIEL GERTRUDE MILLER

ID: 1.1.4.4.1.3.1.5.2 | Source: John
(1.1.4.4.1.3.1.5.2) MURIEL GERTRUDE MILLER born May 1, 1898 Cumberland Township, Barron Co WI and died July 1975 Minneapolis MN; married 1st a Mr. Foster - divorced; married 2d September 5, 1931 at Minneapolis, Herbert John Humble [son of Albert & Annie]. Herbert was a dentist and died by drowning May 1949 during a storm on Mille Lacs Lake, MN. Unable to have children, they adopted Muriel (Penny) Elizabeth born 1937

No children recorded
