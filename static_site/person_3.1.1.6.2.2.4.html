MARILDA JANE HAMMEL

ID: 3.1.1.6.2.2.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.2.2.4) MARILDA JANE HAMMEL born March 13, 1820 Hamilton Co OH and died April 29, 1896, although 1886 is the year of death on her tombstone; married April 15, 1837 in Hamilton Co OH William E. Bridge [son of John Townsend & Mary (Harper) Bridge] born March 13, 1816 Reily Township, Butler Co OH and died September 1, 1893; a farmer and blacksmith; resided Smyrna Township, Jefferson Co IN 1850; both buried at Valley Cemetery, Graham Township, Jefferson, IN; and born to them were:
