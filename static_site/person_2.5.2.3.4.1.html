JACOB HOYT, JR.,

ID: 2.5.2.3.4.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.3.4.1) JACOB HOYT, JR., (#3518) born November 10, 1761 and died December 28, 1844 South Salem NY where he lived. He was constable and collector at Stamford 1794; married April 26, 1787 Ellen Bouton born June 22, 1769. Born to them were:
