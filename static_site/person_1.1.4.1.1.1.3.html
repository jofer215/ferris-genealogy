JULIA FERRIS

ID: 1.1.4.1.1.1.3 | Source: John
(1.1.4.1.1.1.3) JULIA FERRIS (Judah) born (October 22, 1831) 1832 Wayne Co NY; 1880 resided Auburn, Cayuga, NY; married Vaughn Sweet [son of Isaac & Sarah (Vaughn) Sweet] born c1798 Throop, Cayuga, NY; 1850 resided Schuyler, Herkimer, NY; and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • JOSEPH F SWEET 1.1.4.1.1.1.3.2
  • WILLIAM P SWEET 1.1.4.1.1.1.3.3
  • ROBERT VAUGHN SWEET, M.D., 1.1.4.1.1.1.3.4
  • CALEB W SWEET 1.1.4.1.1.1.3.5
