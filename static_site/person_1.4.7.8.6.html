WILLIAM RIEHL COCK - not shown in all sources -

ID: 1.4.7.8.6 | Source: John
(1.4.7.8.6) WILLIAM RIEHL COCK - not shown in all sources - born January 8, 1841 NYC; member of the Free Academy, class of 1860, now the College of the City of New York. He was one of the founders of the Muhlenberg Hospital in Plainfield, and one of its governors from its foundation in 1877; a member of Grace Church, Plainfield, and for over 40 years, a member of its choir, and sometime vestryman.

No children recorded
