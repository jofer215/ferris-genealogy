ELIJAH SCOFIELD

ID: 5.2.4.7.1 | Source: James
(5.2.4.7.1) ELIJAH SCOFIELD born December 11, 1775 Stamford and died April 1, 1838 New Canaan CT; buried North Stamford Cemetery; married 1st December 8, 1803 at New Canaan, Cynthia St. John born c1780 Stamford and died 1804; married 2d 1807 at New Canaan, as his 2d husband, Hannah Studwell [dau of Anthony & Hannah (Whelpley) Studwell] born September 15, 1778 Greenwich and died December 28, 1869 New Canaan; buried with Elijah. Born to him and Hannah were:
