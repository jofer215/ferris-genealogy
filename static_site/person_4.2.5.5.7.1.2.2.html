MARY A LANE

ID: 4.2.5.5.7.1.2.2 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.5.7.1.2.2) MARY A LANE born 1833 Ulster Co NY; received a good education and taught school before marriage; married February 9, 1856 Jacob M VanDeMark [son of J. S. & Catherine (Elmendorf) VanDeMark] born December 18, 1832 Olive Township, Ulster Co NY; farmer and stock raiser in Section 3, Tampico Township; worked with his father in the mechanical pursuits until of age, receiving only a limited school education, but a good training in the area of house building. After attaining his legal age, he worked for years for an architect and then in 1858, came West and located upon 40 acres in Yorktown Township, Henry Co IL and followed his trade; 1858 he moved to Tampico Township and helped organize that division of the county, being elected the first Township Clerk; also served as Road Commissioner for several years; 1866 settled on a farm of 80 acres in Section 3, eventually owning 220 acres; and born to them were:

Children (2)
