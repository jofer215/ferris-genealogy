MARTHA FERRIS

ID: 1.3.4.2 | Source: John
(1.3.4.2) MARTHA FERRIS born November 12, 1743 and died 1829 Peekskill, Dutchess, NY; married 1st Elijah Dingee (c1740-1824) [son of Robert Stites & Sarah (?) Dingee]; married 2d a Mr. Fuller. Elijah Dingee was a Tory and he fled to Nova Scotia with his father at the end of the Rev. War. Born to Martha and Elijah was:

Children (2)

  • DAVID DINGEE 1.3.4.2.1
  • AMOS FULLER 1.3.4.2.2
