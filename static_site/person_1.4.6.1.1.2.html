CAROLINE MATILDA FERRIS

ID: 1.4.6.1.1.2 | Source: John
(1.4.6.1.1.2) CAROLINE MATILDA FERRIS born June 20, 1831 NY and died August 2, 1910 Stamford CT; buried there Woodland Cemetery; married January 14, 1850 Charles William Waterbury [son of Horace & Anne (Miller) Waterbury] born January 18, 1829 Stamford CT and died there June 26, 1864; buried Woodland Cemetery. Mr. Waterbury was occupied through life as an agriculturist, but for a few years carried on a grocery, flour and feed, coal and wood business at Waterside, Stamford, CT, as a member of the firm Henley & Waterbury. In the Civil War, he went forth to battle for his country, enlisting on September 10, 1862, at Stamford, in Company B, 28th Connecticut Volunteer Infantry and served until August 28, 1863; his military records show him present for duty during the above time frame, and shows him at age 34 years when mustered out. He was a member of the Baptist Church and a highly esteemed citizen. Born to Caroline and Charles were:
