NATHANIEL LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.1.4.1.3.4 | Source: James
(5.1.4.1.3.4) NATHANIEL LOCKWOOD born June 15, 1798 Bedford NY and died May 31, 1886 Stamford CT; buried there High Ridge Cemetery (Photo); farmer 1850; married 1st July 1, 1819 in Stamford, Polly Curtis [dau of John & Polly (Fancher) Curtis] born June 8, 1802 Stamford and died there May 18, 1838; buried there High Ridge Cemetery; married 2d Hannah Everett born October 7, 1801 New Canaan CT; and born to him and Polly were:
