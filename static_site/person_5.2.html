DEBORAH FERRIS

ID: 5.2 | Source: James
(5.2) DEBORAH FERRIS (possibly a daughter of James) born 1667 Stamford and died there July 3, 1724; married, as his 2d (of 3) wife January 14, 1701/2 at Stamford, Lt. Jonathan Bell [son of Jonathan & Mercy (Crane) Bell] born February 14, 1663/64 Stamford and died there September 1745 (September 3, 1724). This Deborah is also listed as a possible daughter of (2) Peter (see Note at top of this chapter). Born to them were:

Children (7)

  • FRANCIS BELL 5.2.1
  • HANNAH BELL 5.2.2
  • REBECCA BELL 5.2.3
  • JAMES BELL 5.2.4
  • DEBORAH BELL 5.2.5
  • ABIGAIL BELL 5.2.6
  • NOAH BELL – not listed in all sources – born November 19, 1748 Stamford and died there 5.2.7
