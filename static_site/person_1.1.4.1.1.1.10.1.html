MARIE JUNE FERRIS

ID: 1.1.4.1.1.1.10.1 | Source: John
(1.1.4.1.1.1.10.1) MARIE JUNE FERRIS born May 17, 1897; married Ray Bixler; George Martin; and as 1 of his 3 wives, Claude John O'Tier [son of Frank & Magdalena- Leah (O’Meal) O’Tier] born August 5, 1895 East Palmyra, Wayne, NY and died December 10, 1970; buried East Palmyra Cemetery; and born to her and Ray was:

Children (4)

  • KATHERINE BIXLER 1.1.4.1.1.1.10.1.1
  • JOHN J O’TIER Jr 1.1.4.1.1.1.10.1.2
  • HARRY O’TIER married Susan Avery and they had offspring 1.1.4.1.1.1.10.1.3
  • RALPH O’TIER married Ursula Kramer and they had offspring; 1.1.4.1.1.1.10.1.4
