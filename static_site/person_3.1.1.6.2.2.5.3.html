ALVAREDO ELIJAH HAMMEL

ID: 3.1.1.6.2.2.5.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.2.2.5.3) ALVAREDO ELIJAH HAMMEL born April 9, 1851 Jefferson Co IN and died November 9, 1910 Indianapolis IN; married April 1875 at Manchester, Dearborn, IN, Louannah Blasdel [dau of Rev. Franklin Townsend & Louisa (Cloud) Blasdel] born October 17, 1847 Dearborn Co IN and died November 1917 Madison, Jefferson, IN; both buried Pleasant Ridge Cemetery, Madison IN; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • WALTER H. HAMMEL 3.1.1.6.2.2.5.3.1
  • ARTHUR ROSCOE HAMMEL 3.1.1.6.2.2.5.3.3
  • EMMA MAY HAMMEL died in infancy; buried Pleasant Ridge Cemetery, Madison 3.1.1.6.2.2.5.3.4
