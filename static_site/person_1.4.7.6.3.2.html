ANNA HAVILAND FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.6.3.2 | Source: John
(1.4.7.6.3.2) ANNA HAVILAND FERRIS born April 6, 1827 and died September 13, 1911 Saratoga NY; buried Woodlawn Cemetery with her husband; married May 15, 1851 in West Chester NY, Augustus Taber [son of William Congton & Hannah Tucker (Shearman) Taber] born February 3, 1826 New Bedford MA and died April 6, 1898. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • MARY FERRIS TABER 1.4.7.6.3.2.1
  • CORNELIA TABER 1.4.7.6.3.2.2
