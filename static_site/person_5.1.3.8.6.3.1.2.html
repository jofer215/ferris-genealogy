ARTHUR MILLER FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.6.3.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.3.1.2) ARTHUR MILLER FERRIS born June 11, 1885 DeWitt NY and died April 11, 1955 Orlando FL; buried DeWitt Cemetery; married September 6, 1919 Lela Agnes Farnham [dau of Jasper & Dapne (Minor)] born November (1) 11, 1889 and died July 10, 1954 Syracuse NY; buried Dewitt Cemetery. Arthur was Syracuse City assessor 1929-48, when he retired and then lived near Sandy Creek in the summers and Florida in the winters; and born to them were:
