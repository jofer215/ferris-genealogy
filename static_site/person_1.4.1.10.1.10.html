EDGAR FERRIS died May 2, 1910, committed suicide leaving a wife

ID: 1.4.1.10.1.10 | Source: John
(1.4.1.10.1.10) EDGAR FERRIS died May 2, 1910, committed suicide leaving a wife and a daughter. He and brother William had a drug store in Westchester Village, 2591 Westchester Ave., it was the largest and one of the oldest in that section of New York City; married Margaret Hill and born to them was:

Children (1)
