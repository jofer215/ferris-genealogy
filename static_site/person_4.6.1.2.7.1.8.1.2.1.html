JOHN WAYNE KEELER

ID: 4.6.1.2.7.1.8.1.2.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.7.1.8.1.2.1) JOHN WAYNE KEELER born February 22, 1927 and died July 29, 1979 Minneapolis MN; buried Fort Snelling Cemetery, Hennepin Co MN; USAF Vet 3 years; lawyer in Morris MN; workman’s compensation judge for the state of Minnesota in Minneapolis; married 1st February 3, 1951 in Chokio, Shirley Brethrost born September 30, 1930 Sexton IA – divorced and died December 30, 1995 Burnsville, Dakota, MN; buried Lutheran Cemetery, Chokio MN; married 2d c1974-75 in South Dakota, name unk
