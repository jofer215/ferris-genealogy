MARY FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.6 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.6) MARY FERRIS born February 28, 1817 Eaton, Madison, NY and died August 31, 1842 Newburg OH; married April 5, 1840 in Newburg, Geauga, OH, 1st of his 3 wives, Dr. John Milton Burlingame [son of Rickertson & Lydia (Fitch) Burlingame] born February 7, 1813 Ellicottville, Catteraugus, NY and died April (1) 17/18, 1860 Streetsboro OH; 1851 the family moved to Streetsboro Center, Portage, OH, where Dr. Burlingame continued his practice until his death. After Mary’s death, John married his sister-in-law, Hannah. Born to Mary and John was:
