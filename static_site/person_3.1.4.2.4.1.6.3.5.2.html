ALICE ESTELLE GULICK

ID: 3.1.4.2.4.1.6.3.5.2 | Source: Joseph

(Cunningham) Martyn] born July 16, 1894 Lafayette MI and died December 12, 1978 Gratiot Co MI; buried Fulton Cemetery. He was a WWI Vet and a flourist; and born to them were:
