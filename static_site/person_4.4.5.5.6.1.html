HENRY SMITH LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.5.5.6.1 | Source: Mary
(4.4.5.5.6.1) HENRY SMITH LOCKWOOD (Captain) born April 30, 1828 Greenwich CT and died November 22, 1910, Cos Cob, Greenwich; married December 22, 1850 Sarah Elizabeth White [dau of Jacob & Phebe (Reynolds) White] born February 10, 1828 Stamford CT and died July 20, 1908, Cos Cob, Greenwich; buried Sound Beach (Tomac Ave Cemetery) CT (but not listed on Net). Captain Lockwood built the first steamboat in the US (1878) which was especially constructed for the oyster business Born to them were:
