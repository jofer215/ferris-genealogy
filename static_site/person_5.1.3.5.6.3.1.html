JAMES HALL WHELPLEY

ID: 5.1.3.5.6.3.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.5.6.3.1) JAMES HALL WHELPLEY II born July 23, 1851 NYC and died January 19, 1929 White Plains, Westchester, NY; buried Cypress Hills Cemetery, Jamaica NY; married December 27, 1869 (December 28, 1870 in NYC) Marian T. Hagaman born February 1851 NYC; they had their 50th Anniversary 1920; James was well known in trotting horse circles; 1910 was a stable superintendent, and at one time had a hotel on Ocean Boulevard; he was with Wilson & Co wholesale butchers for 24 years; 1900 they had had 13 kids with 6 living; and born to them were:
