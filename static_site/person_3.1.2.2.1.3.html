ISRAEL BRUNDAGE FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.2.1.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.1.3) ISRAEL BRUNDAGE FERRIS born March 9, 1772 and died July 5, 1836; buried Ferris Cemetery on Smyrna Hill, Chenango Co NY; always referred to as ‘Brundage’; married 1st June 23, 1797 Amy Bentley who died September 25, 1804; and 2d February 3, 1805 widow Thankful Wood born August 31, 1773 and died September 15, 1855; and born to Brundage and Amy were:

Children (4)

  • JOHN S. FERRIS 3.1.2.2.1.3.1
  • JANE FERRIS 3.1.2.2.1.3.2
  • ABIGAIL FERRIS 3.1.2.2.1.3.4
  • ISRAEL BRUNDAGE FERRIS, JR., 3.1.2.2.1.3.5
