ZENOBIA ANGELINE LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.1.4.4.2.3.2 | Source: James
(5.1.4.4.2.3.2) ZENOBIA ANGELINE LOCKWOOD born May 20, 1837 Lafayette IN; married October 20, 1855 in Lafayette IN, William McKey Wilson who was killed during the Civil War; Angeline later became a physician and practiced in Terre Haute IN – no offspring

No children recorded
