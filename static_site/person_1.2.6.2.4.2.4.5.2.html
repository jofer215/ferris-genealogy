MERLYN EUGENE UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.6.2.4.2.4.5.2 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.4.2.4.5.2) MERLYN EUGENE UNDERHILL (Duke) born August 6, 1920 South Wayne, Lafayette, WI; WWII USN Vet; married 1st January 26, 1940 in Dubuque IA Isabelle Violet Kehoe [dau of Edward & Frances] born September 29, 1919 Rockton IL and died April 14, 1988 Las Vegas NV and they had 4 kids – obviously divorced; married 2d July 13, 1963 in Rockton, as her 2d husband, Arlene Mae Allen [dau of William & Emma (Fuess) Allen] born March 30, 1916 Beloit WI; married 3d September 5, 1955 Jean Finkbiener

No children recorded
