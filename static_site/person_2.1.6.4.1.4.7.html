WILLIAM LEWIS PEABODY

ID: 2.1.6.4.1.4.7 | Source: Peter
(2.1.6.4.1.4.7) WILLIAM LEWIS PEABODY born November 13, 1828 Poughkeepsie NY; Civil War Vet – served in Harris’ NY Light Cav, discharged for illness; reenlisted in 6th NY Heavy Artillery and served until end of war; married January 12, 1851 Adelia Stoutenburgh Doane born March 6, 1832 Poughkeepsie NY; and born to them were:
