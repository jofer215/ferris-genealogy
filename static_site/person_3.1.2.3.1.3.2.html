BERINTHA C. FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.3.1.3.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.3.1.3.2) BERINTHA C. FERRIS born c1809/10 Clinton Co NY and died March (1) 10, 1890 St. Joseph Co IN; married three times: 1st Jonathan Gilman; 2d Mr. Tupper, and 3d November 13, 1862 Amos Jewell. Berintha and Jonathan moved to Madison Township, St. Joseph Co IN 1848. Berintha had a total of 14 children of which the following are listed:
