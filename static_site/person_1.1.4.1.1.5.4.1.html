SARAH E. WHITE

ID: 1.1.4.1.1.5.4.1 | Source: John
(1.1.4.1.1.5.4.1) SARAH E. WHITE born c1872, probably IN and was living 1953 East Mishawaka; married c1897 in Mishawaka IN, Walter E. Roe (1874-1926). There are markers for both these individuals at the Mishawaka City Cemetery, however, no death date has been inscribed for Sarah. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • JOHN D. ROE 1.1.4.1.1.5.4.1.1
  • INFANT SON ROE died 1906; buried Mishawaka City Cemetery 1.1.4.1.1.5.4.1.2
