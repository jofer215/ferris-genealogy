(3.1.6.2.6.3) BENEJAH SHERMAN Last Will and Testament of Benajah SHERMAN Deceased Be it remembered that (_); to wit on the eighth day of August, in the year of our Lord one thousand eight hundred and sixty, JEANNETTE SHERMAN, the executor of named in the Last Will and Testament of a BENAJAH SHERMAN, late of the town of Seneca Falls, in the county of Seneca (_), appeared in open court before the Surrogate of the County of Seneca and made application to have the Last Will and Testament which relates to both Real and Personal Estate, proved, and on such application said Surrogate did ascertain by satisfactory evidence who were the widow heirs of (___ _____ _____ ___ _____) as follows, Jennette SHERMAN, now Jannette HAYS, widow of the deceased; FLORINDA VAN LUYL, LUCRETIA VAN VALKENBURGH; and MARY CRAWFORD, daughters of said deceased, and WILLIAM SHERMAN and JAME SHERMAN, sons of said deceased, all of full age, and residing in said county, or in counties adjoining said county of Seneca. And said Surrogate did thereupon issue a citation in the form of law directed to the widow and heirs, in-laws, and next of kin(____blank__) by their respective names stating their places of residences (__BLANK__) requiring them to appear before said Surrogate in his office in the Village of Waterloo in said county on the seventeenth day of October A.D. 1860 to attend the Probate of said will and satisfactory evidence of affidavits being presented to said Surrogate of the (due?) service of (__?) citations in the mode provided by law, such proceedings were held on the (??_ 17th day of October 1860, (_) that since hearing was continued before said Surrogate, undetermined until afterwards, to wit on the (29th?) day of June, 1865, when upon application of HENRY HERRIMAN (HARRISON?) a creditor (_) Surrogate issued the citations in (_) said widow, heairs at law trust of kin to other (____?) Surrogate other office in the Village of Seneca Lakes in said county on the 28th day of July, he showed cause. And afterwards to wit on the said 28th day of July, A.D. 1865 satisfactory evidence by affidavit was (_) and presented to said Surrogate of (_____ _____ _____ _____) (Several lines not readable..) of Real and Personal Estate and the (_) thereof to be sufficient which said last Will and Testament and this last mentioned (_) are as follows, that is to say: (All handwritten) Know all by these present that I, Benajah SHERMAN of Seneca Falls, in the County of Seneca and the State of New York, considering the uncertainty of human life, and being of sound mind and memory, do make and publish this my last will and Testament in manner said form following viz; First I give and bequeath unto my wife, Jennet SHERMAN all my Real Estate, it being all that contain piece and parcel of land or Village lot, situate lying and being on the North side of Main or Fall Street in the Village of Seneca Falls a few rods west of where (Walnut?) Street intersects said Fall Street, extending from Fall Street northward to and bounded on the Northerly land of D.SCHOONOVEN and is the same premises where I now reside, together with all hereditaments and appurtenances thereunto belonging or in any appertaining; To have and to hold the premises above disserted to my said wife, Jennet Sherman, for the sole use and benefit during her natural life. And after her decease to revert to my children of my former wife, and to James SHERMAN, a son of my said wife, Jennet to be equally divided between them their heirs administrators and assigns. Second I give and bequeath to my said wife, Jennet SHERMAN all my personal estate, household furniture, goods and chattels of whatever name or nature and also attributes of all debts due me, if there be any, to have and to hold the same to my wife JENNET during her natural life. And after decease to revert (as above confessed relative to the said estate) to my children by a former wife and James SHERMAN a son of my said wife, Jennet. And I do desire, direct and order that whithin one year after the decease of my said wife, Jennet SHERMAN, or as soon thereafter, as may be, the said Real Estate and the said Personal Estate shall be sold for the best price that can be obtained therefore either at private of public sale, and the avails thereof, after paying the necessary funeral expenses of my said wife JANET, equally divided between all of my said children by a former wife and said James SHERMAN share and share alike, their heirs and assigns forever. Lastly, I hereby appoint my said wife Jennet SHERMAN sole executor of this my said last will and testament hereby revoking and annulling all former wills by me made in testimony whereof I have hereunto set my hand and seal at Seneca Falls this 23rd day of December, 1859. Benajah SHERMAN {L.S.} The above instrument consisting of our sheet was at the date thereof signed, sealed, published and declared by the said Benajah SHERMAN as, and for his last Will and Testament in presence of us, who at his request and in his presence, and in the presence of each other have such scribed our names as witnesses thereto. Hiram A. PALMER residing at Seneca Falls, Seneca County N.Y. Joseph BABCOCK residing at Seneca Falls, Seneca County, N.Y. July 25, 1865 Jennette HAYS-formally Jennette SHERMAN appears and claims that will be admitted to probate on the proof before the Surrogate and that Letters testamentary issue SURROGATE'S COURT to her. Henry (HENION) appears for Creditors and the heirs and next of kin and consents. In the matter of Proving the last Will and Testament thereto. Florinda VAN LUYL, Lucinda Van Of: VALKENBURGH, Mary CRAWFORD, William SHERMAN also appear and present thereto Benajah SHERMAN. Proof of service of citation for this day read and Deceased filed, served on the widow and all the heirs. Ordered that the subscribing witnesses be Re-examined. Seneca County, S.S. Hiram A. PALMER and Joseph BABCOCK being first duly sworn in open Court upon their several corporal oaths, each for himself doth depose and say that they are subscribing witnesses to the last Will and Testament of Benajah SHERMAN late of the town of Seneca Falls in the county of Seneca and the State of New York deceased. And these deponents do further say that the said Benajah SHERMAN deceased, did in the presence of each of these deponents, subscribe his name at the end of the instrument in writing which is now here shown to these deponents, and which purports to be the last Will and Testament of Benajah SHERMAN the said deceased, and which bears date on the Twentythird day of December one thousand eight hundred and fifty nine. That the said deceased did at the time of subscribing his name to the said instrument aforesaid, declare the same to be his last Will and Testament; and these deponents did thereupon subscribe their own respective names at the end of said instrument, as attesting witnesses to the execution thereof; each at the request of the said deceased in his presence and in the presence of each other; That the said deceased at the time of so subscribing his name to said instrument as aforesaid and publishing said instrument as aforesaid, was upwards of twenty-one years of age, and a citizen of the United States; That he appeared to be and deponents believe he was of sound mind, memory and understanding, and not under restraint, and as deponents verily believe, in all respects competent to devise real estate, that each of these deponents was the other sign his name to said instrument in the presence of the said deceased. Deponent PALMER says he wrote the will as requested by Mr. SHERMAN, that after it was written he carefully read it to Mr. SHERMAN, who seemed to understand it and said it was right, and that the will is now in the same condition as when executed, has not been altered. Hiram A. PALMER Joseph BABCOCK Subscribed and sworn to before me, this 28th day of July A.D. 1865 Josiah T (L.) MILLER, Surrogate Seneca County, S.S. (In/It?) appearing upon the proofs duly taken in respect to the Last Will and Testament of Benajah SHERMAN late of town of Seneca Falls County of Seneca and the State of New York, deceased that the said Will was duly executed and that the said Benajah SHERMAN at the time he executed the same, was in all respects competent to devise real estate and not under restraint, the said last Will and Testament and the proofs and examination are hereby recorded, signed, and certified by me, pursuant to the provisions of revised statutes, this 28th day of July A.D. 1865 J.T. MILLER Surrogate
