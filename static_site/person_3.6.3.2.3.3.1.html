JOSHUA L. KNAPP

ID: 3.6.3.2.3.3.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.3.3.1) JOSHUA L. KNAPP born September 15, 1844 and died April 9, 1877 Fort Stevens OR; drowned in the Columbia River; buried Fort Stevens Post Cemetery; a West Point graduate; Civil War soldier, a Lieutenant Colonel with the 4th US Infantry; a Joshua L Knapp joined Phi Gamma Delta at Monmouth College – he was class of 1871 but apparently went to West Point in 1869; listed as a First Lieutenant on the rolls of the Fort Stevens Cemetery (See Photo)

No children recorded
