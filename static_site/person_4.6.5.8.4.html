SAMUEL HAIT

ID: 4.6.5.8.4 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.8.4) SAMUEL HAIT (#3405) born July 18, 1778 Pound Ridge and died November 1851 Weedsport NY; buried God’s Acre, Weedsport. He was a cooper and lived in Pound Ridge, Franklin and Weedsport NY. Samuel married March 14, 1799 Nancy Benedict [dau of Abner]. Born to them were 12 children - only five below [note the name change]:
