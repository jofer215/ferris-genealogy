ELIZABETH KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.3.7.2.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.7.2.3) ELIZABETH KNAPP (Sarah Elizabeth) born c1825 NYC; married as his 2d wife, Adrian V LaRoche [son of Isaac & Elizabeth Sophia McIntosh (Oliver) LaRoche] born c1821 Richmond Co GA and died February 1, 1873 Savannah, Chatham, GA; buried there Laurel Grove Cemetery
