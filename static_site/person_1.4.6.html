BENJAMIN FERRIS

ID: 1.4.6 | Source: John
(1.4.6) BENJAMIN FERRIS born 1729 and died November 25, 1779 (7th born); born and died in Westchester Co NY. [will dated November 25, 1777, proved June 30, 1783]; served in the 4th Regiment of Westchester Co militia under Colonel Crane; married c1769 Sarah Palmer [dau of Aaron] and born to them were [not in birth sequence]:
