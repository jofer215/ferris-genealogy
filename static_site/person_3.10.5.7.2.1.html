THANKFUL LOCKWOOD

ID: 3.10.5.7.2.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.5.7.2.1) THANKFUL LOCKWOOD born March 16, 1783 Greenwich and died February 25, 1854 Stamford; married April 13, 1806 in Stamford, Rufus Webb [son of Ebenezer & Phebe (Todd) Webb] born April 6, 1787 Stamford and died there September 12, 1829; both buried there West Stamford Cemetery; and born to them were: (3.10.5.7.2.1.1) SYLVESTER L’HOMMEDIEU WEBB born January 28, 1807 Stamford and died there March 23, 1856; buried there West Stamford Cemetery
