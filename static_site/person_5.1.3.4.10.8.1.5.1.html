KEITH C. REESE

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.8.1.5.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.8.1.5.1) KEITH C. REESE born April 5, 1920 and died September 21, 1993; married July 27, 1952 Barbara B. Hannum born August 16, 1927. Keith graduated from the University of Indiana and its School of Law; practiced law for 43 years most recently as counsel for Rocap, Witcher and Threlkeld; past president of the Indiana Defense Lawyers’ Association and a fellow of the American College of Trial Lawyers; also an organizer of the Indiana Legal Services Organization. He was a 32d degree Mason, a WWII Veteran, and a member of the St. Paul's Episcopal Church at Indianapolis. Born to them were:
