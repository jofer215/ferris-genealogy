WILLIAM EDMUND HAMMEL

ID: 3.1.1.6.2.2.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.2.2.5) WILLIAM EDMUND HAMMEL born August 28, 1822 Hamilton Co OH and died October 18, 1882 Madison IN; married August 20, 1845 in Cambridge, Dearborn, IN, Jane Fuller Blasdel [dau of Elijah & Clarissa (Waring) Blasdel] born May 5, 1829 Cambridge IN and died June 9, 1891 Madison IN; resided Lancaster Township, Jefferson, IN 1860; both buried Pleasant Ridge Cemetery, Madison; William’s will written September 4, 1882, rec November 2, 1882 – my wife Jane T Hammel, all personal and real estate; and born to them were:
