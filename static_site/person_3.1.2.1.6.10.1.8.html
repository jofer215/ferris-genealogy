ADIN LYMAN ABEL

ID: 3.1.2.1.6.10.1.8 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.6.10.1.8) ADIN LYMAN ABEL born August 18, 1861 Perry, Shiawasse, MI and died July 8, 1945 Lusk, Niobrara, WY; Adin left his Michigan home at age 11 to work on the sheep ranch of George Ferris in Wyoming; married March 11, 1896 in Hall Co NE, Caroline Catherine Wiseman (Calla) [dau of Martin Luther & Martha (Mary) Adelia (Ray) Wiseman] born February 21, 1877 Middletown, Frederick, MD and died April 29, 1935 Harrison NE; and born to them were:
