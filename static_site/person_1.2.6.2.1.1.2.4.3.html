BESSIE LENORE UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.6.2.1.1.2.4.3 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.1.1.2.4.3) BESSIE LENORE UNDERHILL (Photo) born April 25, 1875 Rushville IL and died September 6, 1957 Evans CO; she traveled with her family in 1878 from Schuyler Co IL to a farm near Holdredge NE; married November 16, 1892 in Stockville NE, Franklin Jobe Conklin [son of William & Mariah (Edwards) Conklin] born September 7, 1868 near Iron Hills, Jackson, IA and died July 26, 1954 Evans CO; both buried Sunset Memorial Gardens, Greely CO; Frank traveled with his family in 1887 from Jackson Co IA to a farm near Gayville SD, however in 1888 they moved to Frontier Co NE; Frank and Bessie farmed near Stockville until 1920 when they moved to a farm near New Raymer CO; they retired to Evans CO 1934; and born to them were:
