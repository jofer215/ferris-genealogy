ROY MITCHELL STEVENS

ID: 3.1.1.6.1.4.5.1.3.2.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.1.4.5.1.3.2.6) ROY MITCHELL STEVENS (Photo) born August 3, 1957 Modesto CA; married July 11, 1968 Lisa Anne Winberg [dau of James Milton & Mary Caroline (Rowe) Winberg] born October 3, 1960 Oceanside CA; Roy, a baritone and Lisa, a soprano, majored in music at San Jose State University, earning Bachelor of Music degrees. Lisa, using the name Annalisa Winberg, and Roy, were soloists at Christian Science churches, and have had lead roles in operas separately and together. They have both been in Europe competing in various events, and have had lead roles in operas in Italy, France, and Germany. Roy sang Nabucco and Verdi's Regoletto in Ljubljana, Yugoslavia, 1988; Lisa did a concert version of Tosca near Venice 1988; Roy in the role of Verdi's Falstaff at Macao 1994; and born to them was:
