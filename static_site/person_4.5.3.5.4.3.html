RANSOM SEELY LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.5.3.5.4.3 | Source: Mary
(4.5.3.5.4.3) RANSOM SEELY LOCKWOOD (#1358) born February 13, 1810 Union Village, Warren, OH and lived there among the Shakers until 1833 when he left them and moved to Franklin OH, where he was still residing in 1888 and was Justice of the Peace for 38 years; admitted to the bar 1848; married at Franklin, Hannah Maria Ross [dau of Nathaniel] born March 6, 1814 Indian Hill, Hamilton, OH. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • LAURA LOCKWOOD 4.5.3.5.4.3.1
  • ROSS P. LOCKWOOD 4.5.3.5.4.3.2
  • HOPE DEBOLT LOCKWOOD 4.5.3.5.4.3.3
  • CLARA BELL LOCKWOOD 4.5.3.5.4.3.4
