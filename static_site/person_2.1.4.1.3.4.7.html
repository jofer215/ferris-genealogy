FRANCES J. SEARL

ID: 2.1.4.1.3.4.7 | Source: Peter
(2.1.4.1.3.4.7) FRANCES J. SEARL born c1854 IL and died May 1936; married in Bradford IL John Frank Reed born c1852 and died February 21, 1939; both buried Grove Cemetery, Elmira Township, Stark Co IL; resided Bradford IL. There are several other Reeds buried at Osceola Cemetery - no assurances they are offspring - only speculation - so will not place them at this time.

No children recorded
