JOHN BARTHOLOMEW LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.1.2.3.2.6 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.3.2.6) JOHN BARTHOLOMEW LOCKWOOD (#2280) born August 3, 1826 McLeansboro, Hamilton, IL; married September 24, 1843 in Hamilton Co IL, Sarah Flint born September 25, 1825 Hamilton Co IL and died January 26, 1863. John was appointed from NY, Major and Paymaster, February 21, 1863 and resigned August 5, 1865 (Civil War). Born to them were:

Children (6)
