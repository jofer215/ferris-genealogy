HIRAM D. TOTTEN

ID: 5.2.2.2.9.1.3 | Source: James
(5.2.2.2.9.1.3) HIRAM D. TOTTEN born c1844 (December 1845 CT) and died September 9, 1892 Stamford (Portchester NY); buried Turn of River Cemetery; a Civil War Vet serving in Company F, 22d (23d) New York Volunteer Infantry Regiment; married May 12, 1872 in Poundridge NY, Mary F. Knapp (2.6.2.10.1.4.3.5) and born to them were:
