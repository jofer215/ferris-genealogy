GEORGE MILBURN

ID: 3.11.1.3.1.5.5.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.3.1.5.5.2) GEORGE MILBURN born May 13, 1838 Easton’s Corners, Ontario and died February 5, 1909 Bristol, Elkhart, IN; worked with his Uncle George in the Milburn Wagon Works at one time; 1880 fruit grower; married 1st May 17, 1864 in Bristol, Mary Elizabeth Hanford born July 14, 1843 Bristol and died there March 26, 1866; married 2d October 6, 1868 in Bristol, Elizabeth Perkins Gongdon born January 24, 1850 Bristol and died there December 21, 1933; and born to him and Elizabeth were;
