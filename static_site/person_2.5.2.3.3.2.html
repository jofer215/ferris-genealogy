JEMIMA HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.3.3.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.3.3.2) JEMIMA HOYT (#3514) born November 23, 1767 and died May 26, 1858; unmarried (2.5.2.3.3.3) JOHN HOYT IV (#3515) (Deacon) born September 11, 1770 Stamford and died April 15, 1855 probably at Stamford where he lived and farmed on his father’s homestead; was John IV in 1794; John III, 1796-1809 and John, Jr., 1809-25; married Ruth Scofield (2.6.2.11.6); he was chosen Deacon, January 2, 1835.
