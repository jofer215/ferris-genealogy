WANDA ALMIRA KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.3.2.5.8.4.2.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.2.5.8.4.2.2) WANDA ALMIRA KNAPP born November 30, 1907 Barrington NY and died October 20, 1994 Penn Yan NY where she is buried; married August 14, 1933 at Barrington on the Knapp Homestead, Charles Edward Schlappi born August 22, 1908 Utica, Oneida, NY and was living 1994 at Dundee; a lumber merchant.
