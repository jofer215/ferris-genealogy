GEORGE DAVENPORT

ID: 5.2.4.8.5.2 | Source: James
(5.2.4.8.5.2) GEORGE DAVENPORT born March 24, 1813 CT (Davenport Ridge, North Stamford CT) and died May 10, 1898 Stamford; buried Woodland Cemetery, Stamford; married 1st November 24, 1841 in Stamford, Amanda Buffet Fuller [dau of Henry & Maria B]; married 2d 1850 Charlotte E. Scofield [dau of Isaac & Sally Maria (Warner) Scofield] born 1827 Stamford and died November 15, 1914 Stamford; buried Woodland Cemetery – is this Charlotte (5.2.2.1.4.9.7)? Born to them were:
