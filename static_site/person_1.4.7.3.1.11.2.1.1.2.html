NUALA DALLAS PELL

ID: 1.4.7.3.1.11.2.1.1.2 | Source: John
(1.4.7.3.1.11.2.1.1.2) NUALA DALLAS PELL (Dallas) attended St Michael’s School in Newport RI and graduated from the Madeira School and Boston U; was a literary agent with John Brockman & Associates; married December 19, 1982 at Newport RI, Eames Hamilton Yates [son of Frederick Langdon & Mary (Olberg) Yates]; was a senior production associate for ABC News Closeup Documentaries in NY; former production assistant for NBC Nightly News with Chancellor and Brinkley in Washington, graduated from the Westtown PA School and attended the U of Wyoming

No children recorded
