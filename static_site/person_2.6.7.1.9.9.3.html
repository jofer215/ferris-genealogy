ANN R.WATERBURY

ID: 2.6.7.1.9.9.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.1.9.9.3) ANN R.WATERBURY born 1852 Darien and died there 1920; married after 1880 George W. Clock [son of William & Sally (Mather) Clock] born January 12, 1836 CT and died April 25, 1905 Darien; both buried Spring Grove Cemetery

No children recorded
