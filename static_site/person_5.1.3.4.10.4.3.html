JOHN SMITH FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.3) JOHN SMITH FERRIS (Photo) born November 10, 1856 Sterling IL (near Metamora IN) (one source has his birth place as Metamora, Franklin Co IN) and died December 8, 1944 Sterling IL; married 1st February 10, 1877 Mary Rosalynd "Minnie" Davis [dau of Dr. Reuben & Elizabeth H. Work Davis] born September 20, 1859 and died April 24, 1934; married 2d after Minnie's death, Mrs. Emma Wallick Baker who died July 25, 1938. John was the owner of several farms in Whiteside County IL, which he rented on shares. He is fondly remembered by family members for his diligence as a landlord; he was always on hand at harvest time, and enjoyed weighing the grain and taking part in the various activities of a landowner up until the time of his death at the age of 88. John and Minnie are buried at Sterling IL. Born to them were:
