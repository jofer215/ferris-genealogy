THISBIE ANN

ID: 3.1.2.1.6.10.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.6.10.2) THISBIE ANN (ANN THISBIE) FERRIS born February 19, 1826 Monroe Co MI (Topkins NY?) and died 1904; married October 15, 1846 at Eaton Rapids, Eaton, MI, Morris St. John Annis born May 30, 1820 (stone says 1822) Sparta NY and died 1900 Eaton Rapids NY; they lived across the road from the Franklin Spears Farm, Phebe (Spears) Clark Farm and Samuel & Sally (Spears) Ferris Farm, which were all next to each other and deed December 12/14, 1836. Both buried in the Old Brickyard Cemetery, Eaton Rapids (Photo); and born to them were:
