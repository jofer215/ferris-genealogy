ELIZABETH DAVENPORT

ID: 1.1.4.2.4.3 | Source: John
(1.1.4.2.4.3) ELIZABETH DAVENPORT born December 20, 1825 Philipstown NY and died March 9, 1886; married James Taylor born March 9, 1827 and hanged himself, March 18, 1887 in a barn at Davenport’s Corners while on a visit to his brother-in-law James E. Jaycox. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • JAMES TAYLOR died young 1.1.4.2.4.3.1
  • VENNISSA TAYLOR died young 1.1.4.2.4.3.2
  • JOHN WILBUR TAYLOR died young 1.1.4.2.4.3.3
  • ALBERT TAYLOR died young 1.1.4.2.4.3.4
