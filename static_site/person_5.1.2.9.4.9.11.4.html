HOMER CHRISTIAN SR.

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.9.11.4 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.9.11.4) HOMER CHRISTIAN SR. born April 26, 1914 and died April 16, 1972 Community Hospital, Peekskill; buried Hillside Cemetery; a night foreman for Lakeland High School, Mr. Christian retired in September 1971. A district employee since 1955, he previously worked as a bus driver and custodian in the Lakeland schools. Prior to working for the Lakeland District, Mr. Christian was employed for years by the Mayflower Bus Company and by Joe DeMatteis as a bus driver. He married Fannie Doyle; resided Shrub Oak; and born to them were:
