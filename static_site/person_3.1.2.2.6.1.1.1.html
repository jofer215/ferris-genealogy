EDWIN BOYNTON LOOP

ID: 3.1.2.2.6.1.1.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.6.1.1.1) EDWIN BOYNTON LOOP born November 1, 1845 Bainbridge, Chenango, NY and died March 1, 1906 Savanna IL; married August 23, 1900 in Chicago, Mary Sophia Scharfenstein [dau of Johann & Caroline (Wodrich) Scharfenstein - both from Germany] born December 29, 1863 Cassville, Grant, WI and died October 17, 1943 Turlock CA; served with Company D., 140th Regiment of Illinois Volunteers (Civil War); later a miner and railroad engineer; and born to them was a single child:
