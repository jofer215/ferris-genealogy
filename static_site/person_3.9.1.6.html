JOB AUSTIN

ID: 3.9.1.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.9.1.6) JOB AUSTIN born March 31, 1759 Dutchess Co NY and died February 7, 1847 Red Mills NY; buried Mahopac Falls NY, Presbyterian Church Yard; Sergeant in the American Revolution, Captain John Crane’s Company, Colonel Ludington’s Regiment, Dutchess Co Militia; engaged in the Battle of White Plains; married 1st 1779 Mary Nelson born April 21, 1758 NY and died September 18, 1793; buried with Job; married 2d widow Hannah Hazen born 1755, and died December 13, 1839; buried Mahopac Falls; (this Job is apparently duplicated with (3.9.3.4) – will leave here until correct placement is determined) and born to Job and Mary were:

Children (3)

  • SARAH AUSTIN 3.9.1.6.2
