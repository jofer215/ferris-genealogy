FRANKLIN FERRIS CLOSE

ID: 5.1.2.8.6.3.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.8.6.3.1) FRANKLIN FERRIS CLOSE (Ferris Franklin Close) born 1847 Genoa NY and died April 21, 1887 when he fell into a saw at a sawmill; buried Moravia NY; married May 20, 1866 Lydia Ann Whipple born 1847 Owasco NY and died September 27, 1894 Locke NY [dau of Jonah and Elizabeth of Owasco NY]. Born to them were: (5.1.2.8.6.3.1.1) CHARLES E. CLOSE born April 18, 1867 Genoa NY and died January 28, 1936 Auburn NY; married Minnie Gray born 1871 Venice NY and died November 19, 1949 Genoa NY.
