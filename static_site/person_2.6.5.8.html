SYLVANUS KNAPP

ID: 2.6.5.8 | Source: Peter
(2.6.5.8) SYLVANUS KNAPP born November 30, 1746 Stamford, Fairfield, CT and died there July 23, 1810; Sylvanus served with the Connecticut forces during the Revolutionary War and was a Congregationalist (First Congregational Church, Stamford); married May 7, 1767 in Stamford, Abigail Weed (2.1.2.2.5) born November 20, 1751 Stamford and died there August 29, 1830; both buried West Stamford Cemetery; and born to Sylvanus and Abigail were:
