PETER CORNELL

ID: 1.1.5.1 | Source: John
(1.1.5.1) PETER CORNELL born 1732 and died July 30, 1767; married May 3, 1751 Sarah Haviland [dau of Thomas & Jane (Clements) Haviland] born February 7, 1734 and died March 17, 1787 White Plains NY; buried in Friend’s Cemtery, Mamaroneck NY. After Peter’s death, Sarah remarried to Josiah Hatfield. Sarah is mentioned in her father’s will as receiving funds from the sale of land on Memursing Island. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • MARY CORNELL 1.1.5.1.1
  • RICHARD CORNELL 1.1.5.1.3
  • EBENEZER CORNELL 1.1.5.1.4
  • HAVILAND CORNELL 1.1.5.1.5
