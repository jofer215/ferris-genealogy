JOSHUA SMITH

ID: 2.6.4.3.1.8 | Source: Peter
(2.6.4.3.1.8) JOSHUA SMITH born December 29, 1792 Stamford and died there February 7, 1863; buried North Street Cemetery; he was a car man 1850-60; married April 10, 1814 in Stamford, Betsey Donaldson born August 20, 1794 Greenwich and died December 13, 1870 Stamford; buried with Joshua; and born to them were:
