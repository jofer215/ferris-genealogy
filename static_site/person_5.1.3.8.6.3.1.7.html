EDWIN LAIN FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.6.3.1.7 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.3.1.7) EDWIN LAIN FERRIS (twin) born March 31, 1900 DeWitt (near Syracuse) NY; married August 20, 1927 Margaret Elizabeth Flynn [dau of Richard & Eliza Jane (Cummings)] born December 25, 1903. Edwin was in the insurance business in Syracuse until 1945, when they moved to Philadelphia and he became the purchasing agent for the Barrett Company. Born to them were:

Children (2)
