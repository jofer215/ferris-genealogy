LINDLEY MURRAY FERRIS, JR.,

ID: 1.4.7.6.6.1 | Source: John
(1.4.7.6.6.1) LINDLEY MURRAY FERRIS, JR., born April 1, 1839 and died February 12, 1895 New Orleans LA, shot by a drunken politician in New Orleans on January 28, 1895 and died February 12; buried Poughkeepsie, Rurak Cemetery on February 26, 1895; married 1st November 12, 1861 Martha I. Sands who died January 1, 1880; and 2d October 4, 1887 Agnes May Smith (1863-1888). Born to Lindley and Martha was:

Children (2)

  • DAVID SANDS FERRIS 1.4.7.6.6.1.1
  • CAROLINE FERRIS 1.4.7.6.6.1.2
