GERSHOM LOCKWOOD, Jr.,

ID: 5.1.4.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.4.1.2) GERSHOM LOCKWOOD, Jr., (#1168) born February 9, 1753 Greenwich and died there March 12, 1816; a Sergeant Major in Captain Charles Smith's Company of Militia, of Colonel John Mead's Regiment from December 10, 1776 to January 11, 1777; married Polly (Sally) Waring of Greenwich. Born to Gershom Jr. and Polly were:

Children (7)

  • ALEXANDER LOCKWOOD 5.1.4.1.2.5
