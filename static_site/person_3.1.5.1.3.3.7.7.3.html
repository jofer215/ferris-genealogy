CLARA REESE

ID: 3.1.5.1.3.3.7.7.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.5.1.3.3.7.7.3) CLARA REESE born July 1889 IA and murdered by a jealous would-be suitor in Colfax WA in her early 20s as she got off a train; she had a crippled baby probably named Pearl who died at about 12y; Clara lived in Alaska with her husband and left him to move to Colfax; buried in Wallace ID

No children recorded
