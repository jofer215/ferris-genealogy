WARREN FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.3.6.10 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.3.6.10) WARREN FERRIS born January 30, 1793 Vergennes, Addison, VT and died January 17, 1852, Skaneateles, Onondaga, NY. [Jim - this placement is questionable - I am not sure he is a son of James, but will leave him for now.] Warren was a miller and a native of Vergennes VT; came to Onondaga County and moved to Skaneateles, later to Ohio, thence to Cortlandt County NY and back to Skaneateles, where he died, age 60. He also lived in Mottville NY and in 1820, Marcellus, Onondaga County NY. He married May 5, 1814 Elizabeth ‘Betsey’ Vail [dau of Daniel, Jr and Sally (Bacon) Vail] born September 25, 1796 Camillus, Onandaga, NY and died November 7, 1882 Waukon, Allamakee, IA; buried there. Betsey was residing with her son, Mortimer, according to the 1860 Iowa Census; she is not listed in the 1870 Iowa Census. Born to them were:
