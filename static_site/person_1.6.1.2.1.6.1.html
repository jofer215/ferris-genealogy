WOODBRIDGE NATHAN FERRIS

ID: 1.6.1.2.1.6.1 | Source: John
(1.6.1.2.1.6.1) WOODBRIDGE NATHAN FERRIS (Photographs) born January 6, 1853 in a log cabin four miles southeast of Spencer Township, Tioga Co NY and died March 23, 1928 Washington, D.C.; interred Ferris Mausoleum, Highland View Cemetery, Big Rapids MI; attended district schools and later Spencer Academy, walking 4 miles daily to and from school in town; became principal of the latter school; left Spencer and went to Illinois where he was principal of several schools, the last 5 years of which he was superintendent of schools in Dixon IL; married 1st December 23, 1874 at her home in Fulton NY, Helen Frances Gillespie born September 7, 1853 New Haven NY and died March 23, 1917; married 2d August 14, 1921 Mary Ethel McLoud (18821954) (of Indianapolis). Unsuccessful candidate for Congress 1892; Ferris Industrial School (now Ferris State University) incorporated - 1894; Ferris Industrial School name changed to Ferris Institute - 1898; becomes President of Big Rapids Savings Bank 1902; nominated for Governor of Michigan, defeated - 1904; elected Governor of Michigan -1912; re-elected Governor of Michigan - 1914 [the Good Gray Governor]; nominated for Governor, defeated - 1920; elected US Senator from Michigan - 1922.
