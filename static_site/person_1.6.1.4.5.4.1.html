GEORGE DEWITT DAVIS

ID: 1.6.1.4.5.4.1 | Source: John
(1.6.1.4.5.4.1) GEORGE DEWITT DAVIS born May 30, 1846 Bedford, Westchester, NY and died July 15, 1910 New York City; buried Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla NY; married November 19, 1879 in NYC, Laura Isabel “Belle” Jukes [dau of John & Lydia Ann (King Dashiell Matthews) Jukes] born September 25, 1844 Cincinnati OH and died June 23, 1914 NYC; buried with George. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • LILLIAN BELLE DAVIS 1.6.1.4.5.4.1.1
  • FLORENCE MAY DAVIS 1.6.1.4.5.4.1.2
  • PEARL VERNON DAVIS 1.6.1.4.5.4.1.3
