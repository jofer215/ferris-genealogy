SAMUEL FERRIS, JR.

ID: 5.1.3.2.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.1) SAMUEL FERRIS, JR. [this Samuel is also listed in the John line - confusion exists to the correct placement.] born August 11, 1754 (1764?) Stamford (Greenwich?) CT and died (November 25?) December 24, 1818 (1842?) (age 54y) Greenwich; buried Old Sound Beach Cemetery, Greenwich (tombstone photo); served in the New York Line during the American Revolution as a Private, under Colonels Hopkins, Paulding, and Baldwin; was living at Nine Partners in Dutchess Co when he enlisted; married August 8, 1793 Phebe Ferris (5.3.4.4); 1832 he applied for and received a pension (S23216) while living at Ovid NY. DAR Patriot Index has date of birth as August 11, 1754 and death as December 24, 1842. Both Samuel and Phebe were buried in the Old Sound Beach Cemetery. Samuel probably married, second, Phoebe Sherman. (Jim - Conflict - the DAR says Samuel Jr. married Phoebe Sherman (1767-1840) in 1783.) Born to them were (probably not in birth sequence):
