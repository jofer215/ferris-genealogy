HENRY L. BOGERT

ID: 1.7.2.1.1.1.1.2 | Source: John
(1.7.2.1.1.1.1.2) HENRY L. BOGERT born January 20, 1857 and died March 5, 1933; prominent R. E. lawyer, Trustee Queens County Savings Bank for 50 years, Treasurer 1905-33, member NYC Bar, and Queen Co Bar; married October 8, 1879 Caroline Lawrence Osgood (Carrie) born April 18, 1859 and died April 17, 1924; and born to them were:
