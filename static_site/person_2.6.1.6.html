JAMES KNAPP

ID: 2.6.1.6 | Source: Peter
(2.6.1.6) JAMES KNAPP born May 15, 1727/28 Stamford CT and died there between February 17, 1803 and November 27, 1809; Revolutionary War Veteran - served May 8, 1775 to December 18, 1775, served in the Lexington Alarm; married c1760/61 at Stamford, Prudence (Prudy) {maiden name unk}she died February 24, 1790 Stamford.
