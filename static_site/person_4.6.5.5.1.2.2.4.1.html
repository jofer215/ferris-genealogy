MARY TAGGART

ID: 4.6.5.5.1.2.2.4.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.5.1.2.2.4.1) MARY TAGGART (Annette) born January 18, 1851 Dubuque IA (Black Hawk Co) and died December 30, 1901/02 Waterloo, Black Hawk, IA; buried St. Mary’s of Mount Carmel, Eagle Center IA; Mary was a rare example of Christian sacrifice and the highest type of attractive motherhood. The raising of so large a family of children and bringing them all up to be manly boys and gentle daughters is a great honor and when one thinks of the sacrifices necessary to accomplish such a task he feels like placing the tribute of love as a capsheaf to a well rounded character. Her children were her jewels and in them she lived. She moved with her parents about 1852 and lived there until she was six years old, when the family moved back to Waterloo and settled in Eagle Township. She married July 3, 1870 in Waterloo, Peter J.
