HARRIET MARIA LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.1.4.1.3.4.2 | Source: James
(5.1.4.1.3.4.2) HARRIET MARIA LOCKWOOD born April 19, 1822 CT and died February 10, 1897 Stamford; married November 21, 1848 Stamford, Alvin Weed [son of Horatio & Rachel (Jones) Weed] born December 24, 1812 Stamford and died there July 14, 1894; both buried there High Ridge Cemetery; merchant 1850-60;

No children recorded
