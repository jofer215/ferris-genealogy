JOSEPH FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.7 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.7) JOSEPH FERRIS born August, 1796 Stamford and died there March 7, 1872; buried there Long Ridge Union Cemetery; married August 16 1820 in Stamford, Sally Briggs born June 13, 1803 Stamford and died 1878 [dau of Isaac and Lucy Ann (June) Briggs] [there is some confusion here, as Sally's headstone in the cemetery on Riverbank Road, says she died January 18, 1860 at age 62 years.] ; and born to them were (not in birth order) Note: this Joseph is also listed in the (5) James line – be aware!:
