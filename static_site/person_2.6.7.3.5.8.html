CHARLES NELSON KNAPP

ID: 2.6.7.3.5.8 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.3.5.8) CHARLES NELSON KNAPP born July 10, 1815 Onondaga Co and died June 5, 1874 Toledo, Iowa; married December 25, 1845 at Aurora, Cayuga, NY Mary F. Knapp (Mary Knapp) [dau of Daniel William & Lydia (Morris) Knapp] born May 11, 1825 Warsaw, Genesee, NY, and died February 13, 1874 Toledo, Tama, IA; buried Woodlawn Cemetery, Tama Co IA. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • ALVIRA BEATTIE KNAPP 2.6.7.3.5.8.1
  • CHARLES BARTON KNAPP 2.6.7.3.5.8.2
  • HARRIET MATILDA KNAPP 2.6.7.3.5.8.3
