ABIGAIL FERRIS

ID: 3.6.1.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.1.1.2) ABIGAIL FERRIS (Abby Maria) (known as Abigail 1805) born January 1, 1775 apparently Greenwich and died September 8, 1850; was baptized August 27, 1794 at St. Johns Episcopal Church, Stamford by her mother; married John Fields

No children recorded
