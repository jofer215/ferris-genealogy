SAMUEL POOLE PECK

ID: 3.10.5.10.1.1.9.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.5.10.1.1.9.2) SAMUEL POOLE PECK born February 24, 1851 and died (killed) June 11/16, 1874 while backing his wagon u p to a store door – he had his team by the head, when the pole struck him in the face, knocking him down, the horses tramping him to death (3.10.5.10.1.1.9.3) ISABEL PECK born May 1, 1857 Flushing L.I and died March 7, 1933 Scarsdale NY; buried Flushing Cemetery, Flushing NY; married April 27, 1880 in Ravenswood, Astoria, NY Isaac Edward Ditmars [son of Edward & Elizabeth who were both UEL who fled after the Rev War to Nova Scotia and remained British subjects] born September 9, 1850 Clementsport, Nova Scotia and died February 28, 1934 Scarsdale NY; educated in NYC and employed for a time as a draftsman; architect noted for designs of Roman Catholic churches and institutional buildings; they resided in Brooklyn, then Ravenswood, then Scarsdale; see ‘Net for more info on this guy; and born to them were:

No children recorded
