MABEL CLARA SEELEY

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.1.1.3.4.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.1.1.3.4.5) MABEL CLARA SEELEY born January 6, 1893 and died July 25, 1948 Tawas City, Iosco, MI; married March 13, 1911 in Grand Rapids, Wesley Eugene Coates [son of Miner & Anna Eliza (Courter) Coates] born March 31, 1871 Ionia Co MI and died July 4, 1946 Bay City, Bay, MI; both buried Memory Gardens Cemetery, Tawas City, Iosco, MI; and they had 7 kids, 4 of which were: (3.1.2.1.4.1.1.1.3.4.5.1) ROY E COATES born April 11, 1912 Kent Co MI and died February 9, 1976 Tawas, Iosco, MI; buried Pioneer Cemetery, Iosco Co MI; farmer; married Miss Wrathell and they had 3 kids
