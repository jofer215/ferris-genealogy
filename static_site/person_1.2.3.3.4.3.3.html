SARAH BUSHBY HARRIS

ID: 1.2.3.3.4.3.3 | Source: John
(1.2.3.3.4.3.3) SARAH BUSHBY HARRIS (Photo) born 1821 London, Ontario (October 7, 1821 Port Ryerse, Norfolk, Ontario) and died 1916 (May 29, 1916 England); they moved to England 1878; 1881 resided Dilmaurs, Tormoham, Devonshire, England; married August 27, 1846 in London, Middlesex, Ontario, Robert Alexander George Dalzell [son of Robert Alexander & Andalusia (Brown) Dalzell] born August 19, 1816 Dalziel, Lanark, Scotland and died October 19, 1878 England; Hon, Lieutenant Colonel, and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • ROBERT HARRIS CARNWATH DALZELL OF CARNWATH 1.2.3.3.4.3.3.1
  • AMELIA ANDALUSIA DALZELL 1.2.3.3.4.3.3.2
  • MARY ISABELLA DALZELL, Lady, 1.2.3.3.4.3.3.3
  • ARTHUR EDWARD DALZELL 1.2.3.3.4.3.3.4
  • SHARLOTTE EMMA MAUD DALZELL, Lady, 1.2.3.3.4.3.3.5
