TILDEN TOMPKINS

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.2.2.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.2.2.1) TILDEN TOMPKINS born August 18, 1876 Putnam Valley NY and died September 23, 1953 Cold Spring NY; married 1st Alice Lavenia Hills [dau of Washington] and apparently 2d Mary. Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, Thursday, Oct. 1, 1953: On Wednesday, Sept. 23, 1953 Tilden Tompkins, 77, a well known and prominent resident of Cold Spring, died suddenly of a heart attack following his removal to Butterfield Memorial hospital there. Born in Tompkins Corners on Aug. 18, 1876, he had lived in this section all his life. He was the son of Joshua and Sarah Tompkins. He was a superintendent of estates prior to his retirement. Surviving are his wife, Mary Tompkins; one son, Chester A., and one daughter, Mrs. Joseph (Hilda) Sullivan, both of Garrison; three brothers, Augustus and Jesse, both of Rhinbeck and Frank of Mahopac Falls; two sisters, Mrs. Allan (Martha) Gilbert and Mrs. Thad (Edith) Tompkins, both of Putnam Valley and five grandchildren. Interment will be in Hillside Cemetery. Born to them were:
