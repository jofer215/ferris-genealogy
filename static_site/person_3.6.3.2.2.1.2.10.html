ELLEN M. HILL

ID: 3.6.3.2.2.1.2.10 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.2.1.2.10) ELLEN M. HILL born May 12, 1851 Swartswood NJ and died December 4, 1911 Smithfield PA; married December 10, 1873 in Stillwater NJ, John Huff born July 16, 1845 and died March 30, 1924; buried Tioga Point Cemetery, Athens PA. They lived in Stroudsburg PA, East Smithfield PA and Ulster PA. Born to them were:
