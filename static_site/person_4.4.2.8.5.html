PRISCILLA LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.2.8.5 | Source: Mary
(4.4.2.8.5) PRISCILLA LOCKWOOD (#774) born November 1803 Greenwich and died August 14, 1892 Stamford; buried there North Street Cemetery; in 1880, widowed, living in Stamford with her dau Harriet and her husband; married February 24, 1822 in Stamford, James Alfred Wilmot born 1798 CT and died June 27, 1870 Stamford; both buried North Street Cemetery; he was a stone mason 1850-60; and born to them were:
