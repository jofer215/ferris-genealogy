STEPHEN J. FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.13.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.13.2) STEPHEN J. FERRIS (1835-1892) married Frances VanNortwick (1844-1905). Note: In the 1870 census for Woodstock MI, there is a Mariah Ferris, widow, living with Stephen and Francis. This Mariah’s maiden name was Launders – could be Stephen’s mother. This Mariah died Woodstock, Lenawee County MI, June 26, 1882 at age 74 y 2 m 10 d. Born to them were:

Children (11)
