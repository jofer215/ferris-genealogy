JOHN UPTON UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.1.9.3.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.9.3.1) JOHN UPTON UNDERHILL (1827-1887) at one point resided Rahway NJ; married Balinda Catharine Ten Eyck born July 1827 NY and died 1907; and born to them were: (1.2.5.1.9.3.1.1) GEORGIA T UNDERHILL born November 16, 1851 NYC and died November 3, 1942 Rahway NJ; school teach in Rahway for 25y; Board of Education Secretary; WCTU leader

Children (3)

  • CARRIE S UNDERHILL 1.2.5.1.9.3.1.3
  • M LOUISE UNDERHILL 1.2.5.1.9.3.1.4
