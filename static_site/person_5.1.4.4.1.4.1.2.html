JANE E FRENCH

ID: 5.1.4.4.1.4.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.4.4.1.4.1.2) JANE E FRENCH born March 7, 1832 Bridgewater, Litchfield, CT and died there; buried Mountain Grove Cemetery; married November 25, 1858, as his 2d wife, Homer B Sanford [son of Glover & Elizabeth (Lake) Sanford] born June 17, 1824 Bridgewater and died there January 30, 1903; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • BERTHA SANFORD 5.1.4.4.1.4.1.2.2
