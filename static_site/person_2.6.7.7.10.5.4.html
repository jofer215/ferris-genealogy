JENNIE SHERWOOD

ID: 2.6.7.7.10.5.4 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.7.10.5.4) JENNIE SHERWOOD born April 19, 1868 Marshall Co IA and died February 22, 1942 Holdrege NE; went to Nebraska with her parents c1877 and settled on a homestead SW of Oxford NE; married March 26, 1887 in Beaver City NE, Sanford Lunbery [son of Peter J. & Christina (Parsons) Lunbery – both born in Sweden] born August 21, 1865 Cora OH and died August 12, 1936 NE; and born to them were: (2.6.7.7.10.5.4.1) ROY ELBERT LUNBERY born June 10, 1888 Stamford NE and died October 8, 1955 Grant NE; married April 19, 1910 Elsie Pheobie Renquist born June 26, 1888 and died July 12, 1955; and born to them were:
