ARTHUR LEONARD BROEFFLE

ID: 1.1.4.4.1.3.1.3.1 | Source: John
(1.1.4.4.1.3.1.3.1) ARTHUR LEONARD BROEFFLE born September 16, 1891 Prentice, Price, WI and died December 30, 1983; married April 12, 1917 in Danbury, Burnett, WI, Hilma Losilia Miller [dau of Alex B. & Ann Elizabeth (Olsson) Miller] born September 20, 1891 Pierre, Hughes, SD and died April 6, 1989; 1926, Arthur and Hilma moved back to Burnett Co WI where they lived with her parents until they bought property located about five miles north of Danbury WI. Their home was established in an abandoned house which they had moved onto the property. Their life was hard until Arthur became a school bus driver for the district. He later became a house painter and a farmer. Born to them were:
