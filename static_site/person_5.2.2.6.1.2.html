HENDRICK BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.6.1.2 | Source: James
(5.2.2.6.1.2) HENDRICK BUXTON born July 29, 1796 Fonda NY and died May 29, 1880 Montgomery Co NY; a carpenter; married 1st November 7, 1825 in Lewis Co NY, Polly Tiffany (dau of Daniel & Sally (Puffer) Tiffany] born September 17, 1805 Martinsburg NY and died June 10, 1836 (after her death, Polly’s kids were raised by her parents); married 2d c1837 in Montgomery Co NY, Alida {maiden name unk}. Born to him and Polly were: (5.2.2.6.1.2.1) ALONZO JAMES BUXTON born 1828 Fonda NY and died April 18, 1861; a civil engineer and surrogate court clerk of Lewis Co NY; he surveyed the first railroad across Canada and later surveyed the Croton Aquaduct to New York City – ill health caused by the harsh living conditions in Canada eventually caused his death
