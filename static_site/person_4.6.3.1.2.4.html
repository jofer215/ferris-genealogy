JOHN W. LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.3.1.2.4 | Source: Mary
(4.6.3.1.2.4) JOHN W. LOCKWOOD (#1430) born July 6, 1816 Turn of the River, Stamford and died December 25, 1907 Stamford; farmer 1850; married May 13, 1849 at ‘Hunting Ridge’, Stamford, Huldah J. Scofield (2.1.3.3.2.3.2) [dau of Hezron & Phebe (Holly) Scofield] born August 28, 1820 Hunting Ridge and died December 17, 1898; both buried Lockwood #5 Cemetery, Stamford CT. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • THEODORE WILLIS LOCKWOOD 4.6.3.1.2.4.2
