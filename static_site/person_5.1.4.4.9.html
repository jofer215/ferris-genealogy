GERSHOM JESSUP

ID: 5.1.4.4.9 | Source: James
(5.1.4.4.9) GERSHOM JESSUP born December 6, 1772 Greenwich and died there August 18, 1803; buried Old Sound Beach Cemetery (Tomac Ave); married January 11, 1798, Rhoda Knapp [dau of Enos & Mary (Lockwood) Knapp] born November 25, 1779 Greenwich and died there November 18, 1857; buried Episcopal Church Cemetery, Greenwich with her 2d husband. After Gershom’s death, Rhoda married Benjamin Page and they had 4 kids. Born to Gershom and Rhoda were:

Children (5)

  • BETSEY JESSUP 5.1.4.4.9.4
  • ANGELINA JESSUP 5.1.4.4.9.5
