MARIAH SHAW

ID: 3.6.6.1.5.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.6.1.5.4) MARIAH SHAW born April 17, 1820 Stamford and died there April 17, 1901; married c1838 Matthew Franklin Merritt [son of Nehemiah & Phebe] born March 2, 1815 Flushing, Queens, NY and died May 10, 1896 Stamford; both buried there Woodland Cemetery; 1850 he was a ship chief; 1880 merchant; and born to them were:
