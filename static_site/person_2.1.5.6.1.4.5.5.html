EMILY JANE SCOFIELD

ID: 2.1.5.6.1.4.5.5 | Source: Peter
(2.1.5.6.1.4.5.5) EMILY JANE SCOFIELD born January 24, 1845 Stamford and died there 1927; married June 19, 1884 in Stamford, as his 2d wife, Cyrus W. No children recorded
