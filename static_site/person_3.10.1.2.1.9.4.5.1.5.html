DAVID EDWIN DARBY

ID: 3.10.1.2.1.9.4.5.1.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.1.9.4.5.1.5) DAVID EDWIN DARBY born February 28, 1898 Clarksville, Iowa and died June 8, 1973 Freeport, Stephenson, Illinois; married September 27, 1920 in Greene IA, Ida Bertha Uker born 1903 Greene, Iowa and died there 1975 “Amy & Lucius 50th Anniversary Book says about David Edwin: "Look at his skin, It's so white and fair, And at his head, oh my! It's red curly hair!"
