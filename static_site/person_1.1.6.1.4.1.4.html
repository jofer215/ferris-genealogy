MERRITT CORNELL

ID: 1.1.6.1.4.1.4 | Source: John
(1.1.6.1.4.1.4) MERRITT CORNELL (Merritt G. Cornell) born September 10, 1823 and died December 27, 1890. Merritt began using the name Cornwell. He married, October 5, 1844, Delight Austin born March 25, 1826 and died January 8, 1909. They moved to Summerhill NY prior to 1857/8. Born to them was:
