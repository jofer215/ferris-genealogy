CARLETON F. FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.3.7.3.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.3.7.3.1) CARLETON F. FERRIS born March 21, 1828/9 Ferrisburgh VT and died April 3, 1870; buried Adams Cemetery, Panton VT; married May 1, 1866 at Ferrisburgh, Esther Lavinia Allen [dau of Norman & Ursala Allen] born June 28, 1844 Ferrisburg VT and died January 8, 1909 Swanzey; she remarried after Carleton’s death March 7, 1876 Ansel Morse

No children recorded
