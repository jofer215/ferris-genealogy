MICHAEL BOOMHAUER CLASON

ID: 2.1.3.3.1.4.9 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.1.4.9) MICHAEL BOOMHAUER CLASON born May 24, 1818 Goshen, Orange NY and died at Beaver Dam WI (died March 24, 1893 Lonoke AR); buried Forest Home Cemetery, Milwaukee WI; married 1st April 14, 1843 in Clason Prairie, Dodge Co WI, Sarah A. Phelps [dau of Benjamin & Laura] born September 10, 1825 Constableville, Lewis, NY and died January 6, 1897 Milwaukee WI; buried Forest Home Cemetery; married 2d December 28, 1885 Mary E. Mower (c1822-); lived at Beaver Dam WI; and born to him and Sarah were:
