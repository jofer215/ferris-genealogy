JOHN FERRIS STEARNS

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.4.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.4.4) JOHN FERRIS STEARNS (Photo) born August 29, 1845 Phelps, Ontario, NY and buried September 9, 1903; buried Ferris Center Cemetery, Ferris Township, Montcalm Co MI; married October 3, 1871 Mary Elizabeth Gee [dau of Christopher & Eliza (Hooper) Gee] born December 31, 1853 Orange, Cuyahoga, OH and died May 14, 1942; settled in Sumner MI. After John’s death, Mary married Erastust Osterhoust. Born to John and Mary were:
