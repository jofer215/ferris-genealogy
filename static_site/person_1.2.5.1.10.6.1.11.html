EFFIE ARVILLA SUTTON

ID: 1.2.5.1.10.6.1.11 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.10.6.1.11) EFFIE ARVILLA SUTTON born April 3, 1875 Stamford CT and died before 1953; married apparently 1st William Bouton born November 3, 1882 Stamford; 2d November 2, 1896 in Sound Beach CT, Arthur Joseph Evelyn Reynolds [son of John & Marie] born January 18, 1872 Southport, Lancashire, England and died 1915; buried First Congregational Church, Old Greenwich CT; and born to them were:

Children (2)

  • CONSUELO MARIE REYNOLDS 1.2.5.1.10.6.1.11.2
