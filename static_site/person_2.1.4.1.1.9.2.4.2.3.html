PATRICIA MIZENER

ID: 2.1.4.1.1.9.2.4.2.3 | Source: Peter
(2.1.4.1.1.9.2.4.2.3) PATRICIA MIZENER (Photos) (adopted) born September 22, 1923 Sioux City IA and died September 28, 1997 Downey CA; married September 20, 1942 at Nogales AZ, John Robert Miller [son of Robert Theodore & Edith Mildred (Hubbard) Miller] born May 27, 1921 in St. Paul MN. She graduated from the Brown School for Girls of Glendora CA in 1942. John graduated from Downey High School, Downey CA 1940; Long Beach Junior College. He is a WWII veteran having served in the Army Air Corps as a C-47 pilot (Captain) and flew in the South Pacific, spending a good deal of time in Australia, Guam and the Phillippines. He received the Distinguished Service Cross and the Air Medal with several Oak Leaf Clusters. He was an aerospace engineer. Born to them were:
