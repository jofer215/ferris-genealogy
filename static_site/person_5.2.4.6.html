JESSE BELL

ID: 5.2.4.6 | Source: James
(5.2.4.6) JESSE BELL born March 6, 1745/46 Stamford/Darien CT and died 1834 Milton NY; married 1st November 8, 1767 in Stamford, Comfort Garnsey [dau of Jonathan & Deborah (Finch) Garnsey] born c1748 Stamford and died there March 31, 1772; married 2d January 25, 1773 in Stamford, Mary Scofield (2.5.3.2.4); married 3d after 1777, Sarah {maiden name unk}. Jesse served in the Connecticut Line 1775, as a Sergeant in 1776, and was appointed Lieutenant and in 1777 was made Captain; and born to him and Comfort were: (5.2.4.6.1) WILLIAM BELL born May 18, 1769 Stamford; married c1794 in Poughkeepsie NY, Sarah Grinnell
