ANDREW FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.8 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.8) ANDREW FERRIS born January 3, 1779 Greenwich CT and died June 15, 1849 Cincinnati OH; buried Ferris Family Cemetery, Mariemont, Hamilton, OH; moved to Ohio 1799/1800 and settled at Columbia c1802. The farm he owned was located between Linwood and Madisonville in the Miami River bottoms. The Andrew Ferris home was later the site of the Cincinnati Children's Home and a handsome colonial mansion. The property changed hands many times and at the time it burned, circa 1906, it was an exclusive country club known as The Pillars. He married Ann Ferris (5.1.3.8.1); they both were buried in the same Ohio cemetery with the other members of the family.
