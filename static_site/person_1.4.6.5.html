ELIZABETH FERRIS

ID: 1.4.6.5 | Source: John
(1.4.6.5) ELIZABETH FERRIS married October 16, 1778 John White. John bought from Sarah Ferris (his mother-in-law) executrix of will of her husband, Benjamin Ferris, dated November 25, 1777 all land of Benjamin Ferris in Westchester for 1000 pounds, reserving to the Ferris heirs the burying ground, deed dated February 10, 1797, but recorded May 2, 1848.
