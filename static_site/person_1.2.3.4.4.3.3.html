ISAAC SECOR

ID: 1.2.3.4.4.3.3 | Source: John
(1.2.3.4.4.3.3) ISAAC SECOR born January 8, 1815 Newcastle NY and died August 14, 1899 Ossining NY; married 1st November 8, 1837 Rebecca C Reynolds [dau of Andrew & Susanna (Washburn) Reynolds] born November 26, 1815 and died May 25, 1859; married 2d December 26, 1860 in Brooklyn NY, Ann Maria Reynolds born February 12, 1829 and died February 25, 1905 – sister of Rebecca; and born to him and Ann were:

Children (2)

  • REBECCA REYNOLDS SECOR 1.2.3.4.4.3.3.1
  • GEORGE FISHER SECOR 1.2.3.4.4.3.3.2
