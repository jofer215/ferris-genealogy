CHARLES MUNSON BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.2.9.3.6 | Source: James
(5.2.2.2.9.3.6) CHARLES MUNSON BUXTON born June 17, 1842 Stamford CT and died there December 28, 1923; shoe factory worker 1880; married March (November) 26, 1872 at Stamford, as her 2d husband, Sarah M. Scofield [dau of Ebenezer Bell & Abigail (Crabbe) Scofield] born 1849 Stamford. Her first husband was George E. Seeley.

No children recorded
