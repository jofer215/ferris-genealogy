WILLIAM ROSE

ID: 3.1.6.8.12.1.1.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.12.1.1.1) WILLIAM ROSE born May 27, 1872 Ross Township, Renfrew Co and died June 3, 1940 Pembroke, Renfrew Co; buried Smith Falls, Lanark Co, Ontario; a Railroader; married June 17, 1903 in Haley Station, Renfrew Co, Mary Alice Jeffery [dau of Joseph and Eliza Jane (Guest) Jeffery.] born February 7, 1882 Haley Station and died October 14, 1939 Smith Falls, Lanark, Ontario; buried Smiths Falls, Lanark Co; and born to them were:
