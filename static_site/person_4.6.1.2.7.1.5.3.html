HARRIET KEELER

ID: 4.6.1.2.7.1.5.3 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.7.1.5.3) HARRIET KEELER born 1880 Traer, Tama, IA and died October 31, 1943; buried Evergreen Cemetery, Seattle WA; graduated Iowa State Normal School, Cedar Falls 1900; taught piano there for a year. Married September 10, 1902 in Cedar Falls J. Ralph Magee born c1880 Maquoketa IA and died December 19, 1970 Morton Grove IL; buried with Harriet; graduated from Iowa State Normal School, 1902; attended Morningside College and Boston University School of Theology; ordained 1904 and served several churches in Iowa and Massachusetts before becoming pastor of the First Methodist Church in Seattle 1921; district superintendent for the Seattle district 1929; elected bishop 1932 and assigned to St. Paul MN area; Interim President of Hamline University, 1933-34; resident bishop for Iowa and South Dakota 1939-44; resident bishop of the Methodist Church in the Chicago area 1944 to retirement 1952 and lived in Evanston IL after retirement; and born to them were:

Children (2)

  • JOHN HOMER MAGEE 4.6.1.2.7.1.5.3.1
  • DOROTHY MAGEE 4.6.1.2.7.1.5.3.2
