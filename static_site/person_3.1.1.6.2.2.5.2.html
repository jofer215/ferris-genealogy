RUTH ABIGAIL HAMMEL

ID: 3.1.1.6.2.2.5.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.2.2.5.2) RUTH ABIGAIL HAMMEL born June 17, 1848 Dearborn Co IN and died March 17, 1874 Blocher, Scott, IN; married November 14, 1869 in Blocher, Samuel Alexander Toombs [son of William P. & Martha Ann (Monroe) Toombs] born February 23, 1841 Scott Co IN and died March 19, 1875 Blocher; both buried there Bethel Blocher Methodist Church Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (2)

  • MINNIE ALICE TOOMBS 3.1.1.6.2.2.5.2.1
  • WILLIAM ALVAREDO TOOMBS 3.1.1.6.2.2.5.2.3
