STEPHEN CONKLIN CHADEAYNE

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.4.2 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.4.2) STEPHEN CONKLIN CHADEAYNE born November 5, 1826 on the farm, Tarrytown NY and died February 4, 1878; buried Dale Cemetery, Ossining NY; a Civil War Veteran serving as a Private in Company I., 6th New York Heavy Artillery; a carpenter; married October 2, 1850 at Ossining, Fanny Gregory Truesdell [dau of John Phillips & Catherine (Palmer) Truesdell] born April 16, 1830 Ossining NY and died there March 19, 1892. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • FANNY CHADEAYNE died young 1.2.5.4.2.1.4.2.3
