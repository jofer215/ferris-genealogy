MARIA CHAPMAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.6.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.6.1) MARIA CHAPMAN born June 12 (November 29), 1807 and died November 29, 1883; married February 14, 1830 Matthias Croft [son of John & Mary (Meeks)] born July 26, 1809 Putnam Valley and died December 29, 1898 Peekskill; both buried Hillside Cemetery. Matthias was a farmer, residing in Croft’s Corners, in the town of Putnam Valley. After the death of Maria and the dispersal of his family, he resided with his daughter, Mrs. Strang, near Peekskill; and born to them were:
