DAVID FROST

ID: 1.2.3.7.2.3 | Source: John
(1.2.3.7.2.3) DAVID FROST born December 1, 1766 Carmel, Putnam, NY and died there January 8, 1818; buried there Baptist Church Cemetery; married c1793 in Carmel, Lydia Washburn born December 17, 1767 NY and died December 11, 1816 Carmel. Born to them were: (1.2.3.7.2.3.1) JAMES FROST born March 30, 1797 Carmel NY and died July 30, 1836 IN; buried Baptist Church Cemetery, Carmel; married 1820 in Carmel, Sophia Kelley [dau of Josiah & Mehitable (Cole) Kelley] born December 13, 1800 Putnam Co NY and died 1867 Fayatte Co IN; and born to them were:

Children (1)
