JULIA ANN ANGELINA WANZER

ID: 1.2.5.4.4.5.3.11 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.4.5.3.11) JULIA ANN ANGELINA WANZER born April 8, 1868 IN and died May 25, 1950 Los Angeles CA; married February 15, 1885 in Knox Co IN, Henry Clay Mosier [son of Christian & Sophronia (Blair) Mosier] born February 19, 1860 Georgetown, Floyd, IN and died February 14, 1943 Enid, Garfield, OK; buried Cherryvale Cemetery, Southard, Blaine, OK; the family emigrated in covered wagon from Indiana to OK Territory c1900; and born to them were:

Children (7)

  • HENRY EARL MOSIER 1.2.5.4.4.5.3.11.2

  • ALVIN PAUL MOSIER 1.2.5.4.4.5.3.11.4
  • EPHRONA LINCOLN MOSIER 1.2.5.4.4.5.3.11.5
