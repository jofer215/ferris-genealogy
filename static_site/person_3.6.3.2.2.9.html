WILLIAM HILL, JR.,

ID: 3.6.3.2.2.9 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.2.9) WILLIAM HILL, JR., (William G) born September 14, 1805, near Newton, Walpack Township, Sussex County NJ and died June 18, 1842, Vicksburg, Kalamazoo County MI; buried Hill Cemetery [now called Mint Cemetery], Park Township, St. Joseph County MI. He married, May 10, 1828 at Newton, Sussex County NJ, Margaret Staley [dau of Abraham & Elizabeth (Hammon) Staley] born May 7, 1808, Newton, Sussex County NJ and died April 3, 1889, Vicksburg, Kalamazoo County MI; buried Hill Cemetery. After William’s death, Margaret remarried, to James Grovenberg. Born to William and Margaret were:
