ISRAEL B.

ID: 5.1.3.1.1.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.1.4) ISRAEL B. (BENJAMIN?) FERRIS born March 9, 1772 and died July 5, 1836; buried at the Tuthill-Ferris Cemetery, Smyrna NY; married 1st June 23, 1797 in Dutchess Co NY, Amy Bently [maybe Amy died after childbirth of her daughter Amy?]; married 2d February 3, 1805 in Chenango Co NY, Thankful Wood. According to the family Bible, the family moved from Westchester Co NY between 1797-1800 to Smyrna, Chenango, NY; and born to Israel and Amy were:
