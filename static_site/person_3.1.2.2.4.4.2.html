ID: 3.1.2.2.4.4.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.4.4.2) JOSEPH FERRIS “Ziba” born 1824 Smyrna NY and died there April 14, 1865 of typhoid fever (age 41y); buried at the Ferris Cemetery about 3 miles west of the village of Smyrna, Chenango Co NY; always addressed as Ziba; farmer; married December 25, 1847 Augusta Caroline Alcott born May 19, 1825 and died February 9, 1881; buried with Ziba. [there appears to be some confusion about the placement of this Joseph Z - there is also a Joseph Z with the same year of birth and death in the (5) James line - with a different wife.]

No children recorded
