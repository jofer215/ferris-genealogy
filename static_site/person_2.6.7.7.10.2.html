WILLIAM HANNEL SHERWOOD

ID: 2.6.7.7.10.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.7.10.2) WILLIAM HANNEL SHERWOOD born March 14, 1817 Washington, Morris, NJ and died August 5, 1904, Tama, Tama, IA; served with Company E 6 th Iowa Cav; 6th Iowa Cav was in service between 1863-65 and saw action against the Sioux; married September 1852 at Staten Island NY, Adalaid E. Stonebank born 1830 England and died between July 1890-July 1889 Tama IA. Born to them were:
