IDA E. FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.2.4.1.6.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.4.1.6.4) IDA E. FERRIS born 1864 and died January 7, 1906 Verona MI; married 1886 George N. Andrew; from the Flint Journal, November 30, 1928: George N. Andrews, 65 years old, of 1706 Concord St., died yesterday at Hurley Hospital, from a complication of diseases of one year. He born in Canada, September 25, 1860, son of Mr. & Mrs. John Andrew. He lived at Bad Axe for 40 years where he married Ida E. Ferris in September, 1886 who died in 1906. He came to Flint 15 years ago. He leaves 3 daughters, Mrs. Guy N. French, Mrs. Hoy Pugh, and Miss Edith Andrew, all of Flint; 3 sons, J. E. Andrew and Harry J., both of Flint, and Glen B., of Des Moines IA; 3 sisters, Mrs. Seth Reed, Mrs. F.B. Armstrong, and Mrs. George Davis, all of Flint; 2 brothers, Charles H. Andrew of Flint, and John of Des Moines, and 8 grandchildren. He was a member of the Trinity Methodist Episcopal church in Flint. Born to them were:

Children (6)
