WALTER CHANDLER KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.3.7.10.8.1.7 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.7.10.8.1.7) WALTER CHANDLER KNAPP born March 30, 1906 probably Orange Co NY; married June 23, 1926 at Newburgh, Lillian C. Haas born November 7, 1907. Lillian remarried in 1931 to Frederick W. Bosarth. It is unknown if Walter died and she remarried or if they divorced.

No children recorded
