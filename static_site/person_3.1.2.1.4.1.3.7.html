GEORGE PORTER GODFREY

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.7 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.3.7) GEORGE PORTER GODFREY born January 6, 1821 Bloomingburg, Sullivan, NY and died 1905 in Elkhart, Elkhart, IN – at the home of his son; educated at Newburgh Academy and in 1845 emigrated to MI where he bought several parcels of land – lived first in Quakertown, then Springport before spending his declining year in Elkhart; farmer 1850-80; married March 7, 1843 in Batavia NY, Eunice Coy born June 27, 1824 Rochester, Monroe, NY and died March 1899 Springport, Jackson, MI; and born to them were:
