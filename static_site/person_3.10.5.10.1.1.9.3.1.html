HAROLD EDWARD DITMARS

ID: 3.10.5.10.1.1.9.3.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.5.10.1.1.9.3.1) HAROLD EDWARD DITMARS born January 24, 1883 Hoboken NJ and died June 29, 1952 Brooklyn; graduated from Philips-Exeter and from Harvard 1906; from then to 1919 he was employed in architectural and engineering works in and near NY; then joined the Northern Dock Co, Brooklyn as general pier superintendent until retiring 1932; served as Secretary of Holland Society of NY; served during WWI with the US Shipping Board and was a Corporal in Company F, 10th Regiment, Police Reserve; married April 30, 1912 in Brooklyn, Gertrude Martense Vanderveeer [dau of Edward Bennet & Helen Van Brunt (Prince) Vanderveer] born October 26, 1889 Flatbush and died January 14, 1952 Brooklyn; both buried Flushing Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (2)

  • EDWARD BENNETT VANDERVEER DITMARS 3.10.5.10.1.1.9.3.1.1
  • MARJORIE ISABEL DITMARS 3.10.5.10.1.1.9.3.1.2
