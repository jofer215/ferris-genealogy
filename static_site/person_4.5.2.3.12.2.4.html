CHARLES MELVILLE LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.5.2.3.12.2.4 | Source: Mary
(4.5.2.3.12.2.4) CHARLES MELVILLE LOCKWOOD (#2218) born August 22, 1845 South Norwalk and died there July 8, 1885, being struck by a locomotive on the New York and New Haven Railroad; 2 weeks after the birth of their last child; married Maria Frances Tuttle [dau of William & Maria Frances (Ward) Tuttle] born March 17, 1848. Born to them were:
