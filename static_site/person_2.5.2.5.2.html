SARAH HAIT

ID: 2.5.2.5.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.5.2) SARAH HAIT (#3025) (Hoyt) born February 22, 1738/9 Stamford and died after 1792 Dutchess County NY. She married 1758 Charles Stewart [son of James & Sarah (Clason) Stewart] born May 18, 1733 Stamford and died 1796 South Westerlo NY. Charles served in 1781 as a Corporal. Born to them were:

Children (6)

  • MARY STEWART 2.5.2.5.2.1
  • CHARLES STEWART 2.5.2.5.2.2
  • SARAH STEWART 2.5.2.5.2.3
  • FREDERICK STEWART 2.5.2.5.2.4
  • LYDIA STEWART 2.5.2.5.2.6
  • WILLIAM STEWART 2.5.2.5.2.7
