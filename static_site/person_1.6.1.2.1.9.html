FAYETTE FERRIS

ID: 1.6.1.2.1.9 | Source: John
(1.6.1.2.1.9) FAYETTE FERRIS (FAY) born c1830 Spencer NY and died June 17, 1880 Catlin Hill, Owego, Tioga, NY; buried at the Forest Home Cemetery, Waverly NY. In the 1860 census, was a laborer. He married 1st c1850 Julia Kurtendall [dau of Jacob & Nancy (Cole) Cuykendall] born January 1832/4 and died August 6, 1872; buried in Forest Home Cemetery, Waverly NY; 2d Mary Jane Catlin, widow with 2 sons, Verne and Will.
