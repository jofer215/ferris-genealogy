JOHN FERRIS SEAMAN

ID: 1.4.7.7.4 | Source: John
(1.4.7.7.4) JOHN FERRIS SEAMAN [was named Walter, but changed his name] born March 22, 1801 NYC and died May 26, 1872. He married Anna Drake; no offspring. (1.4.7.7.5) (JAMES) VALENTINE SEAMAN, M.D. born September 30, 1802 NYC and died there March 29, 1899; married 1838 Anna Amelia FERRIS (1.4.7.6.7) and born to them were: (1.4.7.7.5.1) J. (JOHN?) VERNON SEAMAN married Christina E. Barry [also shown to have married a Mrs. Gun; may be a second marriage]

No children recorded
