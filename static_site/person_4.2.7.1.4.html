MESSENGER LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.2.7.1.4 | Source: Mary
(4.2.7.1.4) MESSENGER LOCKWOOD (#618) born August 26, 1765 Greenwich and died January 29, 1849. He entered the Town or Coast Guards at Greenwich, about January 1781, in Captain Timothy Lockwood’s company and again, for nine months ending April 1, 1782 and was employed guarding homes and towns from the British and Tories; listed as Rev War pensioner residing in Greenwich 1840; Messenger married October 1, 1796 in Stamford, Sarah Ferris (5.1.1.3.2).
