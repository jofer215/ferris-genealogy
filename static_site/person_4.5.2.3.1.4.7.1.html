VELMA ELETHEA SKINNER

ID: 4.5.2.3.1.4.7.1 | Source: Mary
(4.5.2.3.1.4.7.1) VELMA ELETHEA SKINNER born September 4, 1855 Susquehanna PA and died February 23, 1906 Chicago IL; U of Illinois B.L., 1895; M.L. 1901; teacher 1877-82; teacher Dom Sci 1897-1905; author of numerous poems; married August 23, 1882 in Champaign IL, Walter Payson Ward [son of George Austin & Susan Uhl (Bailey) Ward] born February 11, 1849 Marietta OH and died October 12, 1913 Webb City, Jasper, MO; both were graduates U of Illinois 1877; he was admitted to the bar 1879 and practiced his profession in Spencer IA until 1898 when he retired and engaged in farming and real estate; and born to them were;
