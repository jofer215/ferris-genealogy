DEAN FRANKLIN BOARDMAN

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.6.6.3.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.6.6.3.1.2) DEAN FRANKLIN BOARDMAN born January 2, 1926 New Haven Township, Huron Co OH; married 1st June 20, 1948 at North Fairfield, Huron, OH, Lois Marie Fife born December 21, 1928; married 2d February 7, 1971, Shirley Ann Frietchen Witchie born September 7, 1934. Dean was schooled in North Fairfield OH; during WWII he served with the Navy; after service he attended Ashland College OH; then worked for the Shelby Insurance Mutual Insurance Co, retiring as a Vice-President. He was always active in sports and received awards for his contributions in that field. He was also active in civic organizations and served organizations in the insurance field. Born to them were:
