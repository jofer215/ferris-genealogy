JOHN W.LEEDS

ID: 5.2.2.6.6.1.1 | Source: James
(5.2.2.6.6.1.1) JOHN W.LEEDS (John N) born 1828 CT and died March 8, 1867 Stamford; buried there Newfield Cemetery; farmer 1850 (5.2.2.6.7) SARAH BUXTON born February 2, 1781 Stamford CT and died November 30, 1851 Darien CT; buried Noroton Cemetery, Darien; married October 20, 1805 in Stamford, Albert Clock [son of Nathaniel & Sarah (How) Clock] born December 20, 1773 Stamford and died there May 8, 1833; buried Noroton Cemetery, Darien CT.
