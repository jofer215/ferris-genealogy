RICHARD DE MILLE

ID: 2.6.5.2.1.1.2.1.2.2.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.5.2.1.1.2.1.2.2.3) RICHARD DE MILLE born 1922 to William C. & Lorna Moon. To avoid a family scandal, Cecil agreed to adopt Richard. When he began to display the de Mille family features, some thought the Richard was actually Cecil’s out-of-wedlock son and the adoption was a ruse to deflect scandal off Cecil. Richard always knew he was adopted, but he was not told the truth about his real parents until his biological father died in 1955. He later researched the family trees of his mother’s parents and published a book on the subject in 1998, My Secret Mother Lorna Moon, from which information and photos have been extracted

No children recorded
