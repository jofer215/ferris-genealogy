JOHN PALMER

ID: 3.1.3.10.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.3.10.5) JOHN PALMER born c1778 in either New York or Vermont and died c1856 Stockholm, St. Lawrence, NY (There is a question if John is a son of Asa Palmer; will leave here for now pending further research.); married 1st Mattie (Martha) (may have been a Palmer) born c1778/80 and died c1825/29 Stockholm NY; married 2d c1826/29 Anna {maiden name unk}born c1789 and died after 1860 Sumpter, Sauk, WI; and born to them was (and probably more, possibly Ebenezer and/or Betsey):

Children (2)
