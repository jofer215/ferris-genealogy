ABIGAIL KNAPP

ID: 2.6.4.7.7 | Source: Peter
(2.6.4.7.7) ABIGAIL KNAPP born April 25, 1787 Stamford and died there October 27, 1872; buried North Street Cemetery; married February 2, 1812 at Stamford, David Hoyt (2.5.2.4.5.8) – see him for offspring (2.6.5) CHARLES KNAPP (8M0K-74) (ENSIGN) born May 9, 1703, Stamford and died there, September 27, 1773. He was a Veteran of the French and Indian Wars. He married, June 17, 1731, at Stamford, Bethiah Weed (1708-1771) [dau of Jonas & Bethiah (Hobby) Weed]. Born to them were:

No children recorded
