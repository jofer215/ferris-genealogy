ANN LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.1.4.4 | Source: James
(5.1.4.4) ANN LOCKWOOD (#504) born 1738 and died April 14, 1825 (or 1730-1800) Greenwich; buried Tomac Avenue Cemetery, Old Greenwich; married Jonathan Jessup Jr of Stamford CT born September 12, 1734 Greenwich and died there April 22, 1805; buried Old Sound Beach Cemetery, with Ann; and born to them were [all baptized in the St. John's Episcopal Church, presumably in Greenwich]:

Children (11)

  • ANNA JESSUP 5.1.4.4.1
  • SAMUEL JESSUP 5.1.4.4.10
  • TIMOTHY JESSUP 5.1.4.4.11
  • SARAH JESSUP 5.1.4.4.12
  • JONATHAN JESSUP, JR., 5.1.4.4.2
  • JAMES JESSUP 5.1.4.4.3
  • EBENEZER JESSUP 5.1.4.4.4
  • PETER JESSUP 5.1.4.4.5
  • EDWARD JESSUP was baptized October 28, 1767 and died June 6, 1833 NYC; buried Old 5.1.4.4.6
  • HANNAH JESSUP 5.1.4.4.8
  • GERSHOM JESSUP 5.1.4.4.9
