FRANCES PELL

ID: 1.4.7.3.1.2.2.1 | Source: John
(1.4.7.3.1.2.2.1) FRANCES PELL born NY; married October 14, 1905 Major Martin Archer Shee [son of Martin Archer & Edith Dennistoun (Pell) Shee] born May 5, 1873 Amoy, China and died January 6, 1935 Ascot, Surrey, UK; 1901 Royal Naval scholar; Lieutenant Colonel Sir Martin Archer Shee, CMG (1900) of Ashurst Lodge, Sunninghill, Berks, Member of Parliment for central Finsbury 1910-19, and for Finsbury 1918-23; Lieutenant Colonel Res of Offrs, late 19th Hussars, formerly Midshipman Royal Navy, served in South African War 1899-1902, wounded, despatches thrice, Brevet and DSO), and in WWI, 19th Royal Hussars, 1914-15, wounded, commanded 12th Battalion Gloucester Regiment 1915-17, 2/4th York and Lancaster Regiment, 1917, 10th K.O.S.B 1918 (despatches 4 times); and born to them were:

Children (7)
