STEPHEN STALEY HILL

ID: 3.6.3.2.2.4.3.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.2.4.3.2) STEPHEN STALEY HILL born September 4, 1845 Sussex Co NJ and died there April 8, 1917; probably a farmer and lived at Middleville NJ, about 2-3 miles from Stillwater and is said to have attended the Harmony Methodist Church there; married October 24, 1868 in Stillwater Township NJ, Angeline Huff [dau of Andrew & Sophia (Ervey) Huff] (1847-1920); both buried Old Stillwater Cemetery, Stillwater NJ; and born to them were:
