WILLIAM OLIVER NEWMAN

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.4.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.4.4) WILLIAM OLIVER NEWMAN born October 2, 1873 Tower Hill IA and died September 15, 1936 Jonesboro, Craighead, AR; married July 31, 1901 in Manchester IA, Clara Jane Stimson [dau of Jason & Mary Ette (Simmons) Stimson] born November 14, 1873 Prairie Township, Delaware Co IA and died August 29, 1966 Newport, Poinsett, AR; both buried Oak Grove Cemetery, Weiner AR; – they moved to Arkansas before 1920 - and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • CLARA LOUISE NEWMAN 3.11.1.1.3.2.4.4.2
  • PAUL WILLIAM NEWMAN 3.11.1.1.3.2.4.4.3
  • CLAIRE ERWIN NEWMAN 3.11.1.1.3.2.4.4.4
  • VIOLA MAY NEWMAN 3.11.1.1.3.2.4.4.5
