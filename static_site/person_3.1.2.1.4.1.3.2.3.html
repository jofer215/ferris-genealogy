JAMES DOUGLAS GODFREY

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.3) JAMES DOUGLAS GODFREY born c1843 Genesee Co NY and died after 1910 Chagrin Falls, Cuyahoga, NY; as a child he went to OH to live with his Douglas grandparents, who lived in Troy OH; in 1878 he and his family moved to a farm near Auburn Corners and later to Welshfield OH and his declining years were spent in Chagrin Falls; married c1869 in Troy OH, Harriet E Harrington [dau of Zilba & Charlotte (Bascom) Harrington] born c1847 Troy OH; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • ARTHUR GODFREY 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.3.1
  • JOHN NEWTON GODFREY 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.3.2
  • JAMIE GODFREY 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.3.3
  • MABELLE EDITH GODFREY 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.3.4
