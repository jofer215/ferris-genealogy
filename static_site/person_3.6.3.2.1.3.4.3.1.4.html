VIVIAN F. SMITH

ID: 3.6.3.2.1.3.4.3.1.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.1.3.4.3.1.4) VIVIAN F. SMITH (twin) born June 24, 1910 Phillipstown, Putnam, NY and died June 25, 1981 Fishkill NY; interred Fishkill Rural Cemetery; married November 2, 1931 in Brooklyn, Henry Stanley Hansen. Vivian resided in Fishkill for 65 years; she was a member of the Protection Engine Company Auxiliary; the Reformed Church of Fishkill; the Reformed Church Women and the Y.P.A. of the First Reformed Church.
