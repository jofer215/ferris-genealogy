LEROY MERLE FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.9.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.9.1.1) LEROY MERLE FERRIS born December 6, 1913 Fabius MO and died January 23, 1964 (1982?) Windsor MO; married three times: 1st Lavina Taylor whom he divorced; 2d Pauline {maiden name unk} whom he divorced; and 3d January 20, 1950 Elena Marie Rodriquez born January 11, 1915 [dau of John Alfred & Mary Juanita (Davis) Rodriquez]; 1935 resided Brasher MO. Made it through high school, but the depression prevented him from going on to college. 6' tall, 185 lbs. Born to them was:
