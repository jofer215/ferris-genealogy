DANIEL P. BEACH

ID: 3.1.2.1.1.2.10 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.1.2.10) DANIEL P. BEACH born February 2, 1825 West Burlington PA and died January 18, 1897 at his home in East Smithfield, Bradford, PA; buried Turnpike Cemetery, Milan, Bradford, PA; his will was probated at Bradford Co PA February 25, 1897; married 1st Lucinda Marion Ballard (Elsie) born October 11, 1825 PA and died February 26, 1860 age 35y 2m 15d; buried at The Hilton Cemetery, Troy Township, Bradford Co PA; married 2d Elsie L. Dibble (1846-1912). Daniel's obit says he was a veteran of Company E, 52d Pennsylvania Volunteers, enlisting October 29, 1861; serving 3 years and was in the 7 days battle in front of Richmond. He reenlisted and served until the Civil War was over and was discharged July 12, 1865. Born to Daniel and Lucinda were:
