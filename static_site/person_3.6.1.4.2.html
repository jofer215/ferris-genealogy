SAMUEL FERRIS WHITING

ID: 3.6.1.4.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.1.4.2) SAMUEL FERRIS WHITING born 1774 Greenwich and died March 19, 1817; married Jeanette/Janet (Stuart) Dickinson, (1763-1845) [dau of James Stuart of Aberdeen, Scotland]; was a captain of a boat in the coastal trade; lived in an old house on the Mianus River. When Samuel died, he left the home to his son, Samuel. Jeanette’s two nieces, Helen and Margaret Murray, became her sisters-in-law when they married her husband’s two brothers. Born to them were:

Children (5)

  • SAMUEL WHITING 3.6.1.4.2.2
