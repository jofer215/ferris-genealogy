MARY VANDERBILT LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.1.2.4.6.2.2 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.4.6.2.2) MARY VANDERBILT LOCKWOOD (#3371) born September 23, 1859 Brooklyn NY; married July 2, 1883 at Tarrytown NY, Joseph Arthur Chanute [son of Octave & Annie R. (James) Chanute] born February 6, 1858 Peoria IL and died February 20, 1895 Denver CO; Yale graduate; soon after his graduation he settled in Leadville CO, where he carried on the business of assaying for a time, but being successful in some mining operations he gave up his office and devoted himself exclusively for several years to mining; 1886 he became interested in the construction of what is now the plant of Globe Smelting and Refining Company, located in one of the suburbs of Denver; made Vice President 1889 and took an active part in its management; eventually to become the largest individual smelter of its kind in the country; and born to them were (one son died before his father):

Children (3)

  • LOUIS LOCKWOOD CHANUTE 4.6.1.2.4.6.2.2.1
  • WILLIAM OCTAVE CHANUTE 4.6.1.2.4.6.2.2.2
  • MATIE CHANUTE 4.6.1.2.4.6.2.2.3
