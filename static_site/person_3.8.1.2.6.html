PETER FERRIS

ID: 3.8.1.2.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.8.1.2.6) PETER FERRIS born January 29, 1777 Fairfield CT; married 1796/97 in VT, Olive Mervin born c1774; resided in Westchester Co NY for a number of years; apparently moved to Wells Township, Bradford Co PA where he appears on the assessment list of 1829, which means he may have arrived in 1828. He was listed as under age 70 (spelled Faris) in the 1830 census.
