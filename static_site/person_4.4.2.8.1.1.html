LEWIS LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.2.8.1.1 | Source: Mary
(4.4.2.8.1.1) LEWIS LOCKWOOD (#1480) born August 12, 1817 (1815), Greenwich CT and died in 1904; buried Woodland Cemetery, Stamford; resided at Shippan Point, Stamford for a number of years; in 1888, he resided at Riverside CT. He married first December 28, 1840 in NYC, Jane Mackie born October 30, 1816, Aberdeen, Scotland and died February 13, 1864, Shippan, Stamford. Lewis married, second, April 12, 1865 at NYC, Mary Jane Fullerton [dau of Robert & Elizabeth] born March 1, 1830, Londonderry, Ireland. Born to Lewis and Jane were: (4.4.2.8.1.1.1) JAMES LEWIS LOCKWOOD (#2399) born October 30, 1841, Shippan, Stamford CT and died there 1896; buried Woodland Cemetery, Stamford; married March 1871 Eliza Fullerton [dau of Robert & Elizabeth].
