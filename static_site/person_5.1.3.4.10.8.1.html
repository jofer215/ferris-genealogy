B FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.8.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.8.1) B FERRIS born December 29, 1865 Franklin Co IN and died June 30, 1940; married February 20, 1887 in Franklin Co, Grace B. Curry [dau of Milton Marion & Ellen (Brooks) Curry] born September 23, 1867 Franklin Co IN and died January 30, 1941; resided in Laurel IN; farmer in Laurel Township; both buried Metamora Cemetery, Franklin Co IN.
