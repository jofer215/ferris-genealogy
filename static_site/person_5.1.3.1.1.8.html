JOSEPH FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.1.8 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.1.8) JOSEPH FERRIS born 1792 and died June 11, 1864; buried at the Tuthill-Ferris Cemetery, Smyrna NY [there is a conflict re placement here - this Joseph is also listed in the (3) Joseph line.]; married Sally Wood who died October 18, 1868 age 75y 4m 14d; buried with her husband.
