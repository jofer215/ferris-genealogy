EDWIN BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.2.9.5 | Source: James
(5.2.2.2.9.5) EDWIN BUXTON born 1809 (1806) Stamford CT and died there after 1883; a shoemaker and farmer; married March 5, 1836 in New Canaan (Stamford) CT, Sarah Jessup [dau of Jonathan & Martha (Scofield) Jessup] born October 6/7, 1806 Strawberry Hill, Stamford CT and died April 19, 1887 Stamford; buried Turn of River Cemetery. Born to them were:
