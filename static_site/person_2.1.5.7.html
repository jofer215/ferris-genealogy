JOHN PURDY

ID: 2.1.5.7 | Source: Peter
(2.1.5.7) JOHN PURDY (PARDEE) born May 28, 1737 Stamford CT and died April 26, 1808 Cortland, Westchester, NY; married January 23, 1766 in Stamford, Sarah Webb [dau of Joseph and Elizabeth (Starr) Webb III] born May 23, 1738 Stamford and died 1821 Cortland. John was a Private in Captain Phineas Bradley’s Company of Artillery Guards, State of Connecticut from April 3, 1780 to January 1, 1781. Born to John and Sarah were:

Children (6)

  • MARY PARDEE 2.1.5.7.2
  • JOHN PARDEE 2.1.5.7.3
  • ELIZABETH PARDEE 2.1.5.7.4
  • EZRA WEBB PARDEE 2.1.5.7.5
  • JOSEPH PARDEE 2.1.5.7.6
