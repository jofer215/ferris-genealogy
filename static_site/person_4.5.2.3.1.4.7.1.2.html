RUSSELL AUSTIN WARD

ID: 4.5.2.3.1.4.7.1.2 | Source: Mary
(4.5.2.3.1.4.7.1.2) RUSSELL AUSTIN WARD born February 21, 1886 Spencer, Clay, IA; carpenter and builder; married March 1, 1923 in Mitchell SD, Jennie Van Deelen [dau of Cornelius & Elizabeth (De Booy) Van Deelen] born April 27, 1901 Alton, Sioux, IA; and they had a child; 1925 living in Milford IA (4.5.2.3.1.4.7.1.3) LEWIS CHESTER WARD (twin) born (Laura Chester Ward) December 24, 1890 Spencer IA and died in infancy

No children recorded
