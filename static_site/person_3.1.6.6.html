SILAS CANFIELD

ID: 3.1.6.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.6) SILAS CANFIELD born April 24, 1741 Ridgefield, Fairfield, CT and died (after 1791 since he was in the census) 1823(?); Silas' death date is an unk; according to family tradition he went to Luzerne County PA to live with/near his son, Hezekiah; does not appear in the 1800 census of VT, NY, or PA, or the 1810 census of PA; in the Revolutionary War, 3 years, with Colonel Seth Warner’s Regiment of Vermont, though there is no record of pension or bounty land; married c1772 at Rye, Westchester, NY Judith Graham; and born to them were:

Children (7)

  • KEZIAH CANFIELD 3.1.6.6.1
  • BETSEY CANFIELD 3.1.6.6.2
  • SILAS CANFIELD 3.1.6.6.3
  • HEZEKIAH GRAHAM CANFIELD 3.1.6.6.4
