JOHN LEE COUNT HOIT

ID: 3.10.1.4.8.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.4.8.1.2) JOHN LEE COUNT HOIT (Hoyt) (#4428) born April 24, 1816 Stamford CT and was living there 1871; and died 1907 Standwich CT; a farmer; married 1st, January 3, 1843 Rebecca Ann Brush [dau of Edward & Ann (Ingersoll) Brush] born April 28, 1831 Greenwich and died April 18, 1866 Stanwich; buried with John; married 2d April 24, 1877 in Stamford Cordelia Lockwood (2.6.4.6.2.3.1) (1826-1905); all buried Long Ridge Cemetery, Stamford; and born to John and Rebecca were:

Children (5)

  • JOHN MOOR HOIT 3.10.1.4.8.1.2.1
  • EDWARD BRUSH HOIT 3.10.1.4.8.1.2.2
  • MARY ANNA HOIT 3.10.1.4.8.1.2.3
  • NORMAN LEE HOIT 3.10.1.4.8.1.2.4
  • MARY ELMA HOIT 3.10.1.4.8.1.2.5
