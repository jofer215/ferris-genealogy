FRANKLYN BATES BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.1.4.5.3.8 | Source: James
(5.2.2.1.4.5.3.8) FRANKLYN BATES BUXTON born February 11, 1858 New Canaan CT and died there December 14, 1918; buried Lakeview Cemetery, New Canaan; a shoemaker; married December 1, 1880 in Poundridge NY, Susan Alice Scofield [dau of Rufus & Sarah (Mead) Scofield] born January 11, 1855 Poundridge, Westchester, NY and died March 31, 1907 New Canaan; buried Lakeview Cemetery.
