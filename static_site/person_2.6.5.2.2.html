LUCY MEAD

ID: 2.6.5.2.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.5.2.2) LUCY MEAD born March 1, 1758 Greenwich (Stamford?) and died there December (10?) 20, 1820; married May 28, 1778 in Greenwich, Isaac Howe [son of Isaac & Keziah (Mead) Howe] born February 11, 1748/49 Greenwich and died there December 29, 1823. He was a Rev. War Veteran serving between August 13, 1776 and January 11, 1777 as an Ensign in Matthew Mead’s Company. Born to them were (at Greenwich?)
