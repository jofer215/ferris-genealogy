ELMER FERRIS LONG

ID: 5.1.3.2.4.5.1.10 | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.4.5.1.10) ELMER FERRIS LONG born August 26, 1873/1876 Vinton, Benton, IA and died April 19, 1943; married May 14, 1914 as her 1st husband, Georgia Editha Harvey [dau of John & Mary (Ogden) Harvey] born October 4, 1889 Levant KS and died February 3, 1964 Bethel Isle CA – and they had 3 kids
