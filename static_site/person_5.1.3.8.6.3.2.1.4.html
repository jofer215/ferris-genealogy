LOIS FRANCES CLARK

ID: 5.1.3.8.6.3.2.1.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.3.2.1.4) LOIS FRANCES CLARK born February 18, 1919 Idaho Falls ID; married December 2, 1942 in New York City, Merlin Sherman Young [son of Earl S. & Lela (Hamilton)] born December 5, 1918. Merlin received an A.B., Whitman College 1940; LLB University of S. California 1946; 1956 they resided Boise ID. Born to them were:
