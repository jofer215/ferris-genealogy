POLLY RANDALL

ID: 5.1.4.4.1.4 | Source: James
(5.1.4.4.1.4) POLLY RANDALL born January 20, 1790 (1780) Bridgewater CT and died there July 3, 1828; married April 23, 1807 in Bridgewater, as his 1st wife, Benjamin Burroughs Beach [son of Nehemiah & Sarah (Middlebrook) Beach] born February 14, 1785 Trumbull, Fairfield, CT and died June 1, 1848 Bridgewater; both buried there Old Ground (Old Bridgewater Burying Place); and born to them were:

Children (2)
