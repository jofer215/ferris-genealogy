WILLIAM LOCKWOOD, Jr.,

ID: 4.6.3.5.1.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.3.5.1.1) WILLIAM LOCKWOOD, Jr., (#1432) born May 24, 1796 Watertown CT and died October 6, 1863. William was adopted in infancy by Thomas Cole II, of Watertown. William married, December 25, 1815 at Watertown, Harriet Leavenworth [dau of Joseph & Tamar (Pritchard) Leavenworth] born November 19, 1798, Waterbury and died July 17, 1880. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • CHAUNCEY ANDREWS LOCKWOOD 4.6.3.5.1.1.1
  • MARY REBECCA LOCKWOOD 4.6.3.5.1.1.2
  • THOMAS COLE LOCKWOOD 4.6.3.5.1.1.3
  • WILLIAM NEWTON LOCKWOOD 4.6.3.5.1.1.4
