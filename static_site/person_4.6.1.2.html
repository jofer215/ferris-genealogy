JOSEPH LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.1.2 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2) JOSEPH LOCKWOOD (#236) (Captain) born June 30, 1731 Stamford and died March 17, 1792 Pound Ridge NY, where he resided; married November 22, 1759 Hannah Close [dau of Solomon, of North Salem NY] born April 25, 1736 and died December 22, 1806. [After Joseph’s death, Hannah married, as his third wife, Captain James Richards of New Canaan CT who died in 1810.] Joseph was elected Town Clerk of Old Pound Ridge, Westchester County NY, in 1760. He was unanimously chosen chairman of the first meeting of the congregation of the Presbyterian Society of Pound Ridge 1760. Joseph was chosen Captain of a military company and his commission was issued September 13, 1775. He and his company apparently went to Ticonderoga. Born to them were:
