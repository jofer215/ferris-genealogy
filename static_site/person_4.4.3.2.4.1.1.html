HIRAM HORTON SLAWSON

ID: 4.4.3.2.4.1.1 | Source: Mary
(4.4.3.2.4.1.1) HIRAM HORTON SLAWSON born October 1, 1825 Orange Co NY and died September 26, 1914 DuQuoin, Perry, IL; (what did you do during the Civil War, Hiram?) married May 8, 1855 in New Orleans LA, Lucy Wright born July 13, 1836 Isle of Wright, Hampshire, England and died January 22, 1914 DuQuoin IL; and born to them were: (4.4.3.2.4.1.1.1) CEPHISE ELIZABETH SLAWSON born July 9, 1856 New Orleans LA and died May 7, 1929 DuQuoin, Perry, IL; married May 11, 1872 in DuQuoin, John Henry Ward [son of Henry & Lucy Adeline (Todd) Ward] born August 4, 1846 Litchfield Co CT and died March 30, 1916 Murphysboro, Litchfield, IL; and born to them was:
