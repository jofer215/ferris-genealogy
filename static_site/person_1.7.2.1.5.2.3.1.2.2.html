MARGARET LOUISE SCHIEFFELIN

ID: 1.7.2.1.5.2.3.1.2.2 | Source: John
(1.7.2.1.5.2.3.1.2.2) MARGARET LOUISE SCHIEFFELIN born November 27, 1893; married January 10, 1914 Frederick Henry Osborn born March 21, 1889 and died January 5, 1981; graduated from Princeton U 1910; attended Trinity College, Cambridge for a postgraduate year; his family had made their fortune in the railroad business and he went into the family business up until the outbreak of WWI, when he served in the American Red Cross in France as Commander of the Advance Zone for the last 11 months of the war; 1928 he became a research associate at the American Museum of Natural History studying anthropology and population; Major General, US Army; an American philanthropist, military leader, and eugenicists; founder of several organizations and played a central part in reorienting eugenics in the years following WWII away from race- and class-consciousness from earlier periods – plenty of info on the “net” on this guy; and born to them was (and possibly more):
