EDWARD LANYON PENNO SLAYTER

ID: 1.2.3.5.8.5.1.3.1 | Source: John
(1.2.3.5.8.5.1.3.1) EDWARD LANYON PENNO SLAYTER born May 7, 1901 Isle of Wight and died 1964; British Army Officer; December 23, 1921 commisioend 2d Lieutenant into the Arguyll and Sutherland Highlanders (Princess Louise’s); Lieutenant 1923; T/Captain 1930; Captain 1932; Major 1936; A/Lieutenant Colonel 1944; T/Lieutenant Colonel 1944; Honorary Lieutenant Colonel 1947; 1926-33, 1933-37 employed with the King’s Rifles; 1942 Commanding Officer, 11th Battalion The Argyll & Sutherland Highlanders; awarded Distinguished Service Order as a member of the Royal Army Medical Corps; married Miss Robinson

No children recorded
