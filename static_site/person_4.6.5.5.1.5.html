LYDIA HOYT

ID: 4.6.5.5.1.5 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.5.1.5) LYDIA HOYT (#4363) (c1795-) married July 10, 1813 Ebenezer Darrow; 1871 living in Greenfield NY (4.6.5.5.1.6) INCREASE HOYT (#4364) born November 27, 1797 and died very suddenly June 2, 1864 Bureau Co IL, of apoplexy; married February 1, 1820 Elizabeth Seymour Wood who died August 18, 1867.
