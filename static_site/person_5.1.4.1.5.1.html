GIDEON WEED LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.1.4.1.5.1 | Source: James
(5.1.4.1.5.1) GIDEON WEED LOCKWOOD born February 27, 1793 North Stamford and died there April 11, 1879; buried North Stamford Cemetery; farmer between 1850-79; married February 25, 1818 at Poundridge NY, Mary Ayres [dau of Ebenezer & Thankful (Lockwood) Ayres] born February 14, 1798 CT and died January 18, 1871 Stamford; buried North Stamford Cemetery. Born to them were:
