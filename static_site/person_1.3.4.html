PETER FERRIS

ID: 1.3.4 | Source: John
(1.3.4) PETER FERRIS [conflict – this Peter is also listed in the Zachariah Ferris family so the placement of this twig is in question] born c1707-1714 (February 18, 1713) in (of) Newtown, Fairfield Co CT and died 1795-98 (January 28, 1795) probably Newtown; married November 11 (30?), 1737 in Newtown, Ruth Northup [dau of John & Mary (Porter) Northup] born January 15, 1717 Milford (Newton) CT and died May 28, 1804.
