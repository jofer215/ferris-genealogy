ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2.3.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2.3.3) MERVIN JOHN FERRIS “Mike” born June 20, 1947 Pierre SD; married May 31, 1968 at Pierre SD, Gail Marie Kruse born June 6, 1947 Murdo SD; Mike graduated from Sioux Falls SD Barber College and operated a barbershop at Pierre SD. He operates 2 gambling casinos at Pierre SD and a Casino-steak house at Winner SD. Gail graduated from South Dakota State U., 1968, and is employed by the State of South Dakota; reside at Pierre.
