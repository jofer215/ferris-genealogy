GEORGE WASHINGTON FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.2.2.2.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.2.2.6) GEORGE WASHINGTON FERRIS born September 3, 1827 Columbus, Chenango, NY and died October 5, 1864 DeRuyter, Madison, NY; George and his family were listed in the 1855 and 1860 census as living in DeRuyter; volunteered to serve in the Civil War on February 20, 1864, returned home and died 8 months later; a carpenter and a joiner; married September 3, 1849 at Smyrna NY, Sophia Ann (Lepha) Tuthill born July 28, 1830 Chenango Co NY. George and Lepha were of Smyrna when married; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • ARABEL HATTIE FERRIS 3.1.2.2.2.2.6.2
  • COURTNEY ELLSWORTH FERRIS 3.1.2.2.2.2.6.3
