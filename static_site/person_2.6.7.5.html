ID: 2.6.7.5 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.5) TAMISON (TAMSON) KNAPP born July 28, 1739 Stamford; married February 13, 1769 at Stamford, Titus Lockwood [LDS has her listed as Damsen Knapp] [another source has a Titus Lockwood marrying Hannah Dan this same date that he married Tamison Knapp – too much of a coincidence – conflict!]

No children recorded
