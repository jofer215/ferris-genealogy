WINNIE BELLE WEED

ID: 4.6.5.9.3.2.13.3 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.9.3.2.13.3) WINNIE BELLE WEED born July 24, 1870 South Allen, Hinsdale, MI and died September 25, 1953 Mentor, Lake, OH; buried there Mentor Cemetery; Nurse; owner of a hat store in Cleveland; married 1st December 12, 1889 James Cochran Lusk born August 8, 1867 Grove City, Butler, PA and died 1943 OK; buried Rose Hill Cemetery, Ardmore OK – divorced 1915; after the divorce she moved to Brooklyn and lived in Henry Ward Beecher’s old home and worked for Watch Tower publications; here she met and married 1920 Thomas Yates born December 27, 1873/75 and died 1958 Mentor OH; buried there Mentor Cemetery – both Jehovah’s Witnesses; and born to her and James were:

Children (2)

  • MYRA LUSK 4.6.5.9.3.2.13.3.1
  • ALICE ISABELLE LUSK 4.6.5.9.3.2.13.3.2
