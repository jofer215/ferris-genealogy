ELEANOR JAY SCHIEFFELIN

ID: 1.7.2.1.5.2.3.1.1 | Source: John
(1.7.2.1.5.2.3.1.1) ELEANOR JAY SCHIEFFELIN born August 4, 1864 and died February 24, 1929; B. A. Williams 1886; LL.B. Columbia 1889; became a partner of his father, practicing law, in the firm E. N. & T. M. Taft; after his father’s retirement, he was in the firm of Taft & Sherman, composed of Theodore and P Tecumseh Sherman; married February 12, 1903 Theodore M Taft [son of Enos Nelson Julia M (Peet) Taft]

No children recorded
