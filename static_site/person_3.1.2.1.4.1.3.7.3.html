HARRISON W GODFREY

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.7.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.3.7.3) HARRISON W GODFREY born March 3, 1847 Jackson Co MI and died May 1915 Elkhart, Elkhart, IN; served with Company H, 30th Michigan Volunteer Infantry; 1880 resided Benton MI; moved to Elkhart where he became Treasurer of the Munger Lumber Co; at one time was Commander of the Elkhart Post of the G.A.R.; married January 1, 1868 in Shelbina, Shelby, MO Jenette Monteith born c1846 Kalamazoo MI and died c1921 Elkhart IN; and born to them was:
