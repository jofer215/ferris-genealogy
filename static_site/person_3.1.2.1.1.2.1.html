JEHIEL BEACH

ID: 3.1.2.1.1.2.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.1.2.1) JEHIEL BEACH born March 30, 1808 PA and died March 1878 Elk Township, Tioga Co PA; married (April 10, 1834) October 4, 1834 Sally Sherman (1809-1899) - Jehiel is listed in the history of Tioga Co as being among the first settlers of Elk Township, Tioga Co in 1856, having moved there from Bradford Co PA. In the 1860 census, the following info is listed for Jehiel: Jehiel, 52 years old, farmer; Sally, 52 years old; John, 23 years old, carpenter; Mary 18 years old; and Edgar 13 years. Don't know if John, Mary, and Edgar are their kids - assume so... from another source, born to them were:
