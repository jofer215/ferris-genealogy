LEWIS ELBERT CHRISTIAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.9.1.4.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.9.1.4.1) LEWIS ELBERT CHRISTIAN born September 28, 1910 Wappinger Falls NY and died January 16, 1971 Vassar Brothers Hospital; buried Poughkeepsie Rural Cemetery; married 1st May 12, 1935 in Jackson Heights, L.I., Virginia Dusenbury [dau of Edwin K. & Katherine (Palmer)] born May 21, 1915 and died February 10, 1958 age 42y Poughkeepsie NY; buried New Hackensack Cemetery; Virginia was educated in local schools and was a graduate of Poughkeepsie High School in 1933. She was a member of Trinity Methodist church; a member of the Spackenkill Home Demonstration unit and was actively associated with the Arlington Parent-Teacher association, and had served as a member of the Arlington High School scholarship committee. Lewis was a painter and decorator and resided at 242 New Hackensack Road. He received most of his education in Wappingers schools and was graduated from Poughkeepsie High School, residing in the city area the last 45 years. He was a member of Trinity Church and a former member of the Kiwanis Club of Poughkeepsie. He married, second, Dorothy G.
