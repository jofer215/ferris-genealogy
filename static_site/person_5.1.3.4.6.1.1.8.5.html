FERRIS ELIPHALET BEEKLEY, M. D.,

ID: 5.1.3.4.6.1.1.8.5 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.6.1.1.8.5) FERRIS ELIPHALET BEEKLEY, M. D., born February 27, 1897 West Chester OH and died June 15, 1986 Cincinnati; graduate of Miami (Ohio) University and received his M. D., from University of Cincinnati; 5’ 8”, 185 lb; played 1921 in NFL with Cinncinnati as a guard and blocking back; the Official Encyclopedia of the National Football League has his name as Ferris Edward Beekley; married December 12, 1930 Elizabeth Jane Marts; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • JANE ELIZABETH BEEKLEY 5.1.3.4.6.1.1.8.5.2
  • BARBARA ANN BEEKLEY 5.1.3.4.6.1.1.8.5.3
