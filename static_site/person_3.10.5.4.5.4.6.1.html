FRANK LOGAN DYKE

ID: 3.10.5.4.5.4.6.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.5.4.5.4.6.1) FRANK LOGAN DYKE born October 2, 1867 North East PA and died January 14, 1932 Rockport, Cleveland, OH; married June 22, 1887 in Rockport, Ada Emily Brown born September 20, 1860 Rockport and died May 2, 1942 Cleveland OH. Born to them were: (3.10.5.4.5.4.6.1.1) LUCINDA DYKE (Lucy) born October 30, 1895 Cleveland OH and died November 26, 1980 San Diego CA; married June 26, 1926 in Fairview OH, Omar Sharpe born October 31, 1894 Wichita KS and died September 18, 1964 LaJolla CA
