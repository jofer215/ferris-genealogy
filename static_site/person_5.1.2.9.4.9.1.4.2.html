ELEANOR CHRISTIAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.9.1.4.2 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.9.1.4.2) ELEANOR CHRISTIAN married September 10, 1935 in Poughkeepsie, Carleton G. Klump [son of John A. & Gertrude (Gresty)] born March 1, 1912 Poughkeepsie NY and died there May 26, 1990 Vassar Brothers Hospital; buried Wappingers Rural Cemetery. Mr. Klump, a lifelong Poughkeepsie resident, worked for IBM for 30 years, retiring in 1971. He was a member of IBM's Quarter Century Club. Other memberships included the Oak Grove Grange in Wappingers Falls and a life membership in the New Hamburg Fire Company. He was an elder and 65-year member of the First Presbyterian Church in Wappingers Falls. He graduated from the former Wappinger Falls High School. Born to them were:
