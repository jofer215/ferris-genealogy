SHERMAN LEGRAND SANFORD

ID: 4.2.5.4.5.1.1.1 | Source: Mary

Harmon born September 9, 1838 Randolph, Portage, OH; and born to them was: (4.2.5.4.5.1.1.1.1) BELLE LEGRAND SANFORD born April 18, 1861 Rootstown, Portage, OH and died in Canton, Stark, OH; married November 21, 1880 Guy Tilden born May 11, 1858 Youngstown, Mahoning, OH; and born to them were:

No children recorded
