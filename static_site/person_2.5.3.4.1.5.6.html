EMELINE HOYT

ID: 2.5.3.4.1.5.6 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.4.1.5.6) EMELINE HOYT (#4654) born June 2, 1805 Stamford and died January 9, 1854 probably at Norwalk, where she lived; married November 20, 1843, as his 2d of 3 wives, (Ebenezer) Eben Whitney [son of Ebenezer & Ruth (Raymond) Whitney] born November 19, 1783 Norwalk CT and died there May 22, 1869

No children recorded
