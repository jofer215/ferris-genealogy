EZRA HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.4.5.1.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.5.1.2) EZRA HOYT (#4539) (Ezra P Hoyt) born September 24, 1795 Stamford and died January 25, 1832 Trivoli, town of Red Hook, Dutchess, NY; moved with his father to Red Hook 1805; married October 20, 1820 in Rhinebeck, Dutchess, NY Eliza W Outwater [dau of Peter & Polly (Kennedy) Outwater] born August 29, 1802 Dutchess Co NY and died 1879; she was living 1871 Hyde Park, Dutchess, NY.
