RUTH PECK

ID: 3.10.7.1.1.15 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.7.1.1.15) RUTH PECK born September/October 21, 1799 Greenwich and died January 23, 1879 Brooklyn NY; married c1836 in Boston, William R Johnson [son of Seth & Mary (Birt) Johnson] born April 21, 1800 Boston and died April 9, 1871; both buried Greenwood Cemetery, Brooklyn; and born to them were: (3.10.7.1.1.15.1) CHARLES JOHNSON born April 20, 1838 NYC and died there April 19, 1865 from suicide; buried Greenwood Cemetery, Brooklyn; butcher; unmarried

Children (2)

  • DAVID PECK JOHNSON 3.10.7.1.1.15.2
  • EDWIN HATFIELD JOHNSON 3.10.7.1.1.15.3
