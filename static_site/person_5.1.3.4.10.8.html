JAMES FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.8 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.8) JAMES FERRIS born November 2, 1832 Franklin Co IN and died May 12, 1867 Sterling IL; killed in a run-away accident; buried Metamora Cemetery, Metamora, Franklin, IN. James served as a Wagoner/Teamster in Company C., (63d) 68th Indiana Volunteer Infantry during the Civil War; he was mustered in August 6, 1862 at Camp Logan IN for 3 years. He was 5 feet one inch tall, with dark complexion, gray eyes and dark hair. He was present for duty from August to October 31, 1862, but after that James was constantly absent because of sickness and he was discharged, May 2, 1863 for disability. [Jim - the National Archives does not have Pension Records on this guy.]; married April 2, 1865 in Franklin Co IN, Sarah Ellen Sherwood born September 25, 1839 Bloomingrove, Franklin, IN and died March 25, 1911 Waterloo Township IN.
