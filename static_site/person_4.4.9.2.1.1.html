RACHEL MEAD

ID: 4.4.9.2.1.1 | Source: Mary
(4.4.9.2.1.1) RACHEL MEAD (Rachel R. Mead) born July 25, 1785 Greenwich and died January 19, 1868 Cass Township, Jones Co IA; married April 21, 1803 in Chester Co NY, Abram Mead [son of Enos & Prudence (Anderson) Mead] born August 1, 1784 Greenwich and died April 11, 1849 Cincinnatus, Cortland, NY; after the death of Abram, the family farm was passed to son Jasper; Rache and daughters Eliza and Hariette continued to live on the farm until c1857 when she and the 2 daughters moved to Jones Co IA where her son George Wesley Mead had already relocated to; and born to them were:
