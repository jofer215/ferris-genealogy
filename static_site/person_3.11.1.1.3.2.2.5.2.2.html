HAROLD WILLIAM HOUGHTON

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.5.2.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.5.2.2) HAROLD WILLIAM HOUGHTON born April 21, 1904 Elmwood Township, Leelanau, MI and died January 3, 1968 Traverse City MI; mechanic, self-employed at Houghton’s Garage; married 1st February 1926 Olive Boon; married 2d 1928 in Chicago IL, Elizabeth Semina Hofman born October 13, 1907 Chicago IL and died November 10, 1983 Traverse City MI; both buried there Grand Traverse Memorial Gardens

No children recorded
