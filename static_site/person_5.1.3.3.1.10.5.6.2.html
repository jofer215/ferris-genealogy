EMILY HARRIET FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.10.5.6.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.10.5.6.2) EMILY HARRIET FERRIS born September 28, 1896 Ottawa Co OH and died July 18, 1985 Tulsa OK; buried Memorial Park Cemetery, Lawrence KS; Valedictorian of her high school class; received bachelors and masters degrees from the University of Kansas, with graduate study at the U. of Chicago. Emily's career was in social work - she was a case supervisor at locations in Kansas, Missouri, and Oklahoma. She drafted the first nursing home standards for the State of Kansas, which were enacted in law by the Legislature. At various times she worked for the Red Cross at flood and tornado disasters - never married

No children recorded
