PHEBE ANN UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.1.2.1.1.2 | Source: John
(1.2.3.1.2.1.1.2) PHEBE ANN UNDERHILL born 1834 NYC and died 1894 West Orange NJ; married 1854 in Verona NJ, William N Williams born c1829 Mt Pleasant NJ and died April 15, 1907 West Orange NJ; he was a cabinet maker 1846 NYC, member of Board of Education, West Orange NJ, Essex Co NJ County Board of Chosen Freeholders, and 1887 tax collector; and born to them were:

Children (2)
