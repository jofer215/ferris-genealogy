JOHN ORVIN WILLIAMSON

ID: 3.10.7.3.5.4.1.1.5.7 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.7.3.5.4.1.1.5.7) JOHN ORVIN WILLIAMSON born November 25, 1918 Santa Rita, Grant, NM and died November 2, 1980 Medford, Jackson, OR; USN WWII Vet; served in the Pacific Theatre – his ship was torpedoed by a Japanese sub and he spent several days on a lifeboat before being rescued; 1950-60 resided Yermo CA, 1960-65 resided Gold Hill OR; 1965-death resided Medford; married in Las Vegas, Leah Thelma Davidson born September 1, 1903 and died April 13, 1996 Albany OR

No children recorded
