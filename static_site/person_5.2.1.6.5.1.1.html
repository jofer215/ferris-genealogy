SARAH ANN BELL

ID: 5.2.1.6.5.1.1 | Source: James
(5.2.1.6.5.1.1) SARAH ANN BELL born May 18, 1840 CT and died June 2, 1914 Stamford CT; buried Long Ridge Cemetery. She married November 15, 1865 in Stamford, William Henry Weed, Jr, [son of James Lewis & Martha S. (Bates) Weed] born April 25, 1835 Stamford and died there September 29, 1930; buried with Sarah. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • EMMET L. WEED 5.2.1.6.5.1.1.1
  • GEORGE W. WEED 5.2.1.6.5.1.1.2
  • ELMER H. WEED 5.2.1.6.5.1.1.3
