THEOPHILUS PECK

ID: 3.10.7 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.7) THEOPHILUS PECK (GJ59-B2) born in March 1702 Greenwich CT and died there November 7, 1783; married February 5, 1728 Elizabeth Mead [dau of Benjamin} born November 2, 1703/05 Greenwich and died November 17, 1783. Theophilus was owner of an extensive tract of land in North Greenwich, called Pecksland and Round Hill, where he resided until his death. Born to them were:
