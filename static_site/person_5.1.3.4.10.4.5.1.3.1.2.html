CHRISTINE MARIE SANDROCK

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.5.1.3.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.5.1.3.1.2) CHRISTINE MARIE SANDROCK born February 3, 1955 Morrison IL; married July 27, 1974 at the Prophetstown Lutheran Church, Douglas Donald Johnson born January 15, 1953; after marrying Doug, Christine worked as an insurance clerk for Country Companies in Champaign IL. While living in Normal IL, she worked as a secretary to the Assistant Principle at Parkside Junior High School. After moving back to Champaign, she worked as a chief clerk for the Library Personnel Office at the University of Illinois. After the birth of their son, Matthew, she retired from working to be a stay-at-Mom. She worked at several part-time jobs, but mainly a Mom and volunteer at school. She was on the PTA Board; vice president of music boosters; served on a steering committee for Mahonet-Symour High School music trip to Hawaii. Born to them were:
