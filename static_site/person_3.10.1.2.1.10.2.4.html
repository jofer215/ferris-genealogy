PHOEBE SOHPHONIA NASH

ID: 3.10.1.2.1.10.2.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.1.10.2.4) PHOEBE SOHPHONIA NASH born July 17, 1842 Smyrna, Chenango, NY and died March 7, 1925 Park Rapids, Hubbard, MN. She came with her family to northwestern Illinois when she was three years old. She married 1st June 5, 1860 at the Methodist Church, Pleasant Valley (Warren) IL, James Plaka Pimley born c1839 England and was killed 1863 at the Battle of Chickamauga.
