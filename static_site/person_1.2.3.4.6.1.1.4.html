GEORGE TINKER

ID: 1.2.3.4.6.1.1.4 | Source: John
(1.2.3.4.6.1.1.4) GEORGE TINKER born January 26, 1825 Bainbridge NY and died 1889 Lebanon NY; married 1st Hannah M Wright (1829-1850); 2d Martha Vrooman (1829-1851); and 3d June 30, 1852 in Guilford NY, Martha Starr born September 14, 1829 and died 1903; buried at Plymouth NY; and born to George and his 2d wife Martha was:
