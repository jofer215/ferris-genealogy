MARY LOUISE SUTTON

ID: 1.2.5.1.10.6.1.10 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.10.6.1.10) MARY LOUISE SUTTON born June 8, 1870 Stamford CT and died December 9, 1953 Greenville SC; buried there Woodlawn Memorial Gardens; married 1st October 28, 1890 Winslow Albert Brown [son of Abraham & Katherine] born July 1863 NYC; buried Newburg; clerk, driver, foreman in Yonkers; 2d June 9, 1919 Floyd M Hyatt born July 1865 NY; and born to her and Winslow were:

Children (3)

  • VIRGIL LOUISE BROWN 1.2.5.1.10.6.1.10.2
  • VIVIAN PRISCILLA BROWN 1.2.5.1.10.6.1.10.3
