ORSON SPENCER HORTON

ID: 1.2.2.9.3.5.2.4.2 | Source: John
(1.2.2.9.3.5.2.4.2) ORSON SPENCER HORTON (Orrie) born February 5, 1884 NY and died April 1943; married c1905 Katherine Chrystal [dau of Thomas & Maria (Thomas) Chrystal] born November 18, 1885 NYC and died March 1, 1937 probably Chappaqua; both buried Ferncliff Cemetery, Greenburgh Township, Westchester, NY; and they had 2 kids (1.2.2.9.3.5.2.4.3) HERBERT M HORTON born February 20, 1887 and died November 16, 1889; buried Woodside Cemetery, Poenstenkill NY

No children recorded
