JOHN BANKS FERRIS

ID: 1.6.1.4.5.2.3.4 | Source: John
(1.6.1.4.5.2.3.4) JOHN BANKS FERRIS born on Bedford Farm on Danbury Post Road September 25, 1896 and lived on the last 87 acres of the Ferris land until it was sold after 1900 by his father John Edward Ferris; died at Hartsdale NY October 3, 1973; married Catherine White born Bronx NY June 25, 1891 and died Valhalla NY August 10, 1973. Born to John and Catherine were:

Children (5)

  • JOHN FERRIS 1.6.1.4.5.2.3.4.1
  • RUSSELL FERRIS 1.6.1.4.5.2.3.4.2
  • THOMAS FERRIS died February 25, 1986; unmarried 1.6.1.4.5.2.3.4.3
  • JAMES FERRIS 1.6.1.4.5.2.3.4.4
  • MICHAEL FERRIS, SR., 1.6.1.4.5.2.3.4.5
