PHEBE MERRITT

ID: 1.2.2.2.6.2 | Source: John
(1.2.2.2.6.2) PHEBE MERRITT (1789-1859) married December 10, 1807, William Franklin Mott (1.10.2.6.1) born January 11, 1785 and died May 3, 1867; buried Prospect Park Cemetery, Brooklyn; he was a drygoods merchant; sources differ on the order of William’s 2 marriages and the offspring assigned to each; for this document, all children are assigned to Phebe – this may NOT be correct; see him for offspring.

No children recorded
