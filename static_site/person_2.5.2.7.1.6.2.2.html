AMANDA ANN HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.7.1.6.2.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.7.1.6.2.2) AMANDA ANN HOYT (#4610) born February 22, 1811 Stamford and died there January 7, 1871; buried there High Ridge Cemetery; married 1st Joseph Stevens; married 2d February 27, 1858 in Stamford, Allen Lockwood (4.5.2.3.9.3); and born to her and Joseph were:

Children (4)

  • MILES J STEVENS 2.5.2.7.1.6.2.2.2
  • ANN AMELIA STEVENS 2.5.2.7.1.6.2.2.3
  • JOHN W STEVENS 2.5.2.7.1.6.2.2.4
