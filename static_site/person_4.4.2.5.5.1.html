JOHN WILLIAM LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.2.5.5.1 | Source: Mary
(4.4.2.5.5.1) JOHN WILLIAM LOCKWOOD (#1482) born July 19, 1832 Greenwich; 1878 resided at Ashtabula OH; married 1st April 25, 1855 at Painesville, Lake, OH, Sarah Potter [dau of Baldwin & Serutia (Whiting) Potter] born Batavia NY; married 2d June 7, 1876 at Jefferson, Ashtabula, OH, Ellen Lina Mann [dau of Billby Porteus & Sarah (Upson) Mann] born Plymouth OH. Born to John and Sarah were:

Children (3)

  • ELIZABETH IDA LOCKWOOD 4.4.2.5.5.1.1
  • NELLIE CHARLOTTE LOCKWOOD 4.4.2.5.5.1.2
  • ALICE CARY LOCKWOOD 4.4.2.5.5.1.3
