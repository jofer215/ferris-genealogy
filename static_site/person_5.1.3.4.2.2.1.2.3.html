EDGAR MUNSON FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.2.2.1.2.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.2.2.1.2.3) EDGAR MUNSON FERRIS (twin) (Photograph) born December 10, 1913 Greenwich CT and died there October 31, 1989; buried Ferris Plot at the cemetery at First Congregational Church, Old Greenwich; picture of his high school band shows him holding a French horn; went to MIT and later Columbia, where he earned a degree in Mechanical Engineering; worked on the Manhattan Project at Oak Ridge TN, during WWII; married June 14, 1941 at Greenwich, Evelyn Pike born February 24, 1913 Troy NY; and born to them were:
