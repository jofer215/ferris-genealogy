EVA MAE CLARK

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.2.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.2.1) EVA MAE CLARK born October 26, 1911 Greilickville, Leelanau, MI and died August 7, 1988 Traverse City; housekeeper at Munson Medical Center, worked at Potter’s Bakery; married 1st June 3, 1931 in Traverse City, Joseph James Lada [son of Joseph V & Anna M (Valish) Lada] born October 14, 1905 Traverse City and died there May 12, 1954; both buried there Oakwood Cemetery; and they had 6 kids; married 2d September 2, 1961 Raymond Nave

No children recorded
