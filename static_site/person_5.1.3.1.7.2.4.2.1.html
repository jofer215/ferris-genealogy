FRANK S.

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.4.2.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.4.2.1) FRANK S. (Stearns?) DITTO (Photos) born September 15, 1857 Newbury, Geauga, OH and died March 17, 1942 Chesterland, Geauga, OH; buried Chester Township Cemetery, Chesterland; married April 8, 1885 in Chesterland (Warrensville), Mary J. Hand (Photo) born August 22, 1859 Warrensville, Cuyahoga, OH and died August 11, 1950 Chesteland OH; buried with Frank. He was of Russel OH when his mother died. After their marriage, they lived in Michigan for 3 years, then returned to Geauga County living in Newbury, Bainbridge, Hambden, Russell and Chester. He gave up farming to operate a store at Russell Center and in 1914 purchased the store at Mulberry corners, which he operated until 1938. Mary was an active member of the Chester W.C.T.U. until its disbandment. She was a member of the Methodist church but was active in the Chester Baptis church and a member of the aid society of the church. Born to them were:
