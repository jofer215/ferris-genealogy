ALFRED LAWSON STEARNS

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.4.6.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.4.6.1) ALFRED LAWSON STEARNS (Photo) born June 7, 1874 Ferris, Montcalm, MI and died April 22, 1955; buried April 25, 1955 Ferris Center Cemetery, Ferris Township, Montcalm Co MI; married 1st September 24, 1893 Jennie D. Jolly [dau of Francis & Margaret (Pyle) Jolly] born January 1875/76 Ferris Township, Montcalm Co MI and died 1947 (1950) Ionia MI; married 2d 1950 in Montcalm Co MI, Edna Ries Conner born November 16, 1883 and died January 27, 1978. Alfred was County Clerk of Montcalm Co MI. He was reared on the paternal farm in Ferris Township, receiving his education in the district school of that township, and remained at home until his marriage in 1893, after which for several years he rented a farm, prospering in his farming operations until he was able to buy a farm or 40 acres to which he added a tract of 20 acres which his wife inherited. During all this time, he had been paying considerable attention to the political affairs of the county and extending his acquaintance and was gradually getting in line for political preferment. He had served the people of the township very acceptably as clerk for a period of 4 years and had been supervisor for 6 years; therefore, when he received the nomination for the office of county clerk on the Repubican ticket in the fall of 1914, he was elected by a good majority in the ensuring election in the fall. He then rented his farm to a tenant and moved to Stanton, the county seat. See History of Ionia and Montcalm Counties, Michigan. Born to him and Jennie were: (5.1.3.1.7.2.4.6.1.1) FLOSSIE ADA STEARNS born April 18, 1894 Ferris Township, Montcalm Co MI and died May 2, 1969 Stanton, Montcalm MI; married 1st Frank Black born September 26, 1892 and died February 10, 1946; married 2d 1969 in Montcalm Co MI, George McKinley Gates (her brother-inlaw) born May 31, 1897 and died June 10, 1981 Greenville, Montcalm MI.
