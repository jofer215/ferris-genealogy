PETER PECK

ID: 3.10.8 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.8) PETER PECK (GJ59-C7) born 1704 Greenwich, Fairfield Co and died there 1757/59; married after 1735 in Greenwich, Sarah Reynolds (3WFK-TX) [dau of Ebenezer & Abigail (Smith) Reynolds] born 1711 Greenwich. After Peter’s death, Sarah removed with the children to New Milford CT. Born to them were:

Children (5)

  • PETER PECK 3.10.8.1
  • SARAH PECK 3.10.8.2
  • ENOS PECK 3.10.8.4
  • ELIZABETH PECK 3.10.8.5
  • MARY PECK died at Weybridge VT, age 83. She married David Terrill. {Peter’s brother} 3.10.8.6
