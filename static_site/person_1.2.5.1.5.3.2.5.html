PAULINE WILLIS

ID: 1.2.5.1.5.3.2.5 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.5.3.2.5) PAULINE WILLIS born 1863 Roslyn, L.I, NY; married October 17, 1888 Manorville, L.I., NY Jesse Merritt [son of John C & Phebe H (Albertson) Merritt] born September 20, 1839 and died April 20, 1903; resided Farmingdale, L.I., NY; Queens Nassau Agricultural Society; Bethpage Friends Meeting Clerk; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • JESSE MERRITT 1.2.5.1.5.3.2.5.1
  • PHEBE ALICE MERRITT 1.2.5.1.5.3.2.5.2
  • MARJORY MAE MERRITT 1.2.5.1.5.3.2.5.3
