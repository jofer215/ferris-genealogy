DAVID JAMES BOWDEN

ID: 4.6.5.9.3.2.13.3.2.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.9.3.2.13.3.2.1) DAVID JAMES BOWDEN born December 11, 1908 Painesville, Lake, OH and died January 1, 1994 Mentor, Lake, OH; buried there Mentor Cemetery; played clarinet in the 1st All Ohio Boy’s State Fair Band – remembers playing under John Phillip Sousa; married 1st August 25, 1934 in Ashtabula OH, Marian Post born April 17, 1907 Ashtabula and died May 4, 1940 Lakewood, Cuyahoga, OH; buried Edgewood Cemetery, Ashtabula; she was an elementary school teacher; and they had 2 kids; married 2d 1941 Helen G Gray (1910-1974); married 3d Margaret Mary Parks (19112000)

No children recorded
