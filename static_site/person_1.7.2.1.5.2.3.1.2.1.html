WILLIAM JAY SCHIEFFELIN

ID: 1.7.2.1.5.2.3.1.2.1 | Source: John
(1.7.2.1.5.2.3.1.2.1) WILLIAM JAY SCHIEFFELIN born November 30, 1891; married May 4, 1918 Annette Markoe The New York Times, March 29, 1987; Announcement has been made by Dr. Joseph Frederick Speyer of Willington, Conn., and Anne Mattison Speyer of Middletown Springs, Vt., of the engagement of their daughter Katherine Elsa Speyer to Michael Markoe Schieffelin, a son of Mr and Mrs William Jay Schieffelin 3d of Mount Kisco NY. The wedding is to be in July. Miss Speyer, a field representative for the Macmillan publishing house, graduated from Skidmore College. Her father is a professor of molecular and cell biology at the University of Connecticut. Her mother is a freelance writer and public relations consultant. Mr Schieffelin, who graduated from the Millbrook School and attended Skidmore College, is a student at Pace University and a group activities coordinator at the Presbyterian Church of Mount Kisco. His father is a consultant for and retired chairman of Schieffelin & Company, the wine and spirits importer; he represented the seventh generation of his family to head the concern, which was founded in 1794 as a pharmaceutical company by Jacob Schieffelin. The future bridegroom is a descendant of John Jay, the first Chief Justice of the United States, and Cornelius Vanderbilt, the railroad magnate. He is a grandson of Mrs. Schieffelin Jr of New York and Ashville, Me., and the late Mr. Schieffelin, who headed the Schieffeline Company, and the later Mr and Mrs William Ross Proctor of Pittstown NJ and Eldred NY. The New York Times, August 30, 1987; At the Presbyterian Church in Mt Kisco NY, Susan Elizabeth Utley, a daughter of Mr and Mrs George Dexter Utley 3d of Pound Ridge NY, was married yesterday to Timothy Proctor Schieffelin, a son of Mr and Mrs William Jay Schieffelin 3d of Mount Kisco. The bride, a magna cum laude graduate of New Hampshire College, is the vice president of Searle & Company, stockbrokers in Greenwich, Conn. She was presented at the Colonial Ball in New York. Her previous marriage ended in divorce. Her father is president of Utley’s Inc, a packaging design concern in Woodside, Queens. Mr Schieffelin is a vice president of Citibank in the Asia-Pacific division of the international financial institutions group. He graduated from the Bedford-Rippowam Schoo, the Berkshire School and Washington and Jefferson College. His father retired as chairman of Schieffelin & Company, the wine and spirits importer, and of Almay Inc., a cosmetics concern. A Timothy Proctor Schieffelin (Photo), Senior Vice President, Bank of America Private Bank (CT), was appointed to the National Forest Foundation’s Board of Directors.
