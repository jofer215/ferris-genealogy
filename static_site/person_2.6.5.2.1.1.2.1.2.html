HENRY CHURCHILL DEMILLE

ID: 2.6.5.2.1.1.2.1.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.5.2.1.1.2.1.2) HENRY CHURCHILL DEMILLE (1853-1893) married 1876 Matilda Beatrice Samuel “Bebe” born January 30, 1853 Liverpool, England and died October 8, 1923 Hollywood CA. Henry was a teacher and poet, as well as a playwright. He taught English at Columbia, preached sermons from the pulpit and wrote plays.
