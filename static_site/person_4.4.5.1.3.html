STEPHEN LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.5.1.3 | Source: Mary
(4.4.5.1.3) STEPHEN LOCKWOOD (#811) born at about the same time his father died; resided at Greenwich until March, 1821 when he moved to Mt. Pleasant {now Ossining}, Westchester Co NY where he purchased about 100 acres of land about a mile and a half northeast from Sing Sing and in 1878, this land was known as the Lockwood Farm; married January 1, 1792 Sarah Ingersoll [dau of Simon & Elizabeth (Scofield) Ingersoll] born January 13, 1766.
