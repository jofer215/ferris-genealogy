LOUISA SCOFIELD

ID: 2.5.3.2.11.1.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.2.11.1.2) LOUISA SCOFIELD born December 1, 1808 Stamford and died there December 4, 1874; buried Turn of River Cemetery; seamstress; married October 14, 1832 in Stamford, George Buxton (5.2.2.2.9.3) – see him for offspring

No children recorded
