WILLIAM HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.7.1.3.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.7.1.3.2) WILLIAM HOYT (#3557) born July 10, 1771 and died September 7, 1820 at Greenfield. He was a merchant in New York City and lived in Black Rock and Greenfield CT; married June 1808 Harriet Hubbell who died October 12, 1842 age 56y 5m; and born to them were:
