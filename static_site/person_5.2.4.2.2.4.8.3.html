CHARLES EDWARD ROGERS WINTHROP

ID: 5.2.4.2.2.4.8.3 | Source: James
(5.2.4.2.2.4.8.3) CHARLES EDWARD ROGERS WINTHROP born February 15, 1847 Tamaroa, Perry, IL and died there July 2, 1877; proprietor of a saloon; married November 23, 1873 in IL, as her 1st of 3 husbands, Nancy Jane Kirkpatrick [dau of John Foster & Hester Ann (Dial) Kirkpatrick] born May 10, 1853 Franklin Co IL and died November 6, 1936 Carthage, Jasper, MO; buried Mound Valley, Labette, KS; and born to them were:
