EVA LEONA FERRIS

ID: 3.4.5.1.2.5.5.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.4.5.1.2.5.5.4) EVA LEONA FERRIS (twin) born March 24, 1857 Hermon, Knox, IL and died March 19, 1947 Monmouth, Warren, IL; married in Knox Co, J. Edwin Day; resided at Roodhouse, Green Co IL and Monmouth. She was a member of the Eastern Start; Presbyterian Church and DAR. She was educated at St. Mary’s School, Knoxville. She had three grandchildren and five greatgrandchildren.

No children recorded
