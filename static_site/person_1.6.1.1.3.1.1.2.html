DEBORAH ANN SEE

ID: 1.6.1.1.3.1.1.2 | Source: John
(1.6.1.1.3.1.1.2) DEBORAH ANN SEE born February 28, 1843 NY (Armonk?) and died March 15, 1925 Armonk NY; buried St. Stevens Cemetery, Armonk; married 1st George H. Lovelett [son of Harey & Mary (Sniffen) Lovelett] born April 1837 NY and died 1922 Armonk NY, a shoemaker; buried St. Stevens Cemetery; married 2d Daniel Searles. Born to Deborah and George were:

Children (5)

  • GRANT LOVELETT 1.6.1.1.3.1.1.2.3
  • ELLSWORTH LOVELETT 1.6.1.1.3.1.1.2.4
