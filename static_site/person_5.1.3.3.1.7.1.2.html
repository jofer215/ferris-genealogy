JOSEPHINE FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.7.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.7.1.2) JOSEPHINE FERRIS born June 25, 1854 Stamford and died there January 31, 1878; buried there Long Ridge Union Cemetery; married November 28, 1871 in Stamford, John William Holly [son of Charles H & Sarah E (Searles) Holly] born May 16, 1850 Stamford and died there January 7, 1924 (after Josephine’s death, John married Sarah Ann Dann (4.6.2.4.4.4.11.1)); he is also buried at Long Ridge; and born to them was: (5.1.3.3.1.7.1.2.1) ARTHUR L HOLLY born July 2, 1875 Stamford and died there February 15, 1883; buried there Long Ridge Union Cemetery

No children recorded
