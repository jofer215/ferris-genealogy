SETH PALMER QUINTARD

ID: 2.6.3.5.1.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.3.5.1.3) SETH PALMER QUINTARD born September 18, 1792 Stamford and died April 19, 1878 Greenwich; buried Woodland Cemetery; married December 23, 1822 in Greenwich, Susan Letitia Ferris (3.6.1.2.2.4). Seth was in the 9th Regiment, War of 1812; farmer 1878; and born to them were:
