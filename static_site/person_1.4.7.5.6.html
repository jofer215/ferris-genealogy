MARIA ANN FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.5.6 | Source: John
(1.4.7.5.6) MARIA ANN FERRIS born November 20, 1813 Swanton and died January 8, 1867/8 Paris, France, with her brother and family; married May 13, 1832 Richard Frederick Fletcher born Ludlow VT April 15, 1809 and died 1895(?) [Richard was the son of Asa and Rebecca (Fletcher) Fletcher.] Richard was a merchant in Ludlow VT and Boston MA and carried on an extensive trade; died of cholera while visiting in St. Paul MN. Born to Maria and Richard were: (1.4.7.5.6.1) RICHARD FREDERICK FLETCHER, JR., born October 28, 1833 Ludlow VT and died after 1906; married July 11, 1855 Emma L. Alvord born August 6, 1838 Indianapolis IN and died California; no issue
