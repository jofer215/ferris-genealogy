NEHEMIAH LOUNSBURY

ID: 4.7.8 | Source: Mary
(4.7.8) NEHEMIAH LOUNSBURY (#92) born December 23, 1717 Stamford and died September 26, 1790 Bedford, Westchester, NY; served in the 2d Regiment, Westchester Co Militia; married Sarah Webb (2.5.1.2.1) and born to them were: (4.7.8.1) JEREMIAH LOUNSBURY born 1746 and died April 12, 1820 Bedford NY; buried there; unmarried
