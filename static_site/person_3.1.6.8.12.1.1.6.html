HARVEY ELMER ROSE

ID: 3.1.6.8.12.1.1.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.12.1.1.6) HARVEY ELMER ROSE born January 1, 1893 Haley Station, Renfrew Co and died July 5, 1925 in Detroit MI; buried in Smiths Falls, Lanark Co, Ontario; a Railroader; married December 18, 1918 in Balderson, Lanark Co, Tena MacNaughton [dau of John and Sarah McNaughton] born January 17, 1892 Perth, Lanark Co and died December 9, 1960 Smiths Falls, Lanark Co; buried there; Tena was a schoolteacher; and born to them were:
