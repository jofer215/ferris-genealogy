ERNEST THORNLEY SLAWSON

ID: 4.4.3.2.2.4.7.2 | Source: Mary
(4.4.3.2.2.4.7.2) ERNEST THORNLEY SLAWSON born March 2, 1887 Stamford CT and died there March 21, 1941; buried High Ridge Cemetery; married September 7, 1914 in Pound Ridge NY, Minnie Elizabeth Northrup [dau of Charles & Jane Elizabeth (Lockwood) Northrup] born May 28, 1880 Stamford and died there after 1944

No children recorded
