JAMES OSCAR FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.5.5 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.5.5) JAMES OSCAR FERRIS born October 6, 1855 Marion, Decatur, IN and died 1932 Upland, Dickinson, KS age 72y with a milk pail and stool in his hand, down on his farm; married 1st December 3, 1878 in Decatur IN, Perlina Ann Robbins [dau of Jonathon & Magaret Ann (Spillman) Robbnis] born June 4, 1859 Marion, Decatur, IN and died 1931 (were they divorced); 2d 1925 in Chapman, Dickinson, KS Tillie Ketchem; and born to him and Perlina were:

Children (4)

  • ALBERT LEONARD FERRIS 5.1.3.4.10.5.5.1
  • DEBORAH ANN FERRIS 5.1.3.4.10.5.5.2
  • CHARLES O FERRIS 5.1.3.4.10.5.5.3
  • ELMER WARREN FERRIS 5.1.3.4.10.5.5.4
