MIRIAM CHADEAYNE

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.4.3.2.2 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.4.3.2.2) MIRIAM CHADEAYNE (Photo) born November 21, 1900 and died June 12, 1984 Ossining NY; buried there Dale Cemetery; never married; attended the Ossining Public School and Connecticut College as well as the New Paltz NY Normal School. She taught for a time in the Ossining Public Schools but retired because of her mother’s ill health.

No children recorded
