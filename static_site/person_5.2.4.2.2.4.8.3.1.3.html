CHARLES EDWARD ROGERS WINTHROP

ID: 5.2.4.2.2.4.8.3.1.3 | Source: James
(5.2.4.2.2.4.8.3.1.3) CHARLES EDWARD ROGERS WINTHROP born June 29, 1905 Oswego, Labette, KS and died August 13, 1977 Columbus, Franklin, OH; CPA, managert of a theater; married 1st 1926 in KS, Viola Gladys Krouse born November 1, 1904 Wheaton, Pottawatomie, KS and died August 18, 1987 Hillsborough Co FL – divorced c1929; married 2d December 31, 1930 in Wichita, Thais Mae Lacazette born January 5, 1911 Fruitvale, Oakland, CA and died April 13, 1996 Columbus OH – divorced c1955

No children recorded
