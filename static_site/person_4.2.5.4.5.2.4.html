ALMON SQUIRES DUNNING

ID: 4.2.5.4.5.2.4 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.4.5.2.4) ALMON SQUIRES DUNNING born April 12, 1827 Rootstown Township, Portage Co OH and died September 15, 1919; dealt in iron and steel for several years but engaged in farming in 1880; owned 164 acres of land of the old home farm; married December 29, 1847 Edith A. Holcomb [dau of William A & Lydia A] born April 19, 1829; and born to them were:
