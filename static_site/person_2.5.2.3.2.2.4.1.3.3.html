WILLIAM EDWARD MARTIN

ID: 2.5.2.3.2.2.4.1.3.3 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.3.2.2.4.1.3.3) WILLIAM EDWARD MARTIN (Jimmy) born April 27, 1902 Bancroft MI died in New Orleans LA – accident/murdered?; musician/composer; hosted his own radio jazz show in Detroit in the 1930s; eventually became one of the composers for Paul Whiteman and his band; married August 29, 1936 in Detroit, as her 1st husband, Rose Rosalee Lopusnak [da of Thomas & Catherine (Majaco) Lopusnak] born September 2, 1905 Pleasant Valley PA and died June 12, 1985 Madison Heightw MI– separated in 1939; she was buried Grandlawn Cemetery, Redford, Wayne, MI; she was the owner of Marwood Market in Gingerville MI; Jimmy befriended a woman by the name of Sophie Tucker, the last of the red-hot mamas with known connections to tbe Mob. He became acquainted with her daughter, against Sophie’s wishes, and after one of his late night performances, was found in the alley with a fatal gunshot wound to the head; and born to them was:
