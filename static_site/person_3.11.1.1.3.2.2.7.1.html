MARTHA ELIZABETH CLARK

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.1) MARTHA ELIZABETH CLARK (Mattie) (Bonnie to her grandkids) born October 14, 1876 Kasson Township, Leelanau Co MI and died April 9, 1958 Traverse City MI; buried there Oakwood Cemetery; practical nurse; machine operator; married Milan Lee Smith [son of Allen Mervin & Emma Jane (Alanson) Smith] born November 21, 1864 MI and died February 18, 1920 Traverse City; laborer, farmer; and born to them were:
