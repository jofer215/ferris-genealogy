JAMES LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.5.3.1.7 | Source: Mary
(4.5.3.1.7) JAMES LOCKWOOD (#656) (Captain) born April 17, 1775 Stamford CT and died 1812. When he became of age, he moved to Madison County NY; married November 9, 1797 at Lebanon, Madison County NY Marilda Ferris (3.1.1.1.5.1). They resided at Lebanon NY. James was a Captain in the War of 1812. After James’ death, Marilda married James Congdon. Born to them were:

Children (6)
