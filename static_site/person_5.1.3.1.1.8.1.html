JOSEPH ZIBA FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.1.8.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.1.8.1) JOSEPH ZIBA FERRIS born 1823 and died April 15, 1865; buried Tuthill-Ferris Cemetery, Symrna NY; married Augusta May born May 19, 1825 and died February 9, 1884; buried with her husband. [Joseph Z. Ferris, 24 of Smyrna, married Caroline Alcott, 22, of Columbus, December 25, 1847 by Rev. Andrew Hull, New Berlin. - - don’t know if this is the same Joseph - may be some confusion with this Joseph Z and a Joseph Z in the (3) Joseph line.]
