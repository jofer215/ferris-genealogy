EVA L.

ID: 2.1.4.1.3.9.5 | Source: Peter
(2.1.4.1.3.9.5) EVA L. (LODUSKA?) FERRIS born August 22, 1873 Markham Township, York, Ontario and died June 8, 1918 Nottawasaga Township, Simcoe, Ontario; buried Stayner Union Cemetery, Stayner, Simcoe Co, Ontario; married February 6, 1889 in Markham Township, Jessie Engle Doner [son of John & Euphemia (Schell) Doner] born August 29, 1865 Markham Township, and died April 15, 1941 Nottawasaga Township, Simcoe Co, Ontario; buried with Eva.
