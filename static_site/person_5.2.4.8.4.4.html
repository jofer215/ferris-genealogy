SALLY ELIZABETH BATES

ID: 5.2.4.8.4.4 | Source: James
(5.2.4.8.4.4) SALLY ELIZABETH BATES born May 1, 1811 CT and died December 21, 1860 Darien CT; married November 7, 1830 in Darien, William Henry Bates [son of Henry & Fanny (Quintard) Bates] born September 21, 1805 and died March 24, 1849 Darien; both buried there Bates Cemetery. Born to them were: (5.2.4.8.4.4.1) HENRY W. BATES born October 8, 1831 CT and died May 8, 1892 Darien CT; buried Bates Cemetery. He married c1855 Louise M. {maiden name unk} born 1836 NY (parents from VT) and born to them was:

Children (3)
