DANIEL LAMONT KINCH

ID: 3.10.1.2.5.6.1.1.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.5.6.1.1.4) DANIEL LAMONT KINCH born 1893 Walton, Delaware, NY and died 1929 Cadosia NY; was killed during a sleet storm when he lost his footing and fell from the top of a moving freight train in the railroad yards of the NYO & WRR; shipping clerk for Bordens Condensed Milk Co; married 1916 Marjorie Murray (c1895-); and born to them were:
