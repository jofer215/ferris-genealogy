ABRAHAM HILL

ID: 3.6.3.2.2.2.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.2.2.1) ABRAHAM HILL born December 23, 1808 probably in Flatbrookville, Sussex, NJ and died January 2, 1889 Warren Co NJ at 80 years of age; interred Cedar Ridge Cemetery, Warren Co NJ; married August 22, 1835 probably in Warren or Sussex Co NJ, Anna Elizabeth LePort. Born to them were:

Children (9)

  • ELIZABETH HILL 3.6.3.2.2.2.1.2

  • JULIAN 3.6.3.2.2.2.1.4
  • JAMES HILL 3.6.3.2.2.2.1.5
  • JOHN HILL 3.6.3.2.2.2.1.6
  • WILLIAM HILL 3.6.3.2.2.2.1.7
  • ISAAC LAPORTE HILL was also referred to as Issas L., or Ike L.; unmarried 3.6.3.2.2.2.1.8
  • LEMUEL HILL 3.6.3.2.2.2.1.9
