KAREN SUE MAGNER

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.10.5.1.1.3 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.10.5.1.1.3) KAREN SUE MAGNER (Photo) born December 20, 1955 White Plains NY; married October 11, 1975 Michael Noll born March 9, 1955 – divorced 1979. "Kerry" born in White Plains, NY, raised in Ossining and attended St. Augustine's school; relocated with her parents to Columbus, Ohio in 1971. There, she attended Worthington HS and married Michael Noll in Oct. 1975. She married Christopher R. Lantz in November 1979. She has two daughters, Andrea Leigh Lantz (adopted by Christopher at age 5) and Stephanie Jo Lantz. In 1984, she moved to Plant City, FL outside Tampa. Kerry was a homemaker on a small farm where we had several horses, dogs, cats, ducks and chickens - a very long way from a former 'city girl' from Ossining! She went back to school in 1989 becoming a medical assistant. Presently, she works from home as a surgical medical coder for the department of surgery at the University of South Florida.
