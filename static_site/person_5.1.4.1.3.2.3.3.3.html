JESSE LEON HALSTEAD

ID: 5.1.4.1.3.2.3.3.3 | Source: James
(5.1.4.1.3.2.3.3.3) JESSE LEON HALSTEAD born 1880 Buchanan Co IA and died 1943 Boone IA; buried Linwoodpark, Boone Co IA; married 1st Dorothy Helen Collins Shuey (c1884-) and they had a child; 2d 1907 in Boone IA, Geneva Lucille Darrock born 1887 MO and died in childbirth 1908 Boone Co IA along with her son; 3d 1912 Margaret Hall (c1885-); and born to them was:
