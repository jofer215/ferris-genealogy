HENRY LELAND WEBB

ID: 2.1.1.1.7.4.5 | Source: Peter
(2.1.1.1.7.4.5) HENRY LELAND WEBB born January 1, 1820 and died January 11, 1885; lived in Plainfield NJ; married 1st November 10, 1841 Anna C. Hull; 2d Susie Louise Davenport. It is not stated which of the wives was mother of his children. Born to them were:

Children (6)

  • CHARLES HULL WEBB 2.1.1.1.7.4.5.1
  • HENRY IRVING WEBB 2.1.1.1.7.4.5.2
  • OTIS WEBB 2.1.1.1.7.4.5.3
  • VICTOR HUGO WEBB 2.1.1.1.7.4.5.4
  • ERNEST CARLETON WEBB 2.1.1.1.7.4.5.5
  • ARTHUR CARROLL WEBB 2.1.1.1.7.4.5.6
