LEWIS KNAPP

ID: 2.6.7.4.4 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.4.4) LEWIS KNAPP born November 18, 1787 Litchfield CT and died April 5 (15), 1868 Farmington (Penfield?) NY; buried with his wives at Friend Cemetery, Farmington NY; married 1st December 13, 1813 at Stanwich CT Betsey Davis; 2d Martha Hall born May 12, 1780 and died January 16, 1845.
