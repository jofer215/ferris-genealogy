BENJAMIN SCOFIELD

ID: 5.2.3.2.5.1 | Source: James
(5.2.3.2.5.1) BENJAMIN SCOFIELD born March 2, 1804 Stamford and died there January 5, 1884; 1850 farmer; married January 21, 1835 in Stamford, Susan Ferris (5.1.3.6.4.2); both buried there Woodland Cemetery; see her for offspring since they were entered there first

No children recorded
