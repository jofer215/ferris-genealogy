SARAH JANE SMITH

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.2.2 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.2.2) SARAH JANE SMITH born March 6, 1856 Carmel NY and died July 21, 1921 Putnam Valley NY; married January1, 1875 at Kent NY, Egbert Joshua Tompkins [son of Andrew Jackson & Phebe Jane (Horton)] (1855-1928). Obituary, Highland Democrat, Peekskill, NY, Saturday, July 23, 1921: Sarah Jane Tompkins, wife of Joshua Tompkins, died at the home of her daughter, Mrs. Allen Gilberts, Thursday night in her 66th year. She had been ill a long time. Mrs. Tompkins born at Carmel, March 6, 1856, the daughter of Alexander and Mary (Christian) Smith, and her life has been spent in Putnam County. She was known for her many womanly virtues and was a faithful wife and devoted mother. She was married to Mr. Tompkins at Kent, on Jan. 1, 1875. She is survived by her husband, two daughters, Mrs. Thaddeus Tompkins and Mrs. Allen Gilberts, and five sons, Tilden, Arthur, Frank, Augustus and Jesse. She also leaves one brother, Alexander Smith of Danbury, Conn. The deceased was a member of the Methodist Church and the funeral services, which will be held from the late residence Monday afternoon at 2 o'clock, will be conducted by Rev. W.G. Gritman of Berlin, a former pastor of the Putnam Valley charge. The interment will be at Hillside. Born to them were:
