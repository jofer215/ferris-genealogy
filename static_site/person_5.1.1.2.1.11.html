BROCKHOLST L. MEAD

ID: 5.1.1.2.1.11 | Source: James
(5.1.1.2.1.11) BROCKHOLST L. MEAD born August 8, 1797 and died June 21, 1874 (5.1.1.2.2) SOLOMON MEAD (8RJX-7K) born July 17, 1754 and died 1777 in the Rev. War; married January 1, 1777 Eunice Mead (8RKB-2B) [dau of Abram] born October 9, 1755 Greenwich – no offspring; after Solomon’s death, she apparently married Benjamin Weed (2.5.3.10.5)

No children recorded
