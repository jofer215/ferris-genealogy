NATHAN PECK

ID: 3.10.1.2.1.9 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.1.9) NATHAN PECK born January 27, 1783 Milan, Dutchess, NY and died April 6, 1872 Cortlandville, Cortland, NY; married February 29, 1804 in Milan Township, Sarah “Sally” Beebe [dau of Ephraim & Elisabeth (Brown)] born November 23, 1787 New London CT and died April 8, 1856 South Bend IN; first settled in Chenango County, whither he went with his father in 1795, and was ordained as a Baptist minister in July, 1814. Though devoting considerable attention to farming, he discharged his official duties for many years with much ability and acceptance. Born to them were:
