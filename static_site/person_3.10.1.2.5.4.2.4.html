WEALTHY A SHAVER

ID: 3.10.1.2.5.4.2.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.5.4.2.4) WEALTHY A SHAVER (Weatha A) born 1845 Steuben Co NY and died after 1920; married 1864 in Aurilius, Ingham, MI Elbridge Franklin [son of John & Lucy L (Carter) Franklin] born January 14, 1840 Lenawee Co MI and died 1915 1920 MI; Civil War Vet served as a Private with Co C, 5th Michigan Infantry; farmer and schoolteacher; and born to them were:
