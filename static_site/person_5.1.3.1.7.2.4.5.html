JOEL STEARNS

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.4.5 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.4.5) JOEL STEARNS (Photo) born December 27, 1847 Phelps, Ontario, NY and died March 4, 1907/8; buried Ferris Center Cemetery, Ferris Township, Montcalm Co MI; a farmer, a stage driver, and mail carrier; purported to have said: "We have been respected and have tried to deserve it; and are all poor enough to be honest"; married July 31, 1879 Nora Robbins born March 16, 1862/63 Oswego Co NY; and born to them were:
