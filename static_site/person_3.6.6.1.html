ID: 3.6.6.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.6.1) JAMES SHAW was a minor in 1765, when his father, William, died, and Thomas Youngs, Deborah's (3.6.2) husband was appointed as his guardian on 31 October 1767. James served as a Private in Captain Jonathan Bell's Company, Lieutenant Colonel John Mead's Regiment from 13 August to 7 September 1776, and again, from 24 October to 24 December 1776, and again, from 31 December 1776 to January 7, 1777; he married on 9 April 1778 at the Congregational Church, Darien, Hannah Weed. James died 16 November 1828, aged 76 years; Hannah died 26 January 1833. Born to James and Hannah were:

Children (7)

  • NANCY SHAW baptized 7 March 1779; died 20 January 1799 age 20y 16d 3.6.6.1.1
  • JAMES SHAW baptized 9 July 1780; died 28 April 1800 age 19y 3.6.6.1.2
  • POLLY 3.6.6.1.3
  • HANNAH SHAW 3.6.6.1.4
  • WILLIAM SHAW 3.6.6.1.5
  • FREDERICK SHAW died on or before 1828; had surviving children: 3.6.6.1.6
  • ANDREW SHAW 3.6.6.1.7
