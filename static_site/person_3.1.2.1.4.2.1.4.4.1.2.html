CHARLES OREN VAIL

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.2.1.4.4.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.2.1.4.4.1.2) CHARLES OREN VAIL born December 12, 1909 Lakewood NJ and died April 7, 1988 Denver CO; graduated from Cornell 1934, degree in Civil Engineering; moved to Venezuela with Chicago Bridge and Iron, employed to clear the jungles and construct an oil camp; later worked most of his career with Creole Petroleum which was the foreign subsidiary of Standard Oil; married July 23, 1940 in Rawlins WY, Marjorie Anna Rendle [dau of Irvine John & Jessie (Denmark) Rendle] born July 23, 1911 Rawlins WY and died October 26, 2000 Denver CO; both buried there Hampden Memorial Gardens (Photo); she graduated from U of Wyoming and at one time taught all 8 grades; immigrated 1937 to teach school in Venezuela where she met Charlie; and they had 2 kids

No children recorded
