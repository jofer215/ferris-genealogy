LUKE ADOLPHUS LOCKWOOD, L.L.D.

ID: 4.4.5.5.2.7 | Source: Mary
(4.4.5.5.2.7) LUKE ADOLPHUS LOCKWOOD, L.L.D. (#1538) born December 1, 1833 Greenwich and died there in November 1905; buried Stamford; married September 11, 1862 at Greenwich, Mary Louise Lyon [dau of William Lewis & Catharine (Mead) Lyon] born March 10, 1841 Greenwich. Luke attended Greenwich Academy and Trinity College; was a lawyer in NYC; trustee of Trinity College; Episcopalian; Democrat; wrote Masonic Law; received his L.L.D., from Trinity College; prominent Mason and President of Masonic Home, Wallingford CT. Resided in Greenwich, though first four kids were born in Brooklyn. Born to them were:
