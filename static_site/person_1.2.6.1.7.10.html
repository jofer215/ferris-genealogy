GEORGE CLARKE UNDERHILL, M.D.,

ID: 1.2.6.1.7.10 | Source: John
(1.2.6.1.7.10) GEORGE CLARKE UNDERHILL, M.D., (1819-1903) buried LaGrange OH; Civil War Surgeon with 11th Ohio Cavalry; served as surgeon at Fort Laramie October – December 1863; the first bivouac site at Fort Laramie was dubbed “Camp Underhill”; Rose Bud Reservation SD; married 1st 1842 Harriett Terrell (1818-1890); and born to them were:
