DANIEL S. FERRIS

ID: 1.6.1.4.4.7 | Source: John
(1.6.1.4.4.7) DANIEL S. FERRIS (1824-) married twice: Jane and Esther - maiden names unk (1.6.1.4.5) DANIEL FERRIS JR., born Bedford October 29, 1785 and died there September 4, 1830; married Mary Ann Holmes (Miller) born Bedford May 29, 1793 and died presumably there February 21, 1858. Daniel was a farmer on the Post Road south of Bedford near his relatives; both buried in the Ferris Cemetery, plots 3 and 41

No children recorded
