HAROLD BRAYTON FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.10.5.6.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.10.5.6.1) HAROLD BRAYTON FERRIS born August 17, 1893 Ottawa Co OH and died March 18, 1966; buried in the Fort Rosecrans National Cemetery, San Diego CA (Photo); left high school after his sophomore year; became self-educated, and taught in a country school, grades 1-8, while a young man; served in WWI in the 353d Infantry, 89th Division, and was severely wounded during the Argonne Forest battle the day before the war ended. Later he became a bank president in Dighton KS; Mayor of Dighton; and then president of the school board. During his school board tenure he had the old elementary school replaced by a modern building. In later life he worked as an auditor for the U. S. Treasury Department, traveling out of St. Louis MO; married Elizabeth Johns (1896-1983) who is buried with Harold; after retirement, they moved to California. Born to them were:
