LEONARD FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.1.6.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.6.5) LEONARD FERRIS born August 4, 1791 Harpersfield NY and died January 25, 1854; War of 1812 Veteran; he purchased the cemetery plot that he and his family are buried in; married December 17, 1815 Jane Losey born December 26, 1790 and died December 30, 1873; both buried Lakeview Cemetery, Interlaken, Seneca, NY (Photos); and born to them were:
