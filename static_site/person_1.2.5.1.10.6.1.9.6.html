HANNAH BIRDSELL

ID: 1.2.5.1.10.6.1.9.6 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.10.6.1.9.6) HANNAH BIRDSELL born October 9, 1892 Stamford CT – WoW – Harriet didn’t get much rest between these 2 kids – and died there c1987; buried there Fairfield Memorial Park Cemetery (Oaklawn); married 1st William John Powell born April 8, 1894 Copley PA and died 1918 Stamford; buried there Long Ridge; 2d John Belasco; 3d Mr Dalianis; and born to her and Willliam were:

Children (3)

  • EARL EDWARD POWELL 1.2.5.1.10.6.1.9.6.2
  • DOROTHY HANNAH POWELL 1.2.5.1.10.6.1.9.6.3
