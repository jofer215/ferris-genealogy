LEVINA

ID: 2.3.3.3.1 | Source: Peter
(2.3.3.3.1) LEVINA (LAVINA) BISHOP born July 29, 1769 Stamford and died there October 16, 1815; buried St. Andrew’s & St. John’s Cemetery; married (April 1, 1794 at Stamford CT, John Haight born July 19, 1773/74; of Cortlandt NY) or (December 20, 1795 in Stamford, Isaac Holly [son of Nathan & Hannah (Jagger) Holly] born May 26, 1768 Stamford and died there January 22, 1827; buried with Levina) (after Levina died Isaac married 1816 Eleanor Bishop and they had 5 kids); will go with Holly marriage for now and born to them were:
