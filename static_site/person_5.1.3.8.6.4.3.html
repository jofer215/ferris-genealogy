HARRY TRISTAM FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.6.4.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.4.3) HARRY TRISTAM FERRIS born May 2, 1868 Greenwich and died May 31, 1921 Miami FL; graduate of Yale U., and Columbia Law School; while at Yale he was on the varsity crew and football team; was with a law office at 30 Williams Street, New York City; moved to Miami 1899 and became one of its leading citizens and served many years as deputy collector of customs for the port of Miami; married March 30, 1903 Mary Margaret Carpenter of Miami FL. Born to them were:

Children (2)
