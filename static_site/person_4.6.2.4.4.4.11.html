SETH S. DANN

ID: 4.6.2.4.4.4.11 | Source: Mary
(4.6.2.4.4.4.11) SETH S. DANN born 1830 North Stamford and died 1902 Stamford; buried there High Ridge Cemetery; cooper 1858-74; married 1st c1852 Mary Emily Slater (2.1.3.3.1.2.9.1); married 2d November 27, 1862 in Stamford, Emily Jane Webb [dau of Alfred & Sarah (Weed) Webb] born January 22, 1840 Stamford and died there 1912; buried with Seth; and born to him and Mary were:

Children (4)

  • SARAH ANN DANN 4.6.2.4.4.4.11.1
  • VALARA DANN 4.6.2.4.4.4.11.2
  • JOSIE LINCOLN DANN 4.6.2.4.4.4.11.3
  • HERBERT IRVING DANN 4.6.2.4.4.4.11.4
