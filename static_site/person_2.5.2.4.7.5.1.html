WILLIAM HAIT

ID: 2.5.2.4.7.5.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.7.5.1) WILLIAM HAIT (Hoyt) born August 25, 1800 Stamford CT and died there 1872; 1850 merchant and 1860 a dry goods merchant; married October 3, 1832 in Stamford Emeline Hoyt (2.5.2.4.5.5.1); both buried Woodland Cemetery, Stamford; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • THEODORE WOOD HOYT 2.5.2.4.7.5.1.1
  • SARAH MATILDA HOYT 2.5.2.4.7.5.1.2
  • ANN AUGUSTA HOYT 2.5.2.4.7.5.1.3
