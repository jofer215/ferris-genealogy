EDWIN LEWIS SCOFIELD

ID: 5.2.1.1.2.1.1.1.1 | Source: James
(5.2.1.1.2.1.1.1.1) EDWIN LEWIS SCOFIELD Jr born August 25, 1887 Stamford and died 1965 Pinehurst NC; LLB 1911 from New York Law School; enlisted 1911 in the Connecticut National Guard; member of the Yale Club of NY; the Wee Burn Gold Club of Noroton CT; and the Stamford Yacht Club; Justice of the Peace; insurance; married July 2, 1918 in NYC, Priscilla Mckraig Beall born September 26, 1887

No children recorded
