WILLIAM AUGUSTUS LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.2.1.1.1 | Source: Mary
(4.4.2.1.1.1) WILLIAM AUGUSTUS LOCKWOOD (#1465) born September 7, 1826 New Canaan CT and died December 20, 1883 Stamford; buried there Woodland Cemetery; blacksmith 1850-70; merchant 1883; married February 14, 1848 at Norwalk CT, Catharine Emily Kellogg [dau of John & Sally (Kellogg) Kellogg] born January 24, 1825 Norwalk; they resided in Stamford in the 1880's; and born to them were:

Children (8)
