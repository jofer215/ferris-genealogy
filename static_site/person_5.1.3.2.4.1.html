CATHERINE FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.2.4.1 | MCV9-3T | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.4.1) CATHERINE FERRIS (AFN: MCV9-3T) born August 23, 1815 and died June 23, 1880 Franklin (New Castle?) IN; married October 10, 1841 in Henry (Franklin) Co IN, as his 2d wife, James Morris Clements (AFN: MCV9-2N) [son of Richard & Tamzen (Morris) Clements Sr] born March 2, 1805 Caroline Co MD and died June 29, 1880/1 IN; he was from Blooming Grove, Indiana. Children (2)
