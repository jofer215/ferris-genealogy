LILLIAN VIRIGINIA WHELPLEY

ID: 5.1.3.5.6.3.2.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.5.6.3.2.1) LILLIAN VIRIGINIA WHELPLEY born December 11, 1885 Gravesend, Kings, NY; married 1st 1906 Walter Church supposedly died Caldwell ID c1919; 2d Charles Fraser supposedly died c1939 Elgin IL; and 3d Charles Wolfe supposed died c1950 Elgin IL – may have been a daughter born to the 1st or 2d marriage

No children recorded
