WILLIAM LANE

ID: 4.2.5.5.7.1.2.1 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.5.7.1.2.1) WILLIAM LANE born 1831 Ulster Co NY; Civil War Vet- Private in Company E, 46th Illinois Infantry Regiment; 5’6” tall, brown hair, grey eyes; mustered out June 16, 1865 Nashville TN; buried Grove Hill Cemetery in Mt Pleasant Township IL; according to the ‘Net there was a William Lane, 1st Lieutenant from Morrison IL entered service December 1, 1861 and discharged September 11, 1862 in Company E – must be a different William Lane – plenty of info on this regiment on the ‘Net

No children recorded
