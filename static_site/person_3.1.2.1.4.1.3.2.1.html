GEORGE H GODFREY

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.1) GEORGE H GODFREY born September 16, 1839 Stafford, Genesee, NY and died January 24, 1925 New Albany, Floyd, IN; buried there Fairview Cemetery (Photo); New Albany Weekly Ledger Obit extract: “Mr. Godfrey was in charge of the local branch of the Western Union Telegraph Co for more than 50 years. He served during the War Between the States and handled many important messages from the Northern Forces. He took an active interest in civic affairs when a young man.” He married December 24, 1863 Emma Louise Johnson [dau of James & Sarah Ann (Ketcham) Johnson] born October 10, 1844 New Albany IN and died there November 1, 1914; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • HARRY AUGUSTUS GODFREY 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.1.1
  • JENNIE GODFREY 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.1.2
  • CARRIE J GODFREY 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.1.3
