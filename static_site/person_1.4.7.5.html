JONATHAN FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.5 | Source: John
(1.4.7.5) JONATHAN FERRIS born December 15, 1765 Litchfield CT (Grove Farm, Throg’s Neck, Westchester County NY) and died October 1, 1829 Swanton (Lownton) VT; buried in Old Cemetery at Swanton. Jonathan and his brother, Elijah, purchased 5,000 acres at Swanton Falls VT in 1803. The family moved from Burlington VT to Swanton in 1807. Jonathan laid out the Village and was a Selectman in 1807. He owned and operated a gristmill, a marble mill, and sawmill. He and his wife were members of Society of Friends, but after Jonathan’s death, Ursual joined the Congregational Church. He married, June 4, 1803, Ursula Catlin (Catfilno) (1780-1858) born in Swanton VT. They owned a marble quarry and became very wealthy. Born to them were:
