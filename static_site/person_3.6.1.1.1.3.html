JOHN WOOD FERRIS

ID: 3.6.1.1.1.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.1.1.1.3) JOHN WOOD FERRIS born October 27, 1808 and died May 8, 1875; buried First Congregational Church Cemetery, Greenwich; married 1st Jane Whiting (3.6.1.4.2.5); 2d widow Addie Wright. This was Addie’s third marriage; both previous husbands had children before she married them, so there were 3 sets of children in this family. Born to John and Jane were:
