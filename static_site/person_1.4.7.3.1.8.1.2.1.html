WALDEN PELL

ID: 1.4.7.3.1.8.1.2.1 | Source: John
(1.4.7.3.1.8.1.2.1) WALDEN PELL II born July 3, 1902 Quogue, Suffolk, NY and died March 23, 1983; diploma St Mark’s School, 1920; student Princeton 1920-23; Rhodes scholar, Oxford U 1923-26, MA, 1930; Union Theological Seminary, Columbia 195859; Master, Lenox School MA, 1926-30; first Headmaster, St Andrew’s School, Middletown DE, 1930-57; ordained deacon 1927; Priest 1928; Who’s Who in America; married August 25, 1928 Edith Minturn Bonsal and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • MELISSA PELL 1.4.7.3.1.8.1.2.1.1
  • STUYVESANT BONSAL PELL 1.4.7.3.1.8.1.2.1.2
  • MARY LEIGH PELL 1.4.7.3.1.8.1.2.1.3
