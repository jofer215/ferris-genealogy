AARON SUTTON

ID: 1.2.5.3.9.6 | Source: John
(1.2.5.3.9.6) AARON SUTTON born c1793 NY and died July 10, 1845 Washington, Dutchess, NY; resided Somers and Washington NY; married 1st December 19, 1816 in New Castle NY, Melissa Pierce [dau of James & Martha] born August 8, 1791 Chappaqua (Mt Pleasant) NY and died July 31, 1828 Mt Pleasant NY; 2d November 2, 1832 in Nine Partners NY, Anna Haight [dau of Silas & Rhoda (Washington) Haight]; 3d July 10, 1845 Hannah Carpenter [dau of William & Deborah (Cock) Carpenter] born January 18, 1798 Nort Castle NY and died 1876; and born to him and Melissa were:
