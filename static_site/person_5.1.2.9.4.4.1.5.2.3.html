PEARL MYRTLE TOMPKINS

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.1.5.2.3 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.1.5.2.3) PEARL MYRTLE TOMPKINS born November 9, 1910 Yorktown NY and died April 3, 1982 Carmel NY; married 1st Ernest E. Adams [son of Edgar & Martha (Barrett)] born November 5, 1903 Brewster NY and died there Feb. 28, 1965; both buried Raymond Hill Cemetery, Carmel NY; married 2d Fay Fletcher. Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, April 7, 1982: Mrs. Pearl M. Fletcher, 71, of Bryant Rd., Lake Carmel, died April 3, in Putnam Hospital Center, Carmel, after a long illness. She was the widow of Fay Fletcher. She was born Nov. 9, 1910, in Mohegan, daughter of the late Hart and Jessie Tompkins. She lived in Brewster and Patterson before moving to Lake Carmel, 12 years ago. She was a retired nurses aide at the Brookside Nursing Home in South Salem. She is survived by a son, Ernest Adams of Poughquag; 3 daughters, Mrs. Chaffie Strang of Lake Carmel, Mrs. Jessie Cole of Patterson and Mrs. Hazel Hayes of Abeline, Texas; 4 brothers, James Tompkins of Carmel, Ernest Tompkins of Somers, Robert Tompkins of Patterson and George Tompkins of Florida; 2 sisters, June Tompkins of Carmel and Dorothy Zaniboni of Massachusetts; 25 grandchildren and 19 great-grandchildren. Burial was in Raymond Hill cemetery, Carmel. Born to them were:
