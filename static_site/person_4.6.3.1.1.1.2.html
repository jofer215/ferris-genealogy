SARAH A. LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.3.1.1.1.2 | Source: Mary
(4.6.3.1.1.1.2) SARAH A. LOCKWOOD born March 9, 1824 and died August 4, 1845 Stamford; buried there Lockwood Cemetery #5; married February 25, 1844 in Stamford, James Finch [son of John & Sally (Ferris) Finch] (1819-); James’ 2d wife was Louise J. Knapp (2.6.2.10.1.4.1) – see her for offspring

No children recorded
