MARY HAIT

ID: 2.5.3.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.2) MARY HAIT (Hoyt) (#2764) born February 20, 1711/2 Stamford and was still living in 1761, when her father’s will was written; married January 6, 1736 at Stamford, Joseph Scofield [son of Joseph & Eunice (Buxton) Scofield] born April 29, 1710 Stamford and died there 1775; and born to them were:

Children (10)
