WALLACE WILBER

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.2.1.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.2.1.3) WALLACE WILBER born July 7, 1869 Russell, Geauga OH; married May 17, 1894 Gertrude Nutt born December 8, 1877 Russell OH (5.1.3.1.7.2.2.2) HARVEY FERRIS (Harvey H. Ferris) (Old Reliable) born July 26, 1833 Newbury, Geauga, OH and died April 3, 1924 Chagrin Falls OH age 90y 8m 7d; prosperous farmer in West Newbury OH; married 1st January 11, 1865 Maria Blair [dau of Roswell & Ann (Dean) Blair] born March 12, 1839 Newbury OH and died March 19, 1867; married 2d June 10, 1868 Ellen M. Waite (Wait) [dau of Samuel and Rachel] born August 27, 1837 Streetsboro OH and died February 17, 1932 Chagrin Falls OH at her daughter, Cora’s home, age 94y 5m 20d; both buried Munn Cemetery, Newbury Center, Geauga, OH; They made their home in Newbury until about 1908 when the moved to Chagrin Falls. Harvey was a Civil War Veteran, having served in the 171st Ohio Volunteery Infantry Regiment. He took great pleasure in helping others, and told his daughter with whom he lived, not to smother him with flowers, but to use the money flowers would cost to help helpless people.
