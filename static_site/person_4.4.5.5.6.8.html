JOSEPH ALBERT LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.5.5.6.8 | Source: Mary
(4.4.5.5.6.8) JOSEPH ALBERT LOCKWOOD born July 8, 1843 Greenwich CT and died in Ossining NY; 1888 resided in Mianus CT and was Chairman of the Selectmen of the town; married 1st December 1 (15), 1865 at Greenwich, Sarah A. Wescome [dau of William B. & Betsey (Bunker) Wescome]; married 2d July 21, 1888 widow Ida F. (Snyder) Greene. Born to Joseph and Sarah, were: (4.4.5.5.6.1.1) (ALBERTINE) BERTIE LOCKWOOD (#1540a) died January 29, 1875

No children recorded
