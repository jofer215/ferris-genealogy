ABBY MARIA UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.6.2.4.2.5 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.4.2.5) ABBY MARIA UNDERHILL born March 6, 1858 Wiota WI and died February 18, 1933 Madison WI; married 1st Will Schellenger; married 2d March 30, 1884 in South Wayne, as his 2d wife, Charles Clinton Bennett (C.C.) [son of Jehial & Mary B (Crain) Bennett] born October 11, 1855 Jo Daviess Co IL and died January 18, 1936 Madison, Dane, WI; buried there Forest Hill Cemetery; C.C. was deputy sheriff of Lafayette Co WI for 20 years later becoming Sheriff; and born to her and Will was:

Children (3)

  • EDITH SCHELLENGER 1.2.6.2.4.2.5.1
  • JESSIE BENNETT 1.2.6.2.4.2.5.2
  • BESSIE BENNETT 1.2.6.2.4.2.5.3
