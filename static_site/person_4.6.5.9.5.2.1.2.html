WILLILAM ANTHONY RUSSELL

ID: 4.6.5.9.5.2.1.2 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.9.5.2.1.2) WILLILAM ANTHONY RUSSELL born March 21, 1859 Knox Co IL and died November 5, 1905 Smith Prairie, Elmore, ID; educated Galesburg, Knox, IL; 1862 moved to Salem OR; 1864 moved to Boise ID; served in the Bannock Indian War under C. W. Moore; State Senator from Elmore Co; married October 31, 1883 Josephine McNew and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • BESSIE OLIVE RUSSELL 4.6.5.9.5.2.1.2.2
  • JOE MCNEW RUSSELL 4.6.5.9.5.2.1.2.3
  • WILLIAM RUSSELL 4.6.5.9.5.2.1.2.4
