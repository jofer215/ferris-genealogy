ALBERT JAMES BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.6.1.2.2.4 | Source: James
(5.2.2.6.1.2.2.4) ALBERT JAMES BUXTON born July 20, 1868 Stockbridge WI and died December 26, 1917 Racine WI; married November 8, 1893 in Brillion WI, Clelia Werner born January 11, 1868 Centerville WI and died August 10, 1956 Racine WI. This was Clelia’s second marriage and she had a son from her previous marriage. Born to Albert and Clelia were:
