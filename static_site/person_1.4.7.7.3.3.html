MARIANNA W. SEAMAN

ID: 1.4.7.7.3.3 | Source: John
(1.4.7.7.3.3) MARIANNA W. SEAMAN born March 24, 1828 and died March 2, 1898; married April 20, 1853, the Honorable Jordan Lawrence Mott (Photo) born November 10, 1829 NYC and died July 26, 1915. They were listed in the 1860 NY Census as residing in Morrisania, Westchester County. {There was a Jordan L. Mott who was the inventor of the cast iron stove and also involved in real estate in NYC – it might be this guy’s Dad – in addition there is a Jordan L. Mott Middle School – C.I.S.22, in NYC.} Born to them were:

Children (2)
