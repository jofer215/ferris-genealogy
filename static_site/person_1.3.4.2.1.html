DAVID DINGEE

ID: 1.3.4.2.1 | Source: John
(1.3.4.2.1) DAVID DINGEE born Peekskill, Dutchess, NY and died Juy 11, 1843 Putnam Co NY; buried Baptist Churchyard Cemetery, Ludingtonville, (near) Carmel NY; a farmer and Coroner of Putnam Co 1820-21 and 1832; married Clarine Northup; buried with David. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • JOHN FERRIS DINGEE 1.3.4.2.1.1
  • ALMIRA DINGEE 1.3.4.2.1.2
  • MARTHA DINGEE 1.3.4.2.1.3
  • HANNAH DINGEE 1.3.4.2.1.5
