DAISY PAULINE SEELEY

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.1.1.3.4.11 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.1.1.3.4.11) DAISY PAULINE SEELEY born June 5, 1905 and died February 19, 1972 Owosso, Shiawassee, MI; married January 29, 1927 in Detroit, Dale Pond born October 8, 1902 Indianapolis IN and died January 25, 1972 Owosso, and they had a child; both buried there Oak Grove Cemetery; he was with the Detroit Bank and Trust Co, Detroit for 15y; with the Mitchell-Bentley Corp for 32y, retiring from ther 1968 as personnel director

No children recorded
