EDMUND MOE

ID: 2.6.1.6.1.5.1.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.1.6.1.5.1.3) EDMUND MOE born c1842 Tompkins Co NY and died May 14, 1864 from wounds received in the Civil War – listed as Edmond Moe, Private, 109th New York Infanty Regiment; the 109th was involved in the Spottsylvania Court House action May 12-21, 1864

No children recorded
