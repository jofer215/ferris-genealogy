STEPHEN W. FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.2.4.2.3.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.4.2.3.2) STEPHEN W. FERRIS born January 23, 1842 Manlius NY and died of pneumonia March 11, 1884; married Florence {maiden name unk} (speculation here - there was a Stephen Ferris who married October 1866 in Otselic, Florence Adelia Raymond [dau of Charles & Sarah (Bishop) Raymond] born March 4, 1849 Corry PA and died March 11 1884, Otselic.
