REBECCA HOLMES

ID: 5.2.3.4 | Source: James
(5.2.3.4) REBECCA HOLMES born January 29, 1738/39 Stamford and died December 23, 1822 Warwick, Oranger, NY; married February 7, 1763 in Stamford, Samuel Pelton (son of Robert & Martha (Beachgood) Pelton] born December 21, 1739 Stamford and died April 1, 1799 Darien; buried there Spring Grove Cemetery; Rev War Sergeant 1776; and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • REBECCA PELTON 5.2.3.4.4
  • SAMUEL PELTON 5.2.3.4.5
  • RICHARD PELTON 5.2.3.4.6
