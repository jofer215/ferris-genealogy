SARAH MATILDA FERRIS

ID: 1.1.3.10.6 | Source: John
(1.1.3.10.6) SARAH MATILDA FERRIS born April 16, 1825 NYC and died August 19, 1869 Yorktown NY; buried Crompond East Burying Ground; married October 28, 1845 as his 2d of 3 wives, Silas Constant Whitney [son of Amos & Rosetta (Lewis) Whitney] born August 13, 1810 Yorktown NY; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • DAVID LEWIS WHITNEY 1.1.3.10.6.1
  • CONSTANT FERRIS WHITNEY 1.1.3.10.6.2
  • ALEXANDER STEWART WHITNEY 1.1.3.10.6.3
