RUBERTA L. PIERCE

ID: 5.1.2.9.10.2.6.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.10.2.6.1) RUBERTA L. PIERCE born January 3, 1886 Putnam Valley NY and died June 10, 1961 Peekskill NY; married Ralph Perry [son of Miles & Emily (Baxter)] born October 25, 1878 Putnam Valley NY and died October 23, 1915; both buried Hillside Cemetery, Peekskill NY. Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, June 15, 1961: Mrs. Ruberta L. Perry, 75 died suddenly last Saturday, June 10, 1961, in the early morning in Peekskill hospital from what was believed to be a heart ailment. Mrs. Perry had been rushed to the hospital the evening before, after she had become ill at her home, on the Peekskill Hollow road. Born Putnam Valley on Jan. 3, 1886, Mrs. Perry was a life-long resident of the Valley. She was the daughter of Emerson and Cornelis Travis Pierce. Mrs. Perry was the Communion Steward on the Tompkins Corners Methodist church since the death of her mother-in-law, Mrs. Emily Perry, in 1950, and had served previously as Assistant Communion Steward for many years. Just the Saturday before her death, she took care of the necessary details for the Union Circuit at Tompkins Corners. Mrs. Perry is survived by one son, Carlton Perry of Peekskill; one grandson, Richard Perry, also of Peekskill; and one brother, Cyrus Pierce of Church road. Interment was at Hillside Cemetery.
