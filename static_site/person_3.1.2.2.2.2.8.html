JAMES MUNROW FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.2.2.2.8 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.2.2.8) JAMES MUNROW FERRIS born February 23, 1832 and died December 29, 1923 Fredericka, Bremer, IA; married Frances ‘Fanny’ Dutcher born June 7, 1837 Tompkins Co NY and died July 5, 1917 Ionia IA; both buried Alcock Cemetery, Bremer Co IA (Photo); there is a James M. Ferris listed in the 1869-70 Symrna Business Directory as a dairyman and farmer – don’t know if this is same one; and born to them were:
