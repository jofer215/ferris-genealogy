FERRIS THARP

ID: 5.1.2.6.6.2.2.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.6.6.2.2.1) FERRIS THARP born 1871 Bath NY and died there July 8, 1950; married c1890 Mary E. Vogt [dau of Nicholas & Mary] (1870-1931); both buried Grove Cemetery, Bath; and born to them was: (5.1.2.6.6.2.2.1.1) VERNELIA THARP born July 14, 1907 Bath and died there November 22, 1993; married 1st c1927 in Steuben Co, Raymond Smith (1907-1971); 2d c1972 a Mr. Green; both Vernelia and Raymond buried Mitchellville Cemetery, Wheeler NY; lived on Bull Hill Road, Prattsburg, Steuben Co

No children recorded
