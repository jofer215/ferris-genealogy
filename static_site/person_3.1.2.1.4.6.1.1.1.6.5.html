MARY ALETHA WINSLOW

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.6.1.1.1.6.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.6.1.1.1.6.5) MARY ALETHA WINSLOW born January 31, 1925 Winslow Hill, Benezette PA; married December 4, 1945 in New Castle PA, Robert Edward Nay (Jake) born September 15, 1921 Batesville, Albemarle, VA and died April 20, 1979 Youngstown, Mahoning OH; buried Graceland Memorial Cemetery, New Castle PA. Mary was a waitress and bank supervisor. She enjoyed bowling and softball. Robert was a WWII veteran, serving between November 9, 1942 and November 17, 1945 in Europe with the 774 Railway Grand Division –campaigns Italy-Naples/Foggio; Rome/Arno; Purple Heart 1944, Africa, Southern France; North Appennies.
