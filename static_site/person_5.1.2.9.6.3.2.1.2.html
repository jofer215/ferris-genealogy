LILLIAN GRACE CHAPMAN “Dot” born November 15, 1913 Peekskill and died

ID: 5.1.2.9.6.3.2.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.6.3.2.1.2) LILLIAN GRACE CHAPMAN “Dot” born November 15, 1913 Peekskill and died there March 19, 1998; the assistant librarian at the Field Library, Peekskill 1952 – unmarried; was active in the Salvation Army until her death – she received an award for 65 years service
