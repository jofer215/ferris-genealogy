DONNA JEAN RANKIN

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2.2.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2.2.1) DONNA JEAN RANKIN born August 10, 1930 Chamberlain SD; married November 1, 1957 Paul Joseph Katona born May 25, 1927 PA; Donna graduated from SD State College and received nurses training at Sioux Valley Hospital, Sioux Falls SD, taking her post-graduate at Barnes Hospital, St. Louis MO. After spending 3 years in Alaska, Donna and Paul settled in San Diego where she is a nurse anesthetist and Paul is retired from the USN. Born to them were:
