MABEL LIVINGSTON FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.6.6.2.2 | Source: John
(1.4.7.6.6.2.2) MABEL LIVINGSTON FERRIS born August 8, 1873 Elizabeth NJ; married February 12, 1907 at Poughkeepsie, Edward Newman Mennell born Shirley, England Brooklyn Eagle, June 24, 1894: Excerpt – The annual tournament of the Hudson River Lawn Tennis association was completed yesterday on the grounds of the Yonkers Tennis Club at Yonkers NY. Miss Mabel L. Ferris, last year single champion, was beaten by the challenger, Miss Augusta Bradley, after a five set match. Miss Ferris won the first two sets, but then became tired and lost the next three. Men’s singles, first round – R. M. Ferris Jr, Poughkeepsie Tennis club beat E. W. Lancaster, Yonkers Tennis club, by default. Mixed doubles, semi final round – Miss Ferris and R. M. Ferris Jr, Poughkeepsie tennis club, beat Miss Atkins and E. W. Lancaster, Yonkers tennis club. Final round – Miss Mabel L. Ferris and Robert M. Ferris Jr, Poughkeepsie tennis club beat Miss Elizabeth Hale and John Kingman, Yonkers tennis club, 6-4, 3-6, 6-3. Ladies doubles - Final round - Miss Mabel L. Ferris and Miss C. E. Ferris, Poughkeepsie tennis club beat Miss Bertha Strang and Miss Kate Atkins, Yonkers T.C., 6-2, 2-6, 6-4 – believe these folks are Mabel here and her brother Robert below.

No children recorded
