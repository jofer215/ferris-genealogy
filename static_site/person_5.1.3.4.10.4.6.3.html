EDWIN JAMES GLASSBURN

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.6.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.6.3) EDWIN JAMES GLASSBURN born June 7, 1887 Tampico IL and died November 14, 1947 Greenville Township, Bureau Co IL (Tampico IL); married March 16, 1918 Helen A. Hines of Tampico [dau of Jacob Henry & Anna (Shepler) Hines] born April 16, 1896 and died July 18, 1979 Tampico; they resided in Tampico and both buried at Tampico Cemetery; no offspring
