EDWARD HOYT MARTIN

ID: 2.5.2.3.2.2.4.1.3 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.3.2.2.4.1.3) EDWARD HOYT MARTIN born 1869 Bancroft MI and died there January 6, 1950; he was a farmer and gas station owner; married October 5, 1892 in Bancroft, Ruth Maryette Watson [dau of William Ebenezer & Martha Belle (Norton) Watson] born November 1873 Bancroft and died there 1952; and born to them were:
