ALMIRA ELIZABETH FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.5.7 | Source: John
(1.4.7.5.7) ALMIRA ELIZABETH FERRIS born April 5, 1816 Swanton and died March 13, 1848 Woodstock VT; married August 5, 1839 Peter Thacher Washburn (18141870). Peter received a B.A. from Dartmouth, 1835; lawyer; Lieutenant Colonel, 1st Vermont Regiment; Adjutant General State of Vermont; elected Governor of Vermont in 1869; died in office.
