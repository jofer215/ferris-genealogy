CATHERINE FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.6.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.6.3) CATHERINE FERRIS (or Catharine) born February 1805 and died January 11, 1840 Cincinnati OH; age 25y 11m 25d; buried Ferris Farm Cemetery, Columbia Township, Hamilton Co OH; unmarried. From her original stone marker ‘She has gone to the grave but we will not deplore her, the sorrows and darkness encompass the tomb, the Savior has passed through its portals before her, and the lamp of his love was her guide through the gloom.’

No children recorded
