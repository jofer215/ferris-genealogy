ADELINE MERRITT SKELDING

ID: 2.5.2.5.3.5.1.4.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.5.3.5.1.4.2) ADELINE MERRITT SKELDING born October 6, 1873 Stamford CT and died there November 26, 1952; buried there Woodland Cemetery; married 1892 as his 2d wife, Edward Everett Rowell [son of Frederick & Rosalind] born October 28, 1863 VT and died June 27, 1938 Stamford; 1930 physician; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • DOROTHY SKELDING ROWELL 2.5.2.5.3.5.1.4.2.2
  • DORIS ROWELL 2.5.2.5.3.5.1.4.2.3
