HEZEKIAH KNAPP

ID: 2.6.5.9 | Source: Peter
(2.6.5.9) HEZEKIAH KNAPP born October 14, 1749 Stamford CT and died there November 25, 1840; buried in the West Stamford Cemetery. Hezekiah was a Private in the Revolutionary War in Captain Jonathan Whitney’s Company, Colonel Wooster’s 9th Regiment in 1776 and was in the New York campaign after the battle of White Plains, serving two months and four days. He married, in Greenwich, August 8 (September 7?), 1775 Mary Peck (5.1.1.3). He was a carpenter. Born to Hezekiah and Mary were:
