CARLOS RICE ADAMS, M.D.,

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.4.1.2.2 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.4.1.2.2) CARLOS RICE ADAMS, M.D., born August 13, 1892; married November 10, 1923 at Mineola NY Elizabeth Lucy Birdsall [dau of Ernest Eugene]. Dr. Adams graduated from Bordentown Military Academy, Princeton NJ, Class of 1915 and later, from the Long Island Medical College. He practiced medicine in Kathonah NY.

No children recorded
