(3.1.6.2.6.2) PETER SHERMAN – one source placed this Peter here with dates that obviously would not fit – another source has this Peter, known as Peter 2d in Seneca Co and Tomkins Co NY born March 1788 and died 1872 Decatur Co IN, but his parents cannot be verified, although he is listed in the list of heirs of Peter (3.1.6.2.5) and Keziah (3.1.6.2.7); might be a son of one of Solomon’s brothers. Thanks to Susan Kramer for this info!
