CHARLES AUGUSTUS STRINGHAM

ID: 4.2.5.4.6.3.2 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.4.6.3.2) CHARLES AUGUSTUS STRINGHAM born January 1835 NYC (or Yorkshire, Cattaraugus, NY) and died 1900-10 in/of Elwah, Clallam, WA; 1870 lawyer, Newbury KS; 1880 painter Del Norte CO; 1900 farmer Elwah WA; married January 3, 1866 in Shawnee KS, Mariette C Sexton (c1837-) (or July 1845 OH) and died after 1920 in/or Port Angeles, Clallam, WA; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • CARRIE A STRINGHAM 4.2.5.4.6.3.2.3
  • ALICE S STRINGHAM 4.2.5.4.6.3.2.4
