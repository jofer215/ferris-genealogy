JAMES WRIGHT KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.7.1.4 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.7.1.4) JAMES WRIGHT KNAPP born January 27, 1796 Colchester NY and died August 5, 1853 Hancock NY; served in the War of 1812 attaining the rank of Colonel; married 1 st at Colchester, Ann Agnes Doll born c1802 and died November 1, 1830; married 2d January 24, 1833, at Colchester, Jane Elwood (1803/13?-1885).
