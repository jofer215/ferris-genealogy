CORA M. HAYMAKER

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.6.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.6.1.2) CORA M. HAYMAKER born October 15, 1864 Franklin Township, Portage Co OH and died May 12, 1931; attended the district school at Earlville, when she was taught for several terms by her father; she also attended school in Kent one year, and then 3 years at Bucktel College in Akron; married August 4, 1886 in her father's home in Franklin, Elmer Ellsworth France [son of James & Martha (Littlewood) France] born April 22, 1863 Franklin OH (Kent, Portage, OH) and died December 10, 1935 Cleveland, Cuyahoga, OH. Elmer's father at that time resided on a farm, where he remained some 5 years. He then sold the farm and engaged in the real estate business, in Kent, for a time, and then entered into the mercantile business there. The son bought out the father's business in 1883 and carried on the business. Born to them were:
