SHIRLEY ANN WESCOTT

ID: 5.1.3.4.2.2.1.2.4.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.2.2.1.2.4.2) SHIRLEY ANN WESCOTT (Photograph) born November 17, 1943 Milford CT; attended Nasson College, Springvale ME; married James Arthur Powers, Jr. – divorced; Shirley followed her Naval pilot husband until they divorced, when she moved to Washington, D.C. She works for the Central Intelligence Agency and writes poetry in her spare time. Born to them were:
