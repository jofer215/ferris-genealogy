EUGENE UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.4.2.1.2 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.4.2.1.2) EUGENE UNDERHILL (KNIGHT) CHADEAYNE (Photos) born July 30, 1884 Chicago IL and died in 1955; buried Forest Lawn Cemetery, Glendale CA. Eugene took his stepfather’s name – Knight; a salesman of lithography; married 1909 Lulu Tanner (1885-1974) [dau of Louis & Augusta (Schlich) Tanner]; buried Forest Lawn Cemetery. Born to them were:

Children (2)
