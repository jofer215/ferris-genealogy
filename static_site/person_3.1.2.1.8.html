THEODOSIA FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.1.8 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.8) THEODOSIA FERRIS born December 7, 1759 (baptized April 9, 1760) and died August 11, 1855; married in Ulster Co NY, (Petrus) Peter Davis born February 3, 1752 Boiceville, Ulster, NY (Wales?) and died 1848; served as a Private in the Ulster Co New York militia. (A RootsWeb.com source has Theodosia being born in Scotland – don’t think so.) Born to them were:
