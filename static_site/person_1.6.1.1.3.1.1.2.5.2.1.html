FORD ELLIOTT FERRIS

ID: 1.6.1.1.3.1.1.2.5.2.1 | Source: John
(1.6.1.1.3.1.1.2.5.2.1) FORD ELLIOTT FERRIS born June 9, 1916 Armonk NY and died April 20, 1971 Bronx NY; buried NJ; a construction worker; married 1st July 12, 1940 in Purdy NY Madaline Clara Craig [dau of Alfred & Edith (Jennings) Craig] born October 1, 1923 Purdy NY and died March 10, 1999 Towanda PA; buried Camptown PA. She was a waitress, hospital helper, animal shelter, and dog trainer. During their marriage they traveled extensively following Ford’s construction work. Living in California, New York, Connecticut, Florida, South America, and Africa and Spain. Ford was killed in an accident on the job while working as Supervisor for the Board of Water Control in the Bronx; an earthmover backed over his car while he was in it.
