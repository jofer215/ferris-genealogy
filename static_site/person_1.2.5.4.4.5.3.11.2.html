HENRY EARL MOSIER

ID: 1.2.5.4.4.5.3.11.2 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.4.5.3.11.2) HENRY EARL MOSIER born April 9, 1887 Mount Carmel, Wabash, IL and died April 13, 1966 Homedale, Owyhee, ID; 1943 resided Enid OK; married 1st 1911 at Vincennes, Knox, IN Margaret Mae Stevens born March 8, 1892 Hooker, Texas, OK and died November 20, 1922 Okeene, Blaine, OK; 2d name unk and they had 3 kids; and born to Henry and Margaret were: (1.2.5.4.4.5.3.11.2.1) KAYE MOSIER born March 4, 1912 Oakwood, Dewey, OK and died February 1, 1953 (1.2.5.4.4.5.3.11.2.2 & 3) TWINS MOSIER born and died 1915 OK
