CLARISSA SCOFIELD

ID: 2.5.3.2.3.6 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.2.3.6) CLARISSA SCOFIELD born February 3, 1784 Stamford and died there August 10, 1857; married Richard Fox [son of Jonathan & Margaret (Cole) Fox] born January 29, 1783 Wilton CT and died September 23, 1862 Stamford; both buried there Newfield Cemetery; farmer 1850; and born to them were:
