SETH HAIT

ID: 3.10.1.4.10 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.4.10) SETH HAIT (#2943) born c1767 Stamford and died there July 26, 1815; may have been the Seth Hoyt who was a Private in the Jesse Bell’s Company; married November 24, 1788 in Stamford Sarah Smith [dau of Nathaniel & Abigail (Peck) Smith] born October 17, 1771 and died April 10, 1833 Stamford; both buried Rundle Cemetery, Stamford; and born to them were (listed as both Hait and Hoyt) (not in birth order):
