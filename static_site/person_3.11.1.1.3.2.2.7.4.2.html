FLORENCE MANDEY NINK

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.4.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.4.2) FLORENCE MANDEY NINK (Flossie) (Photos) born May 22, 1907 Elmwood Township, Leelanau Co MI and died July 16, 2004 Traverse City MI; Michigan Bell Telephone Bookkeeper and Operator; housewife; married May 19, 1930 Sidney Thor Koontz [son of Moses Andrew Ross & Myra Mae (Washburn) Koontz] born February 21, 1895 Rossville, Allamakee, IA and died June 6, 1970 Traverse City MI; both buried there Grand Traverse Memorial Gardens; WWI Army (Aero) Veteran; watch repair; operated own jewelry business; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • SIDNEY KEITH KOONTZ 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.4.2.2
