LLOYD RUSH ATWATER WINSTON

ID: 5.2.2.6.1.2.3.1 | Source: James
(5.2.2.6.1.2.3.1) LLOYD RUSH ATWATER WINSTON born February 13, 1879 Porter, Rock, WI and died April 5, 1964; a watchmaker and resided in Colorado Springs CO at one time; married 1st November 25, 1903 in Chicago IL, Susanna McCorkell [dau of John James & Phillippa (Kent) McCorkell] born April 1885 Quebec, Canada – divorced 1906 and died June 26, 1980 Los Angeles CA; married 2d April 30, 1909 Helen Owen Holloway born November 8, 1880.
