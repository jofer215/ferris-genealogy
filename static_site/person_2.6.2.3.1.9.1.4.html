WILLIAM B. FERRIS

ID: 2.6.2.3.1.9.1.4 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.1.9.1.4) WILLIAM B. FERRIS (1868-) married 1887 Luella Alma Banks [dau of Francis Bradley & Hannah Jennie (Middleworth) Banks] born c1864 CT – 1915 they were living in Boulder CO – since his nephew worked for him as a steam shovel operator, William must have had some type of company

No children recorded
