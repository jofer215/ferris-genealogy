ANN HAWKINS

ID: 1.4.6.2.2.2 | Source: John
(1.4.6.2.2.2) ANN HAWKINS (1.4.6.2.2.3) E. ADELAIDE HAWKINS married Joseph Augustus Cutter [son of John & Marthe (Sellers) Cutter] born September 10, 1833 Boston MA. He graduated from Dartmouth, 1857; studied law with Horace Green Hutchings, Esq., of Boston; and practice law in NY; no issue.

No children recorded
