ISAAC BRIGGS FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.7.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.7.1) ISAAC BRIGGS FERRIS born July 31, 1828 Stamford and died February 10, 1901; married November 27, 1850 Harriet Emily Ayres [dau of Alfred & Harriet E. (Lockwood) Ayres] born November 7, 1833 Stamford and died April 2, 1886 age 52y, 1m, 26d; both buried Long Ridge Cemetery, Stamford CT (Photos); no GAR marker, but apparently Civil War Vet.
