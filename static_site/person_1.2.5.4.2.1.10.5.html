WILLIAM FRANKLIN CHADEAYNE

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.10.5 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.10.5) WILLIAM FRANKLIN CHADEAYNE (Photos) born June 22, 1859 “Just a Mere” farm, Yorktown NY and died January 30, 1916 South Norwalk CT; buried Fair Ridge Cemetery, Chappaqua NY; married November 25, 1887 Charlotte Eva Palmer [dau of Richard & Charlotte] born April 2, 1864 NYC and died December 3, 1958 Trenton NJ; buried Fair Ridge Cemetery. They operated a grocery store in Tarrytown NY. Born to them were:
