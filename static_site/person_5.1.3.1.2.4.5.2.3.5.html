MABEL LUCILE DUNLAP

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.4.5.2.3.5 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.4.5.2.3.5) MABEL LUCILE DUNLAP born December 15, 1901 Allen, Dixon, NE and died August 24, 1960 Alliance, Box Butte, NE; buried Alliance Cemetery; a housewife, a businesswoman and a teacher; a high school graduate with some college; married September 4, 1926 in Bridgeport, Morrill Co NE, William Leopold Kastner [son of Leopold, Jr & Mary Myrtle (Thomas) Kastner] born December 25, 1899 Council Bluffs, Pottawattamie, IA and died November 8, 1986 Alliance NE.
