NATHAN GARNSEY PECK

ID: 3.10.1.2.6.3.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.6.3.2) NATHAN GARNSEY PECK born April 12, 1829 Watervliet NY and died July 17, 1923 Manhattan KS; married 1st Catharine H. Williams who died February 2, 1857 age 24y; and 2d 1858 Content Brockway [dau of Chauncey] born September 6, 1834 Schodack NY and died November 18, 1888 Junction City KS; stones for all are at Clifton Park Baptist Church Cemetery, Clifton Park NY – probably just memorial stones since they died in KS.
