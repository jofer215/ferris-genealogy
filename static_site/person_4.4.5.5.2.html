FREDERICK LOCKWOOD,

ID: 4.4.5.5.2 | Source: Mary
(4.4.5.5.2) FREDERICK LOCKWOOD, Jr. (#792) born February 3, 1788 Greenwich (Riverside) CT; married at Greenwich, Mary Ann Jessup (5.1.4.4.9.1). Frederick was a farmer; Episcopalian; Democrat. During the War of 1812, he was a Lieutenant of Militia. They lived in Greenwich. Born to them were:

Children (9)

  • FRANCES LOUISA LOCKWOOD 4.4.5.5.2.3
  • BENJAMIN PAGE LOCKWOOD 4.4.5.5.2.4
  • ISAAC JESSUP LOCKWOOD 4.4.5.5.2.5

  • LUKE ADOLPHUS LOCKWOOD, L.L.D. 4.4.5.5.2.7
