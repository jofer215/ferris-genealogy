JOSEPH DEVALSON NEWMAN

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.12 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.12) JOSEPH DEVALSON NEWMAN (Joseph Devolson) born February 26, 1810 Butternuts Township, Ostego Co NY and died April 14, 1898 Chicago IL; buried West Aurora Cemetery, Aurora IL; 1848 settled in DeKalb Co IL; Civil War Vet; enlisted August 13, 1862 into Company E, 124th Illinois Regiment and was discharged August 14, 1865 Chicago IL, Private; info on this Regiment on the ‘Net; married 1835 in Jefferson Co NY, (Rosy) Roxey/Roxie Wilkie [dau of Rev Matthew & Phebe Wilkie] born November 10, 1809 Jefferson Co NY and died December 31, 1886 Chicago IL; buried with Joe; and born to them were:

Children (2)

  • ALSON MOLONSO NEWMAN 3.11.1.1.3.12.2
