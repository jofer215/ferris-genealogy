HARRIET FERRIS

ID: 1.6.1.2.1.4 | Source: John
(1.6.1.2.1.4) HARRIET FERRIS born January 13, 1816/17 Spencer NY and died April/ May 14, 1882; married as his second wife, James R. Hazen [son of Abraham & Rachel (Schoonover) Hazen] born August 27, 1808 Orange Co NY and died January 4, 1877 Candor NY; and born to Harriet and James were:

Children (5)

  • FRANCES HELEN HAZEN 1.6.1.2.1.4.3
  • ELIZA L. HAZEN 1.6.1.2.1.4.4
  • MELISSA HAZEN 1.6.1.2.1.4.5
