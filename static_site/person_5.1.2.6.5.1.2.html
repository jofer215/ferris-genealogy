WILLIAM SMITH FERRIS

ID: 5.1.2.6.5.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.2.6.5.1.2) WILLIAM SMITH FERRIS born July 3, 1829 Steuben Co NY and possibly died young JOHN SIMMONS FERRIS (Photographs) born February 6, 1832 (Seneca Co) Steuben Co NY and died September 27, 1910 Bath NY, in his 79th year; a Civil War Veteran, enlisting August 23, 1862 in Company D., 161st New York Infantry; married July 14, 1855 in Steuben Co, Martha Jane Mowers born March 9, 1835 Yates Co NY and died December 12, 1912 Bath NY; resided on Kanona Road, Bath; both buried Nondaga Cemetery, Bath; and born to them were:

No children recorded
