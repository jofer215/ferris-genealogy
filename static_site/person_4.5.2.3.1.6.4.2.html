JAMES EDGAR LOUD

ID: 4.5.2.3.1.6.4.2 | Source: Mary
(4.5.2.3.1.6.4.2) JAMES EDGAR LOUD born October 12, 1855 DeKalb IL and died November 23, 1914; married August 6, 1883 in Washingtonville, Orange, NY Minnie B. Turner [dau of William A.] born January 6, 1865 Newburg, Orange, NY. Born to them were: (4.5.2.3.1.6.4.2.1) MABEL ETHEL LOUD born September 20, 1884 Washingtonville NY and died July 27, 1966; married October 3, 1911 in Monroe NY, William Davenport Earl [son of Earl] born November 25, 1880 Oxford Depot, Orange, NY and died January 16, 1968. Born to them were 5 kids.
