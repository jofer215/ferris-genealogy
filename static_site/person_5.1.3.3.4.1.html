CATHERINE KNAPP

ID: 5.1.3.3.4.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.4.1) CATHERINE KNAPP born April 14, 1781 Greenwich CT and died October 20, 1867 Cincinnati, Hamilton, OH; married March 9, 1799 Eliphalet Ferris(5.1.3.4.6). Catherine and Eliphalet moved to Ohio, date unk; both buried Mariemont Cemetery, Cincinnati OH; see Eliphalet for offspring

No children recorded
