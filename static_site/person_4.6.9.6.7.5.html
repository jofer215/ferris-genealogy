SAMUEL BIRDSALL LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.9.6.7.5 | Source: Mary
(4.6.9.6.7.5) SAMUEL BIRDSALL LOCKWOOD (#1446) born June 5, 1820 Pound Ridge NY and died March 1888; buried April 1st, Easter Day; married November 2, 1859 at Lowell MA, Josephine Lucinda Hard [dau of F. N. & Lucinda (Black) Hard] born April 10, 1825 Boston MA (born April 24, 1823 and died December 2, 1909; both buried Quaker Cemetery, Greenville NY. They resided at Irvington, on Hudson NY. Children (2)
