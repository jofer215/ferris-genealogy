EDWIN STEWART UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.5.1.2.5.3 | Source: John
(1.2.3.5.1.2.5.3) EDWIN STEWART UNDERHILL born October 7, 1861 Bath, Steuben, NY and died February 1929 Coopers, Steuben, NY as a result of an automobile accident; buried Grove Cemetery, Bath. He attended the common schools of his native city and Haverling High School at Bath; graduated from Yale College, 1881; engaged in journalism and became editor of the Steuben Farmers’ Advocate at Bath; presidential elector on the Democratic ticket in 1888; became editor and publisher of the Corning NY Daily Democrat in 1899; elected as a Democract to the 62d and 63d Congresses (Mach 4, 1911 – March 3, 1915); chairman, Committee of Industrial Arts and Expositions; was not a candidate for renomination in 1914; resumed the newspaper publishing business in Corning; engaged in banking, serving as VP of the Farmers & Mechanics’ Trust Co., Bath; delegate to the Democratic National Convention at Houston TX, 1928.
