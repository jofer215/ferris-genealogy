JOHN ELWOOD KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.7.1.4.6 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.7.1.4.6) JOHN ELWOOD KNAPP born May 11, 1843 Colchester and died June 25, 1916 Walton NY; a Civil War Veteran with the New York Light Artillery 1861-65 being promoted to Sergeant and then Lieutenant. By occupation he was a general merchandiser. He married 1st July 23, 1866 at Walton NY Georgiana Pine and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • CORA B. KNAPP 2.6.2.7.1.4.6.1
  • JAMES LEONARD KNAPP 2.6.2.7.1.4.6.2
  • MARIE ANTOINETTE KNAPP 2.6.2.7.1.4.6.3
