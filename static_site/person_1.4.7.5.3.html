LYNDE CATLIN FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.5.3 | Source: John
(1.4.7.5.3) LYNDE CATLIN FERRIS born May 6, 1808 at Swanton VT and died May 3, 1874 at Brookline, Mass., at his brother, Mortimer’s home; buried Mt. Auburn Cemetery, Cambridge, Mass.; M.A. U.Vermont, 1924; M.D. College of Physicians & Surgeons, New York City, where he practiced medicine; rendered services to the poor of the Second Ward during the prevalence of chlorea in 1832; married May 19, 1840 Mary Ann Drake (1810-1857); no issue

No children recorded
