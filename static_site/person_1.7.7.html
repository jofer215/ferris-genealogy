EDWARD BURLING

ID: 1.7.7 | Source: John
(1.7.7) EDWARD BURLING born February 3, 1713/14 NYC; baptized at Mamaroneck NY and died about February 1789 White Plains, Westchester County NY; member of the Society of Friends Hempstead Meeting, Hempstead NY; married in July 1735 Rebecca Cornell (a twin) [dau of Richard & Hannah (Thorne) Cornell] born July 30, 1716 Hempstead NY and died 1735. Edward and Rebecca were Quakers. Born to them were:
