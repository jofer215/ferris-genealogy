ELIHU SLAUSON

ID: 4.4.3.2.4 | Source: Mary
(4.4.3.2.4) ELIHU SLAUSON born September 27, 1769 New Canaan CT and died May 23, 1842 Middletown, Orange, NY; buried Hill Cemetery near Middletown; married June 6, 1801 in Orange Co NY, Esther Case [dau of Joseph & Esther (Budd) Case] born February 6, 1779 Morris Co NJ and died May 29, 1845 Middletown, Orange, NY; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • DEWITT SILAS SLAWSON 4.4.3.2.4.1
  • JOSEPH LOCKWOOD SLAWSON bon July 28, 1804 Bull’s Hack, Orange, NY and died 4.4.3.2.4.2
  • MILTON SLAWSON 4.4.3.2.4.3
  • JOHN BUDD SLAWSON 4.4.3.2.4.4
