ANNA JULIETTE SMITH

ID: 2.1.3.3.2.4.1.9 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.2.4.1.9) ANNA JULIETTE SMITH born October 16, 1857 Koloa, Kauai, HI and died 1900; married Joseph K. Farley. Anna and her brother, Jared, founded and conducted Malumalu School for Hawaiian children. (2.1.3.3.2.5) HULDAH HOLLY born c1793; married December 14, 1815 at Stanwich, Robert R. Barlow
