JOSEPH MERRITT LOUNSBERRY

ID: 4.7.5.4.4 | Source: Mary
(4.7.5.4.4) JOSEPH MERRITT LOUNSBERRY born March 8, 1776 Stamford and died April 15, 1828 Conklin, Broome, NY; married 1st May 17, 1801 in NY, Mary Whitney (Polly) [dau of Gen William & Ruth (Rodman) Whitney VII] born July 22, 1781 Hillsdale, Columbia, NY and died July 2, 1814 Conklin NY; married 2d February 2, 1815 in Conklin, Hannah Whitney; and born to him and Mary were: (4.7.5.4.4.1) LAURA LOUNSBERRY (Laury) born March 15, 1802 (Binghamton) Conklin, Broome, NY and died June 30, 1890 Bazetta, Trumbull, OH; married June 14, 1822 in Binghamton, Broome, NY, Nathaniel Lattin (1.1.1.2.3.2) – see him for offspring
