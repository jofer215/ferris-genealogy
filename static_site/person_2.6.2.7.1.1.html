PRUDENCE KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.7.1.1 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.7.1.1) PRUDENCE KNAPP born June [or January] 20, 1787 Colchester NY (Yorktown, Westchester, NY) and died August 31, 1880 Middletown, Orange, NY; buried Liberty Town Cemetery, Liberty NY; married 1st January 1, 1807 at Colchester, Isaac Horton [son of William & Elizabeth (Covert) Horton] born April 13, 1780 Somers NY and died May 10, 1855 Liberty NY.
