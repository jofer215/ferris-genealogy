SUSAN ANN CHAPAM listed as 26y in the 1850 census

ID: 5.1.2.9.7.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.7.1) SUSAN ANN CHAPAM listed as 26y in the 1850 census (age 18 on June 4, 1832 living in Philipstown NY – lived with her grandfather because her mother died when she was an infant. She is surely the child of John and his unk first wife.) (5.1.2.9.7.2) NAPOLEON B. CHAPMAN listed as 22y and a teacher in the 1850 census. He may have married a Susan {maiden name unk} and born to them may have been a Fanny who is listed as 6 months old in the 1850 census.

No children recorded
