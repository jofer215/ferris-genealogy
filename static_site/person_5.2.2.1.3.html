GIDEON LOUNSBURY

ID: 5.2.2.1.3 | Source: James
(5.2.2.1.3) GIDEON LOUNSBURY born August 7, 1751 Stamford CT and died there March 19, 1825; married 1779 in Stamford, Margaret {maiden name unk}. Born to them were: (5.2.2.1.3.1) JAMES LOUNSBURY born in January 1780 Salem, Westchester, NY and died August 20, 1841 Carmel NY; buried in Methodist Cemetery; married Rebecca Newman born 1774 Shrub Oak NY and died 1862.
