EDWIN WEED

ID: 2.5.3.10.1.4.6 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.10.1.4.6) EDWIN WEED (Edwin C Weed) born March 18, 1795 Stamford and died 1851 Washington, D.C.; Town Supervisor of Town of Greenfield, 1835-38, 1842; lumber business in Greenfield; he went to Greenfield when a young man and established himself in the mercantile business in that town, keeping a general store there unil 1847. In that year he took up residence in Fairfax Co VA and bought land there, but at the end of four years moved to Washington, D.C. and engaged in the insurance business in that city a short time and then death closed his career in 1851; married Hannah Seymour [dau of Nathaniel] born February 2, 1797 Saratoga Co NY and died January 28, 1873 Jackson Co IA; buried there Mount Hope Cemetery; and born to them were:
