FREDERICK LEROY DUNNING

ID: 4.2.5.4.5.2.2.1 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.4.5.2.2.1) FREDERICK LEROY DUNNING born May 23, 1833 Brimfield Township, Portage Co OH and died there March 8, 1889 (killed instantly by cars); painter; received a limited education in the common schools and when 14 years old he entered his father’s foundry (manufactured plows) where he worked until 1862; enlisted August 2, 1862 in Company F, 45th Ohio Volunteer Infantry Regiment; see ‘Net for unit history and honors during the Civil War; mustered in as Corporal but was promoted to 2d Lieutenant, then to 1st Lieutenant, and finally to Captain; at the siege of Knoxville TN; with Sherman at Atlanta; discharged August 12, 1865 at Camp Harker TN; returned to Kent; married 1st January 18, 1858 in Kent, Ann Maria Fessenden born March 5, 1843 Kent OH and died there March 16, 1868; 2d Clara Fowler; 3d Ellen Spencer; and born to him and Ann were:

Children (2)

  • ELLA MARIE DUNNING 4.2.5.4.5.2.2.1.1
  • CORA LOVINA DUNNING 4.2.5.4.5.2.2.1.2
