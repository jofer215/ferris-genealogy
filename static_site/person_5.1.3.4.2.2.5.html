MARGARET ANNA FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.2.2.5 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.2.2.5) MARGARET ANNA FERRIS born April 4, 1845 Riverside CT and died December 26, 1916 Greenwich CT, of chlorea; buried Sound Beach; married October 27, 1867, as his 2d wife, her brother-in-law, Thomas C. Lowden [son of Obadiah & June (Ruschardt) Louden/Lowden] born November 21, 1841 Greenwich and died there August 13, 1915; and they supposedly had six children, three of which were: (5.1.3.4.2.2.5.1) ELLA L. LOUDEN/LOWDEN born 1875 Greenwich CT and died 1945; married July 8, 1922 in Greenwich, Russell L. Eddy [son of John & Eleanor (Diaz) Eddy] born 1875 Greenwich and died 1966

Children (2)

  • THOMAS O LOUDEN 5.1.3.4.2.2.5.2
  • SAMUEL LOUDEN/LOWDEN born August 21, 1881 Greenwich CT and died 5.1.3.4.2.2.5.3
