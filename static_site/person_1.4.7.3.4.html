FERRIS PELL

ID: 1.4.7.3.4 | Source: John
(1.4.7.3.4) FERRIS PELL born August (October) 15, 1790 New York and died 1850; received a B.A. from Columbia U., 1806; was a Colonel with the Army and the Military Secretary to Governor DeWitt Clinton during the War of 1812; married November 6, 1827 Marianna Channing (Mary Hannah Channing) (1803-1869) [dau of Walter & Hannah (Smith) Channing] – this was her 2d marriage. Born to them were:

Children (6)

  • FANNY ROSALIE PELL 1.4.7.3.4.1
  • GERTRUDE PELL 1.4.7.3.4.2
  • JULIA PELL 1.4.7.3.4.3
  • EMILY PELL 1.4.7.3.4.4
  • SOPHIE PELL 1.4.7.3.4.5
  • LAURA PELL 1.4.7.3.4.6
