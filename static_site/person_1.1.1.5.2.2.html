ADELAIDE FERRIS

ID: 1.1.1.5.2.2 | Source: John
(1.1.1.5.2.2) ADELAIDE FERRIS (or ADELINE) born c1820; married a Mr. Eldredge (1.1.1.5.2.3) GEORGE B. FERRIS (1822-); married Hannah J. Lee born 1824 NY [dau of William]; produce merchant in NYC – sold produce to ships – later branched to shipyards in NJ; George listed in the 1910 census living in Palisades NJ with what appears to be some of his deceased wife’s family. Born to them were:

No children recorded
