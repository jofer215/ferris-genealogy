MABEL THERESA LOUD

ID: 4.5.2.3.1.6.1.1.1 | Source: Mary
(4.5.2.3.1.6.1.1.1) MABEL THERESA LOUD born April 3, 1869 Fort Davis, Jeff Davis, TX and died December 4, 1939 Detroit, Wayne, MI. She married November 21, 1888 in Detroit, Wellington Quelos Hunt born August 1, 1860 in MI. Born to them were: (4.5.2.3.1.6.1.1.1.1) HARRIET MABEL HUNT born June 2, 1890 Detroit MI and died September 26, 1944. She married August 22, 1917 in Detroit, Graham Howard Lindley born April 13, 1888. Born to them were 3 kids, one of which was:
