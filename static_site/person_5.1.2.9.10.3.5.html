WALTER M. GILBERT

ID: 5.1.2.9.10.3.5 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.10.3.5) WALTER M. GILBERT born c1853 Putnam Valley NY and died January 7, 1929; married March 11, 1887 at Philipstown NY Margaret Jane Haight; he has a Estate Probated in Putnam County, NY, # 6096, it names his heirs as: Judson H. Gilbert, brother of Clydepark, Montana; James Arthur Gilbert, nephew son of deceased brother James F., residing in Clydepark, Montana; Gertrude Lent (wife of Fred Lent), niece, daughter of deceased brother James F., residing in Cortlandt, Westchester Co., NY; and Allen Gilbert, nephew; Ollie M. Harris, niece, and Hattie B. Gilbert, niece, all being the children of deceased brother, Benson L. Gilbert, all residing in Warwick, North Dakota. (5.1.2.9.10.4) HENRY TRAVIS born July 26, 1820 Putnam Valley NY and died October 20, 1910; married Sarah E. Fowler [dau of James & Eliza (Crawford)] born Shrub Oak NY and died April 20, 1908 Putnam Valley NY; both buried Adams Corners Cemetery, Putnam Valley NY; no children

No children recorded
