CALEB MEKEEL

ID: 5.2.10.11.1 | Source: James
(5.2.10.11.1) CALEB MEKEEL born Philipstown NY; married November 26, 1851 Euphemia Davenport born Philipstown and died December 30, 1906 at home in Nelsonville from the effects of carbolic acid which she had taken while the family were at breakfast; she had been an invalid for a long time. Caleb was engaged in curing ship-timber in the South for many years, but later lived a retired life at his home in Nelsonville. Born to them were:
