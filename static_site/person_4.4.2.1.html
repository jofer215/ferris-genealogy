ELNATHAN LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.2.1 | Source: Mary
(4.4.2.1) ELNATHAN LOCKWOOD (#299) born (c1751) 1752 Greenwich and died November 3, 1850 (1830) New Canaan CT, where he lived, at age 78; buried North Stamford Cemetery. He enlisted May 6, 1775 in Captain Thomas Hobby’s Company and was discharged October 30, 1775. Elnathan married Betsey Clawson (Clason) [dau of Jonathan & Elizabeth (Curtis) Clason] born c1754 Stamford and died May 19, 1812 New Canaan CT; buried Old Parade Ground Cemetery, New Canaan; she joined the Congregational Church, New Canaan, August 14, 1791.
