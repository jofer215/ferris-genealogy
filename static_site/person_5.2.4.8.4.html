SALLY DAVENPORT

ID: 5.2.4.8.4 | Source: James
(5.2.4.8.4) SALLY DAVENPORT born March 5, 1783 Stamford and died February 17, 1839 Darien CT; married June 24, 1804 Jonathan Bates [son of John Jr. & Sarah E. (Bostwick) Bates] born November 5, 1772 Stamford and died October 31, 1854 Darien; both buried there Bates Cemetery. Born to them were:
