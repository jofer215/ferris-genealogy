LYDIA EVANGELINE AVERY

ID: 4.2.5.4.5.1.8.7 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.4.5.1.8.7) LYDIA EVANGELINE AVERY born July 12, 1863 Charleston MI and died in St. Paul MN, apparently before 1916 when Frederick married a 2d time; married August 14, 1882 Frederick Elmer Leavitt [son of David Sheldon & Martha Ann (Tirrill) Leavitt] (c1859-) (November 10, 1861 Grand Rapids, Kent, MI); and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • HAZEL VIOLA LEAVITT 4.2.5.4.5.1.8.7.1
  • EDITH MARTHA LEAVITT 4.2.5.4.5.1.8.7.2
  • JOHN AVERY LEAVITT 4.2.5.4.5.1.8.7.3
