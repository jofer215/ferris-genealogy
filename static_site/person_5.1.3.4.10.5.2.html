ELMER F. FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.5.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.5.2) ELMER F. FERRIS born December 23, 1849 Laurel, Franklin, IN and died c1910-20 Bluff, Sumner, KS; married 3 times: first 2 wives names unk; married 3d February 19, 1884 in Franklin Co IN, Isabelle Jenks [dau of Samuel & Lura (Potts) Jenks] born April 5, 1846 Laurel, Franklin, IN and died by 1910 in Minneha, Sedgwick, KS; their children died infancy, Wellington KS

No children recorded
