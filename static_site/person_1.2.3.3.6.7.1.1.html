ELIZABETH MARY SYPHERS

ID: 1.2.3.3.6.7.1.1 | Source: John
(1.2.3.3.6.7.1.1) ELIZABETH MARY SYPHERS born September 4, 1852 Tumut, NSW and died August 19, 1918 Brobenah, Yanco Shire, NSW; married March 3, 1870 in Tumut, Michael Joseph O’Connor who arrived at Port Phillip July 7, 1854 on board the Hilton, he was 5 years old along with his parents and 2 sisters; both buried Roman Catholic Cemetery, Narrandera – these are the great-grandparents of Marg Smith, from Australia, who has kindly provided this updated information – thanks, Marg!

No children recorded
