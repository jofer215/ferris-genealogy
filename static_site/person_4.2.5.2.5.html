SALLY LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.2.5.2.5 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.2.5) SALLY LOCKWOOD (#602) (Sarah) born December 18, 1780 (twin of Isaac?) and died December 17, 1861 Stamford; married 1st c1801 Timothy Jessup (5.1.4.4.11) and 2d after 1816 in Newburgh NY, William Waterbury born October 10, 1766 Stamford CT. See Timothy for their offspring. Born to her and William were:
