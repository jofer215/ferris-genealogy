RUTH JOURDAN HOYT

ID: 2.5.3.4.1.1.4.3.3 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.4.1.1.4.3.3) RUTH JOURDAN HOYT born January 26, 1886 Sonoma CA; married 1917 Commander Brownlee Fisher [son of Samuel Brownlee & Agnes (Crooks) Fisher] (1882-) Lt, USNR; c1923 living in San Francisco; 1939 living in St. Louis MO; he completed his education at Washington U at St Louis, Secretary of the Van Zandt Gas Applicance Co of St Louis; and born to them were:

Children (2)

  • BROWNLEE HOYT FISHER, JR., 2.5.3.4.1.1.4.3.3.1
  • LURA JOURDAN FISHER 2.5.3.4.1.1.4.3.3.2
