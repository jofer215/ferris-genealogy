CYNTHIA ANN KNAPP

ID: 3.11.4.3.2.9.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.4.3.2.9.1) CYNTHIA ANN KNAPP born March 14, 1823 Shafford NY (Spafford?) and died May 30, 1894 Plainfield Township, Kent Co MI; married 1st June 8, 1843 Albion NY Ashel Nobel Lee; married 2d August 31, 1847 Coldwater, Branch, MI Nathaniel Throop Waterman [son of Flavius & Martha (Throop) Waterman] born February 7, 1814 NY and died February 5, 1885 Paris Township, Kent Co MI.
