LEVI HOLLINGSWORTH WOOD

ID: 1.2.5.3.4.3.7.3 | Source: John
(1.2.5.3.4.3.7.3) LEVI HOLLINGSWORTH WOOD born August 14, 1873 Mt Kisco NY and died there July 21, 1956; married 1st 1915 Helen Underhill (1887-1924) [dau of Samuel Jackson & Emma (Albertson) Underhill]; 2d April 23, 1925 in Swathmore PA, Martha Speakman [dau of William W & Anna (Travilla) Speakman] born October 17, 1892 Swathmore and they had a child

No children recorded
