(1.4.1.5.2.4) FRANK (FRANCIS M. ) FERRIS; bachelor (1.4.1.5.3) JAMES FERRIS born September 29, 1795 and died August 13, 1869; bachelor; Alderman 6th Ward, New York City. His mother and sister, Sarah Hicks, were living with him in the 1850 Census.
