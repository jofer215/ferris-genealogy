SAMUEL R. LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.2.7.4 | Source: Mary
(4.4.2.7.4) SAMUEL R. LOCKWOOD born 1827 Stamford and died there March 17, 1893; buried there Woodland Cemetery; Civil War Vet served with Company B, 28th Connecticut Volunteers; carpenter 1866-93; married c1853 Harriet A. Wilmot (4.4.2.8.5.3) and born to them were:

Children (6)

  • DOUGLAS A. LOCKWOOD 4.4.2.7.4.3
  • ARTHUR A. LOCKWOOD 4.4.2.7.4.4
  • CLARENCE E. LOCKWOOD 4.4.2.7.4.5
  • BURDETT LOCKWOOD 4.4.2.7.4.6
