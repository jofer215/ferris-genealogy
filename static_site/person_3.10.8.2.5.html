LEVI GARNSEY

ID: 3.10.8.2.5 | Source: Joseph

War) [son of Nathaniel & Sarah (Husted) Newman] born August 3, 1742/43 Stamford CT and died before March 1822, Queensburg NY; buried Quaker Cemetery, Warren Co NY. Born to them were:
