BLANCHE IRVING LEGGETT

ID: 1.4.7.7.10.12 | Source: John
(1.4.7.7.10.12) BLANCHE IRVING LEGGETT born May 22, 1860 Waterford Township, Oakland Co MI and died August 8, 1922 Santa Barbara CA; married June 14, 1882 in Detroit at her parent’s home, James Whittemore [son of Joseph P. & Margaret Cleopatria (Fay)] born October 31, 1860 Detroit. James was a patent attorney. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • EMMA FERRAND WHITTEMORE 1.4.7.7.10.12.1
  • MARGARET FAY WHITTEMORE 1.4.7.7.10.12.2
  • NELSON WRIGHT WHITTEMORE 1.4.7.7.10.12.3
