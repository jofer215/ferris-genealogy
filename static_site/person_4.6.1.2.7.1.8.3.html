MARY NERVIE KEELER

ID: 4.6.1.2.7.1.8.3 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.7.1.8.3) MARY NERVIE KEELER born June 18, 1877 Traer, Tama, Iowa and died December 4, 1960; buried Lakeside Cemetery, Loveland CO; attended Cedar Falls Normal; school teacher 18951902; Bachelor Degree, Didactics, Iowa State Normal in 1898; Doctor Osteopathy, 1904 Still College, Des Moines; Teacher Iowa county and graded schools 1895-1902; practice osteopathy Loveland, Colorado 1904-16; Secretary Board of Education of Loveland 1909-21; founder of chapter and national treasurer of DAR; married June 29, 1916 in Loveland CO, George Washington Foster [son of George] born May 3, 1862 Glencoe, Mcleod, MN and died November 2, 1923 Loveland CO; had some type of store in Loveland and was Mayor of Loveland at the time of death. Born to them were:

Children (2)
