CLARISSA CLARA LOUNSBURY

ID: 5.2.2.1.5.2 | Source: James
(5.2.2.1.5.2) CLARISSA CLARA LOUNSBURY born July 29, 1787 Stamford CT and died February 28, 1859 New Canaan CT; buried Lakeview Cemetery, New Canaan CT; married 1st 1806 Stamford, Nehemiah Lounsbury [son of Jacob & Bethiah (Newman) Lounsbury] born June 19, 1782 Stamford; married 2d Jonathan Buxton [son of Samuel & Abigail (Knapp) Buxton] born January 16, 1781 Stamford and died there February 28, 1859.
