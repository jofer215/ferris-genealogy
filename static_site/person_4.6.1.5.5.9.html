SARAH ANN LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.1.5.5.9 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.5.5.9) SARAH ANN LOCKWOOD (#1411) born February 9, 1807 and died August 9, 1838; resided in NYC; married February 26, 1828, as his 2d wife Joseph Warren Hoyt (2.5.3.4.1.6.2); see him for offspring.

No children recorded
