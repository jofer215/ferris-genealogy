EMILY FRANCES SELLECK

ID: 2.6.5.3.4.5.1 | Source: Peter
(2.6.5.3.4.5.1) EMILY FRANCES SELLECK born January 25, 1829 Stamford and died there September 3, 1915; married April 16, 1849 in Stamford, Harvey Hoyt [son of Joseph Blachley & Mary Blachey (Weed) Hoyt] born August 9, 1821 Stamford and died there December 2, 1893; both buried there Woodland Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • WILLIAM HENRY HOYT 2.6.5.3.4.5.1.1
  • JOSEPHINE HOYT 2.6.5.3.4.5.1.2
  • OLIVER PAXTON HOYT 2.6.5.3.4.5.1.3
