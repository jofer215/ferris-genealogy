SALLY WARDWELL

ID: 2.6.5.9.2.7 | Source: Peter
(2.6.5.9.2.7) SALLY WARDWELL (4th born) born May 22, 1807 Stamford CT and died there March 1, 1875; buried there St. John’s & St. Andrew’s Cemetery; married c1828 in Stamford, William Henry Adams [son of Ebenezer & Elizabeth (Matthias) Adams] born February 26, 1801 Stamford and died there July 12, 1869; buried with Sally; shoemaker 1860; and born to them were:
