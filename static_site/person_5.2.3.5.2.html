SARAH FANCHER

ID: 5.2.3.5.2 | Source: James
(5.2.3.5.2) SARAH FANCHER born September 24, 1767 Stamford and died March 13, 1840 Darien CT; married January 17, 1789 in Darien, John Clock [son of Albert & Comfort (Clark) Clock] born November 22, 1753 Stamford and died April 11, 1838 Darien; both there buried Spring Grove Cemetery; he was a Private in Jonathan Bell’s Company 1776; and born to them were:
