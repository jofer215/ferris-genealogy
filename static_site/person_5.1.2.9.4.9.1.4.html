THEODORE WINFIELD CHRISTIAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.9.1.4 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.9.1.4) THEODORE WINFIELD CHRISTIAN born March 25, 1884 Fishkill NY and died May 31, 1961 at the Jennings Nursing Home, Highland; married May 27, 1908 in New Hackensack NY Estella Moore Scofield [dau of William Monroe & Emma Jane (Myers)] born March 30, 1889 Poughkeepsie NY and died September 28, 1958. Theodore was a native of the town of East Fishkill and had made his home there the greater part of his life. He was a member of the Presbyterian Church in Fishkill. Estella was a lifelong resident of the county and was educated in local schools and was a member of the First Presbyterian Church and of the Harper Circle of the church; both buried New Hackensack Cemetery; resided Wappingers Falls 1922; and born to them were:
