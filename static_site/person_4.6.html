JOSEPH LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6 | Source: Mary
(4.6) JOSEPH LOCKWOOD (#14) (K7FQ-3X) (4th born) born 1675 Stamford CT and died 1759 Pound Ridge, Westchester, NY; admitted a freeman February 7, 1697; moved to Pound Ridge 1743; married 1st May 19, 1698 Elizabeth Ayers born October 15, 1657 Preston CT and died December 16, 1715, childbirth; married 2d August 10, 1716 in Stamford, Margery Webb [dau of Joseph & Hannah (Scofield) Webb] born October 4, 1683 in Stamford CT and died there January 2, 1736/37. Margery is related to Epenetus Webb who married Deborah Ferris (2.1.1.1). Born to Joseph and Elizabeth were:
