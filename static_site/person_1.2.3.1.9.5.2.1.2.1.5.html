EVELYN FLORA CHRISTIAN

ID: 1.2.3.1.9.5.2.1.2.1.5 | Source: John
(1.2.3.1.9.5.2.1.2.1.5) EVELYN FLORA CHRISTIAN born May 21, 1907 Baldwin Place NY and died January 7, 1998 Brattleboro VT; buried Pine Grove Cemetery, Fitzwilliam NH; graduated from Walpole High School 1926 and attended the Keene School of Elocution; wrote poetry and at one time was the Keene Sentinel correspondent for Westmoreland NH; married 1st Warren H Whitman born August 30, 1903 Bellows Falls VT and died there January 19, 1974; 2d October 17, 1935 in Walpole, Paul Phillip Christian born March 16, 1929 Boston MA; offspring

No children recorded
