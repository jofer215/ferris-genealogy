WILLIAM DAVENPORT

ID: 5.2.4.8.3 | Source: James
(5.2.4.8.3) WILLIAM DAVENPORT born March 25, 1781 Stamford and died January 16, 1860 Brooklyn NY; married 1st September 12, 1802 in New Canaan, Abigail Benedict [dau of Isaac & Jane (Raymond) Benedict] born July 24, 1781 New Canaan and died there October 9, 1839; both buried there Old Parade Ground Cemetery.
