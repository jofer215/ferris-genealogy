ANDREW BRADISH GODFREY

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.1.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.3.1.6) ANDREW BRADISH GODFREY born August 5, 1843 Elba, Genesee, NY and died May 7, 1913 Batavia, Genesee, NY; buried there Maple Lawn Cemetery; as a young man, he lived in NYC and worked for Tiffany & Co – in 1862 returned to Elba, bought a farm and raised his family – his childhood Bible is still in existence with the inscription on the flyleaf – “earned when boy weeding garden during harvest”; married 1st Ellen A McCrory born September 12, 1844 MI and died July 20, 1891 Batavia NY; buried with Andrew; married 2d Addell Clark (c1856-); and born to him and Ellen were:

Children (2)

  • GRACE ESTELLE GODFREY 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.1.6.1
  • CLARENCE CALDER GODFREY 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.1.6.2
