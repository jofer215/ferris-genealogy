SAMUEL AUGUSTUS HOLLY

ID: 2.1.3.3.2.1.1 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.2.1.1) SAMUEL AUGUSTUS HOLLY born August 22, 1812 (born before his parents were married?) (or 1815 which makes more sense) and died August 22, 1897 Stamford; buried there Scofield Cemetery #3; 1850 shoemaker; married 1st Phebe Seely born January 1, 1817 and died August 24, 1850 Stamford; 2d May 8, 1853 in Stamford, (Amelia) Elizabeth Ingersoll (1814-1890) (2.5.1.1.5.1.3); and born to him and Phebe were:

Children (6)

  • JANE ANN HOLLY 2.1.3.3.2.1.1.3
  • MARY ALETHEA HOLLY 2.1.3.3.2.1.1.4
  • STEPHEN C HOLLY 2.1.3.3.2.1.1.5
