HOWLAND PELL

ID: 1.4.7.3.1.5.2.1 | Source: John
(1.4.7.3.1.5.2.1) HOWLAND PELL born March 19, 1856 Flushing, L.I., NY and died June 9, 1937; graduated from Columbia 1876 and School of Mines, Columbia 1887; involved in insurance and served in the New York National Guard 1917-19, Companies I and K, 7th Regiment, NGSNY; Adjutant, Veteran Corps fo Artillery, SNY; 2d Lieutenant Company E.; 1st Lieutenant, Company G; Captain Company A, 12th Regiment, NGSNY. He was awarded the state decoration for 20 years service. Served as secretary general and Governor general of the Society of Colonial Wars. Howland was interested in genealogy and is responsible for much of the Pell genealogy; married April 12, 1887 in NYC, Almy Goelet Gallatin [dau of Frederic & Almy Goelet (Gerry) Gallatin] and 1926, they resided at 14 E. 54th Street, New York City.
