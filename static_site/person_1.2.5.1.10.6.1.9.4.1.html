FREDERICK SPENCER BARNEY

ID: 1.2.5.1.10.6.1.9.4.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.10.6.1.9.4.1) FREDERICK SPENCER BARNEY born December 15, 1912 Stamford CT and died May 12, 2001 Millerton NY; buried there Irondale Cemetery; National Guard service; Yale & Towne Co, tool and die maker apprentice; WWII War Department aware for breech block machining technique; 1977 Electrolux, Old Greenwich; married Miss Collins and they had 3 kids

No children recorded
