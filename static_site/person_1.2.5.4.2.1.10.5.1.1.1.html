BRUCE RICHARD MAGNER

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.10.5.1.1.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.10.5.1.1.1) BRUCE RICHARD MAGNER (Photo) born June 10, 1944 Ossining NY; married June 8, 1968 Joan Marie Sullivan born June 9, 1948 Tampa FL. By Bruce: “Bruce born 6/10/44 at the Ossining Hospital. Later it became a factory. Go figure. As I remember, it rained that day. Perhaps it was some other day. I grew up in Ossining, attending Roosevelt elementary school then onto Brookside, where I fell in love with Sandy O'Brien. It was not mutual. Went to Ossining high school, graduating in the upper third of the lower right quadrant. I joined the Maryknoll Missionary order to become a priest, which lasted six months. I then went to college where I discovered beer. I actually graduated Marist College in Poughkeepsie in 1967. While in college, I earned some money playing in an Irish pub group, the Frobisher Bay Volunteers. I got my draft notice and escaped to the Air Force. On base in California, I met Joan Marie Sullivan and got married 6/8/68. We set up housekeeping in the Philippines. Eventually, we got back to the states and had two girls. Actually, Joan had the girls and I was no help at all. Now we live in Tampa, Florida. Ain't life funny?” Born to them were:
