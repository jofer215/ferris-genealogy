RUTH ETHELYN DELP

ID: 4.2.5.5.1.5.2.1.1 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.5.1.5.2.1.1) RUTH ETHELYN DELP born April 11, 1900 Marysville, Marshall, KS and died September 3, 1987 Englewood, Arapahoe, CO; 1920 resided Oxnard CA – servant for private family; married April 11, 1921 in Moorpark, Ventura, CA Charles Enoch Picklum [son of William & Laura Aurelia (Hogle) Picklum] born August 1, 1886 Liberty, Gage, NE and died January 1985 Aurora, Adams, CO; and they had 5 kids

No children recorded
