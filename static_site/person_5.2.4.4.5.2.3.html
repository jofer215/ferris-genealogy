WILLIAM WATERBURY LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.2.4.4.5.2.3 | Source: James
(5.2.4.4.5.2.3) WILLIAM WATERBURY LOCKWOOD born August 9, 1821 Stamford and died there August 7, 1894; buried Woodland Cemetery (Photo); in 1850 was a carpenter; in 1860 was a boss carpenter; married c1846 Nancy Jane Slauson (5.2.4.4.8.1); and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • JARED LOCKWOOD 5.2.4.4.5.2.3.3
  • JOHN HARVEY LOCKWOOD 5.2.4.4.5.2.3.4
  • LAURA LOCKWOOD 5.2.4.4.5.2.3.5
