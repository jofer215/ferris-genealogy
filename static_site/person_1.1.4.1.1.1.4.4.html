VAUGHN SWEET HALL

ID: 1.1.4.1.1.1.4.4 | Source: John
(1.1.4.1.1.1.4.4) VAUGHN SWEET HALL born March 30, 1861 Savannah, Wayne, NY and died October 22, 1933 Bladen, Webster, NE; dealer in coal and lumber; 1920-30 banker; married April 28, 1886 in Red Cloud, Webster, NE Nettie Blanche Arnold [dau of John & Lorinda] born 1866 Marcellus, Cass, MI and died October 11, 1946 Bladen NE; both buried there Plainview Cemetery, NE (cemetery and tombstone photo); and born to them were: (1.1.4.1.1.1.4.4.1) GRACE HALL born March 1887 NE
