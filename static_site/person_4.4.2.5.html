ANDREW LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.2.5 | Source: Mary
(4.4.2.5) ANDREW LOCKWOOD (#303) born April 6, 1762 Greenwich and died there August 12/13, 1834; buried Old Sound Beach Cemetery, Greenwich CT; married 1st 1788 Esther Nash born 1765 and died June 14, 1795 age 30y; buried at Old Sound Beach Cemetery; married 2d 1798 Elizabeth Webb [dau of Epenetus, Jr & Sarah (Judson) Webb] born March 16, 1771 and died May 9, 1860 Ashtabula OH. (See 2.1.1.1) Born to Andrew and Esther, were:
