ADELBERT DEXTER GAIL

ID: 4.5.2.3.4.1.2.1.2 | Source: Mary
(4.5.2.3.4.1.2.1.2) ADELBERT DEXTER GAIL born December 27, 1858 Aurora, Erie, NY and died August 16, 1916 Buffalo, Erie, NY; married 1st 1878 in Buffalo, Minnetta Parker (Nettie) [dau of Nelson & Mary] born February 15, 1861 Port Dover, Ontario Canada and died March 1, 1885 Buffalo NY; married 2d May 20, 1886 in Camden, Kent, DE, Margaret Sarde [dau of Robert & Sarah (Rickards) Sarde] born May 23, 1860 Camden DE and died February 21, 1953 Smyrna, Kent, DE; and born to him and Nettie were:
