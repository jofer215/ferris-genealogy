ISAAC FERRIS WADE

ID: 3.1.1.6.9.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.9.6) ISAAC FERRIS WADE born January 9, 1811 near Middletown OH and died March 31, 1898 Crawfordsville IN; moved to Crawfordsville, Montgomery, IN c1830; married 1 st August 9, 1832 Alethia Clark Heaton [dau of James] who died 1847 Crawfordsville; and 2d Margaret Maxwell, who lived for just a few months afterwards; Isaac was a member of the firm of Wade & Bryant, who published the first newspaper in Crawfordsville; first issue of which, the Crawfordsville Record, is dated October 18, 1831. At that time, no newspaper was published west of Crawfordsville. To buy supplies for his paper, Isaac made trips on horseback to Cincinnati, following the old trails or taking the National Road and stopping for a visit in Butler County. One of these trips he bought, in Cincinnati, the first church bell for the Baptist Church in Crawfordsville. Born to Isaac and presumably Alethia, were:
