ELIZABETH MOTT

ID: 1.10.2.4 | Source: John
(1.10.2.4) ELIZABETH MOTT born February 5, 1747 and died September 7, 1829; married 1773 David Underhill [son of Amos & Elisabeth (Seaman) Underhill] born April 2, 1743 and died February 7, 1811; resided in NYC; and born to them were: (1.10.2.4.1) WILLIAM UNDERHILL born November 9, 1774 and died 1860; resided in NYC and moved to Wolcott, Wayne, NY; married 1798 Sarah Coles Stoddard (17801866) and born to them were:
