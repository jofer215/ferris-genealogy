BENJAMIN STRONG FERRIS

ID: 3.1.1.5.2 | L18R-7V | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.5.2) BENJAMIN STRONG FERRIS (AFN: L18R-7V) (3d born) born c1771 and died after 1850 Ulysses Township, Seneca Co NY; married January 21, 1797 Hannah Levina Pelton [dau of Benjamin & Jane (Griffiths) Pelton] born November 12, 1780 and died October 8, 1880; he bought land 1801 Ovid NY which he sold in 1807; various deeds show he owned land in the towns of Hector and Scipio as well as Ovid, and he was in Ulysses at the time of the 1830 census; at one time he resided near Cayuga Lake NY; and born to them were:

Children (6)

  • CHARLES FRANKLIN FERRIS 3.1.1.5.2.1
  • ASAPH FERRIS transferred land to Lufanna Feris 3.1.1.5.2.2
  • CAROLINE FERRIS 3.1.1.5.2.3
  • RICHARD P. FERRIS 3.1.1.5.2.4
  • HARRIET FERRIS was age 33y in the 1850 census 3.1.1.5.2.5
  • LUFERNA FERRIS 3.1.1.5.2.6
