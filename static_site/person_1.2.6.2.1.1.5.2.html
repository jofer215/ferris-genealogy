WILLIAM DUNCAN ARTER

ID: 1.2.6.2.1.1.5.2 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.1.1.5.2) WILLIAM DUNCAN ARTER born July 29, 1842 Zanesville OH and died April 9, 1914 Salem Township, Muskigum Co OH; Civil War Vet enlisted at Colchester IL December 1861 in Company D, 12th Illinois Calvary Regiment; promoted to Sergeant; captured at Tunstall Station May 4, 1863 and held in Libby Prison just 3 hours; discharged at New Orleans LA March 1, 1866; the 12th was a very active regiment – see the ‘Net; he was for 30y+ an engineer at the Brown Manufacturing Works in Zanesville; married December 9, 1863 in Muskigum Co OH Mary Ann Ragor born August 8, 1840 near Deavertown, Morgan, OH and died February 2, 1916 Salem Township, Muskigum Co OH; both buried Greenwood Cemetery, Zanesville; and born to them were:
