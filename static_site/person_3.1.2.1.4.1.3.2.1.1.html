HARRY AUGUSTUS GODFREY

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.1.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.1.1) HARRY AUGUSTUS GODFREY born June 24, 1864 New Albany, Floyd, IN and died June 1939 Indianapolis IN; telegrapher, ticket agent 1880; he worked at The Indianapolis News late at night to receive the news stories; married February 7, 1884 in Mattoon IL, Maie Idelle McFadden [dau of Robert Hugh & Sarah Ann (Norvell) McFadden] born July 1, 1857 Mattoon, Coles, IL and died December 16, 1936 Indianapolis; both buried Anderson Cemetery, Warren Park, Marion, IN; and born to them were: (3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.1.1.1) RUTH MARGARET GODFREY born February 7, 1885 Indianapolis, Marion, IN and died there May 16, 1931; buried there Anderson Cemetery; married 1st May 1903 in Indianapolis, Joseph Reeves Dew [son of Joseph Addison & Ella Sarah (Kearns) Dew] born May 25, 1882 Paducah, McCracken, NY – divorced 1919 – and died October 1948 Indianapolis; chief clerk, State Labor Department; married 2d Frank Orval Main; and born to her and Joseph were 2 kids, 1 of which was:
