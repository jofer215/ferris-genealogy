ENOCH HAIT

ID: 2.5.2.11.12 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.11.12) ENOCH HAIT (Hoyt) (#3061) born January 8, 1772 Stamford and died July 1, 1827 probably in North Stamford where he lived and farmed; buried North Stamford Cemetery; married 1st c1805 Nancy L. Smith who died April 7, 1811 age 27y; 2d Sally Ann Smith (sister of Nancy) born June 23, 1791 Stamford. Born to Enoch and Nancy were (Hait or Hoyt - will go with Hoyt):
