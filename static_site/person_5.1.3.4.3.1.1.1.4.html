FERRIS LOUNSBURY BISHOP

ID: 5.1.3.4.3.1.1.1.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.3.1.1.1.4) FERRIS LOUNSBURY BISHOP married Maude Platt [dau of David & Emogene (Hopkins) Platt]; received the Automatic Machine Company from his father and he gave the company to an employee. After he depleted his fortune, through various misadventures, he moved to Vineland NJ and started a chicken farm and for the first time in his life was happy. Ferris was a drinking pal of Hicks, the artist famous for The Peaceable Kingdom.
