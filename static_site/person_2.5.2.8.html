JAGGER HAIT

ID: 2.5.2.8 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.8) JAGGER HAIT (Hoyt) (#2758) (NZLM-C2) born December 21, 1714 and died June or July 1747 Stamford where he lived; a landowner in Stamford; married 1st November 1, 1739 Sarah Weed born c1714 and died June 30, 1740; 2d January 21, 1741 Jemima Clason who was living in Stamford 1750 when she bought land there. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • SILVANUS HAIT 2.5.2.8.1
  • AMOS HAIT 2.5.2.8.2
  • SARAH HAIT 2.5.2.8.3
  • JEMIMA HAIT 2.5.2.8.4
