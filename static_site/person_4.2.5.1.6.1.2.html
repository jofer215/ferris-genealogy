SARAH LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.2.5.1.6.1.2 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.1.6.1.2) SARAH LOCKWOOD (#2152) (Sarah E. Lockwood) born October 24, 1826 Stamford and died there May 1, 1894; married December 2, 1850 in Stamford, William Hay [son of John William & Isabella (Brund) Hay] born July 1822 Scotland and died November 20, 1882 Stamford; both buried North Street Cemetery, Stamford; and born to them were:
