JOHN L SHAVER

ID: 3.10.1.2.5.4.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.5.4.1) JOHN L SHAVER born 1813 NY and died 1886 St Louis, Gratiot, MI; married c1846 in Steuben Co NY, Polly Mary Rose born 1824 NY; and born to them were: (3.10.1.2.5.4.1.1) TRUMAN C SHAVER born 1847 Canisteo Township, Steuben Co NY; Civil War Vet served as a Private in Company F, 6th New York Cav Regiment 1864-65
