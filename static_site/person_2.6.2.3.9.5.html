HIRAM KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.3.9.5 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.9.5) HIRAM KNAPP born May 16, 1805 Cornwall, near Canterbury, in Sullivan Co NY and died May 8, 1854 Narrowsburg NY; a shoemaker and followed the Presbyterian faith; married at Narrowsburg-Tusten, Nancy Jane Sears born 1812 and died February 2, 1886 Narrowsburg; both buried at Glen Cove Cemetery, Narrowsburg NY.
