JANE UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.1.14.2 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.14.2) JANE UNDERHILL born June 14, 1806 Mt Pleasant, Westchester, NY and died January 30, 1849; married January 21, 1830 in New Castle, Westchester, NY as his 2d wife, Alfred Underhill [son of Richard & Pamele (Townsend) Underhill] born February 1, 1806 White Plains NY and died October 18, 1889 New Castle NY; Quaker; his 3d wife was Rebecca Burling (1.7.7.12.1.5); and born to her and Alfred were 6 kids, 3 of which were:

Children (3)

  • CAROLINE UNDERHILL 1.2.5.1.14.2.1
  • SOLOMON UNDERHILL 1.2.5.1.14.2.2
  • PHEBE C UNDERHILL 1.2.5.1.14.2.3
