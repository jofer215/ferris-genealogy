(1.4.7.1) JOHN FERRIS, JR ??? d July 21, 1806 – one source has John being born February 9, 1753, which is obviously incorrect considering his parents wedding date. Same source has him dying April 9, 1837 at Bedford NY; buried Lake Marie Cemetery, Bedford; marrying in September 1784 at Bedford, Susan Parker born February 19, 1764 at Bedford NY and dying there November 30, 1849.
