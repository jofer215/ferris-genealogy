BELDEN WEED

ID: 2.6.7.8.3.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.8.3.3) BELDEN WEED born March 31, 1805 Westerlo, Albany, NY and died May 26, 1890 Elkhorn WI. He settled April 19, 1856 Lafayette Township, Walworth County WI. He married first September 17, 1828 NY, Mary Sherman; married second February 27, 1844 NY, Rachel Maria Cherevoy [dau of Henry L. & Millie (Abbey) Cherevoy] born October 18, 1820. Born to him and Mary was:

Children (3)
