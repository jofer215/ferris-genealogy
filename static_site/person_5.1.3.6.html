STEPHEN FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.6 | Source: James
(5.1.3.6) STEPHEN FERRIS born December 27, 1740/42 Greenwich and died there February 12, 1824; (82y) buried Old Sound Beach Cemetery (tombstone photos); a Private in Captain George Peck's Company of Colonel John Mead's Regiment of Militia from April 13 to September 5, 1776, and from November 1, 1776 to January 23, 1777, when he was discharged having served 2 months 23 days; married Sarah Hanford Lockwood born September 6, 1754 and died November 23, 1848; buried Tomac Avenue Cemetery (Old Sound Beach), Greenwich. Born to them were [there are many variations in dates for this family]:

Children (3)

  • HANNAH FERRIS 5.1.3.6.1
  • MARY LOCKWOOD FERRIS “Polly” born November 12, 1777 and died 5 May 1842; 5.1.3.6.2
  • STEPHEN FERRIS JR., 5.1.3.6.3
