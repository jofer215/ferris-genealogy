PETER HAIT

ID: 2.5.3.6.9 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.6.9) PETER HAIT (#3077) (Hoyt) born October 24, 1764 Stamford and died in Middletown, Orange, NY; during the revolutionary war, he rode a horse before a yoke of oxen, to draw a chain to stretch across the Hudson River; a farmer and lived in Middletown, Orange, NY; married c1789 Obedience Haynes [dau of John] born c1768; and born to them were (note name change):

Children (6)
