ALFRED HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.4.5.5.3 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.5.5.3) ALFRED HOYT (#4546) born January 13, 1813 Stamford and in 1871 was living on the old Stamford homestead of his father and grandfather and died there October 31, 1896/97; buried North Street Cemetery. He early engaged in business activity and in 1838 joined with Captain William Lockwood in certain West India trading ventures, a partnership that existed until 1845 when it was dissolved by the death of Captain Lockwood and then Alfred sold his interest. Alfred represented Stamford in the Connecticut Legislature in 1864-65; one source has him unmarried but another has him marrying November 4, 1868 Emily Knowlton [dau of Farnham & Sarah (Ingersoll) Knowlton] born June 17, 1843 in Greenwich and died April 28, 1885 Stamford; buried North Street Cemetery; and born to them was:
