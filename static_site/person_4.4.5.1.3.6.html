ALBERT LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.5.1.3.6 | Source: Mary
(4.4.5.1.3.6) ALBERT LOCKWOOD (#1546) born November 18, 1802 Sing Sing NY (Stanwich CT); achieved an enviable reputation as a learned jurist, and man of the strictest probity and irreproachable character. Nature intended him for a judge, so natural, so impressive was his dignity of manner and depth of insight into human nature. He served several terms of the County Judge of Westchester County NY, only refusing the Supreme Court judgeship on account of his unwillingness to relinquish his private practice.
