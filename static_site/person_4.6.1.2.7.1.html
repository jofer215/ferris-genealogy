HANNAH LOCKWOOD WARING

ID: 4.6.1.2.7.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.7.1) HANNAH LOCKWOOD WARING born January 13, 1806 and died c1871 Howe, Lagrange, IN; married June 7, 1827 Benjamin Keeler [son of Benjamin & Eunice (Olmsted) Keeler] born November 2, 1796 Ridgefield CT and died 1859, of Ogden NY.
