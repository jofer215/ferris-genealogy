ANDREW SEE, JR.,

ID: 1.6.1.1.3.1.1.4 | Source: John
(1.6.1.1.3.1.1.4) ANDREW SEE, JR., born May 19, 1848 Near Mile Square and died January 10, 1929 Armonk NY; married 1870 in North Castle, Westchester Co NY, Sarah Ann Reynolds [dau of William & Elizabeth (Ferris) Reynolds] born January 1849 (1851) and died 1891. Andrew was a life-time resident of Armonk and a shoemaker. Born to them were:
