ANSON HENRY NASH

ID: 3.10.1.2.1.10.2.8 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.1.10.2.8) ANSON HENRY NASH born November 4/5, 1853 Yankee Hollow, JoDaviess, IL and died March 18, 1903 Jo Daviess Co IL; his father died when Anson was only a few months old, leaving the family in literally poor circumstances; he was obliged to work on the farm till he was 19y, having little opportunity for obtaining any thing more than a common school education, but having true courage and determination, he went to Humboldt Co IA where he spent 2 1/2 y attending Humboldt College; taught school and learned the trade of watchmaker and jeweler; then he spent most of his time in JoDaviess Co teaching school and working at his trade located at Elizabeth IL where he did a thriving business; married September 24, 1879 in Jo Daviess Co, (Margaret J) Margaret S. Price born April 25, 1857 and died April13, 19151; both buried Elizabeth City Cemetery (Old Section), Elizabeth, JoDaviess, IL, ; and born to them were:
