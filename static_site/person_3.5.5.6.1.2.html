ZOPHAR MEAD, of NYC,

ID: 3.5.5.6.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.5.5.6.1.2) ZOPHAR MEAD, of NYC, born December 20, 1790, died October 3, 1838. He married, April 26, 1820, Martha Seaman [dau of David] born February 14, 1799 and died May 29, 1869. Born to them were: (3.5.5.6.1.2.1) ARAMINTA MEAD born March 6, 1821 and died May 1, 1871
