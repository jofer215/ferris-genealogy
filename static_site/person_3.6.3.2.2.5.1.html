MICHAEL HILL

ID: 3.6.3.2.2.5.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.2.5.1) MICHAEL HILL born April 1811 Stillwater, Sussex, NJ and died there March 17, 1887; interred Harmony Hill Cemetery, Stillwater NJ; married Susan Martin [dau of Jacob] born July 26, 1810 Stillwater NJ and died February 28, 1889 Hardwick, Warren, NJ; interred Jacksonburg Cemetery, Blairstown NJ.
