SARAH ELIZABETH FERRIS

ID: 2.1.3.3.1.9.1 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.1.9.1) SARAH ELIZABETH FERRIS born October 4, 1823 and died December 25, 1903 Stamford CT; married 1st as his second wife January 19, 1848 in Stamford, Samuel Hoyt Reynolds [son of Timothy & Hannah (Hoyt) Reynolds] born June 20, 1811 Stamford and died there February 25, 1887. Born to Sarah and Samuel were:

Children (6)
