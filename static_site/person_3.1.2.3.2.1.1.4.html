SOPHIA FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.3.2.1.1.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.3.2.1.1.4) SOPHIA FERRIS born April 10, 1820 NY (MA?) and died December 4, 1887 at the home of her son-in-law, Frank Mace, in Winoski, Chittendon VT; buried Allen Cemetery, Ausable NY; married May 14, 1835 at Gill, NY/MA, Ebenezer Severance [son of Elijah & Charlotte (Starkweather) Severance] born June 5, 1813 Gill, Franklin, MA and died February 14, 1881 Peru, Clinton, NY; buried Allen Cemetery, Ausable NY; a blacksmith, farmer and butcher; and born to them were:
