CHARLOTTE HOYT

ID: 4.6.5.8.5.3 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.8.5.3) CHARLOTTE HOYT (#4390) born 1810 Phillipstown NY; married Dr. Graves and they resided in Shelby NY. (George Washington Graves (1797-) [son of Rufus & Experience] – Charlotte was his 1st wife – she must have died before 1834 since he married again before 1834 and had 2 daughters with his 2d wife

No children recorded
