GERSHOM LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.1.4.1 | Source: James
(5.1.4.1) GERSHOM LOCKWOOD (#501) born c1728 Greenwich and died 1796/98 Greenwich (Stanwich) and buried there; served in Captain Abraham Mead's Company 1776 as a Private; married Eunice Close [dau of Joseph, Jr. & Eunice (Hoyt) Close] of Horseneck Parish, Greenwich born May 10, 1731 and died August 9, 1807 Greenwich.
