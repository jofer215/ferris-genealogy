BENJAMIN FRANKLIN FERRIS

ID: 3.1.1.6.6.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.6.4) BENJAMIN FRANKLIN FERRIS (Frank) born October 19, 1815 Cincinnati OH and died March 23, 1904 near Sunman, Ripley, IN; moved from Cincy to Franklin Township IN 1830 to Aurora 1834, back to Franklin Township 1836 to Switzerland Co IN 1849, then back to Franklin Townhip 1853 and then remained on his farm in Franklin Township, about 2 miles from Sunman; served as trustee of his township for 7y; married June 13, 1839 in Dearborn Co IN, Martha Jane Taylor [dau of Townsend John & Sarah Elizabeth (Moore) Taylor] born July 20, 1822 Cincinnati and died December 17, 1906 Paris KY; both buried St Paul M.E. Cemetery, Ripley Co IN (St Paul Lutheran Cemetery, Sunman, Ripley Co) (interesting her name is spelled Farris on their tombstone) (Photos); Benjamin was an accomplished fiddler and member of Indiana state House of Representatives 1865/1846; Mason; and born to them were:
