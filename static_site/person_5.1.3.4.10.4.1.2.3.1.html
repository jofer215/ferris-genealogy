KENNETH LAVERNE FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2.3.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2.3.1) KENNETH LAVERNE FERRIS born May 22, 1936 Pierre SD; he is the provider of much of the info for this “twig” - thanks Ken! Ken married December 26, 1958 at Highmore SD, Joyce Janette Hinkle born May 18, 1936 Sully Co SD. Ken worked in dirt construction, 1955-64, when he began the business that today is Ferris Crane Service operating out of Huron SD. Joyce was a school teacher at Selby, Phillip, and Highmore SD. They reside outside of Huron. Born to them were:

Children (2)
