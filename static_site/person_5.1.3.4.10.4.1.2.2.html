IVA IOLA B. FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2.2) IVA IOLA B. FERRIS born March 16, 1904 Draper (Canton) SD and died July 27, 1961 Pierre SD; married May 28, 1928 Raymond Rankin [son of Henry R. & Annie O. (Brown) Rankin] born August 24, 1905 Winterset IA and died February 26, 1987 Pierre SD; both buried in Draper SD (headstone photo). Both spent their lives in the oil business, associated with Standard Oil, Draper SD. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • RODNEY RAYMOND RANKIN 5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2.2.2
