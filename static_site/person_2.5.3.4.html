SAMUEL HAIT

ID: 2.5.3.4 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.4) SAMUEL HAIT III (Hoyt) (#2765) born c1709-18 (1716 Stamford) (February 8, 1713/14 Stamford) and died April 6, 1756 probably at Stamford where he lived; a multiple landowner; married 1st March 8, 1738/9 in Stamford, Abigail Bell (5.2.6); and 2d c1743-48 Martha Blachley born December 13, 1717 Stamford and who, after Samuel’s death, remarried, before August 1758, to Nehemiah Weed and they were both living in 1774. Born to him and Abigail were:

Children (5)
