MARY FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.1.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4) MARY FERRIS (previously listed in the (2) Peter line) born January 17, 1750 Stamford; baptized June 10, 1750 at St. John's Episcopal Church, Stamford; and died 1784 New Canaan CT while visiting with friends; married 1763 in Stamford, as his 1st wife, David Slawson [son of David & Eunice (Scofield) Slawson] born August 18, 1735 Stamford and died May 15, 1805 Pochuc, Warwick Township, Orange Co NY; Rev. War Vet; after Mary’s death, he married Deborah Christy and they had 2 kids. Born to Mary and David were:

Children (6)

  • ESTHER SLAWSON 3.1.2.1.4.2
  • DAVID SLAWSON 3.1.2.1.4.3
