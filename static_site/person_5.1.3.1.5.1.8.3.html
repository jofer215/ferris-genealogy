WELLS T. FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.5.1.8.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.5.1.8.3) WELLS T. FERRIS born October 12, 1861 Smyrna NY and died January 17, 1938 Oelwein IA; married February 25, 1891 Ruth Ellen Alcock born October 30, 1867 Grant City WI and died January 21, 1938 Oelwein IA. Wells is in the Bremer County IA census of 1895 as a farmer. They later moved to Osceola MO where Wells, Ruth and son, Joseph, worked at the County Home there. By 1923, they were back in Oelwein.
