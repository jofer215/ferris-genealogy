MARTHA FERRIS

ID: 1.8 | Source: John
(1.8) MARTHA FERRIS born circa 1682 (1677?) and died circa 1754/55 [listed in her father’s will]. She married, as his second wife, Daniel Clark (Clarke) [son of Samuel] born January 20, 1674. Martha is listed as recipient of New York Administrative Bonds in 1754 presumably from her husband's will.

No children recorded
