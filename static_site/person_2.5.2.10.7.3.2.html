JAMES JAUNCEY HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.10.7.3.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.10.7.3.2) JAMES JAUNCEY HOYT (#4616) (Colonel) born September 22, 1840 New Haven CT; living unmarried on Staten Island 1871; a broker in New York City; enlisted August 1862 in the 156th New York Regiment; was Captain commanding a Regiment in Shenandoah Valley at Winchester, Fisher’s Hill, and Cedar Creek; wounded October 19, 1864; honorably discharged January 1865. He was brevet Major for services at Winchester and Lt. Col. for services at Cedar Creek.

No children recorded
