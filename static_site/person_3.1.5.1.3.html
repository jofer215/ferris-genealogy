EZRA RUNDLE

ID: 3.1.5.1.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.5.1.3) EZRA RUNDLE born March 11, 1760 New Fairfield CT and died February 13, 1839 Penfield, Monroe, NY; married April 25, 1782 at New Fairfield, Elizabeth Knapp (1765-1842) [Ezra migrated to Freehold, Albany County NY in 1790; removed to (by) 1805 Boylestown, Ontario County NY which was changed to Penfield in 1806; in 1812/13, Penfield was changed to Smallwood and in 1814, it was divided into two parts and named Brighton and Pittsford.] (we have a problem with Ezra’s date of birth and his brother, Joseph’s - Ezra March 1760 and Joseph Aug 1760) and born to them were:
