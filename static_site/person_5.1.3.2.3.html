JONATHAN FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.2.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.3) JONATHAN FERRIS born c1767/9 Stamford and died April 18, 1846 Greenwich; buried 1st Congregational Church Cemetery, Greenwich; will dated June 22, 1842, probated May 11, 1846 mentioned his wife (Elizabeth Waring), and children James, Samuel, Hannah, Ann, and Susan; married Elizabeth (Betsy) Waring (5.1.3.10.7). Born to Jonathan and Betsy were:
