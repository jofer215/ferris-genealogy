FANNIE FERN COLE

ID: 1.2.3.7.2.6.3.3.2 | Source: John
(1.2.3.7.2.6.3.3.2) FANNIE FERN COLE born September 10, 1853 Beaver Dams NY and died September 17, 1906; married November 19, 1874 at Beaver Dams, Lyman Smith born June 23, 1849 Post Creek [son of John and Lany (Traver) Smith] (1.2.3.7.2.6.3.3.3) ORVILLE SEYMOUR COLE born January 10, 1855 Beaver Dams NY; a carpenter; married May 21, 1890 Delphine Van Loom born May 28, 1855 [dau of Samuel and Mary (Harvey) Van Loom] - no offspring
