HARRIET NEWELL GODFREY

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.2) HARRIET NEWELL GODFREY born May 20, 1842 Stafford, Genesee, NY and died April 4, 1874 Mitchell, Lawrence, IN; married April 1866 in Washington Co IN, David William Lee [son of Samuel & Rebecca (Colglazer) Lee] born March 29, 1838 Washington Co IN and died 1908 Mitchell, Lawrence, IN; carpenter 1870-80; after Harriet’s death, David married Lucinda; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • ALONZO ELLSWORTH LEE 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.2.1
  • EMMA LOUISE LEE 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.2.2
  • ORLO MITCHELL LEE 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.2.3
  • CARRIE ISABELLA LEE 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.2.2.4
