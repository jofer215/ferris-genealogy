ZILPHA CHRISTINE COLE

ID: 1.2.3.7.2.3.1.6.1.1.4 | Source: John
(1.2.3.7.2.3.1.6.1.1.4) ZILPHA CHRISTINE COLE born December 22, 1917 Pittsburgh PA and died in February 1981 Chandler AZ. She married September 30, 1943 in Jackson MS, Frank Frederick Ferryman [son of Francis Revez & Margery Ferryman] born February 13, 1915 London, England and died July 11, 1997 Chandler AZ.
