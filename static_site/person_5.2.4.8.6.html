JULIA ANN DAVENPORT

ID: 5.2.4.8.6 | Source: James
(5.2.4.8.6) JULIA ANN DAVENPORT born November 30, 1794 Stamford and died there July 2, 1868; buried North Stamford Cemetery. She married January 4, 1817 in Stamford, Jotham Hoyt (2.5.2.11.4.2) – see him for offspring

No children recorded
