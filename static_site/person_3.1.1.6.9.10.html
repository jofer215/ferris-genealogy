MARY WADE

ID: 3.1.1.6.9.10 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.9.10) MARY WADE born November 22, 1804 OH and died July 9, 1868 Darke Co OH; married June 17, 1824 in Butler Co OH, Silas Poyner born August 8, 1794 Currituck Co NC and died April 12, 1858 Darke Co OH; and born to them was (and probably others): (3.1.1.6.9.10.1) REBECCA POYNER born November 26, 1826 OH and died April 2, 1903 Pipe Creek, Bandera, TX; married September 20, 1860 in Darke Co OH, Wyatt Dickerson [son of Joshua D & Elizabeth (Dawson) Dickerson] born July 15, 1837 OH and died June 15, 1915 Pipe Creek; both buried there Pipe Creek Cemetery (Photos); and born to them were:

No children recorded
