MARY ELIZABETH HOYT

ID: 2.5.3.4.1.1.7 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.4.1.1.7) MARY ELIZABETH HOYT (#4635) born June 26, 1811 New York City and was living at Long Ridge, Stamford 1871; married David Tucker - no offspring - resided in Stamford. David was apparently a Civil War Union Vet.

No children recorded
