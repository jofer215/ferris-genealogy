GRACE LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.5.5.6.1.1.1 | Source: Mary
(4.4.5.5.6.1.1.1) GRACE LOCKWOOD born May 2, 1876 Cos Cob, Greenwich CT; married December 17, 1902 Lewis Mead Close [son of Shadrach J. & Hannah E. (Reynolds) Close] born October 13, 1850. They resided in Stamford - no offspring. He was 26 years older than her!

No children recorded
