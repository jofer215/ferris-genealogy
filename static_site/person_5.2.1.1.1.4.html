HANNAH BELL

ID: 5.2.1.1.1.4 | Source: James
(5.2.1.1.1.4) HANNAH BELL born May 16, 1803 CT Born to Deborah and her 3d husband, Samuel, when he was 51y, was: (5.2.1.1.2) EZEKIEL SCOFIELD born July 11, 1767 Stamford and died there July 1, 1853; buried there North Stamford Cemetery; 1850 farmer; married 1st December 21, 1793 in Stamford, Abigail Scofield [dau of Reuben & Lydia (Ayres) Scofield] born c1768 Stamford and died there December 3, 1811; buried with Ezekiel; married 2d Huldah Wildman born 1776 Danbury CT; and born to him and Abigail were:

No children recorded
