PHEBE DAKIN

ID: 3.1.1.1.2.10 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.1.2.10) PHEBE DAKIN born July 1, 1798 Northeast, Dutchess, NY and died February 18, 1861 Ithaca NY; married 1813 in Northeast, Herman Stoddard [son of Samuel & Martha (Moore) Stoddard] born October 13, 1786 Salisbury CT and died December 11, 1837 Hudson NY; and born to them were: (3.1.1.1.2.10.1) SAMUEL STODDARD born September 27, 1814 Danby NY and died September 21, 1891 Ithaca, Tompkins, NY; married September 26, 1839 Ann Cecilia Hutchinson born November 2, 1821; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • EVELINE STODDARD 3.1.1.1.2.10.3
  • MARTHA STODDARD 3.1.1.1.2.10.4
