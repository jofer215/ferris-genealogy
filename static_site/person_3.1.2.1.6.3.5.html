MARY WEYBURN

ID: 3.1.2.1.6.3.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.6.3.5) MARY WEYBURN born March 4, 1813 Ulysses NY and died April 16, 1877; buried Woodlawn Cemetery, Norwalk, Huron, OH; married 1st 1831 in Trumansburg NY Peter C. States [son of Woodward/Woodard & Elizabeth (Butters) States] born May 10, 1812 probably Covert Township, Seneca Co NY and died June 25, 1845 Huron Co OH; they removed to Huron Co OH 1833; after Peter's death, Mary remarried 1846 John Laylin born May 22, 1791 Westmoreland Co PA and died April 26, 1877; buried Woodlawn Cemetery, Norwalk; a prominent widower with young children; and born to Mary and Peter were:

Children (7)
