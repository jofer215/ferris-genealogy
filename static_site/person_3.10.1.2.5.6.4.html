ABIGAIL KINCH

ID: 3.10.1.2.5.6.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.5.6.4) ABIGAIL KINCH born February 8, 1823 NY and died August 17, 1854 Henrietta, Jackson, MI; buried Kinch Cemetery (previously called Coon Hill Cemetery) (Photo), Pleasant Lake, Jackson, MI; married c1849 in NY, Walter R. Kinch (3.10.1.2.5.7.4) and after Abigail’s death, Walter married his sister-in-law, Emeline; and born to Abigail and Walter were:

Children (3)

  • CHARLES E. KINCH 3.10.1.2.5.6.4.1
  • ALICE C. KINCH 3.10.1.2.5.6.4.2
  • WILLARD HETH KINCH 3.10.1.2.5.6.4.3
