MARCUS CLINTON HILL

ID: 3.6.3.2.2.2.2.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.2.2.2.6) MARCUS CLINTON HILL born May 4, 1851 probably in Flatbrookville, Sussex, NJ and died May 11, 1935 at 84y NJ; married probably in Warren Co NJ, Sarah Catherine Warner [dau of John W. & Harriet (Konkel) Warner ] born March 5, 1854 Hardwick, Warren, NJ. Marcus was an assessor for the town of Hardwick NJ 1878-80 and it is said he fought in the Civil War, although he would have been awful young. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • HARRIET LOUISE HILL 3.6.3.2.2.2.2.6.1
  • ANNA MAE HILL 3.6.3.2.2.2.2.6.2
  • GRACE HILL 3.6.3.2.2.2.2.6.3
