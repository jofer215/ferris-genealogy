KATHERINE HENSHAW

ID: 4.5.2.3.4.3.6.1.6 | Source: Mary
(4.5.2.3.4.3.6.1.6) KATHERINE HENSHAW born 1880 Buffalo NY and died October 1927 Evanston IL; married 1898 in E Aurora NY, Bernhart G. Henn [son of Bernhart & Jennie A. (Steele) Henn III] born 1875 Buffalo and died May 27, 1930 Chicago IL; he made up the ‘G’, he actually had no middle name; and born to them were: (4.5.2.3.4.3.6.1.6.1) BERNHART WILLIS HENN born November 1, 1899 Cleveland OH and died there March 3, 1965; buried Rocky River OH; married May 28, 1924 in Buffalo NY, Mae Bernadette Gillard [dau of Albert E. & Mary A. (McMullen) Gillard] born June 1, 1895 Cleveland OH and died there May 6, 1981; buried Rocky River; and they had 2 kids
