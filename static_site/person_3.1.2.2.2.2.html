JESSE FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.2.2.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.2.2) JESSE FERRIS born July 26, 1788 and died May 5, 1864 Chenango Co NY age 76y 9m; buried Smyrna East Cemetery, NY; married at Phelps NY, Zeruiah Decker born 1798 Chenango Co and died there January 8, 1883 [Note: a book by Munson on the Early Settlers of the Western Part of Smyrna, has this Jesse as a son of Robert Ferris. Robert was one of four brothers, Joseph, Benjamin and John being the others, who were early settlers of Smyrna - obvious conflict, but will leave here for now, pending further research.]; and born to them were:
