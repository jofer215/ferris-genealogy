(5.1.3.3.4.4) CHARLES KNAPP, executor of his father's estate in 1826. (?) - NOTE: The above families came to Ohio, with their Stites relatives, residing initially about 1799/1800, in the vicinity of Cincinnati OH. The Mariemont Cemetery is the burial ground for many of them (Stites/Ferris/Knapps). Information on this cemetery is located in the (3) Joseph Chapter.

No children recorded
