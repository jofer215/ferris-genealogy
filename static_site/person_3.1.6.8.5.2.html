CHARLOTTE ROSE

ID: 3.1.6.8.5.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.5.2) CHARLOTTE ROSE born February 28, 1803 Roseville, Lanark, Ontario and died April 3, 1881 Elgin, Bastard Township, Leeds Co, Ontario; married 1st Isaac Lockwood; 2d July 1843 Wolford Township, Grenville Co, Ontario as his 3d wife, Daniel Harvey Shipman born August 28, 1791 Young Township, Leeds Co, Ontario and died October 3, 1853 Almonte, Lanark, Ontario; buried there Methodist Cemetetery – no offspring from this marriage but Daniel was the father of Catherine, wife of Henry Ryan Rose below – so his dau was also his sister-in-law by marriage

No children recorded
