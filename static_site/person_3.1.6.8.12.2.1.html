JOHN GILBERT ROSE

ID: 3.1.6.8.12.2.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.12.2.1) JOHN GILBERT ROSE born September 1, 1844 Cobden, Renfrew, Ontario and died March 4, 1917 Prentice, Price, WI; buried Embarrass Cemetery, Wapaca WI; a farmer; immigrated 1866 to Detroit MI; married September 5, 1869 at Clintonville, Wapaca, WI, Thankful Alfreda Draper [dau of Truman Waller & Martha (Barber) Draper] born February 7, 1847 and died August 13, 1925; and born to them were:
