JOHN GODFRIED REYNOLDS

ID: 4.5.2.1.5.2.5 | Source: Mary
(4.5.2.1.5.2.5) JOHN GODFRIED REYNOLDS (Photo) born March 25, 1821 a mile south of Stanwich in Greenwich CT; received a common school education, with a few terms at the Stamford Academy in Stamford CT; married August 26, 1851 Julia R. (Smith) Reynolds, widow of Augustus L. Reynolds [dau of Jared Smith]. John was a Whig in politics until 1856 and after that be became a Democrat. He held nearly all of the town offices, to include town magistrate and treasurer for 11 years, to the general satisfaction of his constituents. He represented his town in the assembly during the trying years of 1863 and 1864, and again in 1874, ’75, and ’77, and while there occupied important places on different committees and was chairman on education on term. He was a trustee and director of the Greenwich Savings Bank. They were members of the Episcopal Church at Greenwich, and he was a vestry man for many years.

No children recorded
