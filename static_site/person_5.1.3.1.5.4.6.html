BETSEY ELIZABETH DOUGLAS – not listed in all sources – born 1805 Chautaugua

ID: 5.1.3.1.5.4.6 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.5.4.6) BETSEY ELIZABETH DOUGLAS – not listed in all sources – born 1805 Chautaugua Co NY and died November 9, 1835 Washington PA; married as his 2d wife, January 1, 1826 in Fredonia, Chautauqua, NY Arva Owen Austin [son of Nathaniel & Humilis (Owen) Austin] born February 14, 1789 Paris, Oneida, NY and died 1863 Boone Co IL; and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • LORINDA LOVINA AUSTIN 5.1.3.1.5.4.6.3
  • CHAUNCEY H AUSTIN 5.1.3.1.5.4.6.4
  • AMANDA AUSTIN 5.1.3.1.5.4.6.5
