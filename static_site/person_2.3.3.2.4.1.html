MARY ELIZABETH HOWE

ID: 2.3.3.2.4.1 | Source: Peter
(2.3.3.2.4.1) MARY ELIZABETH HOWE born November 11, 1807 Stamford CT and died there September 26, 1891; buried there St. John’s & St. Andrew’s Cemetery; married August 31, 1828 in Stamford, Edwin Selleck Holly (2.3.3.3.1.3); and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • MARY ELIZABETH HOLLY 2.3.3.2.4.1.2
  • EDWIN SELLECK HOLLY, Jr., 2.3.3.2.4.1.3
  • HENRY HOBART HOLLY 2.3.3.2.4.1.4
  • JOHN ISAAC HOLLY 2.3.3.2.4.1.5
