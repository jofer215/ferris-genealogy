JULIA ROSE NINK

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.4.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.4.4) JULIA ROSE NINK (Photos) born May 18, 1912 Leelanau Co MI and died August 27, 1993 Traverse City MI; buried there Grand Traverse Memorial Gardens; telephone operator at Michigan Bell for 31y; married June 26, 1933 in Traverse City, (Al) Albert William Janda [son of John J & Katherine (Valish) Janda] born February 23, 1906 Traverse City and died there June 2, 1980; buried there Oakwood Cemetery; taxi driver and owned his own Gulf gas station in Traverse City (Al’s Gulf Service); director for the Lions in Traverse City; Eagles; and they had 2 kids

No children recorded
