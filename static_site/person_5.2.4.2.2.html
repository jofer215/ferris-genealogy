HANNAH BELL

ID: 5.2.4.2.2 | Source: James
(5.2.4.2.2) HANNAH BELL born December 10, 1758 Stamford and died there May 25, 1832; married Fitch Rogers [son of Nehemiah & Elizabeth (Fitch) Rogers] born February 1745/46 and died June 25, 1828 Stamford; both buried there St. John’s & St. Andrew’s Cemtery; and born to them were:
