MELL HELMS BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.6.1.2.2.9.1 | Source: James
(5.2.2.6.1.2.2.9.1) MELL HELMS BUXTON born May 3, 1904 Wittenberg WI and died April 1983 Appleton WI; married October 20, 1924 in Shioctin WI, Vera Hester Poole born March 25, 1901 Shioctin. Born to them were: (5.2.2.6.1.2.2.9.1.1) KEITH MELL BUXTON married 1st Joan Ellen Keenitz; 2d Carole E. Schroeder; resided Appleton and had 3 kids
