IDA ANN HAYMAKER

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.6.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.6.1.1) IDA ANN HAYMAKER born February 27, 1863 Franklin Township, Portage Co OH and died April 16, 1951; during her early years she was an attendant at Earlville district school. When she was 17, she attended Buchtel College for 3 years, it being the time that her father and mother were engaged there. She married, December (2) 21, 1882 at Franklin, Frank A. Merrill [son of Samuel P & Martha J (Barnard) Merrill] born November 25, 1859, Franklin OH and died March 26, 1916. Frank was brought up on the farm, and at first received a good common school education; and then for a time pursued his studies and recited to a tutor in Kent. He then began a course in Western Reserve College at Hudson, and engaged in teaching in order to defray his expenses. He was obliged, finally, to cease attending school on account of his eyes failing. He was engaged some 3 years in working on his father's farm. When he became of age, he devoted his time principally, to teaching. One of his teaching assignments was at the high school in Ravenna OH. Born to them were:
