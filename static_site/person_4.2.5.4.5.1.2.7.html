EVALINE ELIZABETH SANFORD

ID: 4.2.5.4.5.1.2.7 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.4.5.1.2.7) EVALINE ELIZABETH SANFORD (Eva) born April 28, 1844 Rootstown OH and died November 1923 Kent, Portage, OH; buried there Standing Rock Cemetery; married December 14, 1865 in Marion Co OH, George Lloyd Stauffer born October 26, 1839 Uniontown, Stark, OH and died March 1939 Kent OH; buried there Standing Rock Cemetery; George was reared on a farm until he was 20 and educated at Greensburg Academy; met Evaline while working in a shoe store; went to NYC when he was 22 and entered the employ of the Northern Transportation Co., where he remained for 7 years; then engaged in grocery business in Jersey City NJ for a year; in 1871 he located in Kent OH and worked as a clerk; in 1873 he embarked in the grocery trade in Kent; served as a councilman of Kent one term; and born to them were:

Children (6)
