CHARLOTTE ELIZABETH SORENSEN

ID: 2.1.3.3.2.4.1.2.5.1 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.2.4.1.2.5.1) CHARLOTTE ELIZABETH SORENSEN born March 11, 1915 Honolulu HI; married 1st 1935 John Norman Schenck born October 11, 1911 NY and died 1943 Honolulu; married 2d 1944 in Honolulu, David Paul Johnson born May 18, 1915 OK and died April 23, 1993 New Braunfels TX. Paul was a Navy Veteran and he adopted Patricia and Barbara. Born to Charlotte and John were:

Children (2)

  • BARBARA JULIETTE SCHENCK 2.1.3.3.2.4.1.2.5.1.2
