AMY DENNEY

ID: 1.1.4.4.5.3.3.1 | Source: John
(1.1.4.4.5.3.3.1) AMY DENNEY born December 15, 1882; was living with her grandmother, Rebecca Mekeel on the 1900 census and is listed as Amy Mekeel, granddaughter, age 17, born Dec 1882; married, under the name of Amy Mekeel, June 16, 1900, Joseph B. England, Principal of the Nelsonville School. Born to them was a son, a school teacher.

No children recorded
