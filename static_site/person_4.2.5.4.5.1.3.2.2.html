WORTHY G. SMITH

ID: 4.2.5.4.5.1.3.2.2 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.4.5.1.3.2.2) WORTHY G. SMITH born May 28, 1866 Whiteford, Monroe, MI and died 1952 Adrian, Lenawee, MI; attended Blissfield H. S., worked in Blissfield, Flint, Detroit, and Adrian before being elected Lenawee County Register of Deeds 1917-30; worked as a real estate broker and was very active in volunteer activities in Adrian until his death; married October 11, 1905 Netta Oneta born c1870 Whiteford MI (Meta O Dawley born 1880 Green Springs, Sandusky, OH and died 1970 Adrian MI [dau of Randolph A & Mary Luella (Short) Dawley] and born to him and Meta was:
