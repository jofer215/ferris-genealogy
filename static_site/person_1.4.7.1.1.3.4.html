SARAH ELLEN CALDWELL

ID: 1.4.7.1.1.3.4 | Source: John
(1.4.7.1.1.3.4) SARAH ELLEN CALDWELL born January 20, 1851 Bedford NY and died December 12, 1941 Stamford CT; buried there Long Ridge Union. She married November 30, 1870 in Poundridge NY, Henry A. Haviland [son of William B. & Maria (Van Wart) Haviland] born January 23, 1845 in Mamaroneck NY and died July 4, 1916 Stamford CT; buried there Long Ridge Union. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • FERRIS HENRY HAVILAND 1.4.7.1.1.3.4.1
  • LEVI D. HAVILAND 1.4.7.1.1.3.4.2
