LOUISE I. KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.10.1.4.1 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.10.1.4.1) LOUISE I. KNAPP (Louise J. Knapp) (Louisa J.) died September 17, 1888 age 66y 3m; buried Lockwood Cemetery #5, Stamford; married c1851, as his 2d wife, James W Finch [son of John & Sally (Ferris) Finch] born June 27, 1818 Stamford CT; 1850 and died there August 20, 1904; buried there Turn of River Cemetery; chain pump; Lockwood (4.6.3.1.1.1.2); and born to Louise and James were: James’ 1st wife was Sarah A.

Children (3)

  • HENRY STANLEY FINCH 2.6.2.10.1.4.1.1
  • EMMA LOUISA FINCH 2.6.2.10.1.4.1.2
  • AGNES FINCH 2.6.2.10.1.4.1.3
