LEWIS CASE LAYLIN

ID: 3.1.2.1.6.3.5.7.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.6.3.5.7.1) LEWIS CASE LAYLIN born September 28, 1848 and died November 17, 1923; prosecuting attorney in Huron Co OH for 7 years; member of the State Legislature for 3 terms, the last being Speaker of the House; Secretary of State of 3 terms of 2 year each; 1907 appointed by the Governor to be a member of a State Commission to revise and consolidate the general statues of the State and became chairman of the commission; married November 3, 1880 Francis Latimore Dewey (chairperson of the legislative committee of the DAR of Ohio) and they had 3 kids

No children recorded
