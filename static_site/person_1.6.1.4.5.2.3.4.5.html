MICHAEL FERRIS, SR.,

ID: 1.6.1.4.5.2.3.4.5 | Source: John
(1.6.1.4.5.2.3.4.5) MICHAEL FERRIS, SR., born White Plains NY November 9, 1926; served in the U.S. Navy 1944-46; electrician by trade; married 1st Marianne Fusillo; 2d Joan Wald Solazzo. Michael resided at North White Plains NY and recently moved to East Lyme CT. (Jim - my thanks to Michael and his daughter Kim for all of this information on this “twig”.) Born to Michael and Marianne were:

Children (3)
