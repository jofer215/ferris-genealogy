ADDIE MARIA UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.6.2.4.2.3.1 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.4.2.3.1) ADDIE MARIA UNDERHILL born October 2, 1882 Wiota WI and died July 4, 1925 Monroe WI; buried there Greenwood Cemetery; married November 15, 1913 in Freeport IL, as his 2d of 3 wives, John Steve Shelton [son of Anton Peterson Shelton] born April 19, 1871 Covel KY and died November 27, 1959 Rock City IL; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • BESSIE ALMA SHELTON 1.2.6.2.4.2.3.1.2
  • PEARL E SHELTON 1.2.6.2.4.2.3.1.3
