PETER HAIT

ID: 2.5.2.3.5 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.3.5) PETER HAIT (#3012) born February 16/17, 1738 Stamford and died there August 9, 1810 where he lived and farmed; buried there Hope Street Cemetery #1; served as a Corporal in the 9th Militia Regiment between August 12 – September 11, 1776; married April 5, 1762 in Stamford, Sarah Hait (#3041) (2.5.2.8.3); and born to them were (Hait or Hoyt - will go with Hoyt):

Children (6)

  • AMOS HOYT 2.5.2.3.5.1
  • BETSEY HOYT 2.5.2.3.5.2
  • SALLY HOYT 2.5.2.3.5.3
  • PETER HOYT 2.5.2.3.5.4
  • ENOCH HOYT 2.5.2.3.5.5
  • SALMON HOYT 2.5.2.3.5.6
