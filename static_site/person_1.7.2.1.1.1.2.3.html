DANSKE BEDINGER

ID: 1.7.2.1.1.1.2.3 | Source: John
(1.7.2.1.1.1.2.3) DANSKE BEDINGER (CAROLINE DANE) (Photos) (1854-1914); poet and garden muse; suggest visting www.libraries.wvu.edu/dandridge for information on this lady; attended Mrs. Meade’s Piedmont Female Institute; after her mother’s death, moved to Flushing and lived with her grandparents and attends Flushing Institute; in Flushing attends Mrs. Williams’ Young Ladies School – her last formal education; first book of poems “Joy and Other Poems” published 1888; married 1877 Steve Dandridge who was elected to the West Virginia House of Representatives in Charleston and died 1922; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • VIOLET DANDRIDGE 1.7.2.1.1.1.2.3.1
  • ADAM STEPHEN DANDRIDGE 1.7.2.1.1.1.2.3.2
  • DOROTHEA SPOTTSWOOD DANDRIDGE 1.7.2.1.1.1.2.3.3
