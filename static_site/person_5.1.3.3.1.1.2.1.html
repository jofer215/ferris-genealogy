FRANCES E. FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.1.2.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.1.2.1) FRANCES E. FERRIS born 1836 CT and died after 1880 Stamford; married 1860 Charles Silas Burley (5.1.4.4.11.3.2). In 1880, widowed, age 44, was living in Stamford with her son Charles F. Burley, age 17, her daughter Maria L. Burley, age 15, and her widowed mother Rhoda K. Ferris, age 77. See him for offspring.

No children recorded
