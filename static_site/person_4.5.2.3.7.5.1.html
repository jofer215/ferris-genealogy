CLARISSA MOODIE SMITH

ID: 4.5.2.3.7.5.1 | Source: Mary
(4.5.2.3.7.5.1) CLARISSA MOODIE SMITH born September 5, 1835 Porchester NY and died February 16, 1896 Vienna VA; buried Flint Hill Cemetery, Oakton VA; married George Warner Ponsiby Bouton [son of Charles Augustus & Deborah (Clark) Bouton] born April 8, 1830 NYC and died by drowning May 2, 1881 Gowanus Bay NY; Civil War Vet with 6th New York Heavy Artillery, 1st Lieutenant; 1862-64; and born to them were:

Children (2)

  • EDMUND LOCKWOOD SMITH BOUTON 4.5.2.3.7.5.1.2
