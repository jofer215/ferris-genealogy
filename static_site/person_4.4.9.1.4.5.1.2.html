CHARLES OLMSTEAD, Rev.,

ID: 4.4.9.1.4.5.1.2 | Source: Mary
(4.4.9.1.4.5.1.2) CHARLES OLMSTEAD, Rev., born February 1, 1853 Walton NY; spent his early life in farm work and teaching; graduated at Walton NY High School 1876, and Williams College 1880; entered Union Theological Seminary, NYC, 1880, graduating 1883; meanwhile served as Pastor of a Mission Chapel in NYC; ordained (Congregational) Walton September 27, 1883; Pastor First Congregational Church, Oswego Falls (now Fulton) NY 1883-90; Pastor Pilgrim Congregational Church, Cambridge MA 1897-98; Pastor, Three Rivers (now Palmer) MA 1898-1907; Pastor, Fulton NY 1907-*; married 1st August 17, 1883 at Boston MA, Charlotte Amelia Truesdell [dau of Cornelius & Mary E (Manor) Truesdell] born October 29, 1858 Landsdown, Ontario, Canada and died September 13, 1894 Cambridge MA; 2d May 2, 1899 at Cambridge, Mary Alice Fuller [dau of Lyman L & Mary E (Nay) Fuller] born December 2, 1873 Cambridge; and born to him and Charlotte were:

Children (3)

  • CARROLL HANFORD OLMSTED 4.4.9.1.4.5.1.2.2
  • ALAN CARLISLE OLMSTED 4.4.9.1.4.5.1.2.3
