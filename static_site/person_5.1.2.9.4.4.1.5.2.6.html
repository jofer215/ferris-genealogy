LUCY BELL TOMPKINS

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.1.5.2.6 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.1.5.2.6) LUCY BELL TOMPKINS born August 22, 1916 Putnam Valley NY and died April 15, 1970 Danbury CT; buried there Wooster Cemetery; married Henry John Tompkins [son of William & Pearl (Matt)] born March 9, 1915 Sharon CT and died March 23, 1962 Carmel NY; buried Raymond Hill Cemetery, Carmel NY. Obituary, Danbury Times, Danbury, Conn., April 16, 1970: Mrs. Lucy Bell Tompkins, 53, of 7 Park Ave., Danbury, widow of Henry J. Tompkins, died yesterday morning in Danbury Hospital after a short illness. She born in Continentville, Aug. 22, 1916, daughter of the late Hart and Jessie M. Tompkins. She was an employee of Danbury Pavilion for the past three years. She moved to Brewster, N.Y., as a young girl and attended school there, moving to Danbury about nine years ago. She leaves a son, Robert J. Tompkins of Danbury; six daughters, Mrs. Eugene Stetson and Mrs. Donald Wilson, both of New Milford; Mrs. Donald Banks of Bethel, Mrs. George Curry, Mrs. Bruce Smith and Miss Faye Marie Tompkins, all of Danbury; four brothers, George of Dover Plains, N.Y., Robert of Carmel, N.Y., Ernest of Mt. Kisco, N.Y., and James of Brewster; three sisters, Mrs. Pearl Fletcher of Carmel, Mrs. Dorothy Zaniboni of Pawling, N.Y., and Miss June Tompkins of Brewster; eight grandchildren and several nieces and nephews. Burial will be in Wooster Cemetery, Danbury. Born to them were:
