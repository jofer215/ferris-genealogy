CALVIN PECK

ID: 3.10.1.3.7 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.3.7) CALVIN PECK born December 4, 1764 Greenwich and died September 1, 1837 Sharon CT; married Betsy Pierson (or Parson(s)) [dau of Captain Enoch Parsons of Sharon CT] born March 17, 1762 Newton CT; and they resided at Sharon CT, where he was a deacon of the Congregational Church. Born to them were:
