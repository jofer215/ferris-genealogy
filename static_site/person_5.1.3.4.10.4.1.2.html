CHESTER B. FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2) CHESTER B. FERRIS (Photo) born February 5, 1876 Tampico IL and died July 17, 1964 Custer SD; married January 1, 1901 at Canton SD, Ina Adelia Peebles Walrath [dau of Augustas & Cora (Thayer) Peebles] born March 21,1881 near Lake City IA and died November 26, 1973, Pierre SD. Ina born Peebles, but was adopted by Walrath. Both buried in Draper SD, next to his parents. They homesteaded near and later moved to Draper in 1913, where they operated a grocery until 1941, when they moved to Custer SD, where they remained except for a short time spent in California during WWII. Both are buried next to Chester's parents at the Draper Cemetery, Draper SD (tombstone photo). Born to them were: (5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2.1) CLIFFORD HARLEY FERRIS born December 16, 1901 Draper (Canton), Lincoln Co, SD and died March 31, 1957 Hot Springs SD; buried Draper Cemetery, Draper SD; married December 22, 1923 Edna V. Rankin born October 30, 1902 Winterset IA and died August 6, 1961 Willcox AZ. Harley spent 2 years at the South Dakota School of Mines and was a mail carrier for many years at Draper SD. Edna received an AA degree from Vermillion Teacher's College, SD. There were Methodist Church members. Born to them were:
