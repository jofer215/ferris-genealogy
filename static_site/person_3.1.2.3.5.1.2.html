EDWIN RUTHVAN FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.3.5.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.3.5.1.2) EDWIN RUTHVAN FERRIS born August 22, 1822 Panton VT and died June 13, 1890; buried Vergennes VT; married 1st November 11, 1846 Elizabeth Stevenson; and 2d February 3, 1856 Lucy Ann Whipple; 1846 was of Nicholsville, St. Lawrence , NY

No children recorded
