VALENTINE PENNIMAN FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.5.4.2 | Source: John
(1.4.7.5.4.2) VALENTINE PENNIMAN FERRIS born September 15, 1840 Swanton and died November 29, 1874 while on a buffalo hunting expedition near Denver CO; lost in a snowstorm. His body was later recovered and buried in March 1882 in Church Street Cemetery, Denver. A memorial stone was erected in the old Cemetery, in Swanton. He married Louisa A. Harrell (1849-1900) and born to them were:

Children (2)
