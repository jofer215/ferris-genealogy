JAMES BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.6.1 | Source: James
(5.2.2.6.1) JAMES BUXTON born August 31, 1768 Stamford CT and died between 1840-49 Carthage (Wilna), Jefferson, NY; farmer and postmaster at Carthage NY; married 1st June 8, 1793 in Fonda, Montgomery, NY Margarita Vrooman [dau of Hendrick B. & Margaret (Vanderweken) Vrooman] born February 20, 1773 Fonda NY and died Montgomery Co NY; married 2d August 20, 1812 in Fonda, Maritje Doritje Putman [dau Derik & Aelie (Van Brackel) Putman] born September 12, 1779 Fonda NY and died June 14, 1852 Carthage, Jefferson, NY; buried Old State Cemetery, Carthage. (This was Maritje’s second marriage also – she had 5 kids to first husband.) Born to him and Margarita were:
