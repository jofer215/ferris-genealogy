JAMES B. MCCREARY

ID: 4.5.2.4.1.1.1.2.1 | Source: Mary
(4.5.2.4.1.1.1.2.1) JAMES B. MCCREARY (JR?) born September 9, 1893 and died July 5, 1919; went to France with Richard Norton Ambulance Service as a driver in 1916; joined the First French Foreign Legion, returned home on sick leave and was honorably discharged. He attempted to join the American, then French Forces, but was rejected by both. He joined the Y.M.C.A. and served as a trouble man in France; then sent to Prague where he died.
