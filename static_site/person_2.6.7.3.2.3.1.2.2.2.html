JOEL ERNEST KNAPP

ID: 2.6.7.3.2.3.1.2.2.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.3.2.3.1.2.2.2) JOEL ERNEST KNAPP born August 12, 1923 Fayetteville, Onondaga, NY and died May 17, 2004 Anderson, Madison, IN; buried Fayetteville NY; graduated from Fayetteville High School; USAF WWII Vet; Air Traffic Controller retired from Government service 1978; married November 3-5, 1945 in Peoria IL, Phyliss Jane Paape [dau of Walden & Willa] born December 15, 1926 Marietta, Duval, FL and died August 10, 2002 Columbus, Bartholomew, IN; buried there – divorced 1951 – they adopted Barbara Jane Knapp

No children recorded
