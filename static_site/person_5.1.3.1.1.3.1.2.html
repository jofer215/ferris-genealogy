RUTH P VAN BENSCHOTEN

ID: 5.1.3.1.1.3.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.1.3.1.2) RUTH P VAN BENSCHOTEN born March 11, 1813 Sullivan Co NY and died April 15, 1878 Scott, Cortland, NY; married April 27, 1834 in Summerhill, Cayuga, NY Edwin P Spencer [son of Asa & Bathsheba (Phelps) Spencer] born July 22, 1812 Sempronius, Cayuga, NY and died June 22, 1888 Ludlowville, Tompkins, NY; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • TRYPHENA E SPENCER 5.1.3.1.1.3.1.2.1
  • JAMES B SPENCER 5.1.3.1.1.3.1.2.2
  • PHEBE JANE SPENCER 5.1.3.1.1.3.1.2.3
