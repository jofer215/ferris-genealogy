LAURA MAY JONES

ID: 1.2.2.6.2.1.1.1.3 | Source: John
(1.2.2.6.2.1.1.1.3) LAURA MAY JONES (1892-1980) married Garth Elsmere Bingham (1889-1962). Laura was from a Loyist family from White Plains NY, who fled the U. S. to Canada during the Revolutionary War and no one returned to the U. S. until Laura married Garth. Laura was a legal secretary for Judge Slipp in St. John, New Brunswick and Garth, a U. S. Government official, was employed there. Born to them was (and possibly more):
