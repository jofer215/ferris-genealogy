LINDLEY MURRAY FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.6.6 | Source: John
(1.4.7.6.6) LINDLEY MURRAY FERRIS born November 15, 1810 Throggss Neck and died March 1, 1872 Jacksonville FL; 1847, he sold his interest in the firm of Ketcham, Ferris & Woodward, Inc.; married 1st May 31, 1836 Caroline Murray (18131852); and 2d April 12, 1854 at Coldenham, Mary Murray (1815-1881) (related?).
