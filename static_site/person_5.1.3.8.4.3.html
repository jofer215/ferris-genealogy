CHARLES FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.4.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.4.3) CHARLES FERRIS (tombstone photo) born October 3, 1821 Greenwich and died November 14, 1893; married 1st October 13, 1839 Margaret Ritch Lockwood (3.6.1.4.2.3.1) and 2d February 4, 1844, his sister-in-law, Harriet C. Lockwood (3.6.1.4.2.3.3). He was a grocer in the Greenwich 1850 census; later moved to Port Chester NY. Born to Charles and Harriet were:
