BYRON C GODFREY

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.7.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.3.7.6) BYRON C GODFREY born December 11, 1854 Jackson Co MI; soon after marriage, he moved to Eaton Co MI and in 1892 to Elkhart IN, where he was President of the Godfrey Conveyor Co; farmer 1880 Brookfield MI; married 1st Jenette Hall (Nettie) born September 5, 1858 MI and died June 6, 1915 Elkhart IN; married 2d Carrie Scales born July 18, 1868; and born to him and Nettie were:

Children (6)
