EUGENE DANIEL SUTTON

ID: 1.2.5.1.10.7.4 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.10.7.4) EUGENE DANIEL SUTTON born March 6, 1856 Pennfield MI and died August 26, 1933 Battle Creek MI; resided 1880 Orleans Township, Ionia Co MI; 1900 Battle Creek MI; 1910 Pennfield MI; woodworker for Nichols & Shepard, Battle Creek; married February 22, 1875 in Battle Creek, Patience Lillian Webb [dau of Caleb & Sarah (Green) Webb] born August 21, 1857 MI and died August 7, 1933 Battle Creek; both buried Hicks Cemetery, Pennfield MI; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • LILLIAN EUGENIA SUTTON 1.2.5.1.10.7.4.1
  • LUELLA JOSEPHINE SUTTON 1.2.5.1.10.7.4.2
  • VERN DENIS SUTTON 1.2.5.1.10.7.4.3
  • ANNIE MATILDA SUTTON 1.2.5.1.10.7.4.4
