WILLIAM STUART FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.2.1.3.6.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.1.3.6.1) WILLIAM STUART FERRIS born August 31, 1862 Brooklyn NY; married June 17, 1890 in North Adams MA, Beulah Lillian Briggs born July 21, 1867; he was a capitalist and in the insurance business; resided NYC, San Francisco and later Salt Lake City; and they had 2 kids

No children recorded
