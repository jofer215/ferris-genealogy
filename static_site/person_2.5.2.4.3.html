ISAAC HAIT

ID: 2.5.2.4.3 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.3) ISAAC HAIT (Hoyt) (#3015) born September 14, 1734 and died January-March, 1777 Stamford where he lived; landowner in Stamford; married 1st August 5/6, 1761 Mary Skelding who died August, 14, 1767 at age 31y; 2d May 26, 1768 Sarah Hait (#2947) born April 13, 1736; widow Sarah Hait married Captain Charles Smith before July, 1784. Born to Isaac and Mary were (Hait or Hoyt - will go with Hoyt):

Children (2)
