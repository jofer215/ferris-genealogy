LYDIA ELLA UNDERHILL

ID: 1.7.7.2.2.9.1.1 | Source: John
(1.7.7.2.2.9.1.1) LYDIA ELLA UNDERHILL born April 7, 1860 Philadelphia PA and died March 13, 1926 Germantown PA resulting from an auto accident; married April 1884 in Brooklyn, as his 1st wife, Rev. James McConaughy born March 30, 1857 Gettysburg PA and died December 6, 1934 Germantown; MA, Litt D; Mt Hermon School, Department of Bible head; Sunday School Union editor; both buried Prospect Park Friends Cemetery, Brooklyn; and born to them were:

Children (4)
