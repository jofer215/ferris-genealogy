AMANDA FERRIS

ID: 3.1.1.5.1.8 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.5.1.8) AMANDA FERRIS born June 2, 1810 Carmel NY and died December 29, 1898 Cleveland OH; married 1st as his second wife, August 31, 1840 David Cole [son of Elisha & Rebecca Townsend (Hopkins) Cole] born December 5, 1808 and died July 9, 1844 Steuben OH; married 2d A Whitman. Children (2)
