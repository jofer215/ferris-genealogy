MARGARET A. FERRIS

ID: 3.1.9.1.9.5.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.9.1.9.5.1) MARGARET A. FERRIS born December 4, 1815 Saratoga Springs, Saratoga, NY and died January 19, 1885 Fulton Co NY age 69y 1m 15d;.
