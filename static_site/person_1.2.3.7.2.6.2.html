CHARLES GREEN COLE

ID: 1.2.3.7.2.6.2 | Source: John
(1.2.3.7.2.6.2) CHARLES GREEN COLE born August 14, 1800 and died April 7, 1846 Beaver Dams NY; married 1st January 14, 1824 Susan Wood who died February 9, 1827 age 21y 4m 1d; Charles and his brother, Ira, settled in Beaver Dams, Schuyler Co NY 1830. They came from Carmel via the Erie Canal to Geneva, up Seneca Lake to Watkins and from there to Beaver Dams; the journey took one week. The two brothers bought a farm of 64 acres and worked it together and lived in a log house with their families for three years. Then Charles built another log house in the same yard and they lived in this way until Charles died. Charles married, second, February 6, 1830, Mary Jane Bailey born December 12, 1808 in Brewster NY and died June 17, 1897 [daughter of Samuel and Hannah (Wright) Bailey].
