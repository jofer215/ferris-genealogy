URIAH LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.5.5.6 | Source: Mary
(4.4.5.5.6) URIAH LOCKWOOD (#796) born September 18, 1805 Greenwich CT and died and buried there August 14, 1880; married September 24, 1827 Rebecca Smith [dau of Joseph & Rebecca (Mills) Smith] born June 19, 1805 and died January 21, 1893; a farmer and owner of a market-boat carrying produce down the Sound to NYC; Protestant – Democrat; lived in Greenwich where the kids were born.
