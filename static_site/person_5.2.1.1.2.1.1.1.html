EDWIN LEWIS SCOFIELD

ID: 5.2.1.1.2.1.1.1 | Source: James
(5.2.1.1.2.1.1.1) EDWIN LEWIS SCOFIELD born June 18, 1852 Stamford and died there January 14, 1918; graduate of Columbia U Law School practiced in Stamford starting in 1873; elected in 1881 to State House and then to the State Senate; Stamford’s 2d mayor; Commissioner of Building and Loan Associations 1897-99; appointed as State Insurance Commissioner until 1902; Vice President of the First National Bank; president of the Morris Plan Bank; director of other banks and savings institutions; married October 15, 1879 in Norwalk CT, Annie Weed Candee [dau of Julius A & Evelina (Weed) Candee] born January 17, 1855 NYC and died February 8, 1908 Stamford; and born to them was:
