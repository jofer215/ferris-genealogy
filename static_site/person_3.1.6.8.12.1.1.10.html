LAURA ANN ROSE

ID: 3.1.6.8.12.1.1.10 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.12.1.1.10) LAURA ANN ROSE born June 25, 1904 Haley Station, Renfrew, Ontario; married June 30, 1931 in Admaston Township, Renfrew Co, Angus Roberts Hamilton [son of Samuel and Margaret Jane (LeBarron) Hamilton] born June 24, 1900 Bromley Township, Renfrew Co and died August 27, 1968 Ottawa Civil Hospital, Ontario; buried Cobden Cemetery, Renfrew Co; a farmer; and born to them were:

Children (3)
