THADDEUS HUSTED baptized at New Canaan March 26, 1758 and died August 30, 1832

ID: 3.1.4.2.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.4.2.5) THADDEUS HUSTED baptized at New Canaan March 26, 1758 and died August 30, 1832 White Oak Shade, New Canaan CT; buried New Canaan; a Private in Captain Hait’s Company in 1775 for 8 days; was a Cornet in the 3d Regiment of Militia Light Horse in 1776; married January 24, 1782 in Stillwater NY, Abigail Seymour [dau of Thomas & Elizaebth] born 1761 New Canaan and died October 3, 1835 {first cousin once removed}; and born to them were:

Children (10)

  • POLLY HUSTED 3.1.4.2.5.1
  • MARY ALMIRA HUSTED baptized August 28, 1803 3.1.4.2.5.10
  • THOMAS SEYMOUR HUSTED 3.1.4.2.5.2
  • NANCY HUSTED 3.1.4.2.5.3
  • THOMAS SEYMOUR HUSTED 3.1.4.2.5.4
  • THADDEUS B. HUSTED 3.1.4.2.5.5
  • SAMUEL HUSTED 3.1.4.2.5.6
  • MORRIS HUSTED 3.1.4.2.5.7
  • MORRIS HUSTED 3.1.4.2.5.8
  • ALFRED HUSTED baptized August 30, 1799; married Joan Caroline Gardiner and born 3.1.4.2.5.9
