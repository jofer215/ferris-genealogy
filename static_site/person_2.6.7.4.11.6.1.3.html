ARTHUR PEMBROKE NEWELL

ID: 2.6.7.4.11.6.1.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.4.11.6.1.3) ARTHUR PEMBROKE NEWELL born October 12, 1854 Provo UT and died June 26, 1883 Smithville, Gila, AZ. He married November 1, 1875 at SLC UT as her first husband, Elizabeth Caroline Haws [dau of Amos Whitcomb & Mary Elizabeth (Bean) Haws] born October 6, 1856 Provo UT and died July 9, 1893 Pima, Graham, AZ. After Arthur’s death, Elizabeth married 1885 Thomas W. Hundley. Born to Arthur and Elizabeth were:
