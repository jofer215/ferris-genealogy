ABRAHAM KNAPP

ID: 2.6.7.4 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.4) ABRAHAM KNAPP (8M0J-N7) born December 28, 1737 Stamford and died date unk most probably in Genesse Co NY; married 1st September 17, 1770 at Stamford, Elizabeth Wright; 2d January 27, 1757 in Norfolk CT, Martha Comstock [dau of Samuel & Dorothy (Rogers) Comstock] (1738-); he was a farmer and Veteran of French & Indian Wars, and the Rev.
