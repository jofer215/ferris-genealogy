DANIEL DEAN FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.1.10.1.1.7 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.10.1.1.7) DANIEL DEAN FERRIS born March 1, 1866 Provo (Goshen) UT and died June 7, 1936; buried Eureka Cemetery, Juab Co UT (Photo); married December 12, 1893 Minnie Bell Till [dau of Robert & Mary Ann (Greening) Till born August 3, 1865 Provo UT and died March 31, 1907 – buried in the same cemetery are Eliza (November 27, 1888 – January 29, 1955), Robert Reed Ferris (March 17, 1896 – June 30, 1928), William Ferris (December 7, 1908 – June 12, 1972), and Betsy Ferris (June 12, 1939 – October 12, 1977) – there is also a Daniel Dean Ferris (August 12, 1894 – December 28, 1894) buried Goshen Town Cemetery - wonder if these are offspring/descendants of Daniel’s

No children recorded
