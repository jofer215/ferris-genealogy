AMELIA MOFFAT

ID: 1.1.4.4.1.3.5 | Source: John
(1.1.4.4.1.3.5) AMELIA MOFFAT born 1858 Sullivan WI and died young; listed in the 1860 census as 2 years old; Necedah, Juneau County WI, which is where she was probably buried. She probably died before her sister born in 1861, thus her sister’s middle name.

No children recorded
