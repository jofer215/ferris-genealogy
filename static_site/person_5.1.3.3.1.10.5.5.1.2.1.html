DIANE LOUISE SUPROCK

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.10.5.5.1.2.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.10.5.5.1.2.1) DIANE LOUISE SUPROCK born October 12, 1954 Kansas City MO; graduated from John F. Kennedy U., with a B.S. in Business and received an MBA from Golden State U. She worked for several years in the manufacturing section, then worked as a consultant, and is currently (2002) the Senior Projects Manager for Amgen, Inc., a biotechnical/pharmaceutical company; resides in Thousand Oaks CA; and has provided the updated information on this twig of the Ferris Tree- thanks Diane.

No children recorded
