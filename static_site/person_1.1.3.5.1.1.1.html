HARRY FERRIS TAYLOR, M.D.,

ID: 1.1.3.5.1.1.1 | Source: John
(1.1.3.5.1.1.1) HARRY FERRIS TAYLOR, M.D., born July 31, 1862 Mt Clemens MI and died 1921; buried Clinton Grove Cemetery MI; read medicine with his father; attended Detroit College of Medicine; Medical Director of the Original Bath House, a position he held for 12y; married December 19, 1886 Kittie Anna Avery born November 4, 1868 Detroit MI and died 1964; and they had 2 kids - see ‘Net on the Original Bath House in Mt Clemens (1.1.3.5.1.2) MARTIN V.B. FERRIS (1834-1868) Note: Also listed in the (3) Joseph line – appears to be conflicting info on this line

No children recorded
