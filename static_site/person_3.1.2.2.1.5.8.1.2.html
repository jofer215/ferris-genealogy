JEROME BENEDICT

ID: 3.1.2.2.1.5.8.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.1.5.8.1.2) JEROME BENEDICT born August 11, 1873 Saratoga NY and died October 6, 1946; married June 27, 1899 Martha Louise King [dau of John Benedict & Betsey Ann (Allen) King] born July 20, 1878 Porters Corners NY and died April 11, 1927; Jerome was an architect, shoe merchant and a realtor. They lived a large part of their adult life in Poughkeepsie NY; both buried Saratoga NY; and born to them were:
