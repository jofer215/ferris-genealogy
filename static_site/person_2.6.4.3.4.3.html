AMY SMITH

ID: 2.6.4.3.4.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.4.3.4.3) AMY SMITH born February 8, 1780 Stamford and died there May 15, 1856; buried St. John’s & St. Andrew’s Cemetery; married June 30, 1802 in Stamford, Stephen Newman [son of Stephen & Sarah (Maltby) Newman] born September 26, 1775 Stamford and died there July 20, 1843; buried with Amy; and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • REBECCA ELIZABETH NEWMAN 2.6.4.3.4.3.3
  • JANE A. NEWMAN 2.6.4.3.4.3.4
  • SARAH LOUISE NEWMAN 2.6.4.3.4.3.5
