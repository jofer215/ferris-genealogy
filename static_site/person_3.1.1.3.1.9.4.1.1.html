MARTIN REYNOLDS SILKMAN

ID: 3.1.1.3.1.9.4.1.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.3.1.9.4.1.1) MARTIN REYNOLDS SILKMAN born February 20, 1904 Cross River NY; married October 14, 1931 at Katonah NY, Gladys Prigge born March 22, 1907 White Plains NY and died February 26, 1986 Largo FL; lived in Katonah for 40 years and in Largo FL for 18 years.
