DAVID FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.2.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.2) DAVID FERRIS born 1766 Stamford CT and died June 22, 1810; buried Old Sound Beach Cemetery CT (tombstone photo) [or Methodist Church Yard, east side of Lake Mahopac, Putnam Co NY]; married Betsy (Elizabeth) Adams born 1776/7 and died July 1, 1848 Carmel NY age 72y; the daughter of a Rev War soldier, John Adams, and his unidentified first wife.
