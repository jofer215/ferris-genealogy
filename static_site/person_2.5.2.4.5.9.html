BENJAMIN HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.4.5.9 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.5.9) BENJAMIN HOYT (#3537) born January 16, 1791, Stamford CT and died January 27, 1851 at San Francisco CA. He married, January 9, 1816 at Stamford, Arna Smith, born October 2, 1796, Stamford; and living in 1871 in Illinois. Benjamin was a farmer. He lived in Morristown NJ, then Cherry Valley IL and finally California. Born to them were:
