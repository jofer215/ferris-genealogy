MARY OLMSTEAD

ID: 4.4.9.1.4.5.1.1 | Source: Mary
(4.4.9.1.4.5.1.1) MARY OLMSTEAD born August 30, 1850 Walton NY; graduated from Walton High School 1875, and Vassar College 1880; after 5y service as a high school teacher in Fond du Lac WI, moved to Newark NJ where she taught high school until 1892, when she entered upon missionary work in India; died suddenly at Amroati, April 12, 1897 of cholera, contracted in aid of people during the famine of that year.

No children recorded
