ISRAEL UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.1.9.11 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.9.11) ISRAEL UNDERHILL (1804-1869) at one time resided NYC; married 1828 Mary Louisa Williams (1810-1873); and born to them were: (1.2.5.1.9.11.1) JOHN W UNDERHILL (1830-1901) resided at one point NYC and Brooklyn; married Sarah C Fleet [dau of Jonathan Gardner & Lydia (Seaman) Fleet] born c1832 NY and died 1886; and born to them were:

Children (5)
