FRED LUTHER STEARNS

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.4.4.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.4.4.4) FRED LUTHER STEARNS (Photo) born December 6, 1883 Ferris, Montcalm, MI and died December 25, 1958 Tampa FL: buried Riverside Cemetery, Alma MI. Fred was fire chief at Alma for 25 years; married July 4, 1902 Clara B. Slater born October 11, 1884 and died May 30, 1946. (Fred’s obituary said he was survived by his wife, Lenore – did he remarry after Clara’s death?) Born to them were:
