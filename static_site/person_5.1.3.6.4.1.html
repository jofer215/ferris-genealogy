CAROLINE FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.6.4.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.6.4.1) CAROLINE FERRIS born May 3, 1812 Greenwich CT and died there February 22, 1848; married October 15, 1834 in Greenwich, Joseph Oscar Keeler born September 4, 1807 [son of Walter and Hannah (Waring) Keeler, of Ridgefield CT]. After Caroline's death, he married 2d in October 1849 Emeline Purdy and 3d Ann Maria Ferris (5.1.3.8.4.6). Born to Caroline and Joseph were:
