AMZI BENEDICT DAVENPORT

ID: 5.2.4.8.3.4 | Source: James
(5.2.4.8.3.4) AMZI BENEDICT DAVENPORT (Photo) born October 30, 1817 New Canaan CT and died 1894; grew up in Davenport Ridge, Stamford; became an ordained ruling elder in the Presbyterian Church 1843; deacon in the Plymouth Church, Brooklyn 1857 and in the New Canaan Church 1887; insurance agent 1862 and real estate agent 1866 in Brooklyn; his interest in family history and genealogy led to his compiling and authoring the Genealogy of Davenport Family, 1851, with an enlarged revised edition 1876; married c1849 Jane I {maiden name unk} (1828-); and born to them were:
