RUTH L. REAZOR

ID: 1.6.1.2.1.9.1.3.1.4 | Source: John
(1.6.1.2.1.9.1.3.1.4) RUTH L. REAZOR born March 11, 1924; married Douglass D. Lagenor (1.6.1.2.1.9.1.3.2) ELMER PENDELL born July 28, 1894; Cornell U., Ph.D., George Washington, LLB.; Oregon, B. S. ; Chicago, M.A.; First Lieutenant in World War I receiving a Purple Heart and the Distinguished Service Cross. Author of Why Civilizations Self-Destruct; retired from Baldwin-College where he had taught; removed to Tuscaloosa AL and was living there 1978; married Lucille Hunt and they divorced 1946. Born to them was: (1.6.1.2.1.9.1.3.2.1) M. JANE PENDELL born July 15, 1931; married James E. Griffin born September 9, 1927; U. of Virginia, B.E.E., 1949; First Lieutenant, US Army, Retired.
