JESSICA ANN NEWMAN

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.9.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.9.3) JESSICA ANN NEWMAN born May 26, 1894 Maple City MI and died there July 31, 1933; buried Greenbrier Cemetery, Benzie Co MI; married December 15, 1913 in Leland, Leelanau, MI James Xevia Sullivan [son of Timothy & Mary (Dunn) Sullivan] born September 4, 1885 Cleveland Township, Leelanau Co MI and died March 28, 1939 Maple City MI; buried there Maple City Cemetery; farm laborer

No children recorded
