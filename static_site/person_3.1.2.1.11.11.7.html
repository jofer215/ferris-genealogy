CHARLOTTE ANNE MORRISON

ID: 3.1.2.1.11.11.7 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.11.11.7) CHARLOTTE ANNE MORRISON born August 21, 1850; 1850 Roxbury census as 1y; married 1st Edmund Wheeler of Hancock NY; and 2d Charles Augustus Dolliver; and born to Charlotte and Edmund was: (3.1.2.1.11.11.7.1) VAN RENSSALAER MORRISON WHEELER was a professional opera singer with DeKovan and Smith Company in New York City and London

No children recorded
