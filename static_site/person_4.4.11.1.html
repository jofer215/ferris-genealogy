MARY JOHNSON

ID: 4.4.11.1 | Source: Mary
(4.4.11.1) MARY JOHNSON (#338) born July 23, 1755 and died April 30, 1833; buried at Old Sound Beach Cemetery (tombstone photo); married 3 times: 1st Joshua Ferris (3.6.1.2) - see him for offspring; 2d December 30, 1777, Thomas Peck (3.10.2.13); see him for offspring; and 3d December 15, 1784, Nathaniel Ferris (5.1.3.8) - see him for offspring. Mary was a handsome, vivacious woman with sparkling dark eyes and was of great energy, quick in her actions, and kept perfect discipline among her children and grandchildren. After her last husband’s death, Mary was considered the wealthiest woman in Greenwich as she had acquired large and prosperous farms from each of her three husbands.

No children recorded
