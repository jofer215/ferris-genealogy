ALBERT FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.4) ALBERT FERRIS born March 4, 1859 near Metamora IN and died November 25, 1921 (1927) Tampico IL; a farmer who moved with his family to Whiteside Co IL, when he was 12; 1884 he owned 160 acres in Hahnaman Township, Whiteside Co; married October 12, 1882 in Whiteside Co, Rebecca Jane Glassburn born November 5, 1864 Tampico and died 1945 [dau of Albert James & Mary Jane (Wood) Glassburn]; both buried on the Ferris family plot, Tampico Cemetery, Tampico IL.
