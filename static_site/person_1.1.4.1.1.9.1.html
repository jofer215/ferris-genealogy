CATHERINE E. HAWES

ID: 1.1.4.1.1.9.1 | Source: John
(1.1.4.1.1.9.1) CATHERINE E. HAWES born September 12, 1850 Bates Co NY and died February 6, 1889 Allegen Co MI; buried there in Pearson Cemetery; married June 29, 1865 in Allen Co, William Daniel Wait born 1840 and died October 23, 1918 Waverly Township, Van Buren Co MI; buried there in Glendale Cemetery, Waverly Township by the Glendale Church (Photos). William remarried after Catherine’s death. Born to Catherine and William were:

Children (3)

  • MARY WAIT 1.1.4.1.1.9.1.1
  • WILLIAM H WAIT 1.1.4.1.1.9.1.2
  • JOHN D. WAIT 1.1.4.1.1.9.1.3
