MARY LYDIA HABBERTON

ID: 3.10.5.10.1.7.2.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.5.10.1.7.2.1) MARY LYDIA HABBERTON born c1845 NY and died 1874-80 MI; married May 15, 1866 in Wabash Co IL, John G Hawley [son of Richard & Evangelia] born c1845 MI; in 1870 they resided in Buffalo, Brooke, WVA where he was a student, probably studying law; in 1880 he was a lawyer and single living with his parents and 2 kids at his parents in Detroit; and born to them were:

Children (2)
