ELIZABETH UNDERHILL WOOD

ID: 1.2.5.3.3.9.3 | Source: John
(1.2.5.3.3.9.3) ELIZABETH UNDERHILL WOOD born April 7, 1842 NYC and died there February 10, 1922; married October 21, 1869 David Shearman Taber [son of William Congton & Hannah Tucker (Shearman) Taber] born July 30, 1840 New Bedford MA and died December 31, 1914 NYC; buried Woodlawn. He was a book publisher. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • AUGUSTUS FREDERICK TABER 1.2.5.3.3.9.3.1
  • DAVID SHEARMAN TABER 1.2.5.3.3.9.3.2
  • ELEANOR WOOD TABER 1.2.5.3.3.9.3.4
