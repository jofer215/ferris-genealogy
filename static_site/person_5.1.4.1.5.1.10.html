JOSEPH LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.1.4.1.5.1.10 | Source: James
(5.1.4.1.5.1.10) JOSEPH LOCKWOOD born July 7, 1836 Stamford and died there February 24, 1912; buried there Woodlawn Cemetery; married c1861, as her 3d husband, Clarissa Keeler [dau of Albert Burr & Sarah Ann (Olmstead) Keeler] (Sarah Ann is of the Richard Olmsted line) born February 2/15, 1837 Ridgefield CT and died January 2, 1902 Stamford; buried Woodlawn Cemetery

No children recorded
