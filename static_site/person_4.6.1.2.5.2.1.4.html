WILLIAM L. BROWN

ID: 4.6.1.2.5.2.1.4 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.5.2.1.4) WILLIAM L. BROWN born October 1, 1844 and died April 5, 1903 Norwalk CT; Civil War Vet; involved in a shoe store with his father-in-law; married January 18, 1865 at Pound Ridge NY, Minerva Fancher [dau of Darius & Anna (Lines) Fancher] born February 20, 1848 New Canaan CT; and they had a son and a daughter

No children recorded
