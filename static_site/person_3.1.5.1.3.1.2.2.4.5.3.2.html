CATHERINE ANN SHULTZ

ID: 3.1.5.1.3.1.2.2.4.5.3.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.5.1.3.1.2.2.4.5.3.2) CATHERINE ANN SHULTZ born January 10, 1950 Grand Island NE; married presumably as his 2d wife June 11, 1988 at Cario NE, Willie Edward Garrett, Jr., [son of William Edward & Catharine Lois (Arvidson) Garrett] born May 7, 1945 Freeport IL. Willie apparently brought a son with him from a former marriage: William Edward Garrett III born February 11, 1973, Great Lakes Naval Base IL

No children recorded
