DEWITT CLINTON REYNOLDS

ID: 3.1.1.3.1.4.10 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.3.1.4.10) DEWITT CLINTON REYNOLDS born June 2, 1828 Cross River, Westchester, NY and died there March 17, 1889; married September 21, 1858 at Bedford NY, Joanna Emily Silkman born March 19, 1841 Cross River and died September 15, 1914 Goldens Bridge; both buried Reynolds Cemetery, Cross River NY; lived in Cross River and borne their children there. The house where their children were born was one of the homes destroyed during the building of the Cross River Dam. Joanna, at age 20, was one of the makers of a Quilt, which now resides at the Museum of American Folk Art in New York City. Born to Dewitt and Joanna were:
