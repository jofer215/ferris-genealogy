BENJAMIN FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.2.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.1) BENJAMIN FERRIS born c1747 Greenwich CT and died c1845 age 100y Athens, Calhoun, MI; served in the Revolution; resided Sherburne and Smyrna NY 1797; moved to Athens MI 1832, where he resided with his grandson, Benjamin. He deeded property to his sons Israel B. and Benjamin P., in Chenango Co NY before he left for MI; married Abigail or Mary, {maiden name unk} {or Meribah Howland?}; and born to them were:

Children (6)

  • JOHN FERRIS 3.1.2.2.1.1
  • RUTH FERRIS 3.1.2.2.1.2
  • ISRAEL BRUNDAGE FERRIS 3.1.2.2.1.3
  • ABIGAIL FERRIS 3.1.2.2.1.4
  • PHOEBE FERRIS 3.1.2.2.1.5
  • BENJAMIN PALMER FERRIS 3.1.2.2.1.6
