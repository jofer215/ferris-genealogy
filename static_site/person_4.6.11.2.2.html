NANCY LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.11.2.2 | Source: Mary
(4.6.11.2.2) NANCY LOCKWOOD (#730) born January 12, 1785 Stamford CT and died March 11, 1867 Ithaca NY; died a widow age 82; married, on her 16th birthday, Allen Brown who was one of the founders of the New York State Bank; Trustee of the 2d Presbyterian Church; Trustee of Albany Insurance Company; President of the Committee for the Celebration of the opening of the Eric Canal; and the intimate friend of Governors Tompkins, Clinton and Yates. He died April 22, 1852 at age 74.

No children recorded
