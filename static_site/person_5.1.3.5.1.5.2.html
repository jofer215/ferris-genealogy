HENRY WHELPLEY NORTON

ID: 5.1.3.5.1.5.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.5.1.5.2) HENRY WHELPLEY NORTON born January 29, 1837 Westchester Co NY and died December 30, 1904 North Salem, Westchester, NY; a farmer and influential citizen; married 1st December 16, 1863 Martha Ann Isaach Gerow born August 21, 1839; 2d September 27, 1892 in NYC, Margaret Stuart Sedgwick [dau of Roderick & Sarah Justina (ritch) Sedgwick] born April 25, 1859 NYC and died February 22, 1940 Iowa City, Johnson, IA where she resided with her daughter; and born to him and Margaret were:
