FRANK HABBERTON

ID: 3.10.5.10.1.7.2.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.5.10.1.7.2.5) FRANK HABBERTON born c1857 Wabash Co IL and died May 22, 1916 Oak Park, Cook, IL; married June 25, 1885 in Vanderburgh Co IN, Margaret Belle Adams [dau of Isaac & Nancy E (Sargent) Adams] born c1861 IN and died July 23, 1930 Evanston IL; and born to them was: (3.10.5.10.1.7.2.5.1) HAZEL HABBERTON born June 8, 1888 Vanderburgh Co IN and died February 9, 1946 Evanston, Cook, IL; married January 26, 1910 in IL, Ralph Whallon Woodbury [son of George William & Harriet Frances (Whallon) Woodbury] born August 26, 1886 Chicago IL and died July 22, 1941 Los Angeles CA; graduate U of Michigan Law School and practice law in Chicago until 1923; after 1926 lived in or near LA; and born to them was:

No children recorded
