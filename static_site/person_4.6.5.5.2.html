MARY HAIT

ID: 4.6.5.5.2 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.5.2) MARY HAIT (#3395) born December 30, 1763 at Bedford (Pound Ridge) NY and died July 21, 1844; married June 30, 1785 Ebenezer Robertson (Robinson) who died January 5, 1859. (4.6.5.5.3) WILLIAM HAIT (#3396) born May 13, 1765 Pound Ridge, Westchester, NY and died September 13, 1847 Wales, Erie, NY; married Charlotte Merritt and they lived in Wales NY; had children.

No children recorded
