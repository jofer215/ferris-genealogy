EDWIN AUGUSTAS FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2.3) EDWIN AUGUSTAS FERRIS born May 20, 1908 Draper SD and died June 28, 1995 Pierre SD (tombstone says died 1994); married January 9, 1931 at Draper, Emma Frieda Maria Mueller [dau of Gustav & Margaret (Stelling) Mueller] born December 8, 1911 Jersey City NJ and died October 30, 1993 Pierre SD. Edwin entered the store business with his father, and started farming in the mid-1930s, north of Draper, and in 1950 bought a farm 3 miles west of Highmore SD. Emma was a teacher at Jones County and Hyde County SD. After retiring in the 1970s, they spent the remainder of their lives in Pierre. Emma published Jones County in Memory, 1989. Both buried at the Draper Cemetery SD (tombstone photo). Born to them were:

Children (3)
