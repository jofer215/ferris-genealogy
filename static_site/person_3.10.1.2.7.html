ABIGAIL PECK

ID: 3.10.1.2.7 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.7) ABIGAIL PECK born September 30, 1760 Greenwich CT and died April 17, 1823 Columbia, Herkimer, NY; married 1787 in Greenwich, Alexander Baird. Resided in Greenwich until 1795 and then removed to Clifton Park NY. Alexander accidentally drowned in the Mohawk River November 12, 1807 and soon afterwards, Abigail removed with her children to Columbia, Herkimer, NY. Born to them were:

Children (6)
