FOSTER EUGENE SMITH

ID: 3.6.3.2.1.3.4.3.1.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.1.3.4.3.1.6) FOSTER EUGENE SMITH born April 26, 1915 Phillipstown, Putnam, NY and died about August 16, 1968 Beacon NY; buried Fishkill Rural Cemetery, Fishkill; married October 3, 1942 in Beacon NY, Marie L. Ireland (1918-). Foster was a WWII veteran, serving in the Army Air Corps. He was a technician with the Texaco Research Laboratory; resided in Brinckerhoff NY for his last 20 years; a member of the Southern Dutchess Rod and Gun Club and a past commander of the Manny Bacon American Legion Post of Hopewell Junction.
