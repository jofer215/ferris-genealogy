EDWARD AUGUSTUS QUINTARD

ID: 2.6.3.5.1.4.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.3.5.1.4.3) EDWARD AUGUSTUS QUINTARD (Photo) born 1826 Stamford and died June 26, 1899 NYC; merchant 1867-69; went to New York as clerk to the Scanton Coal Company and became extensively engaged in the coal business; married 1st c1851 Mary Matilda Gillespie born November 22, 1829 NYC and died September 11, 1864 NYC leaving two children (names unk); married 2d April 12, 1855 (date is an apparent error, probably 1865 instead) in Stamford, Mary Skiddy [dau of William Taylor & Mary Ann (Anderson) Skiddy] born May 29, 1844 Douglas, Isle of Mann, England and died August 31, 1898 Intervale NH; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • WILLIAM E. QUINTARD 2.6.3.5.1.4.3.2
