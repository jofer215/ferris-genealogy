JOHANNA SUTTON

ID: 1.2.5.1.10.7.3 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.10.7.3) JOHANNA SUTTON born April 6, 1855 Pennfield MI and died January 16, 1935; resided 1880 Greenville MI; dressmaker in Battle Creek MI; married 1st 1874 George F Payne born 1846 MI; 2d June 26, 1907, her brother-in-law Dennis McAllister; 3d G M Brown (c1846-); and born to her and George were:

Children (4)

  • MAUDE PAYNE 1.2.5.1.10.7.3.2
  • ADELLA PAYNE 1.2.5.1.10.7.3.4
  • RUTH PAYNE 1.2.5.1.10.7.3.5
