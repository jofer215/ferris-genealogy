JESSE LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.3.5 | Source: Mary
(4.4.3.5) JESSE LOCKWOOD (#313) born November 10, 1750 (July 2, 1745), Salem NY and died January 13, 1823. In July, 1776 Jesse and others ask the representatives of New York for two brass field pieces, for their defence, at Cortlandt’s Manor, in order to assist the militia of this manor and to form a company of artillery on own expense. He served as orderly sergeant in the Continental line in Captain Delavan’s Company, General Wayne’s Division. He married, Mary Hawes who died May 23, 1818 at age 68 years. Born to them were:

Children (2)
