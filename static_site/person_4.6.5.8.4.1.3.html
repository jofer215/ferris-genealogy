ABNER BENEDICT HOYT

ID: 4.6.5.8.4.1.3 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.8.4.1.3) ABNER BENEDICT HOYT born November 13/16, 1842/43 Peru NY and died 1918; buried Weedsport Cemetery - served in Battery I, 3d New York Light Artillery, Civil War enlisted as a Private August 25, 1862 Auburn NY and mustered out July 8, 1865 Syracuse; promoted from the ranks to 2d Lieutenant; married Isabella Forman born November 23, 1870; and born to them were:
