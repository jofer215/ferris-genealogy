REBECCA ANGEVINE

ID: 1.1.1.2.4.10 | Source: John

Angevine [son of Bartholomew G. & Sarah (Peters/Peterson) Angevine] born September 19, 1826 Clinton, Dutchess, NY; and they had 7 kids, 4 of which were:
