PETER KNAPP

ID: 2.6.4.7 | Source: Peter
(2.6.4.7) PETER KNAPP Jr born July 14, 1740 at Stamford and died there February 9, 1811; buried North Street Cemetery; between 1775-82 served as Private; married February 1, 1770 at Stamford, Sarah Reynolds (4.5.2.1.3); and born to them were:

Children (7)

  • LEYDEMA KNAPP 2.6.4.7.6
