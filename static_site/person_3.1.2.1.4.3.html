DAVID SLAWSON

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.3) DAVID SLAWSON born August 12, 1768 Danbury CT and died November 19, 1841 Greenville NY; married 1st Fanny McLain born October 28, 1779 and died July 13, 1810; and 2d Mercy Harding (Elliott) born September 27, 1776 and died January 6, 1819; and 3d Elsie Wiggins

No children recorded
