DORIS RUTH FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.10.5.4.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.10.5.4.1.2) DORIS RUTH FERRIS born August 10, 1921 Albert KS and died November 4, 1997 Houston TX where she had lived for over 40y; graduated from Great Bend High School; married September 15, 1941 Wesley Reed Lund, a petroleum engineer, who died August 18, 1974 Houston. They raised their 3 sons living in The Hague, Netherlands; Calgary, Canada; and in New Orleans before settling in Houston. Doris was an active member of her church serving as a good shepherd for those in need; a charter member of the PEO, Chapter DU, providing continuing education scholarships for women, and a longtime member of Lakeside Country Club. She loved traveling, gardening, couturier sewing, and the theatre. Born to them were:
