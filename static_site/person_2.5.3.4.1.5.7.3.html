JAMES HENRY HOYT

ID: 2.5.3.4.1.5.7.3 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.4.1.5.7.3) JAMES HENRY HOYT (#6010) born May 1, 1839 Marshall Co IL and was living at Martinsburg MO 1871; served 3 years during the Civil War in the Springfield Artillery and was with General Steele in Louisiana and Arkansas; married December 8, 1867 Esther Ready

No children recorded
