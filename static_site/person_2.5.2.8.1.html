SILVANUS HAIT

ID: 2.5.2.8.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.8.1) SILVANUS HAIT (#3039) born January 22, 1740 Stamford CT and died September 8, 1776 probably in Stamford where he lived. A descendant says that he was in the Revolutionary Army, but was taken sick and got within 5 miles of home, when he died; married August 20, 1761 Elizabeth Buxton and born to them were (Hait or Hoyt - will go with Hoyt) [this Elizabeth may be the dau of Samuel & Hannah (Bell) Buxton, born April 27, 1740 (5.2.2.4)]:

Children (4)
