STEPHEN BELL

ID: 5.2.1.1.1 | Source: James
(5.2.1.1.1) STEPHEN BELL (source does not assign Holley/Hulce as surname) born July 28, 1755 Stamford and died there November 28, 1832; between May 8 – September 17, 1775 served as a Private in Sylvanus Brown’s Company; 1776 served as a 2d LT in Joseph Hoyt’s Company; married October 17, 1777 in Stamford, Leah Scofield [dau of David & Eunice (Seely) Scofield] born August 28, 1854 Stamford and died there 1831; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • FREDRICK BELL 5.2.1.1.1.1
  • HANNAH BELL 5.2.1.1.1.2
  • DAVID BELL 5.2.1.1.1.3
  • HANNAH BELL 5.2.1.1.1.4
