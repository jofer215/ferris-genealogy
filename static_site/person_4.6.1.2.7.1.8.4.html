BESSIE FLORELLA KEELER

ID: 4.6.1.2.7.1.8.4 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.7.1.8.4) BESSIE FLORELLA KEELER born 1880 Tama Co IA and died 1968 Loveland CO; attended Cedar Falls Normal; teaching certificate from Iowa, 1897; taught in Greeley CO before marriage; active Sunday school teacher; member of First Methodist Church, PEO, DAR and the local Woman’s Club; she was a “birder”, writer of several articles on birds which appeared in print and spoke frequently to various clubs and at school about conservation of birds; married February 24, 1908 in Loveland, John Hasler Weldon [son of George & Sarah Jane (Samuels) Weldon] born February 6, 1876 Kingston Co, Dublin, Ireland and died April 17, 1960 Loveland; buried there Lakeside Cemetery; took over the family’s 660-acre ranch, keeping cattle and doing general farming; large apple orchard and sold fruit and vegetables; a good singer; taught Sunday School and was school superintendent for 30+ years; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • ROBERT KEELER WELDON 4.6.1.2.7.1.8.4.1
  • WALTER LOCKWOOD WELDON 4.6.1.2.7.1.8.4.2
  • JOHN HASLER WELDON 4.6.1.2.7.1.8.4.3
