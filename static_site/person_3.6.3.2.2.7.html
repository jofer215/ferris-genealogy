JUDITH HILL

ID: 3.6.3.2.2.7 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.2.7) JUDITH HILL born September 3, 1799 Walpack Township, Sussex, NJ and died December 24, 1859; buried Stillwater Cemetery, Sussex Co; married September 18, 1817 William Titman [son of Baltis & Magdalena] born 1799 and died January 4, 1878 Stillwater NJ

No children recorded
