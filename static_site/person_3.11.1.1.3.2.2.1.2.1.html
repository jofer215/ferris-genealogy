LESLIE E JAMIESON

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.1.2.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.1.2.1) LESLIE E JAMIESON born February 13, 1902 Grand Traverse Co MI and died April 1, 1996 Traverse City MI; buried St Joseph Catholic Church Cemetery, Old Mission, Grand Traverse Co MI; teacher for 33y and retired as principal of Sabin Elemetary School in Traverse City; taught at Mapleton School and coached basketball on peninsula; principal of schools in Detour where he coached basketball and football, and Hubbel and Drummond Island before he became principal at Sabin; and successful fruit grower for more than 70y; member of Cherry Growers Cooperative; married 1st Elma Marshall {maiden name unk unless Marshall is it} who died in 1965; married 2d September 26, 1967 in Laredo TX, Martha M Villaneuva; 2 daughters were born to presumably the 1st marriage

No children recorded
