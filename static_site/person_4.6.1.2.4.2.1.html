JOSEPH BARNUM LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.1.2.4.2.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.4.2.1) JOSEPH BARNUM LOCKWOOD (#2284) born October 27, 1822 Jamaica NY; had an office at 52 Wall Street, NYC; married December 3, 1856 NYC, Caroline Monroe Putnam [dau of Tarrant & Cornelia Titus (Van Nortwick) Putnam] born October 27, 1832. Joseph was a merchant and retired from business in 1875. He was a member of the Saint Nicholas Society of NYC; a Trustee of the Bowery Savings Bank; a Director in the NY Juvenile Asylum; a Director in the NY Equitable Insurance Company; and an officer in the South Reformed Church on Fifth Avenue and Twenty-first Street, which was the oldest church on Manhattan Island.
