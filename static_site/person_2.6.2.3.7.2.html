JEREMIAH L KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.3.7.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.7.2) JEREMIAH L KNAPP born August 30, 1791 Stamford and died there July 7, 1860; married 1st August 24, 1820 at Stamford, Hannah Trevor; married 2d a Miss Hulse. Born to Jeremiah and Hannah were:

Children (6)

  • ELIZABETH KNAPP 2.6.2.3.7.2.3
  • LAURA KNAPP never 2.6.2.3.7.2.4
  • ANNA KNAPP never 2.6.2.3.7.2.5
  • JOHN HANFORD KNAPP 2.6.2.3.7.2.6
