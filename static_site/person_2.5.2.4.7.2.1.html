PHEBE SCOFIELD

ID: 2.5.2.4.7.2.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.7.2.1) PHEBE SCOFIELD born September 4, 1795 Stamford and died November 9, 1825 Darien CT; buried there in Bates Cemetery; married January 23, 1825 in Darien, as his 1 st wife, Abram Clock (5.2.3.5.2.2); after the death of Phebe, Abram married his sister-in-law, Julia – see below

No children recorded
