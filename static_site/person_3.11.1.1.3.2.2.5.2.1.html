DOROTHY I HOUGHTON

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.5.2.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.5.2.1) DOROTHY I HOUGHTON (Dottie) born July 21, 1902 Leelanau Co MI and died January 10, 1998 Traverse City MI; buried there Grand Traverse Memorial Gardens; stenographer at Grand Traverse Auto for 36y; volunteer at Munson Medical Center for several years; married 1st November 14, 1925 in Traverse City, Louis B Thacker [son of Raymond & Elizabeth (Blackburn) Thacker] born c1904 Traverse City; shipping clerk; married 2d October 21, 1955 in Stockbridge MI, Truman B Pemberton [son of Truman R & Zella (Stone) Pembertson] born September 10, 1910 Pontiac, Livingston, IL and died February 23, 1996 Pensacola FL; buried Grand Traverse Memorial Gardens; teacher and insurance saleman; Michigan State U graduate and baseball team member; past president of Kiwanis; Elk of the year 1972-73; numerous other activities

No children recorded
