CLARENCE LESTER CLASON

ID: 2.1.3.3.1.4.1.4.9.1 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.1.4.1.4.9.1) CLARENCE LESTER CLASON born May 2, 1899 WI and died October 8, 1984 Sioux City, Woodbury, IA; an ordained minister for the The Disciples of Christ for 33y holding pastorates in Kansas, Missouri, and Illinois until poor health forced him to retire; married 1 st July 12, 1925 Maude Captiola Weitmier born October 31, 1901; 2d September 14, 1947 in Hamilton IL, Myrtle G Morris; and born to he and Maude had 3 kids, 2 of which were:
