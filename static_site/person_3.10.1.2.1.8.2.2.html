SARAH ELIZABETH FISKE

ID: 3.10.1.2.1.8.2.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.1.8.2.2) SARAH ELIZABETH FISKE born April 24, 1826 and was living in Detroit MI 1877; married November 24, 1853 Salon/Solon Prentiss born c1828 OH and died August 8, 1882; he moved to MI some time prior to 1856 when their dau was born; appears in the 1870-80 Detroit census as a hardware merchant; Sarah is listed as a widow in the Detroit Directory 1891; and born to them was:

Children (1)
