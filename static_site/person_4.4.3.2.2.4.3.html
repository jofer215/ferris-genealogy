LEWIS SLAWSON

ID: 4.4.3.2.2.4.3 | Source: Mary
(4.4.3.2.2.4.3) LEWIS SLAWSON born 1830 New Canaan NY and died June 14, 1885 New Canaan (Selleck’s Corners) CT; buried Selleck’s Corner; married 1st c1849 Catherine Stevens born August 17, 1828 and died March 31, 1851 Stamford CT; buried there High Ridge Cemetery; married 2d 1852 in Pound Ridge NY, Emily J. Bloomer [dau of William & Mary (Newman) Bloomer] born March 17, 1832 and died October 14, 1854 New Canaan CT; buried High Ridge Cemetery, Stamford; married 3d 1855 Julia Waters [dau of Elisha & Sarah (Jones) Waters] born August 5, 1833 CT and died December 8, 1915 New Rochelle NY; buried Selleck’s Corners; and born to Lewis and Emily was:
