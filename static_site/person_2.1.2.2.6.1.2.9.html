IDA AMADELLA VAN MARTER

ID: 2.1.2.2.6.1.2.9 | Source: Peter
(2.1.2.2.6.1.2.9) IDA AMADELLA VAN MARTER (Della) born August 9, 1858 and died November 23, 1905 IA; married August 4, 1879 Arthur Henry Davison [son of Wesley & Cynthia Amelia (Thompson) Davison] born March 6, 1857 Crawford Co PA and died December 1, 1928 Des Moines IA; he was a teacher, relator, legislator, administrative assistant to five governors and compiler of History and Genealogy-Davison-Davisson-Davidson Families; and born to them were: (2.1.2.2.6.1.2.9.1) SUSAN MAY/MARY DAVISON
