STEPHEN FERRIS

ID: 1.1.1.8 | Source: John
(1.1.1.8) STEPHEN FERRIS born February 24, 1772 Eastchester NY and died January 30, 1827; buried New Castle, Mt. Kisco NY; married Rachael Fish (1774-1841) and born to them were: (1.1.1.8.1) CORNELIUS FISH FERRIS born 1798 Pine Bridge (Yorktown)? NY and died August 19, 1843/4 Crompound NY; buried White Burial Ground, Yorktown NY; married in November 1833 Elizabeth (Eliza) Whitney [dau of Seth & Elizabeth (Strang) Whitney] born June 5, 1810 Yorktown NY and died 1886.
