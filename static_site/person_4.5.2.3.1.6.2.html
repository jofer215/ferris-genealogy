ESTHER ANN LOUD

ID: 4.5.2.3.1.6.2 | Source: Mary
(4.5.2.3.1.6.2) ESTHER ANN LOUD (#2211) born August 17, 1820 Sugar Loaf, Orange, NY and died there July 20, 1890; married October 7, 1841 in Sugar Loaf, John Murray Hanford [son of Daniel & Sallie (Crans) Hanford] born September 24, 1820 and died March 7, 1904. Born to them were: (4.5.2.3.1.6.2.1) AUGUSTUS EMMIT HANFORD born October 5, 1842 Middletown, Orange, NY and died August 30, 1881; married October 9, 1878 Alice Van Kleeck born January 19, 1851 and died September 6, 1884.
