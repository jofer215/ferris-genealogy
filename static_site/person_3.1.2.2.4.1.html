ROBERT R. FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.2.4.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.4.1) ROBERT R. FERRIS born April 2, 1780 near Manny’s Corner, Smyrna, Chenango, NY and died June 11, 1847 (“accident”) Chenango Co NY; buried Ferris Cemetery, Smyrna Hill NY (about 3 miles west of the village of Smyrna) [cemetery records say he died age 65y, so the above listed date of birth is apparently wrong]; sold his farm to Joseph Ferris; married January 9, 1811/12 at the Dutch Reform Church of Manny’s Corners, Amsterdam NY, Abigail ‘Abbie’ Halsey Lindley {Abigale Halsey Lindsley} [dau of Aaron and Elizabeth] born September 21, 1788 and died September 15, 1862 {June 14, 1867, age 79 years} Lansing, Ingham, MI; buried there Mt. Hope Cemetery. Abigail lived at Lansing, with her son Jessie, after Robert’s death and then was living with her daughter Eliza according to the 1860 census; and born to them were:

Children (6)

  • JESSIE CALVIN FERRIS 3.1.2.2.4.1.1
  • AARON LINDSLEY FERRIS 3.1.2.2.4.1.2
  • JANE FERRIS 3.1.2.2.4.1.4
  • SUSAN ELIDA FERRIS 3.1.2.2.4.1.5
  • SILAS HALSEY FERRIS 3.1.2.2.4.1.6
  • ORESTA FERRIS 3.1.2.2.4.1.8
