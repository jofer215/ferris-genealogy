BETSY NEWMAN

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.1) BETSY NEWMAN born December 10, 1789 Butternuts, Otsego, NY (Freehold, Albany, NY) and died October 24/26, 1868; in 1844 was living in Burlington, Otsego Co NY; married George C. Smith [son of Dr. George Warden & Lucinda (Crippen) Smith] born April 11, 1780 and died November 20, 1829 West Burlington, Otsego, NY; buried West Burlington Village Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (7)

  • ABRAHAM NEWMAN SMITH 3.11.1.1.3.1.1
  • LUCINDA CRIPPEN SMITH 3.11.1.1.3.1.2
  • MARTHA RITTA SMITH 3.11.1.1.3.1.3
  • WELCOME W. SMITH 3.11.1.1.3.1.4
  • WILLIAM POTTER SMITH 3.11.1.1.3.1.5
  • MOSES GAGE SMITH 3.11.1.1.3.1.6
  • CORNELIA BETSEY SMITH 3.11.1.1.3.1.7
