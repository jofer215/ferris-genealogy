SAMUEL MOTT

ID: 1.10.2.6 | Source: John
(1.10.2.6) SAMUEL MOTT born December 16, 1750 and died April 1, 1791; a New York merchant; married April 7, 1794 (1784) Sarah Franklin [dau of Henry & Mary (Seaman) Franklin] [this listing of parents of Sarah Franklin appears to be in error] born (June) July 25, 1756 and died December 11, 1801.
