DEBORAH ANGEVINE

ID: 1.1.1.2.3 | Source: John
(1.1.1.2.3) DEBORAH ANGEVINE born 1780 NY and died between 1831-49, Bazetta, Trumball, OH; married c1800 in Dutchess Co NY, as his 2d wife, Josias Latting (Josiah Lattin) [son of Benjamin & Deborah (Holmes) Latting] born January 29, 1767 (Lattingtown, L. I., NY) Dutchess Co NY and died April 22, 1832 (1823) Old Paltz, Ulster, NY; his 1st wife was Betty Bailey and they had 4 kids; and born to Deb and Josias were: (1.1.1.2.3.1) JONATHAN ANGEVINE LATTIN born before 1800 (c1792) Ulster Co NY (or Dutches Co NY) and died March 16, 1826

Children (6)
