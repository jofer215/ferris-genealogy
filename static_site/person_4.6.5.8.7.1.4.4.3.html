HOWARD FORMAN

ID: 4.6.5.8.7.1.4.4.3 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.8.7.1.4.4.3) HOWARD FORMAN born June 16, 1878 probably Alcove, Coeymans, Albany Co NY and died May 25, 1959 West Coxsackie, New Baltimore, Greene, NY; worked as a train brakeman before his marriage; later became a dairy farmer for the rest of his life; died sitting on his tractor which is where he loved to be; farm was located along Rt 9W halfway between West Coxsackie and Ravena; married September 20, 1906 in Coxsackie, Mary Emma Truesdell [dau of William Casper & Julia Tryphenia (Squire) Truesdell] born October 8, 1881 East Orange, Essex, NJ and died July 31, 1964 Coxsackie, Greene, NY; both buried Chestnut Lawn Cemetery, New Baltimore; Mary was a great paperhanger and did this for many prominent people in the Coxsackie area; they took in boarders who came from NYC for vacations on the farm; and born to them were 3 kids, 2 of which were:
