GRACE ANN ANGEVINE

ID: 1.1.1.2.4.8.3 | Source: John

Children (4)

  • JOSEPH EARL COZINO died August 8, 1887 Cold Spring NY 1.1.1.2.4.8.3.2
  • RALPH COZINO died 1953 Willsboro, Essex, NY; buried East Park 1.1.1.2.4.8.3.3
  • ELLA K. COZINO 1.1.1.2.4.8.3.4
