FRANCES CROFT STRANG

ID: 5.1.2.9.6.1.8.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.6.1.8.1) FRANCES CROFT STRANG born June 2, 1884 and died March 25, 1923; married Elias Smith Chapman (5.1.2.9.6.3.2.4). Frances was a member of the First M.E. Church and for a number of years was very active in Church and Sunday school work. See him for offspring.

No children recorded
