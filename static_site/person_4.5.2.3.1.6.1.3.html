WILLIAM LOUIS LOUD

ID: 4.5.2.3.1.6.1.3 | Source: Mary
(4.5.2.3.1.6.1.3) WILLIAM LOUIS LOUD born April 17, 1848 Warwick or Brooklyn NY and died November 25, 1923. He married first March 4, 1871 Lillie A. Holly born March 4, 1852 Brooklyn NY; second November 24, 1914 Yonkers NY Margaret Busch born March 26, 1882 in Germany; and third, Henrietta Holstein born March 6, 1866. William and Margaret had 2 kids. Born to William and Lillie were:

Children (3)

  • WILLIAM LUDLUM LOUD 4.5.2.3.1.6.1.3.2
