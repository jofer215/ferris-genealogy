DAVID DEAN FERRIS

ID: 1.1.3.2.1.6.9.2 | Source: John
(1.1.3.2.1.6.9.2) DAVID DEAN FERRIS born December 31, 1900 Peekskill NY and died May 15, 1969 Fort Lauderdale FL; married June 6, 1925 in Yonkers NY, Mary Madelyn Wise born January 25, 1900 Yonkers and died June 23, 1961 Albany NY. David was listed in the 1920 census as living with his parents age 19, and employed as a machinist with the railroad. Born to them were: (1.1.3.2.1.6.9.2.1) MARGARET MARY FERRIS born June 10, 1926 Yonkers NY and died 1967 North Tarrytown NY; married September 19, 1953 in Ossining NY, Joseph Moore born December 29, 1929 and died 1975 Tarrytown NY.
