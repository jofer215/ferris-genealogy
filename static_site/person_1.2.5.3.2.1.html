EDWARD BURROUGHS UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.3.2.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.3.2.1) EDWARD BURROUGHS UNDERHILL born February 17, 1809 Yorktown NY and died 1888; unmarried; he made his will in Yorktown NY October 23, 1886, which was proved July 30, 1888, and is recorded in White Plains NY, Liber 107, page 29. He directs his remains to be interred in the family plot in Woodlawn Cemetery, and names the following legatees, rarely stating the relationship: Foreman, Henry C Kear; housekeeper Catherine F Kear; Charles W Underhill Sr, and John Warren Underhill and his infant son; Richard Mott Underhill; William W., Mary Jane and Lydia Underhill; Augusta Patten; Phoebe W Underhill; Mary U Walker; Reuben H., Adelaide and Fanny, children of Robert and Charlotte Underhill; Jacob and Hannah Mekeel; Stephen Wood; David L., son of Jacob Mekeel; former housekeeper, Patty A Miller; Lois A Varney, now teacher in Yorktown; Phoebe and Gertrude Mekeel; Jane C and Lizzie C., children of George and Ellen Dunn; Moses Sutton; Caroline and Mary Underhill, grandchildren of Moses Sutton; Alexander Underhill for his father Alexander; children of Albert Underhill; Euretta E., Samuel and Edward B., children of M. H. Dorsett of Richmond, Tioga County, Pa; William U., son of Richard L Walker of Croton Point; Mary U Walker; Abbie W Underhill; cousin, Mary H Thomas of Union Springs, Cayuga County, ‘the portrait of my grandmother, Phebe Field, and the likeness of my mother, Rebecca F Underhill’; Charles A Underhill; James Wood. Residuary estate divided into certain shares between the following heirs: Robert Underhill, four shares; James Wood, William U Underhill, Mary Jane and Charlotte A Underhill and Mary U Walker, two shares each; Lydia, Abbie E., Phoebe W., Elizabeth, Alfred M., Reuben H Underhill, Augusta Patten, Jacob Mekeel, William C and Edward B Kear, one share each. Executors, James Wood of Bedford, Robert Underhill of Yonkers, Abraham S Underhill of Sing Sing.

No children recorded
