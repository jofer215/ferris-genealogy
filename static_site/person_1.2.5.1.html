ABRAHAM UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1) ABRAHAM UNDERHILL (JR?) (II) born 1723 New Castle, Westchester, NY and died there September 9, 1798; Quaker; married 1st March 20, 1745/46 Phebe Hallock [dau of John & Martha (Quinby) Hallock] born 1716/1720 and died May 29, 1758; married 2d 1759 in White Plains NY, Keziah Farrington [dau of Thomas & Keziah (Baker) Farrington] born 1739 White Plains (New Castle) and died in Philipsburg, Westchester, NY; and born to him and Phebe were (not in birth sequence) (according to one source Abraham had 17 kids with his 2 wives):
