MILTON WILLIAM KNAPP

ID: 2.6.7.6.3.1.7.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.6.3.1.7.3) MILTON WILLIAM KNAPP born September 17, 1882 Clymer, Chautauqua, NY and died March 26, 1930 Aurora IA [died from an infection of a pimple under his nose]; buried there at the Madison Cemetery.
