JONATHAN LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.5 | Source: Mary
(4.4.5) JONATHAN LOCKWOOD (#77) (K7FM-WN) born 1719 Greenwich and died January 24, 1798 at age 79 years; buried at Old Sound Beach Cemetery (tombstone photo); Revolutionary War Veteran; married Mercy {maiden name unknown} [LDS says she was Mercy Finch]. Jonathan bought in 1744 a house and eight acres, in Rye NY, but in 1751 had removed to North Castle NY. Born to them were (not in birth sequence): (4.4.5.1) SILAS LOCKWOOD (#328) (K7FM-D3) born September 3, 1743 Greenwich and died September 10, 1768; buried First Reformed Dutch Church graveyard, Fishkill NY; married name unk, but born to them were:
