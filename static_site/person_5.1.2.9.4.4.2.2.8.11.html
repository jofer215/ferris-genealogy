JOAN TOMPKINS

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.2.2.8.11 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.2.2.8.11) JOAN TOMPKINS born February 19, 1933 Carmel NY and died August 14, 1993 Poughkeepsie NY; married September 30, 1951 at Carmel NY, Orlando D'Angelo Sr.; Obituary, Poughkeepsie Journal, Poughkeepsie, NY, Tuesday, Aug. 17, 1993: Beacon- Joan D'Angelo, 60, a homemaker and seven year Beacon resident, formerly of Carmel, Putnam County, died Saturday at Vassar Brothers Hospital in Poughkeepsie after a long illness. Mrs. D'Angelo was a member of the Philipstown Assembly of God Church. Born Feb. 19, 1933 in Carmel, she was the daughter of Frank and Alice Tompkins. She attended Mahopac schools in Putnam County. On Sept. 30, 1951, in Carmel she married Orlando D'Angelo Sr. who survives at home. Other survivors include her five daughters, Joan Hall, Lisa Killmer and Darlene Zolotas, all of Beacon, Brenda Samuelson of Gloversville, Fulton County, and Judy Benecasa of Cold Spring; two sons, Orlando D'Angelo Jr. of Fishkill and Robert D'Angelo of Clinton Corners, 18 grand children and 4 great-grandchildren. Burial will be in the Ballard-Barrett Cemetery in Mahopac Falls, Putnam County. Born to them were:
