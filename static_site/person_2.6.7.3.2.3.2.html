JOEL EDWIN KNAPP

ID: 2.6.7.3.2.3.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.3.2.3.2) JOEL EDWIN KNAPP born April 15, 1820 Onondaga Hill NY and died May 16, 1895 Camillus, Onondaga, NY; buried there; married August 19, 1841 in DeWitt NY, Mary A. Smith [dau of Ambrose & Mary (Mead) Smith] born September 21, 1823 Perry’s Corner near Amenia, Dutchess, NY and died May 14, 1900 Camillus, Onondaga, NY; buried there. Born to them were:
