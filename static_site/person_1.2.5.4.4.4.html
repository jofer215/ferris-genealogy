CHARITY HAVILAND

ID: 1.2.5.4.4.4 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.4.4) CHARITY HAVILAND (1785-1856) buried Orthodox Friends’ Ground, Clinton Corners, Dutchess Co NY; married 1st 1806 Micajah Hallock [son of Samuel & Deborah] born August 24, 1776 and died September 12, 1828; 2d Joshua Putney; and born to her and Micajah were:

Children (9)

  • DANIEL H HALLOCK died March 12, 1817 1.2.5.4.4.4.5
