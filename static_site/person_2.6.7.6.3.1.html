HARLEY KNAPP

ID: 2.6.7.6.3.1 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.6.3.1) HARLEY KNAPP born September 26, 1799 {December 2, 1798 according to death certificate?} at Orwell, Rutland County VT and died May 22, 1885 at Crystal, Oceana County MI, while visiting a daughter. He ran a boat on the St. Lawrence River until 1840, then operated a mill and later was a farmer. He married, January (June) 7, 1829 in Otsego County NY, Sarah (Stoel) Stowell born December 4, 1809 in Genesee County NY and died December 29, 1895 at Aurora, Buchanan County IA where she was buried at the Madison Cemetery.
