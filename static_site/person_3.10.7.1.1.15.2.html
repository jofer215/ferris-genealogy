DAVID PECK JOHNSON

ID: 3.10.7.1.1.15.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.7.1.1.15.2) DAVID PECK JOHNSON born October 7, 1839 NYC and died August 4, 1902 Minneapolis MN; buried there Lakeside Cemetery; lost one eye in a childhood accident; Civil War Vet; bookkeeper- stock and bond clerk at Comptroller’s Office, NYC; married June 20, 1872 in NYC, Carolyn Augusta Newhall [dau of Harison & Nancy Caroline (Goodrich) Newhall] born April 30, 1844 Chicago and died November 16, 1922 Roanoke AL; buried there; cases of political graft and scandal in NY in the 1870-80s put pressure on David and he finally moved his family to Minneapolis; many of his letters are at the Swedenborgian School Library, Walton MA; and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • WALTER NEWHALL JOHNSON 3.10.7.1.1.15.2.2
