BIRDIE XARIPHA FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.3.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.3.1) BIRDIE XARIPHA FERRIS (BERDIE HARIPHA) born July 5, 1878 IL and died February 2, 1964 Sterling IL; married November 7, 1905 at Sterling, Clarence Maxfield Frye [son of Samuel & Elizabeth Gavitt Maxfield Frye] born April 4, 1877 and died December 1, 1950. Birdie was a teacher in the Rock Falls and Sterling elementary schools from 1901 until the time of her marriage. Later she returned to teaching for a few years, and was her youngest daughter's first grade teacher. Birdie in later years satisfied her love for children and teaching by having several private kindergartens in her home. Clarence graduated from Rush Medical College, Chicago, 1902, and was a practicing physician and surgeon at Rock Falls and Sterling until his death. He was coroner of Whiteside County for 40 years. Both are buried at Morrison IL. Born to them were:
