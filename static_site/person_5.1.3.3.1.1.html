SOLOMON FERRIS, JR was baptized February 9, 1780 at St. John's Episcopal Church,

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.1) SOLOMON FERRIS, JR was baptized February 9, 1780 at St. John's Episcopal Church, Stamford; said he went West; married Deborah Peck (3.10.1.3.5.1) and born to them were: [Chaplain Ferris has a footnote that Solomon's son - this one - according to the Boston Transcript of 9/6/1922 says, married Sarah Maco Ferris and had Nicholas, Abram, Ellen, Jane, Nathan and Elkanah, of course, which does not agree with the below - - so will leave here for now pending further research.] (5.1.3.3.1.1.1) MARY PECK FERRIS (1801-) married 1st Stephen Tredwell Jackson who died January 20, 1833; married 2d Samuel Peck (3.10.1.3.2.1.6)
