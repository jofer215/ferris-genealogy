MARY ELECTA KNAPP

ID: 2.6.7.3.2.3.2.1 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.3.2.3.2.1) MARY ELECTA KNAPP born June 5, 1842 Edwardsburg, Cass, MI and died after 1907 Camden, Onondaga, NY (last known residence); DAR member; married August 16, 1864 in Camillus NY, Francis Philo Hale (Frank) [son of Francis & Harriet (Murray) Hale] born August 27, 1839 Otisco, Otsego, NY and died March 25, 1907 Camden NY; both buried there.
