EMILY FRANCES FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.6.3.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.3.2) EMILY FRANCES FERRIS born February 25, 1857 DeWitt NY and died July 11, 1936 Los Angeles CA at the home of her sister and brother; married May 29, 1878 Clinton G. Peck (son of Dewitt & Salome (Kinne)] who died October 24, 1924 Missoula MT. They moved to Iowa 1883 and 1889 settled in Idaho Falls ID, where he was with the Farmer's State Bank for many years; about 1920 they moved to Missoula. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • ETHEL SALOME PECK 5.1.3.8.6.3.2.1
  • CHARLES CLINTON PECK 5.1.3.8.6.3.2.2
