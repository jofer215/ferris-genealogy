FANNY TRUESDELL CROMWELL

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.4.2.2.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.4.2.2.1) FANNY TRUESDELL CROMWELL born 1880 Ossining NY and died November 3, 1946 Bronxville NY; buried Dale Cemetery, Ossining; was a clerk in the New York Central System and resided with her brother, Stanley, at the time of her death

No children recorded
