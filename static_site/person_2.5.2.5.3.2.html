NANCY SKELDING

ID: 2.5.2.5.3.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.5.3.2) NANCY SKELDING born July 1, 1766 Salem NY and died August 15, 1832 Danbury OH; buried Sackett Cemetery, Danbury; married February 25, 1784 in Stamford, Isaac Ambler [son of Joshua & Bethia (Weed) Ambler] (2.5.2.1.1.3) – see him for offspring

No children recorded
