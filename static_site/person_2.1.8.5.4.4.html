SALLY SMITH

ID: 2.1.8.5.4.4 | Source: Peter
(2.1.8.5.4.4) SALLY SMITH born September 29, 1796/97 Stamford and died there in 1877; married 1822, as his 2d wife, Jonathan Brown Waterbury born April 19, 1792 Stamford and died 1865; both buried Woodland Cemetery, Stamford; and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • MARY A. WATERBURY 2.1.8.5.4.4.5
