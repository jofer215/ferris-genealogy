MABEL IRENE DARBY

ID: 3.10.1.2.1.9.4.5.1.8 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.1.9.4.5.1.8) MABEL IRENE DARBY born March 3, 1903 Clarksville IA; married February 26, 1926 Charles Oakley born Durand IL; “Amy & Lucius Darby's 50th anniversary booklet says Mabel was for lovely, & Irene for peace, for Mabel Irene's name. She also had red curls according to the anniversary booklet. Aunt Lucille says that in the year 2000, Aunt Irene is happy in the home where she lives yet. Uncle Art passed away some time ago.”

No children recorded
