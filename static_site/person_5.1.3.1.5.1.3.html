NATHANIEL T. FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.5.1.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.5.1.3) NATHANIEL T. FERRIS (Nathaniel F Ferris) (Nathaniel Tallman Ferris) born January 2/21, 1823 and died December 16, 1887 Smyrna NY; buried there West Cemetery (Ferris Cemetery); married March 26, 1850 Polly Maria Cleveland born January 12, 1828 and died April 14, 1903; buried with her husband. Nathaniel owned a carding and cloth-dressing establishment in Smyrna, which he sold in 1858. It later became the Smyrna Tannery. [In addition to the below listed kids, there was a M. Lavern Ferris (1888-1921), listed as their daughter, buried with them in the Ferris Cemetery. This individual born the year following Nathaniel's death - may this individual was a grandchild, rather than a child.?] Born to them were:
