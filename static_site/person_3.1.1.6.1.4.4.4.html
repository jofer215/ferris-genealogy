LILLIE AMANDA HETZLER

ID: 3.1.1.6.1.4.4.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.1.4.4.4) LILLIE AMANDA HETZLER born February 8, 1860 Indian Hill OH and died January 2, 1891 Vickery OH; married July 1878 (Frank) Francis Kennedy born October 30, 1853 and died September 14, 1928; a Methodist circuit rider; born to them were 2 children; after Lillie's death, Frank married Ida Leatitia Bush

No children recorded
