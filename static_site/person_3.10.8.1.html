PETER PECK

ID: 3.10.8.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.8.1) PETER PECK born January 1746 Greenwich CT and died June 17, 1813 Queensburg, Warren, NY; buried there. Went with his mother to New Milford CT. Peter served in the Rev. War and moved to Queensburg, near Glen’s Falls NY 1786 and resided there until his death; married December 7, 1768 Sarah Terrill [dau of Paul, of New Milford] who died August 21, 1830 Glen’s Falls age 81y

No children recorded
