STEVEN JON CHRISTESON

ID: 1.1.4.4.1.3.6.4.1.5 | Source: John
(1.1.4.4.1.3.6.4.1.5) STEVEN JON CHRISTESON born September 16, 1963 MI; graduated from high school in Arlington Heights IL; shortly after graduation, he enlisted in the U. S. Army and served with the 82d Airborne Div., at Fort Bragg NC. He has worked at various jobs since his discharge and was last known to be living with his mother at St. Petersburg FL.

No children recorded
