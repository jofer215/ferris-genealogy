JONATHAN SLAUSON

ID: 4.4.3.2.1 | Source: Mary
(4.4.3.2.1) JONATHAN SLAUSON born September 15, 1763 New Canaan, Fairfield County CT; married April 16, 1788 at Stamford, Hannah Slason [dau of Nathaniel & Lydia (Bates) Slason] born February 26, 1768 Stamford, Fairfield County CT and died in 1844. Born to them were: (4.4.3.2.1.1) ALVA SLAWSON [name change?] born 1792/99 New Canaan, Fairfield County CT and died April 17, 1860 Monroe NY; married c1824 in Monroe, Orange, NY, Elizabeth Lewis born 1798 (c1803) Orange or Rockland County NY and died 1890; buried Queensboro, Orange, NY; and born to them were:
