JOHN MANLEY CLOSE

ID: 5.1.2.8.6.3.1.6 | Source: James
(5.1.2.8.6.3.1.6) JOHN MANLEY CLOSE born October 25, 1881 Genoa NY and died August 7, 1953; moved to California c1902 where he attended Stanford U; married June 17, 1907 in San Jose, Santa Clara, CA, Edna Ray Gould born March 12, 1885 CA and died June 14, 1967; both buried Forest Lawn Cemetery, Glendale; resided in Los Angeles Co where he first farmed near Downey and later sold real estate in the Beverly Hills area; Re Construction History Society Newsletter No 65, February 2003, page 8.......
