THEODORE LOUIS FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.6.4.5.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.4.5.2) THEODORE LOUIS FERRIS born October 18, 1915 New York City; graduated Colgate University; assistant to manager of Marine Division of Johns Manville Corp, in NY, prior to assuming charge of marine activities of the same company in Washington DC, during WWII; commissioned in 1946 as member of Admiralty of Flag Ship Fleet for meritorious professional service during the war; member of National Press Club of Washington, DC; Society of Naval Architects and Marine Engineers; member of the firm of T. E. Ferris & Sons, Naval Architects and Marine Engineers, NYC; married May 13, 1944 Harriet Marlowe

No children recorded
