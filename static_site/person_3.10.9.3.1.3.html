JULIA ANN PECK

ID: 3.10.9.3.1.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.9.3.1.3) JULIA ANN PECK born April 10, 1805 Stamford CT and died there October 28, 1881; buried North Street Cemetery; married 1st January 20, 1831 in Stamford, William A. Lockwood (4.6.2.4.3.4.1) – see him for offspring; and 2d 1870 Hiram Raymond (1799-) – Julia was appointed guardian of her children in 1845 and in 1880 was living with Hiram Raymond

No children recorded
