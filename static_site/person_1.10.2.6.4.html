SARAH MOTT

ID: 1.10.2.6.4 | Source: John
(1.10.2.6.4) SARAH MOTT born September 25, 1791 and died April 16, 1875; married October 9/19, 1816 in New York Monthly Meeting, John Wood [son of Samuel & Mary U. (Searing) Wood] born April 20, 1791 Cow Neck, L. I., NY and died July 25, 1850; Quaker; both “disowned 1829 NY Monthly meeting”; and born to them were:
