CHARLES UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.5.1.2 | Source: John
(1.2.3.5.1.2) CHARLES UNDERHILL born August 25, 1794 Elmira NY (West Farms NY) and died 1879 (Breesport, Chemung, NY); migrated to Missouri 1839,, but returned to NY 1845; he was a juror for the trail of the abductors of Morgan, who exposed Masonry; of Elmira NY; married 1st Caroline Hart born November 20, 181* and apparently died c1829/1830; 2d c1829/1830, Portia M. Brooks (1807-); and 3d July 26, 1851, (Sarrah) Sarah Miranda Miller born January 24, 1823/25 NY and died August 26, 1914 [the marriage sequence and thus the mothers of offspring may be out of sequence]; and born to Charles and Caroline were:
