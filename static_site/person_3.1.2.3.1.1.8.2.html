GEORGE H. BLOOMER

ID: 3.1.2.3.1.1.8.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.3.1.1.8.2) GEORGE H. BLOOMER born 1842 (Nov 1841 Vanderburgh IN) and died June 5, 1863 of wounds in the Civil War in Mississippi with Company A., 48th Indiana Infantry Regiment; unmarried. The 48th I.I.R. was organized at Goshen IN December 5, 1861 to January 28, 1862; left the state for Paducah KY February 1, 1862; Regiment was involved in the Siege of Vicksburg May 18 July 4, 1863

No children recorded
