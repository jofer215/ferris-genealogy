KENNETH POOLE LOCKITT

ID: 3.10.5.10.1.1.9.4.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.5.10.1.1.9.4.2) KENNETH POOLE LOCKITT born March 2, 1890 and died March 13, 1945; married April 11, 1917 at Providence RI, Marjorie Whritner [dau of Harry Charles & Lily Eugene (Penhale) Whritner] born August 9, 1894 Black Lake, Quebec – they resided at Beatyestown NJ; and born to them were: (3.10.5.10.1.1.9.4.2.1) ROBERT PENHALE LOCKITT born December 7, 1921 Winsted CT; a mechanical engineer; 1951 was not married and living with his mother
