STEPHEN FERRIS JR.,

ID: 5.1.3.6.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.6.3) STEPHEN FERRIS JR., born May 8, 1783 Greenwich CT and died August 25, 1875 Darien CT [A family Bible has this Stephen, the son of Stephen, dying in September, 1786 - so this may not be the right Stephen Ferris here - will leave for now. The Lockwood Book has a Stephen, Jr, here.] The farmhouse built for him was still standing in 1934 and kept in its original condition by the owner. The farm was part of Tokeneke, a beautiful summer resort; resided DeWitt NY 1850 and also in Darien CT; a very prosperous farmer and director of bank in South Norwalk; married Sarah Elizabeth Dixon born May 20, 1782 Norwalk CT and died December 6, 1876 (1871?) Darien CT. Born to them were:
