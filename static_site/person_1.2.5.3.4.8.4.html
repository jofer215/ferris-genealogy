MARIA BOERUM UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.3.4.8.4 | Source: John
(1.2.5.3.4.8.4) MARIA BOERUM UNDERHILL born April 6, 1846 Whitestone, L.I., NY and died July 3, 1918 Mabbettsville NY; Dutch Reformed Church; 1863 Whitestone, L.I., to Chestnut Ridge; Nine Partners Quaker Academy; Quaker Academy at Moore’s Mills; married November 26, 1867 at Chestnut Ridge, Joseph Hoxie Butler (Peter L & Mary (Haight) Butler] born 1839 Chestnut Ridge and died November 16, 1917 Mabbettsville NY; farmer in Dutchess Co; Quaker then Methodist; and born to them were:

Children (7)

  • JOHN UNDERHILL BUTLER 1.2.5.3.4.8.4.2
  • MINNIE MAUD BUTLER 1.2.5.3.4.8.4.3
