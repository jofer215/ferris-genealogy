CHARLES FOX CONGLETON

ID: 3.1.1.3.1.4.10.6.6.2.2.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.3.1.4.10.6.6.2.2.1) CHARLES FOX CONGLETON (Photograph) born September 26, 1984 Virginia Beach VA; baptized June 2, 1985 Groton School Chapel, Groton MA; a bright, engaging young man; loves baseball and football; attended the second and third grades at the Bement School, Old Deerfield MA; the Applewild School, Fitchburg MA; played soccer in the fall of 1994 and made the winning goals at some of the games. In the winter he was chosen as one of two students to represent his class in the National Geographic Geography Bee.

No children recorded
