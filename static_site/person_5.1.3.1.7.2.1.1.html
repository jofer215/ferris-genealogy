ALBERT FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.1.1) ALBERT FERRIS born December 12, 1833 Newbury, Geauga, OH; married August 22, 1858 Mary Anderson born June 26, 1840 Bellefontaine, Logan, OH and died January 11, 1896; they resided in Michigan, Missouri, and Ohio. The records of the Ferris Center Cemetery, Ferris Township indicate that two of Albert's children are buried there, but with no names or dates. Also indicates that Albert's wife, with no first name, was buried there January 13, 1896. Born to them were:
