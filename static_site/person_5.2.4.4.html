SARAH BELL

ID: 5.2.4.4 | Source: James
(5.2.4.4) SARAH BELL born June 20, 1741 Stamford CT and died April 27, 1811; married 1st c1761 Gideon Waterbury [son of David & Mary (Bouton) Waterbury] born December 26, 1737 Stamford and died there 1780; married 2d July 20, 1784 in Darien CT, Jonathan Bishop born April 11, 1752 Stamford. Gideon served as an Ensign, 1776. Born to her and Gideon were:

Children (7)

  • WILLIAM WATERBURY 5.2.4.4.1
  • GIDEON WATERBURY, 5.2.4.4.2
  • JACOB WARING WATERBURY 5.2.4.4.4
  • SARAH WATERBURY 5.2.4.4.5
  • EZRA WATERBURY 5.2.4.4.6
  • STEPHEN WATERBURY 5.2.4.4.7
  • NANCY BISHOP 5.2.4.4.8
