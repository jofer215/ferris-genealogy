JOHN BELL

ID: 5.2.1.2 | Source: James
(5.2.1.2) JOHN BELL born November 8, 1735 Stamford and died March 1, 1798 Darien CT; Rev War Vet; married December 18, 1768 as her 2d husband, Sarah (Pelton) Canady [dau of Robert & Martha (Beachgood) Pelton] died 1822 Darien; both buried there Noroton River Cemetery; apparently John had another wife, Keziah Waterbury, not sure which was 1st; and born to him and Sarah were:

Children (4)

  • JOHN BELL 5.2.1.2.1
  • KITCHELL BELL 5.2.1.2.2
  • SARAH BELL 5.2.1.2.3
  • RICHARD BELL 5.2.1.2.4
