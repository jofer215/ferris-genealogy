OSCAR FITZALLEN FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.3.6.10.8 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.3.6.10.8) OSCAR FITZALLEN FERRIS born March 10, 1829 Jefferson Co NY and died February 3, 1914 Iowa; married September 23, 1852 Zilpha Kellicutt; listed as a miller in the 1860 Iowa Census; Zilphia is listed as age 20 in the same census and there was a George age 2 with theml that means that Zilpha would have been born c1840, thus age 12 when married

No children recorded
