EL FLEDA FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.1.5.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.1.5.1) EL FLEDA FERRIS (Elfreda) born December 10, 1876 Nashville TN and living after 1943 in San Diego CA; married January 1911 Rev. John Paul Stafford [son of Rev John M & Virginia (Kramer) Stafford] died 1943; Methodist minister in Rock River Conference of the Methodist Church; public relations with Swift Co; and born to them were:

Children (2)
