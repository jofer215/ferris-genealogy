JEDUTHAN FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4) JEDUTHAN FERRIS (Geduthan) born February 22, 1737 Greenwich CT and died there June 23, 1809 age 72y 1m; his will dated August 24, 1807, probated July 14, 1809,. Mentioned his children John, Jeduthan, Ethan, Phebe (wife of Samuel Ferris), Ann Stites (Ann Ferris, wife of Benjamin Stites), Eliphalet, Joseph, Andrew, Mary Peck (Mary Ferris, wife of Solomon Peck), and the children of deceased daughter Deborah; a Private in Captain George Peck's Company, 9th Militia Regiment from August 13 to September 5, 1776; he marched again November 1, 1776 in the same company until January 23, 1777, when was discharged after serving 2 months 23 days. Jeduthan was buried in the Tomac Avenue Cemetery, (Old Sound Beach) Old Greenwich, beside his wife (tombstone photo); married c1760 Phebe Peck (3.10.6.6). Born to them were:
