JOSEPHINE AMELIA FERRIS

ID: 1.4.6.1.1.4 | Source: John
(1.4.6.1.1.4) JOSEPHINE AMELIA FERRIS (Photograph) born 1834 (February 21, 1835); married 1st Spencer H. (or Walter S.) Coon and 2d April 7, 1868, Theodore Coleman [son of Ira & Emily (Dunning) Coleman] born January 30, 1822 Hamptonburgh, Orange, NY and died May 9, 1893 Sheldrake, Seneca, NY.
