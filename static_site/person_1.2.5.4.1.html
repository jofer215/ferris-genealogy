JAMES UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.4.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.1) JAMES UNDERHILL born March 1, 1757 Phillips Manor NY and died December 28, 1817 Yorktown NY; at one time resided NYC; married 1st 1778 Charity Barnes; 2d 1785 Phebe Cock [dau of Reese & Hannah (Robbins Cock] born April 12, 1763 Buckram, L.I., NY and died April 11, 1843 Yorktown; and born to them were:
