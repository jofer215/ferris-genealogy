ALONZO LICKLY AUSTIN

ID: 3.9.1.3.1.3.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.9.1.3.1.3.6) ALONZO LICKLY AUSTIN born April 19, 1839 Oscawana Heights, Putnam, NY and died October 21, 1919 Peekskill NY; buried there; married November 3, 1866 at Adams Corners NY, Margaret Ann Tompkins born December 15, 1849 Tompkins Corners and died December 5, 1927 Peekskill NY. Born to them were: (3.9.1.3.1.3.6.1) ANNA MARGARET AUSTIN born July 29, 1868 Peekskill NY; married April 25, 1888 in New York City, Dr. George Pratt Wygant born September 11, 1862 Catskill NY and died November 28, 1940 Peekskill NY.
