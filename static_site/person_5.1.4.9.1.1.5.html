MARY ANN LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.1.4.9.1.1.5 | Source: James
(5.1.4.9.1.1.5) MARY ANN LOCKWOOD born August 30, 1797 Stamford and died after 1860 Port Chester, Westchester, NY; married c1816 in Darien, Stephen Smith [son of Jacob & Hannah (Thorpe) Smith] born September 24, 1794 Stamford and died after 1860 Port Chester, Westchester, NY; buried New York City Marble Cemetery; shipbuilder; [According to the New York City Marble Cemetery Website there are several Smiths buried in Plot 260: Stephen Smith died August 9, 1875; Stephen Smith died March 13, 1879; John D. Smith December 26, 1907; Mary A. Smith died April 19, 1867 and another Mary A. Smith died February 22, 1884; and an Olive Smith died December 23, 1877; and there are others] and born to them were:

Children (2)

  • JOHN DIMON SMITH 5.1.4.9.1.1.5.1
  • STEPHEN SMITH 5.1.4.9.1.1.5.2
