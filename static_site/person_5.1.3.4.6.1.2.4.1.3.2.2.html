JILL HUGHES MORRISON

ID: 5.1.3.4.6.1.2.4.1.3.2.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.6.1.2.4.1.3.2.2) JILL HUGHES MORRISON born August 16, 1976; 1998-1999 Peace Corps volunteer in Kyrgyzstan teaching English in a Kyrgeze high school; she developed a self-esteem and health and personal protection program for the Balychi women and teenage girls; 2002-2003 taught in a private school in Istanbul; 2005 in Baku Ajerbaijian teaching English in a BP school; completing her Masters work at George Mason UVA
