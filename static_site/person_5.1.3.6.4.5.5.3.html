EDWIN ALDEN FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.6.4.5.5.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.6.4.5.5.3) EDWIN ALDEN FERRIS born March 30, 1902; an electrical engineer; married 1st August 21, 1926 Philippa Donald Coram born October 14, 1901 and died October 1, 1963; 2d February 4, 1964 Elaine Violet (Stevens) Nystedt born March 9, 1932 (Elaine’s second marriage also, she brought 2 children with her to the marriage, Merryn and Craig.)
