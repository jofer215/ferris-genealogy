SARAH BURCH

ID: 3.10.8.3.6.7 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.8.3.6.7) SARAH BURCH (Sarah E. Burch) born September 2, 1823 Fort Ann, Washington, NY and died March 7, 1911 (Howard IA) Leroy MN; buried there; married October 10, 1842 at Fairview, Erie, PA, Samuel J. Miles [son of Samuel & Anna (Townley) Miles] born May 10, 1823 Erie PA and died December 2, 1904 Mower MN. Born to them were: (3.10.8.3.6.7.1) MATHILDA A. MILES born c1843 PA; married January 4, 1863 in Dane WI, Charles A. Smith born before 1849 NY
