HELENA

ID: 1.1.4.4.4 | Source: John
(1.1.4.4.4) HELENA (or ELEANOR) LECOUNT born February 6, 1797 and died January 1, 1865; also called Elena and Hellner; married 1817 George Wallace, a Scotsman, born 1797; a veteran of the Civil War and member of the G.A.R. When he returned from the war, his farm was terribly run down; his wife had died and he was very poor. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • JOHN H WALLACE 1.1.4.4.4.1
  • GEORGE WASHINGTON WALLACE 1.1.4.4.4.2
  • JAMES HENRY WALLACE c1825/1832 and died apparently while 1.1.4.4.4.3
  • HANNAH JANE WALLACE 1.1.4.4.4.4
