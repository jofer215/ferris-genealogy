ABRAM BEADLE HILL

ID: 3.6.3.2.2.2.2.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.2.2.2.3) ABRAM BEADLE HILL born Warren Co NJ and died September 5, 1924 at 85y (tombstone says 82y); married Ellen Chandler who died May 19, 1923 age 79y; and they lived in Fredon NJ and were members of the Newton Methodist Church; both buried Old Stillwater Cemetery, Stillwater NJ (Photo); no offspring

No children recorded
