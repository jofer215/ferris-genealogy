FRANCES DELPHINE FERRIS

ID: 1.6.1.2.1.9.1 | Source: John
(1.6.1.2.1.9.1) FRANCES DELPHINE FERRIS born July 22, 1850 in a log cabin outside of Spencer NY and died October 20, 1921 (Waverly?) Owego, Tioga Co NY; Eastern Star; Ladies Auxilary Sons of Veterans; Rebekah Lodge; membership POA, UCTC; married October 3, 1866 Sobieski Reuben Harris [son of Reuben Thorp & Louisa Lucinda (Dann) Harris] born January 3, 1847 Harrison Valley, Potter, PA and died December 12, 1937 Waverly, Tioga, NY; both buried Glenwood Cemetery, Waverly NY; and born to them were:

Children (7)

  • FRED FAY HARRIS 1.6.1.2.1.9.1.1
  • ARTHUR DANN HARRIS 1.6.1.2.1.9.1.2
  • IDA MAY HARRIS 1.6.1.2.1.9.1.3
  • GUY M. HARRIS 1.6.1.2.1.9.1.4
  • ELMER HARRIS 1.6.1.2.1.9.1.5
  • DEWITT ELLSWORTH HARRIS 1.6.1.2.1.9.1.6
  • JOHN HOWARD HARRIS, M.D., 1.6.1.2.1.9.1.7
