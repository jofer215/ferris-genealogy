MYRNA LAVONNE FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2.3.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.1.2.3.2) MYRNA LAVONNE FERRIS born December 31, 1938 Pierre SD; married September 23, 1961 at Highmore SD, Lawrence Anthony ‘Swede’ Weber born September 20, 1938. She graduated from Northern State College, 1961 and was a schoolteacher in her earlier years. He served in the Army and today, they are engaged in the construction business in Gladstone SD.
