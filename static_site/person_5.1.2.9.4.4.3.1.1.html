ZACCHEUS TOMPKINS

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.3.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.3.1.1) ZACCHEUS TOMPKINS (c1877-) married Emma Curry [dau of George & Melissa (Austin)] born June 21, 1875 and died September 1, 1963 Peekskill NY; both buried Hillside Cemetery, Peekskill NY. Obituary, Star, Peekskill, NY, Tuesday, Sept. 3, 1963: Mrs. Emma Curry Tompkins, of the Gateways, Shrub Oak, died Sunday in Peekskill. She was 88 years old. Born here on June 21, 1875, she was the daughter of George and Melissa Austin Curry. She had lived in this area all of her life. Since the death of her husband, Zaccheus Tompkins, in 1954, she had resided at the Gateways with a nephew, Roland E. Tompkins. Survivors also include a sister, Mrs. Carrie L. Tompkins, of Lake Mohegan; a brother, Cleveland Curry, of Valhalla, and several nieces and nephews. Interment was at Hillside Cemetery.
