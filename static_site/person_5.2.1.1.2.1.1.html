ERASTUS ELLSWORTH SCOFIELD

ID: 5.2.1.1.2.1.1 | Source: James
(5.2.1.1.2.1.1) ERASTUS ELLSWORTH SCOFIELD (Photo) born November 11, 1816 Stamford and died there February 5, 1905; miller 1862; married c1851 in Stamford, as her 2d husband, Jane Ann (Brown) Waterbury [dau of Aaron Thomas & Mary (Remington) Brown] born July 2, 1822 Stamford and died there December 24, 1869; both buried there Woodland Cemetery; he started in business in Stamford 1857 when he first rang a grist mill; five years later he opened a feed store at the corner of Main and South Streets; this place was burned a couple of years later and he occupied a store opposite the Stamford National Bank; later he built the Star Mills on Canal St; did a large wholesale and retail business; held the offices of Selectman, Justice of the Peace, Burgess and member of the Board of Relief; and born to them were:

Children (2)

  • FREDERICK S SCOFIELD 5.2.1.1.2.1.1.2
