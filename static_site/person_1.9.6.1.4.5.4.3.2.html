OSCAR LYNN DEAVOR

ID: 1.9.6.1.4.5.4.3.2 | Source: John
(1.9.6.1.4.5.4.3.2) OSCAR LYNN DEAVOR born February 16, 1902 Hustontown, Fulton, PA – resided in Lewistown 1934; and died May 10, 1977 Lewistown PA; employed in the spinning department of American Viscose for 35 years until his retirement in 1957; owned and operated the Burnham-Reedsville Taxi Co for a number of years and served as turnkey for the Mifflin Co Jail for 7 years; married as her 1st husband, Vera Leona (Chamberlain) Morris [dau of Earl Morris & Bertha Fletcher – adopted by Howard and Jesse Chamberland/Chamberlain] born April 4, 1909 Bedford Co PA and died November 23, 2000 Lewistown PA; employed by the American Viscoe Corp for 35 years before working as a cook at Angelo’s where she retired in 1982; both buried Hustontown Cemetery; Hustontown PA; and they had 4 kids, 1 of which was:
