CAROLINE PECK

ID: 3.10.1.2.1.2.7.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.1.2.7.4) CAROLINE PECK (Carrie) born April 27, 1847 North Norwich NY and died at home October 14, 1925, Earlville, Madison, NY; buried North Norwich Cemetery; prominent church worker and Sunday school teacher; married 1899 Willard Keech [son of Elisha & Hannah (Swan) Keech] born October 12, 1863 and died January 8, 1942 Mercer Co PA

No children recorded
