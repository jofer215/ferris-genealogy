ELIZABETH UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.5.4 | Source: John
(1.2.3.5.4) ELIZABETH UNDERHILL born August 8, 1762 Tarrytown NY and died May 22, 1841 Mt Pleasant NY; married March 1779 (July 19, 1779) in Westchester Co NY, Captain Thaddeus Avery born October 31, 1749 Tarrytown and died November 16, 1836 Mt Pleasant; both buried Old Dutch Burying Ground, Sleepy Hollow, North Tarrytown; He was a farmer. His descendants say that he was a captain of cavalry in the Revolutionary war. He was the paymaster for Westchester Co. His home, still standing, has its fire-place marked with a tablet that states that "Capt. Thaddeus Avery was branded with hot irons in this room, and his wife threatened with death by the Hessians, when they refused to divulge the hiding place of money for the Continental army." and born to them were:
