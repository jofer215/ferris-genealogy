DOROTHEA SPOTTSWOOD DANDRIDGE

ID: 1.7.2.1.1.1.2.3.3 | Source: John
(1.7.2.1.1.1.2.3.3) DOROTHEA SPOTTSWOOD DANDRIDGE (Photos) born January 29, 1896 Poplar Grove (Rosebrake), Shepherdstown WVA (1.7.2.1.1.1.2.4) MARY BEDINGER (Photo) born August 3, 1850; wrote an article of her experiences caring for wounded soldiers from the Battle of Antietam/Sharpsburg (2 miles away from Poplar Grove) under the pseudonym Mary Blunt, for The Century magazine, later published in Vol 2 of Battles and Leaders, A Woman’s Recollections of Antietam

No children recorded
