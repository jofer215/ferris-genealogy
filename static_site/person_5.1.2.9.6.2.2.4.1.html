LOUIS ARMSTRONG BARMORE, JR.,

ID: 5.1.2.9.6.2.2.4.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.6.2.2.4.1) LOUIS ARMSTRONG BARMORE, JR., born May 18, 1907 Jacksonville FL and died July 20, 1997 at his home in Poughkeepsie; married 1st Adeline Rolandelli; married 2d Elsa Doolittle. Louis was an employee of IBM Poughkeepsie from 1942-57 – he and his family owned and operated farms in Shenandoah in East Fishkill and also in LaGrange before be was transferred to IMB Essex Junction VT, where they bought a farm in Richmond. Previously, he had worked on Wall Street. He was a 32d degree Mason; a member of the Eastern Star; and the Richmond Congregational Church. Born to Louis and Adeline were:
