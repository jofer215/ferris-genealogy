CLARA PELL

ID: 1.4.7.3.1.11.3 | Source: John
(1.4.7.3.1.11.3) CLARA PELL born October 13, 1855; married May 7, 1879 in NYC, Captain Thomas Gerry Townsend [son of Edward Davis & Ann Overing (Wainwright) Townsend] born November 9, 1849 Washington, D.C. and died there May 13, 1902; buried Arlington Cemetery; a West Point Graduate; and born to them were:
