WILLIAM O. MEKEEL

ID: 5.2.10.11.1.2 | Source: James
(5.2.10.11.1.2) WILLIAM O. MEKEEL born July 8, 1870 and died January 12, 1910 Riverside CA, his home; married at Wappingers Falls NY, Elizabeth Britner; lived at Wappingers Falls until failing health forced him to seek the sunny climes of the Pacific Ocean thinking to prolong his days, but death it seems was inevitable. His remains were brought to Cold Spring for burial. His widow and daughters lived at Wappingers Falls. Two daughters were born to them; names unknown.

No children recorded
