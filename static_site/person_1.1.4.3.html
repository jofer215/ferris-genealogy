CALEB FERRIS, JR.

ID: 1.1.4.3 | Source: John
(1.1.4.3) CALEB FERRIS, JR. born New Rochelle NY and died Phillipstown NY; purported to have been a scout in the Revolutionary War and was closely associated with General Washington. About 1800, Caleb and John LeCount, settled on about 8,000 (218?) acres (Lot #22) of the confiscated Beverly Robinson lands in Phillipstown, Putnam County [then Dutchess Co NY, also known as Nelsonville.] He married, wife's name unknown and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • JOSHUA FERRIS 1.1.4.3.1
  • CALEB FERRIS 1.1.4.3.2
  • ELIJAH FERRIS 1.1.4.3.3
  • HANNAH FERRIS 1.1.4.3.4
  • JOSEPH FERRIS 1.1.4.3.5
