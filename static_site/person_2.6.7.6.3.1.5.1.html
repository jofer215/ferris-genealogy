ORPHA ANN DUVALL

ID: 2.6.7.6.3.1.5.1 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.6.3.1.5.1) ORPHA ANN DUVALL (Orphiah) born March 13, 1860 Russell, St. Lawrence, NY and died September 21, 1935 Jamesville, Onondaga, NY; buried there at Walnut Grove; married March 15, 1879 at Vermillion, Oswego, NY, George Harvey Leiboult born May 1856 Oswego Co NY (Russell, St. Lawrence, NY) and died December 6, 1940 Onondaga Hill, Oswego, NY. He was a dairy farmer and worked on canal boats when younger. [Sharon states the Leiboult surname is also found as Lyboult, Lybolt, LayBoult, Laybolt, Libolt, and Leibolt.] Born to them were:
