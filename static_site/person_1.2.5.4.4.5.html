HANNAH HAVILAND

ID: 1.2.5.4.4.5 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.4.5) HANNAH HAVILAND born October 10, 1787 and died August 10, 1843; married November 11, 1807 at Franklin, Nassau, NY, Daniel Wanzer [son of Husted & Lucy (Leach) Wanzer] born October 16, 1786 New Fairfield, Fairfield, CT and died May 18, 1859 Poughkeepsie, Dutchess, NY; he moved from New Fairfield when young to Poplar Ridge near Poughkeepsie; Quaker; and born to them were: (1.2.5.4.4.5.1) HUSTED WANZER born c1808 Poughkeepsie, Dutchess, NY
