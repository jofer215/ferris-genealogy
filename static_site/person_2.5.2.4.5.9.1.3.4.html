CLARK VERNE CAMPBELL

ID: 2.5.2.4.5.9.1.3.4 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.5.9.1.3.4) CLARK VERNE CAMPBELL born May 6, 1882 Page Co or Page Center IA and died 1955 Prairie City IL; married March 17, 1910 M. L. Blanche Pierson born December 28, 1882 Westchester, Stark, IL and died October 31, 1978 Crawfordsville IA; buried Prairie City IL. Clark attended a business college in Des Moines IA; he farmed and enjoyed working with fruit trees; manager of Baden Elevator in Prairie City; played the trumpet and trombone. Blanche played the piccolo and gave lessons until she was 85 years of age. Born to them were:

No children recorded
