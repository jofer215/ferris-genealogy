JAMES HINKLEY FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.1.5.6 | Source: John
(1.4.7.1.5.6) JAMES HINKLEY FERRIS born June 5, 1829 - moved to California c1850; and died My 30, 1905 Sacramento CA; buried in the GAR section of the old Sacramento City Cemetery; Civil War Vet served with Company C, 7th California Infantry Regiment; married December 11, 1866 in Stockton CA, as her 2d husband, Mrs. Rebecca (Buckius) Caine [widow of Philip P Caine member of California State Assembly, 1859-60] born April 19, 1828 OH and died December 29, 1914 at the Scripps Home, Altadena CA; buried there Mountain View Cemetery; and orn to them was:
