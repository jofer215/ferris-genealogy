PETER HUSTED

ID: 3.1.4.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.4.2) PETER HUSTED born c1725 {under 21 years of age in 1733} and died September 1783 New Canaan CT; married at New Canaan, October 23, 1746 Ann Seymour [dau of Samuel & Mary (Lyon) Seymour] born 1728 New Canaan and died there September 10, 1784; and born to them were:

Children (7)

  • JONATHAN HUSTED 3.1.4.2.1
  • SAMUEL HUSTED baptized at New Canaan July 15, 1750 3.1.4.2.2
  • HANNAH HUSTED baptized at New Canaan October 22, 1752 3.1.4.2.3
  • ANDREW HUSTED baptized at New Canaan June 1, 1755 and died April 12, 1812 3.1.4.2.4
  • THADDEUS HUSTED baptized at New Canaan March 26, 1758 and died August 30, 1832 3.1.4.2.5
  • NATHAN HUSTED baptized at New Canaan April 11, 1762 and died 1781 3.1.4.2.6
  • ELIZABETH HUSTED 3.1.4.2.7
