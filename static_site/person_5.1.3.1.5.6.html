SALLY FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.5.6 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.5.6) SALLY FERRIS born May 31, 1795 Sherburne, Chenango, NY and died probably there December 27, 1863; married October 21 (25), 1821 at Sherburne, Daniel Benedict [son of Jared and Sarah (Bouton) Benedict] born October 14, 1792 Sherburne and died 1874, probably at Brokenstraw, Chautauqua, NY; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • WILLIAM JORDAN BENEDICT 5.1.3.1.5.6.1
  • SARAH MARIA BENEDICT 5.1.3.1.5.6.2
  • MARY ANN BENEDICT 5.1.3.1.5.6.3
