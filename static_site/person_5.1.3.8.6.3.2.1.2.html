MARY ELIZABETH CLARK

ID: 5.1.3.8.6.3.2.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.3.2.1.2) MARY ELIZABETH CLARK born June 25, 1909 Idaho Falls ID; married at Elko NV, Frederick Henton Laidlaw [son of James & Genevieve (Treadgold)], born March 27, 1910. Frederick graduated from Columbia University, School of Business 1930; 1956 they lived on a ranch near Rupert ID. Born to them were:

Children (2)
