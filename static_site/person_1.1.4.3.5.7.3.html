(1.1.4.3.5.7.3) EMMA (EMILY/AMELIA/ANGELICA) FERRIS (c1844-) Note: This could be Amelia H. Ferris, who married, December 29, 1887 at Tivoli NY, Levi Christian born in January, 1852, Philipstown NY and died April 30, 1928, Poughkeepsie NY. This Amelia died September 22, 1916 and buried at Cold Spring Cemetery NY - she was the dau of Rev. M. L. & Elizabeth Ferris. No info that her father, Leonard, above as a Rev., or was M. L., as opposed to just Leonard - speculation! Info provided by Jean C. Carlson. Please see note at beginning. (1.1.4.3.5.7.4) MEADEY (HANNAH) FERRIS (c1846-)
