ID: 1.2.3.1.2.2 | Source: John
(1.2.3.1.2.2) JOSHUA UNDERHILL, of Scarsdale NY – born February 22, 1776 and died December 1, 1852 Scarsdale, Westchester, NY; married 1st August 26, 1810 New Hachensack, Dutchess, NY Peggy Angevine (Margaret) [dau of Eli & Charity (Bonnet/Bonet) Angevine] born May 10, 1772 Westchester Co NY and died September 12, 1816; 2d 1821 Mary Underhill (1.2.3.1.6.14); 3d after 1825 Susan Angevine [dau of James & Susan (Ferris) Angevine]; and born to Joshua and Peggy were: (1.2.3.1.2.2.1) JONATHAN UNDERHILL born about July 1811 and died September 5, 1811
