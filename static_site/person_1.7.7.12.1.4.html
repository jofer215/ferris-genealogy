SUSAN MATILDA BURLING

ID: 1.7.7.12.1.4 | Source: John
(1.7.7.12.1.4) SUSAN MATILDA BURLING born February 2, 1820 New Rochelle, Westchester County NY and died June 15, 1901 Bedford Hills NY; buried in Amawalk Meeting House Cemetery; married December 4, 1843 Benjamin Weeks (1.2.2.2.2.2.2); see him for offspring.

No children recorded
