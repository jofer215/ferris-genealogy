COURTLAND CUSHING CLEMENTS

ID: 5.1.3.2.4.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.4.1.1) COURTLAND CUSHING CLEMENTS born March 26, 1843 Blooming Grove, Franklin, IN and died October 18, 1933 Washington DC; married August 26, 1868 Ella Grose [daughter of General Crose of New Castle IN] (Almira Grose born 1845 New Lisbon IN dau of William & Rebecca (Needham) Grose); a Courtland Clements, Esq, special agent conducted an investigation authorized by an item in the sundry civil appropriation cat of August 7, 1882; his resport upon Eastern and Western Cherokee differences was submitted to Congress February 8, 1883 – presume same guy; and born to them were:
