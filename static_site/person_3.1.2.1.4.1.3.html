CATHERINE SLAWSON

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.3) CATHERINE SLAWSON born March 30, 1791 Orange Co NY and died September 3, 1870 Jackson Co MI; married September 24, 1807 Elijah Godfrey [David & Elizabeth (Harris) Godfrey] born May 7, 1786 Orange Co NY and died November 9, 1862 Jackson Co MI; both buried Campbell Cemetery, north of Parma, Jackson, MI (Photos); built a flower mill Batavia NY and c1850 moved his family to Michigan; and born to them were:
