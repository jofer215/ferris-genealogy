RICHARD WOOD ROSE

ID: 3.1.6.8.3.7.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.3.7.6) RICHARD WOOD ROSE born March 15, 1849 Liberty Township, Licking Co OH and died February 18, 1931 Sapulpa, Creek, OK; buried there South Heights Cemetery (Photos); married November 30, 1873 at Alexandria MO, Mary Elizabeth Sheets born April 16, 1848 Concord, Miami, OH and died age 92y, January 1941; buried with her husband; the sisters-in-law were sisters! Richard made a living hauling wood, cutting and hauling ice during the winter, and farmed. The lived in Alexandria, moved to Iowa for a short time and then moved back; 1901 they left and moved to Sapulpa OK with their son and family; Richard joined the Union Army 1864 and was in Company F., Wisconsin Cavalry at age 15 - his father went with him to Wisconsin and declared that he was old enough to fight; he was in the western campaign and did see some action fighting Indians besides the rebels; active in the G.A.R.; a member of the I.O.O.F and Masonic Lodge.
