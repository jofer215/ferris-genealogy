EVA ESTELLE WINSLOW

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.6.1.1.1.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.6.1.1.1.1) EVA ESTELLE WINSLOW born October 16, 1875 Benezette, Elk, PA and died March 29, 1967 Pontiac MI; buried Parklawn Memorial Cemetery, Ridgeway PA; a dressmaker; married June 29, 1904 Harvey Edward Smith [son of Samuel & Martha (Krebs) Smith] born June 26, 1871 Cameron City PA and died April 13, 1926 Jamestown KY; a mercantile owner of Sinnemahoning – marriage was short-lived and they separated before the birth of their son Bruce – divorced 1911; and born to them were:

Children (2)

  • MARY SMITH 3.1.2.1.4.6.1.1.1.1.1
  • BRUCE WINSLOW SMITH 3.1.2.1.4.6.1.1.1.1.2
