JOHN BARTON TINKER

ID: 1.2.3.4.6.1.1.7 | Source: John
(1.2.3.4.6.1.1.7) JOHN BARTON TINKER (John Bathrick Tinker) born March 28, 1830 Bainbridge NY and died February 9, 1887 Plymouth NY; married 1st January 20, 1854 in Guilford NY, Adeline Starr [dau of Rev Charles & Caroline (Park) Starr] born May 12, 1835 Lee Center NY and died April 23, 1857 in childbirth, Definance OH; buried Plymouth NY – related to his mother?; married 2d 1858 in Norwich CT, Frances Jane Mowry [dau of Simon & Marcy (Waterman) Mowry] born April 3, 1834 Plymouth NY and died there February 18, 1889; and born to him and Adeline was a child; born to him and Frances were:
