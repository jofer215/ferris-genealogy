PHEBE UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.2.3.6 | Source: John
(1.2.2.3.6) PHEBE UNDERHILL [not listed is some sources] born November 6, 1764 Somers Center, Westchester, NY (or June 11, 1764 Philipsburgh Manor, Westchester Co) and died April 24, 1817 (May 26, 1817) Amawalk NY; married November 17, 1790 in Somers NY, Isaac Cock [son of Joseph & Rhoda (Frost) Cock] born May 4, 1764 Clinton, Oneida, NY and died July 26, 1834 Amawalk, Westchester, NY.
