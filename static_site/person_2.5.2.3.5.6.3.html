HANNAH HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.3.5.6.3 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.3.5.6.3) HANNAH HOYT (#4534) (Photo) born June 10, 1805 and died 1871; for a number of years Principal of the North Stamford Academy, during which she prepared a number of young men for college and business life; 1871 she was proprietor and principal of a large and prosperous female school in New Brunswick NJ, and ranked among the successful educators of the country.

No children recorded
