SYLVANUS F.

ID: 5.2.10.11.2.7 | Source: James
(5.2.10.11.2.7) SYLVANUS F. (FERRIS?) MEKEEL born November 16, 1838 Philipstown NY and died at his home in Nelsonville, January 30, 1915; married 1864 Martha Warren. Sylvanus was a farmer until the year 1891, when he moved to Nelsonville, where he lived until his death. Born to them were:
