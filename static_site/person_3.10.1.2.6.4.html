SOLOMON C. PECK

ID: 3.10.1.2.6.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.6.4) SOLOMON C. PECK born September 12, 1793 Galway, Saratoga, NY and in 1877 was living in Clifton Park, Saratoga, NY; died there July 31, 1885; married 1st Lydia Schanber/Schauber who died September 7, 1820 at age 23y; 2d after September 7, 1820, Maria Margaret Terpenning [dau of Petrus & Margaret (Reneaux) Terpenning] born January 1, 1799 Clifton Park and died there June 18, 1884; all buried Clifton Park Baptist Church Cemetery, Clifton Park; and born to Solomon and presumably Lydia, was:
