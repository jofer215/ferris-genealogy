JOHN HAIT

ID: 4.6.5.3 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.3) JOHN HAIT (#2899) born March 25, 1733 Stamford and died November 12, 1798 Pound Ridge NY where he was a farmer. When the Pound Ridge Presbyterian Church was incorporated in 1788, John was a trustee. John married, April 19, 1764 at New Canaan, Bethiah Jones who died March 13, 1811 at age 70 years.
