ELDORAS E. FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.1.3.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.1.3.2) ELDORAS E. FERRIS born September (7) 12, 1907 and died 1959; married October 30, 1926 Clayton Phipps born March 4, 1895 and died May 18, 1998 age of 103y. They moved to Reedville 1937 and to Hillsboro OR 1940 where they remained. Born to them were: (5.1.3.4.10.4.1.3.2.1) MERVIN EUGENE PHIPPS born June 5, 1929 Ellsworth MN; married December 30, 1956 at Seattle WA, Marian Lind born February 23, 1928; he was employed by a farm equipment dealer at Portland OR; Marian was a nurse; resided Beaverton OR. Born to them were:
