JULIA ANN FERRIS

ID: 2.1.4.1.3.6 | Source: Peter
(2.1.4.1.3.6) JULIA ANN FERRIS born January 22, 1821 and died November 22, 1860; married December 23, 1844, as his 2d wife, Samuel Seiler (previously listed as Saylor) [son of Peter & Margaretha (Schumacher) Seiler] born July 17, 1799 and died April 13, 1859; buried Hollenback Cemetery, Willkes-Barre, Luzerne, PA (Photo). His first wife was Hannah Gardner and they had 6 kids. Born to Julia and Samuel were:

Children (3)

  • HATTIE EMARETTA SEILER 2.1.4.1.3.6.1
  • ANNIS ELIZABETH SEILER 2.1.4.1.3.6.2
  • BENJAMIN ERNEST SEILER 2.1.4.1.3.6.3
