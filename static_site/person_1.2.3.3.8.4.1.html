ANDREW J UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.3.8.4.1 | Source: John
(1.2.3.3.8.4.1) ANDREW J UNDERHILL born September 27, 1832 Brooklyn, Kings, NY; fisherman 1858-63 Pentwater, Oceana, MI; town supervisor 1860 and 1873; town clerk 1877; President of Pentwater, Oceana, MI 1878-79; owned store, 2 lumber and shingle mills 1890; postmaster; Orderly Sergeant in Company A, 26th Michigan Infantry, 1863; 2d Lieutenant 1863; 1900 resided Chicago IL; married August 1864 Elizabeth Pringle and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • CHARLES M UNDERHILL 1.2.3.3.8.4.1.1
  • GRACE UNDERHILL 1.2.3.3.8.4.1.2
  • JESSIE UNDERHILL 1.2.3.3.8.4.1.4
