VALENTINE SEAMAN FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.5.4 | Source: John
(1.4.7.5.4) VALENTINE SEAMAN FERRIS born December 7, 1809 Swanton and died there December 21, 1879; buried Swanton VT; went by the name of ‘Judge’. He was selectman of Swanton, 1847-49; Town Agent to 1877; Justice of the Peace, 183548, 53-53, 61-65. In 1833, he erected a marble-finishing mill on the same site as the old Underhill and Ferris Mill, which was erected in 1820, but discontinued after a few years. He married, first, June 14, 1837, Cornelia Brayton (1818-1855) [dau of Judge Brayton] and second, May 4, 1859, Emma (Everest) Gates (1826-1879), widow of Horatio Gates, M.D. Born to Valentine and Cornelia were:
