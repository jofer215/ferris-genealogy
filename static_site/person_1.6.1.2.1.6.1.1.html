CARLETON GILLEPSIE FERRIS

ID: 1.6.1.2.1.6.1.1 | Source: John
(1.6.1.2.1.6.1.1) CARLETON GILLEPSIE FERRIS (Photograph) born September 18, 1876 Dixon IL and died November 1961 Purdue MI. B. S. Purdue U., 1898; LL.D. U. of Michigan 1901; admitted to Michigan Bar 1901 and practiced at Detroit as a member of the firm, Hatch & Ferris until 1909. He was engaged in banking and farm loan financing in Michigan and Illinois, 1909-23; secretary to his father, 1923-24; manager transportation department and in-charge national legislation, Illinois Chamber of Commerce, 1924-27; general secretary to same, 1927; later executive Vice President of same; resided in Chicago; married 1st Maud Detering (Osyerding?); 2d Marguerite Bourg (1929-)

No children recorded
