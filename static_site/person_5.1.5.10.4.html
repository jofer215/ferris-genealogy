CHARLES HENDRIE

ID: 5.1.5.10.4 | Source: James
(5.1.5.10.4) CHARLES HENDRIE (Captain) (Deacon) born August 26, 1790 and died October 8, 1873; became Treasurer of he First Society of Greenwich 1824; member of committee to see if land could be obtained so a new meeting house could be built 1828; 1837 elected Deacon (First Congregational Church, Old Greenwich); married 1st November 16, 1818 Hannah Maria Lockwood (4.2.5.1.4.1) and 2d October 12, 1850 Sarah Ann Lounsbury born December 10, 1818 and died September 25, 1875; and born to Charles and Hannah were:
