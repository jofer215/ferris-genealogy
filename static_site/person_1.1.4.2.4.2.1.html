ISAAC FRANCIS DAVENPORT

ID: 1.1.4.2.4.2.1 | Source: John
(1.1.4.2.4.2.1) ISAAC FRANCIS DAVENPORT born at Davenport’s Corners 1869 and killed on the Burlington and Missouri R.R., October 19, 1887 from the collision of two freight trains on one of which he was brakeman; buried with his father at the North Highland Cemetery

No children recorded
