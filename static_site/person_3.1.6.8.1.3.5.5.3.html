HUGH JOHN MCKIM ROSS

ID: 3.1.6.8.1.3.5.5.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.1.3.5.5.3) HUGH JOHN MCKIM ROSS born May 1, 1910 Rossburn, Manitoba and died March 28, 1968 Edmonton, Alberta; enlisted in the RCNVR September 1943, training first at HMCS Tecumseh and later at HMSC Cornwallis; promoted to subLieutenant in August 1944; served on board the minesweeper HMCS Fort William, which had been used to clear mines in the Channel for the DDay Invasion; he was demobilized March 1946 in Vancouver; teacher/administrator; married October 3, 1936 at Edmonton, Margaret Janet Phillips (Peggy) [dau of John Harrison & Margaret (Osborn) Phillips] born February 7, 1913 Red Deer, Alberta and died April 26, 1971 Edmonton; she was a teacher; and they had 2 kids

No children recorded
