APOLLIS FERRIS

ID: 2.1.3.3.1.1.6.1 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.1.1.6.1) APOLLIS FERRIS born June 14, 1828 NJ and died 1910; a stone mason by trade and built a lot of bridges in the Mehoopany area; married 1 st May 31, 1850 Miriah Robinson who died June 18, 1887 and born to this union were at least 6 kids; married 2d June 18, 1892 Elmina Sheffler – he was 64y and she was 23y and born to this union was 4 kids, 1 of which was: (3.1.3.3.1.1.6.1.1) FRANK APOLLIS FERRIS (the eldest) born February 20, 1892 Lovelton PA and died October 13, 1984; married 1st Agnes Sands – divorced; married 2d September 31, 1935 Edna Caroline Sickler born May 31, 1918 and died November 11, 2000; both buried Northflat Cemetery, Laceyville, Wyoming, PA; and born to Frank and Agnes was:

No children recorded
