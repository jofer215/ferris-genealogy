CHARLES KNAPP FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.7.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.7.4) CHARLES KNAPP FERRIS born September 15, 1819 and died September 2, 1883; buried Ferris Family Cemetery, Mariemont, Hamilton, OH; never married {A Charles K. Ferris' will was filed October 13, 1883, Hamilton County OH - the executor and beneficiary was Andrew J. Ferris.}

No children recorded
