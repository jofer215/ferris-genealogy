LORANIA F. TRAVIS

ID: 5.1.2.9.10.11 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.10.11) LORANIA F. TRAVIS born August 31, 1837 Putnam Valley NY and died July 10, 1925; married John B. Christian [son of Rufus G. & Sarah (Travis)] born February 26, 1822 Putnam Valley NY and died February 12, 1918 Peekskill NY; both buried Hillside Cemetery, Peekskill NY; no children
