FERRIS CHAPMAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.6.3.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.6.3.1) FERRIS CHAPMAN born October 23, 1838 and died December 18, 1922 at his home in Philadelphia PA; married Joanna {maiden name unk} (Joanna Courtney). He moved away from Peekskill over 40 years before his death. Born to them were: (5.1.2.9.6.3.1.1) EMMA A.
