JOHN QUINTARD WILSON, Judge,

ID: 2.6.3.4.2.1 | Source: Peter
(2.6.3.4.2.1) JOHN QUINTARD WILSON, Judge, born February 3, 1781 Stamford (NYC) and died May 11, 1863 Albany NY; Judge in Fairfield CT for 25y; afterwards moving to Albany and was an Albany County Judge for several years; married in 1806 at Albany NY, Maria Lush [dau of Richard & Lyntje (Fonda) Lush] born February 19, 1785 Albany, Albany, NY and died March 29, 1862 Albany NY; and born to them were:
