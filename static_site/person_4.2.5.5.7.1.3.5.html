WILLIAM LANE

ID: 4.2.5.5.7.1.3.5 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.5.7.1.3.5) WILLIAM LANE born June 18, 1837 Niles Township, Berrian Co, MI and died May 14, 1916 Whiteside Co IL; buried Leon Methodist Cemetery south of Prophetstown IL; spent his boyhood and youth on the homestead farm in Berrien Co MI and his education was acquired in the common schools of Niles MI; removed with his father and family to Henry Co IL, age 19y; Civil War Vet – Company K, 57th Illinois Infantry Regiment – listed as Recruit from Yorktown IL who enlisted Mar 12, 1862, 26y, 5’4”, sandy hair and blue eyes and was mustered out May 21, 1865; owned 439 acres of land in Prophetstown Township, Whiteside Co IL; cast his first vote for Lincoln; served as school director for a number of years; married 1st May 26, 1852 (14y) in Niles Township, Berrien Co MI, Jane E Slusher born in Shandaken NY and presumably died before 1868; married 2d October 18, 1868 in Prophetstown, Elizabeth Francis Wroe [dau of Benjamin & Matilda (Kennedy) Wroe] born March 11, 1852 Morgantown VA and died June 20, 1927 Leon, Whiteside, IL; buried with William; and born to him and Jane was:
