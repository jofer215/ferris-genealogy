JOSHUA AMBLER

ID: 2.5.2.1.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.1.1) JOSHUA AMBLER born September 9, 1723 Stamford CT and died 1790-93 Pound Ridge NY; between June 1, 1780 and January 1, 1782 served in Reuben Scofield’s company and in Isaac Lockwood’s Sea Coast Guards; married 1st April 6, 1749 in Stamford, Bethiah/Bethia Weed [dau of Benjamin & Mary (Pennoyer) Weed] (c1726-c1773); married 2d May 24, 1774 in Stamford, Millescent Hoyt born October 14, 1722 Stamford; and married 3d December 9, 1788 in Stamford, Martha Blachley born December 13, 1717 Stamford; and born to him and Bethia were:

Children (7)

  • ISAAC AMBLER 2.5.2.1.1.3
