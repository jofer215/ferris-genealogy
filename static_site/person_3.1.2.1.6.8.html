JOSEPH FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.1.6.8 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.6.8) JOSEPH FERRIS (twin) born May 8, 1798 Harpersfield NY and died March 8, 1881 Franklin Co OH; married May 16, 1819/20 at Franklin Co OH, Sarah Smith [dau of Joseph, who with his wife and seven children moved from Ovid NY, 1816, to Franklin Co] who died March 3, 1882 Perry Township, Franklin Co OH; Joseph came to Ohio 1818 and worked first, as a carpenter, in Chillicothe a few weeks; then in Circleville for six months before settling in Perry Township, on a part of Sarah's father's farm, about a mile north of Dublin. Joseph always worked at his trade and never farmed but little; helped build the corduroy road {laying logs and brush across the track and covering with dirt} from Dublin to Worthington OH; paid 18 cents per rod; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • LEROY FERRIS was apparently 3.1.2.1.6.8.2
  • CATHERINE FERRIS 3.1.2.1.6.8.4
