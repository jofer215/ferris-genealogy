SHERWOOD BISSEL FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.2.1.3.9 | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.1.3.9) SHERWOOD BISSEL FERRIS born December 15, 1844 Greenwich; he was a salesman of wholesale drygoods in the firm Bartlett, Beery, Reed & Co; married June 23, 1868 Augusta Moore Dwight [dau of Theodore & Ellen (Boyd) Dwight] born November 18, 1840; resided Lakewood NJ; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • ELEANOR AUGUSTA FERRIS 5.1.3.2.1.3.9.1
  • MARY DWIGHT FERRIS resided Bronxville NY 5.1.3.2.1.3.9.2
  • ISABEL STUART FERRIS resided Bronxville NY 5.1.3.2.1.3.9.3
  • ANNA EDWARDS FERRIS 5.1.3.2.1.3.9.4
