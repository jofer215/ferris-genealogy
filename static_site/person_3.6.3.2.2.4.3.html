JOHN H. HILL

ID: 3.6.3.2.2.4.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.2.4.3) JOHN H. HILL born July 21, 1821 NJ and died July 30, 1876 NJ; buried Harmony Methodist Church, Stillwater NJ; probably a farmer; married January 10, 1843 Rhoda Van Horn [dau of Abraham & Esther (Bell) Van Horn] born May 5, 1821 and died September 22, 1899, probably in Stillwater; both buried at the Harmony Methodist Church Cemetery (Old Stillwater Cemetery), Stillwater NJ. Born to them were:

Children (8)
