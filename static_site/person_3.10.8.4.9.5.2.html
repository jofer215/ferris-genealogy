FRED D WATERMAN

ID: 3.10.8.4.9.5.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.8.4.9.5.2) FRED D WATERMAN born August 16, 1860 Van Buren NY and died December 26, 1906 Chicago IL; farmer in Onondaga Co 1895; federal employee with Bureau of Animal Industry 1896-1906 Chicago; married November 12, 1885 in Van Buren, Florence Lydia Barnes born August 13, 1864 Van Buren and died there March 12, 1937 – she must have gone home after Fred died

No children recorded
