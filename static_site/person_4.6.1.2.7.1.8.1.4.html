MARTHA ALMIRA KEELER

ID: 4.6.1.2.7.1.8.1.4 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.7.1.8.1.4) MARTHA ALMIRA KEELER born December 11, 1909 Chokio, Stevens, MN and died March 15, 1995 Ashtabula, Ashtabula, OH; buried Greenlawn Cemetery, North Kingsville OH; educated in one-room schoolhouse, riding to it on a horse; high school in Chokio, boarding in town; B.A. Hamline U, 1931; did not go every year because of depression; majored in economics and minor in chemistry; M.Ed, Western Reserve, 1953; during WWII worked at Aeroquip Corp, Jackson MI; began teaching in 1948 to help with college expenses for children; began at Jefferson, then in 1949 Ashtabula city schools; became guidance counselor at Ashtabula High School 1964; in 1972 bought an orchard east of town, apples, peaches and vegetables and also began a baking business with sales at farm markets; DAR member #350438; retired from teaching 1980; married August 24, 1931 in Bowling Green, Wood, OH, Robert Alexander Horrocks [son of Thomas Ainscow & Alma Gladys (Philips) Horrocks] born October 10, 1902 Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada and died May 1, 1998 Ashtabula Co OH; buried with Martha; moved to Minneapolis 1905; graduated Minneapolis South High School; B.A. Hamline U, 1927; President of the Midwest Conference of the Association of Secretaries of YMCA; President of the Kiwanis Club of Ashtabula and Lieutenant Governor of Div 22, 1959; see Who’s Who in the Midwest; all this updated information has been provided by their son, Lloyd – thanks Lloyd!!

No children recorded
