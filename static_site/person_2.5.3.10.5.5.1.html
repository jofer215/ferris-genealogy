WILLIAM B WEED

ID: 2.5.3.10.5.5.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.10.5.5.1) WILLIAM B WEED born 1830 Stamford and died there 1916; married 1st November 16, 1856 in Stamford, Josephine R Smith born September 27, 1835 NYC and died November 18, 1884 Stamford; married 2d May 21, 1889 in Stamford, Hester H Scofield born October 1, 1858 Stamford (whose sister Lucinda married (5.1.3.1.3.4.3.3)); William and Josephine buried North Street Cemetery, Stamford

No children recorded
