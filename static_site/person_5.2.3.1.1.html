REBECCA JEFFREY

ID: 5.2.3.1.1 | Source: James
(5.2.3.1.1) REBECCA JEFFREY born June 27, 1757 Stamford; married November 12, 1797 in Stamford, as his 2d wife, John Hoyt (2.5.3.4.1) – see him for 1st marriage offspring – no offspring from this marriage. Rebecca inherited a ready-made family with 11 kids and numerous grandkids.

No children recorded
