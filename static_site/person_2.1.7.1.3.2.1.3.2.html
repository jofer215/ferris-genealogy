GRACE HOBART DAYTON

ID: 2.1.7.1.3.2.1.3.2 | Source: Peter
(2.1.7.1.3.2.1.3.2) GRACE HOBART DAYTON born January 20, 1879 Stamford; married November 16, 1904 in Boston MA, Samuel Bradlee Doggett born May 29, 1858 Boston MA; offspring – a Samuel Bradlee Doggett authored A History of the Doggert – Daggert Family, Boston, 1894 – also A History of the Bradlee Family, Boston, 1878 - presume this is the same guy

No children recorded
