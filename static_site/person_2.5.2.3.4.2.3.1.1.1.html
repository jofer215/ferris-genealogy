WILLIAM RAYMOND KEELER

ID: 2.5.2.3.4.2.3.1.1.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.3.4.2.3.1.1.1) WILLIAM RAYMOND KEELER born October 31, 1866 Ridgefield CT and died May 25, 1941 Fairfield Co CT; 1900-10 he had a livery stable in Ridgefield; married November 1898 in Fairfield Co, as her 2d husband, Florence Olmstead Walker born 1873 Ridgefield and died 1960 Fairfield Co (2.5.2.3.4.2.3.1.1.2) MORTIMER COLE KEELER born August 10, 1868 Ridgefield CT and died there August 24, 1940; buried there Ridgefield Cemetery; farmer; married 1st 1896 in Ridgefield, Grace Russell born January 1868 Ridgefield and died there January 20, 1931; buried there Ridgefield Cemetery; 2d Orenna Betts [dau of Samuel & Harriet F (Jennings) Betts] born September 3, 1877 Ridgefield; and born to him and Grace were:

No children recorded
