SAMUEL WARING KEELER

ID: 4.6.1.2.7.1.5 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.7.1.5) SAMUEL WARING KEELER (S. J.) born October 11, 1835 Monroe Co NY and died May 11, 1884 Traer, Tama, IA; buried there Buckingham Cemetery; married November 13, 1862 Minerva Serena Shafer born c1841 and died January 22, 1925 Seattle WA; “the cutest little lady, not much over 5’ tall; there were paintings all over the house that she had done in the years gone by”; and born to them were:
