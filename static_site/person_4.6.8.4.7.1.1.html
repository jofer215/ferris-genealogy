WILLIAM THOMAS LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.8.4.7.1.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.8.4.7.1.1) WILLIAM THOMAS LOCKWOOD (#2344) born October 19, 1843 Cold Spring Harbor, Long Island, NY where he also resided in 1888 and died 1925; married 1st October 17, 1866 at Oyster Bay NY, Rosine L. Valentine [dau of Benjamin & Phebe T. (Van De Water) Valentine] born August 6, 1846 Flushing, NY and died May 29, 1874; married 2d November 29, 1876 at Cold Spring, Lucy L. Buckingham [dau of William & Mary (Seaman) Buckingham] born August 1, 1858 Cold Spring and died March 14, 1909. Born to William and Rosine were:
