JONATHAN FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.2) JONATHAN FERRIS born April 25, 1778 Dutchess Co NY and was struck by lightning and died June 17, 1823; a Baptist minister in Chenango and Yates Counties NY; married July 21, 1798 Rhoda Purdy [dau of James and Phebe Purdy] born August 15, 1781 and died September 5, 1820; and born to them were:
