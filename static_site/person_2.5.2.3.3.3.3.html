JOHN HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.3.3.3.3 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.3.3.3.3) JOHN HOYT (#4510) born November 27, 1795 Stamford and was living in Fishkill, Dutchess, NY 1871; engaged in wool carding, cloth coloring and dressing and afterwards in farming and mechanical affairs, receiving two patents; married March 9, 1820 Susanna Scofield (c1799-); removed to Phillipstown, Putnam, NY and thence to Fishkill; and born to them were:

Children (6)

  • HENRY HOYT 2.5.2.3.3.3.3.1
  • PHEBE HOYT 2.5.2.3.3.3.3.2
  • CHARLES HOYT 2.5.2.3.3.3.3.3
  • JOHN NEWTON HOYT 2.5.2.3.3.3.3.4
  • SETH S. HOYT 2.5.2.3.3.3.3.5
  • SARAH A. HOYT 2.5.2.3.3.3.3.6
