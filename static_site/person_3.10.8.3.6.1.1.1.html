GEORGE FRANKLIN JONES

ID: 3.10.8.3.6.1.1.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.8.3.6.1.1.1) GEORGE FRANKLIN JONES born August 30, 1856 Erie PA and died April 1, 1926 Willoughby OH; buried W. Park Cemetery, Cleveland OH; married December 18, 1880 at Erie, Emma Florence Messler [dau of Jacob & Amelia] born August 16, 1858, St. Catherine, Ontario, Canada and died December 10, 1921, Cleveland OH. George worked the Erie Life Saving Station and he saved the life of his granddaughter, Marion, when she was 4 years old. Born to them were:
