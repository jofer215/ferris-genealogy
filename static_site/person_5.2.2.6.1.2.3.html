CLEMENZA ROSALIA BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.6.1.2.3 | Source: James
(5.2.2.6.1.2.3) CLEMENZA ROSALIA BUXTON born August 13, 1834 Lowville, Lewis, NY and died October 3, 1886 Evansville, Rock, WI; a teacher; married November 2, 1867 Richard Rush Winston [son of Aaron & Polly (Atwater) Winston] born January 26, 1834 Page Brook Farm, Chenango Co NY and died March 31, 1903 Janesville WI; both buried Maple Hill Cemetery (Photo), Evansville; a farmer. Born to them were:

Children (2)
