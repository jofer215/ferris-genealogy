REBECCA HULL

ID: 1.2.5.4.3.6.5 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.3.6.5) REBECCA HULL born May 5, 1818 Stanfordville NY and died February 26, 1861 Brooklyn NY; married October 8, 1846 in NYC, as his 2d wife, Robert Barclay Haviland [son of William & Anna (Griffin) Haviland] born November 21, 1803 Harrison’s Purchase NY and died September 1, 1885 Brooklyn; of the Haviland China dynasty; and born to them were:

Children (5)
