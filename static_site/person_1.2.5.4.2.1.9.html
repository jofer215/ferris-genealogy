SON CHADEAYNE

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.9 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.9) SON CHADEAYNE born and died December 25, 1807 (1.2.5.4.2.1.10) LEONARD CHADEAYNE (Photos) born June 12, 1809 at “Just a Mere” farm, Yorktown NY and died there February 11, 1893; buried Fair Ridge Cemetery, Chappaqua NY; a farmer at the “Just a Mere” farm; for many years one of the prominent and influential citizens of Westchester Co NY. He was reared on his father’s farm in Yorktown and was engaged in agricultural pursuits all of his life. He married January 12, 1847 Mary Ann Thorne [dau of Thomas P. & Elizabeth (Gerow) Thorne] born February 14, 1823 Orange Co NY and died March 26, 1911 Yorktown NY; buried Fair Ridge Cemetery. Mary was raised in the Newburg NY area; in 1900 she was residing next door to her son, David. She was a member of the Orthodox Society of Friends. Born to them were:

No children recorded
