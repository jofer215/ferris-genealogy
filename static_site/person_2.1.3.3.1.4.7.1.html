GEORGE WASHINGTON HART

ID: 2.1.3.3.1.4.7.1 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.1.4.7.1) GEORGE WASHINGTON HART born August 31, 1835 Hartsville, Steuben, NY and died 1905 Noble Township, Wabash IN; married Ella Martha Wheeler [dau of Isaac & Martha (Norcross) Wheeler] born September 23, 1846 Frankfort ME. He moved to Ordway SD 1881 and ran a blacksmith shop there; and born to them were:
