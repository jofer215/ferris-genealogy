HENRY COLLARD

ID: 4.5.2.3.1.5.1 | Source: Mary
(4.5.2.3.1.5.1) HENRY COLLARD (#2204) born April 27, 1809 NY and died August 24, 1890; married December 23, 1841 (Sarah Maria Mapes) Sarah (Susan) Mapes [dau of Gabriel & Kesiah (Oakley) Mapes] born April 27, 1820 NY and died March 7, 1903. Henry was a railroad laborer and they lived in Mount Hope, Orange, NY according to the 1870 census. Born to them were:
