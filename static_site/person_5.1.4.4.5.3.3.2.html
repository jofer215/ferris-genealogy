EMMA JESSUP

ID: 5.1.4.4.5.3.3.2 | Source: James
(5.1.4.4.5.3.3.2) EMMA JESSUP born 1853 Mianus CT; married November 1, 1883 in Stamford, Joseph H. Knapp [son William Henry & Adelia Ann (Bell) Knapp] born 1851 (June 9, 1850) Stamford and died there December 29, 1928; mason/builder 1885-1900; councilman from the Third Ward in the first Common Council of Stamford 1894; member of the Board of Relief , member of the Republican Town committee and assistant postmaster; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • MABEL IRENE KNAPP 5.1.4.4.5.3.3.2.1
  • PARIS J KNAPP 5.1.4.4.5.3.3.2.2
  • RALPH E KNAPP 5.1.4.4.5.3.3.2.3
