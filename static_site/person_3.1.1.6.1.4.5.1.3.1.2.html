DOROTHY ANNE STEVENS

ID: 3.1.1.6.1.4.5.1.3.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.1.4.5.1.3.1.2) DOROTHY ANNE STEVENS (Photo) born May 29, 1940 St. Louis MO; married as his 2d wife November 23, 1974 James Ronald Hermans [son of Norbert & Margaret] born June 29, 1942 Green Bay WI; Dorothy is a graduate of the University of Missouri at Kansas City with a B. A., History; she worked for Harry Reese, a private investigator and lawyer, even working on stakeouts; she worked for the Missouri Inspection Bureau and is currently working for Industrial Risk Insurers (formerly Factory Insurance Association) as a Special Agent & Underwriter; she earned the C.P.C.U. designation 1985

No children recorded
