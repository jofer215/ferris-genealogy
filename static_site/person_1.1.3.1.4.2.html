MARY CATHERINE BLAKENEY

ID: 1.1.3.1.4.2 | Source: John
(1.1.3.1.4.2) MARY CATHERINE BLAKENEY born November 3, 1822 Peekskill-onthe-Hudson NY and died November 21, 1901 Nyack NY; married c1844 in NY, Martin G. Knapp, of Haverstraw, born February 9, 1816 Rockland Co NY and died 1894 Nyack NY. She was the devoted, self-sacrificing mother. Martin was a shoemaker and the Town Clerk in 1860, of Nyack NY. He was a man of extraordinary brilliancy as a scholar and writer. His contributions to the press in both prose and verse, over the nom de plume, "Samson Broadaxe" have been accorded a high place in contemporaneous literature. Born to them were:
