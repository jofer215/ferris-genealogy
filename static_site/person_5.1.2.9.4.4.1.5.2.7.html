HART TOMPKINS

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.1.5.2.7 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.1.5.2.7) HART TOMPKINS born August 8, 1920 Philipstown NY and died October 24, 1966 Beacon NY; buried Maple Ave Cemetery, Patterson NY; married Rose A. Perry [dau of Joseph & Anna (Cardella)]. Obituary, Oct. 1966: Hart Tompkins, 46, of Rte 311, Patterson, N.Y., died suddenly at Castle Point Veterans Hospital, Beacon, N.Y. on Oct. 24, 1966. He suffered a cerebral hemorrhage. A native of Continental Village, N.Y., he was the son of the late Hart and Jessie O'Dell Tompkins. He was a farmer and a member of the Patterson Volunteer Fire Department also an Army veteran of World War II. He leaves his widow, Rose Perry Tompkins; two daughters, Mrs. Marcella Bartrow and Miss JoAnne Tompkins, and a son, William H. of Patterson; four brothers, George of Dover Plains; Robert of Lake Carmel; Ernest of Mount Kisco and James of Brewster, four sisters, Mrs. Pearl Fletcher of Kent, NY; Mrs. Dorothy Zaniboni of Hartsdale, Miss Lucy Tompkins of Danbury and Miss Dorothy Tompkins of Brewster. Burial will be in the Maple Ave. Cemetery. Born to them were:
