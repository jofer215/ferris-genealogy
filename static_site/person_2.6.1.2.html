SAMUEL KNAPP

ID: 2.6.1.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.1.2) SAMUEL KNAPP born December 2, 1722 Stamford or Fairfield CT and died there August 21, 1751. One source says he never married. Chaplain Ferris shows that Samuel married Mercy Bouton with no issue. (2.6.1.3) REUBEN KNAPP born April 3, 1724 Stamford and died there 1764; married 1 st June 2, 1749 Mary Bouton who died March 14, 1750 (after childbirth); married 2d October 20, 1751 (Fred Knapp says c1759) Anne Johnson. Born to Reuben and Mary was:

No children recorded
