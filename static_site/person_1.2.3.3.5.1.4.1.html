WILLIAM H UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.3.5.1.4.1 | Source: John
(1.2.3.3.5.1.4.1) WILLIAM H UNDERHILL (Rev) born July 1, 1842 Niagara NY and died 1904 Vanderbilt MI; orphan, raised by his Uncle Whitehouse in Vienna MI; 186265 served with 23d Michigan Infantry, fought in Atlanta; Tretford, Genesee, MI farmer and mason; 1881 Clio MI; 1886 Pine Run MI Christian Church (Disciples of Christ) minister; 1900 Vanderbilt and Berryville MI minister; married May 10, 1866 Ellen L Tinker born May 1846 Ashtabula OH; and born to them were:
