HARVEY HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.3.4.1.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.3.4.1.2) HARVEY HOYT (#4523) born April 26, 1792 and was living and farming in Stamford 1871; married October 11, 1814 Laura Clark who died November 19, 1848 age 61y; and born to them were: (2.5.2.3.4.1.2.1) CLARISSA HOYT (#5926) born July 25, 1815 South Salem and died July 11, 1858 Cato NY; married January 7, 1835 Samuel Fancher

  • WILLIAM ROCKWELL HOYT 2.5.2.3.4.1.2.3
  • THOMAS MEAD HOYT 2.5.2.3.4.1.2.4
  • ELLEN HOYT 2.5.2.3.4.1.2.5
