EDWIN MOSES FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.5.5 | Source: John
(1.4.7.5.5) EDWIN MOSES FERRIS born January 18, 1812 Swanton and died there May 2, 1856/59; buried there; Justice of the Peace of Swanton 1845-46; married May 14, 1844 Catherine Everest (1815-1902), a sister of Emma Everest Gates, and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • ALEXANDER JOHN FERRIS 1.4.7.5.5.1
  • URSULA CATLIN FERRIS 1.4.7.5.5.2
  • VALENTINE EDWIN FERRIS 1.4.7.5.5.3
