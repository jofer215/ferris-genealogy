NELSON L. BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.2.9.8 | Source: James
(5.2.2.2.9.8) NELSON L. BUXTON born November 27, 1814 Stamford CT and died there May 12, 1900; a shoemaker and grocer; married October 22, 1839 in Stamford, Sally Ann Scofield [dau of Alvah & Polly (Wilmot) Scofield] born February 21, 1820/23 Stamford and died there September 13, 1892; buried Lockwood #5 Cemetery, Stamford. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • ALBERT L. BUXTON 5.2.2.2.9.8.2
  • HENRY W. BUXTON 5.2.2.2.9.8.3
  • EMMA FRANCES BUXTON 5.2.2.2.9.8.4
