GEORGE WASHINGTON LANE

ID: 4.2.5.5.7.1.3.6 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.5.7.1.3.6) GEORGE WASHINGTON LANE born August 9, 1839 Niles Township, Berrien Co MI and died March 6, 1906 Pomona, Riverside, CA; moved to Henry Co IL 1854 with his father and family; Civil War Vet – Company K, 57th Illinois Infantry Regiment – enlisted as a Private December 26, 186, re-enlisted as a Veteran January 17, 1864 and was mustered out July 7, 1865; wounded at Shiloh and carried the lead in his body all his life; moved to California 1893 making his home in or near Pomona; married 1st July 11, 1858 in Whiteside Co IL, Sarah Winchell [dau of Joseph] born August 31, 1840 Ulster Co NY and died April 2, 1863 probably Henry Co IL, while George was away during the War; married 2d May 28, 1866 in Henry Co IL, Nancy Lee Mooney [dau of Rev I.] born 1847 OH and died 1918. Card of Thanks: “To all the dear friends and neighbors whose loving care and thoughtfulness ministered in any way to him who loved him all so well in his life and whose sympathy is a solace to those benefit; to the members of the GAR who so nobly responded to their comrade’s last call, and whose simple, beautiful rites were most fitting and appropriate for the loyal heart now still; to the local press correspondents who have accorded much space in their respective papers, and to the dear minister whose beautiful words of hope and comfort fell like dew from heaven on our sorrowful hearts – these lines are lovingly dedicated, with a fervent prayer that God’s choicest blessings rest upon them one and all” - Mrs. G W Lane and children.
