DAVID WILLIAM WATERBURY

ID: 4.2.5.2.5.1.5.1 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.2.5.1.5.1) DAVID WILLIAM WATERBURY born October 12, 1882 Stamford and died there November 4, 1959; buried there Woodland Cemetery; came into his father’s business 1904 and upon his father’s death, became proprietor; David Waterbury & Sons, Inc was one of Stamford’s oldest concerns; married 1903 Ida M Zahn [dau of Frederick] born August 18, 1882 NY and died August 16, 1961 Stamford; and born to them were:

Children (2)
