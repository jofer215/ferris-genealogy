FRANCIS FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.2.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.2.3) FRANCIS FERRIS (Francis B) born September 17, 1836 Newbury, Geauga, OH and died there July 18, 1901; age 66y 10m 6d; Civil War Vet served with Co B, 41st Ohio Volunteer Infantry; married 1st December 25, 1856 Lucinda Warren born March 4, 1840 Orange, Guyhoga, OH and died August 4, 1871 at age 30y Newbury OH; married 2d July 31, 1873 Newbury OH Hattie Downing [dau of John and Jane (Hope)] born October 30, 1845 Roughmont, Canada and died March 30, 1923 Newbury OH; both buried Newbury Cemetery Cemetery. Hattie always lived on the farm, making a good home for many who were homeless, no one ever went from her door hungry or cold; known as “Aunt Hat”; Born to Francis and Lucinda were: (5.1.3.1.7.2.2.3.1) WILLIAM FERRIS born December 27, 1859 Newbury, Geauga, NY and died April 18, 1881
