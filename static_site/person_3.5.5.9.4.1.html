ELIZA LYON

ID: 3.5.5.9.4.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.5.5.9.4.1) ELIZA LYON born October 17, 1798 Genoa, Cayuga, NY and died March 24, 1847; married April 5, 1814 Wines Palmer born May 18, 1795 and died July 26, 1859 (?) They lived on the farm with Deacon Caleb Lyon. An obit notice in the NY Observer speaks of Eliza’s “nobility of character and the fidelity with which she discharged her obligations in every relation of life.” Born to them were:

Children (5)

  • AARON LYON PALMER 3.5.5.9.4.1.2
