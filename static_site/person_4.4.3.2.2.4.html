NATHAN DANN SLAUSON

ID: 4.4.3.2.2.4 | Source: Mary
(4.4.3.2.2.4) NATHAN DANN SLAUSON born February 20, 1803 Clove, Orange, NY and died January 3, 1873 Stamford CT; married c1824 Mary Lewis born June 1, 1802 Orange Co NY and died there November 16, 1878; both buried at High Ridge Cemetery, Stamford; and born to them were (source changes name again): (4.4.3.2.2.4.1) DANIEL RANDALL SLAWSON born 1826 NY and died 1902 Stamford CT; buried High Ridge Cemetery, Stamford; shoemaker 1880; married c1851 Matilda Provost [dau of Samuel & Electa (Miller) Provost] born November 27, 1830 NY and died 1906 Stamford; buried with Dan; and born to them were:
