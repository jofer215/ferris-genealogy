JOHN LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.3 | Source: Mary
(4.6.3) JOHN LOCKWOOD (#64) born September 18, 1703 Stamford and died there 1776; married 1st April 8, 1737 Sarah Scofield [dau of Daniel] born 1715 Stamford and died there before 1776; 2d Abigail {maiden name unknown}. Born to John and Sarah, were:

Children (6)

  • EZRA LOCKWOOD 4.6.3.5
  • ABIGAIL LOCKWOOD 4.6.3.6
