DAVID C. CHADEAYNE

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.4.3 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.4.3) DAVID C. CHADEAYNE born September 28, 1828 at the farm, Tarrytown NY and died January 11, 1883; buried Fair Ridge Cemetery, Chappaqua NY; an assessor; married January 7, 1857 at New Castle NY Phoebe Elizaebeth Lounsberry (Photo) [dau of Robert & Anna (Vail) Lounsberry] born February 8, 1839 Somers NY and died January 21, 1905 Maplehurst farm, Ossining NY; buried at Fair View Cemetery, Chappaqua NY.
