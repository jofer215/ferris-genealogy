JOHN FISHER FERRIS

ID: 1.6.1.3.5 | Source: John
(1.6.1.3.5) JOHN FISHER FERRIS born at North Castle December 11, 1811 and died there May 11, 1852; married 1841 Elizabeth Smith [dau of Henry] born North Castle 1815 and died San Francisco CA 1888. John was born and lived all his life on his father’s farm of 200 acres, which he and his brother William inherited from their father. He was a farmer and justice of the peace in North Castle 1844-52. After his early death, Elizabeth remarried to Samuel Drake and moved to San Francisco. John is buried at the Ferris Cemetery (Lake Marie Cemetery, Bedford NY) (plot 15) with his baby son, John (plot 14). The rest of the family scattered across the country.
