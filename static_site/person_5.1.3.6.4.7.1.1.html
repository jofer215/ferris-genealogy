CHARLES ROBERTSON FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.6.4.7.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.6.4.7.1.1) CHARLES ROBERTSON FERRIS born 1878 and buried November 10, 1942 Manlius Village Cemetery, Manlius NY; age 64y 9m 24d; married Bertha Buck [dau of Lafayette] who died June 26, 1951 Utica NY; no offspring [there is a Mrs. Charles Ferris who was buried July 4, 1931 in the same Cemetery as Charles - would think this is the same Mrs. Ferris, which would make the above death date wrong.] Charles is buried in the same Lot and Section as his parents.

No children recorded
