MARY ESTHER WEED

ID: 2.5.2.7.1.7.1.1.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.7.1.7.1.1.1) MARY ESTHER WEED born July 5, 1843 Stamford and died there December 11, 1917; buried there St. John’s & St. Andrew’s Cemetery; married October 20, 1881 in Stamford, as his 2d wife, William Harvey Smith (2.1.8.5.4.3.3) – apparently no offspring from his marriage

No children recorded
