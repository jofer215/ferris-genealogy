ANNA LYDIA HABBERTON

ID: 3.10.5.10.1.7.2.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.5.10.1.7.2.4) ANNA LYDIA HABBERTON born February 12, 1855 Vincennes, Knox, IN and died March 14, 1930 Burlington, Chittenden, VT, probably with her daughter who was a college teacher in VT; buried Evansville IN; married May 7, 1874 in Mt Carmel IL, North Storms [son of John & Lucy T (Hick) Storms] born c1842 Carmi, White, IL and died August 30, 1893 Evansville IN; [had a brother named Hail – apparently his folks had a sense of humor] and born to them were (must have dropped the ‘s’):

Children (5)
