MARY FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.8.1.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.8.1.4) MARY FERRIS born August 7, 1896 and died November 22, 1989 Connersville IN; married July 1, 1932 Arthur Jones [son of Joseph & Jane] born March 23, 1882 and died September 17, 1971; Arthur was a farmer of Laurel Township and also worked on the New York Central Railroad. Mary was associated with the Moster & Cox Funeral Home at Laurel for many years and lived in the old Ferris family home at Laurel, which is today (1998) a bed and breakfast. Both buried at Metamora Cemetery, Franklin County IN. Born to them was:

Children (1)
