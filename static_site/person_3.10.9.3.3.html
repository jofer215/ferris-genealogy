ELIZABETH PECK

ID: 3.10.9.3.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.9.3.3) ELIZABETH PECK born July 7, 1776 Greenwich CT and died there November 27, 1865; married January 1, 1800 in Stamford, Augustus Lockwood [son of Isaac & Rebecca (Seely) Lockwood] born November 24, 1776 Stamford and died there January 14, 1857; both buried North Street Cemetery, Stamford; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • WILLIAM AUGUSTUS LOCKWOOD 3.10.9.3.3.4
  • MARY REBECCA LOCKWOOD 3.10.9.3.3.5
