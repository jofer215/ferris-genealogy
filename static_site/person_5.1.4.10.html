HANNAH LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.1.4.10 | Source: James
(5.1.4.10) HANNAH LOCKWOOD (#228) born August 26, 1750 Greenwich CT and died there August 23, 1837; married M. William Hendrie (William Hendrie) born December 11, 1745 Broom of Moy, Parish of Dike Scotland and died October 21, 1804 Greenwich CT; both buried Old Sound Beach Cemetery, Greenwich. [Jim - this assignment is based on the correction to the Lockwood Book that is emphatic that Hannah was Gershom & Mary's daughter, not Theophilus & Hannah (Close) Lockwood's daughter.] Born to Hannah and William were supposedly eleven children, nine of which are identified below (not in birth sequence):

Children (5)

  • MARY HENDRIE 5.1.4.10.1
  • JANE HENDRIE 5.1.4.10.2
  • ALEXANDER HENDRIE 5.1.4.10.3
  • DAVID HENDRIE 5.1.4.10.8
  • HANNAH HENDRIE died September 10, 1794 age 10y 7m 8d; buried Old Sound Beach 5.1.4.10.9
