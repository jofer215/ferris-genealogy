HARRY THORPE KNAPP

ID: 2.6.4.7.5.2.1.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.4.7.5.2.1.3) HARRY THORPE KNAPP born November (5?), 1871 probably, Washington County NY. He is thought to have resided IN in Washington County NY; Keene NH; Schenectady NY; Troy NY; and Watervliet NY. He was a manager of F. W. Woolworth & Co; married March 12, 1893 Louise Jennie Fischer born c1872/73; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • VIVIAN EMMA KNAPP 2.6.4.7.5.2.1.3.1
  • GERALD WALTER KNAPP 2.6.4.7.5.2.1.3.2
  • WILLIAM BURNHAM KNAPP 2.6.4.7.5.2.1.3.3
