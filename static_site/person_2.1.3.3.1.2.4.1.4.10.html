MARY FAYE COATES

ID: 2.1.3.3.1.2.4.1.4.10 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.1.2.4.1.4.10) MARY FAYE COATES born January 20, 1901 Toledo OH and died December 24, 1981 Cedar Springs, Kent, MI; buried there Elmwood Cemetery; married 1 st September 20, 1917 in Kalamazoo MI, John Lowe [son of John & Mary (Soarborough) Lowe] born September 14, 1896 Snover, Sanilac, MI and died August 1966 Sandusky MI; 2d January 8, 1955 in Grand Rapids MI, Elmer Benedict [son of Elmer & Bertha (Palmatier) Benedict] born February 25, 1913 Kankakee IL and died 196 Phoenix AZ

No children recorded
