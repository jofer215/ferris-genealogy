JOHN WESLEY LOCKWOOD,

ID: 4.4.4.1.4.5 | Source: Mary
(4.4.4.1.4.5) JOHN WESLEY LOCKWOOD, Rev. (#1520) born May 1, 1855 Madisonville OH and in 1888 resided in Wilmington OH; married November 23, 1882 at Martinsville OH, Sarah Ellen Fisher born Gainsboro VA; Rev. Lockwood was a delegate to the General Methodist Episcopal Conference held in May 1888 NYC

No children recorded
