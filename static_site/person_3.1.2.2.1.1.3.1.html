ORRIS S. FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.2.1.1.3.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.1.1.3.1) ORRIS S. FERRIS born April 14, 1832 NY and died April/May 7, 1894; married 1st October 5, 1859 Maria A. Wilcox (1838-); married 2d November 22/24, 1878 in Leonidas MI, (Sophronia) Fronia (Purchase) Kitchen [dau of Pracklett Blake & Lucy (Poster) Purchase] born 1854 Allegancy Co NY and died May 31, 1922 MI. Orris served in Company K., 16th Infantry, 1861-62, Civil War. [? Jim - there was an Orrin (Surgeon) who served in the 15th and 123d Ohio Infantry Regiments - same guy?] – there was an Orris S Ferris who was involved in dry goods and a grocer in Leonidas, St. Joseph, MI in the 1880 census

No children recorded
