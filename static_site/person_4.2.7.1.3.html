ENOS LOCKWOOD Jr.,

ID: 4.2.7.1.3 | Source: Mary
(4.2.7.1.3) ENOS LOCKWOOD Jr.,(#617) born March 1, 1763 Greenwich and died June 22, 1837. Enos was a Private in the Town Guards under Captain Timothy Lockwood of Greenwich in 1780 for two years. He was also a delegate to the Constitutional Convention in Hartford in August 1818, which adopted the State Constitution. He married, September 15, 1793, Sarah Hoyt; both buried in the Old Greenwich Cemetery. Born to Enos and Sarah were:
