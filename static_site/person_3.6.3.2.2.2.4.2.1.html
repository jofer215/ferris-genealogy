ROXANNE ZENNA HILL

ID: 3.6.3.2.2.2.4.2.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.2.2.4.2.1) ROXANNE ZENNA HILL (Roxy) born January 3, 1866 Blairstown, Warren, NJ and died December 22, 1966 (100 years of age) Sussex Co NJ; married Eliken Webster Crowell [son of Sanford Clark VanDoozer & Amelia (Dunning) Crowell] born July 8, 1877 Mt. Salem, Sussex, NJ and died November 30, 1935 Port Jervis NY; interred Mt. Salem NJ.
