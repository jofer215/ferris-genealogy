JONAS SEELY BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.2.3.4 | Source: James
(5.2.2.2.3.4) JONAS SEELY BUXTON born March 27, 1792 Stamford CT and died Orleans County NY. He was a farmer. He married June 13, 1820 in Stamford, Rebecca Weed [dau of David & Jerusah (Chichester) Weed] born January 30, 1798 Stamford. Born to them were:
