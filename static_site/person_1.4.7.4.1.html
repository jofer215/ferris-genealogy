JOHN M. FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.4.1 | Source: John
(1.4.7.4.1) JOHN M. FERRIS (John Muckleworth Ferris?) (1788-1858) born Throgg’s Neck, Westchester Co; a boat owner and lumberman in Hamilton and Herkimer Counties NY; received a good patrimony and never engaged in business. Both he and his wife belonged to Society of Friends. He married Elizabeth U. Powell [dau of Thomas & Esther (Lax) Powell] born 1805. Born to them were:

Children (5)

  • ANNA AUGUSTUS FERRIS took her own life by drowning; married 1.4.7.4.1.1
  • WILLIAM P. 1.4.7.4.1.2
  • GEORGE P. 1.4.7.4.1.3
  • JOHN THOMAS FERRIS removed to Michigan; married Adeline Morse 1.4.7.4.1.4
  • VALENTINE SEAMAN FERRIS 1.4.7.4.1.5
