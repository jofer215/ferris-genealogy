SARAH UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.4.3.1 | Source: John
(1.2.3.4.3.1) SARAH UNDERHILL (1777-1844) baptized Grace Episcopal Church, Jamaica, Long Island, January 25, 1788; married November 27, 1799 Garret Van Wicklen born March1, 1775 and died 1851; both buried in the Dutch Reformed Cemetery in Norwich, Long Island NY. Born to them were:

Children (6)

  • HANNAH VAN WICKLEN 1.2.3.4.3.1.1
  • GARRET VAN WICKLEN was buried on his property near Mt. Olivet 1.2.3.4.3.1.2
  • HELENA VAN WICKLEN 1.2.3.4.3.1.3
  • JONATHAN UNDERHILL VAN WICKLEN 1.2.3.4.3.1.4
  • PHEBE MATILDA VAN WICKLEN 1.2.3.4.3.1.5
  • JANE ELIZA VAN WICKLEN 1.2.3.4.3.1.6
