JAMES SIMPSON NEWMAN

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.14.2.1.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.14.2.1.1) JAMES SIMPSON NEWMAN (Bun) born February 27, 1904 Cedar Falls, Black Hawk, IA and died September 1986 Austin TX; buried Greenwood Cemetery, Cedar Falls; attorney, owner of truck leasing company in Waterloo IA; married 1st 1939 in Waterloo, Evelyn McElhinney [dau of Fayette Floyd & Dessie (Anderson) McElhinney] born January 30, 1907 Waterloo, Black Hawk, IA and died November 1989 Corvallis OR; ashes scattered on a farm near Frederika IA which she owned at that time – divorced 1977; married 2d Marian C Ford; and he and Eve had 4 kids and adopted 1

No children recorded
