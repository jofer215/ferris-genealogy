ROSE ALICE LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.1.4.4.2.3.3 | Source: James
(5.1.4.4.2.3.3) ROSE ALICE LOCKWOOD (Photo) born November 5, 1842 Lafayette IN and died January 10, 1917 Thorntown IN; buried Mattoon IL next to a daughter; married 1st October 18, 1859 in Lafayette, Henry Arnold Parker [son of Jonas & Nancy (Gatchell) Parker] born June 22, 1833 Lyons, Wayne, NY and died April 19, 1910 Dayton, Tippecanoe, IN; Civil War Vet served in Co C, 72d Indiana Infantry Regt, Mar-Sep 1865; a highly respected citizen of Dayton; married 2d c1881 Lemuel Edward Morris born 1842 England and according to the 1880 US Census was a a hired hand on the Parker farm; and born to her and Henry were:
