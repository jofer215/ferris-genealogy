WILLIAM KING COFFIN

ID: 4.6.1.2.3.1.1.2 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.3.1.1.2) WILLIAM KING COFFIN (#3333) born August 9, 1850 Jacksonville IL and died 1925; graduated from Knox College 1871, B.S.; was a banker at Eau Claire WI 1888. William was President of the Wisconsin State Historical Society; founder, Wisconsin Banker’s Association; executive council, American Banker’s Association, 1903-05; married October 3, 1872 at Batavia IL, Mary Grove Burroughs [dau of Dr. Lester Morgan & Elmira (Wheeler) Burroughs] born November 11, 1851 and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • MARY ELMIRA COFFIN 4.6.1.2.3.1.1.2.1
  • GRACE BURROUGHS COFFIN 4.6.1.2.3.1.1.2.2
  • WILLIAM LESTER COFFIN 4.6.1.2.3.1.1.2.3
