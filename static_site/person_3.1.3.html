RUTH FERRIS

ID: 3.1.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.3) RUTH FERRIS (8RLL-2J) born c1700/01 probably Greenwich and died there {OR Dutchess Co NY} on or after January 16, 1733/34 from childbirth; married March 5, 1721/22 at Greenwich, as his first wife, Joseph Reynolds, Jr., [son of Joseph and Abigail (Finch) Reynolds] born May 15, 1699 Greenwich and died after 1749 Pine Plains, Dutchess, NY; commissioned Ensign of the West Company (Train Band), Horseneck, Greenwich Militia May 9, 1728; a large landowner in the Pine Plains area; and born to Ruth and Joseph were:
