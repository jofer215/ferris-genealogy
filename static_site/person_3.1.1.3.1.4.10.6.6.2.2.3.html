GRADY MACGREGOR CONGLETON

ID: 3.1.1.3.1.4.10.6.6.2.2.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.3.1.4.10.6.6.2.2.3) GRADY MACGREGOR CONGLETON (Photograph) born October 20, 1989 Brunswick ME; baptized at the Groton School Chapel; an extremely bright boy is a challenge to us all. “The early discovery that he was severely hearing impaired hit all of us like a ton of bricks. The tears we shed were not only for him, but also for us - and for the unanswerable questions. Grady attends the Clarke School for the Deaf in Northampton MA. ‘The tears are now of joy. To hear him speaking, to see him lip-read, to know that he understands us, is to know that God exists and answers prayers.”

No children recorded
