SYDNEY BISHOP, M.D.,

ID: 5.1.3.4.3.1.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.3.1.1.1) SYDNEY BISHOP, M.D., born April 7, 1848 Bridgeport and died March 24, 1910 Bridgeport CT; attended business college in Burlington NJ; Yale College and Bellevue Medical College, New York City. He was in the firm of Maurice, Bishop & Howland for 20 years, resigned, and returned to Bridgeport to administer family properties and businesses; also president of New York and Bridgeport Steamboat Company and the Automatic Machine Company, which he gave to his son Ferris. Sydney apparently did not practice medicine but for a few years. He married, November 19, 1873, Mary Helen Staples born October 28, 1853 and died April 19, 1873 (1898). Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • HELEN FERRIS BISHOP "Aunt Maime" married Robert S. Hinks, a stockbroker; 5.1.3.4.3.1.1.1.3
