JOHN AUGUSTUS PELL

ID: 1.4.7.3.2.1 | Source: John
(1.4.7.3.2.1) JOHN AUGUSTUS PELL (3d Born?) (1825-1894) married Susan Maria Field [dau of Benjamin] who died in 1893. Born to them was: (1.4.7.3.2.1.1) ELEANOR PELL ‘Ella’ married Charles Harris Phelps II – is the same Harris Phelps who changed his name, Yale B.A. 1868; A.M., 1869?
