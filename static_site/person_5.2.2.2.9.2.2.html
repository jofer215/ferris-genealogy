JAMES NICHOLS BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.2.9.2.2 | Source: James
(5.2.2.2.9.2.2) JAMES NICHOLS BUXTON born September 1836 Stamford CT and died there January 21, 1918; buried New Woodland Cemetery, Stamford; a Civil War Vet serving August 28 – November 29, 1863 as a Private, Company A, 28th Connecticut Volunteer Infantry Regiment; a farmer at Belltown; married c1856/57 Sarah A. {maiden name unk} born in January 1839 and died December 22, 1907 Stamford; buried New Woodland Cemetery. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • BETSEY A. BUXTON 5.2.2.2.9.2.2.2
  • WILLIAM H. BUXTON 5.2.2.2.9.2.2.3
