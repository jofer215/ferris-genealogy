JOSEPH KRIBS HART

ID: 2.1.3.3.1.4.7.6.6.8 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.1.4.7.6.6.8) JOSEPH KRIBS HART born October 20, 1918 Carl Township, McPherson, SD and died April 26, 1991 Sturgis, Meade, SD; buried there Plot E59, Black Hills National Cemetery (Photo); WWII USMC Private; 1950 the family moved to Lead SD where Joe was employed for several years with the Homestake Gold Mine; married November 16, 1937 Helena Heinrich [dau of Julius & Pauline (Wolff) Heinrich] born September 19, 1918; and born to them were:
