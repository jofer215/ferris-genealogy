MARY PECK

ID: 3.10.2.3.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.2.3.1) MARY PECK born 1786 Greenwich and died there August 25, 1854; married 1st September 20, 1805 in Stamford, Andrew Newman born December 10, 1786 Stamford; married 2d John Holmes Lockwood (3.10.5.7.2.4); and born to her and Andrew were:

Children (3)

  • ALEXANDER SYLVESTER NEWMAN 3.10.2.3.1.1
  • AUGUSTUS RALPH NEWMAN 3.10.2.3.1.2
  • DAVID MALTBY NEWMAN 3.10.2.3.1.3
