JAMES S. FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.2.4.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.4.3) JAMES S. FERRIS born September 14, 1818 and died September 23, 1870 Winchester, Randolph, IN; married 1st March 1, 1828 (obvious date error) Frances Williams; and 2d December 18, 1844 Mary Hull. Offspring from first marriage is unknown. [Note: there was a James S. Ferris, who was Superintendent of Sunday School of the Methodist Church, Winchester, Randolph, IN - pretty sure same guy.] James was long a teacher in the seminary at Winchester and occupied a high place as an educator - referred to as Professor Ferris. He located to Winchester about 1847, removing to Muncie and later to New Castle, serving 8 years as Auditor of Henry County; elected to House of Representatives 1852 served in 37th Session 1853 before being elected Auditor Henry Co; after several changes of location including Iowa, he finally returned to Winchester, remaining there until this death.
