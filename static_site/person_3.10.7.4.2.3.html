GIDEON REYNOLDS

ID: 3.10.7.4.2.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.7.4.2.3) GIDEON REYNOLDS (1782-1847) buried Cross River Cemetery, Cross River NY; married 1st December 27, 1803 in Cross River, Betsey Reynolds [dau of Nathaniel & Hannah (Todd) Reynolds] born September 1, 1786 Cross River, Westchester, NY and died there June 12, 1830; buried there Cross River Cemetery; married 2d Theodosia Smith born March 3, 1796; married 3d October 13, 1846 Hannah Rundle born September 12, 1794 Greenwich CT; and born to him and Bestey were:

Children (3)

  • CYRUS H REYNOLDS 3.10.7.4.2.3.2
  • DEWITT CLINTON REYNOLDS 3.10.7.4.2.3.3
