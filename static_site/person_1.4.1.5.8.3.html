EDWARD FERRIS

ID: 1.4.1.5.8.3 | Source: John
(1.4.1.5.8.3) EDWARD FERRIS born May 3, 1847 Michigan and died there January 25, 1937; as a young man, Edward returned to New York with his Father in 1865. He later returned to Michigan to his father’s land and finished clearing it and built a mansion-type house. In 1963, the farm was still owned by his two daughters, who have since died.
