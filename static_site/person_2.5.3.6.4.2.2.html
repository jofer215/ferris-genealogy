LEWIS HOYT

ID: 2.5.3.6.4.2.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.6.4.2.2) LEWIS HOYT (#4677) born January 24, 1815 Coxsackie, Green, NJ and was living in Medina, Ridgeway, Orleans, NY 1871; a farmer; married 1st July 4, 1839 Fanny Howe born 1820 and died March 9, 1859 at age 39y; 2d November 29, 1859 Clorinda Davis born c1820; and born to Lewis and Fanny were:

Children (6)

  • CORNELIA HOYT 2.5.3.6.4.2.2.2
  • EMORY LEWIS HOYT 2.5.3.6.4.2.2.3
  • FANNY JANE HOYT 2.5.3.6.4.2.2.4
  • EVA FANNY HOYT 2.5.3.6.4.2.2.5
  • IRVING CHAPIN HOYT 2.5.3.6.4.2.2.6
