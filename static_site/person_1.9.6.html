ELIZABETH HARRISON

ID: 1.9.6 | Source: John
(1.9.6) ELIZABETH HARRISON born October 30, 1729 Philadelphia PA and died Radnor, Delaware, PA; buried there; married c1750 Richard Horton born 1736 (172627) Southold PA (Southold, Suffolk, L. I., NY) and died in Radnor PA; they were Quakers; and born to them were: (1.9.6.1) NATHAN PORT HORTON born 1754 Radnor, Delaware, PA and died 1843 Broad Top, Huntingdon, PA; buried Evans Cemetery, Six Mile Run PA; blacksmith; served in Captain Comfort Starr’s Company of Guilford VT as a Private in 1780; married 1789 in Old Swedes Church, Norriton Township, Montgomery Co PA, Rebecca Priest [dau of Henry & Mary (Hardford) Priest] born February 22, 1769 Norriton Township, Montgomery Co PA and died December 28, 1844 Wells PA; and born to them to were:

Children (6)

  • JOSIAH HORTON 1.9.6.3
  • JESSE HORTON 1.9.6.4
