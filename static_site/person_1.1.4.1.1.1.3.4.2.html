PAULINE SWEET

ID: 1.1.4.1.1.1.3.4.2 | Source: John
(1.1.4.1.1.1.3.4.2) PAULINE SWEET born June 16, 1893 Rochester, Strafford, NH and died June 17, 1968; resided Springfield MA 1946; married Frank L. Harold Sjostrom born June 24, 1889 RI and died March 30, 1960; a WWI Vet; they lived in Florida and owned a motel, possibly in the Fort Lauderdale area

No children recorded
