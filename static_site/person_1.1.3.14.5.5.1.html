FANNY ROBINA HILDRETH

ID: 1.1.3.14.5.5.1 | Source: John
(1.1.3.14.5.5.1) FANNY ROBINA HILDRETH born December 5, 1882 Caistor, Ontario and died April 8, 1965 Dundas, Ontario; married July 31, 1904 George William Gowland [son of John & Elizabeth (Oliver) Gowland] born November 17, 1876 Binbrook, Ontario and died May 25, 1946 Tapleytown, Ontario. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • IRENE MAY GOWLAND 1.1.3.14.5.5.1.1
  • VERNA JEAN GOWLAND 1.1.3.14.5.5.1.2
  • GEORGE EDGAR ELWOOD GOWLAND 1.1.3.14.5.5.1.3
  • LUELLA MAUDE VIOLA GOWLAND 1.1.3.14.5.5.1.4
