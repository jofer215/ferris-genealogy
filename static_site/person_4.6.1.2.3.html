JOSEPH LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.1.2.3 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.3) JOSEPH LOCKWOOD (#680) born December 3, 1764 Pound Ridge NY and died there October 25, 1799 of Yellow Fever; married 1st October 9, 1788 Mary Drake [dau of Colonel Samuel] born September 8, 1765 and died May 2, 1819; married 2d September 27, 1800 Duncan McCall. Born to Joseph and Mary were:

Children (4)

  • SAMUEL DRAKE LOCKWOOD 4.6.1.2.3.1
  • JESSE CLOSE LOCKWOOD 4.6.1.2.3.2
  • REBECCA ANN LOCKWOOD 4.6.1.2.3.3
  • CORNELIUS LOCKWOOD 4.6.1.2.3.4
