ARTHUR ALLEN BLISS

ID: 2.6.7.3.5.8.1.1.2.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.3.5.8.1.1.2.2) ARTHUR ALLEN BLISS born May 12, 1917 Grand Junction, Mesa, CO and died March 27, 2001 Beckley WV. He married first Madge Virginia Darby [dau of Grant M. & Mabel S.] and they had 1 child; married second Dedee (Nila Bailey) O’Neal.

No children recorded
