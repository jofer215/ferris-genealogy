MARGARET FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.2.2.1.2.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.2.2.1.2.1) MARGARET FERRIS (Photograph) born March 19, 1907 Greenwich CT and died July 15, 1980 Stamford CT; buried Ferris Plot, at the cemetery at the First Congregational Church, Old Greenwich. Margaret played the piano and would improvise. She worked as a secretary - her notes were in shorthand; wrote poems, some of which were published in the local newspaper upon occasion, and others of which were done for birthdays or get-well notes; married 1st Steven Mackcloud (a Native American); and 2d Russell Rowsey - no offspring

No children recorded
