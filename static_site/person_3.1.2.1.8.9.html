JACOB PETER DAVIS

ID: 3.1.2.1.8.9 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.8.9) JACOB PETER DAVIS born July 13, 1798 Marbletown, Ulster, NY and died July 16, 1882 Olive NY; buried Shokan, Ulster Co NY; married in Ulster Co, his cousin Maria Snyder Davis born May 24, 1812 Montgomery, Orange, NY. He went to Macomb Co MI as a young man before railroads had been built and established a farm; when he was 84y old he decided to pay a visit to the place of his childhood, Olive in Ulster Co. Soon after his arrival he became ill and within a few weeks died. He was buried beside his parents, but it was necessary some years later when the Ashokan Dam was built to move the bodies to the property of his cousin, Marthias Burher at Shokan. (According to these dates, he Jacob was 42y old when his first child born and 56y old when the last one born.) Born to them were:
