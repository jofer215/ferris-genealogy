JACOB LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.11.3 | Source: Mary
(4.6.11.3) JACOB LOCKWOOD (#293) born c1750 Stamford; baptized June 21, 1758 St. John’s Episcopal Church, Stamford; moved to New Canaan CT and died January 11, 1816, age 66 years; buried in the Pound Ridge Cemetery at Murphy’s Corners; a drummer in the American Revolution; married Mary Pellum (or Pelham) and born to them were:

Children (2)
