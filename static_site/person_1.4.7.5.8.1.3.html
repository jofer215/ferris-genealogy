RAYMOND WEST FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.5.8.1.3 | Source: John
(1.4.7.5.8.1.3) RAYMOND WEST FERRIS born December 4, 1885 Brookline MA and died March 2, 1945 Newport, RI; buried Auburn Cemetery, Cambridge MA; S.B., M.I.T. 1908; Assistant Director State Ohio Board of Health; Akron Water Works; Goodyear Company; Lieutenant Commander, USN, WWII; ocean yachting enthusiast winner of Bermuda Race in 1930; married December 28, 1908 Henrietta Davis (18851975) and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • EDWARD MORTIMER FERRIS 1.4.7.5.8.1.3.1
  • RAYMOND WEST FERRIS, JR., 1.4.7.5.8.1.3.2
  • HENRY DAVID FERRIS 1.4.7.5.8.1.3.4
