HARMON UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.6.1.6.2 | Source: John
(1.2.6.1.6.2) HARMON UNDERHILL born January 8, 1801 Dorset, Bennington, VT and died April 23, 1868 Rupert, Bennington, VT; married 1st March 14, 1825 at Dorset VT, Orpha Terrell born May 28, 1801 Dorset VT and died September 12, 1838 Dorset VT; married 2d November 1, 1838 Dorset, Betsey McClure Brewster [dau of Ohel & Betsey (McClure) Brewster] born May 8, 1805 Middletown VT.
