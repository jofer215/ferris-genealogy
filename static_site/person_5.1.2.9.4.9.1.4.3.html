HELEN ESTELLE CHRISTIAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.9.1.4.3 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.9.1.4.3) HELEN ESTELLE CHRISTIAN born November 5, 1924 Poughkeepsie NY and died January 15, 1991 Vassar Brothers Hospital, Poughkeepsie NY; buried New Hackensack Cemetery; married December 9, 1945 in Poughkeepsie, George W. Irwin [son of David Irwin & Anne (Smith)] born February 22, 1920 St. Johnsbury VT and died January 7, 1999 Lutheran Care Center, Poughkeepsie NY; both buried New Hackensack Cemetery. Helen was a graduate of Poughkeepsie High School and the Krissler Business Institute in Poughkeepsie and a lifelong resident of that city. George served in the U.S. Army Air Corps during World War II. He was a parishioner of First Presbyterian Church in Poughkeepsie, where he served as a deacon. He was a 50-year Poughkeepsie area resident and was a self-employed carpenter in Poughkeepsie. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • MICHAEL C. IRWIN resided Poughkeepsie 1991 and Rhinbeck 1999 5.1.2.9.4.9.1.4.3.1
  • DAVID E. IRWIN resided Vail CO 1991 and 1999 5.1.2.9.4.9.1.4.3.2
  • RICHARD ALLAN IRWIN resided Colorado Springs CO 1991 and 1999 5.1.2.9.4.9.1.4.3.3
