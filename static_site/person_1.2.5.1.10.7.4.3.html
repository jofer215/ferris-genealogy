VERN DENIS SUTTON

ID: 1.2.5.1.10.7.4.3 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.10.7.4.3) VERN DENIS SUTTON born May 13, 1881 Greenville MI and died April 15, 1969 Battle Creek MI; buried Hicks Cemetery, Pennfield MI; Argubright Business College, Battle Creek; reporter for Battle Creek Review & Herald (St Louis World’s Fair); safety supervisor and personal secretary to C W Post, Battle Creek; photographer; married May 13, 1903 in Battle Creek, Georgia May Wheeler [dau of Charles Edgar & Mary Gilletti (Halsted) Wheeler] born September 18, 1881 Topeka KS and died December 10, 1960 Battle Creek; apparently spent her younger years in Herkimer Co NY; teacher, magazine writer; and born to them were: (1.2.5.1.10.7.4.3.1) HAWTHORN WHEELER SUTTON born May 15, 1906 Battle Creek, Calhoun, MI and died April 21, 1950 Hastings, Barry, MI result of motorcycle accident; buried Hicks Cemetery, Pennfield MI; Argubright Business College; movie projectionist; married Miss Holds
