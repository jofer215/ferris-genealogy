JOSEPH LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.5.3.5.4 | Source: Mary
(4.5.3.5.4) JOSEPH LOCKWOOD (#661) married Phebe Seely. Around 1806-1808, Joseph and Phebe joined the Shakers, at Union Village, Warren County OH. Joseph was a carpenter and built houses for the ‘United Society of Believers in Christ’s Second Appearing’ as the Shakers called themselves. Joseph (and Phebe?) moved to IN, where he died. Born to them were:

Children (3)
