WILFRED ORVAL KNAPP “Willie” born March 20, 1923 Barrington NY and

ID: 2.6.2.3.2.5.8.4.2.6 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.2.5.8.4.2.6) WILFRED ORVAL KNAPP “Willie” born March 20, 1923 Barrington NY and living in Dundee 1996; and died January 24, 2001 Elmira, Chemung, NY; buried Old Knapp Cemetery, Barrington, Yates, NY. Wilfred was a merchant-forester [lumber; wholesale/retail] and belonged to the Free Will Baptist Church. He married February 22, 1949 at Dundee, Yates, NY Marilyn Estella Wells “Peggy” born February 15, 1930 Potter NY and was living in Dundee 1996. Born to them were:
