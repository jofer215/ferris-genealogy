CAROLINE SHAW HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.4.7.5.2.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.7.5.2.1) CAROLINE SHAW HOYT born September 6, 1839 Stamford and died there July 10, 1863; buried there St. John’s & St. Andrew’s Cemetery; married November 14, 1861 in Stamford, Chester P. Staples born c1835 Sandwich MA; he was a US Army Paymaster 1863; and they had a child
