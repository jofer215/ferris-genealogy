SALLY MINERVA NEWMAN

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.3) SALLY MINERVA NEWMAN born October 15, 1827 Jefferson Co NY and died June 14, 1913 Manchester, Delaware, IA; buried Silver Springs (Silver Creek Cemetery), Delaware Co IA; married 1st December 29, 1853 in Leyden, Lewis, NY, George Potter Arnold born March 6, 1828 St. Lawrence NY and died September 23, 1863 of disease at Little Rock AR; buried Union Cemetery, Pulaski AR – George was a school teacher, farmer, and a Private in Company F., 27th Iowa Infantry Regiment (Civil War); married 2d December 20, 1871 in IA, John Magirl/MaGirl/MaGill born c1819 Ireland and died December 1899 Sioux City IA; buried there Calvary Cemetery; and born to Sally and George were:

Children (3)

  • ALICE ALMIRA ARNOLD 3.11.1.1.3.2.3.1
  • CHARLES W. ARNOLD 3.11.1.1.3.2.3.2
  • GEORGE ALBERT ARNOLD 3.11.1.1.3.2.3.3
