JOANNE AGNES BREON

ID: 5.2.2.6.1.2.2.2.2.1 | Source: James
(5.2.2.6.1.2.2.2.2.1) JOANNE AGNES BREON born January 9, 1932 Rockford, Winnebago, IL and died October 22, 1995 Mystic, New London, CT; buried North Stonington CT. She received a BFA from Beloit; was a Christian Scientist; book restorer and binder; married August 26, 1954 in Newark NJ, Frederic Augustus Bliss born June 30, 1909 Corte Madeira CA; and they had a child

No children recorded
