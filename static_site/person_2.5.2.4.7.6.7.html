ELIZABETH HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.4.7.6.7 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.7.6.7) ELIZABETH HOYT born 1820 Darien and died August 5, 1877 Stamford; buried St. John’s & St. Andrew’s Cemetery, Stamford; married March 27, 1856 in Stamford as his 2d wife, Solomon L. Crosby born February 2, 1817 NY and died March 2, 1894 Stamford; buried with Elizabeth.

No children recorded
