NEHEMIAH HAIT

ID: 3.10.1.4.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.4.5) NEHEMIAH HAIT (#2938) (Deacon) born May 14, 1752 Stamford CT and was living 1804; resided Castleton VT, where he went when he was about 19; served in the Rev. Army and was with Nathan Allen at the taking of Ticonderoga; afterwards taken prisoner and carried to Montreal. He married widow Moulton.
