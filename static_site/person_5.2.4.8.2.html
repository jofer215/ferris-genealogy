THEODOSIA DAVENPORT

ID: 5.2.4.8.2 | Source: James
(5.2.4.8.2) THEODOSIA DAVENPORT born September 27, 1779 Stamford and died there October 29, 1809; buried North Stamford Cemetery; married November 17, 1799 in Stamford, William D. Davenport [son of Hezekiah & Ruth (Ketchum) Davenport] born September 17, 1770 Stamford and died November 13, 1850 Darien CT; buried North Stamford Cemetery. After Theodosia’s death, William married Nancy Hoyt and they had 4 kids. Born to Theodosia and William were: (5.2.4.8.2.1) MARY ANN DAVENPORT born c1800 and died after 1841; married after 1826, as his 2d wife, Ralph Hoyt (2.5.3.4.1.2.2) – see him for offspring
