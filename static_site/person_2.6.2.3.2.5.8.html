JESSE COOLEY KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.3.2.5.8 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.2.5.8) JESSE COOLEY KNAPP born July 16, 1816 Barrington, Yates, NY and died February 7, 1908 Penn Yan, Yates, NY. Jesse became a prominent citizen and was acclaimed for his prowess as a blacksmith and farmer; became a town supervisor and served as Highway Commissioner. He was affiliated with the Free Will Baptist Church. He married 1840 at Barrington, Rachel Mariah Hopkins born 1820 Dutchess Co NY and died 1894 Penn Yan NY; both buried at Penn Yan NY. Jesse purchased land from his father and he and Rachel resided on the farm with his father and mother, where they shared a large double house. It was at this house that their children were born.
