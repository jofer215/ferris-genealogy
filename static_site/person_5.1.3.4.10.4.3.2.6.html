JOHN LEE GEIDNER

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.3.2.6 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.3.2.6) JOHN LEE GEIDNER born October 1, 1915, Harmon IL and died February 1, 1943 in the California desert near Needles - plane crash in the last days of his training as a Army Air Corps pilot.

No children recorded
