HUGH WESLEY DOBSON

ID: 3.1.6.8.1.3.4.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.1.3.4.5) HUGH WESLEY DOBSON born March 4, 1879 Molesworth, Huron, Ontario and died June 9, 1956; Hugh was Associate Secretary of Evangelism and Social Services for the Methodist, then the United Church, 1914-52; he had an enthusiasm for genealogy, and in his travels, searched for information; in fact it was Hugh’s research in the New York Public Library, which first identified the Ross family as descending from Thomas Taylor Ross, a common and understandable mistake, since only recently has the Ross family been traced back to Donald Ross. He married 1908 in Mount Pleasant, Ontario, Edythe Gertrude Thomas born April 14, 1884 and died April 23, 1969; and born to them were:
