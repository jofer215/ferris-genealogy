JOHN ADDISON QUINTARD

ID: 2.6.3.5.1.3.8 | Source: Peter
(2.6.3.5.1.3.8) JOHN ADDISON QUINTARD born June 2, 1840 Greenwich and died October 23, 1905 Brooklyn; married 1st December 10, 1864 Mary Amelia Waterbury [dau of Jonathan Brown & Sally (Smith) Waterbury] born July 6, 1840 Stamford and died November 3, 1887 Brooklyn; 2d December 17, 1888, Sarah Elizabeth Boyd [dau of Andrew & Jane (Waterbury) Boyd] born March 19, 1856 Stamford and died February 22, 1924

No children recorded
