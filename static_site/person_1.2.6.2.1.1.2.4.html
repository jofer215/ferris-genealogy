JAMES SAMUEL UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.6.2.1.1.2.4 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.1.1.2.4) JAMES SAMUEL UNDERHILL born March 31, 1847 Zanesville OH and died November 10, 1897 Fordland, Webster, MO; buried Franklin Co NE; married 1st October 28, 1869 in Rushville IL, Rosanna Seybold [dau of Samuel & Malinda (Kellams) Seybold] born February 17, 1850 Orange Co IN and died May 30, 1883 Holdredge, Franklin, NE; married 2d February 24, 1884 in Frontier Co NE, Mary Ellen Rice; and born to him and Rosanna were:

Children (4)

  • GEORGE WILLIAM UNDERHILL 1.2.6.2.1.1.2.4.1
  • CHARLES EDWARD UNDERHILL 1.2.6.2.1.1.2.4.2
  • BESSIE LENORE UNDERHILL 1.2.6.2.1.1.2.4.3
  • JAMES MILLARD UNDERHILL 1.2.6.2.1.1.2.4.4
