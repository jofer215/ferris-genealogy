MINERVA MEKEEL

ID: 1.1.4.4.5.3.3 | Source: John
(1.1.4.4.5.3.3) MINERVA MEKEEL born c1863 on the family farm; married 1st in the Court House at Carmel NY, Charles Wesley Denney [son of Joseph G. & Charlotte (Brewer)] born September 10, 1893 Philipstown and died August 28, 1915 Petaluma CA; married 2d Eugene Ireland and they resided at Yonkers NY.
