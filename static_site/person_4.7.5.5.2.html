SARAH WATERBURY

ID: 4.7.5.5.2 | Source: Mary
(4.7.5.5.2) SARAH WATERBURY born August 20, 1763 Stamford CT and died there 1806; married May 9, 1781 in Stamford, Enos Waterbury born June 19, 1762 Stamford; Enos remarried twice after Sarah’s death. (This Sarah is also listed as married to Isaac Selleck – another conflict) Born to her and Enos were:

Children (7)

  • HANNAH WATERBURY 4.7.5.5.2.1
  • NANCY WATERBURY 4.7.5.5.2.2
  • HORACE WATERBURY 4.7.5.5.2.3
  • JONATHAN BROWN WATERBURY 4.7.5.5.2.4
  • MARIA WATERBURY 4.7.5.5.2.5
  • CONFLICT; buried Turn of River Cemetery, Stamford. Born to them were: 4.7.5.5.2.6
  • HARRIET WATERBURY 4.7.5.5.2.7
