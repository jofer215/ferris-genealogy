MOSES KNAPP

ID: 2.6.7.6.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.6.3) MOSES KNAPP born January 21, 1771 Salisbury CT and died October 2, 1823 Clay, Onondaga County, NY; buried Pine Plains Cemetery, Clay NY; married c1798/9 at Salisbury [or possibly at Adams MA], Abigail Williams born in August 1776 Vermont and died Clay, March 25, 1852.
