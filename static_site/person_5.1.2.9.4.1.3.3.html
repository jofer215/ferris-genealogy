IDA MAY CHRISTIAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.1.3.3 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.1.3.3) IDA MAY CHRISTIAN born July 8, 1882 and died 1884 (5.1.2.9.4.1.3.4) JOSEPH ALEXANDER CHRISTIAN born November 23/24, 1884 Tompkins Corners, Putnam Valley NY and died January 16/26, 1972 at the Northern Westchester Hospital; married June 4, 1905 in Mt. Kisco NY, Marie Emma Moore who died March 1964 at 80y; both buried Raymond Hill Cemetery, Carmel NY; resided on Huntsville Road, Katonah NY. Joseph was a carpenter.
