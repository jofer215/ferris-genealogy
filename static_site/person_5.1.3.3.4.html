RUTH FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.3.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.4) RUTH FERRIS born 1761 Greenwich CT and died August 15, 1826 age 65y at Sound Beach (East Greenwich) CT; married c1779/80 Charles Knapp born 1757 Greenwich and died March 3, 1825 [son of Joseph Knapp {according to Fred Knapp, Joseph may not be the father of Charles}]. Charles served as a Private in Captain Abraham Mead's Company in 1775 and a Sergeant in Colonel Lamb's 2d Regiment of Artillery in 1777, serving for three years. Charles died March 3, 1825 at Sound Beach (East Greenwich) and both he and Ruth were buried in the Tomac Avenue Cemetery, (Old Sound Beach Cemetery) (East Greenwich), Fairfield County CT.
