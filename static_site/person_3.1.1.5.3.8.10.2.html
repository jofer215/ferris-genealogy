LOUIS BLODGETT

ID: 3.1.1.5.3.8.10.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.5.3.8.10.2) LOUIS BLODGETT (NEWELL) born January 11, 1871 Chicago IL and died September 5, 1966 Hennepin Co MN; obtained his education in Griswold College, Davenport IA then returned to Minneapolis to enter his step-father’s business in 1889, and in 1921 upon the death of his step-father, became president of the wholesale grocery house of George R. Newell & Company; married June 15, 1898 (1893) Helen Winston (dau of Fednall Gregory & Alice L. (Olmstead) Winston] born July 17, 1877 MN and died April 24, 1968 Hennepin Co MN; and born to them was:
