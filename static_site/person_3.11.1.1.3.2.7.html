LODUSKY NEWMAN

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.7 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.7) LODUSKY NEWMAN born October 8, 1837 and died November 10, 1882 Delaware Co IA; buried there at Silver Springs; married February 22, 1860 in Delaware Co IA, Bradford Wilcox Kenyon [son of Thomas] born May 20, 1837 Chenango, Broome, NY and died July 15, 1898 Coffins Grove, Delaware, IA; buried Greenwood Cemetery, Masonville IA; Civil War Vet with 27th Iowa Infantry Regiment; merchant in Ryan and Masonville IA; and born to them were (conflicting dates between marriage and 1st born – maybe this was his 2d marriage):
