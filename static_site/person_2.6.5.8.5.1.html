MARY ELIZABETH KNAPP

ID: 2.6.5.8.5.1 | Source: Peter
(2.6.5.8.5.1) MARY ELIZABETH KNAPP born December 28, 1804 Stamford and died there November 6, 1854; buried North Street Cemetery, Northfield-Stamford CT; married June 25, 1823 at Stamford, Elias Taylor Jr, of Poughkeepsie NY, born November 21, 1790 Warsaw CT (Warren, Litchfield, CT) and died May 1, 1842 Warren CT, buried there; they divorced c1830; and born to them was an only child:
