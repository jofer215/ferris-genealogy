MARTHA FERRIS

ID: 3.8.1.2.2.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.8.1.2.2.4) MARTHA FERRIS born September 9, 1799 VT and died January 18, 1872 probably Panton/Vergennes VT; married January 1, 1824 Philander Hanks [son of Eleazer & Betsey (Pond) Hanks] born c1800, probably in Panton/Vergennes VT and died there October 19, 1881. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • PHILENA L. HANKS 3.8.1.2.2.4.1
  • FIDELIA VIOLA HANKS 3.8.1.2.2.4.2
  • ELIZABETH AMELIA HANKS 3.8.1.2.2.4.3
  • MARTHA JANE HANKS 3.8.1.2.2.4.4
