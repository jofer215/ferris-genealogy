PEARL ROSCOE

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.2.1.3.3 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.2.1.3.3) PEARL ROSCOE born June 1, 1906 Mahopac NY and died March 15, 1997 Carmel NY; married November 2, 1926 in Carmel, George Lockwood Sr. [son of Louis & Minnie (Tompkins)] born August 17, 1902 Carmel NY and died August 8, 1977 North Tarrytown NY; both buried Raymond Hill Cemetery, Carmel NY. Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, Thursday, March 20, 1997: Pearl Lockwood, a lifelong Carmel resident, died March 15 at Putnam Hospital Center. She was 90. She born June 1, 1906, to Sherman and Ida Smith Roscoe in Mahopac. Raised in Carmel, Mrs. Lockwood attended Towners School, a one-room schoolhouse in Patterson. A founding member of the Carmel Fire Department Auxiliary, Mrs. Lockwood had worked as a postal clerk at the Carmel post office for many years prior to her retirement in 1976. Survivors include one son, George Jr. of Carmel; one daughter, Pauline Kurtz Green of Carmel; seven grandchildren; 14 great grandchildren and 10 great-great grandchildren. Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, Wednesday, Aug. 10, 1977: George Lockwood Sr., 75, of Circle Drive, Carmel, died August 8, in Philps Memorial Hospital, North Tarrytown, after a brief illness.
