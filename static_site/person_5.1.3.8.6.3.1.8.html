ELSIE LOUISE FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.6.3.1.8 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.3.1.8) ELSIE LOUISE FERRIS (twin) born March 31, 1900 DeWitt (near Syracuse) NY; married August 30, 1924 Edwin Adams Birdseye [son of Victory & Ida May (Adams) of Syracuse]] born January 29, 1897; Edwin was a WWI Navy Vet; with Underwriters Association of New York, 1922-53 in Syracuse; then in Buffalo. Born to them were: (5.1.3.8.6.3.1.8.1) ELLEN FRANCES BIRDSEYE born May 2, 1927 Syracuse NY; graduated Syracuse University College of Fine Arts 1949; fabric designer NYC; married January 15, 1955 at New York City, Telford N. Fink, Jr. [son of Telford N., of Tryone PA]

Children (2)

  • DAVID EDWIN BIRDSEYE 5.1.3.8.6.3.1.8.2
  • JOHN CLINTON BIRDSEYE 5.1.3.8.6.3.1.8.3
