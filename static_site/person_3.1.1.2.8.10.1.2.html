HARRY ELMO FERRIS

ID: 3.1.1.2.8.10.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.2.8.10.1.2) HARRY ELMO FERRIS (Photograph) born 1873 Newark NJ and died 1962 (1972?) Minneapolis MN; buried in the Roales plot, Newark; married Christiana Roales [dau of Andrew] born January 10, 1873 and died February 14, 1935; buried in the Roales plot, Newark. Harry did not have a secondary school education. He worked for a Mr. Close who owned the N.J. Pipe & Tube Company in Newark, as a bookkeeper. Having been a traveling salesman, he was impressed with the prosperity of the hotel business and invested almost exclusively in several hotels, which failed during the 1929 depression; lost his investment.
