MABEL LEONE BUSS

ID: 3.10.1.2.1.10.2.4.3.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.1.10.2.4.3.1) MABEL LEONE BUSS born August 28, 1894, Stockton, JoDaviess County IL and died November 9, 1975, DuPage Convalescence Nursing Center, Naperville, DuPage County IL; buried Morris MN. Mabel attended school and graduated from Park Rapids High School, where she learned to be a teacher; her report cards show that she received high marks. She graduated from Normal School, May 28, 1915. With her degree, she taught several years in northwestern Minnesota; taught Native Americans on the Reservation; and was remembered as one of the foremost school teachers in the county for several years. She married, June 25, 1924, at Alexandria MN, Earn Julius (1903-1973), who farmed near Morris MN.
