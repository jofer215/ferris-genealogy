HANNAH HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.3.3.3.5 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.3.3.3.5) HANNAH HOYT (#4512) born November 10, 1799 Stamford and died August 19, 1862 Darien CT; buried there Leeds Cemetery; married May 2, 1826 in Stamford, Charles Thorp Leeds (2.1.7.1.3.4) – see him for offspring (2.5.2.3.3.3.6) RUTH HOYT (#4513) born c1802 (1804) Stamford; married (June) July 2, 1857 as his 2d wife, Edson Hoyt (2.6.4.7.1.5) (#3489), of Stamford born September 12, 1801 (1805) at New Canaan.

No children recorded
