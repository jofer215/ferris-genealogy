IRVING JAY TOMPKINS

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.2.2.4 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.2.2.4) IRVING JAY TOMPKINS born September 30, 1880 Putnam Valley NY and died October 19, 1913; buried Hillside Cemetery, Peekskill NY. Obit Highland Democrat, Saturday, Oct. 25, 1913, Listed under Tompkins Corners: The funeral of the late Irving Jay Tompkins, who passed away at his home on Sunday morning, was held Wednesday at his late home. Rev. G.W. Gritman preached a most touching sermon. The numerous floral pieces and the crowded house and porch were just symbols of the high esteem and love with which 'Jay" (as he was familiarly called) was held by his friends and associates. Our deepest sympathy goes out to the widow and little Laura, who survive him, not forgetting the father and mother, Mr. and Mrs. Joshua Tompkins, five brothers, Tilden, Arthur, Augustus, Frank and Jesse, and two sisters, Mrs. Thaddus Tompkins and Mrs. Allen Gilberts. Emma J. (Miller) Tompkins was his wife and after Irving’s death, she married Archibald Barger (5.1.2.9.2.4.1.2.1). Born to them was:
