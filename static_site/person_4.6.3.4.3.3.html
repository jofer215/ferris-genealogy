MARY SCOFIELD

ID: 4.6.3.4.3.3 | Source: Mary
(4.6.3.4.3.3) MARY SCOFIELD born April 11, 1805 Stamford and died there June 10, 1836 age 31y 2m; married December 24, 1827 in Stamford, as his 1st wife, Amos Scofield [son of Jared & Dorcas (Weed) Scofield] born September 1, 1802 Stamford and died there March 7, 1867; shoemaker 1850; both buried Scofieldtown-Lockwood #4 Cemetery; after Mary’s death Amos remarried and he and Marilla Burgess had 4 kids (and a son Martin, married Louise M Weed (2.5.3.10.5.5.3); and born to Mary and Amos was:
