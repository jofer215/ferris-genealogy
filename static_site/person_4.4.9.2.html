NATHANIEL MEAD

ID: 4.4.9.2 | Source: Mary
(4.4.9.2) NATHANIEL MEAD (#332) (1728-1814) (Captain) married 1st 1759 Charity Bush (17391840); 2d Hannah Hobby Seymour (widow of David Hobby) born October 3, 1753 (a Hannah Mead wife of Nathaniel, died February 15, 1835 at age 87y; buried North Greenwich Congregational Church Cemetery).
