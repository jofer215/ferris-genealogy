FERN HESTER STEARNS

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.4.4.4.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.4.4.4.3) FERN HESTER STEARNS (Photo) born July 14, 1907 Sumner Township MI and died October 4, 1991 Lansing MI. She lived much of her life in Alma and also resided in Houghton Lake and Zephyrhills FL. She was mentioned in her father’s obituary – was of Farmington at her father’s death.

No children recorded
