BEULAH ALVERA HALSTEAD

ID: 5.1.4.1.3.2.1.4.5.1 | Source: James
(5.1.4.1.3.2.1.4.5.1) BEULAH ALVERA HALSTEAD born September 13, 1898 IA and died November 27, 1945 Storm Lake IA; married 1917 Clifford Clair Teachout [son of Morton Augustus & Rebecca Jane (Stanley) Teachout] born 1894 Coleta, Whiteside, IL and died 1951 Storm Lake; and they had 2 kids (5.1.4.1.3.2.1.4.5.2) ORLO JESS HALSTEAD born August 30, 1900 Webb, Clay, IA and died 1979 Brookings SD; WWI draft registration; married 1919 in Sioux City IA, Edna Margarett Lamster born 1895 Seward NE and died 1966 Mitchell SD; and they had 6 kids

No children recorded
