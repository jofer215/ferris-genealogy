MURRAY WHITING FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.2.1.3.10 | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.1.3.10) MURRAY WHITING FERRIS born December 15, 1850; author of The Boy on the Farm, 1916; married 1st Ada Morris; and 2d Edith {maiden name unk}; resided Orange NJ ********************** (5.1.3.2.1.*) LEONARD FERRIS (1791-1854) [Leonard does not appear to belong here, since he born 2 years before Samuel and Phebe married.] [He is not listed as a son of Samuel's in Chaplain Ferris' book.] He married 1815 Jane Losey (1790-1873). They were living in Ovid NY in 1832. Born to them was (and may be more): (5.1.3.2.1.*.1) CAROLINE FERRIS born April 17, 1819 and died June 8, 1878; married January 3, 1844 as his first wife, Jacob B. Van Doren [son of Abraham] born September 17, 1819 Somerset Co NJ and died March 3, 1903. Born to them were: (5.1.3.2.1.*.1.1) MIRANDA JANE VAN DOREN born 1844 and married Anson Morehouse (18451910). Born to them was (and may be more): (5.1.3.2.1.*.1.1.1) CORA MOREHOUSE born Linden, Michigan; married Otto B. Dagg (DAR#133410) (5.1.3.2.1.*.1.2) EVA VAN DOREN (1853-1860) (5.1.3.2.1.*.1.3) ESTELLA VAN DOREN (1855-1860) (5.1.3.2.1.*.1.4) JANE VAN DOREN (1856-1856) (5.1.3.2.1.*) PHEBE FERRIS born August 26, 1801 and died July 17, 1812; buried Old Sound Beach Cemetery (Tombstone photo) (5.1.3.2.1.*) JOHN FERRIS born November 19, 1804 and died March 27, 1806 (5.1.3.2.1.*) ANNE FERRIS born July 28, 1808 and died February 1, 1809 (5.1.3.2.1.*) EDWIN FERRIS born July 19, 1810 and died August 19, 1811 *******************
