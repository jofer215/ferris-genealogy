JAMES FERRIS

ID: 1.6.1.4.4 | Source: John
(1.6.1.4.4) JAMES FERRIS born Bedford June 23, 1780 and died there February 15, 1832; married April 10, 1810 Deborah Clark [dau of Nathan & Lydia (Holmes) Clark] born Bedford March 27, 1781 and died there December 13, 1860. He was a farmer and resided his entire lifetime on his father's farm on the Danbury Post Road, south of Bedford Village. A member a light infantry company of militia from Bedford, he held the rank of Sergeant. He was drafted in the U.S. Army in 1814 and stationed at Brooklyn to defend the New York Harbor. He and Deborah are both buried in the Ferris Cemetery, plots 1 and 2, respectfully. Born to them were:
