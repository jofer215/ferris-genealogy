BENJAMIN MEAD WEED

ID: 2.5.3.10.5.5 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.10.5.5) BENJAMIN MEAD WEED born May 14, 1791 Stamford and died there October 1, 1868; farmer 1850-68; married December 10, 1828 in Stamford, Emeline Hanford [dau of Thaddeus & Betsy (Weed) Hanford] born December 12, 1804 Stamford and died there September 15, 1890; both buried there North Street Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • MARY E WEED 2.5.3.10.5.5.2
  • LOUISE M WEED 2.5.3.10.5.5.3
