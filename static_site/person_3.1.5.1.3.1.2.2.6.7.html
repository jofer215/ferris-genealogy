LELA GRETCHEN LAWSON

ID: 3.1.5.1.3.1.2.2.6.7 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.5.1.3.1.2.2.6.7) LELA GRETCHEN LAWSON born September 11, 1905; married March 1, 1924 in Julesburg, Sedgewick, CO James Kennett Flynn [son of James & Sarah Ann (Manion) Flynn] born February 7, 1894 North Platte, Lincoln, NE and died October 11, 1965 North Platte, Lincoln, NE; buried there North Platte Cemetery; WWI Vet, 12th Engineers, Corporal, served in France, discharged May 16, 1919; switchman, Union Pacific Railroad; and they had 2 kids

No children recorded
