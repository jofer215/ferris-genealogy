CLARISSA LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.1.5.6 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.5.6) CLARISSA LOCKWOOD (#693) born December 29, 1773 Pound Ridge, Westchester, NY and died October 1854; married 1st Solomon Cox who died c1808; married 2d October 2, 1814, as his 2d wife, Captain Jesse Richards [son of Captain James & Hannah (Waring) Richards] born July 24, 1763 and died December 18, 1839 on his father’s homestead, New Canaan CT; Jesse and his 1st wife Lydia Hickok had 6 kids; and born to Clarissa and Solomon was:

Children (2)
