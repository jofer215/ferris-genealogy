JOHN FERRIS UNDERHILL

ID: 1.7.7.2.2.2.1 | Source: John
(1.7.7.2.2.2.1) JOHN FERRIS UNDERHILL born April 10, 1818 NYC and died January 7, 1892; resided Westchester NY; married October 24, 1839 at NYC Sarah Smith [dau of Ezekiel G. & Mary L. (Mott) Smith] born January 14, 1820 and died March 3, 1892. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • LAWRENCE MOTT UNDERHILL 1.7.7.2.2.2.1.3
  • JOHN BURLING UNDERHILL 1.7.7.2.2.2.1.4
