CHARLES W. CALDWELL

ID: 1.4.7.1.1.3.2 | Source: John
(1.4.7.1.1.3.2) CHARLES W. CALDWELL born April 4, 1843 Stamford CT and died May 24, 1911 Bridgeport CT; buried Roxbury Cemetery, Stamford. He was a wire drawer; Civil War Veteran – Company B, 28th Connecticut Infantry Volunteers; married Harriet Lockwood [dau of Hanford & Joseph (Blake) Lockwood] born June 15, 1850 Stamford CT and died there April 1, 1923; buried Long Ridge Union, Stamford. {Is this Hanford Lockwood (4.5.2.3.10.1) and his unnamed first wife?} Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • PERCY CALDWELL 1.4.7.1.1.3.2.1
  • ERNEST CALDWELL was buried Norwalk CT; married a Miss Scott 1.4.7.1.1.3.2.2
  • INA MAE CALDWELL 1.4.7.1.1.3.2.3
  • EDITH CALDWELL was buried at Long Ridge Union, Stamford CT. 1.4.7.1.1.3.2.4
