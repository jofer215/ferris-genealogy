LEONARD THOMPSON FERRIS Dr.,

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.7.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.7.2) LEONARD THOMPSON FERRIS Dr., born January 4, 1817 NY and died 1900; employed early in life on the farm with his father and after receiving his first education in the common schools, he graduated in the study of medicine at the medical department in the St. Louis University, in the spring of 1848, and began practicing soon afterwards. He practiced medicine in Carthage and owned a farm of 80 acres, town residences and a few other lots; married May 23, 1850 in Hillsgrove, McDonough, IL, Helen Minerva Gilchrist [dau of Charles Grandison and Minerva (Holton) Gilchrist] born October 23, 1831 Saxton River VT and died 1912.
