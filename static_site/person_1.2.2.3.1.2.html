ANNA UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.2.3.1.2 | Source: John
(1.2.2.3.1.2) ANNA UNDERHILL born July 2, 1791 Westchester Co NY; married William Smith (Willis R Smith) born New Castle, Westchester NY; they resided Cayuga NY and in 1825 Greenwich OH; she was a teacher; and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • AMELIA SMITH 1.2.2.3.1.2.4
  • WILLIAM T SMITH 1.2.2.3.1.2.5
