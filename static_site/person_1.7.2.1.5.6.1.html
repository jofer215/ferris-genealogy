CLINTON EMANUEL DEL PELA SCHIEFFELIN

ID: 1.7.2.1.5.6.1 | Source: John
(1.7.2.1.5.6.1) CLINTON EMANUEL DEL PELA SCHIEFFELIN born February 16, 1823 NYC and died April 15, 1884 East Los Angeles CA; in 1844, he traveled to Wisconsin and Illinois, where he may have mined; later tried his luck in the California gold mines; moved on to Oregon 1853 where he mined, farmed and ran a tavern; eventually moved his family to Jackson Co OR and settled on a farm; married c1842 in Tioga Co PA, Jane L.
