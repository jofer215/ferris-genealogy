OSCAR COLES FERRIS

ID: 1.4.1.11.2 | Source: John
(1.4.1.11.2) OSCAR COLES FERRIS born 1835 and died February 8, 1903 Morris Plains NJ; proprietor of Homestead, Throggs Neck; resided at 762 Madison Ave., New York City; married Ada Stuart Woodworth who died November 13, 1887 (an Ada S. Ferris was interred November 15, 1887, Green-Wood Cemetery, Brooklyn NY). Born to them were:
