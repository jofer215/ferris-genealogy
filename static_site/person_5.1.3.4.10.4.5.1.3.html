BERNICE MARIE PEARSON

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.5.1.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.5.1.3) BERNICE MARIE PEARSON (Photo) born November 16, 1905 at home in Tampico, Whiteside, IL and died March 13, 1982 at home in Prophetstown, Whiteside, IL; married December 20, 1927 in her parents home near Tampico, Ernest Casper Sandrock born October 13, 1905 at home in Prophetstown IL and was killed in a tractor accident January 7, 1955 on the Prophetstown blacktop (road); both buried at Riverside Cemetery, Prophetstown. Bernice was a housewife and later worked at Hillcrest Greenhouse and Gualrapp Farms; a Lutheran. Ernest was a farmer; a Lutheran, Rock Falls Immanuel Lutheran Church. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • CARLYLE ERNEST SANDROCK, JR., 5.1.3.4.10.4.5.1.3.1
  • CHARLOTTE LAVONNE SANDROCK 5.1.3.4.10.4.5.1.3.2
