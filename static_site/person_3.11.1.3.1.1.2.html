HARRIET CHARLOTTE ROSE

ID: 3.11.1.3.1.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.3.1.1.2) HARRIET CHARLOTTE ROSE born 1804 Manchester, Bennington, VT and died c1851 Richmond, Canada; married 1st a Mr Carr; 2d December 27, 1836 in Merrickville, Ontario, Hugh Ross [son of Alexander & Anne (Munro) Ross] born July 19, 1804 Williamstown, Ontario and died January 21, 1892 Palmerston, Ontario; and born to her and Mr Carr were:

Children (6)

  • RACHEL CARR 3.11.1.3.1.1.2.1
  • ELIZA CARR 3.11.1.3.1.1.2.2
  • EMILY JANE ROSS 3.11.1.3.1.1.2.3
  • RICHARD ROSE ROSS 3.11.1.3.1.1.2.5
  • HANNAH ROSS 3.11.1.3.1.1.2.6
  • ARMANELLA EUNICE ROSS 3.11.1.3.1.1.2.7
