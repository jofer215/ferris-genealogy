MILLICENT AMELIA SMITH

ID: 2.6.4.3.4.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.4.3.4.2) MILLICENT AMELIA SMITH born April 17, 1778 Stamford and died there May 19, 1856; buried Simsbury Cemetery; married July 27, 1803 in Stamford, Ezra Scofield [son of Samuel & Hannah (Lounsbury) Scofield] born November 27, 1754 Stamford and died there June 29, 1840; buried Simsbury Cemetery; served as Private 1775-79; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • MARY S. SCOFIELD 2.6.4.3.4.2.1
  • SAMUEL SMITH SCOFIELD 2.6.4.3.4.2.2
  • NANCY SCOFIELD 2.6.4.3.4.2.3
  • HANNAH ELIZABETH SCOFIELD 2.6.4.3.4.2.4
