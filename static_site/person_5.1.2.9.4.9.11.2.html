SELDEN CHRISTIAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.9.11.2 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.9.11.2) SELDEN CHRISTIAN born August 24, 1905 and died September 24, 1972 New Paltz Nursing Home; married Dorothy Wise [dau of Charles F. & Florence (Wise)] who died March 30, 1971 Upstate General Hospital, Syracuse; buried Hillside Cemetery. She apparently brought a son with her to the marriage. He had been employed for 10 years at the Veterans Administration Hospital in Peekskill and earlier had been a painter in the construction field, and with the New York Central Railroad police. He lived on Scofield Lane at Rock Tavern. He was survived by a stepson William O. Gilbert, Jr. Dorothy was a dressmaker in Peekskill.

No children recorded
