MARY ETTA STEARNS

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.4.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.4.2) MARY ETTA STEARNS (Photo) born June 12, 1835 (Phelps, Ontario, OH/NY) Newbury, OH and died April 11, 1906; married December 25, 1856 at her home in Newbury, Geauga, OH Joseph Henry Ditto born November 1, 1827 near Montreal, Quebec, Canada and died October 27, 1891 Geauga Co OH (Mary’s obit says Joseph died October 1892); both buried Ferris Center Cemetery, Ferris Township, Montcalm Co MI. According to Mary’s obit, “She was converted at the age of 12 years and united with the Methodist Church of which she has been a faithful member her entire life. She was a kind and loving mother and a devoted wife. Always thoughtful of others, even in her last illness, fearful lest she should tire them out. She came with her husband and son to Petoskey in 1885 (1865?) and has resided here ever since. When she learned that the end was coming she said, “It’s all right. I am ready.” Although her mind was clear only a part of the time she repeated the 23d Psalm entire. During her last few hours of consciousness she was repeating passages of scripture and urging her friends to meet her over there, almost her last words were “beautiful, beautiful,” and then she passed to her reward, fully trusting in the Savior whom she loved.” Born to them were:
