EMMA JANE FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.4.1.6 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.4.1.6) EMMA JANE FERRIS (tombstone photo) born March 10, 1850 Sound Beach CT and died March 1, 1885 Norton CT; married March 8 (18), 1874 in Sound Beach, as his 2d of 3 wives, Henry Mortimer Dayton [son of Stephen & Jane Anna (Newman) Dayton] born August 26, 1845 Greenwich and died November 11, 1922 Darien CT; buried Spring Grove Cemetery, Darien.
