ALTHEA FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.2.2.1.2.3.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.2.2.1.2.3.2) ALTHEA FERRIS born October 23, 1949 Greenwich CT; has a degree in Music Education from Ithaca College NY; a singer and performs in musicals in the Avon CT theater groups; there was an Althea Vanderpoel on the Board of the Simsbury Light Opera Company and a vocal teacher at Oliver Ellsworth School in Windsor CT – presume this same lady; married July 14, 1979 at Greenwich, Richard E.
