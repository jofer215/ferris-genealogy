JOAN FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.5.8.1.2.5 | Source: John
(1.4.7.5.8.1.2.5) JOAN FERRIS born July 2, 1926 Newton MA; Smith College 1947; B.A. U.Wisconsin 1950; married February 17, 1950 Charles Edward Curran born May 27, 1923 North Adams MA; A.B. Harvard 1946; M.A. U. New Hampshire 1950; M.B.A. Harvard 1951; Ph.D. Harvard 1960; President, Kansas City Association of Trusts & Foundations; resides Kansas City MO. Joan is a Certified Genealogist and currently, Assistant Secretary of the New England Historic Genealogical Society; author of genealogy related books; and born to Joan and Charles were:
