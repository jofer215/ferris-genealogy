MARY FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.2.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.2.3) MARY FERRIS born November 6, 1809 and died January 5/6, 1900; married July 13, 1835 in Greenwich, William Henry Keeler born November 17, 1807 Norwalk CT and died January 19, 1862 [son of David and Mary (Burley) Keeler]; both buried in the cemetery of First Congregational Church, Old Greenwich.
