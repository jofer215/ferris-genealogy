JAMES FERRIS PALMER

ID: 2.1.1.3.5.1 | Source: Peter
(2.1.1.3.5.1) JAMES FERRIS PALMER born March 3, 1780 Greenwich and died April 14, 1842 Stamford. [Will dated April 14, 1842 and probate May 14, 1842]; married 1 st April 17, 1811 Catherine Davenport born February 24, 1783 and died August 13, 1812, a month after giving birth to her son, and buried in the North Stamford Cemetery; married 2d December 15, 1822 in Darien CT, Sally B. Scofield born October 28, 1786 Stamford and died there April 3, 1863 and buried at the same cemetery. Born to James and Catherine was:
