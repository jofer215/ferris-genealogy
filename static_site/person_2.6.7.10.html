ISAAC KNAPP

ID: 2.6.7.10 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.10) ISAAC KNAPP (8M0J-WF) born April 14, 1750 probably Stamford and died December 3, 1833 Bethany, Genesee, NY; a Rev. War Veteran serving 1775-1776-1777; a farmer and a Congregationalist; married c1770/71 in Litchfield CT, Mary Gould, who died sometime during the Rev. War. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • MARY KNAPP ‘Polly’ born 1772 Clarkson, Monroe, NY and died there 1847; married 2.6.7.10.1
  • ISAAC KNAPP, JR., 2.6.7.10.2
