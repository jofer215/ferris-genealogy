PERMELIA FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.3.1.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.3.1.1.2) PERMELIA FERRIS born August 23, 1809 Clinton Co NY and died October 18, 1896 Mishawaka IN; one of the last surviving members of the First Baptist Church of Penn Township; married December 7, 1837 in St. Joseph Co IN, Joseph Moore and they had a child; resided Pleasant Valley IN

No children recorded
