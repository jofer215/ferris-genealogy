CECIL ALLAN UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.6.2.4.2.4.5.3 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.4.2.4.5.3) CECIL ALLAN UNDERHILL born January 4, 1923 South Wayne, Lafayette, WI and died September 8, 1988 Des Moines IA; buried Luther Valley Cemetey, Beloit WI; WWII Army Air Force Vet; married 1st October 9, 1952 in Belvidere IL, Lyla Chalmers and they had a child; married 2d Margaret Lane

No children recorded
