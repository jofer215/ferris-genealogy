ORRIN HOLT HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.7.1.6.2.3.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.7.1.6.2.3.1) ORRIN HOLT HOYT born May 24, 1838 NY and died May 24, 1918 Stamford; buried Emmanuel Chapel Cemetery, Stamford; teaching in Hartford County in 1867; in 1880 boarding with Sarah E. Rosborough, age 49; buried together sharing a headstone

No children recorded
