NATHANIEL JAMES FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.6.4.5.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.4.5.1) NATHANIEL JAMES FERRIS born July 13, 1913 New York City; graduated from Cornell University; associated with Newport News Shipbuilding and Dry Dock Company as staff supervisor during WWII, where he supervised construction of many naval vessels including the aircraft carrier, Midway; member of the firm of T.E. Ferris & Sons, Naval Architects and Marine Engineers. He married April 15, 1942 Ruth Wills of Hampton VA and born to them were:
