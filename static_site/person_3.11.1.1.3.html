ABRAHAM NEWMAN

ID: 3.11.1.1.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3) ABRAHAM NEWMAN born January 13/14, 1759 Stafford CT and died June 19, 1841 Plessis, Jefferson, NY; buried there Plessis Brookside Cemetery; Rev. War Vet, Private in the New York Continental; early pioneer of Alexandria Township, Jefferson Co NY; married April 10, 1788 in Hillsdale, Columbia, NY Lucinda Crippen [dau of Joseph & Tabetha (Rowley) Crippen] born April 11, 1769 Sharon CT and died March 2, 1844 Alexandria Township, Jefferson, NY; buried with Abraham; and born to them were:
