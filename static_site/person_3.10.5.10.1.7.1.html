WILLIAM HOWARD PECK

ID: 3.10.5.10.1.7.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.5.10.1.7.1) WILLIAM HOWARD PECK born May 18, 1821 Savannah GA and died January 27, 1852 Brooklyn NY; married December 1, 1841 Laura C. Kinne, of Port Jefferson NY (3.10.5.10.1.7.2) THEODOSIA PECK (Theodosia L) born April 19, 1823 Flushing NY; living Mt. Carmel, Wabash, IL 1860; and died there February 2, 1883; buried there Old Rosehill Cemetery; married March 31, 1842 William S Habberton [son of William & Mary Anne (Tufts) Habberton] born c1810 England, of Brooklyn NY, and died January 15, 1865 Mt Carmel; and born to them were:

No children recorded
