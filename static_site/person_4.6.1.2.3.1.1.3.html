MARY ELLEN COFFIN

ID: 4.6.1.2.3.1.1.3 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.3.1.1.3) MARY ELLEN COFFIN (#3334) born July 22, 1855 Batavia IL; married August 14, 1877 Rev. John William Bradshaw [son of Rev. John & Sarah F. (Williams) Bradshaw] born July 7, 1849 Crown Point NY. John was a Congregational minister at Batavia IL, Rochester MN and in 1888, Galesburg IL. Born to them were: (4.6.1.2.3.1.1.3.1) JOHN WILLIAM BRADSHAW (#3893) born June 14, 1878 Batavia IL

Children (2)

  • MARY LOCKWOOD BRADSHAW 4.6.1.2.3.1.1.3.2
  • CHARLES PORTER BRADSHAW 4.6.1.2.3.1.1.3.3
