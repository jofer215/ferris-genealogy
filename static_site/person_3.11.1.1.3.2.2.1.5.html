RODOLPHUS FREEMONT NEWMAN

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.1.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.1.5) RODOLPHUS FREEMONT NEWMAN born March 16, 1882 Dubuque IA and died March 7, 1943 Charlevoix MI; buried there Brookside Cemetery; WWI Army Veteran; veterinarian and horse trainer; married September 19, 1908 in Maple City MI, Nita Mary Crain [dau of Elmer & Adela (Wagner) Crain] born July 18, 1890 Bluffton, Wells, IN and died September 25, 1986 Grandvue, East Jordan, MI; buried with Rod; postmistress; and born to them were:
