CHARLES H. WEED

ID: 2.3.3.2.3.1.1 | Source: Peter
(2.3.3.2.3.1.1) CHARLES H. WEED born March 11, 1835 New Canaan CT and died 1884; married February 27, 1859 Mary Olmstead – there was a Private Charles H Weed enlisted September 3, 1961 as a Private in Company D, 6th Regiment Connecticut Volunteers; wounded October 29, 1862 Pocotaligo SC; reenlisted veteran January 4, 1864, wounded May 10, 1864 Chester Station VA, transferred to Company A, 14th Regiment VRC January 12, 1865; discharged on disability August 5, 1865 – not sure this is same guy
