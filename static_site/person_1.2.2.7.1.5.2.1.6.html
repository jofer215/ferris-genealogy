CHARLES HOOK TOMPKINS

ID: 1.2.2.7.1.5.2.1.6 | Source: John
(1.2.2.7.1.5.2.1.6) CHARLES HOOK TOMPKINS born November 30, 1883 Baltimore MD and died December 12, 1956 Washington, D.C.; married November 30, 1906 in Washington, Lida Roberts Tompkins [dau of Francis Meade & Emma Cornelia (Henry) Tompkins] born October 13, 1882 Fauquier Co VA and died January 28, 1953 Washington, D.C.; and born to them were: (1.2.2.7.1.5.2.1.6.1) FRANCIS MEADE TOMPKINS II born May 1, 1909 Washington, D.C. and died June 30, 1961; married 1st May 1931 Adair Childress [dau of John William & Rita Worthington (Ridgely) Childress] born December 16, 1910; 2d 1939 name unk; 3d 1954 name unk
