ISRAEL LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.5.8.3 | Source: Mary
(4.5.8.3) ISRAEL LOCKWOOD (#231) born November 28, 1755 Greenwich; enlisted May 12, 1775 in Captain Thomas Hobby’s company of Colonel Waterbury’s Regiment; went up North River to Albany, thence to Ticonderoga and constructed batteaux and proceeded down to Lake Champlain and was discharged December 8, 1775. He resided in New York City, September 28, 1832 and was 77 years old then.

No children recorded
