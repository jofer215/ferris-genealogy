CLARA MAUDE VALLY

ID: 1.1.4.4.1.3.1.3 | Source: John
(1.1.4.4.1.3.1.3) CLARA MAUDE VALLY born 1869/71 WI; married c1892 Silas Arthur Broeffle [son of Jacob Ira & Fannie (Bell) Broeffle] born September 1869 Canada. He came with his parents to Cumberland, Barron, WI 1883; his date and place of death are unknown. At the death of his mother in 1925, he was a resident of Ladysmith, Rusk County WI. Silas was listed as a farmer in the 1900 and 1910 census, but as a general laborer in the 1920 census. Born to them were:
