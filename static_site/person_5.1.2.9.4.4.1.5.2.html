HART GEORGE TOMPKINS

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.1.5.2 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.1.5.2) HART GEORGE TOMPKINS born January 26, 1880 (death records shows birth of 1882-it is wrong) and died July 23, 1958 Brewster NY; resided Brewster NY 1910 & 1934; married Jessie Mae Odell [dau of Tunis & Hannah Elizabeth (Head) born January 14, 1888 Baldwin Place NY and died May 27, 1957 Brewster NY; both buried Shrub Oak Methodist Cemetery, Shrub Oak NY. Obituary, The Brewster Times: Mrs. Jessie Mae Tompkins, died at her home on Starr Ridge road in Southeast on Monday, May 27, 1957, in her 69th year. She was the daughter of Tunis and Hannah Head Odell and born Jan. 14, 1888, in Baldwin Place. When a young woman she was united in marriage with Mr. Tompkins and they had resided on Starr Ridge road for the past 35 years. Surviving besides her husband are four daughters, Mrs. Ernest Adams of Salem Center, Mrs. Henry Tompkins of Carmel, Mrs. Robert Zaniboni of Hartsdale and Miss June Tompkins of Brewster; and four sons, George William of Dover Plains, Hart, Jr. and Robert of Patterson, Ernest and James Tompkins both of Brewster. She also leaves two sisters, Mrs. William Blenis and Mrs. James Cargain, both of Baldwin Place; and three brothers, Herbert Odell of Carmel, Howard of Patterson and Roy Odell of Baldwin Place. Her survivors also include 23 grandchildren. Interment was made in the Shrub Oak cemetery. Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, Thursday, July 24, 1958: Hart George Tompkins, who operated a farm on Starr Ridge in Southeast for 35 years, died at his home there on Wednesday, July 23, 1958. He was 76. The deceased was a son of Oscar and Lucy Christian Tompkins and born in Peekskill Hollow Putnam Valley, Jan. 26, 1882. His wife, the former Jessie Odell, died about a year ago. Surviving are nine children, George of Wassaic; Mrs. Pearl Adams of Brewster; Miss Lucy Tompkins of Towners; Hart and Robert, both of Patterson; Ernest of Brewster; Mrs. Dorothy Zaniboni of Hartsdale; James of Brewster and Miss June Tompkins of Brewster. He also leaves 20 grandchildren. Interment will be made in the Methodist cemetery in Shrub Oak. Born to them were:
