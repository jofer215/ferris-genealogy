EMMA RUNDLE

ID: 3.1.5.1.3.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.5.1.3.1.2) EMMA RUNDLE born October 24, 1808 Boylestown NY and died c1885 Hamilton County IA, buried Lawn Hill Cemetery, Stanhope IA; married 1st February 25, 1825 at Conneautville, Crawford, PA, Joel W. Stuart (Stewart) born September 22, 1803 New York and died 1843 Kendall Co IL; Emma married 2d c1845 in Kendall Co IL, widower John Robinson who died September 1847; and born to Emma and Joel were:

Children (6)
