NATHANIEL SYKES BACHMAN

ID: 5.1.3.4.6.1.1.8.5.3.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.6.1.1.8.5.3.4) NATHANIEL SYKES BACHMAN born January 6, 1969 Greensburg PA; UPS employee; Nathaniel Sikes "Nate" Bachman, 38, of Mt. Lebanon, died Thursday, Jan. 25, 2007. He was born Jan. 6, 1969, in Greensburg, the beloved son of Barbara Bachman, of Mt. Lebanon, and the late Joseph A. Bachman. Nate was a 1987 graduate of Mt. Lebanon High School and a graduate of the University of Pittsburgh. Also surviving are sisters Ann Ferris Bachman, of Boca Raton, Fla., and Sarah Stewart Bachman (Jason Bram), of Manhattan, New York City; brother Frederick Huff Bachman (Patricia Ann), of Pittsburgh, and niece Emily B. Bram. A memorial service will be at 1:30 p.m. Saturday in St. Paul Episcopal Church, 1066 Washington Road, Mt. Lebanon. Arrangements by WILLIAM SLATER II FUNERAL SERVICE, Scott. Contributions may be made to the St. Paul Episcopal Church Memorial Fund.
