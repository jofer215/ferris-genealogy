HERBERT JABEZ UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.3.5.1.3.7 | Source: John
(1.2.3.3.5.1.3.7) HERBERT JABEZ UNDERHILL born December 8, 1862 MI and died May 27, 1948 East Lansing, Ingham, MI; 1893 homesteaded farm at Bellaire; married September 11, 1884 in Lisbon MI, Clarissa Ezelia Holmes [dau of Clark W & Lydia M] born July 12, 1863 Lisbon and died November 28, 1941 Bellaire; both buried Lake View Cemetery, Bellaire, Antrim, MI; and they had 6 kids, 4 of which were:

Children (4)

  • ALFRED CLARK UNDERHILL 1.2.3.3.5.1.3.7.2
  • ALICE MADGE UNDERHILL 1.2.3.3.5.1.3.7.3
  • ALTA MARJORIE UNDERHILL 1.2.3.3.5.1.3.7.4
