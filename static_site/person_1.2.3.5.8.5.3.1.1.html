HOWARD HEWLETT CLARK

ID: 1.2.3.5.8.5.3.1.1 | Source: John
(1.2.3.5.8.5.3.1.1) HOWARD HEWLETT CLARK (Photo) born April 23, 1903 and died January 21, 1983; buried Beechwood Cemetery, Ottawa, Ontario; attended Trinity University College in Toronto, and was awarded his degree on Doctor Divinity 1945; and several other degrees between 1965-66; a native of McLeod, Alberta, he served as an Assistant Curate to the Church of Christ Cathedral in Ottawa, Ontario 1932; Rector 1939-53; Dean of Ottaw, Ontario 1945-53; Bishop of Edmonton, Alberta 1953-59; and Archibishop and Metropolitan of Rupert’s Land 1961-69; made a Companion of the Order of Canada 1970; was at one time the Primate of the Anglican Church of Canada

No children recorded
