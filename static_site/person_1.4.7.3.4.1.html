FANNY ROSALIE PELL

ID: 1.4.7.3.4.1 | Source: John
(1.4.7.3.4.1) FANNY ROSALIE PELL born 1848 Brattleboro VT and died January 2, 1892 Boston MA; married April 23, 1867 at Grace Church NY Frank Wright (18441903) [his mother was Martha Coffin Wright, active in women’s rights and abolition movement – her great-grandson has written a book about her, A Very Dangerous Woman: Martha Wright and Women’s Rights; they resided first in Auburn NY, later in Europe, Florida and NY; they were separated by the 1880s when she reportedly had an affair with Charles Longfellow, the poet’s son, which led relatives to call her “the scarlet woman of Boston”, as per her g.grandson, Jim.
