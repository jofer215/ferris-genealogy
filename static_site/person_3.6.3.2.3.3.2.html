HENRY HULBERT KNAPP

ID: 3.6.3.2.3.3.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.3.3.2) HENRY HULBERT KNAPP born September 14, 1845 Cold Springs NY; married July 25, 1867 in Springfield IL, Fanny Waggoner [dau of Henry & Esther (Sherman) Waggoner]. Henry served with the 7th Illinois Cavalry during the Civil War and apparently lived in Monmouth, Warren, IL.
