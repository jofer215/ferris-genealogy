HANNAH I UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.3.10 | Source: John
(1.2.5.3.10) HANNAH I UNDERHILL born July 15, 1769 and died 1847; married as his 1st wife, James Quinby [son of Aaron & Elizabeth (Cornell) Quinby] born October 12, 1751 and died in Westchester Co NY; his 2d wife was Anna Underhill (1.2.5.4.8) – see her for their offspring; Hannah apparently married 1818 Isaac Mekeel (1756-1826); and born to Hannah and James were: (1.2.5.3.10.1) MOSES QUINBY (1797-)

Children (3)

  • SARAH QUINBY 1.2.5.3.10.2
  • ELIZABETH CORNELL QUINBY 1.2.5.3.10.3
  • SUSAN MEKEEL 1.2.5.3.10.4
