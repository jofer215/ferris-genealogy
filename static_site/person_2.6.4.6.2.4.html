DEBORAH MILLS

ID: 2.6.4.6.2.4 | Source: Peter
(2.6.4.6.2.4) DEBORAH MILLS born September 9, 1798 Stamford and died there April 23, 1873; buried Roxbury Cemetery, Stamford; married August 29, 1830 in Stamford, Michael Welch born 1805 Ireland and died January 2, 1876 Stamford; buried with Deborah; 1850 agent; and born to them were: (2.6.4.6.2.4.1) MARY POWERS WELCH born 1832 Stamford and died there 1908; buried Roxbury Cemetery; married December 4, 1851 in Stamford, Lorenzo Lockwood (4.6.3.1.1.1.2) – see him for offspring

Children (1)
