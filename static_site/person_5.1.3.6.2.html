MARY LOCKWOOD FERRIS “Polly” born November 12, 1777 and died 5 May 1842;

ID: 5.1.3.6.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.6.2) MARY LOCKWOOD FERRIS “Polly” born November 12, 1777 and died 5 May 1842; married Jeduthan FERRIS, Jr. (5.1.3.4.2); see him for offspring; her will dated April 25, 1842, probated May 28, 1842, all to her grandchildren, the heirs of William H and Mary Keller (5.1.3.4.2.3), viz: Phebe Ann Keeler, Josephine Keeler, and Mariana Keeler and all the children that said William H and Mary Keeler may have hereafter have.

No children recorded
