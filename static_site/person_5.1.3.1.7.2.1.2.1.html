ALBERT H FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.1.2.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.1.2.1) ALBERT H FERRIS born June 26, 1862 West Point, Columbia. WI and died April 18, 1911 at his home in Exeter MO; married October 8, 1902 Etta May Ferris (5.1.3.1.7.2.2.2.) – see her for offspring; both buried Munn Cemetery, Newbury OH; After their marriage, they moved to Wagoner OK and lived there for one year, then moved to Exeter MO, and purchased a farm, living there until he died.

No children recorded
