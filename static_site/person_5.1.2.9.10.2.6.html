CORNELIA J. TRAVIS

ID: 5.1.2.9.10.2.6 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.10.2.6) CORNELIA J. TRAVIS (c1862-) married Charles Emerson Pierce [son of Samuel & Rachel] born 1862 and died August 6, 1931. Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, Aug. 14, 1931: C. Emerson Pierce, who had been in failing health for the past two years, passed away at the home of his daughter, at Adams Corners, on Thursday, Aug. 6, 1931. He was a member of Putnam Valley Grange, the Canopus M.E. Church, where he was a steward and was also superintendent of the Sunday school for twenty years. The funeral service was held at the home of his daughter, Mrs. Roberta Perry, on Sunday afternoon at 2:30 and was one of the largest funerals ever held Putnam Valley. Three pastors were present, Rev. Mr. Benedict, of Pleasantville; Rev. Yoxtheimer, of Pennsylvania, and Rev. Eugene Crabb, pastor of the Putnam Valley charge, each minister taking a part in the service. Two hymns, "In the Garden", and "Face to Face," were sung by Mrs. B.C. Edwards, Mrs. Leander Currey, Mrs. Edward Perry and Mrs. P.E. Schmittman. There were many beautiful floral offerings. Interment was made at Adams Corners with the impressive Grange service at the grave, beside his wife who passed away a few years ago. Mr. Pierce is survived by one son, Cyrus Pierce and one daughter, Mrs. R. Perry, and one grandson, Carlton Perry. Born to them were:
