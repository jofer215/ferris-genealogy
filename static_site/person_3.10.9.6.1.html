ANN PECK

ID: 3.10.9.6.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.9.6.1) ANN PECK born April 1, 1785 Bedford NY and died there April 10, 1812; married c1804 in Pound Ridge NY, Stephen Scofield [son of Enos & Amy] born April 1792 in Pound Ridge NY and died March 9, 1855 Stamford; was of Bedford when married; buried Stillwater Road Cemetery #2, Stamford; after Ann’s early death, Stephen married Betsey Brown and they had 3 kids; and born to Ann and Stephen was:
