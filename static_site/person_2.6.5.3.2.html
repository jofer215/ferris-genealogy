JONAS CLOCK

ID: 2.6.5.3.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.5.3.2) JONAS CLOCK born September 9, 1756 Stamford and died May 20, 1810 Darien CT; served as a Private in Jonathan Bell’s Company August 13, 1776 and January 8, 1777; married September 16, 1784 in Stamford, as her 2d husband, Sarah Weed [sister of Israel (2.5.3.10.1.4)] born October 18, 1757 Stamford and died November 26, 1809 Darien; both buried there Noroton River Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (2)

  • NANCY CLOCK 2.6.5.3.2.1
  • JACOB WEED CLOCK 2.6.5.3.2.3
