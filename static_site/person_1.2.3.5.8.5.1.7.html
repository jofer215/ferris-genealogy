JAMES MORROW SLAYTER

ID: 1.2.3.5.8.5.1.7 | Source: John
(1.2.3.5.8.5.1.7) JAMES MORROW SLAYTER born September 9, 1876 and died 1945; a lawyer until 1904; then a Royal Canadian Artillery Captain; married October 1900 in Seneca Falls NY, Florence Wells Lewis and they had 3 kids. Note: Florence Lewis was a half-sister of Charles Keeler Lewis, son of Jane (Clark) and Selah Lewis; Jane Clark Lewis was the daughter of Nancy (Clark) and Charles Baldwin Keeler; Nancy Clark was a sister of Dr John S Clark (above) who married Frances Wheeler.

No children recorded
