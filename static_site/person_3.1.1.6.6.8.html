RHODA WHEELER FERRIS

ID: 3.1.1.6.6.8 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.6.8) RHODA WHEELER FERRIS born August 5/6, 1825 and died March 23, 1906; married May 17, 1866 at Columbus IN, William Herod born March 31, 1801 Bourbon Co KY and died October 20, 1871 Columbus IN; buried there Columbus City Cemetery, Columbus; this was apparently his 2d marriage since he had a son born 1835; he completed his preparatory studies and studied law, was admitted to the bar in Bracken Co KY, later moved to Columbus IN, admitted to the bar in Bartholomew Co 1825 and began practice in Columbus IN; member of the State house of representatives in 1829,1830, and 1844; served in the State senate 1831-34, 1845, and 1846; elected prosecuting attorney of Batholomew Co and served 1833-37 when he resigned; elected as a Whig to the 24th Congress to fill the vacancy caused by the death of George L Kinnard; reelected to the 25th Congress and served from January 25, 1837 to March 3, 1839; unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1838; resumed his law practice in Columbus, clerk of the circuit court of Bartholomew Co 1853; became a Republican upon the formation of that party; engaged in the law practice until his death; and born to them was:
