MARY HOLMES

ID: 5.2.3.2.4.5 | Source: James
(5.2.3.2.4.5) MARY HOLMES born October 28, 1813 Stamford CT and died there March 20, 1901; married March 11, 1840 in Stamford, as his 2d wife, James Bell Scofield (2.1.5.6.1.4.5); both buried there Woodland Cemetery – see him for offspring
