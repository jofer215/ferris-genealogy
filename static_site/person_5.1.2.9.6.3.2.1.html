JOHN FRANKLAND CHAPMAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.6.3.2.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.6.3.2.1) JOHN FRANKLAND CHAPMAN born April 18, 1871 and died June 9/12, 1952; buried Hillside Cemetery, Peekskill; married Addie Rysedorph [dau of Lyman & Emeline (Winchester)] (1878-1958). They lived at 807 Main Street, the same house where John born. He attended local schools and like most youths of the time, he went to work in the Peekskill foundries and soon learned the trade of making patterns. Later, he worked for the Standard Gas Equipment Company in NJ. Died one day short of their 56th wedding anniversary. He was a member of the St. Paul’s Methodist Church and the Cortlandt Lodge No. 6 IOOF. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • HAROLD EDGAR CHAPMAN 5.1.2.9.6.3.2.1.1
  • LILLIAN GRACE CHAPMAN “Dot” born November 15, 1913 Peekskill and died 5.1.2.9.6.3.2.1.2
