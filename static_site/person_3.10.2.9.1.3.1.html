MAJOR LINKLETTER

ID: 3.10.2.9.1.3.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.2.9.1.3.1) MAJOR LINKLETTER born 1855 PEI and died there; married there Ellen Jane Stewart [dau of Donald] born 1862 PEI and died there 1935; when Major was 25y old, he went to Colorado to work in a lumber camp; he was only there a few months when his father broke his leg, so he packed up and went home to help plant the crops; and born to them were:
