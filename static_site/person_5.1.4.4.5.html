PETER JESSUP

ID: 5.1.4.4.5 | Source: James
(5.1.4.4.5) PETER JESSUP (JESSOP) was baptized August 21, 1763, St. Johns Episcopal Church, Stamford (born c1777 Greenwich) and died January 7, 1802 by drowning on his way home from New York City; married c1794 Mary (Polly) Ferris (5.1.3.2.5). Born to them were:
