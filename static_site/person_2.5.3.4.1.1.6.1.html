OLIVIA GRIFFITH HOYT

ID: 2.5.3.4.1.1.6.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.4.1.1.6.1) OLIVIA GRIFFITH HOYT born c1833 and died May 15, 1912 San Diego CA; married three times: 1st John Hanby Scott (1828-1868) who was an importer of Sheffield Table and Pocket cutlery; married 2d Dr. Ranney; married 3d Dr. J. Whitney Bates of San Diego CA who died August 20, 1912. Born to Olivia and John were:

Children (2)

  • FLORENCE SCOTT 2.5.3.4.1.1.6.1.2
