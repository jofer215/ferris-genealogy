SARAH MEAD

ID: 2.6.5.2.1 | Source: Peter
(2.6.5.2.1) SARAH MEAD born November 1, 1756 Stamford and died there November 28, 1777; married January 3, 1776 at Stamford, Richard Mead [son of Amos & Ruth (Bush) Mead] born September 5, 1753 Stamford (Greenwich) and died April 20, 1826, probably also at Stamford (Greenwich).
