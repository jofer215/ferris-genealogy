HELEN ELIZABETH KEELER

ID: 2.5.2.3.4.2.3.1.1.2.5 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.3.4.2.3.1.1.2.5) HELEN ELIZABETH KEELER born January 2, 1909 Ridgefield CT and died there June 1990; married August 10, 1933 Oliver Kallos born April 7, 1907 Bridgeport, Fairfield, CT and died January 26, 1995 Ridgefield; both buried there Ridgefield Cemetery; he was a picture frame manufacturer with a shop in Wilton CT
