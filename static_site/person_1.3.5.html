SAMUEL FERRIS, JR.,

ID: 1.3.5 | Source: John
(1.3.5) SAMUEL FERRIS, JR., married April 9, 1763 at Newtown, Fairfield, CT Phebe Beers; According to an article from The New York Post Boy “Phebe eloped from her husband”. Note that this Samuel and Samuel (1.3.4.1) are the same – another conflict!

No children recorded
