LEANDER FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.1.8.1 | Source: John
(1.4.7.1.8.1) LEANDER FERRIS born May 2, 1831 Westchester Co NY and died July 5, 1904; buried New Albin Cemetery, New Albin, Allamakee, IA; removed to Iowa 1857; was Justice of the Peace for 18 years and held different township offices; enlisted in Company F, 6th Iowa Cav 1864 and served until end of Civil War; married 1st July 5, 1853 Caroline Jean McDonald born October 28, 1831 and died August 1, 1869 from childbirth; buried Sand Cove Cemetery, Allamakee Co IA; married 2d Annie M. Callahan. Born to Leander and Caroline was:
