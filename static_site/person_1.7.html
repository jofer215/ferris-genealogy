PHEBE FERRIS

ID: 1.7 | Source: John
(1.7) PHEBE FERRIS (PHOEBE?) born c1667 (1677?) (1680?) and died 1720-23. [mentioned in her father’s will]; married June 11, 1700 at Westchester NY, Edward Burling (Edward Carpenter Burling) [son of Edward & Grace (Norrington) Burling] born November 4, 1674 Barking, Essex, England and died May 26, 1749, Flushing NY; member of the Society of Friends Hempstead Meeting, Hempstead NY. One source has Phebe as the 1st of 4 wives for Edward. Born to them were:
