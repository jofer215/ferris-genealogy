NINA ELIZABETH NINK

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.4.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.4.3) NINA ELIZABETH NINK (Photos) born August 22, 1909 Grand Traverse Co MI and died April 11, 2002 Traverse City MI; telephone operator for Michigan Bell; housewife; married August 22, 1928 in Traverse City, John K Leonard [son of Kostonous & Mabel (Katehatus) Leonardis] born July 15, 1898 Stera, Greece and died January 17, 1995 Traverse City MI; both buried there Grand Traverse Memorial Gardens; waiter, co-owner of Pete’s Café in Traverse City; and born to them were: (3.11.1.1.3.2.2.7.4.3.1) ROBERT JOHN LEONARD (1929-2003)
