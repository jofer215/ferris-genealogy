RAYMOND RUMSEY

ID: 1.2.2.2.3.1.6.6.5 | Source: John
(1.2.2.2.3.1.6.6.5) RAYMOND RUMSEY born May 30, 1889 Brady Township, Saginaw Co MI and died January 27, 1963 Maple Rapids, Clinton, MI; married 1st 1916 in Clare Co MI, Ethel Elizabeth Freed born February 20, 1891 Clare MI and died 1929 Maple Rapids; 2d 1934 Marjorie Louisa Smith born March 9, 1906 Clinton Co MI and died 1984; all buried Ridge Road Cemetery, Saginaw; offspring from both marriages

No children recorded
