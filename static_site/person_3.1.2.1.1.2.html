MARGARET BETHANA FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.1.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.1.2) MARGARET BETHANA FERRIS born January 22, 1789 Unadilla NY (March 18, 1789 Duanesburg, Schenectady, NY) and died August 20, 1867; married February 10, 1807 at Burlington PA, Jesse Mansfield Beach [her step-brother, son of Timothy and Abigail (Bennett) Beach] born February 3, 1788 Sidney (Unadilla) NY and died May 26, 1871 Burlington PA; both buried at Hilton Cemetery, Troy, Bradford, PA; resided in Troy Township, Bradford Co PA; and born to them were:
