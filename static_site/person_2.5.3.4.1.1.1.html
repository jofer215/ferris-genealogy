HENRY EDWIN HOYT

ID: 2.5.3.4.1.1.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.4.1.1.1) HENRY EDWIN HOYT (#4629) born October 26, 1791; 1871 living in Illinois or Minnesota; married Julia Richards; he lived in New York City 1850 and was a jeweler on Cherry Street. Julia was deaf.

No children recorded
