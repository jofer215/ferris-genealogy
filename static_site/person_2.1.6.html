ABIGAIL FERRIS

ID: 2.1.6 | Source: Peter
(2.1.6) ABIGAIL FERRIS born June 13, 1701 {LDS says April 13, 1701} Stamford CT and died there April 12, 1737; buried with her husband at North Stamford Cemetery, Stamford (Photo); married April 15, 1725 at Stamford, as his 1st wife, John Penoyer, Sr., [son of Thomas & Lidia (Lydia) (Knap) Penoyer] born May 26, 1698 Stamford and died there1775. Born to Abigail and John were:

Children (4)

  • JOHN PENOYER, Jr., 2.1.6.1
  • ABIGAIL PENOYER 2.1.6.2
  • JONATHAN PENOYER 2.1.6.3
  • JACOB PENOYER 2.1.6.5
