ISAAC FERRIS EARLE

ID: 3.1.1.6.3.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.3.1) ISAAC FERRIS EARLE born June 17, 1793 Stamford CT and died June 30, 1841; buried at Fern Bank, near Lawrenceburg IN; went west and settled in the vicinity of Cincinnati, where many of his Ferris relatives were living; married October 4, 1832 Abby Carpenter; she married second after Isaac's death, a Mr. Yates and was living Fern Bank 1900; she died 1906 age 92y; and born to Isaac and Abby were:
