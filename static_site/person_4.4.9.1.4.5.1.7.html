JULIAN HANFORD OLMSTEAD, Rev.,

ID: 4.4.9.1.4.5.1.7 | Source: Mary
(4.4.9.1.4.5.1.7) JULIAN HANFORD OLMSTEAD, Rev., born August 25, 1868 Walton NY and died July 26, 1934 Perris CA; graduated from Walton High School 1889; Amherst College 1893; Andover Technical Seminary 1896; and after spending some months in foreign travel, he settled at Tyndall SD where he was Pastor of Congregational Churches at Tyndall and Bon Homme, 1897-1903; Milford IA 1903-06; Clarion IA 1906-12; Homer NY 1911-23; Rapid City SD 1923; ordained (Congregational) at Tyndall, October 26, 1899; married November 30, 1899 at Egan SD, Mabel Jesse Robinson (Belle) [dau of Richard T & Emma Frances (Fisher) Robinson] born August 31, 1871 Chelsea MA; she graduated from Mitchell SD high school and the State Normal School at Madison SD; taught school, mostly primary grades, at Madison, Chamberlain and Tyndall SD; and born to them was:
