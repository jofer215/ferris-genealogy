CHARLES HOOK TOMPKINS

ID: 1.2.2.7.1.5.2.1.6.3 | Source: John

Tompkins Endowment Fund created in 1958 the Charles H. Tompkins Co and gifts in in honor of Charles H. Tompkins, Honorary D. Engineer, 1946, the income to provide critical support to a highly visible engineering project each year
