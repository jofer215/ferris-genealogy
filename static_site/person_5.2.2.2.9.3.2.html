HANNAH MARIA BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.2.9.3.2 | Source: James
(5.2.2.2.9.3.2) HANNAH MARIA BUXTON born January 10, 1835 Stamford CT and died there December 28, 1909; buried Roxbury Rural Cemetery; married April 24, 1865 in Stamford, as his 2d wife, Charles Young Baldwin [son of Isaac A. & Elizabeth] born November 13, 1828 Bloomfield NJ and died December 7, 1914 Stamford; buried Roxbury Rural Cemetery. Charles was a carpenter and builder. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • CLARA LOUISE BALDWIN 5.2.2.2.9.3.2.1
  • WILLIAM A. BALDWIN 5.2.2.2.9.3.2.2
