PHEBE BURLING

ID: 1.7.3 | Source: John
(1.7.3) PHEBE BURLING born October 24, 1705 NYC and died after 1767; member of the Society of Friends Hempstead Meeting, Hempstead NY; married 1st October 19, 1724 in Dutch Reformed Church NYC, Thomas Fitch; 2d, also his 2d marriage, 1741 Philip Pell (brother of Caleb (1.4.8)) [son of Caleb & Anna (White) Pell] – his first wife was Hannah Mott (1.10.3) (1.7.4) MARTHA BURLING born April 7, 1708 NYC

No children recorded
