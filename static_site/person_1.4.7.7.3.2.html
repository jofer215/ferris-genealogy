MARGARET LEGGETT SEAMAN

ID: 1.4.7.7.3.2 | Source: John
(1.4.7.7.3.2) MARGARET LEGGETT SEAMAN born May 20, 1826 and died March 18, 1912; married November 5, 1855, the Honorable Stephen Gorham Paddock, of Hudson NY born April 22, 1828 and died January 19, 1921 Princton IL. Born to them were: (1.4.7.7.3.2.1) JAMES VALETINE SEAMAN PADDOCK born August 18, 1856 and died August 7, 1907; married May 15, 1880 Annie Jeanette Gilmore of Philadelphia PA born September 25, 1857

Children (2)

  • RICHARD BOLLES PADDOCK, Captain, 1.4.7.7.3.2.2
  • STEPHEN WRIGHT PADDOCK 1.4.7.7.3.2.3
