GEORGE MILFORD PARDEE

ID: 2.1.5.7.5.4.2 | Source: Peter
(2.1.5.7.5.4.2) GEORGE MILFORD PARDEE born March 12, 1846 Westchester Co NY and died 1918 Poughkeepsie, Dutchess, NY; laborer in Poughkeepsie 1880, gardener in Easthcester NY 1900; married June 18, 1874 in Wappingers Falls, Mary Graham born May 19, 1853 Ulster Co NY anddied February 26, 1908 Poughkeepsie; both buried Wappingers Rural Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • LILLA CORA PARDEE 2.1.5.7.5.4.2.1
  • MARY EMMA PARDEE 2.1.5.7.5.4.2.2
  • ELIZABETH PARDEE 2.1.5.7.5.4.2.3
  • LENOX PARDEE 2.1.5.7.5.4.2.4
