WARREN WELLINGTON FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.1.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.1.4) WARREN WELLINGTON FERRIS born January 3, 1840 Phelps (Newbury), Geauga, NY (OH?) and died October 26, 1911 Edgar Co IL; at age 15 he entered school and received a common education; 1857 began the study of medicine in Henry Co IN; 1859 entered Michigan State University, Ann Arbor and graduated; settled Vermilion, Edgar, IL where he began the practice of his profession and operated a well-stocked drug store; married June 5, 1862 in Henry Co IN Eliza Burner born June 6, 1842 Henry Co IN; and born to them was:
