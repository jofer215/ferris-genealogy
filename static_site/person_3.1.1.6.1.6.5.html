ISAAC NEWTON WADE

ID: 3.1.1.6.1.6.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.1.6.5) ISAAC NEWTON WADE born May 13, 1846 Lockland, Hamilton, OH and died July 25, 1926 Philadelphia PA; graduated from Hillsdale College, Hillsdale MI 1868 B.S.; A.M. 1877; taught school in MI near Schoolcraft and in OH until 1870 when he moved to IL; taught school in IL and was superintendent of schools at Sadorus, Rantoul, Toulon and Bement until 1881 when he became a salesman for American Book Co being its representative for 36y in IL, IN, OH, WI, MO, MN, NE, IA, CO, AR and TX; accidently discovered the only diamond mine in the US while traveling in AR for Book Co; retired to Champaign 1917; married August 21, 1870 in Douglas Co IL, Nannie Brian and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • GEORGE LEONARD WADE 3.1.1.6.1.6.5.1
  • THOMAS BRIAN WADE 3.1.1.6.1.6.5.2
  • FRED ALONZO WADE 3.1.1.6.1.6.5.3
