OLIVE MELVINA CHASE

ID: 3.1.1.6.7.1.6.4.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.7.1.6.4.1) OLIVE MELVINA CHASE born May 13, 1889 Weedman IL and died March 22, 1920 Farmer City, DeWitt, IL; married January 25, 1909/10 William Samuel Mitchell [son of Joseph Horner & Mary Olive (Drybread) Mitchell] [Joseph's Aunt was Mary Jane (Mitchell) Ferris(3.1.2.1.6.8.2)] born October 1881 and died January 29, 1939; Olive's mother died while Olive was an infant, thus her Grandmother Eleanor Chase raised her; Olive was a life-long member of the Christian Church and was the pianist for the church choir; and born to them was:
