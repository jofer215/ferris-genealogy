GEORGE WARNER PECK

ID: 3.10.5.10.1.4.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.5.10.1.4.4) GEORGE WARNER PECK born January 5, 1822 Flushing, Queens, NY; living there 1877; and died October 26, 1889; merchant 1850-60, insurance agent 1870, coal broker 1880; married September 7, 1843 in Poughkeepsie, Adaline Ann Vassar [dau of Charles & Sarah (Taylor) Vassar] born c1827, of Poughkeepsie NY and died January 7, 1901; her Uncle Matthew Vassar was founder of Vassar College; and born to them were: (3.10.5.10.1.4.4.1) GEORGE WEBSTER PECK born c1844 NY; clerk 1870, hardware merchant 1880; according to the census he resided in Flushing in 1870 and 1880, but apparently moved to Minnesota and returned in between; married Augusta H {maiden name unk} (c1846-); and born to them were:

Children (2)
