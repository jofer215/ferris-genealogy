EMILY J. FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.4) EMILY J. FERRIS (Photos) born April 1, 1813 Milford, Otsego, NY (her obit says she was born April 2, 1813 Phelps, Ontario Co) and died April 8, 1896 Sumner (Ferris) MI; married April 20, 1831 Lawson Stearns [son of Joel & Sarah (Hayden) Stearns] born May 19, 1808 Phelps, Ontario, NY and died November 25, 1873 Ferris MI. During the Civil War, Lawson enlisted in Company G., Bartlett's Battery, Ohio Artillery and was at the battle of Pittsburg Landing - he was discharged in 1862 on account of rheumatism and after recovering a measure of strength went to Ferris, Montcalm, MI 1863; resided Ferris MI and Sumner MI; a farmer; both buried Ferris Center Cemetery, Ferris Township, Montcalm Co MI; and born to them were:
