EVELINE E. HUSTED

ID: 3.1.4.2.4.1.6.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.4.2.4.1.6.3) EVELINE E. HUSTED born 1844 and died July 9, 1910 Manistee MI; married February 18, 1864 Dammon Converse Stone [son of Daniel & Mary (Wildman) Stone] born February 18, 1834 RI and died August 9, 1908 East Townsend, Huron, OH; buried Methodist Cemetery, Clarksfield, Huron, OH. He farmed near Breckenridge; and born to them were:
