PETER BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.2.3 | Source: James
(5.2.2.2.3) PETER BUXTON born August 11, 1759 Stamford CT and died there January 2, 1836; buried St. John’s Episcopal Cemetery, Stamford; a farmer; married November 18, 1784 Susanna Seeley [dau of Abijah & Lydia (Hoyt) Seeley] born May 25, 1760 Stamford and died there November 11, 1841. (Note: Susanna’s sister, Elizabeth, married Jacob Howe, Jr.(2.3.3.2.2). Born to them were:
