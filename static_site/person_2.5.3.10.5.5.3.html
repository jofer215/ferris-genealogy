LOUISE M WEED

ID: 2.5.3.10.5.5.3 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.10.5.5.3) LOUISE M WEED born December 19, 1836 Stamford and died there April 4, 1926; married December 3, 1862 Stamford, Martin Monroe Scofield [son of Amos & Marilla (Burgess) Scofield] born November 4, 1837 Stamford and died there December 3, 1866; both buried there North Street Cemetery; Amos’ first wife was Mary Scofield (4.6.3.4.3.3)

No children recorded
