ANN MARIA DUNBAR

ID: 4.4.2.5.9.1 | Source: Mary
(4.4.2.5.9.1) ANN MARIA DUNBAR (#1500) born April 15, 1832 Philadelphia PA and died September 28, 1867 Minneapolis MN; married April 27, 1858 Richard Price [son of Thomas Callander & Sarah Ann (Paul) Price] who died March 2, 1869 Minneapolis. Born to them were: (4.4.2.5.9.1.1) DUNBAR PRICE (#2451) born March 13, 1859; married January 5, 1881 Sarah Clement Burton [dau of George W. & Josephine (Clement) Burton] born October 14, 1854

Children (4)

  • ELIZABETH DUNBAR PRICE 4.4.2.5.9.1.3
  • SALLY PAUL PRICE 4.4.2.5.9.1.4
  • RICHARD PRICE 4.4.2.5.9.1.5
