LEONARD JESSE CHADEAYNE

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.10.4.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.10.4.1) LEONARD JESSE CHADEAYNE born February 22, 1890 at the “Just a Mere” farm, Yorktown NY and died there June 6, 1956; buried Dale Cemetery, Ossining; married Laura J. Jackson [dau of Stephen & Annie (Cullen) Jackson] who died October 31, 1967; buried St. Augustine’s Cemetery, Ossining. No offspring. Leonard is buried in the Veteran’s Section; monument reads NY Pvt Btry A 1 Prov. Dev. Brig. World War I. They were the last Chadeaynes to live on “Just a Mere” farm.

No children recorded
