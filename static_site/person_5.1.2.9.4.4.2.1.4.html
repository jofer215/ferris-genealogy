ALEXANDER SMITH

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.2.1.4 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.2.1.4) ALEXANDER SMITH born September 30, 1886 Mahopac Falls NY and died July 8, 1959 Danbury CT; married February 5, 1917 in Dover Plains NY, Lucy Ann Benson; both buried in the family plot at Wooster Cemetery, Danbury CT. Obituary, Danbury Times, Danbury, Conn., Thursday, July 9, 1959: Alexander Smith, 72, of 31 1/2 Osborne street, died Danbury hospital last evening at 10:20 o'clock. He had been admitted earlier in the day and death was the result of heart failure. Mr. Smith born in Mahopac Falls, NY, Sept. 30, 1886, son of the late Orlando and Sarah Smith. He had lived in Danbury since 1917. Mr. Smith was employed as a switchman with the New Haven Railroad for 40 years until his retirement about seven years ago. He was a member of the Railroad Trainmen's Brotherhood. Besides his wife, Lucy Ann Benson Smith, survivors include one son, Robert W. Smith of New Milford; three brothers, Charles and George Smith of Mahopac, and Chauncey Smith of Danbury; three sisters, Mrs. Ida Rosco, Mrs. Josephine Harkins and Mrs. Mary Nelson, all of Mahopac; five grandchildren and several nieces and nephews.
