NATHANIEL HOLMES FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.6.4.7 | Source: James
(5.1.3.6.4.7) NATHANIEL HOLMES FERRIS born February 5, 1824 Greenwich and died 1921 Manlius NY [there is a Nathaniel H. Ferris who was buried February 23, 1918, age 94 years, 4 months, 8 days; Manlius Village Cemetery, Manilus NY - probably the same guy, which means the 1921 death date is incorrect.]; married Mary E. Butler (-1894) [daughter of Rev. Butler of Old Congregational Church, Old Greenwich]. [there is a Mary Elizabeth Ferris who was buried May 6, 1897 at age 69 years, in the same Lot and Same Section as Nathaniel - probably his wife, Mary, which makes the above death date incorrect.] Mary's sister-in-law was Hannah (Ferris) Butler (5.1.3.6.4.11). They moved to Syracuse NY, circa 1850, thence to Pompey NY and finally, Manlius NY. Born to them were:

Children (2)
