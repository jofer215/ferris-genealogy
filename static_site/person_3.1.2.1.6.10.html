SAMUEL FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.1.6.10 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.6.10) SAMUEL FERRIS (Samuel A. Ferris) born April 19, 1800 Harpersfield, (Ashtabula County OH) Delaware, OH and died April 3, 1889 Eaton Co MI; buried Brickyard Cemetery, Eaton Rapids, Eaton, MI; married 1st (Betsey) Anna Crissey born 1800 Tompkins or Cayuga Co NY; and 2d 1831 in Washtenaw Co MI widow Sally (Spears) Newell born October 7, 1803 Clinton Co NY and died May 19, 1891 Eaton Co MI age 89y 2m 15d; buried with Samuel; they moved to Eaton Co MI 1836; and born to Samuel and Anna were: (3.1.2.1.6.10.1) SALLY FERRIS born September (December) 27, 1822 Seneca Co (Topkins?) NY and died December 4, 1900 Perry MI; married October 12, 1842 in Topkins Co NY Oliver Abel [son of William M. & Alma (Sager) Abel] born May 6, 1820 Ulysses, Topkins, NY and died March 16, 1883 Perry, Shiawassee, MI; and born to them were:
