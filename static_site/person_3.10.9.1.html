ROBERT PECK

ID: 3.10.9.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.9.1) ROBERT PECK born June 30, 1739 Greenwich CT and died there July 25, 1827; married January 15, 1767 Ann Reed born 1742-43 Darien CT and died June 21, 1828 at age 85; both buried Tomac Ave Cemetery, Greenwich. They resided in Greenwich where he was a Deacon of the church. Born to them were:
