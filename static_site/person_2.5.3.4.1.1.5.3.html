ELIZA MARIA HOYT

ID: 2.5.3.4.1.1.5.3 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.4.1.1.5.3) ELIZA MARIA HOYT (#5990) born January 31, 1842 Stamford and died May 20/29, 1864 Middletown CT (Cromwell CT); married February 15, 1859/60 in Stamford, Rev. Henry S. Stevens born 1833 Middletown CT; and they resided in Cromwell CT; no offspring.

No children recorded
