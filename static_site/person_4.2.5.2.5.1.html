DAVID WATERBURY

ID: 4.2.5.2.5.1 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.2.5.1) DAVID WATERBURY born April 17, 1819 Stamford CT and died there December 22, 1894; buried there Woodland Cemetery; 1850 carpenter; 1857-60 mariner (Captain); married 1st January 23, 1842 in Stamford, Sarah M. Selleck (2.6.5.3.4.2.3); buried with David; married 2d April 28, 1875 in Stamford Josephine E. Selleck (sister of Sarah) born 1836 Stamford; and born to him and Sarah were:

Children (6)

  • JOHN S. WATERBURY 4.2.5.2.5.1.2
  • MARY ELIZABETH WATERBURY 4.2.5.2.5.1.3
  • SARAH M. WATERBURY 4.2.5.2.5.1.4
  • CHARLES F. WATERBURY 4.2.5.2.5.1.5
