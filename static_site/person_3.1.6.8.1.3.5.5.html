CHARLES WHITFIELD WATSON ROSS

ID: 3.1.6.8.1.3.5.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.1.3.5.5) CHARLES WHITFIELD WATSON ROSS born June 16, 1877 Molesworth, Grey, Ontario and died January 27, 1943, Lamont, Alberta; Methodist minister in Northern Alberta; hospital administrator; married March 2, 1904 in Estevan, Sask, Jane Bacon Gallaway [dau of John Thomas & Elizabeth Ann (Davis) Gallaway] born November 11, 1881 Grey County, Ontario and died 1963 Lamont; both buried Hacket Cemetery, Lamont; Jane was a teacher; and born to them were:
