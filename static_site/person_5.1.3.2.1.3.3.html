GILBERT HOPKINS FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.2.1.3.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.1.3.3) GILBERT HOPKINS FERRIS (tombstone photo) born August 21, 1831 Riverside (Greenwich) CT and died October 2/4, 1908 Sound Beach, Greenwich. Gilbert was a contractor in Brooklyn; married April 16, 1855 Mary (Mc) Knight born February 22, 1831 Northamptonshire, England. She arrived at New York City about 1838 and died December 10, 1891 (1870?). Born to them were:
