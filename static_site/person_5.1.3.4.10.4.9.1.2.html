HAROLD WAYNE FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.9.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.9.1.2) HAROLD WAYNE FERRIS born November 6, 1915 Fabius MO; married March 17, 1940 Frances Marie Johnston born September 16, 1915; 1935 resided in Brasher MO. Made it through high school, but depression prevented him from going on to college. 5'10", 150 lbs. Born to them were:
