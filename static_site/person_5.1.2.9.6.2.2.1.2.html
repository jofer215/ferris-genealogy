THEODORE W. AUSTIN, JR.,

ID: 5.1.2.9.6.2.2.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.6.2.2.1.2) THEODORE W. AUSTIN, JR., born November 30, 1902 on Oscawana Lake Road, Putnam Valley and died May 25, 1992 at the Bethel Home, Ossining; married 1st name unk; married 2d June 25, 1957 in CT Marguerite A. Morgan who brought a daughter with her to the marriage. He grew up Putnam Valley and later moved to Shrub Oak. After 25 years before his death he moved to Mahopac, where he lived until he moved to Ossining about 2 years before his death. He was educated in the Peekskill schools and attended the Mount Herman Academy in Massachusetts. He was the proprietor of the Austin Insurance Agency in Peekskill from the 1930s to the late 1960s. He formerly attended the United Methodist Church of Shrub Oak; served with the Mohegan Lake Volunteer Fire Department for more than 65 years and was a former fire commissioner; a member of the Peekskill Lodge of the BPOE No. 744, he had been the exalted ruler in 1947.
