HANNAH HORTON

ID: 1.9.6.1.2.1 | Source: John
(1.9.6.1.2.1) HANNAH HORTON born March 21, 1819 Broad Top PA and died May 20, 1904; married Amos Figard (1814-1899); buried Orbisonia Cemetery, Orbisonia PA [son of James & Nancy] [or James & Abigail (Evans) Figard]; Mason and stable boss for coal mines near Powelton PA; and born to them were:
