JARVIS KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.3.7.8 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.7.8) JARVIS KNAPP born September (2?) 26, 1801 Stamford and died December 2, 1892 New Windsor, Orange, NY; a farmer and a member of the Sessions Presbyterian Church in Little Brittain; married 1st March 25, 1830 Mary Jane Gregg born 1807 and died February 23, 1862 New Windsor; married 2d 1865 widow Melissa R.
