A DAUGHTER- CATHARINE FERRIS

ID: 1.1.4.2.4 | Source: John
(1.1.4.2.4) A DAUGHTER- CATHARINE FERRIS [Jim - this is pure speculation based on information in the book, Thomas Davenport and His Descendants.] born July 3, 1798 Philipstown NY and died there December 31, 1871 [Jim - note her father lived in Philipstown in 1796.]; married Benjamin Davenport born July 23, 1798 Philipstown NY and died at home in Davenport Corners, Philipstown April 3, 1863; and born to them were: (1.1.4.2.4.1) EMELINE DAVENPORT born October 13, 1821 Philipstown NY and died at her home in the North Highlands, May 1, 1889; married James Knapp born July 15, 1819 Philipstown and died there November 6, 1883; both buried in the North Highland Cemetery. He was a farmer in the North Highlands. Born to them were:
