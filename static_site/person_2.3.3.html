MARY FERRIS

ID: 2.3.3 | Source: Peter
(2.3.3) MARY FERRIS born March 22, 1717 Stamford and died October 21, 1750; buried Noroton River Cemetery, Darien CT (photo); married January 22, 1745/6 at Stamford (Darien), as his first wife, John Bates III (John Bates Jr.) [son of Jonathan & Joanna (Selleck) Bates] born October 23/26, 1716 Stamford and died there, November 27, 1800. Born to Mary and John were:

Children (3)

  • SARAH BATES 2.3.3.2
