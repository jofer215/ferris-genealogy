JOSHUA LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.2.7.8 | Source: Mary
(4.2.7.8) JOSHUA LOCKWOOD (#191) (Commodore) born September 26, 1743 Greenwich. Joshua, of Pound Ridge, was chose by the freeholders and inhabitants of Westchester Co, one of the committee of that county, from the second Monday in May 1776 to the second Monday in May 1777.

No children recorded
