SAMUEL FERRIS

ID: 1.3 | Source: John
(1.3) SAMUEL FERRIS born c1676 (1670-73?) [mentioned in his father’s will]; was among the Westchester men who took the oath of allegiance to King of England January 31, 1698; residing Eastchester 1732. Samuel was one of the first assistant aldermen and member common council in organization Westchester; married 1699 Sarah Pinckney [dau of John & Abigail (Hunt) Pinckney]; resided Westchester and in 1701-03, were heavily in debt and sold his inherited land to Edmund/Edward Avery September 27, 1703.
