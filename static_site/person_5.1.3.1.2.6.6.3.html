HATTIE MERINDA FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.6.6.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.6.6.3) HATTIE MERINDA FERRIS born September 15, 1862 Ripley Township, Huron Co OH and died 1943 North Fairfield, Huron, OH; married September 15, 1885 in Ruggles OH, Elmer Leander Maynard [son of George & Polly (Woodward) Maynard] born October 16, 1863 Ripley Township, Huron Co OH and died c1949 North Fairfield, Huron, OH. For a number of years, Hattie & Elmer had the telephone exchange for Boughtonville, Huron Co in their home. Elmer was a section foreman for the B&O railroad, laying and repairing the tracks. About 1935 they retired and moved to North Fairfield OH, living for several years with their daughter, Wauneta, and then moving to a home of their own. Born to them were:

Children (2)
