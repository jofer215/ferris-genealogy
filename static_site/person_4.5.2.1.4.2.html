EZEKIEL REYNOLDS

ID: 4.5.2.1.4.2 | Source: Mary
(4.5.2.1.4.2) EZEKIEL REYNOLDS born August 7, 1788 Somers NY and died 1881; a farmer; a staunch Republican and a member of the Methodist Episcopal Church as was his wife. He was always very liberal to the poor and to the church; married December 24, 1809 Phebe Reynolds (c1788-) and born to them were (not in birth sequence):
