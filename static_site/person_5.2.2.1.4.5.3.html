CATHERINE M. LOUNSBURY

ID: 5.2.2.1.4.5.3 | Source: James
(5.2.2.1.4.5.3) CATHERINE M. LOUNSBURY born December 3, 1822 New Canaan CT and died there February 22, 1900; buried Lakeview Cemetery (Photo), New Canaan; married March 21, 1841 in Poundridge NY, John Buxton [son of Jonathan & Clarissa (Lounsbury) Buxton] born April 24, 1820 Stamford CT and died February 15, 1900 New Canaan CT; buried Lakeview Cemetery, New Canaan. John was a Civil War Veteran serving as a Private in Company G, 10th Connecticut Infantry Regiment, October 18, 1861 to April 14, 1862. Born to them were:
