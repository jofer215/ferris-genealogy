ROBERT EDWARD MORRISON

ID: 5.1.3.4.6.1.2.4.1.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.6.1.2.4.1.3) ROBERT EDWARD MORRISON born August 5, 1911 Cincinnati OH and died June 5, 1990 Trier, West Germany; remains buried Laurel Cemetery, Columbia Township OH; employed by Cincinnati Milling Machine Co for 35y; married June 5, 1937 in Cincinnati, Maxine Geneva Hughes [daughter of Raymond Finley and Geneva Belle (Black) Hughes] born October 23, 1913 Columbus, Franklin, OH.
