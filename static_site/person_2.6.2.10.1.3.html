SMITH KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.10.1.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.10.1.3) SMITH KNAPP born July 3, 1787 Stamford and died there April 6, 1846; buried West Stamford Cemetery; was a Veteran of the War of 1812; a Private on August 13, 1813; married November 29, 1808 at Stamford, Nancy Mead [dau of Reuben & Nelllie (Martin) Mead] born in 1786 (July 15, 1787) and died at Stamford, March 18, 1838; buried West Stamford Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • MARY KNAPP 2.6.2.10.1.3.1
  • HARRIET KNAPP 2.6.2.10.1.3.2
  • ALMIRA KNAPP 2.6.2.10.1.3.3
  • SARAH ANN KNAPP 2.6.2.10.1.3.4
