ANNE OLEITA LEWIS

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.6.7.3.4.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.6.7.3.4.2) ANNE OLEITA LEWIS born July 26, 1896 Reynoldsville, Jefferson, PA and died March 1, 1955 Johnstown, Cambria, PA; buried there Benshoff Hill Cemetery; married July 22, 1915 in Ebensburg PA, Park Green born March 14, 1886 Tunstall, Wolstanton, Stafford, England – apparently left a wife and 2 kids in England – and died March 27, 1947 Windber, Somerset, PA from injuries to his head and back from a rock fall while digging for coal at the Dean Shaffer Mine near Walsall; buried Richland Cemetery, Richland Township, Cambria Co PA; and born to Anne and Park were 5 kids, 4 of which were:

Children (3)

  • LAWRENCE EDGAR GREENE 3.1.2.1.4.6.7.3.4.2.1
  • ANN MARIE GREENE 3.1.2.1.4.6.7.3.4.2.2
  • PETER LEWIS GREENE 3.1.2.1.4.6.7.3.4.2.3
