MAJOR GRAEME LINKLETTER

ID: 3.10.2.9.1.3.1.12 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.2.9.1.3.1.12) MAJOR GRAEME LINKLETTER (photo) born March 15, 1907 Summerside, PEI and died July 1975 Marion IA; graduated Eureka College 1931; social worker for 8y; deputy auditor of Link Co IA 4y; Linn Co Assessor for 13y; President of Marion Lions Club 1956-57; President Marion Chamber of Comerce 1964; President of Iowa State Association of Assessors 1958-59; married December 31, 1932 in Omaha NE, Edith Irene Hunsinger [dau of John & Hanna Henrietta (Ludeman) Hunsinger] born March 22, 1911; and they had 3 kids

No children recorded
