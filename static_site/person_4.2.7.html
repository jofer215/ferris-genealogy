DAVID LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.2.7 | Source: Mary
(4.2.7) DAVID LOCKWOOD (#51) (Captain) born August 1707 Greenwich and died December 4, 1755. He was commissioned Lieutenant of the East Company or trainband of Greenwich. In 1745, he was a representative from Greenwich. In 1752, he was confirmed as Captain of the 1st Company or trainband on Greenwich. He married April 9, 1728 wife’s name unk (Hannah Hanford), who was deceased when he died. Born to them were (not in birth sequence):
