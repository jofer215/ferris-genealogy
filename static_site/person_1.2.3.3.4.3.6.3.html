PERCY EDWARD CRUTCHLEY

ID: 1.2.3.3.4.3.6.3 | Source: John
(1.2.3.3.4.3.6.3) PERCY EDWARD CRUTCHLEY born July 24, 1855 Kings Co, Ireland and died October 16, 1940; farmer of 138 acres in 1881, Sunninghill; married February 12, 1890 in England, Frederica Louise FitzRoy [dau of Charles & Ismay Catherine (Nugent) FitzRoy of Southampton] born July 4, 1864 London, England and died April 9, 1932

No children recorded
