RUTH MURRAY UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.3.9.2.3.1.2 | Source: John
(1.2.5.3.9.2.3.1.2) RUTH MURRAY UNDERHILL (Photo) born August 22, 1883 Ossining NY and died August 15, 1984; at age 16y her family traveled across Europe, which sparked her interest in languages and human culture; 1905 Vassar College, AB; 1905-07 U of Munich & London School of Economics; NYC social worker with Italian poor; 1909-11 Massachusetts Society for Prevention of Cruelty to Children; 1911-12 New York Charity Organization Society; 1917-20 American Red Cross, Abruzzi, Italy, orphanages administration; 1937 Columbia U PhD, plus numerous other information on the ‘Net; author of many books; her papers at the U of Oregon Library; married Charles C Crawford - divorced

No children recorded
