PHOEBE MEAD

ID: 3.5.5.1.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.5.5.1.5) PHOEBE MEAD born in 1774 and died March 29, 1850 in her 77th year; married Jehiel Mead Jr., [son of Jehiel & Deborah (Mead)] who died October 28, 1846 in his 78th year; both buried Old Cemetery at North Greenwich CT (Quaker Ridge Cemetery). Born to them were:

Children (8)

  • LEWIS MEAD 3.5.5.1.5.1
  • HARRIET MEAD 3.5.5.1.5.2
  • MARY MEAD 3.5.5.1.5.3
  • WILLIAM MEAD 3.5.5.1.5.4
  • MARY ANN MEAD died September 25, 1815 in her 15th year; buried Old Cemetery at 3.5.5.1.5.5
  • WILLIAM E. MEAD 3.5.5.1.5.6
  • CALEB MEAD 3.5.5.1.5.7
  • POLLY MEAD died May 12, 1802 at 4 years; buried Old Cemetery at North Greenwich 3.5.5.1.5.8
