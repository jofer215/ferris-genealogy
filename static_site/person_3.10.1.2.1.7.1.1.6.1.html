FAY V LEBARRON

ID: 3.10.1.2.1.7.1.1.6.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.1.7.1.1.6.1) FAY V LEBARRON born August 29, 1905 Omaha NE and died October 25, 1969; buried Santa Fe National Cemetery. Section O, Site 628; WWII Vet - Army First Lieutenant; listed nurse on marriage license; married 1st August 17, 1927 in Cleveland OH, James Timmins who died 1937 Cleveland; 2d Mr Huttenlocher; there is a William K Huttenlocher (1906-1984), US Army Staff Sergeant buried at the Santa Fe National Cemetery – don’t know if he was her 2d husband; and born to her and James was:
