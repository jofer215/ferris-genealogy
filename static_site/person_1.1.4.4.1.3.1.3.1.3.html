EVELYN LYDIA BROEFFLE

ID: 1.1.4.4.1.3.1.3.1.3 | Source: John
(1.1.4.4.1.3.1.3.1.3) EVELYN LYDIA BROEFFLE born May 21, 1923 Pierre, Hughes, SD; married August 15, 1947 at Danbury, Burnett, WI, George Matthew Etter [George Rudolph & Christina (Trumm) Etter] born December 29, 1912 Ramsey Co MN. At the time of their marriage, George was a musician living in St. Paul MN and Evelyn was a factory worker, also living in St. Paul. George was a WWII veteran and after his discharge, returned to St. Paul and resumed his profession as a musician, playing in dance bands in the area. In 1962, he became a postal clerk retiring in 1978, but continued playing in a 13 piece band at senior citizen functions. Born to them were:
