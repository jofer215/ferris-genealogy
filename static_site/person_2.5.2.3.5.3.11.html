SYLVESTER EDWIN WATERBURY

ID: 2.5.2.3.5.3.11 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.3.5.3.11) SYLVESTER EDWIN WATERBURY born 1808 Stamford and died there June 24, 1883; carriage builder 1883; married June 11, 1877 in Stamford, Sarah E Bland [dau of Charles & Margaret] born April 1849 Stamford and died there October 30, 1915; buried there Woodland Cemetery

No children recorded
