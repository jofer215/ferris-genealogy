ARTHUR FARNHAM FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.6.3.1.2.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.3.1.2.1) ARTHUR FARNHAM FERRIS born June 30, 1920 Syracuse; BCE, Syracuse University 1942; Ph.D., Cornell 1950; a research chemist Oklahoma and Syracuse 1942-45; Cornell University 1946-50; research chemist with Rohm & Haas 1949-54 Philadelphia and Huntsville AL; 1955 moved to St Albans WVA with Westvaco Division, Food Mach & Chemical Corporation; married September 6, 1947 at Buffalo, Fent Jamison Hoffman [dau of David] of Buffalo; 1956 resided South Charleston WVA; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • ARTHUR HOFFMAN FERRIS 5.1.3.8.6.3.1.2.1.1
  • JOHN REYNOLDS FERRIS 5.1.3.8.6.3.1.2.1.2
  • SUSAN JAMISON FERRIS 5.1.3.8.6.3.1.2.1.3
