WILLIAM FREDERICK GILLESPIE

ID: 2.1.3.3.2.2.2.4.2 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.2.2.2.4.2) WILLIAM FREDERICK GILLESPIE born November 19, 1878 Stamford and died there March 17, 1952; Secretary & Treasurer of the Stamford Rubber Supply Co; married 1st November 24, 1908 at New York City, Mabel Grace Miner born March 15, 1880 New York City and died June 23, 1943 Stamford; married 2d December 30, 1946 probably at Stamford, widow Jetta Rowena (Bailey) Hall born April 28, 1889 East Masonville NY. William and Mabel are buried at Woodlawn Cemetery, Stamford

No children recorded
