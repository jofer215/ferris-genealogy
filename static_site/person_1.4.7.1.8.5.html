JESSE SYLVESTER FERRIS

ID: 1.4.7.1.8.5 | Source: John
(1.4.7.1.8.5) JESSE SYLVESTER FERRIS (Photos) born February 26, 1846 Westchester Co NY and died June 23, 1937 Waukon, Allamakee, IA; married February 22, 1868 at Lansing IA, Jennie Gordon [dau of John & Rachel (Thompson) Gordon] born June 10, 1853 Vestal, Broome, NY and died April 18, 1910 Spencer, Clay, IA; both buried New Albin Cemetery, Iowa Township, Allamakee Co Born to them were: (1.4.7.1.8.5.1) MABEL G. FERRIS born April 6, 1869 Lansing, Allamakee County IA and died December 5, 1934 Spencer, Clay, IA; buried there Riverside Cemetery; married June 11, 1886 in Lansing, John Lachner
