JOHN ANTHONY FERRIS, M.D.,

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.10.5.6.3.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.10.5.6.3.2) JOHN ANTHONY FERRIS, M.D., born April 23, 1944 Tulsa OK; served seven years in the US Naval Submarine Service; then attended and graduated from Oklahoma State U., then from the U. of Oklahoma Medical School; served as a flight surgeon the USAF for several years after graduation; a family practice physician in Watertown WI; moved to Stilwell OK where he has his practice; married 1st in Oklahoma City, Christy Nguyen who died and is buried in Memorial Park Cemetery, Tulsa OK; married 2d in August 13, 1998 in Buena Vista CO, Grace Eva Bull, of Saratoga Springs NY.
