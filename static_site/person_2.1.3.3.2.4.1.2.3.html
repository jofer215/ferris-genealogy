MADELINE PERRY HARTWELL

ID: 2.1.3.3.2.4.1.2.3 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.2.4.1.2.3) MADELINE PERRY HARTWELL born May 26, 1875 Honolulu HI and died 1941/61; married 1899 Albert Francis Judd II – descendant of the Judd family who came to Hawaii to spread Christianity but stayed on as adviser to the Hawaiian Kingdom and trusted confidant of the royal family; and in generation became landed gentry. In 1908, Albert joined the board of trusttes of Kamchameha Schools, which at that time also governed Bishop Museum.
