JANE HUSTED

ID: 2.1.8.5.4 | Source: Peter
(2.1.8.5.4) JANE HUSTED born February 8, 1772 and died November 20, 1844 Stamford; buried West Stamford Cemetery; married February 5, 1792 in Stamford, Frederick Smith born January 1768 Stamford and died September 14, 1834; a Baptist Minister; and born to them were: (2.1.8.5.4.1) BETSEY SMITH born January 30, 1793 Stamford and died there February 12, 1865; buried West Stamford Cemetery; unmarried in 1850
