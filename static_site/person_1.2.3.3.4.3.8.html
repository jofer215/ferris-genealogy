JOHN FITZJOHN HARRIS

ID: 1.2.3.3.4.3.8 | Source: John
(1.2.3.3.4.3.8) JOHN FITZJOHN HARRIS (Photo) born 1830 London, Ontario (March 15, 1830 Port Ryerse, Norfolk, Ontario) and died June 14, 1861 Toronto, York, Canada; married November 9, 1859 Elizabeth Loring (c1808-); John was the first son to survive to adulthood; completed his legal studies in 1854 and established a law practice in London, Ontario; was often ill and in 1857 spent 3 months in England seeking medical treatment

No children recorded
