VIOLA MAY HUEY

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.4.5.2.4.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.4.5.2.4.1.2) VIOLA MAY HUEY born January 12, 1912 Indiana (possibly Peru, Miami Co) and died May 19, 1997 Flint MI; buried Flint Memorial Park Cemetery, Mt. Morris MI. She bore 2 sons with a man, name unk, who she may or may not have been married to; married August 30, 1940 in Dubuque IA, Charles Papp who adopted her 2 sons and changed their last name to Papp. Charles is still living –2003.
