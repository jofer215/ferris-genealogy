JACOB FERRIS

ID: 2.1.3.4.1 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.4.1) JACOB FERRIS born c1780 Stanwich CT and died date unk in Vermont; married January 3, 1802 Elizabeth Jordan.[dau of William and Mercy (Palmer) Jordan] born c1794 Greenwich and died after 1822 VT. They moved from Stanwich to Chester, Warren Co, NY where they resided in 1818, thence to Vermont where they died. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • JACOB FERRIS JR 2.1.3.4.1.1
  • SARAH or SALLY FERRIS 2.1.3.4.1.2
