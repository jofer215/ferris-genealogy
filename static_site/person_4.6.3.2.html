JOSIAH LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.3.2 | Source: Mary
(4.6.3.2) JOSIAH LOCKWOOD (#252) born September 25, 1741 Stamford and died unmarried. He served with the 5th Regiment, December 26, 1776 to February 1, 1777. He also enlisted July 5, 1780 in Captain Douglas’s Company, Colonel Starr’s Regiment and was discharged December 4, 1780.

No children recorded
