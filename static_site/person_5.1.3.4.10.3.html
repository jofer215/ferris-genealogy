DEBORAH ANN FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.3) DEBORAH ANN FERRIS born March 23, 1820 Metamora, Franklin, IN and died there February 29, 1896; unmarried (5.1.3.4.10.4) B. FERRIS (Photo) born March 18, 1822 Metamora, Franklin, IN and died May 18, 1877 Tampico, Whiteside, IL; was reared upon his family's farm, attending to the duties that feel to the lot of farmer's boys, and going to school during the winter months and in this way, he developed an excellent manhood and obtained a good common-school education; married December 28, 1848 in Franklin Co IN, Martha Jane Currey [dau of John Smith & Lucy (Williams) Currey] born August 9, 1829 on the Currey farmstead in Franklin Co IN and died November 30, 1901 Tampico, Whiteside, IL; 1873 he moved his family further west to Section 13, Tampico Township, Whiteside Co IL where he farmed 640 acres. Land records in Tampico IL list a Benjamin Ferris - don't know if this is the same fella. Both were members of the Methodist Episcopal Church and he was a Republican. Both buried in Tampico Cemetery, Tampico IL. Following the relatively early death of her husband, Martha took over the active management of the family farm, along with the help of her children, and ‘possessed rare business ability, managing the affairs of her late husband with great credit’. She remarried, 1893, Giles A. Stilson. Born to them were:

No children recorded
