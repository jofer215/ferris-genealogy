SAMANTHA SHEPARD FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.3.7.2.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.3.7.2.5) SAMANTHA SHEPARD FERRIS born August 21, 1830/1 VT and died September 16 (22), 1907 Burlington VT; family believes that Betsey was Samantha’s mother; married Franklin VanCore born 1819 Hinesburg VT and died 1867 Monkton VT; 1850 census they were living in Monkton; at Franklin’s death, they were either divorced or separated as Samantha was living with her brother-in-law, Charles; and born to them were: (3.1.2.3.7.2.5.1) ABBIE FERRIS VANCORE born October 31, 1851 and died March 4, 1917; married Elias Collins and born to them was:

Children (5)

  • ANN ELIZABETH VANCORE 3.1.2.3.7.2.5.3
  • CARLYLE MELANCTON VANCORE 3.1.2.3.7.2.5.4
  • CHARLES WESLEY VANCORE 3.1.2.3.7.2.5.5
