MARY FRANCES LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.1.4.1.3.4.3 | Source: James
(5.1.4.1.3.4.3) MARY FRANCES LOCKWOOD born May 23, 1825 Pound Ridge NY and died February 14, 1888 Stamford; buried there High Ridge Cemetery; married William Henry Saunders [son of John & Betsey (Dixon) Saunders] born June 24, 1826 Pound Ridge NY and died September 11, 1897 Stamford CT; buried with Mary; blacksmith 1850-65; this may have been his 2d marriage; and born to them were:

Children (8)
