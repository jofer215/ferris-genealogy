HALCYON L. MORSE

ID: 3.1.2.2.4.1.6.2.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.4.1.6.2.2) HALCYON L. MORSE born April 2, 1883 Shiawasse Co MI and died November 21, 1920 Flint MI; married John Albert Buhl born December 6, 1878 and died December 1980 Flint MI; age 102y; an upholsterer; spent his 102d birthday in the St. Joseph Hospital, after he tried to act the part of a good Samaritan. John resided in a home in Grand Blanc Township with two other men, one in his 80s and the other, a youngster, in his 60s. The 80ish fellow, who was arthritic, fell out of bed one night. Buhl, upon hearing the fall, scurried to the younger man's room and picked him up and received a hernia for his trouble. [Jim -this came from the Flint Journal, dated December 7, 1980. Chaplain Ferris has him dying in December, so I wonder if John did not survive the hernia surgery?]; and born to Halcyon and John was:
