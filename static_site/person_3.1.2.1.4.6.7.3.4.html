MARY CATHERINE CRAWFORD buried March 1915 Scalp Level Cemetery,

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.6.7.3.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.6.7.3.4) MARY CATHERINE CRAWFORD buried March 1915 Scalp Level Cemetery, Cambria Co PA; married November 15, 1893 Reynoldsville, Jefferson, PA as his 1 st wife, David Alexander Lewis [son of Edward Alexander & Mary (Morgan) Lewis] born November 6, 1872 Winslow Township, Jefferson, PA and died October 18, 1925 Kittanning, Armstrong, PA; buried there Kittanning Cemetery; miner 1915 Scalp Level; after Mary’s death, David married 1917 Rozella Henry; and born to Mary and David were:
