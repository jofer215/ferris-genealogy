ABRAHAM I UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.3.2 | Source: John
(1.2.5.3.2) ABRAHAM I UNDERHILL born June 27, 1763 and died May 6, 1841; he was interested in the purchase of lands in the ‘western country,’ now the central part of New York State, and some in Pennsylvania. He was the first to introduce an iron plow in Westchester Co NY; married December 19, 1805 Rebecca Field [dau of Jessie & Phoebe] born March 1, 1788 and died February 6, 1854; both buried Westchester Friend’s Cemetery, but on April 22, 1882, their remains were removed to Woodlawn Cemetery; see memoranda of conversation with Abraham from the Manuscript of Abraham S Underhill at the end of this chapter; and born to them were:
