GILBERT WASHINGTON HOPKINS

ID: 5.1.3.2.1.1.5 | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.1.1.5) GILBERT WASHINGTON HOPKINS born August 28, 1828 and was killed in Arizona by Apaches; a mining engineer; cost his family $1600.00 to bring his body back and they did not feel sure it was him, but buried Sound Beach Cemetery, Greenwich; Mount Hopkins in the Santa Ritas south of Tuscon is named for him.

No children recorded
