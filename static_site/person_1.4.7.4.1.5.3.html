EUGENE B. FERRIS; a farmer

ID: 1.4.7.4.1.5.3 | Source: John
(1.4.7.4.1.5.3) EUGENE B. FERRIS; a farmer (1.4.7.4.1.6) ESTHER AMELIA FERRIS (Photos) born May 13, 1838 Whitestone, Long Island, NY and died August 8, 1916; married October 25, 1859 Eugene Hyatt [only son of John Sherwood & Lucretia (Warner) Hyatt] born May 1829 Stratford, Fulton, NY and died September 23, 1895 Troy NY. His early education was obtained in the district schools. He prepared for the law practice in the offices of Mr. Johnson, of Little Falls. He passed the law board at Albany about 1850, and opened a law office in Lansingburg (Troy). He later removed to NYC, where he passed two years. Returning to Troy, he again engaged in the practice of his profession, continuing until ill health compelled his retirement, five years previous to his death. For 24 years he had as his law partner, Albert C. Comstock, whose law preceptor he had been. As preceptor, student and partners they were associated for nearly 30 years in the city of Troy. Mr. Hyatt was a republican and was a member of the New York assembly one session. He was a member of the Masonic fraternity of Troy, belonging to the lodge, chapter and commandery.
