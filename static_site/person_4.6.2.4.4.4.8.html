HENRY DANN

ID: 4.6.2.4.4.4.8 | Source: Mary
(4.6.2.4.4.4.8) HENRY DANN born December 28, 1822 Stamford and died there March 31, 1873; buried there High Ridge Cemetery; married 1st c1850 Lucy Boughton (c1829-1855); 2d November 4, 1856 in Stamford, Sarah Ann Mead [dau of Jonathan & Catharine (Town) Mead] born April 19, 1820 Greenwich and died 1902 Stamford; buried with Henry
