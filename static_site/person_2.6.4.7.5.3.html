PETER WILLIAM KNAPP

ID: 2.6.4.7.5.3 | Source: Peter
(2.6.4.7.5.3) PETER WILLIAM KNAPP born February 11, 1812 Stamford and died May 10, 1857, Charleston, Charleston County SC; buried there. He resided at Stamford and Charleston; Congregationalist. He married c1832 at Charleston, Cecelia Croval born c1812/15, probably Charleston. Peter was labeled “a rabid southerner” and by religion, a hard core “Southern Baptist”. Little is known of the future of his children and descendents according to Fred Knapp. Born to them were (order of birth is uncertain):
