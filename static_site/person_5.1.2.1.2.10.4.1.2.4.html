WARD REYNOLDS ANDERSON

ID: 5.1.2.1.2.10.4.1.2.4 | Source: James
(5.1.2.1.2.10.4.1.2.4) WARD REYNOLDS ANDERSON born September 21, 1946 (5.1.2.1.2.10.4.1.3) HERBERT HENRY FERRIS, JR., born July 28, 1919 Glen Ridge NJ; graduate of Worchester Polytechnical Institute as a mechanical Engineer; WWII USMC Veteran of Guadacanal; married 1st Jean Rohr [dau of Frederick H. Rohr – founder of Rohr Aircraft Corp.] born July 10, 1923 divorced; and 2d Barbara Ann Smith born June 26, 1931 Kansas City - divorced; and 3d 1989 Jane Carmichael Fitz, M.D.(an anesthesiologist who practiced in the San Diego area for 30 years); 2003 resided Alpine CA. Henry at first managed an insurance company that his father-in-law, Fred Rohr, owned; when the company was sold, Henry became a stockbroker and worked for various firms, retiring from Prudential Securities.
