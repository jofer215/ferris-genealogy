CORA PHOEBE FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.6.3.5.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.3.5.1) CORA PHOEBE FERRIS born May 16, 1895 New York City; married January 16, 1919 at Nutley NJ, John Musser Walter [son of Christian G & Wilmina (Musser)] born November 3, 1890. Cora taught piano, organ and directed music in the Methodist Church, Emporium PA. John was with Sylvania Company in Emporium. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • LOIS JEAN WALTER 5.1.3.8.6.3.5.1.1
  • MURIAL ANNA WALTER 5.1.3.8.6.3.5.1.2
  • RUTH ALICE WALTER 5.1.3.8.6.3.5.1.3
