ANDREA LEIGH

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.10.5.1.1.3.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.10.5.1.1.3.1) ANDREA LEIGH (NOLL) LANTZ (Photos) born December 1, 1976 Columbus OH; married March 7, 1998 Darin Kent Holland born February 18, 1976 Lakeland FL. He graduated from Plant City High School, 1995; works for James Hardie Building Products in Plant City, an international concrete specialty products manufacturing and distribution company. Andrea was adopted by her stepdad on May 15, 1984, Christopher Lantz at age five. She moved with her family to Plant City Florida where she went to Knight's elementary school, and got her first pony, "Disco", at age 7. By age 9, Andrea was ready for her first horse, and her Aunty Jo Ann bought "Fancy", for her. Fancy was a beautiful 15.3 hand paint mare. Andrea was very good about grooming, feeding and riding her horse. Andrea started showing in Western classes, moved to rodeo-type events such as barrel-racing, moving eventually to English hunt seat . She spent most of her teen-age years practicing and showing various horses at state shows around the US. Andrea attended a magnet high school in Tampa, the Academy of Health Professions. She was a member of student government, the National Honor Society, and an athletic trainer. Andrea earned her Associate's degree from Hillsborough Community College. She married Darin Kent Holland 3/7/98, and is manager and trainer at Legend's Farm in Plant City, Florida. Andrea is now professionally training and showing paint horses.
