MARY ELIZABETH HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.4.5.5.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.5.5.2) MARY ELIZABETH HOYT (#4545) born January 2, 1810 Stamford CT and died there January 15, 1871; buried Newfield Cemetery; married June 30, 1836 in Stamford, Charles Brown (2.5.2.4.7.7.2) – see him for offspring.

No children recorded
