ELMER J. KIMBLE

ID: 3.6.3.2.2.9.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.2.9.1.2) ELMER J. KIMBLE born May 24, 1861 Vicksburg, Kalamazoo, MI and died there May 10, 1926; married 1st December 12, 1881 in Vicksburg, Ida Esther Daniels [dau of Henry John & Eunice Maria (Hall) Daniels] born September 16, 1857 Chatham, Medina, OH and died September 30, 1898 Vicksburg MI; 2d Lizzie Battes.
