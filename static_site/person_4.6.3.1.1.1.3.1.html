MELANCTHON C. LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.3.1.1.1.3.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.3.1.1.1.3.1) MELANCTHON C. LOCKWOOD (#3389) born January 29, 1853 NYC and died 1897 Stamford CT; buried there Roxbury Cemetery (Photo); married March 3, 1874 at NYC, Jennie A. Hatch [dau of Z. Paten & Marintha H. (Burton) Hatch] born November 17, 1849, Brooklyn. In 1888, Rev. Lockwood resided at Cincinnati OH and was pastor of the First Baptist Church. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • KENNETH FRAZER LOCKWOOD 4.6.3.1.1.1.3.1.2
  • PAUL LORENZO LOCKWOOD 4.6.3.1.1.1.3.1.3
