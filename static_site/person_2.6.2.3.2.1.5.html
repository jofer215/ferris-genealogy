ALANSON KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.3.2.1.5 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.2.1.5) ALANSON KNAPP born October 2 (22?), 1806 Sugar Loaf NY and died May 15 (17?), 1878 Chester NY, by burning to death; married in Orange Co Hannah Maria Pitts (1810-) and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • HARRISON KNAPP 2.6.2.3.2.1.5.1
  • SARAH KNAPP 2.6.2.3.2.1.5.2
  • ELMER KNAPP died at Sugar Loaf probably in infancy 2.6.2.3.2.1.5.3
  • HANNAH POTTER KNAPP - adopted 2.6.2.3.2.1.5.4
