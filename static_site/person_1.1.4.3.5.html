JOSEPH FERRIS

ID: 1.1.4.3.5 | Source: John
(1.1.4.3.5) JOSEPH FERRIS born May 15, 1775 and died December 3, 1851; buried at the Old Cemetery at Mekeel's Corners, Phillipstown NY; married Mary Denny [dau of Richard & Asenath (Booth) Denny] born September 20, 1778 and died January 1 (22), 1850 at age 72-4-2; buried with her husband.
