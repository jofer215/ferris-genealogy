OSCAR E GODFREY

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.1.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.3.1.2) OSCAR E GODFREY born September 10, 1833 Elba, Genesee, NY and died there October 31, 1905; buried there Maple Lawn Cemetery; farmer; married Anna Shepherd born September 17, 1850 and died September 17, 1894; apparently no offspring (3.1.2.1.4.1.3.1.3) MALVINA ESTHER GODFREY born c1837 NY and died May 24, 1922 Batavia, Genesee, NY; married Charles E Pease, a farmer, who apparently died before 1880 as Malvina was living with her parents then; apparently no offspring
