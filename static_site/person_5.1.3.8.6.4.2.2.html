RUTH KEELER FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.6.4.2.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.4.2.2) RUTH KEELER FERRIS born May 17, 1908 Brooklyn NY and died July 11, 1955 Kennebunk Beach ME; married September 1933 Franklin George McArthur born Canada. Ruth graduated from Smith College. They moved from Westfield NJ to Montreal 1935. Born to them were:
