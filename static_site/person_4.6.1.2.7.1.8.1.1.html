MARY ELEANOR KEELER

ID: 4.6.1.2.7.1.8.1.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.7.1.8.1.1) MARY ELEANOR KEELER born September 25, 1896 Traer, Tama, IA and died December 14, 1980 Grand Marsh, Adams, WI; buried there; graduate Chokio High School, 1913; graduate of Hamline U; joined DAR 1923-24; married August 6, 1923 in Chokio, Harry O. Spindler [son of Frank & Mary] born January 8, 1895 Winsted, Wright, MN and died March 30, 1972 Grand Marsh WI; buried with Mary; WWI Vet (4.6.1.2.7.1.8.1.2) HERBERT WINGET KEELER (twin) born March 10, 1900 Lake View, Sac, IA and died July 19, 1963 Chokio, Stevens, MN while working in a field on his farm; buried Calvary Cemetery, Morris MN; graduate of West Central School of Agriculture in Morris, 1917; played football at this high school for farm children who attended classes 6 months each year; WWI Army Vet; connected with the Chokio Equity Exchange for 23 years, secretary at the time of death; member of the Stevens County Board for 40 years and a member of the School District 52 board for many years; member of the American Legion and St. Mary’s Church; married February 21, 1926 in St. Cloud MN, Julia Mary Brown born August 13, 1903 Minneapolis MN and died January 25, 1978 Graceville, Big Stone, MN; buried with Herbert; and born to them were:

No children recorded
