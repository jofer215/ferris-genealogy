HULDAH ANN WILSON

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.5.2 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.5.2) HULDAH ANN WILSON born July 16, 1840 and died May 25, 1924 Peekskill NY; married Smith Saxton Townsend [son of Wollsey & Martha (Barger)] born February 4, 1840 and died March 29, 1919; both buried Shrub Oak Methodist Cemetery, Shrub Oak NY Obituary, Highland Democrat, Peekskill, NY, May 31, 1924: “Mrs. Huldah A. Townsend died suddenly at the home of Mr. and Mrs. Edwin Hadden, 957 Orchard street, Sunday last. She had been visiting the family. Mrs. Townsend born at Tompkins Corners, July 16, 1840, consequently was not quite 84 years old. She was the daughter of Oakley and Delia Wilson. Over sixty years ago, she married to Saxton Smith Townsend. He died five years ago last March. She is survived by two children, Spencer A. Townsend and Mrs. Susie A. Treadwell. There are also eight grandchildren and six great grandchildren. She was a member of the Shrub Oak M.E. church for over fifty years and one of her former pastors, Rev. E. M. Corneille, assisted by the present pastor, Rev. Mr. Withey, conducted the funeral services, Tuesday at the Shrub Oak M.E. church. The interment was in the family plot there. She was a member of the Mariposa Sunday School class of the M.E. church. Mrs. Townsend was a woman of wonderful character, and a most amiable and companionable disposition. During her long life, she had made a host of loyal, intimate friends, who will miss her happy smile and cheering greetings.” Born to them were:
