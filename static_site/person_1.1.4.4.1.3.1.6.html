OSCAR L. VALLY

ID: 1.1.4.4.1.3.1.6 | Source: John
(1.1.4.4.1.3.1.6) OSCAR L. VALLY born 1877 WI and died 1922 Park Falls, Price, WI; married 1901 Kathryn (or Catherine) Hickey (1880-1961); 1910 U. S. Census, Oscar was a saw mill laborer living in Park Falls, Price County WI and Katherine was a dressmaker; 1920 Census, Oscar is listed as a farmer living in Lake Township, Price, WI; and born to them were:
