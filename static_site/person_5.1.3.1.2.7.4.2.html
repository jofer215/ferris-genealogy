ESTA MAUDE FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.7.4.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.7.4.2) ESTA MAUDE FERRIS born August 31, 1860 Carthage, Hancock, IL and died July 22, 1939 Spokane, Spokane, WA; married September 7, 1882 in Carthage, Frank Hyde Graves [son of John James & Orilla Landon (Berry) Graves] born June 15, 1857 St Mary’s Township, Hancock, IL and died January 19, 1948 Spokane; lawyer; both buried there Greenwood Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (4)
