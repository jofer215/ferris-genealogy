JOAN MAUDE SLOTE

ID: 3.1.1.5.1.9.4.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.5.1.9.4.3) JOAN MAUDE SLOTE born March 4, 1876 New York City and died September 28, 1967 Newtonville MA; married November 25, 1899 in MI, Winfield S. Smyth and they had 3 kids. A granddaughter, Joan Fraser, has graciously provided this updated info. Thanks, Joan.
