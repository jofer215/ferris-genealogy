ANNA MARIA FERRIS

ID: 3.1.1.6.2.3.1.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.2.3.1.3) ANNA MARIA FERRIS born June (May) 28, 1844 Linwood OH and died March 6, 1912; married December 24, 1865 (or December 15, 1864) James Alexander DeArmond born March 1, 1840 and died January 1, 1917; a member of his father-in-law's company and was the head of J. A. DeArmond Manufacturing Company; resided Linwood; and born to them were:

Children (6)

  • ELLA DeARMOND 3.1.1.6.2.3.1.3.2
  • MARY FERRIS DeARMOND ‘Bird’ born April 19, 1872 and died June 10, 1954; 3.1.1.6.2.3.1.3.3
  • ALBERT ELMER DeARMOND 3.1.1.6.2.3.1.3.4
  • CLARA MELDRUM DeARMOND 3.1.1.6.2.3.1.3.5
