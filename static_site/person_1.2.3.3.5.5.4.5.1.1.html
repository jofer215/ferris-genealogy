HARRY ROLLAND HALL

ID: 1.2.3.3.5.5.4.5.1.1 | Source: John
(1.2.3.3.5.5.4.5.1.1) HARRY ROLLAND HALL born December 5, 1898 Elgin Co, Ontario and died February 20, 1960 Coldwater, Simcoe, Ontario; married October 5, 1921 in Parry Sound, Parry Sound District, Ontario, Florence Murdock born 1903 Longford Mills, Simcoe, Ont and died 1983 Coldwater, Simcoe, Ont; both buried there Coldwater Cemetery; and born to them was: (1.2.3.3.5.5.4.5.1.1.1) DONELDA ISABEL SHIRLEY HALL born in MacTier, Muskoka District, Ont; married George Richard Shaw born 1922 St Thomas, Elgin Co, Ont and died March 18, 1999 Cobough, Northumberland, Ont; offspring

No children recorded
