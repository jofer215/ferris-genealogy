ELEANOR BRANDOW

ID: 3.9.5.7.4.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.9.5.7.4.1) ELEANOR BRANDOW born May 17, 1815 Greene Co NY and died August 31, 1872; buried Huttersfield Cemetery, Prattsville; married c1842 Stephen W. Truesdell born September 15, 1815 Delaware Co NY; after Eleanor’s death, Stephen married Clarissa B Cook (3.9.5.7.6.6); and born to her and Stephen were:
