CHARLES OGDEN COLE

ID: 5.1.2.9.9.2.2 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.9.2.2) CHARLES OGDEN COLE born July 2, 1852 Putnam Valley NY and died December 30, 1921 Peekskill NY; married July 22, 1871 (Selema) Selenia Hoffman [dau of William J. & Mary (Haight) Hoffman] born February 1852 and died September 5, 1920; both buried Hillside Cemetery, Peekskill.
