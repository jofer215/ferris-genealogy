ABIJAH HAIT

ID: 4.6.5.1.2 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.1.2) ABIJAH HAIT (#3384) lived in Pound Ridge and died there before 1830. He was a soldier in the revolution, under Captain Mosier; was engaged in various skirmishes in the southern part of Westchester County. (4.6.5.1.3) ABIGAIL HAIT (#3385) married Nathaniel (Hait) Hoyt (#3402) (4.6.5.8.1). Lived in Pound Ridge and she died c1845. See him for offspring.

No children recorded
