DAVID RUNDLE

ID: 3.1.5.1.9 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.5.1.9) DAVID RUNDLE born May 23, 1773 probably New Fairfield CT and died June 19, 1853 Spring Township, Crawford Co PA; married August 23, 1796 at Catskill NY, Sabra Mygatt (1771-1853) {both buried at Rundel Cemetery, Spring Township PA - note spelling of surname - whether correct or a literacy problem is unknown - Post Office at one time was named the 'Rundel Post Office', located at Rundel PA, named for David and his brother, Luther Rundel} [David moved to Cussawego, now Meadville, Crawford County PA in 1818] and born to them were:
