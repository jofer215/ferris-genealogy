UNDERHILL QUINBY

ID: 1.2.2.9.3.8 | Source: John
(1.2.2.9.3.8) UNDERHILL QUINBY born May 5, 1808 North Castle NY and died July 23, 1859; married 1st Anna Loretta Van Voorhis born September 2, 1820 and died February 17, 1849; both buried Friends Cemetery, Chappaqua NY; married 2d Sarah Barmore – sister-in-law

No children recorded
