WILLIAM MARTENS LEE, M.D.,

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.10.6.1.3 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.10.6.1.3) WILLIAM MARTENS LEE, M.D., born January 30, 1942 Peekskill NY; graduated 1963 Amherst College, BA cum laude; graduated 1967, Columbia U., M.D.; was a gastroenterologist. He received advanced training at Presbyterian Hospital in NY and at St. Elizabeth’s Hospital in Washington, D.C. He has been a Professor of Internal Medicine at the College of Physicians and Surgeons and at the Medical School of the U. of South Caroline Charleston. He is the Professor of Internal Medicine and the Director of the Clinical Center for Liver Disease at the U. of Texas South Medical Center, Dallas TX, joining their staff in 1989. He has published a number of articles on liver disease in medical journals. He married December 17, 1966 in NYC, Elizabeth Lyon Maxwell [dau of Charles E. & Elizabeth] born August 5, 1943 NYC. Elizabeth was headmistress at the Hockaday School for Girls, Dallas TX. Born to them were:
