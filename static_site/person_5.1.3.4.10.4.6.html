MARY JANE FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.6 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.6) MARY JANE FERRIS (Photo) born April 5, 1864 Metamora, Franklin, IN and died 1947 Tampico, Whiteside, IL; married November 23, 1882 in Whiteside Co IL, John Perry Glassburn [son of Albert James & Mary Jane (Wood) Glassburn] born November 10, 1862/63 Gallia Co OH and died July 6, 1934 Tampico. Mary Jane was a member of the Methodist Church and the Royal Neighbors. Both buried Tampico Memorial Cemetery, Tampico IL. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • RALPH LEROY GLASSBURN 5.1.3.4.10.4.6.2
  • EDWIN JAMES GLASSBURN 5.1.3.4.10.4.6.3
  • CLAUDE W. GLASSBURN 5.1.3.4.10.4.6.4
