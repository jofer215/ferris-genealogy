FREDRICK B KENYON

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.7.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.7.6) FREDRICK B KENYON born January 20, 1872 Adams Township, Delaware Co IA and died there August 15, 1877 (3.11.1.1.3.2.7.7) MABEL LODUSKY KENYON born January 13, 1876 Tower Hill IA and died September 20, 1943 Hopkinton, Delaware, IA; buried Greenwood, Masonville IA; married February 6, 1895 in Delaware Co, Martin L. Kaster [son of Shilling G & Sarah Jane (Lanning) Kaster] born May 21, 1862 Spring Branch, Delaware, IA and died September 11, 1924 Chicago IL; buried with Mabel; he was a farmer in Oneida, a clerk in the Kenyon store in Masonville in 1895; worked at the Melford Creamery in Dickinson Co IA, and worked as a bridge carpenter for the Illinois Central RR; and born to them were:

No children recorded
