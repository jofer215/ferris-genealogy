ANN AUGUSTUS COCK

ID: 1.4.7.8.2 | Source: John
(1.4.7.8.2) ANN AUGUSTUS COCK born January 20, 1813, NYC and died November 18, 1852 Pau, France; married January 8, 1834 in NYC, Edward Willis [son of John Robbins & Martha (Willits) Willis] born February 6, 1812 and died June 30, 1884. He was a merchant in NYC. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • ANNA FRANCES WILLIS 1.4.7.8.2.1
  • THOMAS WILLIS 1.4.7.8.2.2
  • EMILY AUGUSTA WILLIS 1.4.7.8.2.3
