GIDEON CLINTON FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.8.6.1.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.8.6.1.2) GIDEON CLINTON FERRIS born November 11, 1851 DeWitt NY and died August 19, 1931 Syracuse NY; buried DeWitt Cemetery (Photo); married 1st 1879 Mary Pitkin who died September 27, 1920; and 2d July 8, 1922 Dr. Ruth Watson of Spencer NY born August 15, 1885 and died November 21, 1955 Dunkirk NY; no offspring. [A G.C. Ferris owned 106 acres, Lot 61 in the Town of Dewitt, Onondaga County NY, 1874.] He was Superintendent of the Syracuse Division of the D. L. & W railroad for many years.

No children recorded
