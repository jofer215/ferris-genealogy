ANN DELIA FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.2.4.5 | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.4.5) ANN DELIA FERRIS born February 22, 1806 CT and died July 11, 1894 Jennings Co IN [Chaplain Ferris has Ann dying in Decatur IL] [Ann's date of birth is 3/4 years before her parent's purported wedding date, plus, her mother would have been 15 years of age, if Ann born in 1806.?]; married September 6, 1827 at Brookville IN, Michael Crawford Snell, Jr., born c1803 Albany NY and died August 1872 Jennings Co IN.
