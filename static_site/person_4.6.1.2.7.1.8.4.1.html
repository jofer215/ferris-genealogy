ROBERT KEELER WELDON

ID: 4.6.1.2.7.1.8.4.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.7.1.8.4.1) ROBERT KEELER WELDON (Bob) born January 22, 1911 Loveland CO and died there February 18, 2001; buried there Lakeside Cemetery; graduated from Colorado St U, 1933; forester in the Soil Conservation Service, 1935-38; WWII Army Vet served with the 10th Mountain Division spending 2 years in Italy with a special mountain warfare detachment attached to the British 8th Army; career with National Park Service at various national parks, including Hot Springs National Park AR, Mammoth Cave KY, Yellowstone National Park WY, Mount Rainier National Park WA, Whitman National Historical Site WA, and Rocky Mountain National Park CO; moved back to Loveland 1966 to the ranch that was homesteaded by his family; avid fly fisherman, gardener and photographer; married and had at least 3 kids

No children recorded
