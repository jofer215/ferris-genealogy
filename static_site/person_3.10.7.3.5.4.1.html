JAMES MARTIN PADDOCK

ID: 3.10.7.3.5.4.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.7.3.5.4.1) JAMES MARTIN PADDOCK born August 15, 1824 Port Bryan, Tioga, NY and died May 29, 1904 Percival, Fremont, IA; moved with his parents to MI when he was 14y and there grew to manhood and completed his education; held numerous offices of trust in Michigan; farmer 1850-80; 1850 resided Springfield, Oakland, MI; 1870 resided Benton, Fremont, IA; married 1st April 7, 1847 in Springfield MI, Elmira Chloe Green (Elmina Cloe) [dau of Horace & Diantha (Powell) Green] born March 18, 1829 Livonia, Livingston, NY and died December 30, 1897 Zeandale, Riley, KS – apparently divorced, since she also remarried; married 2d 1860 in Iowa, Olive Blanchard born June 1838 in Kansas; and born to him and Elmira were (marriage sequence reversed in some sources, but based on dates, this appears to be correct):
