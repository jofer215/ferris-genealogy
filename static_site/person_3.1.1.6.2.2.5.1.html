WILLIAM DEBURLING HAMMEL

ID: 3.1.1.6.2.2.5.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.2.2.5.1) WILLIAM DEBURLING HAMMEL born June 6, 1846 (1847) Dearborn Co IN and died October 3, 1908 Blocher, Jefferson, IN; married November 1847 in Cambridge, Dearborn Co IN, Mary Campbell [dau of Charlie and Mary] born 1851 IN; apparently no offspring, but did get guardianship of the Toombs kids, when their parents died young; both buried Pleasant Ridge Cemetery, Madison IN; he is listed as William B

No children recorded
