AMOS W. CHRISTIAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.9.8.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.9.8.1.1) AMOS W. CHRISTIAN born July 6, 1911 Beacon NY and died December 15, 2000 St. Francis Hospital, Poughkeepsie; private cremation took place in Ferncliff Crematory, Hartsdale NY; resided Cold Spring 1942; a retired heavy equipment operator for the operating engineer’s local #137 having worked on local projects, such as the construction of the first Newburgh-Beacon Bridge.
