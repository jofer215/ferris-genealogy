CAROLINE BOWNE LAWRENCE

ID: 1.7.2.1.1.1.2 | Source: John
(1.7.2.1.1.1.2) CAROLINE BOWNE LAWRENCE born September 17, 1827 and died August 17, 1869; married as his 2d wife, October 14, 1847 Henry Bedinger III (Photo) [son of Daniel & Sarah (Rutherford) Bedinger] born 1810 VA (February 3, 1812 (at Bedford) near Shepherdstown, Jefferson, VA – now WVA) and died November 26, 1858 Shepherdstown; interred Elmwood Cemetery (Old Bedinger Burying Ground and later relocated); attended the common schools, studied law, and was admitted to the bar 1832 and commenced practice in Shepherdstown, moved to Charlestown VA and continued the practice of law; elected as a Democrat to the 29th and 30th Congress (March 4, 1845 – March 3, 1849); unsuccessful candidate for reelection 1848; an eloquent speaker and an exceptionally able debater and was called the Eagle of Harpers Ferry; resumed the practice of law, appointed first Charge d’ Affaires to Demark on May 24, 1853 and Minister Resident June 29, 1854, in which capacity he served until August 10, 1858, when he resigned; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • MINNIE MITCHELL BEDINGER 1.7.2.1.1.1.2.1
  • HENRY BEDINGER 1.7.2.1.1.1.2.2
  • DANSKE BEDINGER 1.7.2.1.1.1.2.3
