NILES MOSES TOWNSEND

ID: 5.1.2.6.11.7.4 | Source: James
(5.1.2.6.11.7.4) NILES MOSES TOWNSEND born March 31, 1857 Steuben Co NY and died March 29, 1918 Bath NY; married c1877 in Steuben Co, Julia Seigner born 1857 and died 1942 Bath; both buried Unionville Cemetery, Coss Corners, Bath. Born to them were: (5.1.2.6.11.7.4.1) CASS R. TOWNSEND born December 12, 1890 Bath and died February 7, 1972 at 29 E. Morris Street, Bath NY; buried there Nondaga Cemetery
