THOMAS PECK

ID: 3.10.2.13 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.2.13) THOMAS PECK born September 1, 1750 Greenwich CT and died there September 30, 1781; married Mary (Johnson) Ferris (4.4.11.1). He was a Sergeant in Captain George Peck’s Company, Colonel John Mead’s Regiment, from August 13 to September 2, 1776 at New York and he served again as a guard at Greenwich from November 1, 1776 to January 8, 1777.
