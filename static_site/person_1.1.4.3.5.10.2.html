GEORGE ROBINSON

ID: 1.1.4.3.5.10.2 | Source: John
(1.1.4.3.5.10.2) GEORGE ROBINSON born c1842 Philipstown NY; married February 6, 1866, Emily Croft (1843-1885) [dau of Henry & Mary (Nelson)]; both buried at South Highland Cemetery, Philipstown. He volunteered for service in the Union Army in 1862 and continued until the end of the War. After his marriage, he embarked in the wood business at Garrison NY and for a time was very successful; he became involved financially and left the State. (?) Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • WILLIAM HENRY ROBINSON 1.1.4.3.5.10.2.1
  • LILIAN ROBINSON 1.1.4.3.5.10.2.2
