ABRAM D. FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.5.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.5.1.1) ABRAM D. FERRIS born 1818 Farmington, Ontario, NY and died May 30, 1896 Smyrna, Chenango, NY; Civil War Vet served with Company F, 157th NY Infantry, August 22, 1862 – July 10, 1865; married Lucy Ann Coleman (1824-1903) [dau of Noah & Chloe (Colburn) Coleman]; both buried Westhill Cemetery, Sherburne, Chenango, NY; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • SARAH E. FERRIS 5.1.3.1.5.1.1.1
  • LEWIS MONELL FERRIS 5.1.3.1.5.1.1.2
  • MORELL E FERRIS 5.1.3.1.5.1.1.3
