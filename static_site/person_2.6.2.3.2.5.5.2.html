THEODORE D KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.3.2.5.5.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.3.2.5.5.2) THEODORE D KNAPP (T.D.) born June 7, 1831 Steuben Co NY and died December 1915 Waycross (Ware) GA; brought from York NE to Waycross GA to be buried; teacher; married October 5, 1855 in Shelby Co KY, Martha Ellen Doak (Ella) [dau of Robert S & Susan (Hamlet) Doak] born January 26, 1835 Shelby Co KY and died July 16, 1904 Hastings NE; buried Greenwood Cemetery, York NY; 1861 moved from KY to IL; 1868 resided McDonough Co IL; 1873 moved toYork NE; c1902 moved to Hastings NE; and born to them were:
