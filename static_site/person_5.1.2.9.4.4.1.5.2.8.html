ROBERT DANIEL TOMPKINS

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.1.5.2.8 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.1.5.2.8) ROBERT DANIEL TOMPKINS born May 7, 1922 Philipstown NY and died June 1, 2001 Somers NY; married Frances Paula Perry [dau of Joseph & Anna (Cardella)] [sister of Rose above] born March 8, 1925 Sharon PA and died June 19, 1988 Carmel NY; both buried Maple Avenue Cemetery, Patterson NY. Obituary, Pawling News Chronicle, June 7, 2001: Robert D. Tompkins, 79, formerly of Patterson, died June 1, 2001 at the Somers Nursing Center, Somers, after a long illness. Mr. Tompkins was employed as a truck driver and heavy equipment operator, and most of his working career he had worked for Romanoff Builders and McGlasson Builders, both of Carmel. He was a former member of the Wacabuc Fire Dept. Born May 7, 1922 in Peekskill, he was the son of the late Hart and Jessie (O’Dell) Tompkins. He attended schools in Brewster and had lived in the northern Westchester and Putnam area all of his life. On March 17, 1945 he married Frances (Perry) Tompkins in Farrow, Pa. She died June 19, 1988. Mr. Tompkins is survived by six sons, James E. and Michael D., both of Carmel; Robert F. and Joseph V., both of Dover Plains and Garrett L. and Jay M. both of Pawling. He also is survived by a brother, James Tompkins and a sister, June Tompkins, both of Carmel; 10 grandchildren and five great grandchildren. Interment was in Maple Avenue Cemetery, Patterson.
