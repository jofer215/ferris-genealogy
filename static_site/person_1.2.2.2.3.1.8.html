JOSHUA UNDERHILL RUMSEY

ID: 1.2.2.2.3.1.8 | Source: John
(1.2.2.2.3.1.8) JOSHUA UNDERHILL RUMSEY born December 8, 1823 Monroe, Orange, NY and died December 23, 1895 Van Etten NY; married Sarah Eleanor Getman born September 21, 1836 Johnstown, Fulton, NY and died February 11, 1899 Van Etten NY. Born to them were: (1.2.2.2.3.1.8.1) SYLVANUS EARL RUMSEY born December 4, 1853 Van Etten NY and died November 10, 1927 Chemung Co NY; married Mary Isabella Shelford born March 10, 1859 Chemung Co NY and died there September 3, 1920. Born to them were:

Children (5)
