LUCIUS DARBY

ID: 3.10.1.2.1.9.4.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.2.1.9.4.6) LUCIUS DARBY born circa January 1845 and died August 04, 1865 Cortland NY; Lucius Darby died at age 20 and is buried along side his mother & father, 1/2 brother Thomas D. Darby who died at age 9, his older brother Wayland D. Darby who died at age 39 at his brother Thomas's home in Clarksville, Iowa. They all share a headstone with inscriptions on every side of the stone.

No children recorded
