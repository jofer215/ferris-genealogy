GEORGE FROST COLE

ID: 1.2.3.7.2.6.3.3 | Source: John
(1.2.3.7.2.6.3.3) GEORGE FROST COLE born April 2 or 29, 1829 and died September 8, 1884 Beaver Dams NY; buried Beaver Dams Cemetery; a farmer; married December 24, 1850 at Post Creek NY, Mary Almira Stewart born May 8, 1825 Ulysses, Tompkins, NY and died October 19, 1908 Beaver Dams NY [dau of Henry & Hepsibah (Van Loom) Stewart]; and born to them were:

Children (6)

  • JACK COLE 1.2.3.7.2.6.3.3.1
  • FANNIE FERN COLE 1.2.3.7.2.6.3.3.2
  • ALBERT GEORGE COLE 1.2.3.7.2.6.3.3.4
  • IDA MAY COLE 1.2.3.7.2.6.3.3.5
  • EDWARD EARLE COLE 1.2.3.7.2.6.3.3.6
  • BURR MARION COLE 1.2.3.7.2.6.3.3.7
