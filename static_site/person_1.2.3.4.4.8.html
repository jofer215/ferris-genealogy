DEBORAH SECOR

ID: 1.2.3.4.4.8 | Source: John
(1.2.3.4.4.8) DEBORAH SECOR born August 29, 1793 and died September 1851; married November 19, 1829 in Yonkers, (Elijah S) Elijah Tompkins [son of Noah Bishop & Rachel (Coles) Tompkins] born April 26, 1793 White Plains NY and died June 27, 1888; farmer Rye NY; and born to them were: (1.2.3.4.4.8.1) NOAH TOMPKINS (Noah S) born November 6, 1834 and died March 12, 1885 South Orange NJ; married October 3, 1871 Mary G Haviland born August 25, 1841 and died June 3, 1905; and born to them were:

Children (2)
