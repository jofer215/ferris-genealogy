LISETTE BELLE REYNOLDS

ID: 3.1.1.3.1.4.10.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.3.1.4.10.4) LISETTE BELLE REYNOLDS born July 2, 1864 Cross River and died April 5, 1927 Greenwich CT (one day after her second husband); buried at the Reynolds Cemetery, Cross River; married 1st June 29, 1884 Herbert Lawton Miller born October 30, 1864 and died July 5, 1897 Stamford CT; married 2d c1899 Charles Henry Schifferdecker born April 29, 1859 Germany and died April 4, 1927 Greenwich CT; Lisette was a member of the Christ's Church of Greenwich; and born to Lisette and Herbert were:

Children (6)

  • DAVID DEWITT MILLER 3.1.1.3.1.4.10.4.2
  • DOUGLAS ALBERTIS MILLER 3.1.1.3.1.4.10.4.3
  • CLARENCE HERBERT MILLER 3.1.1.3.1.4.10.4.4
  • LEONA BELLE MILLER 3.1.1.3.1.4.10.4.5
  • CHARLES HENRY SCHIFFERDECKER 3.1.1.3.1.4.10.4.6
