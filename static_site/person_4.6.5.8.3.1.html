AARON HOYT

ID: 4.6.5.8.3.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.8.3.1) AARON HOYT (#4373) born, 1800 and died May 14, 1844 at age 43 years, 6 months; buried at a private cemetery near corner at Sarles Corner, Pound Ridge NY - records have him as Aaron H. Hoyt. He married, 1820, Anna Hoyt (#4395) (4.6.5.11.2.1); no offspring. They resided in Pound Ridge.

No children recorded
