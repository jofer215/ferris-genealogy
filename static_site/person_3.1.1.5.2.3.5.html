EMMA BEERS PELTON

ID: 3.1.1.5.2.3.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.5.2.3.5) EMMA BEERS PELTON born June 22, 1837 Ridgeway, Elk, PA and died September 10, 1912 Coleville, McKean, PA; married January 22, 1869 at Sierra Ville CA, George Wallace of St. Louis MO; 1890 resided at Farmer's Valley PA. Maybe this was her 2d marriage; another source has her marrying John Herzog born c1819 in Germany and born to them was a George Herzog

No children recorded
