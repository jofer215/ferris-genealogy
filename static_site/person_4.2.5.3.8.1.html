JERUSHA LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.2.5.3.8.1 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.3.8.1) JERUSHA LOCKWOOD born c1822 Greenwich (Jerusha Rebecca Lockwood born March 30, 1823 Boardman OH); married April 5, 1843 in Canfield, Mahoning, OH, William Wallace Whittlesey [son of Elisha & Polly (Mygatt) Whittlesey] born April 10, 1820 Canfield OH and died July 5, 1890 Washington, D.C; and born to them were (25y between 1st and last child – Jerusha would have been 46y when last child born) (a William W Whittlesey is listed as a company secretary for Mahoning Plank Road Company 1863 and earlier; a William W Whittlesey is listed as a clerk in the Pension Office in D.C, 1882; William’s father Elisha was one of the founders of the Whig Party):

Children (5)

  • CLAYSON LOCKWOOD WHITTLESEY 4.2.5.3.8.1.1
  • AARON WHITTLESEY 4.2.5.3.8.1.2
  • HARRIET WHITTLESEY 4.2.5.3.8.1.3
  • LUCY WHITTLESEY 4.2.5.3.8.1.4
  • SARAH WHITTLESEY 4.2.5.3.8.1.6
