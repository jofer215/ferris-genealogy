JOHN BANKS FERRIS

ID: 1.6.1.4.5.2 | Source: John
(1.6.1.4.5.2) JOHN BANKS FERRIS born Bedford June 1, 1812 and died there June 27, 1898 (Plot 17); married 1st Mary Jane Lyons born February 3, 1825 and died Bedford October 23, 1859 (Jim-10 days after the birth of John) (Plot 18); and 2d Hanna Reynolds born North Castle May 28, 1843 and died Bedford June 24, 1928.
