WILLIAM FERRIS

ID: 2.1.4.1.4.1 | Source: Peter
(2.1.4.1.4.1) WILLIAM FERRIS (1794-1878) “Uncle Bill” married 1819 Anna Van Luvannee of Philadelphia PA. William came to Blakely Township PA when it was part of Providence, his father, James Ferris, being the first settler on the east side of the Lackawanna River, and building a cabin in 1798. William, although for a long lifetime in rather delicate health, became an extensive farmer and the first practicing physician of the place, his skill in preparing roots and herbs making him widely known among the pioneers.
