HARRY VERNON UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.6.2.1.2.2.4.6.9 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.1.2.2.4.6.9) HARRY VERNON UNDERHILL born August 12, 1917 near Millbank SD and died September 19, 1987 San Diego CA; buried there in family plot; Obit: Anchorage Daily News, Harry V. Underhill, known to his friends as Harry the Barber, died at age 70, while vacationing at San Diego. He fished commercially out of Seldovia, worked in construction, and was a barber for many years in Anchorage working at the Union Club Barber Shop in downtown Anchorage; married Mary Winters. (1.2.6.2.1.2.2.4.6.10) KATHRYN SHIRLEY UNDERHILL married Willford Morrison; resided San Diego CA 1980

No children recorded
