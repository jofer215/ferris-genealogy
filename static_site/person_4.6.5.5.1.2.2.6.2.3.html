NETTIE COWLES

ID: 4.6.5.5.1.2.2.6.2.3 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.5.1.2.2.6.2.3) NETTIE COWLES born August 24, 1890 Riverside KS and died February 4, 1957 Nickerson, Reno, KS; married May 17, 1910 in Riverside KS, as his 1st wife, Lewis Alpheus Don Jeffrey [son of William Lewis & Lodemia Jane (Marine) Jeffrey] born July 23, 1888 Bazine, Ness, KS and died February 7, 1963 Hutchinson KS; both buried Wildmead Cemetery, Nickerson KS (Photos); farmer and barber; they moved to Nickerson from Colorado in 1930 where he was a barber until 1946, when he owned “Jeff’s Fixit Shop”; and born to them were:

Children (6)
