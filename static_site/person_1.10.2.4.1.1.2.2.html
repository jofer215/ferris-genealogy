BENJAMIN MOTT UNDERHILL

ID: 1.10.2.4.1.1.2.2 | Source: John
(1.10.2.4.1.1.2.2) BENJAMIN MOTT UNDERHILL (1863-1930) attended Swarthworth College PA; 1895 U of Penn School of Veterinary Medicine; 1908-30 U of Penn Pathology and Parasitology Professor; author; Delaware Co Institute of Science Secretary; active member Providence Friends Meeting, Media PA; married 1912 Georgia Cook Myers (1877-1926) [dau of John Toner & Sarah Ann (Cook) Myers]; attended Swarthworth College; and they had 2 kids

No children recorded
