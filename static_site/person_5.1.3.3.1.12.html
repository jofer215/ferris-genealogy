AMY FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.12 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.12) AMY FERRIS married Benjamin Johnson; they moved to Wisconsin ********************************************************************** At one time I made the assumption that the following Amy Ferris may have been a daughter of Solomon, but she is not according to Chaplain Ferris’ book - I am going to leave her here for now, pending further research - the below numbers are not valid - are just for reference. AMY FERRIS - Jim - the following is an assumption/ speculation based on information from the Thomas Davenport book - Amy born March 20, 1811 at New Castle, Westchester County NY and died July 18, 1900. She married Peter Mekeel born in Philipstown NY and died at his home there, September 29, 1885, aged 62 years. Mr. Mekeel passed his entire life as a farmer in Philipstown. He seems to have occupied the old Snook farm where apparently his children were all born. About 1846, he moved to the Mekeel homestead, where he died. Born to them were:

No children recorded
