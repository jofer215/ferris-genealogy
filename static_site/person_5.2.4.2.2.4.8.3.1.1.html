PSYCHE SUE WINTHROP

ID: 5.2.4.2.2.4.8.3.1.1 | Source: James
(5.2.4.2.2.4.8.3.1.1) PSYCHE SUE WINTHROP (what are parents thinking?) born January 17, 1897 Fairland, Cherokee Nation, Indian Territory and died July 16, 1975 Annapolis MD; graduate U of Kansas 1919; bacteriologist; married September 10, 1919 in Wichita, Bruce Wetmore [son of Frank W & Mary Alice (Patterson) Wetmore] born September 5, 1893 Wichita and died December 16, 1943 Washington, D.C.; both buried Wichita; salesman, driver, aircraft parts inspector

No children recorded
