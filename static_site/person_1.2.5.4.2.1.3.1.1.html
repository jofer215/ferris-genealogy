JULIA ROBBINS

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.3.1.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.3.1.1) JULIA ROBBINS born December 7, 1861 and died December 24, 1932; buried Friends Cemetery, Jericho NY. (It is noted that an extra ‘R’ has been added to Julia’s name.); married 1888 George E. Schryver born September 1862 and died November 16, 1919 Jericho NY; buried there in Friends Cemetery. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • ARTHUR CARPENTER SCHRYVER 1.2.5.4.2.1.3.1.1.2
  • ETTA S. SCHRYVER 1.2.5.4.2.1.3.1.1.3
  • GEORGE UNDERHILL SCHRYVER 1.2.5.4.2.1.3.1.1.4
