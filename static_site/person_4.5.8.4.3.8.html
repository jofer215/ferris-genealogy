SAMUEL THEOPHILUS LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.5.8.4.3.8 | Source: Mary
(4.5.8.4.3.8) SAMUEL THEOPHILUS LOCKWOOD (#1375) born April 23, 1834 Hastings, Oswego, NY; 1880 resided at Chicago IL; 1896 in Portland OR; and died June 3, 1913 Pasadena CA; married December 31, 1856 at Burlington WI, Juliet Paddock [dau of Aaron Burr & Susan Maria (Norton) Paddock] born July 29, 1835 Onondaga Co NY and died August 20, 1921 Long Beach CA; resided in Portland OR and moved to CA in 1907; and born to them were: (4.5.8.4.3.8.1) FLORA EUGENIA LOCKWOOD (#2267) born May 11, 1859 Burlington Village, Racine, WI and died May 26, 1873 Chicago IL (?)
