CHARLES W SAWYER

ID: 5.1.3.6.4.11.2.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.6.4.11.2.2) CHARLES W SAWYER (Photo) born February 10, 1887 Cincinnati OH and died April 7, 1979 Palm Beach FL; graduated Oberlin College, A. B., 1908; Graduated U. of Cincinnati, L. L. B., 1911; admitted to Ohio Bar, 1911; L.L.D, 1950; member Cincinnati City Council, 1911-15; enlisted in the Army, 1917, commissioned Captain, Infantry, 1917 and served with the AEF, 1918-19 honorably discharged as Major, 1919; Lieutenant Governor of Ohio, 1933-34; Democratic candidate for Governor, 1938; member of Democratic National Committee from Ohio, 1936-44; Ambassador to Belgium, 1944-45; and U. S. Secretary of Commerce, May 6, 1948 to January 20, 1953; Member of Cincinnati law firm Dinsmore, Shohl, Sawyer, & Dinsmore; married 1st July 15, 1918 Margaret Sterrett Johnston (Margaret Sterrer Johnston) born 1887 and died July 1, 1937; and 2d June 10, 1942 Elizabeth L. DeVeyrac (Elizabeth Lippelman). Born to Charles and Margaret were:
