STEPHEN HAIT

ID: 4.6.5.3.2 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.3.2) STEPHEN HAIT (#3388) born in Pound Ridge NY. He was commissioner of highways in Pound Ridge, 1807-09. Circa 1849, he moved to western New York, probably Mt. Morris, Livingston County. No information available on his wife, but born to them were:

Children (6)

  • WILLIAM HAIT 4.6.5.3.2.1
  • LOCKWOOD HAIT lived at Mt. Morris NY. 4.6.5.3.2.2
  • WEALTHY HAIT 4.6.5.3.2.3
  • HARRIET HAIT who died before she reached 18 years of age 4.6.5.3.2.4
  • MARY ANN HAIT 4.6.5.3.2.5
  • JULIET HAIT 4.6.5.3.2.6
