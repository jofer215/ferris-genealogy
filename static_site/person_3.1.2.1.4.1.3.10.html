HARRIET GODFREY

ID: 3.1.2.1.4.1.3.10 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.4.1.3.10) HARRIET GODFREY born February 24, 1831 Genesee Co NY and died December 31, 1907 Calhoun Co MI; married November 20, 1852 in Jackson Co MI, Sylvester G (or C.) Ludlow who died March 1, 1871 age 39y 6m 5d; both buried Campbell Cemetery a few miles north of Parma, Jackson Co MI; farmer 1870; and born to them was:
