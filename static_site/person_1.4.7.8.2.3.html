EMILY AUGUSTA WILLIS

ID: 1.4.7.8.2.3 | Source: John
(1.4.7.8.2.3) EMILY AUGUSTA WILLIS born March 5, 1843 NYC; married May 30, 1866 in NYC, John Frederick Scott [son of William & Louisa (Hoyt) Scott] born May 27, 1841 NYC and died there December 25, 1895.There were members of the Episcopal Church and he was a broker; also Treasurer and later President of the New York Coffee Exchange. Born to them were:
