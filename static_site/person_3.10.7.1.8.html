REBECCA PECK

ID: 3.10.7.1.8 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.7.1.8) REBECCA PECK born August 1, 1771 Greenwich and died October 13, 1838 Bedford NY; married Thomas Purdy who died December 8, 1843 Bedford NY; after Rebecca’s death, Thomas married Mary Peck (3.10.7.1.2.3)

No children recorded
