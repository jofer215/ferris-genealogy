WILLIAM BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.2.9.2 | Source: James
(5.2.2.2.9.2) WILLIAM BUXTON born May (March) 25, 1802 Stamford CT and died there November 4, 1858; buried Newfield Cemetery (Photo), Stamford; 1850 shoemaker; married December 12/13, 1827 in Stamford, Anna Maria Wilmot [dau of James & Abigail (Lockwood) Wilmot] born in August 1812 (1808) Stamford and died there December 10, 1862; buried Newfield Cemetery. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • JAMES NICHOLS BUXTON 5.2.2.2.9.2.2
  • SARAH A. BUXTON 5.2.2.2.9.2.3
