HELEN ELIZABETH FRYE

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.3.1.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.3.1.1) HELEN ELIZABETH FRYE born August 21, 1906 Albany NY and died 1995 Sterling, Whiteside, IL; married January 1, 1927 Walter Carlyle Cameron, Sr., [son of Walter Henry & Lamina Stone Cameron] born January 24, 1903 and died August 4, 1979. Walter owned the Cameron Bakery in Sterling. Helen is credited with providing much of the information for Jim Lieland's book, referenced at the end of this chapter. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • MARJORIE HELEN CAMERON 5.1.3.4.10.4.3.1.1.1
  • WALTER CARLYLE CAMERON, JR., 5.1.3.4.10.4.3.1.1.2
  • JOYCE MARIE CAMERON 5.1.3.4.10.4.3.1.1.3
