WILLIAM HENRY LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.4.2.1.1.1.1 | Source: Mary
(4.4.2.1.1.1.1) WILLIAM HENRY LOCKWOOD (#2363) born December 1, 1848 Stamford CT and died after 1900; farmer 1880-82; married 1st c1874 Anna L Saunders [dau of William Henry & Mary Frances (Lockwood) Saunders] born July 2, 1853 Stamford and died there August 22, 1878; buried there Woodland Cemetery; married 2d c1879 as her 2d husband, Mary F Fields born August 1846 NY and died 1928 Stamford; buried there Emmanuel Cemetery; and born to him and Anna was:
