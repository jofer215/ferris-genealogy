ALFRED HOLLY SMITH

ID: 2.1.3.3.2.4.1.7 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.2.4.1.7) ALFRED HOLLY SMITH (Fred) born February 11, 1853 Koloa, Kauai, HI and died May 27, 1928 Honolulu HI; buried Smith-Waterhouse Cemetery, Koloa; married April 25, 1888 in Brooklyn NY, Edith Whitaker [dau of Willliam] born September 21, 1859 Cincinnati OH and died November 2, 1940 Honolulu; buried there. He was Governor of the Lunalilo Home. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • RAYMOND WHITAKER SMITH 2.1.3.3.2.4.1.7.1
  • ALICE WINONA SMITH 2.1.3.3.2.4.1.7.2
