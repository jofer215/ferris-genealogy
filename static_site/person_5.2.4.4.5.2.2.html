SAMUEL LOCKWOOD, JR.,

ID: 5.2.4.4.5.2.2 | Source: James
(5.2.4.4.5.2.2) SAMUEL LOCKWOOD, JR., born September 13, 1819 Stamford and died there March 13, 1869; between 1850-60 was a dentist; married September 10, 1846 in Stamford, Sarah Elizabeth Leeds (2.1.7.1.3.3.2); both buried there Woodland Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (5)

  • MARY CATHERINE LOCKWOOD 5.2.4.4.5.2.2.1
  • CORNELIA LEEDS LOCKWOOD 5.2.4.4.5.2.2.2
  • SARAH ELIZA LOCKWOOD 5.2.4.4.5.2.2.3
  • JOSEPHINE EDNA LOCKWOOD died young September 15, 1862 5.2.4.4.5.2.2.4
  • EDWARD F. LOCKWOOD 5.2.4.4.5.2.2.5
