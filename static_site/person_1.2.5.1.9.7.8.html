DAVID ROGERS UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.1.9.7.8 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.9.7.8) DAVID ROGERS UNDERHILL born January 23, 1834 and died March 27, 1908; at one point resided Brooklyn; married 1st 1855 Phebe Field born September 6, 1833 and died 1891; 2d, as her 2d husband, 1896 Elizabeth Davenport (1833-1923); and born to him and Phebe were:

Children (3)

  • JOHN THOMAS UNDERHILL 1.2.5.1.9.7.8.2
  • CAROLINE UNDERHILL 1.2.5.1.9.7.8.3
