(5.1.2.9.4.4.2.1.5) GEORGE E. SMITH Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, Wednesday, March 9, 1977: George E. Smith, 89, of Old Rte. 6, Brewster, died March 3 in Putnam Community Hospital, Carmel, after a long illness. Mr. Smith born Dec. 2, 1887, in Kent, son of the late Orlando and Sarah Bennett Smith. A lifelong resident of the area, he attended Carmel schools and was a retired farmer. He is survived by his wife, Pearl Ganun Smith; five sons, Supervisors Donald B. Smith of Patterson, George Smith of Brewster, Rodney and Gene Smith, both of Virginia and David Smith of Clifton Park, NY; three daughters, Mrs. Joe Bove and Mrs. Gerry Remer, both of Brewster and Mrs. Fred Vieria of Texas; a sister, Mrs. Mary Nelson of Carmel; 24 grandchildren, 8 great grandchildren and several nieces and nephews. Obituary, Danbury Times, Danbury, Conn., Friday, Sept. 5, 1987: Pearl G. Smith, 86, of Old Route 6, Brewster, wife of the late George Smith, died yesterday at Putnam Center after a short illness. She born in Brewster, Jan. 9, 1901, daughter of the late Irving and Jennie Slocum Smith. She attended Brewster schools and had been a Brewster resident all her life. Mrs. Smith had been a grade school teacher and a Guideposts retiree, as well as a member of the Brewster United Methodist Church. She is survived by three daughters, Alma Viera of Texas, Nina Bove and Hilda Remer, both of Brewster, five sons, Donald of Patterson, George Jr. of Brewster, Rodney and Gene, both of Virginia, and David of Clifton Park, NY, and a sister, Mable GaNum.
