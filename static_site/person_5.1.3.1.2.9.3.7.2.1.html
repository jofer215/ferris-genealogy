MARJORIE ELLEN PAGE

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.9.3.7.2.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.9.3.7.2.1) MARJORIE ELLEN PAGE born February 13, 1921 West Seneca, Erie, NY and died August 6, 1992 Syracuse NY; buried Onondaga Co Veterans Cemetery, Syracuse, Onondaga, NY; Masters of Library Science, Syracuse U; Librarian; graduated from Watertown H. S.; Potsdam Normal School 1942 with a teacher’s certificate; married in Washington, D.C., Merwin Jones – WWII Army Vet; school teacher; and they had 7 kids

No children recorded
