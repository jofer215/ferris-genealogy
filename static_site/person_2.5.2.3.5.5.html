ENOCH HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.3.5.5 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.3.5.5) ENOCH HOYT (#3525) born July 8, 1771 Stamford and died there April 8, 1819 (consumption) where he lived and was a farmer and lumber dealer; buried there Woodland Cemetery; married July 21, 1805 in Stamford, Hannah Lockwood (4.2.5.2.6); and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • SARAH HOYT 2.5.2.3.5.5.1
  • WILLIAM CLASEN HOYT 2.5.2.3.5.5.3
  • JOHN PETER HOYT 2.5.2.3.5.5.4
