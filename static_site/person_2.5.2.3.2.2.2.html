DAVID HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.3.2.2.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.3.2.2.2) DAVID HOYT (#4488) born September 16, 1799 Stephentown NY; a farmer who moved from New Haven to St. Lawrence Co NY 1827 to Allen Co IN 1843 and 1871 was living in Edgewood, Effingham Co IL; married December 28, 1824 Submit L. Burnham

No children recorded
