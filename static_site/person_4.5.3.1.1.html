DAVID LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.5.3.1.1 | Source: Mary
(4.5.3.1.1) DAVID LOCKWOOD (#650) born May 15, 1758 (?) and died September 6, 1825; enlisted May 8, 1775 in the 5th Regiment, Colonel Waterbury’s, and was discharged December 13, 1775 and afterwards served in an Artillery Company and also in Captain Keeler’s company; married November 7, 1779 at Stamford, Hannah Holly born c1759 Stamford and in 1837 was in her 78th year. Born to them were:

Children (2)
