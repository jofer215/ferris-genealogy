EMILY HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.3.2.8.7 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.3.2.8.7) EMILY HOYT (#4501) born April 17, 1825 (2.5.2.3.2.9) HENRY HOYT, JR., (#3511) - twin - born December 31, 1780 and died 1823 on Ossabaw Island GA. He was selectman 1810 and representative 1811 and 1812 from Stamford and was in the ship-timber business [Jim-There was a 2nd Lieutenant Henry Hoyt listed on a muster roll of a company of infantry under the command of David Waterbury, Captain in the 37th Regiment of the U.S., commanded by LtCol Aaron Benjamin from the 31st Oct when last mustered to the 31st Dec 1813. probably the same one, War of 1812.]; married November 13, 1803 Sarah Brown who died November 27, 1852.
