HANNAH MOTT

ID: 1.10.2.7 | Source: John
(1.10.2.7) HANNAH MOTT born April 18, 1753 and died February 8, 1805 {Mott Book states she was unmarried as 1782} {Mott Book lists only one Hannah and it is presumed to be this one, particularly since the earlier child died young and before this Hannah born.}

No children recorded
