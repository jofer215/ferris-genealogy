BALTIS T. HILL

ID: 3.6.3.2.2.1.2.9 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.2.1.2.9) BALTIS T. HILL born April 20, 1849 (?) and died 1929; married Elizabeth Morris (1849-1927). He was known as B.J. - he was a teacher and they lived on a farm in Swartswood NJ. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • FRANK HILL was a railroad mail clerk and lived in Washington, D.C. 3.6.3.2.2.1.2.9.1
  • GEORGE HILL - no offspring 3.6.3.2.2.1.2.9.2
  • WILLIAM HILL - no offspring 3.6.3.2.2.1.2.9.3
  • CHARLES HILL 3.6.3.2.2.1.2.9.4
