BENJAMIN SELLECK

ID: 3.10.5.3.1.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.5.3.1.3) BENJAMIN SELLECK born 1785 Stamford and died April 16, 1846 Greenwich; owned large tracts of land in Texas and had been located at a place called Selleck’s Bluff – certain evidence suggests that he died at Selleck’s Bluff TX; married February 28, 1811 Deborah Peck (3.10.2.10.3) – see her for offspring because the info was entered there first

No children recorded
