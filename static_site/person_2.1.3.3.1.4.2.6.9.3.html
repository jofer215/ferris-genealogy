BERNARD WILLIAMSON

ID: 2.1.3.3.1.4.2.6.9.3 | Source: Peter
(2.1.3.3.1.4.2.6.9.3) BERNARD WILLIAMSON (2.1.3.3.1.4.2.7) ISAAC W. CLASON born June 25, 1839 Hornellsville NY and died January 18, 1865 Cedar Grove, Olmsted, MN; buried Pleasant Prairie Cemetery, Olmsted Co, MN (Photo); Civil War Vet with Company B, 2d Minnesota Infantry Regiment; discharged as a Corporal; Isaac kept a diary through part of the Civil War and that diary is at www.rootsweb.com/~widodge/clason.htm and much more info on this family can be found at the Beaver Dam Community Library

No children recorded
