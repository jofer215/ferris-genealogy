RUTH GENEVA PETTIS

ID: 5.2.2.6.1.2.2.2.4 | Source: James
(5.2.2.6.1.2.2.2.4) RUTH GENEVA PETTIS born January 12, 1891 Winnebago, Winnebago, IL; married 1st April 24, 1924 in Rockford IL, Harry Merton Jewett (c1890-); married 2d September 1, 1946 in Rockford, George Walter Greenhalgh (c1890-); and born to her and Harry was: (5.2.2.6.1.2.2.2.4.1) LEWIS BUXTON JEWITT married Marilyn Jean Hopkins and they had 3 kids – resided in San Diego CA

No children recorded
