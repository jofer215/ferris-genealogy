MINDWELL FERRIS

ID: 3.1.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.5) MINDWELL FERRIS (8RLL-4V) born c1703/1704/1710 probably Greenwich and died c1755 New Fairfield CT; married August 15, 1729 at Greenwich, as his 1st wife, Joseph Rundle Sr., born c1701/02 Horseneck (Greenwich) CT and died June 16, 1778 New Fairfield CT. [Mindwell’s father John, deeded some land to Joseph][Jim - wonder if this Rundle was any relation to the Rundle that married a Ferris lady in Knox County, Ill. in the 1800s?][The surname Rundle was variously spelled by each succeeding generation, i.e., Randall, Rundell, Rundall, etc - Rundle has been used throughout here]{Joseph’s second wife was Experience Peck; I do not know if the last 3 children show under Mindwell and Joseph are in fact from Mindwell or from Experience.} Born to them were:
