ADELLA ALMIRA WATERMAN

ID: 3.11.4.3.2.9.1.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.4.3.2.9.1.3) ADELLA ALMIRA WATERMAN born January 18, 1851 Coldwater, Branch, MI and died April 15, 1940; married October 28, 1874 in Grand Rapids, Kent, MI, Herman Starbuck Bailey [son of Sluman Shuman & Delia Gertrude (Starbuck) Bailey] born April 27, 1846 Sumerset, Niagra, NY and died March 25, 1922 MI.
