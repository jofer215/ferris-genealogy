BETSEY JESSUP

ID: 5.1.4.4.3.1 | Source: James
(5.1.4.4.3.1) BETSEY JESSUP born 1784 Bridgewater, Litchfield, CT and died January 16, 1877 Franklin, Delaware, NY; married 1st John Fenn who died February 18, 1819; 2d June 1, 1825 as his 2d wife, David Hawley [son of Benjamin & Anna (Baldwin) Hawley] born 1777 Brookfield, Fairfield, CT and died November 23, 1862 Franklin NY; and born to her and John was:

Children (2)

  • BETSEY A FENN died February 8, 1870; married September 18, 1828, her step-brother, 5.1.4.4.3.1.1
  • LAURA ANN HAWLEY 5.1.4.4.3.1.2
