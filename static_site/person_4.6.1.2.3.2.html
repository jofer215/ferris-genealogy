JESSE CLOSE LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.1.2.3.2 | Source: Mary
(4.6.1.2.3.2) JESSE CLOSE LOCKWOOD (#1377) born January 7, 1791 Pound Ridge NY and died June 15, 1847 IL; married January 7, 1816 Elisa Runion Bartholomew born January 14, 1795 and died April 2, 1857. They moved from NY to IL, circa 1820 and settled at McLeansboro, Hamilton County. Jesse was commissioned Clerk of the Circuit Court on March 10, 1821; appointed County Clerk April 9, 1821; appointed Notary Public February 18, 1821; and appointed Probate Judge, February 13, 1821 [4 public offices at one time]. He was appointed Postmaster at McLeansboro, 1824; elected Probate Judge, 1825; elected Justice of Peace, 1827; appointed to the office of Circuit Clerk, 1833; commissioned Justice of Peace, 1835; commissioned as Recorder of Deeds, 1835. He assisting in organizing the County of Hamilton and held all of the offices for twenty years with honor and credit to himself and many friends. Born to them were:
