ID: 4.6.11.5.8 | Source: Mary
(4.6.11.5.8) ANNA ST. JOHN baptized July 29, 1804 Greenfield, Saratoga, NY and died September 25, 1886 Eden, Erie, NY; married 1st before 1820, Welcome Betts Chadderdon [son of Abraham & Lydia (Stone) Chadderdon]; 2d probably after 1839,Benjamin Clark, her step-brother. In 1866 they were living about two miles south of the Village of Hamburg on the road to Eden. No offspring from the second marriage. Born to Anna and Welcome were:

Children (3)

  • SARAH JANE CHADDERDON 4.6.11.5.8.1
  • LAURA CHADDERDON 4.6.11.5.8.2
  • JACOB L. CHADDERDON 4.6.11.5.8.3
