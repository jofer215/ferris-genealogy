RUTH KNAPP

ID: 5.1.3.3.4.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.4.2) RUTH KNAPP born March 25, 1789 and died February 13, 1871 near Cincinnati OH; married c1810/11 John Jay Ferris (5.1.3.8.2); moved to Linwood OH and both were buried at the Mariemont Cemetery, Cincinnati OH; see John for offspring

No children recorded
