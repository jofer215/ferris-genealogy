FERRIS JAYCOX

ID: 1.1.4.3.5.11.1 | Source: John
(1.1.4.3.5.11.1) FERRIS JAYCOX born February 15, 1847 Putnam Valley NY and died there May 2, 1919; buried Cold Spring Cemetery, Philipstown NY; married June 28, 1911 in Garrison NY, Susan Post [dau of Henry W. & Sarah J. (Galloway)] born May 1858 Putnam Valley and died there August 5, 1927. He was a proprietor of a black smith and wagon making business at Garrison; was connected officially with the affairs of the South Highland Church and also as teacher and superintendent in the Sunday School there and at Mountain Chapel. No offspring. Interesting that he married at age 64 for the first time (?).

No children recorded
