SARAH HUSTED

ID: 2.1.8.5.7 | Source: Peter
(2.1.8.5.7) SARAH HUSTED born April 13, 1766 New Canaan CT and died November 3, 1843; married November 4, 1790 in Greenwich, Daniel Reynolds [son of Daniel] born November 9, 1768 and died June 7, 1837; buried Sound Beach; and born to them was: (2.1.8.5.7.1) JOHN H. REYNOLDS born July 23, 1791; married c1816 Deborah Knapp born c1795 Stamford

No children recorded
