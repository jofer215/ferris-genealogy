ABIGAIL FERRIS

ID: 3.1.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.4) ABIGAIL FERRIS (8RLL-3P) born c1702 (April 13, 1701)[Chaplain Ferris has c1712 which is inconsistent with the birth dates of her children] [another sources has her date of birth 1708] probably at Greenwich; married 1st c1722 Jonathan Husted [son of Angell & Mary (Mead) – grandson of Robert] (various birth dates: 1683, 1690/98,1700 - died 1733); and 2d c1736 as his 2d wife Ebenezer Gregory [son of Thomas and Elizabeth (Pardee) Gregory] born October 27, 1683 and died May 30, 1760; and born to Abigail and Jonathan were:

Children (5)

  • JONATHAN HUSTED 3.1.4.1
  • PETER HUSTED 3.1.4.2
  • JOSEPH GREGORY 3.1.4.3
  • JABEZ GREGORY 3.1.4.4
  • ABIGAIL GREGORY 3.1.4.5
