PRUDENCE BELL

ID: 5.2.4.8 | Source: James
(5.2.4.8) PRUDENCE BELL born January 28, 1750/51 Stamford and died there December 23, 1794; buried North Stamford Cemetery. She married May 4, 1772 in Stamford as his 1st wife, John Davenport (2.5.2.1.3.1) and born to them were:
