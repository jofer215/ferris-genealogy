FLORENCE ELIZABETH COLE

ID: 1.2.3.7.2.6.3.7.2 | Source: John
(1.2.3.7.2.6.3.7.2) FLORENCE ELIZABETH COLE born October 22, 1871 Watkins NY; married 1st October 1, 1888 at Elmira NY, Walter Colgrove who died at Burdett NY [son of Silas C. and Mary E. Colgrove]; married 2d January 13, 1906 at Watkins, Edward Clarence Cooper born September 19, 1874 Elizabeth PA [son of William and Mary (Martin) Cooper]
