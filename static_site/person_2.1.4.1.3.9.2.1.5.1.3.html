DANNY LEE COTNOIR

ID: 2.1.4.1.3.9.2.1.5.1.3 | Source: Peter
(2.1.4.1.3.9.2.1.5.1.3) DANNY LEE COTNOIR born July 3, 1959 Grand Rapids MI – Danny Lee Cotnoir, Rte 1, Paw Paw, age 14, passed away at Three Mile Lake Wednesday (25 Aug 1971) evening. Danny Lee was born July 3, 1957 in Grand Rapids and was a student in the Paw Paw School School System. He is survived by his mother and step-father, Mr and Mrs Robert Burris of Paw Paw; his father, Mr. Leland Cotnoir of Kalamazoo; a brother and sister, Sandra Lee and David Alan, at home; grandparents, Mr and Mrs. Clarence Ferris of Grand Rapids, Mr and Mrs Raymond Cotnoir of Scottsville, Mich. Committal services at Lakeview Cemetery, Ludington, Mason Co – Kalamazoo Gazette 27 Aug 1971

No children recorded
