JESSIE CALVIN FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.2.4.1.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.2.4.1.1) JESSIE CALVIN FERRIS (Rev.) born December 30, 1817/21 (1819) Smyrna, Chenango, NY and died July 17, 1901 Lansing, Ingham, MI; buried there Mt. Hope Cemetery; Jessie joined the Free Baptist Church, Smyrna at age 20 and decided to preach at age 23; taught school in the winter and attended Whitestone Seminary, ordained 1849; had pastorates at Smyrna and Saverine NY; Orange, Iona County MI and Grand Ledge, Eaton County MI. [Jim - Chaplain Ferris has him marrying, but another source has him single?]; married 1st January 11, 1854 Deborah Bacon who died April 14, 1875; and 2d October 27, 1875 Harriet M. Allen - no offspring

No children recorded
