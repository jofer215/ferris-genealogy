NANCY FERRIS legally changed her name to ALEXANDRA

ID: 1.4.7.5.8.1.2.2.2 | Source: John
(1.4.7.5.8.1.2.2.2) NANCY FERRIS legally changed her name to ALEXANDRA FLEMING FERRIS. Obit Chicago Tribune, January 20, 2003: Alexandra “Alex” Fleming Ferris, mother of Alexander Hamilton Crosby (born 1991) and Devon Scott Crobsy (born 1993), passed away on January 14, 2003. Born December 14, 1950 in Duluth MN, she was the daughter of John Alexander and Claire Fleming Ferris. Alex graduated from Duluth East High School in 1968; attended Hamline U. in St. Paul and graduated from U. of Minnesota. A Chicago area resident for over 20 years, she had been employed by United Airlines since 1987. Alex Ferris is survived by her beloved sons; her brother Scott Ferris of Portland OR; her sister Jane LeRoy of Plano TX; her nephew Jack LeRoy; her niece Jessica LeRoy. She was preceded in death by her brother Cyrus and her parents. Visitation will be held on Sunday, January 19, from 7 to 9 p.m., at Ahlgrim & Sons Funeral Home, 201 N. Northwest Highway in Palatine. The funeral service of Alex, scheduled from 11 a.m. on Monday January 20, will also be held at Ahlgrim & Sons Funeral Home. The family requests that memorials be sent to the Chicago area affiliate of the Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Foundation at 368 N. Myrtle, Elmhurst IL 60126. Born to her and Mr. Crosby were:
