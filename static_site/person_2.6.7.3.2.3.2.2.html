LYDIA PERRY KNAPP

ID: 2.6.7.3.2.3.2.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.3.2.3.2.2) LYDIA PERRY KNAPP born November 8, 1844 Edwardsburg, Cass, MI and died after September 12, 1892 Camillus, Onondaga, NY; buried there; married June 18, 1868 in Syracuse NY, Samuel Cone Trowbridge [son of Milo & Lucy (Cone) Trowbridge] born April 12, 1842 Tully, Onondaga, NY and died September 12, 1892 Brown’s Valley MN; buried there – Civil War Vet 186265, First Sergeant, Company K, 122d New York Infantry – apparently a Lieutenant when he mustered out – the 122d was a very active regiment during the Civil War, including the Battle at Gettysburg – more info available on the Internet.
