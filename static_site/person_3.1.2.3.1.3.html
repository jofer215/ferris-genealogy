JOHN FERRIS was baptized February 6, 1779 Schenectady

ID: 3.1.2.3.1.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.3.1.3) JOHN FERRIS was baptized February 6, 1779 Schenectady (Cayuga Co) NY; married September 28, 1803 in Essex Co NY, Elizabeth Ferris (*) [Deborah Wilcox, which was the first marriage in Moriah Township, Essex Co NY]; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • SARAH FERRIS 3.1.2.3.1.3.1
  • BERINTHA C. FERRIS 3.1.2.3.1.3.2
  • HARRIET N. FERRIS died before 1848; married, December 31, 1844 in St. Joseph 3.1.2.3.1.3.3
