EVA LOUISE FERRIS

ID: 2.1.4.1.3.3.1.2 | 35ZN-L9 | Source: Peter
(2.1.4.1.3.3.1.2) EVA LOUISE FERRIS (AFN:35ZN-L9) (Photo) born April (12?)22, 1869 Chilcoot, Plumas, CA and died September 29, 1958 Susanville, Lassen, CA; married 1st October 30, 1891(1892), Oscar Hill Doyle (AFN:C3KQ-B3) [son of John William & Caroline Hill (Thompson) Doyle] born September 5, 1862 Long Valley, Lassen, CA and died July 24, 1937 Doyle, Lassen, CA. They must have divorced because Oscar did not died until 1937. Eva married 2d March 16, 1897 Fred B. Robinson born March 15, 1866 Nevada and died June 23, 1952 Susanville CA.
