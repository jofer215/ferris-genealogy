RODMAN CORSE PELL

ID: 1.4.7.3.1.5.1.1 | Source: John
(1.4.7.3.1.5.1.1) RODMAN CORSE PELL born March 31, 1861 Flushing, L. I., NY; enlisted in Company I., 7th Regiment, N.G.S.N.Y. 1883; married 1887 Antoinette G. Pell [dau of Charles S. & Mary Ann (Elliott) Pell]. Rodman was connected with the American Biscuit Company in San Francisco. Born to them was: (1.4.7.3.1.5.1.1.1) RODMAN CORSE PELL, JR., born July 19, 1893; 1918 he enlisted for war service; married June 30, 1916 Helen M. Kendrick; resided in San Francisco. Born to them was:

No children recorded
