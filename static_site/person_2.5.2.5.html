JEREMIAH HAIT

ID: 2.5.2.5 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.5) JEREMIAH HAIT (Hoyt) (#2755) (NZJW-02) born c1706 Stamford and died February 3, 1789 at Salem NY; married, May 27, 1736, Lydia Weed who died November 16, 1784 at Salem. He was a land owner. They lived in Stamford CT and Salem NY. (Other sources have changed Hait to Hoyt.) Born to them were:

Children (8)
