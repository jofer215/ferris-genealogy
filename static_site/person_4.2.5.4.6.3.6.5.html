THEODORE C CARR

ID: 4.2.5.4.6.3.6.5 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.4.6.3.6.5) THEODORE C CARR (T.C.) born March 23, 1861 Yorkshire, Cattaraugus, NY; left Topeka and went to Los Angeles 1888; a printer/compositor; was in Los Angeles until the mid-1920s when he went to the Union Printer’s Home in Colorado Springs CO where he stayed until he died January 20, 1931; buried Los Angeles Rosedale Cemetery; married July 23, 1891 in San Francisco, Carrie Fisher born June 1873 IL; and born to them was:
