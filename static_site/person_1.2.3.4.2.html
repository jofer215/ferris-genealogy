GILBERT UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.4.2 | Source: John
(1.2.3.4.2) GILBERT UNDERHILL born March 20, 1763 and died August 11, 1848 Eastchester, Westchester, NY; Rev War Vet served 1776, American spy for General Scott; married c1786 in Eastchester, Westchester, NY, Phebe Gedney (1761-1840) [dau of Absalom & Patience (Barnes) Gedney] and born to them were:

Children (8)

  • MARGARET UNDERHILL 1.2.3.4.2.1
  • ABSOLOM UNDERHILL 1.2.3.4.2.2
  • PATIENCE UNDERHILL 1.2.3.4.2.3
  • JOHN BARNES UNDERHILL 1.2.3.4.2.4
  • ELIZA UNDERHILL 1.2.3.4.2.5
  • JOSEPH GEDNEY UNDERHILL 1.2.3.4.2.6
  • FANNY UNDERHILL 1.2.3.4.2.7
  • GILBERT UNDERHILL died October 21, 1833; buried St Paul Church 1.2.3.4.2.8
