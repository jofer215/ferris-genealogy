FANNY BATES

ID: 5.2.4.8.4.8 | Source: James
(5.2.4.8.4.8) FANNY BATES born June 2, 1822 Stamford CT and died there October 10, 1906; married September 29, 1857 in Stamford, as his 2d wife, Josiah Smith, Jr., [son of Josiah & Sarah (Reynolds) Smith] born June 20, 1803 Stamford and died there February 10, 1878; both buried Spring Grove Cemetery.
