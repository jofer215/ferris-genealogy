JOSEPH AVERY SUTTON

ID: 1.2.5.1.10.6.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.10.6.1) JOSEPH AVERY SUTTON born June 4, 1831 Mt Pleasant NY and died December 28, 1897 Sound Beach, Greenwich, CT; Civil War Vet served in 28th Connecticut Infantry Regiment 1862-63; married August 1850 Hannah Hatter [dau of George Thomas & Sarah (Flat) Hatter] born February 22, 1831 New Castle NY and died June 24, 1902 Sound Beach, Greenwich CT; both buried First Congregational Church, Old Greenwich CT; and they had 13 kids, 11 of which were: (1.2.5.1.10.6.1.1) SAMUEL EDWARD SUTTON born August 1851 Mt Kisco, Westchester, NY and died October 28, 1936 Old Greenwich CT; married 1873 Mary L Holley born June 4, 1848 Cross River NY and died May 1927 Old Greenwich; both buried there First Congregational Church; and born to them were:

Children (10)
