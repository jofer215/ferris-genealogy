FREDERICK FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.2.4 | MCV8-MG | Source: James
(5.1.3.2.4) FREDERICK FERRIS (AFN: MCV8-MG) (1784-1845) married 1809/10(?), Susannah Nichols (AFN: MCV8-NM) (1791-1832). It is said that Frederick came from Connecticut to Ohio in May, 1813/14 - in his party were John Ferris, Captain Robert Barnard and family, David Hobby and family and two other families. His own family consisted of Susannah, William, Ann, Susan, John and Caroline, all born in CT. [This conflicts with the birth dates as shown below] Each family, with a wagon and two horses crossed overland to Pittsburgh, where they bought flat boats in which they descended the Ohio River to Columbia OH, a few miles above Cincinnati. In July of the same year, he moved to a farm four miles northeast of Brookville IN, where he lived until the time of his death. It is said that their children. Catherine, James, Mary, Samuel, Allison, Isaiah and an infant were born in IN.
