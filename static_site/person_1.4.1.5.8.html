RICHARD FERRIS

ID: 1.4.1.5.8 | Source: John
(1.4.1.5.8) RICHARD FERRIS born August 18, 1806 and died November 26/29, 1866 New York City; married Christina (Catherine) Cook (1816-1858) and they moved by boat and oxen team to Onondaga, Ingham, MI 1842/3. They built a lean-to and hewed a log cabin. Richard started to clear the land when his wife and several children died from typhoid and blood poisoning. He buried them on the farm, had a sale of his equipment, and returned to New York City where he died the following year. Born to Richmond and Christina were:
