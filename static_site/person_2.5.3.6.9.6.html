JOEL HOYT

ID: 2.5.3.6.9.6 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.6.9.6) JOEL HOYT (#3636) born February 20, 1798 Middletown, Orange, NY; married Esther King [dau of John Young & Elizabeth (Godfrey) King] born February 20, 1798 NY and died April 9, 1861; and they resided in Middletown NY.

No children recorded
