JOHN WILLIS HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.11.4.2.1 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.11.4.2.1) JOHN WILLIS HOYT born October 30, 1820 Stamford and died after 1880 New Canaan CT; was a shoemaker between 1850-60; married October 17, 1842 in Stamford, Maria Elizabeth Smith born 1824 NY and died January 28, 1899 Stamford; buried North Stamford Cemetery; and born to them were (must have been an epidemic in Nov-Dec 1852):
