JACOB FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.3.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.3.2) JACOB FERRIS born c1747/50/53 probably in Nine Partners, Dutchess, NY {probably by Peter’s first wife} and died October 27, 1814 (October 7, 1811), Panton VT; buried Lake Cemetery, Panton; was called Captain and Esquire; was one of the first town officers - assessor, path master 1786 overseer of the poor; resided Plattsburg NY 1770-83 where he was one of the first ten settlers to receive lots of 80 acres under offer of 1781; 1787 owner of a sawmill and gristmill near Plattsburg; married Sarah Coel or Cole; and born to them were:

Children (10)

  • WILLIAM FERRIS 3.1.2.3.2.1
  • MARIA FERRIS 3.1.2.3.2.10

  • NANCY FERRIS was baptized September 6, 1774 at the Dutch Reformed Church of 3.1.2.3.2.4
  • JOHN HORATIO FERRIS was baptized November 19, 1776 at the Dutch Reformed 3.1.2.3.2.5
  • ISAAC FERRIS baptized October 3, 1778 at the Dutch Reformed Church of Saratoga at 3.1.2.3.2.6
  • HESTER FERRIS was baptized October 27, 1781 at the Dutch Reformed Church of 3.1.2.3.2.7
  • MARY FERRIS was baptized September 1, 1783 at the same church as her siblings. 3.1.2.3.2.8
