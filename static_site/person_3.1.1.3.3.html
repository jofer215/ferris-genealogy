BENJAMIN REYNOLDS

ID: 3.1.1.3.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.3.3) BENJAMIN REYNOLDS born Lower Salem NY, date unk; married May 15, 1782 at the Church of Christ, Salem, Abigail Murphy; a farmer and a Revolutionary War Veteran, who was badly wounded when his right arm was fractured by a musket ball, October 26, 1781 while serving in Captain Lawrence's Company, Colonel Drake's Regiment of New York Troops. The 1790 census shows two females and a son under 16.

No children recorded
