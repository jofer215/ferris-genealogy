CLARENCE E PARTCH

ID: 4.6.9.8.2.2.3.2.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.9.8.2.2.3.2.1) CLARENCE E PARTCH born March 12, 1884; Professor of Education, Dean of School of Education and Director of summer sessions at Rutgers U; author of several books and articles on education; Who’s Who in America; married July 31, 1912 Vera Howard who died January 20, 1947; there is a Clarence E Partch Scholarship at Rutgers Graduate School of Education

No children recorded
