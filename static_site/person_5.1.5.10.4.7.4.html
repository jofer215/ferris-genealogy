CHARLES WILLIAM HENDRIE

ID: 5.1.5.10.4.7.4 | Source: James
(5.1.5.10.4.7.4) CHARLES WILLIAM HENDRIE born October 29, 1862 Greenwich CT and died March 28, 1922 Stamford; salesman 1886; Colonel in Connecticut National Guard; owner of a drygoods business on Atlantic Square; deputy sheriff; city tax collector; founder of Elks Lodge in Stamford; an Off Fellow and member of the Knights of Pythias and the Red Men; married 1st February 5, 1885 in Stamford, Lucy Clement Dean [dau of George William & Emily (Clement) Dean] born August 30, 1863 Middletown NY and died July 24, 1888 Stamford; married 2d 1896 Rebecca M Smith [dau of James & Margaret] born December 1859 Stamford and died there 1926; and born to him and Lucy was:

Children (2)

  • MARION H HENDRIE 5.1.5.10.4.7.4.2
