REUBEN LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.8 | Source: Mary
(4.6.8) REUBEN LOCKWOOD (#69) (Captain) born December 15, 1715 Stamford; married 1st Sarah Cramp and 2d December 21, 1749 in Stamford, Elizabeth Stevens. Apparently he had a company of Westchester County NY, 1758. Born to Reuben and Sarah were: (4.6.8.1) LYDIA LOCKWOOD (#269)
