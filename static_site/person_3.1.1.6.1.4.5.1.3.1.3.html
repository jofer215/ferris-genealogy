MYRON BUSH STEVENS,

ID: 3.1.1.6.1.4.5.1.3.1.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.1.4.5.1.3.1.3) MYRON BUSH STEVENS, Jr. (Photo) born March 12, 1942 Springfield MO; married 1st April 21, 1962 Florence Marie Antrobus [dau of Robert & Edora] born December 14, 1942 Morrisville PA - divorced 1977; married 2d December 19, 1981 Patricia Anne (Gorman) Heinz born March 21, 1935 Heinz MO; Myron served in the US Coast Guard in the Atlantic Ocean on an Icebreaker; worked for the New Jersey Rating Bureau; for the US Post Office as an extra; and as an 18wheel truck driver for long and short hauls; was a dispatcher for Kissick Truck Lines and now is back to driving; a member of the Delaware Crossing Chapter, Kansas and National Sons of the American Revolution, National # 123534; and born to Myron and Florence were:
