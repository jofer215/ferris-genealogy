GEORGE BEECHER HARRIS

ID: 1.2.3.3.4.3.11 | Source: John
(1.2.3.3.4.3.11) GEORGE BEECHER HARRIS (George Becher) (Photo) born July 29, 1836 London, Ontario (the first child born Eldon House, London) and died there November 15, 1923; like his brothers, he became a laywer and joined their practice; married September 5, 1867 in Windsor, Canada, Mary Elizabeth Lucy Roland (E Lucy Ronalds) (Mary Elizabeth Lucy Ronalds) born November 17, 1845 Ontario, Canada and died August 16, 1901 at Eldon House, London, Ontario; George inherited the Eldon House and lived there until his death; his heirs eventually donated the house to the city of London, Ontario (Photos); and born to them were:
