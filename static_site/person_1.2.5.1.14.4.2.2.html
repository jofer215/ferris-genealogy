SARAH ELIZA SMULL

ID: 1.2.5.1.14.4.2.2 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.14.4.2.2) SARAH ELIZA SMULL born 1873 Caldwell NJ and died February 29, 1952 Owego NY; buried at Arlington National Cemetery; married 1st June 5, 1897 in NYC, Charles Platt Storrs [son of Aaron Putnam & Louise Trivett (Platt) Storrs] born 1874 Owego NY and died October 2, 1958 Sayre PA; buried Owego NY (must have divorced); 2d George M Alden (Captain); presume this is George Marshall Alden, Captain, Quartermaster with 2d New York Inf, died January 3, 1931; buried at Arlington; and born to her and Charles were:

Children (2)
