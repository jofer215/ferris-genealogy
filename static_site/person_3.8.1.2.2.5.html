BENJAMIN FERRIS, JR.,

ID: 3.8.1.2.2.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.8.1.2.2.5) BENJAMIN FERRIS, JR., (Benjamin J) (tombstone photo) born November 26, 1801 Ferrisburg VT and died October 11, 1881; buried Carpenter Cemetery, Monkton VT; a farmer; married November 16, 1823 at Ferrisburg VT, Mary Sherman born March 9, 1803 and died May 7, 1881.
