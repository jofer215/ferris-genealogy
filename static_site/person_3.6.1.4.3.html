JOHN JUDSON WHITING

ID: 3.6.1.4.3 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.1.4.3) JOHN JUDSON WHITING born 1786 and died August 25, 1826; married Helen Murray (1789-1871); both buried First Congregational Church Cemetery, Old Greenwich CT – Plot 17, Grave 9; Helen was a niece of Jeanette Stuart's. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • ROBERT MURRAY WHITING 3.6.1.4.3.1
  • SAMUEL F WHITING 3.6.1.4.3.2
  • JOHN JUDSON WHITING, JR. 3.6.1.4.3.3
