SHERMAN FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.1.6.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.1.6.6) SHERMAN FERRIS born August 4, 1792/3 Harpersfield NJ and died September 22, 1880 Union, Branch, MI [or March 28, 1880 Eaton Co MI]; owned property in Webster NY; moved from Ogden, Monroe, NY to Union MI; 1850 census of Ogden, Sherman was listed as a farmer with assets of $6,600; married 1st August 4, 1813 Polly Lake (1792-1836); and 2d widow Sophia (Colby) Hinckley; and born to Sherman and Polly were:
