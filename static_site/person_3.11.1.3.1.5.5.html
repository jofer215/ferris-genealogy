MARTHA ROSE

ID: 3.11.1.3.1.5.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.3.1.5.5) MARTHA ROSE born May 14, 1809 Montague Township, Lanark, Ontario and died September 3, 1879 Memphis, Shelby, TN of yellow fever; buried there; married May 20, 1835 in Johnstown, Ontario, John Milburn [son of Thomas & Ann (Dickinson) Milburn] born March 10, 1810 Alston, Cumbria, England and died August 22, 1847 Smith’s Falls, Ontario of typhoid fever; buried Wolford Township; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • THOMAS HARPER MILBURN 3.11.1.3.1.5.5.1
  • GEORGE MILBURN 3.11.1.3.1.5.5.2
  • JOHN DICKINSON MILBURN 3.11.1.3.1.5.5.3
  • JOSEPH MILBURN 3.11.1.3.1.5.5.4
