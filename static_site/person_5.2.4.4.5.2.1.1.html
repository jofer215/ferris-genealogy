ANNA LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.2.4.4.5.2.1.1 | Source: James
(5.2.4.4.5.2.1.1) ANNA LOCKWOOD born July 6, 1853 Stamford and died December 13, 1934 Hastings-on-Hudson NY; married April 20, 1882 in Stamford, as his 2d wife, George W Dayton born 1848 Stamford and died January 2, 1891 Stamford – see (5.2.2.2.9.3.8) for his 1st wife and offspring.
