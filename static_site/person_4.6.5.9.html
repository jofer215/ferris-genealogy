ABIGAIL HAIT

ID: 4.6.5.9 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.9) ABIGAIL HAIT (Hoyt) (#2905) born January 16, 1745 New Canaan CT; baptized March 24, 1745 and was living in Old Pound Ridge 1773; and died 1823; married March 1, 1764 at New Canaan, (Squire) Gilbert Weed [son of Jonathan, Jr. & Mercy (Drew) Weed] born August 1, 1740 Stamford CT and died January 11, 1832 (September 22, 1823) Scipio, Cayuga, NY; served in the Rev. War 1779-80 as a Private with the New York forces under Lieutenant Colonels Thads Crane and Benedict. Born to them were:
