BERTHA ELLEN LAWSON

ID: 3.1.5.1.3.1.2.2.6.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.5.1.3.1.2.2.6.2) BERTHA ELLEN LAWSON born September 5, 1890 Clarks, Merrick, NE and died March 24, 1962 Long Beach CA; buried there Forest Lawn; married 1st January 17, 1919 at Belle Fouche, Butte, SD Charles Leland Barnett [son of Hiram Lewis & Elizabeth (Yana) Barnett] born September 18, 1891 SD; 2d after 1950 in Los Angeles Co CA, Herman Henry Messner born May 17, 1885 Linn Co IA; and born to her and Charles were:

Children (2)
