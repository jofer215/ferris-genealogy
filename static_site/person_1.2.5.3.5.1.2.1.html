SARAH UNDERHILL KELLOGG

ID: 1.2.5.3.5.1.2.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.3.5.1.2.1) SARAH UNDERHILL KELLOGG born July 26, 1850 Utica, Oneida, NY; married Francis Marion Burdick, LLD (1845-1920); professor of law; graduated Hamilton College 1869, Hamilton College Law School 1872; practice law until 1883; mayor of Utica NY 1882-83; held positions at Hamilton College, Cornell U, and Columbia U and was a commissioner for the State of New York; published numerous papers; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • ANNA V E BURDICK 1.2.5.3.5.1.2.1.1
  • KATHARINE PEARLY BURDICK 1.2.5.3.5.1.2.1.2
  • CHARLES K BURDICK 1.2.5.3.5.1.2.1.3
  • FLORS M BURDICK 1.2.5.3.5.1.2.1.4
