IRA FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.1.11.9 | Source: Joseph

Daniel & Mary (Bowen) Ostrander] born 1803 Coventry RI and died c1883 Grand Gorge; Ira was a Methodist minister; in 1882 (?), resided New Hamburg NY; and born to them were:
