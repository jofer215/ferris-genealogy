AARON PECK

ID: 3.10.1.3.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.1.3.5) AARON PECK born May 3, 1757 Greenwich CT and died there July 17, (January 23?) 1833. Aaron received a pension as an invalid in 1823 due to wounds received at St. John's while serving as a Private under Major Thomas Hobby, General David Waterbury's Regiment, in which he had enlisted in April 1775. He re-enlisted in 1777 for six months in the State Troops under Captain Stevens, as a Corporal; re-enlisted again, in April 1780 as a Sergeant in the Coast Guards under Captain Henry Waring and served until April, 1783; and re-enlisted in the same Company and served until it was disbanded at the end of the war. He married August 22, 1782, Hannah FERRIS (5.1.3.1.6) and born to them were:
