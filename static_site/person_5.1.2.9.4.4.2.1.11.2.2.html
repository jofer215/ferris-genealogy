DAVID C. FARINA

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.2.1.11.2.2 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.2.1.11.2.2) DAVID C. FARINA born April 16, 1959 Mt. Kisco NY and died July 9, 1962 Mahopac NY; buried in family plot at Raymond Hill Cemetery, Carmel NY. Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, Thursday, July 12, 1962: David C. Farina, aged three years, son of Mr. and Mrs. Dominick Farina of Mahopac, died suddenly at the Mahopac hospital on Monday, July 9, 1962 from an acute attack of croup. The child was rushed to the hospital when taken ill, but died before relief could be administered.

No children recorded
