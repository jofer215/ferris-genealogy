MARY FERRIS

ID: 1.1.5 | J3TF-GJ | Source: John
(1.1.5) MARY FERRIS (AFN: J3TF-GJ) born 1708 Scarsdale NY; married March 11, 1732 (LDS - November 3, 1732) Richard Cornell, Jr. (AFN:J3TF-JV) born December 6, 1708/09 (6.10.1708) probably Hempstead NY and died 1758. Richard moved to Scarsdale after 1720, where he owned land as well at Northcastle. (Mary is listed, by Chaplain Ferris, as being possibly a child of Peter.)
