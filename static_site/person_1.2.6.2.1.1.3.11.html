CLARANCE GILBERT UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.6.2.1.1.3.11 | Source: John
(1.2.6.2.1.1.3.11) CLARANCE GILBERT UNDERHILL (Bobby) born September 17, 1877 Colchester IL and died March 3, 1959 St Petersburg FL; married Ardie Wooley born January 21, 1878; he was a farmer and his fine farm (1972) was still managed under the direction of Ardie who at the age of 94y was still active and busy

No children recorded
