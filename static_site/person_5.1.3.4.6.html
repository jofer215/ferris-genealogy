ELIPHALET FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.6 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.6) ELIPHALET FERRIS born July 4, 1774 Greenwich, CT and died February 8, 1859 Columbia Township, [Cincinnati, Hamilton Co] Ohio; buried Ferris Family Cemetery, Mariemont, Hamilton, OH (Photo) [There is a large monument erected at the Cemetery for Eliphalet, his wife, and 6 of their children, signed by Charles F. Stites, probably (5.1.3.4.1.2.1)]; married March 9, 1799 Catherine Knapp (5.1.3.3.4.1) and they settled in Columbia Township, Hamilton Co OH 1799; both buried in a cemetery in what is now Mariemont, Ohio; Ferris Cemetery was land deeded from Eliphalet to his daughter Anna, for ‘a family burying ground’. Eliphalet built a house c1812; farmhouse until 1923; in 1934 housed the Thomas J. Emery Memorial; 1936 housed the Mariemont Historic Museum; historical records spell Eliphalet as Elithalet; has been properly restored and houses an educational consulting firm (photos).
