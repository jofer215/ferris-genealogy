WILLIAM JONES MOTT

ID: 1.10.2.1.4.1 | Source: John
(1.10.2.1.4.1) WILLIAM JONES MOTT born February 22, 1825 and died May 12 (13), 1894, Dixondale VA; married June 21, 1854 Catharine Saunders [dau of Theodore W. of Albany NY] born December 7, 1831; resided at Great Neck, Long Island, on the old homestead of Lieutenant Adam Mott (the progenitor of the Long Island family) until 1873 when he sold the homestead to William R. Grace, Mayor of NYC; 1879 moved to Dixondale, Gloucester Co VA. Born to them were:

Children (2)

  • JAMES WILLIS MOTT 1.10.2.1.4.1.1
  • WILLLIAM SAUNDERS MOTT 1.10.2.1.4.1.2
