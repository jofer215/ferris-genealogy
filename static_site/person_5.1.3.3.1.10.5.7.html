JAMES FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.3.1.10.5.7 | Source: James
(5.1.3.3.1.10.5.7) JAMES FERRIS born February 28, 1868 Ottawa Co OH; as a young man, he went West and there is no further record of him. However, Pauline (Hess) Vetter, adopted daughter of Charles and Cora (Ferris) Hess relates that a family story says that James went on a trip to China or Japan from where he sent a small rug and kimono to his sister Cora. Pauline remembers well the privilege of wearing the kimono for Halloween when she was a little girl.

No children recorded
