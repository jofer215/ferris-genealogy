CHARLES HOPKINS ALLEN

ID: 2.6.7.3.5.2.4 | Source: Peter
(2.6.7.3.5.2.4) CHARLES HOPKINS ALLEN born October 15/16, 1830 Burton, Cattaraugus, NY and died February 18, 1922 Mesa AZ; buried there; he sired 23 children last being born when he was 77y; married 1st June 15, 1864 in Richmond, Cache, UT Elizabeth Adelaide Hoopes [dau of Warner & Priscilla (Gifford) Hoopes] born September 8, 1847 in Council Bluffs, Pottawattamie, IA and died November 19, 1889 Mesa AZ (6 days after having her 15th child at 52y of age); buried there; and 2d October 29, 1890 in Mesa AZ (Logan, Cache, UT), Anna Eliza Jones born November 10, 1869 New Hope, Hawkins, TN and died May 24, 1945 Mesa; buried there; and born to him and Elizabeth were:
