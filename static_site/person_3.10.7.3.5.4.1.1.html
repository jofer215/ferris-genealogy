ORRIN KENELM PADDOCK

ID: 3.10.7.3.5.4.1.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.7.3.5.4.1.1) ORRIN KENELM PADDOCK (Orrin Knelem) born March 24, 1848 Springfield, Oakland, MI and died August 18, 1918 West Los Angeles, Los Angeles, CA; 1858 moved with his family from MI to IA; May 21, 1864 enlisted in Company B, 46th Iowa Infantry (Private) – resided Tabor IA then; moved to South Omaha NE where he was a grain merchant (1880), a livestock broker (1887) and between 1889-90 listed as Paddock & Gosney, Livestock Brokers; Livestock Commissioner (1900); belonged to Nebraska Grand Army of the Republic Post 2 in South Omaha; married August 23, 1870 in Percival IA, Mary Amelia Hawley born August 16, 1851 Rome NY; and born to them were (birth places of the kids do not match above bio info):
