ANNA FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.6.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.6.1) ANNA FERRIS (Ann) born December 4/5, 1799 Cincinnati OH (Columbia Township) and died May 2, 1878; buried Ferris Family Cemetery, Hamilton Co OH; married 1st July 31, 1816 Henry Beekley, a farmhand (1799-c1829); and 2d Solomon Moore (1796-1855). Ann and Henry removed to Franklin Co IN [See The Resident Spirit of the Ferris House at the end of this chapter.]; and born to Anna and Henry were:
