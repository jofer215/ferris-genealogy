ELIJAH CANFIELD

ID: 3.1.6.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.5) ELIJAH CANFIELD born April 4, 1739 Ridgefield, Fairfield, CT and died April 21, 1804 Stamford CT (or Bovina, Delaware County NY); married (first?) 1778/80 at Dutchess Co NY, Hannah Van Campen. [Another source, says Elijah married 1st March 24, 1778 in Bengall, Dutchess, NY, Abigail Ferris (*) and married Hannah Van Campen, second.] Born to Elijah and Hannah were:

Children (5)

  • JOHN CANFIELD 3.1.6.5.2
  • LEONARD CANFIELD 3.1.6.5.3
  • ABRAM CANFIELD 3.1.6.5.4
  • JAMES CANFIELD 3.1.6.5.5
