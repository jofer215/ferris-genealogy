JOHN ANDREW ROSE

ID: 3.1.6.8.12.1.1.9 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.12.1.1.9) JOHN ANDREW ROSE born December 1, 1901 Haley Station, Renfrew Co and died May 28, 1962 Montreal, Quebec; buried Haley Station; a Railroader; married August 29, 1928 in Mountain Township, Dundas Co, Ontario, Mabel Marguerite Carkner [dau of Robert William and Christina (Kennedy) Carkner] born September 15, 1905 Mountain Township; Mabel was a schoolteacher; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • WILLIAM BEATTIE ROSE 3.1.6.8.12.1.1.9.2
  • SHIRLEY CHRISTINA ANNE ROSE 3.1.6.8.12.1.1.9.3
  • ELMER ANDREW MACDONALD 3.1.6.8.12.1.1.9.4
