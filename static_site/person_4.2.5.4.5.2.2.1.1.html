ELLA MARIE DUNNING

ID: 4.2.5.4.5.2.2.1.1 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.4.5.2.2.1.1) ELLA MARIE DUNNING born October 16, 1860 (Rootstown) Kent OH; married March 20, 1878 in Kent, Arthur Stanley Olin [son of Alonzo & Almira/Elmira B (Squires) Olin] born August 12, 1846 Streetsboro OH; lived on their farm 2 miles SE of Kent, until February 25, 1897, when, his health failing, they moved to Kent and resided there 1901+; and born to them were:
