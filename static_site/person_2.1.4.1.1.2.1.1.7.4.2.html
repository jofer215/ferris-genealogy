JOHN LYMAN CRONIN

ID: 2.1.4.1.1.2.1.1.7.4.2 | Source: Peter
(2.1.4.1.1.2.1.1.7.4.2) JOHN LYMAN CRONIN (John Patrick Cronin) (Jack) born January 20/21, 1921 Seattle WA and died October 10, 1943 (October 9, 1942), KIA, North Sea during Marienburg Raid; a pilot; according to the ‘Net this raid was October 9, 1943; married Miss Butterbaugh.

No children recorded
