ALICE IDA REYNOLDS

ID: 3.1.1.3.1.4.3.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.3.1.4.3.4) ALICE IDA REYNOLDS born July 15, 1863 Greenwich CT; married 1 st February 8, 1882 Elmore Avery Selleck [son of Sands & Eliza Jane (Fowler) Selleck] born January 21, 1850 Darien CT and died December 25, 1886 Greenwich; buried Christ Episcopal Churchyard; married 2d, November 27, 1889 William Henry Teed born May 17, 1854 Katonah NY. Born to her and Elmore were:
