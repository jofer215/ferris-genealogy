RUSSELL HAYES BLAIR

ID: 3.1.1.6.2.3.2.6.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.6.2.3.2.6.1) RUSSELL HAYES BLAIR born November 1876 Montgomery OH and died October 4, 1905; killed in explosion in Hamilton Co Courthouse; the road record clerk in the County Commissioner's office for four years; lawyer, Chase Law School 1905; scheduled to be married later in the month

No children recorded
