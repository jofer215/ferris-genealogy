BYRON JUDSON FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.6.6.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.6.6.2) BYRON JUDSON FERRIS born June 3, 1858 and died October 21, 1928 Ruggles Township OH; buried Grove Street Cemetery, New London OH; married February 5, 1879 in North Fairfield, Huron, OH, Leola W. Dickinson who died December 22, 1930 Ruggles Township OH. Bryon was a farmer, located about halfway between New London and Ruggles OH; owned 47 acres, 2 horses and 4 cows according to the 1915 Directory for Ruggles Township. Born to them was:
