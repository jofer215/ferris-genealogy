ELWOOD RUSSEL KNAPP

ID: 2.6.2.7.1.4.8.1 | Source: Peter
(2.6.2.7.1.4.8.1) ELWOOD RUSSEL KNAPP born July 15, 1875 Nelhi NY; married August 11, 1923 Clynda Palmer (2.6.2.7.1.4.8.2) FLORA BELLE KNAPP born Delhi NY April 25, 1879; graduated from the Delaware Academy, Delhi NY 1896; Teacher at the Elmira College 1900-05 and at the Eastman-Gaines Business School in New York 1908-19; lecturer and writer; active in New York historical societies; married August 10, 1918 at New York City, Clarence Cookman Dickinson; 1942 resided at 525 W. 238th St., New York

No children recorded
