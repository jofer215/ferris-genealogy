HANNAH SMITH

ID: 2.6.4.6.2 | Source: Peter
(2.6.4.6.2) HANNAH SMITH born November 16, 1767 Stamford and died there August 1, 1852; buried Old Cemetery Stamford; married November 26, 1789 in Stamford, as his 2d wife, Nathaniel Mills born February 20, 1764 and died September 7, 1847 Stamford; buried Old Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (4)
