AMOS KNAPP

ID: 2.6.1.12 | Source: Peter
(2.6.1.12) AMOS KNAPP born February 28, 1738/39 Stamford and died there July 29, 1781; Amos was a French and Indian Wars Veteran; served one term under Captain REUBEN FERRIS, in the campaign of 1757 and other terms of service through 1762; married May 25, 1765 at New York City, Jane Olgilvie and born to them were [may not be in birth order]:
