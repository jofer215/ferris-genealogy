LETITIA FERRIS

ID: 3.6.1.2.2.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.1.2.2.1) LETITIA FERRIS married James Skelding and they had a son, Arthur - see (4.2.7.1.3.1.1.5)] – this James may very well be (2.5.2.5.3.5), son of James & Mary (Hoyt) Skelding. (3.6.1.2.2.2) JOSHUA BEAL FERRIS (Hon.) born January 13, 1804 Greenwich and died June 8, 1886; graduated Yale 1823; commenced life in Stamford as a teacher, and afterwards, when admitted to the bar, opened an office at Stamford. He was successful in his profession, taking high rank as an advocate among Connecticut lawyers; was elected a State Senator from Stamford in 1850. He was for years judge of probate and State's Attorney; a Whig and a Republican; one of the corporators of the Stamford Savings Bank and member of Board of Directors; married in 1823 Sally Hannah Peters [daughter of William B. Peters, Esq. and granddaughter of Rev. Dr. Peters, of Hebron] born 1805 and died April 26, 1885. Their children were (not in birth sequence):
