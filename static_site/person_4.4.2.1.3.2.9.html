MARTHA J BATES

ID: 4.4.2.1.3.2.9 | Source: Mary
(4.4.2.1.3.2.9) MARTHA J BATES born March 14, 1851 Stamford CT and died there December 25, 1910; married April 5, 1879 in Darien, Levi Wesley Slauson [son of Eleazer & Emily (Brown) Slauson] born April 18, 1842 Pound Ridge NY and died April 29, 1912 Stamford; both buried there Woodland Cemetery; carpenter 1880-81; railroad agent 1884; and born to them were:

Children (6)
