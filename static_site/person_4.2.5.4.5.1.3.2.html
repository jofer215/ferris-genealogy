SAMUEL SANFORD SMITH

ID: 4.2.5.4.5.1.3.2 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.4.5.1.3.2) SAMUEL SANFORD SMITH born April 6, 1838 Orange, Cuyahoga, OH and died January 24, 1929 Blissfield, Lenawee, MI; Civil War Vet served in Company A, 117th Ohio Volunteer Infantry Regiment; he farmed in Riga Township, Lenawee Co; married April 10, 1859 Viola Gleason [dau of Hiram & Hannah Marie (Hubbell) Gleason] born (c1842 Orange OH) (August 17, 1838 Warrensville, Cuyahoga, OH) and died 1929 Blissfield MI; and born to them were:

Children (3)

  • HENRY ADELBERT SMITH 4.2.5.4.5.1.3.2.1
  • WORTHY G. SMITH 4.2.5.4.5.1.3.2.2
  • KENNETH H. SMITH 4.2.5.4.5.1.3.2.3
