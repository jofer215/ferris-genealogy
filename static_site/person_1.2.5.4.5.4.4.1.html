OSCAR FRED UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.5.4.5.4.4.1 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.5.4.4.1) OSCAR FRED UNDERHILL born May 3, 1834 Allen, Allegany, NY and died February 12, 1897 Broken Arrow, Custer, NE; 1851-53 resided Cold Water, Branch, MI as apprentice shoemaker; 1860 to Orland IN; 1862 back to Cold Water; 1862-65 Civil War with 4th Calvary (4th Regiment Michigan Cav), Private; 1888 Broken Bow NE; married 1st May 23, 1859 in Allegany Co NY, Pheobe A Voorhees who died in Camp Douglas IL; 2d August 13, 1865 in Logan OH, Mary Ellen Martin (1832-1906); 3d September 28, 1890 in Broken Arrow NE, as her 2d husband, Mary L Williams [dau of John & Rebecca S (Palmer) Williams] born March 1854 IL; and born to him and Phoebe were:

Children (5)

  • JESSE C UNDERHILL 1.2.5.4.5.4.4.1.2
  • OSCAR FRED UNDERHILL 1.2.5.4.5.4.4.1.3
