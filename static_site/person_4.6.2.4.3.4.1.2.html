HENRY WARING LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.6.2.4.3.4.1.2 | Source: Mary
(4.6.2.4.3.4.1.2) HENRY WARING LOCKWOOD born August 14, 1833 Stamford and died there April 7, 1883; buried Woodland Cemetery; 1880 corn merchant; 1883 barman/farmer; married c1859 Martha J. {maiden name unk} (1838-) (4.6.2.4.3.4.2) MEHITABLE LOCKWOOD born December 2, 1811 Stamford CT and died there July 3, 1893; buried there Woodland Cemetery; married c1831 Hanford Jerman [son of James & Polly (Davis) Jerman] born October 7, 1807 Stamford and died there November 11, 1878; buried there Woodland Cemetery; shoemaker 1850-78; and born to them were:

No children recorded
