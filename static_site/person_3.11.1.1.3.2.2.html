IRA H. NEWMAN

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.2) IRA H. NEWMAN born October 15, 1823 Jefferson Co NY and died July 15, 1904/05 Leelanau Co MI (Long Lake Township, Grand Traverse Co MI); buried Green Briar, Cedar Run MI; carpenter and joiner; served as township clerk; married Prudence Nancy Adams (Prudy) [dau of Robert & Anna (Rice/Reese) Adams Jr] born March 6, 1828 NY and died March 2, 1909 Leelanau Co MI; buried with Ira; migrated from Jefferson Co NY to Michigan 1863 and homesteaded a 160-acre farm; cooper by trade; made maple syrup from the virgin timber; owned a general store in Cedar Run; member of Masonic Lodge; and born to them were:
