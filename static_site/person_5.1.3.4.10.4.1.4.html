HANNAH MAE FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.1.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.1.4) HANNAH MAE FERRIS (Hanna) born October 15, 1890 Canton SD and died 1981 Crawford NE; married 1st 1910 at Canton, Gilbert Rygh; 2d March 28, 1916 at Harrison NE, William Hasebrock born May 5, 1884 and died October 12, 1972. Hanna and William had a child who died infancy. Hanna homesteaded land in Oreilla NE 1912, and later lived north of Crawford NE where they farmed. She and William are both buried in Crawford NE Cemetery. Hanna and William adopted a son named Robert, who is buried at Fort Snelling Cemetery, St. Paul MN. Born to them was:

Children (1)

  • WILMA LENORE HASEBROCK died infancy 5.1.3.4.10.4.1.4.1
