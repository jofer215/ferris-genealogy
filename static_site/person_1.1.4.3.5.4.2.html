JOSEPH FERRIS

ID: 1.1.4.3.5.4.2 | Source: John
(1.1.4.3.5.4.2) JOSEPH FERRIS born August 20, 1829; listed as 40y of age and working in a mine in the 1870 Putnam Co NY census but appears to have died before 1880 as Cornelia is listed as a widow in the 1880 Putnam Co Census; married Cornelia Post who is listed as 44 years old in the 1880 census and 63 years old in the 1900 census living with her son William.
