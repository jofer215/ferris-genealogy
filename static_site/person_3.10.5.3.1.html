MARY BELDING

ID: 3.10.5.3.1 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.5.3.1) MARY BELDING born February 20, 1749/50 Stamford CT and died January 27, 1824 NYC: resided in NYC; married February 28, 1771 in Darien CT, Simeon Selleck [son of Sands & Mary (Raymond) Selleck] born September 14/18, 1744 Stamford and died January 30, 1796; Simeon inherited from his father’s estate, a store located at the Landing in Darien c1772; he was Regimental Quartermaster in the 5th Continental Regiment serving from May 20 to June 20, 1775 being discharged at NY; this company marched from Fairfield Co to NY and then to the Northern Department; he succeeded in capturing the King’s stores at Horse Neck in 1776 which of course isn’t consistent with the above dates; 1790 census he is listed as head of Stamford-Norwalk District; and born to them were:
