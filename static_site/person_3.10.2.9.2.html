JOHN LINKLATER

ID: 3.10.2.9.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.10.2.9.2) JOHN LINKLATER born 1781 NY and died 1865 – youngest son of the Loyalist family was born near Long Island NY and arrived with the family in St Johns Isle 1785 to receive the Loyalist land grant in Lot 17 near where the west boundary of Summerside in at present, but later settling where Linkletter now is; married c1805 Isobella Forbes (1785-1863) [dau of Donald]; and born to them were:

Children (4)

  • MARGARET LINKLETTER 3.10.2.9.2.1
  • THOMAS LINKLETTER 3.10.2.9.2.2
  • JOHN LINKLETTER 3.10.2.9.2.3
  • ELIZABETH ANN LINKLETTER 3.10.2.9.2.4
