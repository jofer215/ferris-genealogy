BENJAMIN HOYT

ID: 2.5.3.4.1.5.5 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.4.1.5.5) BENJAMIN HOYT (#4653) (Captain) born November 26, 1802 Stamford; a tailor and merchant in New Canaan where he removed to when he became of age; was Postmaster during the Taylor and Fillmore administrations and was a member of the legislature in 1856 and 1862; married October 13, 1828 Sally Carter Hoyt (#5046) [dau of Stephen & Polly (Carter) Hoyt] born July 1, 1809 New Canaan. Benjamin and Sally lived on the homestead of her father, in New Canaan, and he resided with them for the last 28-30 years of his life. Born to them were:
