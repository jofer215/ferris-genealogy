MARTHA ELIZABETH MURRAY

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.5.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.5.1) MARTHA ELIZABETH MURRAY (Photos) born April 10, 1881 seven miles northeast of Tampico, Hahnaman Township, Whiteside Co IL and died March 12, 1949 at home in Prophetstown, Whiteside Co IL; raised by her Aunt Mary and Uncle John P. Glassburn of Tampico after the death of her parents; married November 28,1900 in Tampico, Victor Emmauel Pearson [son of John & Lena (Olsson)] born July 4, 1876 Tampico and died January 16, 1958 at home in Prophetstown, Whiteside, IL; both buried at Tampico Memorial Cemetery, Tampico IL. Martha was a member of the Rock Falls Methodist Church. She was a woman of pleasing personality and kindly disposition, which won her an unlimited number of friends. She was a devoted lover of her family and her home. Victor was a farmer. Born to them were:
