CALEB FERRIS JR

ID: 1.1.4.1 | Source: John
(1.1.4.1) CALEB FERRIS JR born c1764 Westchester Co NY and died after 1830 Wayne Co NY; possible Rev War Vet; married Judah {maiden name unk} possibly born in France; it is said that Caleb had been a steward and Judah had been a nurse for the Stewart family in New York City.
