SMITH WEED

ID: 2.5.3.10.7 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.10.7) SMITH WEED born June 16, 1755 Stamford and died July 11, 1839 Albany NY; 1780-83 served as assistant commissary of issues; married 1st August 14, 1777 in Stamford, Mary Skelding born November 28, 1755 and died May 10, 1819 (sister of John (2.5.2.1.1.8)); married 2d May 28, 1820 in Saratoga Springs NY, Sarah Fitch (3.11.4.5.1.2); and born to him and Mary were: (2.5.3.10.7.1) WALTER WEED born March 17, 1782 Stamford; married 1st March 15, 1810 Cornelia Vredenburgh (1793-); married 2d Elizabeth Townsend
