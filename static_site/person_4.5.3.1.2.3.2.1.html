CHARLOTTE F. LOCKWOOD

ID: 4.5.3.1.2.3.2.1 | Source: Mary
(4.5.3.1.2.3.2.1) CHARLOTTE F. LOCKWOOD (middle initial may have been E.) (Photo) born June 16, 1833 and died April 24, 1909, Peekskill, Westchester, NY; buried St. Peter’s Cemetery, VanCortlandtville, Westchester Co; married January 4, 1852 at Peekskill, Abram Hayes (Abraham Hays) [son of James & Rosetta] born c1827 Courtlandtown and died September 4, 1895 Peekskill. Abram was a moulder. Molders cast iron products like potbellied stoves, Franklin stoves, firebacks, cannon balls, bullets, etc. He enlisted as a Private in Company A., 1st Regiment, Excelsior Brigade, 70th NY Infantry, serving from November 15, 1861 to February 6, 1863. He was mustered our by reason of disability; “incapable of performing the duties of a soldier because of sprain of back from being run over by Army wagon”. He is listed as a casualty at Fredericksburg VA. Born to them were:

Children (4)

  • ABRAM H. HAYS 4.5.3.1.2.3.2.1.2
  • EMMA C. HAYS 4.5.3.1.2.3.2.1.3
