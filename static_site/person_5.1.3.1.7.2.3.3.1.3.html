IRETT FRANCIS FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.7.2.3.3.1.3 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.7.2.3.3.1.3) IRETT FRANCIS FERRIS born December 18, 1901 and died August 2, 1983 Detroit MI; a High School teacher for 31 years and was the Manager, Detroit Teachers Credit Union; President, Michigan Council for Exceptional Children, 1950-51; married December 24, 1932 in Detroit MI Moneta Wilkins [dau of Clair & Edna (McClure)] born January 22, 1907 Okemos and died April 5, 2000 Maple City MI; both buried Grandlawn Cemetery, Detroit. Moneta lived in the Traverse City area for 12 years coming from Detroit and Okemos and they were members of Central United Methodist Church in Detroit; she was an art teacher for the Detroit Public Schools - she enjoyed painting.
