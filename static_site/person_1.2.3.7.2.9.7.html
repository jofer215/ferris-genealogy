SIMEON TAYLOR FROST

ID: 1.2.3.7.2.9.7 | Source: John
(1.2.3.7.2.9.7) SIMEON TAYLOR FROST born April 22, 1831 Pleasant Valley NY; graduated Yale 1857; teacher for more than 50y; in charge of Latin and Greek and the Preparatory Dept of the Hudson River Institute; for several years Principal and Proprietor of Amenia Seminary and for 12y Principal of Meridan High School; he rewrote McNally’s High School Geography, adding “Frost’s Geography Outside the Testbooks”; contributed to Harper’s magazine a series of papers on Popular Natural History; married August 19, 1858 Phebe Vincent Wheeler; and born to them were:

Children (7)

  • LAURA REDDINGTON FROST 1.2.3.7.2.9.7.1
  • HELEN VINCENT FROST 1.2.3.7.2.9.7.2
  • ALICE THORN FROST 1.2.3.7.2.9.7.3
  • HADLEY FROST 1.2.3.7.2.9.7.4
  • ROBERT LAUNTIZ FROST lawyer in Milwaukee WI 1.2.3.7.2.9.7.5
  • WALTER ARCHER FROST 1.2.3.7.2.9.7.6
  • EDWARD WHEELER FROST 1.2.3.7.2.9.7.7
