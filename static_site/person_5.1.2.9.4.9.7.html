GEORGE CHRISTIAN

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.9.7 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.9.7) GEORGE CHRISTIAN born c1861 Putnam Valley NY and died March 30, 1916 at his home at 20 Glenwood Avenue, Poughkeepsie NY; married 1st Carrie Butler who died on November 24, 1907 Poughkeepsie NY of burns when a lamp exploded; married 2d December 14, 1915 Poughkeepsie NY Melissa Baxter. George was well known in Peekskill and Putnam Valley, where he born. He spent his early life Putnam Valley. About twenty years prior to his death, he went to Poughkeepsie and started a small grocery store, which he conducted until a few weeks before his death. He was known Poughkeepsie as the "Mayor of Gregory Avenue". Christian was a well-known character about town. Every year saw him a special policeman at boat race time. Born to George and Carrie was:
