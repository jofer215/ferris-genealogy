ADELINE S. SCOFIELD

ID: 2.5.3.6.4.1.1.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.6.4.1.1.2) ADELINE S. SCOFIELD born 1834 Stamford CT and died there 1924; buried Roxbury Cemetery; married September 30, 1858 in Stamford, George W. Briggs [son of Alfred & Julia] born 1829 CT and died 1913 Stamford; buried Roxbury Cemetery; a shoemaker in 1860. Born to them were:

Children (3)

  • GEORGE BRIGGS 2.5.3.6.4.1.1.2.1
  • STEPHEN E. BRIGGS 2.5.3.6.4.1.1.2.2
  • IRENE E. BRIGGS 2.5.3.6.4.1.1.2.3
