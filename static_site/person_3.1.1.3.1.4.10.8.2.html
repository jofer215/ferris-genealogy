LELIA LEAH GIRARD

ID: 3.1.1.3.1.4.10.8.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.1.3.1.4.10.8.2) LELIA LEAH GIRARD born October 14, 1891 Cross River and died there January 6, 1966; never married; spent her life in Cross River; her home was across the street from the Reynolds Family Cemetery; had quite a reputation for her many cats she kept

No children recorded
