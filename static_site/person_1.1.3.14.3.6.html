CHARLES HIRMAN WETMORE

ID: 1.1.3.14.3.6 | Source: John
(1.1.3.14.3.6) CHARLES HIRMAN WETMORE born 1846 Durham, Greene, NY and died May 14, 1896 Cornwallville NY; farmer; married 1870 in Durham NY, (Althea H) Alathea Doolittle [dau of William Sutherland & Phebe (Haight) Doolittle] born June 4, 1851 Durham and died December 15, 1897 Cornwallville NY (NYC); in 1873-74 he was Collector for the Durham School District #4; 1886-87 on Board of Trustees for District #4; and born to them were:
