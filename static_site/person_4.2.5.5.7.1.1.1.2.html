SYLVAN EUGENE HIXSON

ID: 4.2.5.5.7.1.1.1.2 | Source: Mary
(4.2.5.5.7.1.1.1.2) SYLVAN EUGENE HIXSON born 1861 Tampico or Yorktown IL and died June 4, 1939 Geneseo IL; farmer; military veteran; married February 4, 1880 in Lorain IL, Harriet Emma (Fantz) Miller [dau of Jacob & Elizabeth (Fantz) Miller] born January 4, 1861 Atkinson IL and died January 18, 1931 Nampa ID; both buried there Kohlerlawn; their entire twig has removed as per request from a descendant.

No children recorded
