PAULINE LOCKWOOD

ID: 5.1.2.9.4.4.2.1.3.3.1 | Source: James
(5.1.2.9.4.4.2.1.3.3.1) PAULINE LOCKWOOD married 1st Mr. Kurtz; married 2d October 23, 1971 at the Mount Carmel Baptist Church, Bryan Joseph Greene [son of George & Lennie (Allen)] born July 6, 1912 New Brunswick, Canada and died June 26, 1995 Carmel NY; buried Raymond Hill Cemetery, Carmel NY. Obituary, Putnam Courier, Carmel, NY, Thursday, June 29, 1995: Bryan Joseph "Joe" Greene, a longtime Carmel resident, died June 26 at Putnam Hospital Center. He was 82. He was raised and educated in both Canada and Vermont, where the family had moved. Mr. Greene moved to Carmel more than 50 years ago and worked as a carpenter and bricklayer. He later worked as a deputy for the Putnam County Sheriff's Department for 20 years prior to retiring in about 1975. Following his retirement from the Sheriff's Department, Mr. Greene worked with his wife as store manager of Kurtz's Stationery & Paper Store in Carmel. A member of Local 5 of the Masons and Bricklayers Union, and former member of the deputies Police Benevolent Association, Mr. Greene attended Mount Carmel Baptist Church. In addition to his wife, survivors include one son, Jeffrey Greene of Tuscaloosa, Ala; one daughter, Judy Tellier of Peoria, Ill.; two stepsons, James Kurtz and Robert Kurtz, both of Carmel; three stepdaughters, Janet Lindquist and Pauline Moore, both of Carmel; and Patricia Harrison of Woodstock; two sisters, Devota Welsh of Frazier Park, Calif. and Ellen Parker of Dallas, Texas; 13 grandchildren and seven great grandchildren.
