ANDREW HILL

ID: 3.6.3.2.2.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.6.3.2.2.2) ANDREW HILL born October 15, 1788 probably Ulster Co NY and died February 8, 1859 Flatbrookville, Sussex, NJ; buried Flatbrookville NJ; married July 24, 1808 in Sussex Co, Elizabeth Garris [dau of Leonard & Catherine Sabilla (Smith) Garis] born January 12, 1785 probably in Northampton Co PA and died November 1, 1853 Sussex Cou; buried Flatbrookville NJ. Andrew owned 187+ acres, known as the homestead farm and this was sold to Jacob and Samuel Garris, March 4, 1861. Born to them were:
