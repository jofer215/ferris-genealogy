WILLAM HENRY ST JOHN

ID: 2.5.2.4.1.6.2 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.4.1.6.2) WILLAM HENRY ST JOHN born July 3, 1813 Stamford – upon the death of his father, choose Richard Fox, of Stamford, to be his guardian – and died there September 17, 1871; 1850-71 shoemaker; married Mary Barbara Calhoun (1820-); and born to them were:
