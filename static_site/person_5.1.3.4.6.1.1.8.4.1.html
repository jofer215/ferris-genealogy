ANNE FERRIS BEEKLEY

ID: 5.1.3.4.6.1.1.8.4.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.6.1.1.8.4.1) ANNE FERRIS BEEKLEY born January 26, 1928 Charleston WVA; married June 5, 1950 Mark Edward Waldo, an Episcopal clergyman; there was a Rev. Mark Edward Waldo who became the fifth Rector of the Church of the Ascension, Montgomery AL 1961; retired 1989; same one? Both Anne and Mark are graduates of William and Mary College.
