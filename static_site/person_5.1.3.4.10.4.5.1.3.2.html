CHARLOTTE LAVONNE SANDROCK

ID: 5.1.3.4.10.4.5.1.3.2 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.10.4.5.1.3.2) CHARLOTTE LAVONNE SANDROCK born August 11, 1935 Sterling IL; married February 19, 1956 in Rock Falls, Jack Lee Duling [son of James & Lois (Jackson)] born October 30, 1932 Indianapolis IN; USN 1951-55 as a fireman (M/M2); USAF 1956-60, taught electronics and got out as a Staff Sergeant. After leaving the service he was a service engineer with Collins Radio Company, a division of Rockwell Corp after 1972. He was with them for 38 years and traveled 32 years of that. They reside in Shellsburg IA. LaVonne is the provider of the updated info on this “twig” – Thanks LaVonne! Born to them were:

No children recorded
