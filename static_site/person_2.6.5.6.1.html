CHARLES KNAPP

ID: 2.6.5.6.1 | Source: Peter
(2.6.5.6.1) CHARLES KNAPP born March 27, 1779 at Wilton CT and died there December 16, 1826; he was buried at the Hillside Cemetery, Wilton CT with his wives. He married, first, December 19, 1800, at New Canaan CT, Betsey Davenport born October 12, 1777 and died July 28, 1805{2 months after childbirth}; married 2d November 23, 1806 at Wilton, Rachel Marie Sterling born May 25, 1781 and died June 23, 1843. Born to Charles and Betsey were:
