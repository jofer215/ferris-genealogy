STEWART SEDGWICK LANE

ID: 5.1.3.5.1.5.2.2.4 | Source: James
(5.1.3.5.1.5.2.2.4) STEWART SEDGWICK LANE born April 29, 1936 Iowa City IA; married Leslie Phyllis Burt; believe this is the same guy: The Aloes of Malawi by Steward S. Lane: “The Rev. Lane has attempted to produce a book in which the information is accessible and usefl to both novices and experts with two descriptions of each variety; one containing field characteristics for those with little botanical knowledge, and a second more technical and detailed one for those with extensive knowledge of the genus. Stewart Lane came to Malawi in 1965 as an English teacher and quickly became interested in the genus ALOE as something uniquely African and completely different from anything in his native Iowa. Since then, he has taken whatever time was free from his work as a teacher, priest, fellowship leader, author and columnist to travel extensively in Malawi and elsewhere in southern Africa collecting and begging specimens for his garden and, within Malawi, gleaning much hithero unpublished information about the aloes here.” Peculiar Honours is Stewart’s memoir of his time in Malawi; and born to them were:
