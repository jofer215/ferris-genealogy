WILLIAM CHADEAYNE MAGNER

ID: 1.2.5.4.2.1.10.5.1.2 | Source: John
(1.2.5.4.2.1.10.5.1.2) WILLIAM CHADEAYNE MAGNER (Photos) born November 23, 1921 and died 1981 Lady Lake FL; buried Hillcrest Memorial Gardens, Leesburg FL; married April 29, 1945 in Ossining, Virgina Lee Zachary born October 7, 1919 Ashville NC and died June 19, 1986, Lakeland FL; buried Hillcrest Memorial Garden. William was a Staff Sergeant serving in France during WWII. "Bill" Magner was an Ossining High School athlete, competing in many sports. He owned a boat that was kept at Shattemuc in Ossining, and loved to spend time cruising the Hudson River.He worked for the telephone company in Ossining, and moved to Florida in the early 1960's. He traveled in Florida and owned and operated various businesses. No offspring. (1.2.5.4.2.1.10.5.2) HELEN ELIZABETH CHADEAYNE (Photos) born December 28, 1897 and died October 4, 1979; buried Trenton NJ; married November 25, 1933 Samuel Pittenger Scott born July 7, 1896 and died October 7, 1967; buried Trenton NJ. Helen, better known as “Bobby” was an artist who designed pictures for greeting cards. She was very proud of the fact that one of her designs was chosen to decorate the cover of the menu at the State Banquet given for Charles Lindbergh following his historical flight. After being widowed, she moved to Florida and spent her twilight years being cared for by her nephew, William C., and his wife Ginny. Beloved and better known as “Pappy”, Sam had a tremendous sense of humor. He served in WWI and was an active member of the American Legion. He was the tax collector for the City of Trenton NJ for many years.

No children recorded
