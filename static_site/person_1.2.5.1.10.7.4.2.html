LUELLA JOSEPHINE SUTTON

ID: 1.2.5.1.10.7.4.2 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.10.7.4.2) LUELLA JOSEPHINE SUTTON born February 24, 1879 Greenville MI and died Janu 6, 1953 Lake Odessa, Ionia, MI; seamstress, clerk; married October 5, 1904 in Battle Creek, William Samuel Turner born December 23, 1859 and died January 9, 1947 Battle Creek; both buried Hicks Cemetery, Pennfield MI; mailman; and they adopted a daughter

No children recorded
