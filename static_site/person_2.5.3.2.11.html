HAIT SCOFIELD

ID: 2.5.3.2.11 | Source: Peter
(2.5.3.2.11) HAIT SCOFIELD born June 1, 1756 Stamford – interesting that his name is spelled this way – and died July 16, 1840; served as a Private 1776-81; married April 6, 1783 Abigail Weed [dau of Silvanus & Sarah (Waring) Weed] born September 16, 1761 Stamford and died there November 6, 1839; both buried there Newfield Cemetery; and born to them were:

Children (6)

  • ABIJAH SCOFIELD 2.5.3.2.11.1
  • SILVANUS SCOFIELD 2.5.3.2.11.2
  • ABIGAIL SCOFIELD 2.5.3.2.11.3
  • MARIA SCOFIELD 2.5.3.2.11.4
  • HARRIS SCOFIELD 2.5.3.2.11.5
  • LOUISA SCOFIELD 2.5.3.2.11.6
