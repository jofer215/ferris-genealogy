PATIENCE MARY SUTTON

ID: 1.2.5.1.10.7.4.3.3 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.10.7.4.3.3) PATIENCE MARY SUTTON born July 2, 1910 Battle Creek MI and died there August 22, 1982; also resided Hammond & Gary IN; Avoc crafts; married September 12, 1927 as his 1st wife, Maurice Philip Jarvis [son of John T & Mary (Bradley) Jarvis] born August 9, 1908 Bellevue MI; both buried Newbre Cemetery, Battle Creek; WWII Vet Army Corporal; and they had 2 kids

No children recorded
