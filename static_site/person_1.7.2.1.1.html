EFFINGHAM LAWRENCE

ID: 1.7.2.1.1 | Source: John
(1.7.2.1.1) EFFINGHAM LAWRENCE born June 6, 1760 NYC and died December 13, 1800 Flushing NY; married 1786 Elizabeth Watson [dau of Thomas & Elizabeth (Horner) Watson] born 1764 NJ and died 1852 Flushing; and born to them was: (1.7.2.1.1.1) JOHN WATSON LAWRENCE (Photos) born August 19, 1800 NYC (Flushing NY) and died December 20, 1888 Flushing NY; buried Flushing Cemetery; attended local schools; engaged as a mercantile clerk; president of the Village of Flushing 1835-45; member of the State Assembly 1840-41; was extensively interested in banking; elected as Democrat to the 29th Congress (March 4, 1845 – March 3, 1847); declined to be candidate for renomination in 1846 and also declined the Democratic nomination for Lt. Governor of NY; resumed banking pursuits; trustee of the Village of Flushing 1860-75; built Willow Bank 1836, President 7th Ward National Bank; married December 5, 1826 Mary King Bowne [dau of Walter & Elizabeth (Southgate) Bowne] born July 25, 1808 NYC (Flushing, Queens, NY) and died August 3, 1874 Flushing; see The Lawrence Family Letters at Willow Bank, Flushing NY, 1846-96, published in 1996, contains 120 letters exchanged between the children of John and Mary; and born to them were:

No children recorded
