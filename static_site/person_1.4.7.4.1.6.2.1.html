ESTHER HYATT

ID: 1.4.7.4.1.6.2.1 | Source: John
(1.4.7.4.1.6.2.1) ESTHER HYATT born October 5, 1900 and died October 11, 1965; married July 14, 1925 in NYC, Paul Kingsley Read (Photo) born September 16, 1900 Medford MA and died October 3, 1972 Lake Placid NY; and they had a daughter; she was a member of National Society Magna Charta Dames and Barons
