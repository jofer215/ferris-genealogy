LUCY W WEED

ID: 4.6.5.9.3.2.9 | Source: Mary
(4.6.5.9.3.2.9) LUCY W WEED born March 23, 1826 Livingston Co NY and died May 3, 1870; married December 30, 1845 Winsor Otis [son of Dexter & Polly (Waite) Otis] born 1827 Willoughby OH and died in the 1880s; both buried Waite Hill Cemetery, Lake Co OH

No children recorded
