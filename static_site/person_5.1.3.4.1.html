DEBORAH FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.4.1 | Source: James
(5.1.3.4.1) DEBORAH FERRIS born March 31 (per Bible) or May 31 (per tombstone), 1762, Greenwich CT and died September 3, 1805; buried Hamilton Co NY (OH?); married February 12, 1794 Hezekiah Stites, Sr., born August 31, 1761 Scotch Plains NJ and died December 8, 1842 Bath Township, Franklin Co IN [son of Benjamin and Rachel Stites]. They moved to Ohio; pioneers of the West 1788. After Deborah’s death, Hezekiah married, Elizabeth Ferris (3.1.1.1.4.4). See her for their offspring. He was a Revolutionary War soldier; a Sergeant; and received a pension. Born to Deborah and Hezekiah were:

Children (5)

  • PHOEBE STITES 5.1.3.4.1.2
  • MARY STITES 5.1.3.4.1.3
  • JEDUTHAN STITES 5.1.3.4.1.4
  • HEZEKIAH STITES, JR., 5.1.3.4.1.5
