GEORGE SMITH NEWMAN

ID: 3.11.1.1.3.2.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.1.1.3.2.4) GEORGE SMITH NEWMAN born October 31, 1830 Plessis, Jefferson, NY and died July 14, 1898 Delaware Co IA; buried Oakland Cemetery, Manchester IA; George worked on his father’s farm until he was 23 when he went on the overland route to California. After remaining there but a short time, he sailed for Australia, where he remained for 9 months; on the return voyage, he visited places in South America, Central America, and Mexico; he stopped in California, where he worked in the gold mines for several years, returning home by way of Panama and arrived in NY 1862; in February 1865 he moved to Tower Hill IA where he married, May 20, 1865, Mary Ann Bailey [dau of William & Margaret (McBride) Bailey] born October 31, 1835 Theresa, Jefferson, NY and died March 27, 1918 Manchester, Delaware, IA; buried with George; and born to them were:

Children (5)
