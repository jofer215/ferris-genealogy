SHADRACK HOYT

ID: 2.5.2.3.2.8 | Source: Peter
(2.5.2.3.2.8) SHADRACK HOYT (#3510) born May 7, 1776 and died May 26, 1833 probably at Stamford where he lived; was called Shadrack, Jr., on the town records; was a sea-faring man; built vessels and traded between New York and the West Indies; married January 1, 1802, Hannah Shaw (3.6.6.1.4); and born to them were:
