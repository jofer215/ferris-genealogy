FRANK PELL UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.3.1.2.1.3.3.1.1 | Source: John
(1.2.3.1.2.1.3.3.1.1) FRANK PELL UNDERHILL (1877-) (1887-1932) resided Norwalk CT 1900; pharmacologist; Ph.B, Yale 1900, Ph.D, 1903; assistant in physiological chemistry, Yale 1900-03; instructor 1903-07; assistant professor 1907-12; professor of pathological chemistry 1912-18; professor of experimental medicine 1918-21; professor of pharmacology and toxicology 1921-32; research material including data on poison gas collected during WWI by the Chemical Warfare Services and for his book: The Lethal War Gases (1919); his papers are at the Yale library; married 1903 Lavina Reed Chasmor

No children recorded
