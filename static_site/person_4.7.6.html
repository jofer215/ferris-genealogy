JOSHUA LOUNSBURY

ID: 4.7.6 | Source: Mary
(4.7.6) JOSHUA LOUNSBURY (#91) (JOSIAH) (Lounsberry) born July 1, 1716 Stamford and died 1787 North Castle NY; married 1st 1740 (May 3, 1739) Hannah Scofield (1718-1739/49) [dau of Samuel & Hannah (Weed or Mead) Scofield]; there is confusion of Hannah’s death date, either 1739 or 1749, which confuses the mother of the offspring below – I would think 1739 is far fetching meaning she would have been 21 when she died and the mother of 3 kids; married, second, Martha {maiden name unk} (1726-1813); and born to Joshua and Hannah were (all in Stamford):
