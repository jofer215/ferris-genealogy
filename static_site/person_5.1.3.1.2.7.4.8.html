HIRAM BURNS FERRIS

ID: 5.1.3.1.2.7.4.8 | Source: James
(5.1.3.1.2.7.4.8) HIRAM BURNS FERRIS (Major) born January 25, 1872 Carthage IL (or November 11, 1876, Kansas City MO) and died August 15, 1962 Redwood City CA; graduate U of Illinois 1894; a master mechanic; married Minnie D {maiden name unk}; they resided 1900 Leavenworth KS; 1920 Kansas City KS
