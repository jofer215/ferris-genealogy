JOHN LOCKWOOD, Jr.,

ID: 4.6.3.1 | Source: Mary
(4.6.3.1) JOHN LOCKWOOD, Jr., (#251) born January 10, 1737/38, Stamford. John was a Private in the 5th Regiment of New York, January 28, 1777 and served three years, being discharged, January 1, 1780. In December, 1780, John and 37 other petitioners of Norwalk, to the General Assembly, that in July, 1779, all of the subscribers were burned out of house and home, and stripped and plundered by the British of nearly all their household furniture; their barns destroyed by fire, with the greater part of their wheat harvest and hay. He married, May 31, 1761, Hannah Hait (Hoyt) (2.5.3.6.1). Born to them were:
