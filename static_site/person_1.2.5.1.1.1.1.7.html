ALFRED CLEMENT HOVEY

ID: 1.2.5.1.1.1.1.7 | Source: John
(1.2.5.1.1.1.1.7) ALFRED CLEMENT HOVEY born April 15, 1882 White Church, Wyandotte, KS and died January 27, 1957 Olathe KS; married 1st January 20, 1904 Mary Angeline Duncan born July 7, 1886 Sheridan Co IN [dau of William Ewan and Phoebe Ann (McMinds) Duncan] - divorced; and 2d Ione Rhodes born January 30, 1923 Kansas City KS.
