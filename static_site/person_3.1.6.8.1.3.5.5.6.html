PERCIVAL VICTOR ROSS

ID: 3.1.6.8.1.3.5.5.6 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.1.3.5.5.6) PERCIVAL VICTOR ROSS born July 19, 1915 Chipman, Alberta and died Saskatoon, Sask.; investment sales manager; enlisted in the RCNVR as a sub-lieutenant and served on the Athabasca, which did both convoy work in the Atlantic and also served in the English Channel during preparations for D-Day Invasion; Internet says the Athabasca was sunk in the England Channel, 1944, by the Germans; married October 3, 1943 Edna Cammaert born June 8, 1919 Rockyford, Alberta and died Saskatoon, Sasks; she was a public health nurse; and they had 3 kids, 1 of which was:
