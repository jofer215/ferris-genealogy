EZEKIEL JOHN JAMES ISRAEL ROSE

ID: 3.1.6.8.3.7.5 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.6.8.3.7.5) EZEKIEL JOHN JAMES ISRAEL ROSE born July 2, 1847 Liberty Township, Licking, OH and died January 23, 1930 Clark Co MO; buried Frazee Cemetery, Wayland, Clark, MO; married February 28, 1875 at Alexandria MO, Rachel Lewigo Sheets born December 3, 1852 Miami Co OH and died April 3, 1900 Clark Co MO; buried with her husband. Ezekiel went by Israel and always signed on letter, E.J.J.I. Rose; served with Company E., 7th Iowa Infantry as a Private during the Civil War, enlisting January 1864 and discharged July 12, 1865. Rachel was the sister to Mary Elizabeth Sheets who married Richard Wood Rose. Born to them were:
