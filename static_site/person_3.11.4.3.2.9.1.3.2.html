HELEN WATERMAN BAILEY

ID: 3.11.4.3.2.9.1.3.2 | Source: Joseph
(3.11.4.3.2.9.1.3.2) HELEN WATERMAN BAILEY born September 21, 1880 Grand Rapids, Kent, MI and died May 23, 1961 Plainfield Township, Kent Co MI; married October 2, 1901 in Grand Rapids, Judson Grant Ellsworth [dau of John C. & Martha E. (Bird) Ellsworth] born February 6, 1874 Grand Rapids and died there April 6, 1933. Born to them were: (3.11.4.3.2.9.1.3.2.1) RUSSELL BAILEY ELLSWORTH born June 17, 1901 Plainfield Township, Kent, MI and died December 11, 1985 Grand Rapids, Kent, MI; married August 1, 1925 in Grand Rapids, Shirly Louise Purchase [dau of Milo Delos & Clara (Conger) Purchase] born August 12, 1900 Grand Rapids and died there August 9, 1986.
