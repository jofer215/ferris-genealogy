JOHN A. FERRIS

ID: 3.1.2.3.4 | Source: Joseph
(3.1.2.3.4) JOHN A. FERRIS born c1758 (1762-64) probably in Nine Partners, Dutchess, NY and died March 3, 1853 Plattsburg NY {probably by Peter’s first wife}; enlisted in the Dutchess Co Militia; from Oswego, Beekman Patent 1764; applied for his pension April 14, 1819 at Clinton Co NY when age 55 a resident of Plattsburg NY; May 12, 1829 he stated that he was 67y of age; 1829 he owned 5 acres of land in Plattsburg; placed on the pension rolls January 20, 1830; married 1st Mary/Polly DeWitt; and 2d November 5, 1836 at Plattsburg, widow Freelove Addison born c1781 and died February 8, 1856; and born to John and Mary were;

Children (2)

  • JOHN A. FERRIS, JR., 3.1.2.3.4.2
