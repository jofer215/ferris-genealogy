HAROLD ELLIOTT FERRIS “Harry” born September 24, 1888

ID: 1.6.1.1.3.1.1.2.5.2 | Source: John
(1.6.1.1.3.1.1.2.5.2) HAROLD ELLIOTT FERRIS “Harry” born September 24, 1888 Armonk NY and died December 1932 White Plains NY; married October 23, 1908 Maude Hopkins [dau of Hanford & Carrie Eugene (Finkle) Hopkins] born 1893 White Plains NY and died there April 6, 1966; both buried St. Stevens Cemetery, Armonk. Harold was a carpenter by trade and died after getting pneumonia while building a house for his family in the winter. Born to them were:

Children (2)
