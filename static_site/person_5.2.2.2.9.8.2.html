ALBERT L. BUXTON

ID: 5.2.2.2.9.8.2 | Source: James
(5.2.2.2.9.8.2) ALBERT L. BUXTON born September 25, 1843 Stamford CT and died there December 19, 1880; buried Lockwood #5 Cemetery, Stamford; 1860 shoemaker; married 1st Emma A. (Ann) {maiden name unk} born c1845 (born 1850 England); married 2d, October 11, 1865 in Stamford, Sarah Louise Knapp (2.6.2.10.1.4.3.2); marriage sequence has been reversed in some sources

No children recorded
