JOHN UNDERHILL

ID: 1.2.4.1 | Source: John
(1.2.4.1) JOHN UNDERHILL (1719-1784); born Westchester Co.; of Ulster Co.; and died Carlisle, Schoharie, NY; married 1st 1771 Deborah Dickinson (c1740-); and married 2d Sarah Thorne who died at age 84. Born to John and Deborah was (may be more):

Children (8)

  • HENRY UNDERHILL 1.2.4.1.1
  • ISAAC UNDERHILL of Ulster County; died at approximate age of 65 1.2.4.1.2
  • DANIEL UNDERHILL 1.2.4.1.3
  • NATHANIEL UNDERHILL, of Orange County; married Phoebe {maiden 1.2.4.1.4
  • SAMUEL UNDERHILL, of New York; died October 2, 1870. 1.2.4.1.5
  • AMY UNDERHILL 1.2.4.1.6
  • PHOEBE UNDERHILL 1.2.4.1.7
